Mahe Ramazan ke aakhari Juma ki dua’e…

1) Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) ne farmaya, jo shakhs is dua ko Mahe Ramazan ke aakhari Juma ko padhe-ga to Allah (s.w.t) usko ya to agla Mahe Ramazan naseeb karega ya phir uske saare gunah bakhsh dega…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Khudaaya is Mahe Ramazan ke rozo ko meri zindagi ke aakhri roze na qaraar dena aur agar Teri marzi yahi hai to mujhe marhoom qaraar dena, mehroom qaraar na dena.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

2) Dua Sahifa Sajjadiya 45th padhe.

3) Yeh dua padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Khudaaya is Mahe Ramazan ko aur mere roze ko aakhari roza qarar na dena, aur mein us baat se panaah chahta hu keh is raat ki subah ho jaaye siwaaye uske keh mujhe maaf na kar de.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

4) Phir yeh dua padhe…

Inshallah, Coming Soon

5) Al-Wida dua…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Khudaaya yeh woh Mahe Ramazan hai jis mein Tu ne Qur'an naazil kiya aur yeh ab guzar raha hai, mai Teri zaate karim ki panaah chahta hu, us baat se keh Parwadigaar is raat ki subah ho jaaye aur Mahe Ramazan guzar jaaye aur mere zimmeh koi moakhezah ya koi gunah rahe jaaye jis par Tu us deen azaaab kareyga jab mai Tujh se mulaqaat karu.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

AL-WIDA JUMA

Dua for Last Friday of Mahe Ramazan…
1) Holy Prophet s.a.w.a) said, “The one who recite this dua on last Friday of the month of Ramazan would get either of the 2 good things۔ Either he would live till the next Ramazan or the Lord would grant him pardon and limitless mercy…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah let not my days of fasting in (this) month of Ramazan be the last obligation (end of my life term). I take refuge with Thee lest the dawn of this night breaks forth without me having been positively forgiven.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

2) Recite 45th Dua of Sahifa Sajjadiya.

3) Recite the following Dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah the month of Ramazan, in which Thou revealed the Holy Qur’an, now is at its end. I take refuge with Thy kindest disposition, O Lord, lest the dawn of this night breaks forth, or the month of Ramazan comes to an end, and I stand as an accused sinner, liable to be punished, on the Day I am presented before Thee.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

4) Then recite this dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, I beg You for the sake of Your favorite channel through which You are invoked and through the medium of the favorite deed of Muhammad and the Ahle Bayt of Muhammad (a.s.) that You bless him and them and do not make this farewell of this month as my farewell from this world and do not make this worship act my last worship act in the world and decree the rewards of Shab-e-Qadr for me and make it for me better than a thousand months. And accept my supplications and good deeds and forgive my sins with the pleasure of the Lord.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

5) Farewell dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah the month of Ramadhan, in which Thou revealed the Holy Qur'an, now is at its end. I take refuge with Thy kindest disposition, O Lord, lest the dawn of this night breaks forth, or the month of Ramadhan comes to an end, and I stand as an accused sinner, liable to be punished, on the Day I am presented before Thee.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

6) Recite the following Farewell dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, I beg You for the humility of those who humble themselves. For the sincerity of those who have certainty and the companionship of the righteous and the realization of the realities of faith and all the good deeds and safety from every sin and to be eligible for Your mercy and determination to get Your forgiveness and success for Paradise immunity from Hell fire.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
