106 | QURAISH | 4

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Quresh ke uns-o-ulfat ki khaatir.

2. Jo unhe sardi aur garmi ke safar se hai.

3. Lehaaza inhe chaahiye keh is ghar ke maalik ki ibaadat kare.

4. Jisne inhe bhook mein sair kiya hai aur khauf se mehfooz banaya hai.

106 | QURAISH | 4

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. For the protection of the Quraish—

2. Their protection during their trading caravans in the winter and the summer—

3. So let them serve the Lord of this House,

4. Who feeds them against hunger and gives them security against fear.
