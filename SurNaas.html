114 | NAAS | 6

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Ay Rasool!Kahe dijiye keh mai insaano ke Parwardigaar ki panah chahta hu.

2. Jo tamaam logo ka maalik aur badshah hai.

3. Saare insaano ka maabood hai.

4. Shaitaani was-wase ke shar se jo naam-e-Khuda soon kar peechhe hatt jaata hai.

5. Aur jo logo ke dilo mein was-wasey paiyda karaata hai.

6. Woh jinnaato mein se ho ya insaano mein se.

114 | NAAS | 6

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of men,

2. The King of men,

3. The God of men,

4. From the evil of the whisperings of the slinking (Shaitan),

5. Who whispers into the hearts of men,

6. From among the jinn and the men.
