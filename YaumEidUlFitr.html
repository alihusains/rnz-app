YAUM EID-UL-FITR

1) Fitra is the condition of acceptance of the fasts as mentioned by Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) that paying of Fitra completes the fast. Thus fasts are not accepted from one who does not pay the Fitra. Fitra is the Zakat of the body, which purifies it from every spiritual dirt and protects from calamities till the next year. The quantum of Fitra is 3 kilograms per head and must be paid in form of the staple diet of that area, which must be paid on behalf of all the dependents; so much so that even if a child is born before sunset, his Fitra is also obligatory. One person must not be given less than one Fitra, but there is no limit for more. Money equal to the value of the material can also be paid۔ Fitra of a Sayyad can be given to the Sayyad as well as the non-Sayyad but the Fitra of a non-Sayyad cannot be given to the Sayyad. It depends upon the person who is paying the Fitra and not on whose behalf he is paying. The Fitra of the wife is obligatory on the husband even though she might herself be in a profession, but if she comes to know that her husband has not paid the Fitra, it is Mustahab for her to pay it and the same rule applies to guests as well.
1) Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) farmaate hai, keh Fitra, Rozo ki qabooliyat aur mukammal hone ki shart hai, goya jisne Fitra nahi nikaala uske Roze qabool nahi kiye jaayege. Fitra badan ki zakat hai, jo roohani bimariyo aur pareshaniyo ko agle saal tak ka dur karta hai. 1 Fitra ki mighdaar 3kg woh anaaj jo aam taur par gizah ke liye istemaal hoti ho. Ghar ke sabhi afraad ka Fitra jo ghar ka maalik (jo ghar waalo ka kharch sambhalta) ho uspar laazim hai yahan tak keh agar kisi bachhe ki wiladat bhi hoti hai Guroobe Aftaab se pehle (before Sunset) to uska Fitra nikaal na bhi waajib hai. Biwi ka Fitra Shauhar par waajib hai agar-che woh khud kyu na kamaati ho. Magar agar biwi ko pata chale keh uske shauhar ne uska fitra nahi nikaala to us par mustahab hai keh woh khud nikaale aur yahi kanoon Mahemaano ke liye bhi hai. Ek afraad ko kam as kam 1 Fitra diya ja sakta hai aur ziyada dene ki koi qaid nahi hai. Sayyad ka Fitra gaiyr Sayyad ko diya ja sakta hai magar gaiyr Sayyad ka Fitra Sayyad ko nahi diya ja sakhta aur yeh fitra nikaal ne waale se moayyan hoga (Sayyad ya Gaiyr Sayyad) na ke jiska fitra usne nikaala hai.

5) Mustahab hai keh Allah ka naam le jab Eid Namaz ke liye ghar se niklo aur yeh dua bhi padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Jo shakhs amaada hai aaj ke din ya qamar-basta hai, ya taiyyari karta aur taiyyar hota hai logo’n ki taraf jaane ko us ummeed se keh un se naqdi cheeze’n, bakhshishe aur ataae’n le.

Lekin ay mere Aaqa! Meri taiyyari, meri amaadgi aur meri saari koshish Teri taraf aane mein hai keh mai ummeed-waar hu Teri ataa, Tere inaam, Teri inaayato’n, Tere ehsaano, Teri maher-baaniyo’n aur bakhshisho’n ka.

Aur aaj subah ki hai mai ne eke id ke din jo un eido’n mein se hai jo muqarrar ki Tere Nabiye Mohammad (s.a.w.a) ne keh Khuda ki rehmate’n ho un par aur unki Aal (a.s) par aur mai aaj Tere huzoor koi swale amal le kar nahi aaya hu jis ko yaqeeni taur par pesh karu na kisi makhluq ki taraf tawajjoh aur ummeed rakhta hu, haa’n magar Teri jaanib mai aajiz ban kar apne gunaho’n aur khataawo’n ka iqraari ho kar aaya hu.

To ay azmat waale, ay azmat waale, ay azmat waale bakhsh de mere bade-bade gunaho’n ko kyu’n keh Tere siwa koi nahi jo bade-bade gunaho’n ko bakhsh ta ho. Ay woh keh nahi koi Mabood Tere siwa ay sab se ziyada rahem karne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

2) Dua before the Ghusl (bath) of Eid…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, believing in Thee, fully aware of Thy Book, following the way of life of Thy Prophet Muhammad, Allah's blessings be on him and on his children, (I take bath).

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

3) Dua after the Ghusl (bath) of Eid…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah let me make amends and compensate for my wrong doings, (so that) my religion becomes pure and O Allah remove the dirt from me.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

5) It is Mustahab to mention the name of Allah while leaving for Eid prayers and to recite this Dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, if one, on this day, prepares oneself, takes pains, gets ready, or prepares oneself to visit another one, hoping for his aid, gifts, benefits, and grants; for You alone, my Lord, is my readiness, preparations, taking pains, training, and inclination in the hope of gaining Your aid, gifts, grants, benefits, bounties, and donations. I find myself celebrating one of the feasts commemorated by the nation of Your Prophet, Muhammad, blessings of Allah be upon him and his Household. I have not come to You, today, carrying a righteous deeds that I may trust to present before You nor have I introduced a creature as my interceder before You; rather, I submissively draw near You confessing of my wrongdoings and my offenses to myself. So, O All-great, O All-great, O All-great, (please) forgive my grave wrongdoings, for, verily, no one can forgive serious transgressions save You. O He save Whom there is no god! O most Merciful of all the merciful!

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
