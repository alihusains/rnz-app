Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Woh Khuda bada maher-baan hai.

2. Us ne Qur’an ki taaleem di hai.

3. Insaan ko paiyda kiya hai.

4. Aur usey bayaan sikhaaya hai.

5. Aftaab-o-mehtaab sab usi ke muqarrar kardah hisaab ke saath chal rahe hai.

6. Aur bootiya (kali), bele (phool) aur darakht sab usi ka sajdah kar rahe hai.

7. Us ne aasmaan ko buland kiya hai aur insaaf ke taraazu qaayem ki hai.

8. Ta-keh tum log wazn (weight) mein hadd se tajaawuz na karo.

9. Aur insaaf ke saath wazn (weight) ko qaayem karo aur taul-ne mein kam na taulo.

10. Aur Usi ne zameen ko insaano ke liye waazeh kiya hai.

11. Us mein mewe hai aur woh khajoore hai jin ke khosho (daano) par ghilaaf (cover) chadhe huwe hai.

12. Woh daane hai jin ke saath bhus (cover) hota hai aur khushbodaar phool bhi hai.

13. Ab tum dono apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

14. Us ne insaan ko thikre (pakayi hui mitti) ki tarah khankhanaati (awaaz karti) huwi mitti se paiyda kiya hai.

15. Aur jinnaat ko aag ke sholo se paiyda kiya hai.

16. To tum dono apne Parwardigaar ki kis-kis nemat ko juthlaawoge.

17. Woh chaand aur suraj dono ke mashriq aur maghrib ka maalik hai.

18. Phir tum dono apne Rab ki kin-kin nemato ko juthlaawoge.

19. Us ne do dariya bahaaye hai jo aapas mein mil jaate hai.

20. Unke darmiyaan hadde faasil hai keh ek dusre par zayadti nahi kar sakte.

21. To tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ko juthlaawoge.

22. Un dono dariyaao se moti aur mongey (jawaaheraat / jewellery) bar-aamad hote hai.

23. To tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ko juthlaawoge.

24. Usi ke woh jahaaz bhi hai jo dariya mein pahaado ki tarah khade rehte hai.

25. To tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ko juthlaawoge.

26. Jo bhi ruye-zameen par hai sab fanah (destroy) ho jaane waale hai.

27. Sirf tumhaare Rab ki zaat jo saheb-e-jalaal-o-ikraam hai wohi baaqi rehne waali hai.

28. To tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ko juthlaawoge.

29. Aasmaan-o-zameen mein jo bhi hai sab usi se sawaal karte hai aur Woh har roz ek nayi shaan waala hai.

30. To tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ko juthlaawoge.

31. Ay dono giroh Ham an-qareeb hi tumhaari taraf mutwajjah hoge.

32. To tum apne Parwardigaar ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

33. Ay giroh jin-o-ins agar tum mein qudrat ho keh aasmaan-o-zameen ke atraaf se baahar nikal jaawo to nikal jaawo magar yaad rakkho keh tum quwwat aur ghalba ke bagair nahi nikal sakte ho.

34. To tum apne Parwardigaar ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

35. Tumhare upar aag ka sabz shola aur dhuwa (smoke) chhod diya jaayega to tum dono kisi tarah nahi rok sakte ho.

36. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

37. Phir jab aasmaan phatt kar teyl (oil) ki tarah surkh ho jaayega.

38. To tum apne Parwardigaar ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

39. Phir us din kisi insaan ya jin se uske gunah ke baare mein sawaal nahi kiya jaayega.

40. To phir tum apne rab ki kis-kis nemat ko juthlaawoge.

41. Mujreem afraad to apni nishaani hi se pehchaan liye jaayege phir peshaani aur pairo se pakad liye jaayege.

42. To tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

43. Yahi woh Jahannam hai jiska mujremeen inkaar kar rahe thay.

44. Ab uske aur kholte huwe paani ke darmiyaan chakkar laga-te phirenge.

45. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

46. Aur jo shakhs bhi apne Rab ki baargah mein khade hone se darta hai uske liye do-do baaghaat hai.

47. Phir tum apne Parwardigaar ki kis-kis nemat ko juthlaawoge.

48. Aur dono baaghaat darakhto ki tehniyo se hare-bhare mewo se lade hoge.

49. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ko juthlaawoge.

50. Un dono mein do chashme bhi jaari hoge.

51. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

52. Un dono mein har mewe ke jode hoge.

53. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

54. Yeh log un farsho partakya (pellow) lagaaye baithay hoge jin ke astar at-las (resham) ke hoge aur dono baaghaat ke mewe intehaai qareeb se haasil kar lege.

55. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

56. Un jannato mein mehdood nighah waali hurre hogi jinko insaan aur jinnaat mein se kisi ne pehle chhuwa bhi na hoga.

57. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

58. Woh hurre is tarah ki hogi jaise surkh yaaqoot aur mongey (jawaaheraat).

59. Phir tum apne Parwardigaar ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

60. Kya ehsaan ka badla ehsaan ke alaawa kuch aur bhi ho sakta hai?

61. To tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

62. Aur un dono ke alaawa do baaghaat aur hoge.

63. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

64. Dono nihaayat darja sar-sabz-o-shadaab hoge.

65. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

66. Un dono baaghaat mein bhi do josh maarte huwe chashme hoge.

67. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

68. Un dono baaghaat mein mewe, khajoore aur anaar hoge.

69. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

70. Un jannato mein nek seerat aur khoob-surat aurte’n hogi.

71. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

72. Woh hurre hai jo khaimo ke andar chhupi baithi hogi.

73. To tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

74. Inhe un se pehle kisi insaan ya jin ne haath tak na lagaaya hoga.

75. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

76. Woh log sabz qaalino aur behtareen masnado par tek lagaaye baithay hoge.

77. Phir tum apne Rab ki kis-kis nemat ka inkaar karoge.

78. Bada ba-barkat hai aap ke Parwardigaar ka naam jo saheb-e-jalaal bhi hai aur saheb-e-ikraam bhi hai.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. The Beneficent God,

2. Taught the Quran.

3. He created man,

4. Taught him the mode of expression.

5. The sun and the moon follow a reckoning.

6. And the herbs and the trees do adore (Him).

7. And the heaven, He raised it high, and He made the balance,

8. That you may not be inordinate in respect of the measure.

9. And keep up the balance with equity and do not make the measure deficient.

10. And the earth, He has set it for living creatures;

11. Therein is fruit and palms having sheathed clusters,

12. And the grain with (its) husk and fragrance.

13. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

14. He created man from dry clay like earthen vessels,

15. And He created the jinn of a flame of fire.

16. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

17. Lord of the East and Lord of the West.

18. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

19. He has made the two seas to flow freely (so that) they meet together:

20. Between them is a barrier which they cannot pass.

21. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

22. There come forth from them pearls, both large and small.

23. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

24. And His are the ships reared aloft in the sea like mountains.

25. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

26. Everyone on it must pass away.

27. And there will endure for ever the person of your Lord, the Lord of glory and honor.

28. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

29. All those who are in the heavens and the earth ask of Him; every moment He is in a state (of glory).

30. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

31. Soon will We apply Ourselves to you, O you two armies.

32. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

33. O assembly of the jinn and the men! If you are able to pass through the regions of the heavens and the earth, then pass through; you cannot pass through but with authority.

34. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

35. The flames of fire and smoke will be sent on you two, then you will not be able to defend yourselves.

36. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

37. And when the heaven is rent asunder, and then becomes red like red hide.

38. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

39. So on that day neither man nor jinni shall be asked about his sin.

40. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

41. The guilty shall be recognized by their marks, so they shall be seized by the forelocks and the feet.

42. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

43. This is the hell which the guilty called a lie.

44. Round about shall they go between it and hot, boiling water.

45. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

46. And for him who fears to stand before his Lord are two gardens.

47. Which then of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

48. Having in them various kinds.
