Chp. 10:Ver. 85/86
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَا لَا تَجْعَلْنَا فِتْنَةً لِّلْقَوْمِ الظّٰلِمِيْنَ۔ وَنَجِّنَا بِرَحْمَتِكَ مِنَ الْقَوْمِ الْكٰفِرِيْنَ۔
O our Lord! Make us not subject to the persecution of the unjust people. And do Thou deliver us by Thy mercy from the unbelieving people.

Khudaaya! Hamein zaalimo ke fitno aur aazmaayisho’n ka markaz (center) na qaraar dena. Aur apni rehmat ke zariye kaafir qaum ke shar se mehfooz rakhna.
