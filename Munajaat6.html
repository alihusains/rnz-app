The Whispered Prayer of the Thankful (Shakereen)…
Shukr karne waalo ki Munajaat…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اِلٰهِىْ اَذْهَلَنِىْ عَنْ اِقَامَةِ شُكْرِكَ تَتَابُعُ طَوْلِكَ وَاَعْجَزَنِىْ عَنْ اِحْصَآءِ ثَنآئِكَ فَيْضُ فَضْلِكَ وَشَغَلَنِىْ عَنْ ذِكْرِ مُحَامِدِكَ تَرَادُفُ عَوَآئِدِكَ وَاَعْيَانِىْ عَنْ نَشْرِعَوَارِفِكَ تَوَالِىْ اَيَادِيْكَ وَهٰذَا مَقَامُ مَنِ اعْتَرَفَ بِسُبُوْغِ النَّعَمَآءِ وَقَابَلَهَا بِالتَّقْصِيْرِ وَشَهِدَ عَلٰى نَفْسِهٖ بِالْاِهْمَالِ وَالتَّضْيِيْعِ۔

وَاَنْتَ الرَّؤُفُ الرَّحِيْمُ الْبَرُّ الْكَرِيْمُ الَّذِىْ لَا يُخَيِّبُ قَاصِدِيْهِ وَلَا يَطْرُدُ عَنْ فِنَآئِهٖ اٰمِلِيْهٖ بِسَاحَتِ تَحُطُّ رِحَالُ الرَّاجِيْنَ وَبِعَرْصَتِكَ تَقِفُ اٰمَالُ الْمُسْتَرْفِدِيْنَ فَلَا تُقَابِلْ اٰمَالَنَا بِالتَّخْيِيْبِ وَالْاِيَاسِ وَلَا تُلْبِسْنَا سِرْبَالَ الْقُنُوْطِ وَالْاِبْلَاسِ۔

اِلٰهِىْ تَصَاغَرَ عِنْدَ تَعَاظُمِ اٰلَآئِكَ شُكْرِىْ وَتَضَآئَلَ فِىْ جَنْبِ اِكْرَامِكَ اِيَّاىَ ثَنَآئِىْ وَنَشْرِىْ جَلَّلَتْنِىْ نِعَمُكَ مِنْ اَنْوَارِ الْاِيْمَانِ حُلَلًا وَضَرَبَتْ عَلَيَّ لَطَائِفُ بِرِّكَ مِنَ الْعِزِّ كِلَلًا وَقَلَّدَتْنِىْ مِنَنُكَ قَلَآئِدَ لَا تُحَلُّ وَطَوَّقَتْنِىْ اَطْوَاقًا لَا تُفَلُّ فَاٰلَآئُكَ جَمَّهٌ ضَعُفَ لِسَانِىْ عَنْ اِحْصَآئِهَا۔

وَنَعْمَآؤُكَ كَثِيْرَةٌ قَصُرَ فَهْمِىْ عَنْ اِدْرَاكِهَا فَضْلًا عَنِ اسْتِقْصَآئِهَا فَكَيْفَ لِىْ بِتَحْصِيْلِ الشُّكْرِ وَشُكْرِىْ اِيَّاكَ يَفْتَقِرُ اِلٰى شُكْرٍ۔ فَكُلَّمَا قُلْتُ لَكَ الْحَمْدُ وَجَبَ عَلَيَّ لِذَالِكَ اَنْ اَقُوْلَ لَكَ الْحَمْدُ۔

اِلٰهِىْ فَكَمَا غَذَّيْتَـنَا بِلُطْفِكَ وَرَبَّيْتَـنَا بِصُنْعِكَ فَتَمِّمْ عَلَيْنَا سَوَابِغَ النِّعَمِ وَادْفَعْ عَنَّا مَكَارِهَ النِّقَمِ وَاٰتِنَا مِنْ حُظُوْظِ الدَّارَيْنِ اَرْفَعَهَا وَاَجَلَّهَا عَاجِلًا وَّاجِلًا۔ وَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ عَلٰى حُسْنِ بَلَآئِكَ وَسُبُوْغِ نَعْمَآئِكَ حَمْدًا يُّوَافِقُ رِضَاكَ وَيَمْتَرِى الْعَظِيْمَ مِنْۢ بِرِّكَ وَنَدَاكَ يَاعَظِيْمُ يَاكَرِيْمُ بِرَحْمَتِكَ يآاَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِيْنَ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

Shukr karne waalo ki Munajaat…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Mere Mabood!Teri lagaataar bakhshisho ne mujhe Tera shukr adaa karne se ghaafil kar diya hai, aur Tere fazl ke tawaatur wo tasalsul ne mujhe Teri hamd-o-sana ka ehaata karne se aajiz kar diya hai aur Teri pai-dar-pai (ek ke baad ek) hone waali maherbaniyo ne mujhe Teri taareefo ke bayaan se be-dhyaan (ghaafil) kar diya aur Teri musalsal ne’mato’n ne mujhe Tere ehsaanaat ke zikr se thaka kar rakh diya. Yehi maqaam hai us shakhs ka jo Teri ne’mato’n ka mo’tarif hai, aur un-par shukr se qaasir hai. Woh Apni is be-dhyaani (ghaflat) aur na-fikri (la-parvaayi) par khud hi gawaah hai aur Tu hi Woh shafeeq wo maherbaan nek-naam sakhi hai jo Apni baargah ka qasd karne waalo ko na-ummeed nahi karta, aur aarzoo-mando ko Apne aastaane se dur nahi karta. Tere hi dar par ummeed-waaro ke kaarwa utar-te hai aur Tere hi maidaan-e-sakhaawat mein taalebaan-e ne’mat ki tamannaye jagah paati hai. Pas hamari chaahato ke muqaable mein na-ummeedi wo yaas (hopeless) na de. Aur hamein na-ummeedi aur pashemaani ka pairahan na pehna.

Mere Mabood! Teri buzurg-tareen ne’mato’n ke saamne mera shukr wo sepaas (thanks) heech (past/low) hai. Teri azmato’n ke muqaabil meri zabaan se Teri taareef wo zikr be-maaya (be-maana) hai. Teri ne’mato’n ne mujhe imaan ke nooraani poshaako se dhaanp diya, aur Teri khush karne waali bhalaayi ne mujhe izzat ke taaj (crown) pehnaaye hai. Tu ne mujhe fakhr ke woh zewar pehnaaye jo utarte nahi aur gardan mein woh haar (neckless) daala jo toot-ta nahi. Teri maherbaniya zeyaada hai meri zabaan unko shumaar karne se aajiz hai aur Teri ne’mate’n kaseer hai, mera fahem (samajh) unko samajhne se qaasir hai. Che-jaaye keh unki tadaad ko jaan sakey, to mai kaise maqaam-e shukr haasil karu keh mera shukr karna bhi mohtaaje shukr hai. To jab mai kahu keh Tere liye hamd hai, uske liye mujh par waajib hai keh mai kahu Tere liye hamd hai. Pas jaise Tu ne hamein Apne lutf se ghiza (food) di aur Apne ehsaan se parwarish ki hai to ham par Apni kaseer ne’mate’n bhi tamaam farma. Aur azaab ki sakhtiya ham se dur kar de, aur hamein duniya wo aakherat mein behtar aur bishtar (bohot zeyaada) hissa ataa farma. Ab bhi aur tab bhi.

Tere liye hamd hai Teri behtareen aazmaaish aur kaseer ne’mato’n par aisi hamd jo Teri raza ke mowaafiq wo motaabiq ho. Aur Tere azeem ehsaan wo bakhshish ko haasil kare, ay Azeem ay Kareem Teri rehmat ka waasta ay sab se zeyaada rahem karne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

The Whispered Prayer of the Thankful (Shakereen)…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

My God, the uninterrupted flow of Thy graciousness hast distracted me from thanking Thee! The flood of Thy bounty has rendered me incapable of counting Thy praises! The succession of Thy kind acts has diverted me from mentioning Thee in laudation! The continuous rush of Thy benefits has thwarted me from spreading the news of Thy gentle favours! This is the station of him who confesses to the lavishness of favours, meets them with shortcomings, and witnesses to his own disregard and negligence.

Thou art the Clement, the Compassionate, the Good, the Generous, who does not disappoint those who aim for Him, nor cast out from His courtyard those who expect from Him! In Thy yard are put down the saddlebags of the hopeful and in Thy plain stand the hopes of the help-seekers! So meet not our hopes by disappointing and disheartening and clothe us not in the shirt of despair and despondency!

My God, my thanksgiving is small before Thy great boons, and my praise and news-spreading shrink beside Thy generosity toward me! Thy favours have wrapped me in the robes of the lights of faith, and the gentlenesses of Thy goodness have let down over me delicate curtains of might! Thy kindnesses have collared me with collars not to be moved and adorned me with neck-rings not to be broken! Thy boons are abundant - my tongue is too weak to count them!

Thy favours are many - my understanding falls short of grasping them, not to speak of exhausting them! So how can I achieve thanksgiving? For my thanking Thee requires thanksgiving. Whenever I say, 'To Thee belongs praise!', it becomes thereby incumbent upon me to say, 'To Thee belongs praise'!

My God, as Thou hast fed us through Thy gentleness and nurtured us through Thy benefaction, so also complete for us lavish favours, repel from us detested acts of vengeance, and of the shares of the two abodes, give us their most elevated and their greatest, both the immediate and the deferred! To Thee belongs praise for Thy good trial and the lavishness of Thy favours, a praise conforming to Thy good pleasure and attracting Thy great goodness and magnanimity. O All-mighty, O All-generous! By Thy mercy, O Most Merciful of the merciful!

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
