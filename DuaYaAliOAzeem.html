بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

یَاعَلِیُّ یَاعَظِیْمَ یَاغَفُوْرُ یَارَحِیْمُ اَنْتَ الرَّبُّ الْعَظِیْمُ الَّذِیْ لَیْسَ کَمِثْلِہٖ شَیْ ئٌ وَہُوَالسَّمِیْعُ الْبَصِیْرُ وَھٰذَا شَہْرٌ عَظَّمْتَہٗ وَکَرَّمْتَہٗ وَشَرَّفْتَہٗ وَفَضَّلْتَہٗ عَلَی الشُّہُوْرِوَہُوَالشَّہْرُ الَّذِیْ فَرَضْتَ صِیَامَہٗ عَلَیَّ وَہُوَ شَہْرُ رَمَضَانَ الَّذِیْ اَنْزَلْتَ فِیْہِ الْقُرْاٰنَ ھُدًی لِّلنَّاسِ وَبَیِّنَاتٍ مِّنَ الْھُدٰی وَالْفُرْقَانِ وَجَعَلْتَ فِیْہِ لَیْلَۃَ الْقَدْرِ وَجَعَلْتَہَا خَیْرًامِّنْ اَلْفِ شَہْرٍ فَیَاذَالْمَنِّ وَلَا یُمَنُّ عَلَیْکَ مُنَّ عَلَیَّ بِفَکَاکِ رَقَبَتِیْ مِنَ النَّارِ فِیْمَنْ تَمُنُّ عَلَیْہِ وَاَدْخِلْنِیْ الْجَنَّۃَ بِرَحْمَتِکَ یَااَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِیْن۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

YA ALI O
YA AZEEM

Is dua ko Mahe Ramazan mein har waajib namaz ke baad padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay bulandtar, ay buzurghi waale, ay bakhshne waale, ay maherbaan-o-rahem karne waale, Tu hi woh badaayi waala Parwardigaar hai keh jiske jaisi koi cheez nahi, aur woh soonne, dekhne waala hai. Aur yeh woh mahina hai jise Tu ne buzurghi di, izzat ataa ki, bulandi bakhshi, aur sabhi mahino par fazilat inaayat ki hai.

Aur yeh woh mahina hai jiske roze Tu ne mujh par farz kiye hai. Aur woh Mahe Mubarak Ramazan ka hai keh jis mein Tu ne Qur’an utaara hai jo logo ke liye hidaayat hai aur us mein hidaayat ki daleele aur haq-o-baatil ki tafreeq (tameez) waazeh (saaf) taur par bayaan hai.

Tu ne is mahine mein Shabe Qadr rakkhi, aur usey hazaar mahino se behtar qaraar diya hai. Pas ay ehsaan karne waale Tujh par ehsaan nahi kiya ja sakta, Tu mujh par ehsaan farma meri gardan aag se chhuda kar unke saath rakh jin par Tu ne ehsaan kiya hai aur mujhe apni rehmat se daakhile Jannat farma. Ay sab se ziyaada rahem karne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

YA ALI O
YA AZEEM

Recite this dua after every obligatory namaz in the month of Ramazan…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O the High, O the Great, O the Forgiver, O the Merciful, Thou art the Lord of the lords, there is nothing comparable to Thee, and Thou hears and sees everything. This is the month Thou has made great, exalted, distinguished, rendered superior over all other months, and this is the month in which Thou has made fasting obligatory for me, and this is the month of Ramazan in which Thou sent down the Holy Quran, a guidance for the mankind, which has clear signs to show the right path and set apart truth from falsehood; and Thou celebrated it with the “Night of Qadr” a night better than a thousand months. So, O the Owner of favors and benefits whom none can oblige, have mercy on me by cutting off my drift towards the hell-fire and direct me unto Paradise, O the Most Merciful.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
