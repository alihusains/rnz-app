100 | AADIYAAT | 11

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Farraate (gehri sans / deep breath) bharte huwe tez raftaar ghodo ki qasam.

2. Jo taapmaar kar chingaariya udaane waale hai.

3. Phir subhe-dam hamla karne waale hai.

4. Phir ghubbaar-e-jangh udaane waale nahi.

5. Aur dushman ki jameeyyat mein daraane waale hai.

6. Be-shak insaan apne Parwardigaar ke liye bada na-shukra hai.

7. Aur woh khud bhi is baat ka gawah hai.

8. Aur woh maal ki mohabbat mein bohot sakht hai.

9. Kya usey nahi maaloom hai keh jab murdo ko qabro se nikaala jaayega.

10. Aur dil ke raazo ko zaahir kar diya jaayega.

11. To unka Parwardigaar us din ke haalaat se khoob ba-khabar hoga.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. I swear by the runners breathing pantingly,

2. Then those that produce fire striking,

3. Then those that make raids at morn,

4. Then thereby raise dust,

5. Then rush thereby upon an assembly:

6. Most surely man is ungrateful to his Lord.

7. And most surely he is a witness of that.

8. And most surely he is tenacious in the love of wealth.

9. Does he not then know when what is in the graves is raised,

10. And what is in the breasts is made apparent?

11. Most surely their Lord that day shall be fully aware of them.
