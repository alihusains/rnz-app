The Whispered Prayer of the Rememberers (Zakereen)…
Zikre Khuda karne waalo ki Munajaat…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اِلٰهِىْ لَوْلَا الْوَاجِبُ مِنْ قَبُوْلِ اَمْرِكَ لَنَزَّهْتُكَ مِنْ ذِكْرِىْ اِيَّاكَ عَلٰى اَنَّ ذِكْرِىْ لَكَ بِقَدْرِىْ لَا بِقَدْرِكَ وَمَا عَسٰى اَنْ يَّبْلُغَ مِقْدَارِىْ حَتّٰى اَجْعَلَ مَحَّلًا لِّتَقْدِيْسِيْكَ وَمِنْ اَعْظَمِ النِّعَمِ عَلَيْنَا جَرَيَانُ ذِكْرِكَ عَلٰۤى اَلْسِنَتِنَا وَاِذْنُكَ لَنَا بِدُعَآئِكَ وَتَنْزِيْهِكَ وَتَسْبِيْحِكَ۔

اِلٰهِىْ فَاَلْهِمْنَا ذِكْرَكَ فِى الْخَلَآءِ وَالْمَلَآءِ وَاللَّيْلِ وَالنَّهَارِ وَالْاِعْلَانِ وَالْاِسْرَارِ وَفِى السَّرَّآءِ وَالضَّرَّآءِ وَاٰنِسْنَا بِالذِّكْرِا لْخَفِىِّ وَاسْتَعْمِلْنَا بِالْعَلِىِّ الزَّكِىِّ وَالسَّعْىِ الْمَرْضِىِّ وَجَازِنَا بِالْمِيْزَانِ الْوَفِىِّ اِلٰهِىْ بِكَ هَامَتِ الْقُلُوْبُ الْوَالِهَةُ وَعَلٰى مَعْرِفَتِكَ جُمِعَتِ الْعُقُوْلُ الْمُتَبَايِنَةُ فَلَا تَطْمَئِنُّ الْقُلُوْبُ اِلَّا بِذِكْرَاكَ وَلَا تَسْكُنُ النُّفُوْسُ اِلَّا عِنْدَ رُؤْيَاكَ۔

اَنْتَ الْمُسَبَّحُ كُلِّ مَكَانٍ وَّالْمَعْبُوْدُ فِىْ كُلِّ زَمَانٍ وَّالْمَوْجُوْدُ فِىْ كُلِّ اَوَانٍ وَّالْمَدْعُوُّ بِكُلِّ لِسَانٍ وَّالْمُعَظَّمُ فِىْ كُلِّ جَنَانٍ وَّاَسْتَغْفِرُكَ مِنْ كُلِّ لَذَّةٍۢ بِغَيْرِ ذِكْرِكَ وَمِنْ كُلِّ رَاحَةٍۢ بِغَيْرِ اُنْسِكَ وَمِنْ كُلِّ سُرُوْرٍۢ بِغَيْرِ قُرْبِكَ وَمِنْ كُلِّ شُغْلٍۢ بِغَيْرِ طَاعَتِكَ۔

اِلٰهِىْ اَنْتَ قُلْتَ وَقُوْلَكَ الْحَقُّ يَآاَيُّهَا الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا اَذْكُرُوا اللهَ ذِكْرًا كَثِيْرًا وَّسَبِّحُوْهُ بُكْرَةً وَّاَصِيْلًا وَّقُلْتَ وَقَوْلُكَ الْحَقُّ فَاذْكُرُوْنِىْ اَذْكُرْكُمْ فَاَمَرْتَنَا بِذِكْرِكَ وَوَعَدْتَنَا عَلَيْهِ اَنْ تَذْكُرَنَا تَشْرِيْفًا لَنَا وَتَفْخِيْمًا وَّاِعْظَامًا۔ وَهَا نَحْنُ ذَاكِرُوْكَ كَمَا اَمَرْتَنَا فَاَنْجِزْ لَنَا مَا وَعَدْتَنَا يَاذَاكِرَ الذَّاكِرِيْنَ وَيَآ اَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِيْنَ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

(Rememberers)

Zikre Khuda karne waalo ki Munajaat…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Mere Mabood! Agar Tera hukm maanna waajib na hota to mai Tera zikr apni zabaan par na laata, kyu-keh mai Tera jo zikr karta hu woh mere andaaze se hai na-keh Tere shaan ke mutaabiq. Aur meri kya be-saat (auqaat) keh Mai Teri taqdees wo paakeezghi ka mahel qaraar pa saku. Yeh cheez ham par Teri azeem ne’mato mein se hai keh Tera zikr Hamaari zabaano par jaari hai. Aur hamein Tujh se dua maangne aur Teri paakeezghi wo nizaafat bayaan karne ki ijaazat hai.

Mere Mabood! Hamein Apne zikr ki taufeeq de, keh ham neha aur ayaa (chhup kar aur zaaheri taur par) raat-din zaahir wo baatin aur khushi wo ghami mein Tera zikr kiya kare.

Hamein Apne posheeda zikr se maanoos farma, hamein paakeeza amal aur Apne pasandeeda koshish mein laga, aur (poore insaaf se) mizaan-e amal se hamein jaza de, mera mohabbat bhara dil Tujh se lagaau rakkhe huwe hai, Teri maarefat mein mukhtaalif qism ki aqle ittefaaq rakhti hai. Pas dil ko chain wo sukoon nahi milta magar Tere zikr se aur Teri zaat par yaqeen se nafso ko sukoon milta hai. Tu hi hai jiski tasbeeh har jagah hoti hai, aur jo har zamaane mein Mabood hai. Aur har waqt har jagah maujood hai. aur har zabaan se pukaara jaata hai. Aur har dil mein Teri azmat qaayem hai. Mai maafi chaahta hu Tujh se Tere zikr ke siwa har lazzat se, Teri mohabbat ke siwa har raahat se, Teri qurbat ke siwa har khushi par. Aur maafi chaahta hu Tujh se Teri ita’at ke siwa har kaam se.

Mere Mabood! Tu ne hi kaha hai aur Tera qaul haq hai keh “Ay imaan laane waalo zikr karo Allah ka bohot zeyaada zikr aur subha wo shaam uski paakeezghi bayaan karo”. Neez (also) Tu ne hi kaha aur Tera qaul sachcha hai “Pas Tum Mujhe yaad karo (to) Mai tumhe yaad karunga”. Tu ne hamein Apni yaad ka hukm diya aur ham se waada kiya is par keh Tu bhi hamein yaad karega. Yeh hamare liye sharaf wo ehteraam aur badaayi (fakhar ki baat) hai, to ab ham Tujhe yaad kar rahe hai, jaise Tu ne hukm diya hai. Pas Tu bhi ham se kiya huwa waada pura kar. Ay yaad karne waalo ko yaad karne waale, aur ay sab se zeyaada rahem karne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

The Whispered Prayer of the Rememberers (Zakereen)…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

My God, were it not incumbent to accept Thy command, I would declare Thee far too exalted for me to remember Thee, for I remember Thee in my measure, not in Thy measure, and my scope can hardly reach the point where I may be a locus for calling Thee holy! Among Thy greatest favours to us is the running of Thy remembrance across our tongues and Thy permission to us to supplicate Thee, declare Thee exalted, and call Thee holy!

My God, inspire us with Thy remembrance alone and in assemblies, by night and day, publicly and secretly, in prosperity and adversity! Make us intimate with silent remembrance, employ us in purified works and effort pleasing to Thee, and reward us with the full balance!

My God, love-mad hearts are enraptured by Thee, disparate intellects are brought together by knowing Thee, hearts find no serenity except in remembering Thee, souls find no rest except in seeing Thee. Thou art the glorified in every place, the worshipped at every time, the found at every moment, the called by every tongue, the magnified in every heart! I pray forgiveness from Thee for every pleasure but remembering Thee, every ease but intimacy with Thee, every happiness but nearness to Thee, every occupation but obeying Thee!

My God, Thou hast said - and Thy word is true - O you who have faith, remember God with much remembrance and glorify Him at dawn and in the evening! Thou hast said - and Thy word is true - Remember Me, and I will remember you! Thou hast commanded us to remember Thee, and promised us that Thou wilt remember us thereby, in order to ennoble, respect, and honour us. Here we are, remembering Thee as Thou hast commanded us! So accomplish what Thou hast promised, O Rememberer of the rememberers! O Most Merciful of the merciful!

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
