ARAFAH

Imam Hussain (a.s)

The dua of Imam Husain (a.s), on the day of Arafat is one of the famous dua. The sons of Ghalib Asadi, narrated that they, once, accompanied Imam Husain (a.s) at the Arafat Night when he left his tent with submission and reverence. He walked slowly until he, accompanied by a group of his household, stopped at the left side of Mount Arafat and turned his face towards the Holy Ka`bah. He then raised his hands to the level of his face, just like a poor man begging food, and said…
Roze Arafah ba-waqte Asr maidaane Arafaat mein Imam Husain (a.s) apne farzando aur Shiyo ke saath nehaayat aajezana taur par apne khaime se baahar aaye aur pahaad (jabalur-rehmat) ki baayi taraf (left-side) khade ho kar Kaabe ki taraf rukh kar ke apne dono haath chehre ke saamne la kar phaila diye aur phir Khuda ke huzoor aajezi-o-inkesari ki haalat mein yeh dua padhi...

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَلْحَمْدُ لِلہِ الَّذِیْ لَیْسَ لِقَضَآئِہٖ دَافِعٌ وَّلَا لِعَطَآئِہٖ مَانِعٌ وَّلَا كَصُنْعِہٖ صُنْعُ صَانِعٍ وَّہُوَ الْجَوَادُ الْوَاسِعُ فَطَرَ اَجْنَاسَ الْبَدَآئِعِ وَاَتْقَنَ بِحِكْمَتِہِ الصَّنَآئِعَ لَا تَخْفٰی عَلَیْہِ الطَّلَآئِعُ وَلَا تَضِیْعُ عِنْدَہُ الْوَدَآئِعُ جَازِیْ كُلِّ صَانِعٍ وَّرَآئِشُ كُلِّ قَانِعٍ وَّرَاحِمُ كُلِّ ضَارِعٍ مُّنْزِلُ الْمَنَافِعِ وَالْكِتَابِ الْجَامِعِ بِالنُّوْرِ السَّاطِعِ وَہُوَ لِلدَّعْوَاتِ سَامِعٌ وَّلِلْكُرُبَاتِ دَافِعٌ وَّلِلدَّرَجَاتِ رَافِعٌ وَّ لِلْجَبَابِرَۃِ قَامِعٌ فَلَااِلٰہَ غَیْرُہٗ وَلَا شَیْءَ یَعْدِلُہٗ وَلَیْسَ كَمِثْلِہٖ شَیْءٌ وَّہُوَ السَّمِیْعُ الْبَصِیْرُ اللَّطِیْفُ الْخَبِیْرُ وَہُوَ عَلٰی كُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ قَدِیْرٌ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ اِنِّیْ اَرْغَبُ اِلَیْكَ وَاَشْہَدُ بِالرُّبُوْبِیَّۃِ لَكَ مُقِرًّۢا بِاَنَّكَ رَبِّیْ وَاِلَیْكَ مَرَدِّیْ اِبْتَدَاتَنِیْ بِنِعْمَتِكَ قَبْلَ اَنَ اَكُوْنَ شَیْئًا مَّذْكُوْرًا خَلَقْتَنِیْ مِنَ التُّرَابِ ثُمَّ اَسْكَنْتَنِی الْاَصْلَابَ اٰمِنًا لِّرَیْبَ الْمَنُوْنَ وَاخْتِلَافِ الدُّہُوْرِ وَالسِّنِیْنَ فَلَمْ اَزَلْ ظَاعِنًا مِّنْ صُلْبِ اِلٰی رَحْمٍ فِیْ تَقَادُمٍ مِّنَ الْاَیَّامِ الْمَاضِیَۃِ وَالْقُرُوْنِ الْخَالِیَۃِ لَمْ تُخْرِجْنِیْ لِرَاْفَتِكَ بِیْ وَ لُطْفِكَ لِیْ وَاِحْسَانِكَ اِلَیَّ فِیْ دَوْلَۃِ اَئِمَّۃِ الْكُفْرِ الَّذِیْنَ نَقَضُوْا عَہْدَكَ وَكَذَّبُوْا رُسُلَكَ لٰكِنَّكَ اَخْرَجْتَنِیْ لِلَّذِیْ سَبَقَ لِیْ مِنَ الْہُدَی الَّذِیْ لَہٗ یَسَّرْتَنِیْ وَفِیْہِ اَنْشَاتَنِیْ وَمِنْ قَبْلِ ذٰلِكَ رَافَۃًۢ بِیْ بِجَمِیْلِ صُنْعِكَ وَسَوَابِغِ نِعَمِكَ فَابْتَدَعْتَ خَلْقِیْ مِنْ مَّنِیٍّ یُّمْنٰی وَاَسْكَنْتَنِیْ فِیْ ظُلُمَاتٍ ثَلَاثٍۢ بَیْنَ لَحْمٍ وَّدَمٍ وَّجِلْدٍ لَّمْ تُشْہِدْنِیْ خَلْقِیْ وَلَمْ تَجْعَلْ اِلَیَّ شَیْئًا مِّنْ اَمْرِیْ ثُمَّ اَخْرَجْتَنِیْ لِلَّذِیْ سَبَقَ لِیْ مِنَ الْہُدٰی اِلَی الدُّنْیَا تَآمًّا سَوِیًّا وَّحَفِظْتَنِیْ فِی الْمَہْدِ طِفْلًا صَبِیًّا وَّرَزَقْتَنِیْ مِنَ الْغِذَآئِ لَبَنًا مَّرِیًّا۔

وَعَطَفْتَ عَلَیَّ قُلُوْبَ الْحَوَاضِنِ وَكَفَّلْتَنِیْ مِنْ طَوَارِقِ الْجَآنِّ وَسَلَّمْتَنِیْ مِنَ الزِّیَادَۃِ وَالنُّقْصَانِ فَتَعَالَیْتَ یَارَحِیْمُ یَارَحْمٰنُ حَتّٰی اِذَا اسْتَہْلَلْتُ نَاطِقًا بِالْكَلَامِ وَاَتْمَمْتَ عَلَیَّ سَوَابِغَ الْاِنْعَامِ وَرَبَّیْتَنِیْ آیِدًا فِیْ كُلِّ عَامٍ حَتّٰی اِذَا اكْتَمَلَتْ فِطْرَتِیْ وَاعْتَدَلَتْ مِرَّتِیْ اَوْجَبْتَ عَلَیَّ حُجَّتَكَ بِاَنْ اَلْہَمْتَنِیْ مَعْرِفَتَكَ وَرَوَّعْتَنِیْ بِعَجَآئِبِ حِكْمَتِكَ وَاَیْقَظْتَنِیْ لِمَا ذَرَاَتَ فِیْ سَمَآئِكَ وَاَرْضِكَ مِنْ بَدَآئِعِ خَلْقِكَ وَنَبَّہْتَنِیْ لِشُكْرِكَ وَذِكْرِكَ وَاَوْجَبْتَ عَلَیَّ طَاعَتَكَ وَعِبَادَتَكَ وَفَہَّمْتَنِیْ مَا جَآئَتْ بِہٖ رُسُلُكَ وَیَسَّرْتَ لِیْ تَقَبُّلَ مَرْضَاتِكَ وَمَنَنْتَ عَلَیَّ فِیْ جَمِیْعِ ذٰلِكَ بِعَوْنِكَ وَلُطْفِكَ ثُمَّ اِذْ خَلَقْتَنِیْ مِنْ خَیْرِ الثَّرٰی لَمْ تَرْضَ لِیْ۔

یَااِلٰہِیْ نِعْمَۃً دُوْنَ اُخْرٰی وَرَزَقْتَنِیْ مِنْ اَنْوَاعِ الْمَعَاشِ وَصُنُوْفِ الرِّیَاشِ بِمَنِّكَ الْعَظِیْمِ الْاَعْظَمِ عَلَیَّ وَاِحْسَانِكَ الْقَدِیْمِ اِلَیَّ حَتّٰی اِذَا اَتْمَمْتَ عَلَیَّ جَمِیْعَ النِّعَمِ وَصَرَفْتَ عَنِّیْ كُلَّ النِّقَمِ لَمْ یَمْنَعْكَ جَہْلِیْ وَجُرْاَتِیْ عَلَیْكَ اَنْ دَلَلْتَنِیْ اِلٰی مَایُقَرِّبُنِیْ اِلَیْكَ وَوَفَّقْتَنِیْ لِمَا یُزْلِفُنِیْ لَدَیْكَ فَاِنْ دَعَوْتُكَ اَجَبْتَنِیْ وَاِنْ سَئَلْتُكَ اَعْطَیْتَنِیْ وَاِنْ اَطَعْتُكَ شَكَرْتَنِیْ وَاِنْ شَكَرْتُكَ زِدْتَنِیْ كُلُّ ذٰلِكَ اِكْمَالٌ لِّاَنْعُمِكَ عَلَیَّ وَاِحْسَانُكَ اِلَیَّ فَسُبْحَانَكَ سُبْحَانَكَ مِنْ مُّبْدِئٍ مُّعِیْدٍ حَمِیْدٍ مَّجِیْدٍ تَقَدَّسَتْ اَسْمَآؤُكَ وَعَظُمَتْ اٰلَآؤُكَ فَاَیُّ نِعَمِكَ یَااِلٰہِیْ اُحْصِیْ عَدَدًا وَّذِكْرًا اَمْ اَیُّ عَطَایَاكَ اَقُوْمُ بِہَا شُكْرًا وَّہِیَ یَارَبِّ اَكْثَرُ مِنْ اَنْ یُّحْصِیَہَا الْعَآدُّوْنَ اَوْ یَبْلُغَ عِلْمًا بِہَا الْحَافِظُوْنَ ثُمَّ مَاصَرَفْتَ وَدَرَاتَ عَنِّیْ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ مِنَ الضُّرِّ وَالضَّرَّآئِ اَكْثَرُ مِمَّا ظَہَرَ لِیْ مِنَ الْعَافِیَۃِ وَالسَّرَّآئِ وَاَنَا اَشْہَدُ یَااِلٰہِیْ بِحَقِیْقَۃِ اِیْمَانِیْ وَعَقْدِ عَزَمَاتِ یَقِیْنِیْ وَخَالِصِ صَرِیْحِ تَوْحِیْدِیْ وَبَاطِنِ مَكْنُوْنِ ضَمِیْرِیْ وَعَلَآئِقِ مَجَارِیْ نُوْرِ بَصَرِیْ وَاَسَارِیْرِ صَفْحَۃِ جَبِیْنِیْ وَخُرْقِ مَسَارِبِ نَفْسِیْ وَخَذَارِیْفِ مَارِنِ عِرْنِیْنِیْ وَمَسَارِبِ سِمَاخِ سَمْعِیْ وَمَا ضُمَّتْ وَاَطَبَقَتْ عَلَیْہِ شَفَتَایَ وَحَرَكَاتِ لَفْظِ لِسَانِیْ وَمَغْرَزِ حَنَكِ فَمِیْ وَفَكِّیْ وَمَنَابِتِ اَضْرَاسِیْ وَمَسَاغِ مَطْعَمِیْ وَمَشْرَبِیْ وَحِمَالَۃِ اُمِّ رَاسِیْ وَبَلُوْعِ فَارِغِ حَبَآئِلِ عُنُقِیْ وَمَااشْتَمَلَ عَلَیْہِ تَامُوْرُ صَدْرِیْ وَحَمَآئِلِ حَبْلِ وَتِیْنِیْ وَنِیَاطِ حِجَابِ قَلْبِیْ وَاَفْلَاذِ حَوَاشِیْ كَبِدِیْ وَمَا حَوَتْہُ شَرَاسِیْفُ اَضْلَاعِیْ وَحِقَاقُ مَفَاصِلِیْ وَقَبْضُ عَوَامِلِیْ وَاَطْرَافُ اَنَامِلِیْ وَلَحْمِیْ وَدَمِیْ وَشَعْرِیْ وَبَشَرِیْ وَعَصَبِیْ وَقَصَبِیْ وَعِظَامِیْ وَمُخِّیْ وَعُرُوْقِیْ وَجَمِیْعُ جَوَارِحِیْ وَمَاانْتَسَجَ عَلٰی ذٰلِكَ اَیَّامَ رِضَاعِیْ وَمَا اَقَلَّتِ الْاَرْضُ مِنِّیْ وَنَوْمِیْ وَیَقْظَتِیْ وَسُكُوْنِیْ وَحَرَكَاتِ رُكُوْعِیْ وَسُجُوْدِیْ اَنْ لَوْ حَاوَلْتُ وَاجْتَہَدْتُ مَدَی الْاَعْصَارِ وَالْاَحْقَابِ لَوْ عُمِّرْتُہَا اَنْ اُؤَدِّیْ شُكْرَ وَاحِدَۃٍ مِّنْ اَنْعُمِكَ مَا اسْتَطَعْتُ ذٰلِكَ اِلَّا بِمَنِّكَ الْمُوْجِبِ عَلَیَّ بِہٖ شُكْرُكَ اَبَدًا جَدِیْدًا وَّثَنَآئً طَارِفًا عَتِیْدًا اَجَلْ وَلَوْ حَرَصْتُ اَنَا وَالْعَآدُّوْنَ مِنْ اَنَامِكَ اَنْ نُحْصِیَ مَدٰی اِنْعَامِكَ سَالِفِہٖ وَاٰنِفِہٖ مَا حَصَرْنَاہُ عَدَدًا وَّلَا اَحْصَیْنَاہُ اَمَدًا ہَیْہَاتَ اَنّٰی ذٰلِكَ وَاَنْتَ الْمُخْبِرُ فِیْ كِتَابِكَ النَّاطِقِ وَالنَّبَاِ الصَّادِقِ ’’وَاِنْ تَعُدُّوْا نِعْمَۃَ اللهِ لَا تُحْصُوْہَا‘‘ صَدَقَ كِتَابُكَ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ وَاِنْبَآؤُكَ وَبَلَّغَتْ اَنْبِیَآؤُكَ وَرُسُلُكَ مَا اَنْزَلْتَ بِہِمْ مِنْ وَحْیِكَ وَشَرَعْتَ لَہُمْ وَعَلَیْہِمْ مِنْ دِیْنِكَ غَیْرَ اَنِّیْ یَااِلٰہِیْ اَشْہَدُ بِجُہْدِیْ وَجِدِّیْ وَمَبْلَغِ طَاعَتِیْ وَوُسْعِیْ وَاُقُوْلُ مُؤْمِنًا مُّوْقِیًا۔ اَلْحَمْدُ لِلہِ الَّذِیْ لَمْ یَتَّخِذْ وَلَدًا فَیَكُوْنَ مَوْرُوْثًا وَّلَمْ یَكُنْ لَہٗ شَرِیْكٌ فِیْ مُلْكِہٖ فَیُضَآدَّہٗ فِیْمَا ابْتَدَعَ وَلَا وَلِیٌّ مِّنَ الذُّلِّ فَیُرْفِدَہٗ فِیْمَا صَنَعَ فَسُبْحَانَہٗ سُبْحَانَہٗ لَوْ كَانَ فِیْہِمَا اٰلِہَۃٌ اِلَّا اللهُ لَفَسَدَتَا وَتَفَطَّرْتَا۔

سُبْحَانَ اللهِ الْوَاحِدِ الْاَحَدِ الصَّمَدِ الَّذِیْ لَمْ یَلِدْ وَلَمْ یُوْلَدْ وَلَمْ یَكُنْ لَّہٗ كُفُوًا اَحَدٌ۔ اَلْحَمْدُ لِلہِ حَمْدًا یُّعَادِلُ حَمْدَ مَلَآئِكَتِہِ الْمُقَرَّبِیْنَ وَاَنْبِیَآئِہِ الْمُرْسَلِیْنَ وَصَلَّی اللهُ عَلٰی خِیَرَتِہٖ مُحَمَّدٍ خَاتَمِ النَّبِیِّیْنَ وَاٰلِہِ الطَّیِّبِیْنَ الطَّاہِرِیْنَ الْمُخْلِصِیْنَ وَ سَلَّمَ۔

After this, Imam Husain (a.s) starts supplicating. He gives importance to supplication and tears flow from the eyes and he says…
Phir aap ne Khudaaye-taala se haajaat talab karna shuru kiya. Jab-keh aap ke aankho se aansoo (tears) jaari thay, usi haalat mein aap baargah-e-Khudawandi mein you’n arz-guzaar huwe...

اَللّٰہُمَّ اجْعَلْنِیْ اَخْشَاكَ كَاَنِّیْ اَرَاكَ وَاَسْعِدْنِیْ بِتَقْوٰیكَ وَلَا تُشْقِنِیْ بِمَعْصِیَتِكَ وَخِرْ لِیْ فِیْ قَضَآئِكَ وَبَارِكْ لِیْ فِیْ قَدَرِكَ حَتّٰی لَا اُحِبَّ تَعْجِیْلَ مَا اَخَّرْتَ وَلَا تَاخِیْرَ مَا عَجَّلْتَ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ اجْعَلْ غِنَایَ فِیْ نَفْسِیْ وَالْیَقِیْنَ فِیْ قَلْبِیْ وَالْاِخْلَاصَ فِیْ عَمَلِیْ وَالنُّوْرَ فِیْ بَصَرِیْ وَالْبَصِیْرَۃَ فِیْ دِیْنِیْ وَمَتَّعْنِیْ بِجَوَارِحِیْ وَاجْعَلْ سَمْعِیْ وَبَصَرِی الْوَارِثَیْنِ مِنِّیْ وَانْصُرْنِیْ عَلٰی مَنْ ظَلَمَنِیْ وَاَرِنِیْ فِیْہِ ثَارِیْ وَمَآرِبِیْ وَ اَقِرَّ بِذٰلِكَ عَیْنِیْ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اكْشِفْ كُرْبَتِیْ وَاسْتُرْ عَوْرَتِیْ وَاغْفِرْ لِیْ خَطِٓیْئَتِیْ وَاخْسَا شَیْطَانِیْ وَفُكَّ رِہَانِیْ وَاجْعَلْ لِیْ یَااِلٰہِی الدَّرَجَۃَ الْعُلْیَا فِی الْاٰخِرَۃِ وَالْاُوْلٰی۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ لَكَ الْحَمْدُ كَمَا خَلَقْتَنِیْ فَجَعَلْتَنِیْ سَمِیْعًۢا بَصِیْرًا وَّلَكَ الْحَمْدُ كَمَا خَلَقْتَنِیْ فَجَعَلْتَنِیْ خَلْقًا سَوِیًّا رَّحْمَۃً بِیْ وَقَدْ كُنْتَ عَنْ خَلْقِیْ غَنِیًّا رَّبِّ بِمَا بَرَاتَنِیْ فَعَدَّلْتَ فِطْرَتِیْ رَبِّ بِمَا اَنْشَاْتَنِیْ فَاَحْسَنْتَ صُوْرَتِیْ رَبِّ بِمَا اَحْسَنْتَ اِلَیَّ وَفِیْ نَفْسِیْ عَافَیْتَنِیْ رَبِّ بِمَا كَلَاتَنِیْ وَوَفَّقْتَنِیْ رَبِّ بِمَا اَنْعَمْتَ عَلَیَّ فَہَدَیْتَنِیْ رَبِّ بِمَا اَوْلَیْتَنِیْ وَمِنْ كُلِّ خَیْرٍ اَعْطَیْتَنِیْ رَبِّ بِمَا اَطْعَمْتَنِیْ وَسَقَیْتَنِیْ رَبِّ بِمَا اَغْنَیْتَنِیْ وَاَقْنَیْتَنِیْ رَبِّ بِمَا اَعَنْتَنِیْ وَاَعْزَزْتَنِیْ رَبِّ بِمَا اَلْبَسْتَنِیْ مِنْ سِتْرِكَ الصَّافِیْ وَیَسَّرْتَ لِیْ مِنْ صُنْعِكَ الْكَافِیْ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاَعِنِّیْ عَلٰی بَوَآئِقِ الدُّہُوْرِ وَصُرُوْفِ اللَّیَالِیْ وَالْاَیَّامِ وَ نَجِّنِیْ مِنْ اَہْوَالِ الدُّنْیَا وَكُرُبَاتِ الْاٰخِرَۃِ وَاكْفِنِیْ شَرَّ مَا یَعْمَلُ الظَّالِمُوْنَ فِی الْاَرْضِ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ مَا اَخَافُ فَاكْفِنِیْ وَمَا اَحْذَرُ فَقِنِیْ وَفِیْ نَفْسِیْ وَدِیْنِیْ فَاحْرُسْنِیْ وَفِیْ سَفَرِیْ فَاحْفَظْنِیْ وَفِیْ اَھْلِیْ وَمَالِیْ فَاخْلُفْنِیْ وَفِیْمَا رَزَقْتَنِیْ فَبَارِكْ لِیْ وَفِیْ نَفْسِیْ فَذَلِّلْنِیْ وَفِیْ اَعْیُنِ النَّاسِ فَعَظِّمْنِیْ وَمِنْ شَرِّ الْجِنِّ وَالْاِنْسِ فَسَلِّمْنِیْ وَبِذُنُوْبِیْ فَلَا تَفْضَحْنِیْ وَبِسَرِیْرَتِیْ فَلَا تُخْزِنِیْ وَبِعَمَلِیْ فَلَا تَبْتَلِنِیْ وَنِعَمَكَ فَلَا تَسْلُبْنِیْ وَاِلٰی غَیْرِكَ فَلَا تَكِلْنِیْ۔

اِلٰہِیْ اِلٰی مَنْ تَكِلُنِیْ اِلٰی قَرِیْبٍ فَیَقْطَعُنِیْ اَمْ اِلٰی بَعِیْدٍ فَیَتَجَہَّمُنِیْ اَمْ اِلَی الْمُسْتَضْعَفِیْنَ لِیْ وَاَنْتَ رَبِّیْ وَمَلِیْكُ اَمْرِیْ اَشْكُوْ اِلَیْكَ غُرْبَتِیْ وَبُعْدَ دَارِیْ وَہَوَانِیْ عَلٰی مَنْ مَلَّكْتَہٗ اَمْرِیْ اِلٰہِیْ فَلَا تُحْلِلْ عَلَیَّ غَضَبَكَ فَاِنْ لَّمْ تَكُنْ غَضِبْتَ عَلَیَّ فَلَا اُبَالِیْ سُبْحَانَكَ غَیْرَ اَنَّ عَافِیَتَكَ اَوْسَعُ لِیْ فَاَسْئَلُكَ یَارَبِّ بِنُوْرِ وَجْہِكَ الَّذِیْ اَشْرَقَتْ لَہُ الْاَرْضُ وَالسَّمٰوَاتُ وَكُشِفَتْ بِہِ الظُّلُمَاتُ وَصَلُحَ بِہٖ اَمْرُ الْاَوَّلِیْنَ وَالْاٰخِرِیْنَ اَنْ لَّا تُمِیْتَنِیْ عَلٰی غَضَبِكَ وَلَا تُنْزِلَ بِیْ سَخَطَكَ لَكَ الْعُتْبٰی لَكَ الْعُتْبٰی حَتّٰی تَرْضٰیْ قَبْلَ ذٰلِكَ۔

لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ رَبَّ الْبَلَدِ الْحَرَامِ وَالْمَشْعَرِ الْحَرَامِ وَالْبَیْتِ الْعَتِیْقِ الَّذِیْ اَحْلَلْتَہُ الْبَرَكَۃَ وَجَعَلْتَہٗ لِلنَّاسِ اَمْنًا یَّامَنْ عَفَا عَنْ عَظِیْمِ الذُّنُوْبِ بِحِلْمِہٖ یَامَنْ اَسْبَغَ النَّعْمَآئِ بِفَضْلِہٖ یَامَنْ اَعْطَی الْجَزِیْلَ بِكَرَمِہٖ یَاعُدَّتِیْ فِیْ شِدَّتِیْ وَیَاصَاحِبِیْ فِیْ وَحْدَتِیْ یَاغِیَاثِیْ فِیْ كُرْبَتِیْ یَاوَلِیِّ فِیْ نِعْمَتِیْ یَااِلٰہِیْ وَاِلٰہَ اٰبَآئِیْ اِبْرَاہِیْمَ وَاِسْمٰعِیْلَ وَاِسْحٰقَ وَیَعْقُوْبَ وَرَبَّ جَبْرَئِیْلَ وَمِیْكَآئِیْلَ وَاِسْرَافِیْلَ وَرَبَّ مُحَمَّدٍ خَاتَمِ النَّبِیِّیْنَ وَاٰلِہِ الْمُنْتَجَبِیْنَ وَمُنْزِلَ التَّوْرٰیۃِ وَالْاِنْجِیْلِ وَالزَّبُوْرِ وَالْفُرْقَانِ وَمُنَزِّلَ كہٰٓیٰٓعٓصٓ وَطٰٓہٰ وَیٰسٓ وَالْقُرْآنِ الْحَكِیْمِ۔

اَنْتَ كَہْفِيْ حِیْنَ تُعْیِیْنِي الْمَذَاہِبُ فِيْ سَعَتِہَا وَتَضِیْقُ بِیَ الْاَرْضُ بِرُحْبِہَا وَلَوْ لَا رَحْمَتُكَ لَكُنْتُ مِنَ الْہَالِكِیْنَ وَاَنْتَ مُقِیْلُ عَثْرَتِیْ وَلَوْ لَا سَتْرُكَ اِیَّایَ لَكُنْتُ مِنَ الْمَفْضُوْحِیْنَ وَاَنْتَ مُؤَیِّدِیْ بِالنَّصْرِ عَلٰی اَعْدَآئِیْ وَلَوْ لَا نَصْرُكَ اِیَّایَ لَكُنْتُ مِنَ الْمَغْلُوْبِیْنَ یَامَنْ خَصَّ نَفْسَہٗ بِالسُّمُوِّ وَالرِّفْعَۃِ فَاَوْلِیَآئُہٗ بِعِزِّہٖ یَعْتَزُّوْنَ۔

یَامَنْ جَعَلَتْ لَہُ الْمُلُوْكُ نِیْرَ الْمَذَلَّۃِ عَلٰی اَعْنَاقِہِمْ فَہُمْ مِنْ سَطَوَاتِہٖ خَآئِفُوْنَ یَعْلَمُ خَآئِنَۃَ الْاَعْیُنِ وَمَا تُخْفِیْ الصُّدُوْرُ وَغَیْبَ مَا تَاْتِیْ بِہِ الْاَزْمِنَۃُ وَالدُّہُوْرُ یَامَنْ لَّا یَعْلَمُ كَیْفَ ہُوَ اِلَّا ہُوَ یَامَنْ لَّا یَعْلَمُ مَا ہُوَ اِلَّا ہُوَ یَامَنْ لَّا یَعْلَمُہٗ اِلَّا ہُوَ یَامَنْ كَبَسَ الْاَرْضَ عَلَی الْمَآئِ وَسَدَّ الْہَوَآئَ بِالسَّمَآئِ۔

یَامَنْ لَہٗ اَكْرَمُ الْاَسْمَآئِ یَاذَا الْمَعْرُوْفِ الَّذِیْ لَا یَنْقَطِعُ اَبَدًا یَّامُقَیِّضَ الرَّكْبِ لِیُوْسُفَ فِی الْبَلَدِ الْقَفْرِ وَمُخْرِجَہٗ مِنَ الْجُبِّ وَجَاعِلَہٗ بَعْدَ الْعُبُوْدِیَّۃِ مَلِكًا یَّارَآدَّہٗ عَلٰی یَعْقُوْبَ بَعْدَ اَنِ ابْیَضَّتْ عَیْنَاہُ مِنَ الْحُزْنِ فَہُوَ كَظِیْمٌ یَاكَاشِفَ الضُّرِّ وَالْبَلْوٰی عَنْ اَیُّوْبَ وَمُمْسِكَ یَدَیْ اِبْرَاہِیْمَ عَنْ ذَبْحِ ابْنِہٖ بَعْدَ كِبَرِ سِنِّہٖ وَ فَنَآئِ عُمُرِہٖ۔

یَامَنِ اسْتَجَابَ لِزَكَرِیَّا فَوَہَبَ لَہٗ یَحْیٰی وَلَمْ یَدَعْہٗ فَرْدًا وَّحِیْدًا یَّامَنْ اَخْرَجَ یُوْنُسَ مِنْ بَطْنِ الْحُوْتِ یَامَنْ فَلَقَ الْبَحْرَ لِبَنِیْ اِسْرَآئِیْلَ فَاَنْجَاہُمْ وَجَعَلَ فِرْعُوْنَ وَجُنُوْدَہٗ مِنَ الْمُغْرَقِیْنَ یَامَنْ اَرْسَلَ الرِّیَاحَ مُبَشِّرَاتٍۢ بَیْنَ یَدَیْ رَحْمَتِہٖ یَامَنْ لَّمْ یَعْجَلْ عَلٰی مَنْ عَصَاہُ مِنْ خَلْقِہٖ یَامَنِ اسْتَنْقَذَ السَّحَرَۃَ مِنْ بَعْدِ طُوْلِ الْجُحُوْدِ وَقَدْ غَدَوْ فِیْ نِعْمَتِہٖ یَاكُلُوْنَ رِزْقَہٗ وَیَعْبُدُوْنَ غَیْرَہٗ وَقَدْ حَآدُّوْہُ وَنَآدُّوْہُ وَكَذَّبُوْا رُسُلَہٗ۔

یَااَللهُ یَااَللهُ یَابَدِیُ یَابَدِیْعُ لَا نِدَّ لَكَ یَادَآئِمُ لَا نَفَادَ لَكَ یَاحَیًّا حِیْنَ لَا حَیَّ یَامُحْیِیَ الْمَوْتٰی یَامَنْ ہُوَ قَآئِمٌ عَلٰی كُلِّ نَفْسٍۢ بِمَا كَسَبَتْ یَامَنْ قَلَّ لَہٗ شُكْرِیْ فَلَمْ یَحْرِمْنِیْ وَعَظُمَتْ خَطِیْئَتِیْ فَلَمْ یَفْضَحْنِیْ وَرَاٰنِیْ عَلَی الْمَعَاصِیْ فَلَمْ یَشْہَرْنِیْ یَامَنْ حَفِظَنِیْ فِیْ صِغَرِیْ یَامَنْ رَّزَقَنِیْ فِیْ كِبَرِیْ یَامَنْ اَیَادِیْہٖ عِنْدِیْ لَا تُحْصٰی وَنِعَمُہٗ لَا تُجَازٰی۔

یَامَنْ عَارَضَنِیْ بِالْخَیْرِ وَالْاِحْسَانِ وَعَارَضْتُہٗ بِالْاِسَآئَۃِ وَالْعِصْیَانِ یَامَنْ ہَدَانِیْ لِلْاِیْمَانِ مِنْ قَبْلِ اَنْ اَعْرِفَ شُكْرَ الْاِمْتِنَانِ یَامَنْ دَعَوْتُہٗ مَرِیْضًا فَشَفَانِیْ وَعُرْیَانًا فَكَسَانِیْ وَجَآئِعًا فَاَشْبَعَنِیْ وَعَطْشَانَ فَاَرْوَانِیْ وَذَلِیْلًا فَاَعَزَّنِیْ وَجَاہِلًا فَعَرَّفَنِیْ وَوَحِیْدًا فَكَثَّرَنِیْ وَغَآئِبًا فَرَدَّنِیْ وَمُقِلًّا فَاَغْنَانِیْ وَمُنْتَصِرًا فَنَصَرَنِیْ وَغَنِیًّا فَلَمْ یَسْلُبْنِیْ وَاَمْسَكْتُ عَنْ جَمِیْعِ ذٰلِكَ فَابْتَدَاَنِیْ فَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ وَالشُّكْرُ۔

یَامَنْ اَقَالَ عَثْرَتِیْ وَ نَفَّسَ كُرْبَتِیْ وَاَجَابَ دَعْوَتِیْ وَسَتَرَ عَوْرَتِیْ وَغَفَرَ ذُنُوْبِیْ وَبَلَّغَنِیْ طَلَبَتِیْ وَنَصَرَنِیْ عَلٰی عَدُوِّیْ وَاِنْ اَعُدُّ نِعَمَكَ وَمِنَنَكَ وَكَرَآئِمَ مِنَحِكَ لَا اُحْصِیْہَا یَامَوْلَایَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ مَنَنْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَنْعَمْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَحْسَنْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَجْمَلْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَفْضَلْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَكْمَلْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ رَزَقْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ وَفَّقْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَعْطَیْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَغْنَیْتَ۔

اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَقْنَیْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ آوَیْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ كَفَیْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ ہَدَیْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ عَصَمْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ سَتَرْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ غَفَرْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَقَلْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ مَكَّنْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَعْزَزْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَعَنْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ عَضَدْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَیَّدْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ نَصَرْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ شَفَیْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ عَافَیْتَ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَكْرَمْتَ تَبَارَكْتَ وَتَعَالَیْتَ فَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ دَآئِمًا وَّلَكَ الشُّكْرُ وَاصِبًا اَبَدًا ثُمَّ اَنَا۔

یَااِلٰہِیَ الْمُعْتَرِفُ بِذُنُوْبِیْ فَاغْفِرْ ہَالِیْ اَنَا الَّذِیْ اَسَاتُ اَنَا الَّذِیْ اَخْطَاتُ اَنَا الَّذِیْ ہَمَمْتُ اَنَا الَّذِیْ جَہِلْتُ اَنَا الَّذِیْ غَفَلْتُ اَنَا الَّذِیْ سَہَوْتُ اَنَا الَّذِیْ اَعْتَمَدْتُ اَنَا الَّذِیْ تَعَمَّدْتُ اَنَا الَّذِیْ وَعَدْتُ وَاَنَا الَّذِیْ اَخْلَفْتُ اَنَا الَّذِیْ نَكَثْتُ اَنَا الَّذِیْ اَقْرَرْتُ اَنَا الَّذِیْ اَعْتَرَفْتُ بِنِعْمَتِكَ عَلَیَّ وَعِنْدِیْ وَاَبُوْئُ بِذُنُوْبِیْ فَاغْفِرْہَا لِیْ یَامَنْ لَّا تَضُرُّہٗ ذُنُوْبُ عِبَادِہٖ وَہُوَ الْغَنِیُّ عَنْ طَاعَتِہِمْ وَالْمُوَفِّقُ مَنْ عَمِلَ صَالِحًا مِّنْہُمْ بِمَعُوْنَتِہٖ وَرَحْمَتِہٖ فَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ اِلٰہِیْ وَسَیِّدِیْ۔

اِلٰہِیْ اَمَرْتَنِیْ فَعَصَیْتُكَ وَنَہَیْتَنِیْ فَارْتَكَبْتُ نَہْیَكَ فَاَصْبَحْتُ لَا ذَا بَرَآئَۃٍ لِیْ فَاَعْتَذِرُ وَلَا ذَاقُوَّۃٍ فَاَنْتَصِرُ فَبِاَیِّ شَیْٓءٍ اَسْتَقْبِلُكَ یَامَوْلَایَ اَبِسَمْعِیْ اَمْ بِبَصَرِیْ اَمْ بِلِسَانِیْ اَمْ بِیَدِیْ اَمْ بِرِجْلِیْ اَلَیْسَ كُلُّہَا نِعَمَكَ عِنْدِیْ وَبِكُلِّہَا عَصَیْتُكَ یَامَوْلَایَ فَلَكَ الْحُجَّۃُ وَالسَّبِیْلُ عَلَیَّ۔

یَامَنْ سَتَرَنِیْ مِنَ الْآبَآئِ وَالْاُمَّہَاتِ اَنْ یَّزْجُرُوْنِیْ وَمِنَ الْعَشَآئِرِ وَالْاِخْوَانِ اَنْ یُّعَیِّرُوْنِیْ وَمِنَ السَّلَاطِیْنِ اَنْ یُّعَاقِبُوْنِیْ وَلَوِاطَّلَعُوْا یَامَوْلَایَ عَلٰی مَا اطَّلَعْتَ عَلَیْہِ مِنِّیْ اِذًا مَّا اَنْظَرُوْنِیْ وَلَرَفَضُوْنِیْ وَقَطَعُوْنِیْ فَہَا اَنَاذَا یَااِلٰہِیْ بَیْنَ یَدَیْكَ۔

یَاسَیِّدِیْ خَاضِعٌ ذَلِیْلٌ حَصِیْرٌ حَقِیْرٌ لَّا ذُوْ بَرَآئَۃٍ فَاعْتَذِرُ وَلَا ذُوْ قُوَّۃٍ فَانْتَصِرَ وَلَا حُجَّۃٍ فَاَحْتَجُّ بِہَا وَلَا قَآئِلٌ لَّمْ اَجْتَرِحْ وَلَمْ اَعْمَلْ سُوْٓئً وَّمَا عَسَی الْجُحُوْدُ وَلَوْ جَحَدْتُ یَامَوْلَایَ یَنْفَعُنِیْ كَیْفَ وَاَنّٰی ذٰلِكَ وَجَوَارِحِیْ كُلَّہَا شَاہِدَۃٌ عَلَیَّ بِمَا قَدْ عَمِلْتُ وَعَلِمْتُ یَقِیْنًا غَیْرَ ذِیْ شَكٍّ اَنَّكَ سَآئِلِیْ مِنْ عَظَآئِمِ الْاُمُوْرِ وَاَنَّكَ الْحَكَمُ الْعَدْلُ الَّذِیْ لَا تَجُوْرُ وَعَدْلُكَ مُہْلِكِیْ وَمِنْ كُلِّ عَدْلِكَ مَہْرَبِیْ فَاِنْ تُعَذِّبْنِیْ۔

یَااِلٰہِیْ فَبِذُنُوْبِیْ بَعْدَ حُجَّتِكَ عَلَیَّ وَاِنْ تَعْفُ عَنِّیْ فَبِحِلْمِكَ وُجُوْدِكَ وَكَرَمِكَ۔ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ اِنِّیْ كُنْتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِیْنَ۔ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ اِنِّیْ كُنْتُ مِنَ الْمُسْتَغْفِرِیْنَ۔ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ اِنِّیْ كُنْتُ مِنَ الْمُوَحِّدِیْنَ۔ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ اِنِّیْ كُنْتُ مِنَ الْخَآئِفِیْنَ۔ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ اِنِّیْ كُنْتُ مِنَ الْوَجِلِیْنَ۔ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ اِنِّیْ كُنْتُ مِنَ الرَّاجِیْنَ۔

لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ اِنِّیْ كُنْتُ مِنَ الرَّاغِبِیْنَ۔ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ اِنِّیْ كُنْتُ مِنَ السَّآئِلِیْنَ۔ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ اِنِّیْ كُنْتُ مِنَ الْمُہَلِّلِیْنَ۔ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ اِنِّیْ كُنْتُ مِنَ الْمُسَبِّحِیْنَ۔ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ اِنِّیْ كُنْتُ مِنَ الْمُكَبِّرِیْنَ۔ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ رَبِّی وَرَبُّ اٰبَآئِیَ الْاَوَّلِیْنَ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ ہٰذَا ثَنَآئِیْ عَلَیْكَ مُمَجِّدًا وَّاِخْلَاصِیْ لِذِكْرِكَ مُوَحِّدًا وَّاِقْرَارِیْ بِاٰلَآئِكَ مُعَدِّدًا وَّاِنْ كُنْتُ مُقِرًّا اَنِّیْ لَمْ اُحْصِہَا لِكَثْرَتِہَا وَسُبُوْعِہَا وَتَظَاہُرِہَا وَتَقَادُمِہَا اِلٰی حَارِثٍ مَّا لَمْ تَزَلْ تَتَعَہَّدُنِیْ بِہٖ مَعَہَا مُنْذُ خَلَقْتَنِیْ وَبَرَاتَنِیْ مِنْ اَوَّلِ الْعُمْرِ مِنَ الْاَغْنَآئِ مِنَ الْفَقْرِ وَكَشْفِ الضُّرِّ وَتَسْبِیْبِ الْیُسْرِ وَدَفْعِ الْعُسْرِ وَتَفْرِیْجِ الْكَرْبِ وَالْعَافِیَۃِ فِی الْبَدَنِ۔

وَالسَّلَامَۃِ فِیْ الدِّیْنِ وَلَوْ رَفَدَنِیْ عَلٰی قَدْرِ ذِكْرِ نِعْمَتِكَ جَمِیْعُ الْعَالَمِیْنَ مِنَ الْاَوَّلِیْنَ وَالْاٰخِرِیْنَ مَا قَدَرْتُ وَلَا ہُمْ عَلٰی ذٰلِكَ تَقَدَّسْتَ وَتَعَالَیْتَ مِنْ رَّبِّ كَرِیْمٍ عَظِیْمٍ رَّحِیْمٍ لَّا تُحْصٰی اٰلَآؤُكَ وَلَا یُبْلَغُ ثَنَآؤُكَ وَلَا تُكَافٰی نَعْمَآؤُكَ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاَتْمِمْ عَلَیْنَا نِعَمَكَ وَاَسْعِدْنَا بِطَاعَتِكَ سُبْحَانَكَ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ اِنَّكَ تُجِیْبُ الْمُضْطَرَّ وَتَكْشِفُ السُّوْٓئَ وَتَغِیْثُ الْمَكْرُوْبَ وَتَشْفِیْ السَّقِیْمَ وَتُغْنِی الْفَقِیْرَ وَتَجْبُرُ الْكَسِیْرَ وَتَرْحَمُ الصَّغِیْرَ وَتُعِیْنُ الْكَبِیْرَ وَلَیْسَ دُوْنَكَ ظَہِیْرٌ وَّلَا فَوْقَكَ قَدِیْرٌ وَّاَنْتَ الْعَلِیُّ الْكَبِیْرُ یَامُطْلِقَ الْمُكَبَّلِ الْاَسِیْرِ یَارَازِقَ الطِّفْلِ الصَّغِیْرِ یَاعِصْمَۃَ الْخَآئِفِ الْمُسْتَجِیْرِ یَامَنْ لَّا شَرِیْكَ لَہٗ وَلَا وَزِیْرَ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

وَاَعْطِنِیْ فِیْ ہٰذِہِ الْعَشِیَّۃِ اَفْضَلَ مَا اَعْطَیْتَ وَاَنَلْتَ اَحَدًا مِّنْ عِبَادِكَ مِنْ نِعْمَۃٍ تُوْلِیْہَا وَاٰلَآئٍ تُجَدِّدُہَا وَبَلِیَّۃٍ تَصْرِفُہَا وَكُرْبَۃٍ تَكْشِفُہَا وَدَعْوَۃٍ تَسْمَعُہَا وَحَسَنَۃٍ تَتَقَبَّلُہَا وَسَیِّئَۃٍ تَتَغَمَّدُہَا اِنَّكَ لَطِیْفٌۢ بِمَا تَشَآئُ خَبِیْرٌ وَّعَلٰی كُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ قَدِیْرٌ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اِنَّكَ اَقْرَبُ مَنْ دُعِیَ وَاَسْرَعُ مَنْ اَجَابَ وَاَكْرَمُ مَنْ عَفٰی وَاَوْسَعُ مَنْ اَعْطٰی وَاَسْمَعُ مَنْ سُئِلَ یَارَحْمٰنَ الدُّنْیَا وَالْاٰخِرَۃِ وَرَحِیْمَہُمَا لَیْسَ كَمِثْلِكَ مَسْئُوْلٌ وَّلَا سِوَاكَ مَاْمُوْلٌ دَعَوْتُكَ فَاَجَبْتَنِیْ وَسَئَلْتُكَ فَاَعْطَیْتَنِیْ وَرَغِبْتُ اِلَیْكَ فَرَحِمْتَنِیْ وَوَثِقْتُ بِكَ فَنَجَّیْتَنِیْ وَفَزِعْتُ اِلَیْكَ فَكَفَیْتَنِیْ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ فَصَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ عَبْدِكَ وَرَسُوْلِكَ وَنَبِیِّكَ وَعَلٰی اٰلِہِ الطَّیِّبِیْنَ الطَّاہِرِیْنَ اَجْمَعِیْنَ وَتَمِّمْ لَنَا نَعَمَآئَكَ وَہَنِّئْنَا عَطَآئَكَ وَاكْتُبْنَا لَكَ شَاكِرِیْنَ وَلِاٰلَآئِكَ ذَاكِرِیْنَ اٰمِیْنَ اٰمِیْنَ رَبَّ الْعَالَمِیْنَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ یَامَنْ مَلَكَ فَقَدَرَ وَقَدَرَ فَقَہَرَ وَعُصِیَ فَسَتَرَ وَاسْتُغْفِرَ فَغَفَرَ یَاغَایَۃِ الطَّالِبِیْنَ الرَّاغِبِیْنَ وَمُنْتَہٰی اَمَلِ الرَّاجِیْنَ یَامَنْ اَحَاطَ بِكُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ عِلْمًا وَّوَسِعَ الْمُسْتَقْبِلِیْنَ رَاْفَۃً وَّرَحْمَۃً وَّحِلْمًا۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ اِنَّا نَتَوَجَّہٗ اِلَیْكَ فِیْ ہٰذِہِ الْعَشِیَّۃِ الَّتِیْ شَرَّفْتَہَا وَعَظَّمْتَہَا بِمُحَمَّدٍ نَبِیِّكَ وَرَسُوْلِكَ وَخِیَرَتِكَ مِنْ خَلْقِكَ وَاَمِیْنِكَ عَلٰی وَحْیِكَ الْبَشِیْرِ النَّذِیْرِ السِّرَاجِ الْمُنِیْرِ الَّذِیْ اَنْعَمْتَ بِہٖ عَلَی الْمُسْلِمِیْنَ وَجَعَلْتَہٗ رَحْمَۃً لِّلْعَالَمِیْنَ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ فَصَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ كَمَا مُحَمَّدٌ اَھْلٌ لِذٰلِكَ مِنْكَ یَاَظِیْمُ فَصَلِّ عَلَیْہِ وَعَلٰی اٰلِہِ الْمُنْتَجَبِیْنَ الطَّیِّبِیْنَ الطَّاہِرِیْنَ اَجْمَعِیْنَ وَتَغَمَّدْنَا بِعَفْوِكَ عَنَّا فَاِلَیْكَ عَجَّتِ الْاَصْوَاتُ بِصُنُوْفِ اللُّغَاتِ فَاجْعَلْ لَنَا۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ فِیْ ہٰذِہِ الْعَشِیَّۃِ نَصِیْبًا مِّنْ كُلِّ خَیْرٍ تَقْسِمُہٗ بَیْنَ عِبَادِكَ وَنُوْرٍ تَہْدِیْ بِہٖ وَرَحْمَۃٍ تَنْشُرُہَا وَبَرَكَۃٍ تُنْزِلُہَا وَعَافِیَۃٍ تُجَلِّلُہَا وَرِزْقٍ تَبْسُطُہٗ یَااَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِیْنَ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ اَقْلِبْنَا فِیْ ہٰذَا الْوَقْتِ مُنْجِحِیْنَ مُفْلِحِیْنَ مَبْرُوْرِیْنَ غَانِمِیْنَ وَلَا تَجْعَلْنَا مِنَ الْقَانِطِیْنَ وَلَا تُخْلِنَا مِنْ رَحْمَتِكَ وَلَا تَحْرِمْنَا مَا نُؤَمِّلُہٗ مِنْ فَضْلِكَ وَلَا تَجْعَلْنَا مِنْ رَحْمَتِكَ مَحْرُوْمِیْنَ وَلَا لِفَضْلِ مَا نُؤَمِّلُہٗ مِنْ عَطَآئِكَ قَانِطِیْنَ وَلَا تَرُدَّنَا خَآئِبِیْنَ وَلَا مِنْ بَابِكَ مَطْرُوْدِیْنَ۔

یَااَجْوَدَ الْاَجْوَدِیْنَ وَاَكْرَمَ الْاَكْرَمِیْنَ اِلَیْكَ اَقْبَلْنَا مُوْقِنِیْنَ وَلِبَیْتِكَ الْحَرَامِ اٰمِّیْنَ قَاصِدِیْنَ فَاَعِنَّا عَلٰی مَنَاسِكِنَا وَاَكْمِلْ لَنَا حَجَّنَا وَاعْفُ عَنَّا وَعَافِنَا فَقَدْ مَدَدْنَا اِلَیْكَ اَیْدِیَنَا فَہِیَ بِذِلَّۃِ الْاِعْتِرَافِ مَوْسُوْمَۃٌ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ فَاَعْطِنَا فِیْ ہٰذِہِ الْعَشِیَّۃِ مَا سَئَلْنَاكَ وَاكْفِنَا مَااسْتَكْفَیْنَا فَلَا كَافِیَ لَنَا سِوَاكَ وَلَا رَبَّ لَنَا غَیْرُكَ نَافِذٌ فِیْنَا حُكْمُكَ مُحِیْطٌۢ بِنَا عِلْمُكَ عَدْلٌ فِیْنَا قَضَآؤُكَ اِقْضِ لَنَا الْخَیْرَ وَاجْعَلْنَا مِنْ اَھْلِ الْخَیْرِ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ اَوْجِبْ لَنَا بِجُوْدِكَ عَظِیْمَ الْاَجْرِ وَكَرِیْمَ الذُّخْرِ وَدَوَامَ الْیُسْرِ وَاغْفِرْ لَنَا ذُنُوْبَنَا اَجْمَعِیْنَ وَلَا تُہْلِكْنَا مَعَ الْہَالِكِیْنَ وَلَا تَصْرِفْ عَنَّا رَافَتَكَ وَرَحْمَتَكَ یَااَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِیْنَ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ اجْعَلْنَا فِیْ ہٰذَا الْوَقْتِ مِمَّنْ سَاَلَكَ فَاَعْطَیْتَہٗ وَشَكَرَكَ فَزِدْتَہٗ وَثَابَ اِلَیْكَ فَقَبِلْتَہٗ وَتَنَصَّلَ اِلَیْكَ مِنْ ذُنُوْبِہٖ كُلِّہَا فَغَفَرْتَہَا لَہٗ یَاذَا الْجَلَالِ وَالْاِكْرَامِ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ وَوَفِّقْنَا وَسَدِّدْنَا وَاقْبَلْ تَضَرُّ عَنَّا یَاخَیْرَ مَنْ سُئِلَ وَیَا اَرْحَمَ مَنِ اسْتُرْحِمَ یَامَنْ لَا یَخْفٰی عَلَیْہِ اِغْمَاضُ الْجُفُوْنِ وَلَا لَحْظُ الْعُیُوْنِ وَلَا مَااسْتَقَرَّ فِیْ الْمَكْنُوْنِ وَلَا مَاانْطَوَتْ عَلَیْہِ مُضْمَرَاتُ الْقُلُوْبِ اَلَا كُلُّ ذٰلِكَ قَدْ اَحْصَاہُ عِلْمُكَ وَوَسِعَہٗ حِلْمُكَ سُبْحَانَكَ وَتَعَالَیْتَ عَمَّا یَقُوْلُ الظَّالِمُوْنَ عُلُوًّا كَبِیْرًا تُسَبِّحُ لَكَ السَّمٰوٰتُ السَّبْعُ وَالْاَرَضُوْنَ وَمَنْ فِیْہِنَّ وَاِنْ مِّنْ شَیْٓءٍ اِلَّا یُسَبِّحُ بِحَمْدِكَ فَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ وَالْمَجْدُ وَعُلُوُّ الْجَدِّ یَاذَاالْجَلَالِ وَالْاِكْرَامِ وَالْفَضْلِ وَالْاِنْعَامِ وَالْاَیَادِی الْجِسَامِ وَاَنْتَ الْجَوَادُ الْكَرِیْمُ الرَّؤُوْفُ الرَّحِیْمُ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ اَوْسِعْ عَلَیَّ مِنْ رِزْقِكَ الْحَلَالِ وَعَافِنِیْ فِیْ بَدَنِیْ وَ دِیْنِیْ وَ اٰمِنْ خَوْفِیْ وَاَعْتِقْ رَقَبَتِیْ مِنَ النَّارِ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ لَا تَمْكُرْ بِیْ وَ لَا تَسْتَدْرِجْنِیْ وَلَا تَخْدَعْنِیْ وَادْرَئْ عَنِّیْ شَرَّ فَسَقَۃِ الْجِنِّ وَالْاِنْسِ۔

He looked at the sky with tears flowing from the eyes and said in a loud voice…
Phir unho ne aasmaan par nigah ki jab-keh unke aankho se aasoon rawaa thay aur woh buland aawaaz se kahe rahe thay…

َااَسْمَعَ السَّامِعِیْنَ یَااَبْصَرَ النَّاظِرِیْنَ یَااَسْرَعَ الْحَاسِبِیْنَ وَیَااَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِیْنَ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّ اٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ السَّادَۃِ الْمَیَامِیْنَ وَاَسْئَلُكَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ حَاجَتِیَ الَّتِیْ اِنْ اَعْطَیْتَنِیْہَا لَمْ یَضُرَّنِیْ مَا مَنَعْتَنِیْ وَاِنْ مَنَعْتَنِیْہَا لَمْ یَنْفَعْنِیْ مَااَعْطَیْتَنِیْ اَسْئَلُكَ فَكَاكَ رَقَبَتِیْ مِنَ النَّارِ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ وَحْدَكَ لَا شَرِیْكَ لَكَ لَكَ الْمُلْكُ وَ لَكَ الْحَمْدُ وَ اَنْتَ عَلٰی كُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ قَدِیْرٌ یَارَبِّ یَارَبِّ یَارَبِّ۔

Then he repeated 'YAA RABBE'۔ His companions were listening to his supplication with full concentration and saying 'Ameen' only۔ Their voices of crying along with Imam Husain's (a.s) grew louder۔ They started for Masha'rul Haraam after the sunset. Sayyid Ibne Ta'oos writes in Iqbal that Imam Husain (a.s) said after 'YAA RABBE', 'YAA RABBE'

Hazrat Imam Husain (a.s) baar baar Ya Rab Ya Rab kehte rahe, jab-keh aap ke ird-gird khade log aap ki dua soonte huwe aameen kehte rahe. Yahan-tak keh aap ke saath saath un sab ki roney ki aawazein buland ho ne lagi. Groob-e-Aaftaab (sunset) tak yahi haalat rahi, phir sab log Masharul Haraam (Muzdalefa) rawana ho gaye. Lekin Sayyed ibne Tawoos (a.r) ne Ya Rab Ya Rab ke baad yeh izaafi kalemaat bhi tahreer (note) farmaayi hai…

اِلٰھِیْ اَنَاالْفَقِیْرُ فِیْ غِنَایَ فَكَیْفَ لَااَكُوْنُ فَقِیْرًا فِیْ فَقْرِیْ اِلٰھِیْ اَنَا الْجَاھِلُ فِیْ عِلْمِیْ فَكَیْفَ لَا اَكُوْنُ جَھُوْلًا فِیْ جَھْلِیْ اِلٰھِیْ اِنَّ اخْتِلَافَ تَدْبِیْرِكَ وَسُرْعَۃَ طَوَآئِ مَقَادِیْرِكَ مَنَعَا عِبَادَكَ الْعَارِفِیْنَ بِكَ عَنِ السُّكُوْنِ اِلٰی عَطَآئٍ وَّالْیَاْسِ مِنْكَ فِی بَلَآئٍ اِلٰھِیْ مِنِّیْ مَا یَلِیْقُ بِلُؤْمِیْ وَمِنْكَ مَا یَلِیْقُ بِكَرَمِكَ۔

اِلٰھِیْ وَصَفْتَ نَفْسَكَ بِاللُّطْفِ وَالرَّافَۃِ لِیْ قَبْلَ وُجُوْدِ ضَعْفِیْ اَفَتَمْنَعُنِیْ مِنْھُمَا بَعْدَ وُجُوْدِ ضَعْفِیْ اِلٰھِیْ اِنْ ظَھَرَتِ الْمَحَاسِنُ مِنِّیْ فَبِفَضْلِكَ وَلَكَ الْمِنَّۃُ عَلَیَّ وَاِنْ ظَھَرَتِ الْمَسَاوِیْ مِنِّیْ فَبِعَدْلِكَ وَلَكَ الْحُجَّۃُ عَلَیَّ۔

اِلٰھِیْ كَیْفَ تَكِلُنِیْ وَقَدْ تَكَفَّلْتَ لِیْ وَكَیْفَ اُضَامُ وَاَنْتَ النَّاصِرُ لِیْ اَمْ كَیْفَ اَخِیْبُ وَاَنْتَ الْحَفِیُّ بِیْ ھَا اَنَا اَتَوَسَّلُ اِلَیْكَ بِفَقْرِیْ اِلَیْكَ وَكَیْفَ اَتَوَسَّلُ اِلَیْكَ بِمَا ھُوَ مَحَالٌ اَنْ یَصِلَ اِلَیْكَ اَمْ كَیْفَ اَشْكُوْا اِلَیْكَ حَالِیْ وَھُوَ لَا یَخْفٰی عَلَیْكَ اَمْ كَیْفَ اُتَرْجِمُ بِمَقَالِیْ وَھُوَ مِنْكَ بَرَزٌ اِلَیْكَ اَمْ كَیْفَ تُخَیِّبُ اٰمَالِیْ وَھِیَ قَدْ وَفَدَتْ اِلَیْكَ اَمْ كَیْفَ لَا تُحْسِنُ اَحْوَالِیْ وَبِكَ قَامَتْ۔

اِلٰھِیْ مَا اَلْطَفَكَ بِیْ مَعَ عَظِیْمِ جَھْلِیْ وَمَا اَرْحَمَكَ بِیْ مَعَ قَبِیْحِ فِعْلِیْ اِلٰھِیْ مَا اَقْرَبَكَ مِنِّیْ وَاَبْعَدَنِیْ عَنْكَ وَمَا اَرْاَفَكَ بِیْ فَمَا الَّذِیْ یَحْجُبُنِیْ عَنْكَ اِلٰھِیْ عَلِمْتُ بِاِخْتِلَافِ الْاٰثَارِ وَتَنَقُّلَاتِ الْاَطْوَارِ اَنَّ مُرَادَكَ مِنِّیْ اَنْ تَتَعَرَّفَ اِلَیَّ فِیْ كُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ حَتّٰی لَا اَجْھَلَكَ فِیْ شَیْٓءٍ۔

اِلٰھِیْ كُلَّمَا اَخْرَسَنِیْ لُؤْمِیْ اَنْطَقَنِیْ كَرَمُكَ وَكُلَّمَا اٰیَسَتْنِیْ اَوْصَافِیْ اَطْمَعَتْنِیْ مِنَنُكَ اِلٰھِیْ مَنْ كَانَتْ مَحَاسِنُہٗ مَسَاوِیَ فَكَیْفَ لَا تَكُوْنُ مَسَاوِیْہِ مَسَاوِیَ وَمَنْ كَانَتْ حَقَایِقُہٗ دَعَاوِیَ فَكَیْفَ لَاتَكُوْنُ دَعَاوِیْہِ دَعَاوِیَ اِلٰھِیْ حُكْمُكَ النَّافِذُ وَمَشِیَّتُكَ الْقَاھِرَۃُ لَمْ یَتْرُكَا لِذِیْ مَقَالٍ مَّقَالًا وَّ لَا لِذِیْ حَالٍ حَالًا۔

اِلٰھِیْ كَمْ مِّنْ طَاعَۃٍۢ بَنَیْتُھَا وَحَالَۃٍ شَیَّدْتُھَا ھَدَمَ اِعْتِمَادِیْ عَلَیْھَا عَدْلُكَ بَلْ اَقَالَنِیْ مِنْھَا فَضْلُكَ اِلٰھِیْ اِنَّكَ تَعْلَمُ اَنِّیْ وَاِنْ لَّمْ تَدُمِ الطَّاعَۃُ مِنِّیْ فِعْلًا جَزْمًا فَقَدْ دَامَتْ مَحَبَّۃً وَّعَزْمًا اِلٰھِیْ كَیْفَ اَعْزِمُ وَاَنْتَ الْقَاھِرُ وَكَیْفَ لَااَعْزِمُ وَاَنْتَ الْاٰمِرُ۔

اِلٰھِیْ تَرَدُّدِیْ فِیْ الْاٰثَارِ یُوْجِبُ بُعْدَ الْمَزَارِ فَاجْمَعْنِیْ عَلَیْكَ بِخِدْمَۃٍ تُوْصِلُنِیْ اِلَیْكَ كَیْفَ یُسْتَدَلُّ عَلَیْكَ بِمَا ھُوَ فِیْ وُجُوْدِہٖ مُفْتَقِرٌ اِلَیْكَ اَیَكُوْنُ لِغَیْرِكَ مِنَ الظُّھُوْرِ مَا لَیْسَ لَكَ حَتّٰی یَكُوْنَ ھُوَ الْمُظْھِرُ لَكَ مَتٰی غِبْتَ حَتّٰی تَحْتَاجَ اِلٰی دَلِیْلٍ یَّدُلُّ عَلَیْكَ وَمَتٰی بَعُدْتَ حَتّٰی تَكُوْنَ الْاٰثَارُ ھِیَ الَّتِیْ تُوْصِلُ اِلَیْكَ عَمِیَتْ عَیْنٌ لَّا تَرَاكَ عَلَیْھَا رَقِیْبًا وَّخَسِرَتْ صَفْقَۃُ عَبْدٍ لَّمْ تَجْعَلْ لَہٗ مِنْ حُبِّكَ نَصِیْبًا۔

اِلٰھِیْ اَمَرْتَ بِالرُّجُوْعِ اِلَی الْاٰثَارِ فَارْجِعْنِیْ اِلَیْكَ بِكِسْوَۃِ الْاَنْوَارِ وَھِدَایَۃِ الْاِسْتِبْصَارِ حَتّٰی اَرْجِعَ اِلَیْكَ مِنْھَا كَمَا دَخَلْتُ اِلَیْكَ مِنْھَا مَصُوْنَ السِّرِّ عَنِ النَّظَرِ اِلَیْھَا وَمَرْفُوْعَ الْھِمَّۃِ عَنِ الْاِعْتَمَادِ عَلَیْھَا اِنَّكَ عَلٰی كُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ قَدِیْرٌ۔

اِلٰھِیْ ھٰذَا ذُلِّیْ ظَاھِرٌۢ بَیْنَ یَدَیْكَ وَھٰذَا حَالِیْ لَا یَخْفٰی عَلَیْكَ مِنْكَ اَطْلُبُ الْوُصُوْلَ اِلَیْكَ وَبِكَ اَسْتَدِلُّ عَلَیْكَ فَاَھْدَنِیْ بِنُوْرِكَ اِلَیْكَ وَاَقِمْنِیْ بِصِدْقِ الْعُبُوْدِیَّۃِ بَیْنَ یَدَیْكَ اِلٰھِیْ عَلِّمْنِیْ مِنْ عِلْمِكَ الْمَخْزُوْنِ وَصُنِّیْ بِسِتْرِكَ الْمَصُوْنِ۔

اِلٰھِیْ حَقِّقْنِیْ بِحَقَآئِقِ اَھْلِ الْقُرْبِ وَاسْلُكْ بِی مَسْلَكَ اَھْلِ الْجَذْبِ۔ اِلٰھِیْ اَغْنِنِیْ بِتَدْبِیْرِكَ لِیْ عَنْ تَدْبِیْرِیْ وَبِاِخْتِیَارِكَ عَنْ اِخْتِیَارِیْ وَاَوْقِفْنِیْ عَلٰی مَرَاكِزِ اضْطِرَارِیْ۔

اِلٰھِیْ اَخْرِجْنِیْ مِنْ ذُلِّ نَفْسِیْ وَطَھِّرْنِیْ مِنْ شَكِّیْ وَشِرْكِیْ قَبْلَ حُلُوْلِ رَمْسِیْ بِكَ اَنْتَصِرُ فَانْصُرْنِیْ وَعَلَیْكَ اَتَوَكَّلُ فَلَا تَكِلْنِیْ وَاِیَّاكَ اَسْئَلُ فَلَا تُخَیِّبْنِیْ وَفِیْ فَضْلِكَ اَرْغَبُ فَلَا تَحْرِمْنِیْ وَبِجَنَابِكَ اَنْتَسِبُ فَلَا تُبْعِدْنِیْ وَبِبَابِكَ اَقِفُ فَلَا تَطْرُدْنِیْ۔

اِلٰھِیْ تَقَدَّسَ رِضَاكَ اَنْ یَّكُوْنَ لَہٗ عِلَّۃٌ مِّنْكَ فَكَیْفَ یَكُوْنُ عِلَّۃٌ مِّنِّیْ۔ اِلٰھِیْ اَنْتَ الْغَنِیُّ بِذَاتِكَ اَنْ یَّصِلَ اِلَیْكَ الْنَّفْعُ مِنْكَ فَكَیْفَ لَا تَكُوْنُ غَنِیًّا عَنِّیْ۔

اِلٰھِیْ اِنَّ الْقَضَآئَ وَالْقَدَرَ یُمَنِّیْنِیْ وَاِنَّ الْھَوٰی بِوَثَآئِقِ الْشَّھْوَۃِ اَسَرَنِیْ فَكُنْ اَنْتَ الْنَّصِیْرَ لِیْ حَتّٰی تَنْصُرَنِیْ وَتُبَصِّرَنِیْ وَاَغْنِنِیْ بِفَضْلِكَ حَتّٰی اَسْتَغْنِیَ بِكَ عَنْ طَلَبِیْ اَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَشْرَقْتَ الْاَنْوَارَ فِیْ قُلُوْبِ اَوْلِیَآئِكَ حَتّٰی عَرَفُوْكَ وَوَحَّدُوْكَ وَاَنْتَ الَّذِیْ اَزَلْتَ الْاَغْیَارَ عَنْ قُلُوْبِ اَحِبَّآئِكَ حَتّٰی لَمْ یُحِبُّوْا سِوَاكَ وَلَمْ یَلْجَئُوْا اِلٰی غَیْرِكَ اَنْتَ الْمُوْنِسُ لَھُمْ حَیْثُ اَوْ حَشَتْھُمُ الْعَوَالِمُ وَاَنْتَ الَّذِیْ ھَدَیْتَھُمْ حَیْثُ اسْتَبَانَتْ لَھُمُ الْمَعَالِمُ مَا ذَا وَجَدَ مَنْ فَقَدَكَ وَمَا الَّذِیْ فَقَدَ مَنْ وَجَدَكَ لَقَدْ خَابَ مَنْ رَضِیَ دُوْنَكَ بَدَلًا وَّلَقَدْ خَسِرَ مَنْ بَغٰی عَنْكَ مُتَحَوِّلًا كَیْفَ یُرْجٰی سِوَاكَ وَاَنْتَ مَا قَطَعْتَ الْاِحْسَانَ وَكَیْفَ یُطْلَبُ مِنْ غَیْرِكَ وَاَنْتَ مَا بَدَّلْتَ عَادَۃَ الْاِمْتِنَانِ۔

یَامَنْ اَذَاقَ اَحِبَّآئَہٗ حَلَاوَۃَ الْمُؤَانَسَۃَ فَقَامُوْا بَیْنَ یَدَیْہِ مُتَمَلِّقِیْنَ وَیَا مَنْ اَلْبَسَ اَوْلِیَآئَہٗ مَلَابِسَ ھَیْبَتِہٖ فَقَامُوْا بَیْنَ یَدَیْہِ مُسْتَغْفِرِیْنَ اَنْتَ الذَّاكِرُ قَبْلَ الذَّاكِرِیْنَ وَاَنْتَ الْبَادِیْ بِالْاِحْسَانِ قَبْلَ تَوَجُّہِ الْعَابِدِیْنَ وَاَنْتَ الْجَوَادُ بِالْعَطَآئِ قَبْلَ طَلَبِ الطَّالِبِیْنَ وَاَنْتَ الْوَھَّابُ ثُمَّ لِمَا وَھَبْتَ لَنَا مِنَ الْمُسْتَقْرِضِیْنَ۔

اِلٰھِیْ اُطْلُبْنِیْ بِرَحْمَتِكَ حَتّٰی اَصِلَ اِلَیْكَ وَاجْذِبْنِیْ بِمَنِّكَ حَتّٰی اُقْبِلَ عَلَیْكَ۔ اِلٰھِیْ اِنَّ رَجَآئِیْ لَا یَنْقَطِعُ عَنْكَ وَاِنْ عَصَیْتُكَ كَمَا اَنَّ خَوْفِیْ لَا یُزَایِلُنِیْ وَاِنْ اَطَعْتُكَ فَقَدْ دَفَعَتْنِیْ الْعَوَالِمُ اِلَیْكَ وَ قَدْ اَوْقَعَنِیْ عِلْمِیْ بِكَرَمِكَ عَلَیْكَ۔

اِلٰھِیْ كَیْفَ اَخِیْبُ وَاَنْتَ اَمَلِیْ اَمْ كَیْفَ اُھَانُ وَعَلَیْكَ مُتَّكَلِیْ اِلٰھِیْ كَیْفَ اَسْتَعِزُّ وَفِیْ اَلذِّلَّۃِ اَرْكَزْتَنِیْ اَمْ كَیْفَ لَا اَسْتَعِزُّ وَاِلَیْكَ نَسَبْتَنِیْ اِلٰھِیْ كَیْفَ لَا اَفْتَقِرُ وَاَنْتَ الَّذِیْ فِیْ الْفُقَرَآئِ اَقَمْتَنِیْ اَمْ كَیْفَ اَفْتَقِرُ وَاَنْتَ الَّذِیْ بِجُوْدِكَ اَغْنَیْتَنِیْ وَاَنْتَ الَّذِیْ لَا اِلٰہَ غَیْرُكَ تَعَرَّفْتَ لِكُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ فَمَا جَھِلَكَ شَیْءٌ وَّاَنْتَ الَّذِیْ تَعَرَّفْتَ اِلَیَّ فِیْ كُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ فَرَاَیْتُكَ ظَاھِرًا فِیْ كُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ وَّاَنْتَ الظَّاھِرُ لِكُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ۔

یَامَنِ اسْتَوٰی بِرَحْمَانِیَّتِہٖ فَصَارَ الْعَرْشُ غَیْبًا فِیْ ذَاتِہٖ مَحَقْتَ الْاٰثَارَ بِالْاٰثَارِ وَمَحَوْتَ الْاَغْیَارَ بِمُحِیْطَاتِ اَفْلَاكِ الْاَنْوَارِ یَامَنِ احْتَجَبَ فِیْ سُرَادِقَاتِ عَرْشِہٖ عَنْ اَنْ تُدْرِكَہُ الْاَبْصَارُ یَامَنْ تَجَلّٰی بِكَمَالِ بَھَآئِہٖ فَتَحَقَّقَتْ عَظَمَتُہُ الْاِسْتِوَآئَ كَیْفَ تَخْفٰی وَاَنْتَ الظَّاھِرُ اَمْ كَیْفَ تَغِیْبُ وَاَنْتَ الرَّقِیْبُ الْحَاضِرُ اِنَّكَ عَلٰی كُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ قَدِیْرٌ وَّالْحَمْدُ لِلہِ وَحْدَہٗ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

ARAFAH

Imam Hussain (a.s)

Roze Arafah ba-waqte Asr maidaane Arafaat mein Imam Husain (a.s) apne farzando aur Shiyo ke saath nehaayat aajezana taur par apne khaime se baahar aaye aur pahaad (jabalur-rehmat) ki baayi taraf (left-side) khade ho kar Kaabe ki taraf rukh kar ke apne dono haath chehre ke saamne la kar phaila diye aur phir Khuda ke huzoor aajezi-o-inkesari ki haalat mein yeh dua padhi.

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Hamd hai Khuda ke liye jiske faisle ko koi badal-ne waala nahi aur koi uski ataa ko rokne waala nahi aur Us jaisa koi sanat-kaar (banaane waala) nahi, aur woh wus’at-o-kushaadgi ke saath ataa karne waala hai. Us ne mukhtalif qism ke makhluqaat banaaye, aur banaayi huwi cheezo ko apni hikmat se mohkam kiya, duniya mein aane waali koi cheez us se posheeda nahi, uske yahan koi amaanat zaaye (barbaad) nahi hoti.

Woh har kaam par jaza dene waala hai. Woh har qaane (qana’at karne waale) ko ziyaada dene waala, aur har naala-o-faryaad karne waale par rahem karne waala hai. Woh bhalaayiya (khair-o-barkat) naazil karne waala aur chamakte noor ke saath mukammal-o-kaamil kitaab ka utaar-ne waala hai. Woh logo ki dua’o ka soonne waala. logo ke dukh dur karne waala. Darajaat (status) buland karne waala aur sarkasho ki jad (roots) kaatne waala hai.

Pas uske siwa koi ma’bood nahi. Koi cheez uske baraabar aur uske maanind (misl) nahi. Aur woh soonne waala dekhne waala hai aur bareek-been (gahri-nazar) khabar rakhne waala hai. Aur Wahi har cheez par qudrat rakhta hai. Ay Allah! Be-shak mai Teri taraf motwajjeh (tawajjoh) huwa hu.

Tere Parwardigaar ho ne ki gawaahi deta hu aur maanta hu keh Tu hi mera paalne waala hai. Aur Teri taraf lauta hu keh Tu ne mujh par apni nemat ka aaghaaz (dena shuru) kiya isse pehle keh mai wujood mein aata. Aur Tu ne mujh ko mitti se paida kiya phir bade buzurgo (aali nasl) pushto mein jagah di, mujhe maut se aur mah-o-saal (month & year) mein aane waali aafaat (museebato) se amaan diya.

Pas mai pai-dar-pai (ek ke baad dusre) pusht-dar-pusht rahmo mein muntaqil hota raha. Aaya (kya) un dino (days) mein jo guzre hai aur un sadiyo (zamaano) mein jo beet chuki hai. Tu ne be-wajeh apni mohabbat-o-maherbaani ke mujh par ehsaan kiya aur mujhe kaafir baadshaho ke daur mein paida nahi kiya keh jinho ne Tere farmaan ko toda (nahi maana) aur Tere Rasoolo ko jhutlaya.

Lekin Tu ne mujh ko us zamaane mein paida kiya jis mein thode arse (short period) mein mujhe hidaayat mil gayi, keh us mein Tu ne mujhe paala aur us se pehle Tu ne achchhe unwaan se mere liye mohabbat zaahir farmaayi, aur behtareen nemate ataa ki.

Phir mere paida karne ka aaghaaz tapak-ne waale aab-e-hayaat se kiya aur mujh ko teen taareeko (stages) mein thaira diya, yaani gosht, khoon aur jild (skin) ke neeche jahan mai ne bhi apni khilqat (creation) ko na dekha. Aur Tu ne mere is mo’aamle mein se kuchh bhi mujh par na daala. Phir Tu ne mujhe rahm-e-maadar (mother’s stomach) se nikala ke mujhe hidaayat de kar duniya mein sahih-o-saalim jism ke saath bheja. Aur gehwaare (cradle) mein meri nigheh-baani ki. Jab mai chhota bachcha tha.

Tu ne mere liye taaza doodh ki ghiza (khoraak) faraaham (provide) kiya. Aur doodh pilaane waaliyo ke dil mere liye narm kar diye, Tu ne maher-baan maawo (mothers) (mothers) ko meri parwarish ka zimme-daar banaya. Jinn-o-Pari ki buri nazar se meri hifaazat farmaayi. Aur mujhe har qism ki kami-beshi se bachaaye rakkha.

Pas Tu buland-o-bartar hai ay maher-baan ay ataawo waale, yahan tak keh mai baate karne laga. Aur you’n Tu ne mujh par apni behtareen nemate puri kar di. Aur har saal mere jism ko badhaya yahan tak keh meri jismaani taraqqi (physical development) apne kamaal tak pohonch gayi. Aur meri shakhsiyat mein tawazun (balance) paida ho gaya to mujh par Teri hujjat qaayem ho gayi, is liye keh Tu ne mujhe apni maarefat karaayi aur apni ajeeb-o-gharib hikmato ke zariye mujhe khaayef kar (dara) diya.

Aur mujhe bedaar-o-baawar kiya un cheezo ke zariye jo Tu ne aasmaan-o-zameen mein ajeeb niraali andaaz se khalq ki. Is tarah mujhe apne shukr-o-zikr se agah kiya. Aur mujh par apni farma-bardaari aur ibaadat laazim farmaayi. Tu ne mujhe woh cheez samjhaayi jo Tere Rasool (s.a.w.a) laaye, aur mere liye razaawo ki qabooliyat aasaan ki. To in sab baato mein mujh par Tera ehsaan hai. Uske saath Teri madad-o-maherbaani hai.

Phir jab Tu ne mujhe paida kiya behtareen khaak (mitti) se to mere liye ay Allah! Tu ne ek-aadha nemat par hi iktefa (ruka) nahi kiya bal-keh mujhe maash (rozi-roti) ke kahi tareeqe aur faaida ke kahi raaste ataa kiye, mujh par apne bade azeem ehsaan-o-karam se aur mujh par apni saabeqa maher-baaniyo se, yahan tak keh jab mujhe Tu ne yeh tamaam nemate de di aur takleefe mujh se dur kar di (phir bhi) Tere aagey meri jur’at aur meri na-daani us mein rukaawat nahi bani, keh Tu ne mujhe woh raaste bataaye jo mujh ko Tere qareeb kare, aur Tu ne mujhe taufeeq di ke Tera pasandeeda banu.

Pas mai ne jo dua maangi Tu ne qabool ki. Aur jo sawaal kiya woh Tu ne ataa ki. Agar mai ne Teri ita’at ki to Tu ne qadr farmaayi aur mai ne shukr kiya to Tu ne ziyaada diya. Yeh sab mujh par Teri nemato ki kasrat aur mujh par Tera ehsaan-o-karam hai.

Pas Tu paak hai, Tu paak hai keh Tu aaghaaz (shuru) karne waala, lautaane waala, taareef-o-buzurghi waala hai aur paak hai Tere naam aur bohot badi hai Teri nemate. To ay mere ma’bood Teri kis nemat ki ghinti (count) karu aur usey yaad karu. Ya Teri kaun kaunsi ataawo ka shukr baja laawu.

Aur ay mere Parwardigaar! Yeh to itni ziyaada hai ke shumaar karne waale unhe shumaar nahi kar sakte. Ya yaad karne waale unhe yaad nahi rakh sakte. Phir ay Allah jo takleefe aur sakhtiya Tu ne mujh se dur ki aur taal di, un mein aksar aisi hai jin se mere liye aaraam-o-sukoon aur khushi zaahir (haasil) huwi hai.

Aur mai gawaahi deta hu ay Allah! apne imaan ki haqeeqat, apne atal (pakke) iraado ki mazbooti, apni waazeh-o-aashkaar tauheed parasti. Apne baatin mein posheeda zameer ki baate, apni aankho ke noor se paiwast (laga) rahu, apni peshaani ke nuqoosh (naqsh-o-nigaar) ke raazo, apne saans (breath) ki rago (vein) ke suraankho, apni naak ke narm-o-mulaayem pardo, apne kaano ki soonne waali jhilliyo (pardo), aur apne chipak-ne aur theek bandh ho ne waale hoto (lips), apni zabaan ki harkaat se nikalne waale lafzo, apne mooh ke upar-neeche ke hisso ke hilne, apne daanto (tooth) ke ugne ki jago (cavaties), apne khaane-peene ke zaiyeka-daar hone, apne sar mein dimaag ki qaraar-gah (thair ne ki jagah), apni gardan mein ghiza (khaane) ki naaliyo, aur un haddiyo jin se sineh ka ghera bana hai (pasliya), apne galey (throat) ke andar latki huwi sheh-rag (juglar vein), apne dil mein aaweza (lage huwe) parde, apne jigar ke bade huwe kinaaro, apni ek-dusre se mili aur jhuki huwi pasliyo, apne jodo ke halqo, apne aaza (parts of body) ke bandhano, apni ungliyo ke puro, aur apne gosht, khoon, apne baalo, apni jild (skin), apne pattho aur naaliyo, apni haddiyo, apne magaz, (bheja), apni rago, aur apne haath-paiyr aur badan ki jo meri sheer-khwaargi (bachpane) mein paida huwi.

Aur zameen par padne waale apne bojh, apni neend, apni bedaari, aur apne sukoon, aur apne ruku-o-sujood ki harakaat, in sab cheezo par agar Tera shukr adaa karna chahu aur tamaam zamaano aur sadiyo mein koshish karta rahu aur zindagi saath de to bhi mai Teri un nemato mein se ek nemat ka shukr adaa karne ki taaqat nahi rakhta, magar Tere ehsaan ke zariye jisse mujh par Tera ek aur shukr waajib ho jaata hai, aur Teri lagataar hamd-o-sana waajib ho jaati hai.

Aur agar mai aisa karna chahu aur Teri makhluq mein se shumaar karne waale bhi shumaar karna chaahe keh ham Teri guzishta-o-aayenda nemate shumaar kare to ham na unki ta’daad ka aur na unki muddat (period) ka hisaab kar sakte hai aur na kar sakenge.

Yeh mumkin hi nahi hai, kyu’n keh Tu ne apni khabre dene waali goya-kitaab (Qur’an-e-naatiq) mein sachchi khabar de kar bataaya hai keh “Aur agar Tum Khuda ki nemato ko shumaar (count) karna chaaho to unka hisaab na laga sakonge.” Teri kitaab sachchi hai ay Allah aur Teri khabre bhi sachchi hai, jo Tere nabiyo aur Rasoolo (a.s) ne tableegh ki woh sachh hai.

Jo Tu ne un par wahi naazil ki woh sachh hai aur jo unke liye aur unke zariye apne deen ko jaari kiya woh sachh hai. Digar (dusre) yeh keh ay mere Allah gawaahi deta hu mai apni mehnat-o-koshish aur apni farma-bardaari-o-himmat ke saath aur mai imaan-o-yaqeen se kehta hu keh: Hamd Khuda hi ke liye hai jisne apna koi beta nahi banaya jo uska waaris ho, aur na mulk-o-hukumat mein koi uska shareek hai. Jo paida karne mein uska ham-kaar (partner) ho.

Aur na woh kamzor hai ke cheezo ke banaane mein koi uski madad kare. Pas woh paak-o-pakeeza hai. Agar zameen-o-aasmaan mein Khuda ke siwa koi ma’bood hota to yeh toot-foot kar tithar-bithar ho jaate. Paak-o-pakeeza hai woh Khuda, ek-o-tanha aur be-neyaaz hai Woh, jisne na kisi ko jnaa (paida kiya) aur na usko kisi ne janam diya aur na uska koi hamsar (partner) hai.

Hamd hai Khuda ke liye baraabar us hamd keh jo uske moqarrab (qareeb-tar) farishto aur uske bheje huwe nabiyo ne ki hai. Aur uske pasand kiye huwe Mohammad (s.a.w.a) Khaatemun Nabiyeen par durood-o-salaam ho aur Khuda ki rehmat ho unpar aur unke Aale Paak par jo nek paak-o-khaalis hai un par salaam ho.

Phir aap ne Khudaaye-taala se haajaat talab karna shuru ki. Jab-keh aap ke aankho se aansoo (tears) jaari thay, usi haalat mein aap baargah-e-Khudawandi mein you’n arz-guzaar huwe…

Ay Ma’bood! Mujhe aisa darne waala bana de keh goya mai Tujhe dekh raha hu. Mujhe namaaz-o-parhez-gaari ki saadat ataa kar, aur na-farmaani ke saath bad-bakht na bana. Apni qaza-o-qadr mein mujhe nek bana de, aur apni taqdeer mein mujhe barkat ataa farma, yahan tak keh jis amr (affairs) mein Tu taakhir kare us mein jaldi na chahu, aur jis mein Tu jaldi chahe us mein taakhir na chahu.

Ay Ma’bood! paida (create) kar de mere nafs mein be-neyaazi, mere dil mein yaqeen, mere amal mein khuloos, meri nighah mein noor, mere deen mein samajh aur mere aaza’a-o-jawareh ko (parts of body) mere liye mufeed qaraar de aur mere kaano, aankho ko mera mutee (obedient) bana de, aur jisne mujh par zulm kiya uske muqaabil meri madad kar, mujhe us se badla lene ke qaabil bana. Yeh aarzoo puri kar aur us se meri aankhe thandi farma.

Ay Ma’bood! Meri sakhti dur kar de, meri parda-poshi farma (mere aibo (galtiyo) ko chhupa), meri khataaye maaf kar de, mere (andar ke) shaitaan ko zaleel kar aur meri zimme-daari puri kara de, mere liye ay Baare-ilaaha duniya-o-aakherat mein buland se buland-tar martabe (rutba) qaraar de.

Ay Ma’bood! Hamd Tere hi liye hai, keh Tu ne mujhe paida kiya, Tu ne mujh ko soonne waala, dekhne waala banaya. Hamd-o-sana Tere hi liye hai keh Tu ne mujhe paida kiya aur Tu ne apni rehmat se behtareen tarbiyat ataa ki, haala keh Tu mujhe khalq karne se be-neyaaz tha. Mere Parwardigaar! Tu ne mujhe paida kiya hai to motawaazin (miyaana-ro) bhi banaya hai.

Parwardigaar! Tu ne mujhe banaya hai to behtareen shakl-o-surat bhi di hai. Ay Parwardigaar! Tu ne mujh par ehsaan kiya aur mujhe sehat-o-aafiyat di. Ay Parwardigaar! Tu ne meri hifaazat ki aur taufeeq di. Ay mere Parwardigaar! Tu ne mujhe nemat di hai to hidaayat bhi ataa ki hai. Ay mere Parwardigaar! Tu ne mujhe tarjeeh di aur tamaam bhalaayiya ataa ki. Ay mere Parwardigaar! Tu ne mujhe dana-o-paani diya.

Ay mere Parwardigaar! Tu ne mujhe daulat di aur qana’at ataa kiya. Ay mere Parwardigaar! Tu ne mujhe meri madad ki aur izzat bakhshi. Ay mere Parwardigaar! Tu ne apni inaayat se mujhe saaf-sutra libaas ataa kiya. Aur apni cheeze meri dastras (kabze) mein de di. Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur zamaane ki sakhtiyo se chhutkaara aur shab-o-roz (din-raat) ki gardish ke muqabale mein meri madad farma, duniya ke khaufo (sharr/evils) aur aakherat ki sakhtiyo se mujhe najaat ataa farma. Aur is zameen par zaalimo ke phailaye huwe fasaad se mehfuz rakh.

Ay Ma’bood! jahan khauf ka aalam ho wahan meri madad farma, aur har us se mujhe bachaaye rakh jisse mai darta hu. Meri jaan-o-imaan ki nigheh-daari farma, dauraane safar meri hifaazat kar aur meri gair-maujoodgi (absence) mein mere maal-o-aulaad ki nigheh-daasht kar (nazar rakh), jo rizq Tu ne mujhe diya us mein barkat de, mere nafs ko mera mutee (obedient) bana de, aur logo ki nighaho mein mujhe izzat ataa kar. Mujh ko Jinno (Jinnaat) aur Insaano ke sharr (nuksaan) se mehfuz farma. Mere gunaho par mujhe be-parda (sharminda) na kar, mere baatin (kharaabiyo ki wajah) se mujhe ruswa na kar. Mere amal par mera moaakhiza (puchh-taach) na kar. Apni nemate mujh se na chheen. Mujhe apne gair ke hawaale na kar.

Mere Ma’bood! Tu mujhe jiske bhi hawaale karega woh jald hi mujh se apni nazre pher lega ya thode arse (dino) ke baad mujh se nafrat karne lagega. Ya unke hawaale karega jo mujhe past-o-ruswa samjhe. Jab-keh Tu mera Parwardigaar aur mera Maalik hai. Mai apni is be-kasi, apne ghar se duri, aur jise Tu ne mujh par ikhtiyaar diya hai Tujhi se uski shikaayat karta hu, mere Allah mujh par apna ghazab naazil na farma.

Pas agar Tu naaraaz na ho to phir mujhe Tere siwa kisi ki parwah nahi. Teri zaat paak hai, Teri maherbaani mujh par bohot ziyaada hai. Pas mazeed (aur) sawaal karta hu be-waaste Tere noor ke jisse zameen aur saare aasmaan monawwar huwe, taareekiya (andhere) uske zariye se chhat gayi. Aur us se aghle-pichhle logo ke kaam sudhar gaye. Yeh keh mujhe maut na de jab Tu mujh se naaraaz ho, aur mujh par sakhti na daal, tauba karta hu aur Tujh se moaafi ka taalib hu yahan-tak keh Tu meri maut se pehle mujh se raazi ho jaaye.

Tere siwa koi ma’bood nahi hai keh Tu hurmat waale shaher (city) hurmat waale mashaayer (nishaaniya) aur pakeeza ghar Kaabe ka Rab hai. Jis mein Tu ne barkat naazil ki aur usey logo ke liye jaaye-aman qaraar diya, ay woh jo apni narmi (lutf-o-karam) se bade bade gunah maaf karta hai.

Ay woh jo apne fazl se nemate kaamil karta hai, ay woh jo apne karam se bohot ziyaada ataa karta hai. Ay sakhti ke waqt meri madad par amaadah (taiyyaar) ay tanhaayi ke waqt mein mere saathi, ay mushkil mein mere faryaad-ras. Ay mujhe nemat dene waale Maalik. Ay mere ma’bood aur ay mere aaba-o-ajdaad Ibrahim (a.s), Ismaeel (a.s), Isaaq (a.s)-o-Yaqoob (a.s) ke ma’bood aur moqarrib farishto Jibraeel (a.s), Mikayeel (a.s) aur Israfeel (a.s) ke Rab, aur ay Khaatemun Nabiyeen Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unke graami-qadr (most respect full) Aal (a.s) ke Rab.

Ay Taurait, Injeel, Zaboor aur haq-o-baatil ke darmiyaan farq karne waali kitaab (Furqaan) ke naazil karne waale, ay Kaaf-Haa-Yaa-Ain-Soaad, Ta-Ha, Yaseen aur Qur’an-e-Kareem ke naazil karne waale. Tu hi meri jaaye-panah hai. Mere Allah! Mai ne woh kiya jo meri pasti ka taqaza hai aur Tu woh karega jo Teri badaayi ke laayak hai. Mere Allah! Meri kamzori se pehle hi Teri zaat-e-lutf-o-karam ke ausaaf (sifaat) rakhti hai, to kya meri kamzori ke baad mujh se Tu yeh maher-baaniya rok dega? Mere Allah! Agar mujh se nekiya zaahir huwi to yeh Tera fazl hai aur mujh par yeh Tera ehsaan hai, aur agar mujh se buraaiyo ka zahoor huwa hai to yeh Tera adl hai aur mujh par Teri hujjat qaayem ho gayi hai.

Mere Allah! Kaise Tu mujh ko chhod dega jab-keh Tu mera kafeel (paalne waala) hai, kyu’n-kar mai rond diya jaau jab-keh Tu mera madad-gaar hai. Ya kaise be-aas (hopeless) ho jaau jab-keh Tu mujh par maher-baan hai. Ab mai Teri baargah mein apne faqr ke saath aaya hu, aur kis tarah Tujhe apna wasila banaawu jab-keh yeh na-mumkin hai keh koi Tujh tak pohonch paaye, ya kyu’n-kar Tujh se apne haalaat ki shikaayat karu jab-keh woh Tujh se posheeda (chhupa huwa) nahi hai.

Ya kis tarah apni zabaan se unki tashreeh (describe) karu jab-keh woh Teri taraf se aur Tujh par zaahir hai. Ya kyu’n-kar meri aarzooye na-kaam rahegi jab-keh yeh Tere janaab mein pesh ki ja chuki hai. Ya kyu’n-kar mere haalaat behtar na hoge jab-keh mai Teri wajah se qaayem hu.

Mere Allah! Tera kitna lutf hai mujh par jab-keh mai bada hi na-daan hu. Aur Tu kis qadr maher-baan hai mujh par jab-keh mai khata-kaar hu. Mere Allah! Tu kitna nazdeek hai mujh se jab-keh mai tujh se bohot dur hu, aur kitna maher-baan hai Tu mujh par phir kis ne mujhe Tujh se hata diya hai.

Mere Allah! Duniya ke haalaat ki tabdiliyo aur tareeqo ke tageeraat (changing) ke zariye mai jaan gaya keh Teri muraad yeh hai keh Tu har cheez se mujhe apni shanasaayi (pehchaan) karaye, ta-keh mai kisi cheez ke zimn mein Teri qudrat se na-waaqif na rahu.

Mere Allah! Jab meri pasti mujhe goongh kar deti hai to Tera karam mujhe goya (bolne ke laayak) kar deta hai. Jab bhi mere burey ausaaf (sifaat) mujhe mayoos karte hai, Tere ehsaan mujhe housla dete hai.

Mere Allah! Jis shakhs ki khoobiya bhi buraaiya ho, to uski buraaiya kyu’n na buraaiya shumaar ki jaayegi? Aur jis shakhs ki haqeeqat hi mahez (sirf) khokhli baate ho to uski khaali-khooli baate kyu’n na mahez (sirf) baate tasawwur ki jaayegi.

Mere Allah! Tera hukm jaari hai aur Teri marzi qaahir-o-ghaalib hai keh unke muqaabil bolne waala bol nahi sakta aur na koi sahib-e-haal kuchh kar sakta hai.

Mere Allah! Kitni hi baar mai ne ita’at karte huwe ibaadat ki shakl banaayi hai magar Tere adl ke khauf se us par mera bharosa na raha, bal-keh woh Tere fazl se meri bakhshish ka zariya bani.

Mere Allah! Be-shak Tu jaanta hai keh agar-che mai ibaadat mein mustaqillan (hamesha) mashgul nahi hu to bhi Tujh se meri mohabbat hamesha raasikh (deep) hai.

Mere Allah! Kis qadar bandagi ka iraada karu jab-keh Tu ghaalib hai aur kyu’n-kar iraada na karu jab-keh Tu hukm deta hai. Mere Allah! Teri qudrat ke aasaar mein gaur karna Tere didaar se duri ka sabab hai.

Pas mujhe apne huzoor haazir rakh, Teri sarkaar mein pohonch paau. Kyu’n-kar woh cheez Teri taraf rehnumaayi kar sakti hai jo apne wajood hi mein Teri mohtaaj hai. Aaya (kya) Tere gair ke liye aisa zahoor hai jo Tere liye nahi hai? yahan tak keh woh Tujhe zaahir karne waala ban jaaye, Tu kab ghaayab tha keh kisi aise nishaan ki haajat ho jo Tere ho ne ki daleel thahraye. Aur Tu kab dur tha keh aasaar aur nishaan Tujh tak pohonchaane ka zariya-o-wasila bane.

Andhi (blind) hai woh aankh jo Tujh ko apna nigheh-baan nahi paati. Us bande ka sauda (deal) khasaare (nuksaan) waala hai jis ko Tu ne apni mohabbat ka hissa nahi diya. Mere Allah! Tu ne apni qudrat ke aasaar par tawajjoh ka hukm diya pas mujh ko apne noor ke pardo aur baseerat ke raasto ki taraf le chal, ta-keh uske zariye Teri baargah mein aawu. Jis tarah un hi ke zariye Teri taraf raah paayi, unhe dekh kar raaz-e-haqeeqat ki khabar paau aur unke sahaare apni himmat ko buland karu.

Be-shak Tu har cheez par qudrat rakhta hai. Mere Allah! Yeh meri pasti hai jo Tere aagey ayaan (zaahir) hai, aur yeh hai meri buri haalat jo Tujh se posheeda nahi, mai Teri baargah mein pohonchna chahta hu aur Tujh par Tujhi ko daleel thahrata hu. Pas apne Noor se meri apni taraf se rehnumaayi kar aur apne huzoor mujhe sachchi bandagi par qaayem rakh.

Mere Allah! Mujhe apne posheeda uloom mein se taleem de, aur apne mohkam parde ke saath meri hifaazat kar. Mere Allah! Mujhe apne ahle-qurb (qareebi logo) ke haqayeq se bahraawar (jaan-kaari ataa) farma. Aur unki raah par daal jo Teri taraf kheenchte chale jaate hai. Mere Allah! apne tadabbur aur pasand ke zariye mujhe mere tadbeer aur pasand se be-neyaaz kar de aur pareshani ke aalam mein mujhe saabit qadam rakh.

Mere Ma’bood! Mujhe mere nafs ki pasti se nikaal le, mujhe shak aur shirk se paak kar de qabl iske keh mai qabr mein jaau Tujh se madad chahta hu meri madad farma. Tujh par bharosa karta hu pas mujhe chhod na de, Tujh se maangta hu pas mujhe na-ummeed na kar. Tere fazl ki aas (ummeed) lagaayi hai pas mujhe mehroom na kar. Teri baargah se rishta joda hai pas mujhe dur na karna. Tera darwaaza khat-khata ta hu pas mujhe bhaga na dena.

Mere Allah! Teri raza paak hai mumkin nahi is mein Teri taraf se naqs (defect) aaye, phir kaise mumkin hai keh mai isey naqs-daar kahu. Mere Allah! Tu apni zaat mein be-neyaaz hai, isse keh Tujhe apni zaat se nafa (faaida) pohonche, kyu’n-kar Tu mujh se be-neyaaz na hoga.

Mere Allah! Qaza-o-Qadr mujh ko aarzumand banaati hai aur khwahish-e-nafs mujhe aarzuwo ka qaidi bana leti hai. Pas Tu mera madad-gaar ban jaata keh kaam-yaab ho jaau, aur bina (sahib-e-baseerat) ho jaau aur apne fazl se mujh ko be-neyaaz kar de, ta-keh Tere zariye haajat se be-neyaaz ho jaau. Tu woh hai jisne apne dosto ke dilo ko noor ki shuwawo (rays) se roshan kar diya to unho-ne Tujhe pehchaana aur Tujhe ek maana.

Aur Tu woh hai jisne apne dosto ke dilo se gairo ko dur kar diya to woh siwaaye Tere kisi se mohabbat nahi rakhte, aur Tere gair ki panah nahi lete. Jab-keh zamaana unko heraasa (hairaan-o-pareshan) kare, us waqt Tu hi unka ham-dam (saathi) hai aur Tu woh hai jisne unki rehnumaayi ki jab keh woh Tere nishaan-o-burhaan se dur huwe.

Us ne kya paya jisne Tujhe khoya aur us ne kuchh na khoya jisne Tujh ko paya.

Aur Yaqeenan na-kaam huwa woh jo Tere bajaaye kisi aur ko pasand karne laga aur woh ghaate mein pada jo sar-kashi ke saath Tujh se phir gaya, kis tarah Tere gair se ummeed rakkhi ja sakti hai jab-keh Tera ehsaan-o-karam rukta hi nahi. Aur kyu’n-kar Tere gair se sawaal kiya ja sakta hai jab-keh Tere fazl-o-ehsaan karne ki aadat mein tabdili nahi aati.

Aur woh jo apne dosto ko ulfat ki mithaas chakhata hai pas woh uske huzoor taarife karte khade ho jaate hai, ay woh jo apne dosto ko apni haibat ka libaas pehnata hai to woh uske saamne khade hote hai bakhshish maangte huwe, Tu yaad karne waalo se pehle unko yaad rakhne waala hai. Tu ehsaan mein ibteda karne waala hai. Ibaadat guzaaro ke tawajjoh karne se pehle Tu ataa mein izaafa karne waala hai. maang-ne waalo ke maang-ne se pehle Tu bohot dene waala hai phir us mein se ba-taure qarz maangta hai jo kuchh Tu ne hamein diya hai.

Mere Allah! apni rehmat se mujh ko bula le, ta-keh mai Tere huzoor pohonch saku. Mujhe apni taraf kheench le ta-keh Teri baargah mein aa saku. Mere Allah! Be-shak meri aas (ummeed) Tujh se nahi tootegi, agar-che mai Teri na-farmaani karu jaisa keh mera khauf dur na hoga agar-che mai Teri ita’at bhi karu. Pas zamaane ki sakhtiyo ne mujh ko Teri taraf dhakel diya aur Teri nawaazish ke ilm ne Teri baargah mein pohoncha diya.

Mere Allah! Kyu’n-kar na-ummeed ho jaau jab-keh Tu meri aarzoo hai, Ya kaise past hohunga jab-keh mera bharosa Tujh par hai. Mere Allah! Kaise izzat ka daawa karu jab-keh is zillat-o-khwari mein Tu ne mujhe yaad kiya. Ya kaise izzat ka daawa karu keh meri nisbat Teri taraf hai.

Mere Allah! Kyu’n-kar faqir na banu jab-keh Tu ne mujhe faqiro ki saf mein rakkha hai. Ya kaise mai faqir banu jab-keh Tu ne mujh ko apni ataa se gani kiya huwa hai. Aur Tu woh hai keh Tere siwa koi ma’bood nahi. Tu ne har cheez ko apni pehchaan karaayi, pas koi cheez nahi jo Tujhe pehchaanti na ho aur Tu woh hai jisne har cheez ke zariye mujhe apni maarefat karaayi, pas mai ne Tujhe har cheez mein ayaan-o-numaya dekha. Aur Tu har cheez par zaahir-o-aashkaar hai.

Ay woh jo apni rehmat-e-aamma (common mercy) ke saath qaayem hai, keh arsh uski zaat mein posheeda ho gaya hai. Tu ne apni nishaaniyo se digar (dusri) nishaaniyo ko mita diya. Tu ne apne gairo ko noorani aasmaano ke halqo (ghere) mein na’bood kar diya.

Ay woh jo apne arsh ki chilmaano (manzilo) mein posheeda ho (chhup) gaya dekhti aankhe usey dekh nahi paati. Ay woh keh jisne apne noor-e-kamil ka jalwa dikhaya to uski azmat qayem-o-bar-qaraar ho gayi. Kyu’n-kar posheeda hai Tu jab-keh aashkaar hai. Ya kaise Tu posheeda hai jab-keh Tu nigheh-baan aur haazir hai. Be-shak Tu har cheez par qudrat rakhta hai. Aur us Allah ke liye hamd-o-sana hai jo ekta (ek-o-tanha) hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

ARAFAH

Imam Hussain (a.s)

The dua of Imam Husain (a.s), on the day of Arafat is one of the famous dua. The sons of Ghalib Asadi, narrated that they, once, accompanied Imam Husain (a.s) at the Arafat Night when he left his tent with submission and reverence. He walked slowly until he, accompanied by a group of his household, stopped at the left side of Mount Arafat and turned his face towards the Holy Ka`bah. He then raised his hands to the level of his face, just like a poor man begging food, and said…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Praise belongs to God whose decree none may avert, and whose gift none may prevent. No fashioner's fashioning is like His fashioning, and He is the Generous, the All-embracing. He brought forth the varieties of unprecedented creatures and perfected through His wisdom all He had fashioned.

Hidden not from Him are harbingers, nor lost with Him are deposits. He repays every fashioner, feathers the nest of all who are content and has mercy upon all who humble themselves. He sends down benefits and the all-encompassing Book in radiant light. He hears supplications, averts afflictions, raises up in degrees, and knocks down tyrants.

For there is no god other than He, nothing is equal to Him, "Like Him there is naught, and He is the Hearing, the Seeing", the subtle, the Aware, and "He is powerful over all things". O God, I make Thee my quest and bear witness to Thy Lordship, acknowledging that Thou art my Lord and to Thee is my return.

Thou originated me by Thy blessing before I was a thing remembered. Thou created me from dust, then gavest me a place in the loins (of my fathers), secure from the uncertainty of Fate and the vagaries of the ages and the years. I remained a traveller from loin to womb in a time immemorial of past days and bygone centuries.

In Thy tenderness, bounty and goodness toward me Thou didst not send me out into the empire of the leaders of disbelief, those who broke Thy covenant and cried lies to Thy messengers. Rather, Thou sentest me out to that guidance which had been foreordained for me, the way which Thou madest easy for me and in which Thou nurtured me.

And before that Thou wert kind to me through Thy gracious fashioning and abundant blessings. Thou originated my creation from a sperm-drop spilled and madest me to dwell in a threefold gloom among flesh, blood and skin. Thou gavest me not to witness my creation, nor didst Thou entrust me with anything of my own affair.

Then thou sentest me out into the world for the guidance that had been foredained for me, complete and unimpaired. Thou watched over me in the cradle as an infant boy, provided me with food, wholesome milk, and turned the hearts of the nurse-maids toward me. Thou entrusted my upbringing to compassionate mothers, guarded me from the calamities brought by the jinn and kept me secure from excess and lack.

High art Thou, O Merciful! O Compassionate! Then when I began to utter speech Thou completed for me Thy abundant blessings.

Thou nurtured me more and more each year until, when my nature was perfected and my strength balanced, Thou madest Thy argument encumbent upon me by inspiring me with knowledge of Thee, awing me with the marvels of Thy wisdom, awakening me to the wonders of Thy creation which Thou hadst multiplied in Thy Heaven and Thy earth, and instructing me in Thy thanks and remembrance.

Thou madest encumbent upon me Thy obedience and worship, madest me to understand what Thy messengers had brought and madest easy for me the acceptance of Thy good pleasure. Thou wast gracious to me in all of this, through Thy succour and kindness.

Then, since Thou created me from the best soil, Thou wert not satisfied, my God, that I should have one blessing without another. Thou provided me with varieties of sustenance and kinds of garments and Thy tremendous, most tremendous, graciousness to me and Thy eternal goodness toward me.

And finally, when Thou hadst completed for me every blessing and turned away from me all misfortunes, Thou wert not prevented by my ignorance and audacity from guiding me toward that which would bring me nigh to Thee or from giving me success in that which would bring me close to Thee.

For if I prayed to Thee Thou answered, if I asked of Thee Thou gavest, if I obeyed Thee Thou showed Thy gratitude, and if I thanked Thee Thou gavest me more. All of that was to perfect Thy blessings upon me and Thy goodness toward me. So glory be to Thee; Glory be to Thee, who are Producer and Reproducer, Laudable, Glorious.

Holy are Thy Names and tremendous Thy bounties. So which of Thy blessings, my God, can I enumerate by counting and mentioning? For which of Thy gifts am I able to give thanks? Since they, O Lord, are more than reckoners can count or those who entrust to memory can attain by knowledge. But the affliction and hardship, O God, that Thou turned and averted from me is more than the health and happiness that came to me.

And I witness, my God, by the truth of my faith, the knotted resolutions of my certainty, my pure and unadulterated profession of Unity, the hidden inwardness of my consciousness, the places to which the streams of light of my eyes are attached, the lines on my forehead's surface, the openings for my breath's channels, the parts of my nose's soft point, the paths of my ears' canals, what my lips close upon and compress, the movements of my tongue in speaking, the joint at the back of my mouth and jaw, the sockets of my molar teeth, the place where I swallow my food and drink, that which bears my brain, the hollow passages of my neck's fibers, that which is contained in my breast's cavity, the carriers of my aorta, the places where my heart's curtain is attached, the small pieces of flesh around my liver, that which the ribs of my sides encompass, the sockets of my joints, the contraction of my members, the tips of my fingers, my flesh, my blood, my hair, my skin, my nerves, my windpipe, my bones, my brain, my veins, and all of my members, what was knitted upon them in the days when I was a suckling baby, what the earth has taken away from me, my sleep, my waking, my being still, and the movements of my bowing and prostrating, that had I taken pains and had I striven for the duration of the epochs and ages were my life to be extended through them to deliver thanks for one of Thy blessings, I would not have been able to do so, except by Thy grace, which alone makes encumbent upon me never-ending and ever renewed gratitude to Thee, and fresh and ever present praise.

Indeed, and were I and the reckoners among Thy creatures ever so eager to calculate the extent of Thy bestowal of blessings, whether past or approaching, we would fail to encompass it through numbers or to calculate its boundaries. Never how could it ever be done! For Thou announcest in Thy eloquent Book and truthful Tiding, "And if you count God's blessing, you will never number it".

Thy Book, O God, Thy Message, has spoken the truth! And Thy prophets and messengers delivered Thy revelation that Thou hadst sent down upon them and the religion that Thou hadst promulgated for them and through them.

And I witness, my God, by my effort, my diligence, and the extent of my obedience and my capacity, and I say as a believer possessing certainty, "Praise belongs to God, who has not taken to Him a son" that He might have an heir, "and who has not any associate in His dominion" who might oppose Him in what He creates, "nor any protector out of humbleness" who would aid Him in what He fashions.

So glory be to Him, glory be to Him! "Why, were there gods in earth and heaven other than God, they would surely go to ruin" and be rent. Glory be to God, the Unique, the One, "the Everlasting Refuge" who "has not begotten, nor has He been begotten, and equal to Him there is none".

Praise belongs to God, praise equal to the praise of the angels stationed near to Him and the prophets sent by Him. And God bless His elect, Muhammad, the Seal of the Prophets, and his virtuous, pure and sincere household, and give them peace. Then he began to supplicate. He occupied himself with prayer as tears ran from his blessed eyes.

Then he said: O God, cause me to fear Thee as if I were seeing Thee, give me felicity through piety toward Thee, make me not wretched by disobedience toward Thee, choose the best for me by Thy decree (qadha') and bless me by Thy determination (qadar), that I may love not the hastening of what Thou hast delayed, nor the delaying of what Thou hast hastened.

O God, appoint for me sufficiency in my soul, certainty in my heart, sincerity in my action, light in my eyes, and insight in my religion. Give me enjoyment of my bodily members, make my hearing and my seeing my two inheritors, help me against him who wrongs me, show me in him my revenge and my desires, and console thereby my eyes.

O God, remove my affliction, veil my defects, forgive my offence, drive away my Satan, dissolve my debt, and give me, my God, the highest degree in the world to come and in this world.

O God, to Thee belongs the praise, just as Thou created me and made me to hear and to see; and to Thee belongs the praise, just as Thou created me and made me a creature unimpaired as a mercy to me, while Thou hadst no need of my creation.

My Lord, since Thou created me and then made straight my nature; my Lord, since Thou caused me to grow and made good my shape; my Lord, since Thou didst good to me and gavest me well-being in my soul; my Lord, since Thou preserved me and gavest me success; my Lord, since Thou blessed me and then guided me; my Lord, since Thou chosest me and gavest me of every good; my Lord, since Thou gavest me to eat and drink; my Lord, since Thou enriched me and contented me; my Lord, since Thou aided me and exalted me; my Lord, since Thou dothed me with Thy pure covering and smoothed the way for me by Thy sufficient fashioning.

Bless Muhammad and the household of Muhammad, aid me against the misfortunes of time and the calamities of nights and days, deliver me from the terrors of this world and the torments of the world to come and spare me from the evil of that which the evildoers do in the earth.

O God, as for what I fear, spare me from it, and as for what I seek to avoid, guard me against it. in my soul and my religion watch over me, in my travelling protect me, in my family and my property appoint for me a successor, in what Thou hast provided for me bless me, in my soul humble me, in the eyes of men magnify me, from the evil of jinn and men preserve me, for my sins disgrace me not, for my inward secrets shame me not, for my action try me not, of Thy blessings deprive me not and to other than Thee entrust me not.

My God, to whom wouldst Thou entrust me? To a relative? He would cut me off. Or to a stranger? He would look at me with displeasure. Or to those who act toward me with arrogance? But Thou art my Lord and the sovereign over my affair. I would complain to Thee of my exile and the remoteness of my abode, and that he whom Thou hast made sovereign over me despises me.

My God, so cause not Thy wrath to alight upon me. If Thou becomest not wrathful with me I will have no care, glory be to Thee but Thy protection is more embracing. So I ask Thee, O Lord, by the Light of Thy Face by which the earth and the heavens are illuminated, shadows are removed, and the affairs of the ancients and the later folks are set aright, not to cause me to die when Thy wrath is upon me, nor to send down upon me Thy anger.

The pleasure is Thine The pleasure is Thine, to be satisfied with me before that. There is no god but Thou, Lord of the Holy Land, the Sacred Monument, and the Ancient House, upon which Thou caused blessing to descend and which Thou madest a sanctuary for mankind.

O He who pardons the greatest sins by His clemency! O He who lavishes blessings by His bounty! O He who gives abundance by His generosity! O Sustenance to me in my adversity! O Companion to me in my solitude! O Aid to me in my affliction! O Benefactor to me in my blessing!

O my God and God of my fathers, Abraham, Ishmael, Isaac and Jacob! Lord of Gabriel, Michael and Israfil! Lord of Muhammad, the Seal of the Prophets, and his household, the chosen ones! Revealer of the Torah, the Gospel, the Psalms and the Criterion, and Sender down of Kaf Ha' Ya' Ayn Sad, Ta' Ha', Ya' Sin, and the Wise Quran! Thou art my cave (of refuge) when the roads for all their amplitude constrict me and the land for all its breadth is strait for me.

If not for Thy mercy, I would have been among the perishing, and Thou annullest my slip. If not for Thy covering me, I would have been among the disgraced, and Thou confirmest me with help against my enemies. And if not for Thy helping me, I would have been among those overcome.

O He who appropriated loftiness and exaltation to Himself, so His friends (awliya') are mighty through His might! O He before whom kings place the yoke of abasement around their necks, for they fear His overwhelming power! "He knows the treachery of the eyes and what the breasts conceal" and the unseen brought by time and fate.

O He about whom none knows how He is but He! O He about whom none knows what He is but He! O He whom none knows but He! O He who squeezed the earth onto the water and held back the air with the sky! O He to whom belong the noblest Names! O He who possesses kindness, which will never be cut off!

O He who assigned the cavalcade to Joseph in the barren land, brought him out of the well and made him a king after slavery! O He who returned him to Jacob after "his eyes were whitened with sorrow that he was suppressing".

O He who removed affliction and tribulation from Job and restrained Abraham's hands from the sacrifice of his son after he had reached old age and his life had passed by! O He who answered the prayer of Zachariah and bestowed upon him John, not leaving him childless and alone!

O He who brought Jonah out from the stomach of the fish! O He who parted the sea for the Children of Israel, then saved them and drowned Pharoah and his hosts! O He who sends winds heralding His mercy! O He who does not hurry (to act) against those of His creatures who disobey Him!

O He who rescued the sorcerers after (their) long denial! They had early benefitted from His blessing, eating His provision and worshipping other than Him; they had opposed, denied and cried lies to His messengers. O God! O God! O Beginner, O Creator with no compeer!

O Everlasting who has no end! O Living when nothing was alive! O Quickener of the dead! O "He Who is aware of the deserts of every soul". O He toward whom my gratitude was little, yet He deprived me not! My transgression was great, yet He disgraced me not! He saw me committing acts of disobedience, yet he made me not notorious!

O He who watched over me in childhood! O He who provided for me in my adulthood! O He whose favors toward me cannot be reckoned and whose blessings cannot be repaid! O He who has confronted me with the good and the fair, and I have confronted Him with evil and disobedience in return!

O He who led me to faith before I had come to know gratitude for His gracious bestowal O He upon whom I called when I was sick and He healed me, when naked and He clothed me, when hungry and He satisfied me, when thirsty and He gave me to drink, when abased and He exalted me, when ignorant and He gave me knowledge, when alone and He increased my number, when away and He returned me, when empty-handed and He enriched me, when in need of help and He helped me, and when rich and He took not from me.

I refrained from (calling upon Thee in) all of that and Thou caused me to begin (to call). Thine are the praise and the gratitude! O He who overlooked my slip, relieved my distress, heard my prayer, covered my defects, forgave my sins, caused me to reach my desire, and helped me against my enemy!
