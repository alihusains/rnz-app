Chp. 3:Ver. 193
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَا فَاغْفِرْ لَنَا ذُنُوْبَنَا وَكَفِّرْ عَنَّا سَيِّاٰتِنَا وَتَوَفَّنَا مَعَ الْاَبْرَارِ۔
Our Lord! forgive us therefore our faults, and cover our evil deeds and make us die with the righteous.

Parwardigaar! Ab hamare gunaho’n ko maaf farma aur hamari buraaiyo’n ki parda-poshi farma (dhaanp de) aur hamein nek bando’n ke saath mehshoor farma.
