Chp. 25:Ver. 65/66
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَا اصْرِفْ عَنَّا عَذَابَ جَهَنَّمَ اِنَّ عَذَابَهَا كَانَ غَرَامًا۔ اِنَّهَا سَاۗءَتْ مُسْتَقَرًّا وَّمُقَامًا۔
O our Lord! Turn away from us the punishment of hell, surely the punishment thereof is a lasting evil. Surely it is an evil abode and (evil) place to stay.

Parwardigaar! Ham se azaab-e-Jahannam ko pher de keh uska azaab bohot sakht aur paay-daar hai. Woh bad-tareen manzil aur mehl-e-eqaamat (thikana) hai.
