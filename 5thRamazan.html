SHAB KI DUA...

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Khuda-wand mai Tujh se sawaal karta hu Tere naamo ke wasile se, keh jo behtareen naam hai, yahi woh naam hai keh jinke zariye se Tu shifa naazil karta hai aur bimaariyo ko dur karta hai.

Keh Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur mujhe aafiyat aur shifa ataa kar aur apne naam ke wasile se har qism ki bimaari aur bala ko mujh se dur kar de aur mere roze qubool kar.

Aur qaraar de mujh ko un logo mein se jinho ne roza rakkha, namaaz padhi aur unke amal se Tu raazi huwa aur qaraar de mujh ko un logo mein se keh jinke tamaam aaza o jawaare (parts of body) ne roza rakkha ho aur jinho ne apni zabaan ko bad-goyi (vulgar talks) aur sharam-gaaho ko gunaho se mehfuz rakkha.

Aur mujhe aise aamaal ki taufiq de keh jis ko Tu pasand kare aur ehsaan kar mujh par be-mahel (be-faaida) guftagu se bacha kar aur sukoon o itminaan inaayat farma kar aur mujhe aisi parhez-gaari inaayat farma jo Teri na-farmaani se mujhe baaz rakkhe ay sab se zeyaada rahem karne waale!

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Aaj ke din mujhe maafi (bakhshish) maangne waalo mein se qaraar de, aaj ke din mujhe Apne neku-kaar ibaadat-guzaar bando’n mein se qaraar de, aur aaj ke din mujhe Apne nazdeeki dosto mein se qaraar de, Apni chaahat se, ay sab se zeyaada rahem karne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

NAMAZ…

1) Recite 2-rakat namaz. In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) 50 times. After Namaz recite 100 times Salawat.

NIGHT DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, indeed I ask You for the sake of Your names which the best of the names through whom You send down the cure and with whom You remove the maladies; that You bless Muhammad and Aale Muhammad (a.s) and bestow prosperity and cure for me and through the sake of Your names remove every disease and trouble from me and accept my fasts and make me of those who fasted and prayed and by whose deeds You are satisfied and make me of those whose every organ has fasted; whom You have kept away from bad talks and sins of the private parts and give me taufeeq for such deeds that You like and bestow on me by not talking on inappropriate occasions and bless me with peace and tranquility and give such piety that keeps me away from Your disobedience, O the most merciful of the merciful ones.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah let me be in this month, from among those who turn repentant and ask forgiveness for their sins; let me be from among those who do good and obey (Thee); let me be from among those who are Thy close friends, through Thy kindness, O the Most Merciful.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
