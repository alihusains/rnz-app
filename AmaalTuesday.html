TUESDAY

Tuesday has been assigned to Imam Zainul Aabedin (a.s), Imam Muhammad Baqir (a.s) & Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s).
Tuesday Imam Zainul Aabedin (a.s), Imam Muhammad Baqir (a.s) aur Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) se mansub hai.

NAQSH…
One who looks at this Naqsh on Tuesday will remain under divine protection, safe from calamities, ill luck, sins to be forgiven and legitimate desires to be fulfilled.

Jo shakhs is Naqsh ko baroz Tuesday dekhe to woh aafat o bala se Khuda ki hifaazat mein rahega, haadsoa aur bad-bakhti se mehfooz, gunaho’n ki bakhshish aur uski jaayez haajaat qabool hogi.

ZIKR…
Recite 1000 times…
1000 Martaba padhe…

یَااَرْحَمَ الرّٰحَمِیْنَ

YA AR-HAMAR RAAHEMEEN
O! The most merciful, out of all those woh provide mercy.
Ay Rahem karne waalo’n mein sabse ziyada Rahem karne waale.

NAMAZ…
Recite 6-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite last 2 Verses of Surah Baqara (Chp.2) and Surah Zilzaal (Chp.99) 1 time each.
6 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 1 martaba Surah Baqara (Chp.2) ki aakhri 2 aayat aur Surah Zilzaal (Chp.99) padhe.

DUA…
Recite Tuesday Dua.
Tuesday ki Dua padhe.

ZIYARAT…
Recite Tuesday Ziyarat.
Tuesday ki Ziyarat padhe.
