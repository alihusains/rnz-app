TAUBA | 128-129

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

لَقَدْ جَاۗءَكُمْ رَسُوْلٌ مِّنْ اَنْفُسِكُمْ عَزِيْزٌ عَلَيْهِ مَا عَنِتُّمْ حَرِيْصٌ عَلَيْكُمْ بِالْمُؤْمِنِيْنَ رَءُوْفٌ رَّحِيْمٌ۔ فَاِنْ تَوَلَّوْا فَقُلْ حَسْبِيَ اللهُ لَا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا هُوَط عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ وَهُوَ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيْمِ۔

ENGLISH… Certainly an Apostle has come to you from among yourselves; grievous to him is your falling into distress, excessively solicitous respecting you; to the believers (he is) compassionate, merciful. But if they turn back, say: Allah is sufficient for me, there is no god but He; on Him do I rely, and He is the Lord of mighty power.

ROMAN URDU… Yaqeenan tumhaare paas woh Paighambar aaya hai jo tum hi mein se hai aur us par tumhaari har museebat shaaq (na-gawaar) hoti hai woh tumhaari hidaayat ke baare mein hirs (raghbat) rakhta hai aur momenin ke haal par shafeeq (daya karne waala) aur maher-baan hai. Ab iske baad bhi yeh log mooh pher le to kahe dijiye keh Khuda mere liye kaafi hai, uske siwa koi Khuda nahi, mera etemaad usi par hai aur wohi arsh-e-aazam ka Parwardigaar hai.
