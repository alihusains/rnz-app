بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

الۗمّۗﯜ1ﯛج اَحَسِبَ النَّاسُ اَنْ يُّتْرَكُوْٓا اَنْ يَّقُوْلُوْٓا اٰمَنَّا وَهُمْ لَا يُفْتَنُوْنَﯜ2ﯛ وَلَقَدْ فَتَنَّا الَّذِيْنَ مِنْ قَبْلِهِمْ فَلَيَعْلَمَنَّ اللهُ الَّذِيْنَ صَدَقُوْا وَلَيَعْلَمَنَّ الْكٰذِبِيْنَﯜ3ﯛ اَمْ حَسِبَ الَّذِيْنَ يَعْمَلُوْنَ السَّيِّاٰتِ اَنْ يَّسْبِقُوْنَاط سَاۗءَ مَا يَحْكُمُوْنَﯜ4ﯛ مَنْ كَانَ يَرْجُوْ لِقَاۗءَ اللهِ فَاِنَّ اَجَلَ اللهِ لَاٰتٍط وَهُوَ السَّمِيْعُ الْعَلِيْمُﯜ5ﯛ وَمَنْ جَاهَدَ فَاِنَّمَا يُجَاهِدُ لِنَفْسِهٖط اِنَّ اللهَ لَغَنِيٌّ عَنِ الْعٰلَمِيْنَﯜ6ﯛ وَالَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا وَعَمِلُوا الصّٰلِحٰتِ لَنُكَفِّرَنَّ عَنْهُمْ سَيِّاٰتِهِمْ وَلَنَجْزِيَنَّهُمْ اَحْسَنَ الَّذِيْ كَانُوْا يَعْمَلُوْنَﯜ7ﯛ وَوَصَّيْنَا الْاِنْسَانَ بِوَالِدَيْهِ حُسْنًاط وَاِنْ جَاهَدٰكَ لِتُشْرِكَ بِيْ مَا لَيْسَ لَكَ بِهِ عِلْمٌ فَلَا تُطِعْهُمَاط اِلَيَّ مَرْجِعُكُمْ فَاُنَبِّئُكُمْ بِمَا كُنْتُمْ تَعْمَلُوْنَﯜ8ﯛ وَالَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا وَعَمِلُوا الصّٰلِحٰتِ لَنُدْخِلَنَّهُمْ فِي الصّٰلِحِيْنَﯜ9ﯛ وَمِنَ النَّاسِ مَنْ يَّقُوْلُ اٰمَنَّا بِاللهِ فَاِذَآ اُوْذِيَ فِي اللهِ جَعَلَ فِتْنَةَ النَّاسِ كَعَذَابِ اللهِط وَلَئِنْ جَاۗءَ نَصْرٌ مِّنْ رَّبِّكَ لَيَقُوْلُنَّ اِنَّا كُنَّا مَعَكُمْط اَوَلَيْسَ اللهُ بِاَعْلَمَ بِمَا فِيْ صُدُوْرِ الْعٰلَمِيْنَﯜ10ﯛ وَلَيَعْلَمَنَّ اللهُ الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا وَلَيَعْلَمَنَّ الْمُنٰفِقِيْنَﯜ11ﯛ وَقَالَ الَّذِيْنَ كَفَرُوْا لِلَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوا اتَّبِعُوْا سَبِيْلَنَا وَلْنَحْمِلْ خَطٰيٰكُمْط وَمَا هُمْ بِحٰمِلِيْنَ مِنْ خَطٰيٰهُمْ مِّنْ شَيْءٍط اِنَّهُمْ لَكٰذِبُوْنَﯜ12ﯛ وَلَيَحْمِلُنَّ اَثْقَالَهُمْ وَاَثْقَالًا مَّعَ اَثْقَالِهِمْز وَلَيُسْئَلُنَّ يَوْمَ الْقِيٰمَةِ عَمَّا كَانُوْا يَفْتَرُوْنَﯜ13ﯛع وَلَقَدْ اَرْسَلْنَا نُوْحًا اِلٰى قَوْمِهٖ فَلَبِثَ فِيْهِمْ اَلْفَ سَنَةٍ اِلَّا خَمْسِيْنَ عَامًاط فَاَخَذَهُمُ الطُّوْفَانُ وَهُمْ ظٰلِمُوْنَﯜ14ﯛ فَاَنْجَيْنٰهُ وَاَصْحٰبَ السَّفِيْنَةِ وَجَعَلْنٰهَآ اٰيَةً لِّلْعٰلَمِيْنَﯜ15ﯛ وَاِبْرٰهِيْمَ اِذْ قَالَ لِقَوْمِهِ اعْبُدُوا اللهَ وَاتَّقُوْهُط ذٰلِكُمْ خَيْرٌ لَّكُمْ اِنْ كُنْتُمْ تَعْلَمُوْنَﯜ16ﯛ اِنَّمَا تَعْبُدُوْنَ مِنْ دُوْنِ اللهِ اَوْثَانًا وَّتَخْلُقُوْنَ اِفْكًاط اِنَّ الَّذِيْنَ تَعْبُدُوْنَ مِنْ دُوْنِ اللهِ لَا يَمْلِكُوْنَ لَكُمْ رِزْقًا فَابْتَغُوْا عِنْدَ اللهِ الرِّزْقَ وَاعْبُدُوْهُ وَاشْكُرُوْا لَهٗط اِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُوْنَﯜ17ﯛ وَاِنْ تُكَذِّبُوْا فَقَدْ كَذَّبَ اُمَمٌ مِّنْ قَبْلِكُمْط وَمَا عَلَى الرَّسُوْلِ اِلَّا الْبَلٰغُ الْمُبِيْنُﯜ18ﯛ اَوَلَمْ يَرَوْا كَيْفَ يُبْدِیُٔ اللهُ الْخَلْقَ ثُمَّ يُعِيْدُهٗط اِنَّ ذٰلِكَ عَلَى اللهِ يَسِيْرٌﯜ19ﯛ قُلْ سِيْرُوْا فِي الْاَرْضِ فَانْظُرُوْا كَيْفَ بَدَاَ الْخَلْقَ ثُمَّ اللهُ يُنْشِئُ النَّشْاَةَ الْاٰخِرَةَط اِنَّ اللهَ عَلٰى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيْرٌﯜ20ﯛ يُعَذِّبُ مَنْ يَّشَاۗءُ وَيَرْحَمُ مَنْ يَّشَاۗءُج وَاِلَيْهِ تُقْلَبُوْنَﯜ21ﯛ وَمَآ اَنْتُمْ بِمُعْجِزِيْنَ فِي الْاَرْضِ وَلَا فِي السَّمَاۗءِز وَمَا لَكُمْ مِّنْ دُوْنِ اللهِ مِنْ وَّلِيٍّ وَّلَا نَصِيْرٍﯜ22ﯛع وَالَّذِيْنَ كَفَرُوْا بِاٰيٰتِ اللهِ وَلِقَاۗئِهٖٓ اُوْلٰۗئِكَ يَئِسُوْا مِنْ رَّحْمَتِيْ وَاُوْلٰۗئِكَ لَهُمْ عَذَابٌ اَلِيْمٌﯜ23ﯛ فَمَا كَانَ جَوَابَ قَوْمِهٖٓ اِلَّآ اَنْ قَالُوا اقْتُلُوْهُ اَوْ حَرِّقُوْهُ فَاَنْجٰهُ اللهُ مِنَ النَّارِط اِنَّ فِيْ ذٰلِكَ لَاٰيٰتٍ لِّقَوْمٍ يُّؤْمِنُوْنَﯜ24ﯛ وَقَالَ اِنَّمَا اتَّخَذْتُمْ مِّنْ دُوْنِ اللهِ اَوْثَانًالا مَّوَدَّةَ بَيْنِكُمْ فِي الْحَيٰوةِ الدُّنْيَاج ثُمَّ يَوْمَ الْقِيٰمَةِ يَكْفُرُ بَعْضُكُمْ بِبَعْضٍ وَّيَلْعَنُ بَعْضُكُمْ بَعْضًاز وَّمَاْوٰكُمُ النَّارُ وَمَا لَكُمْ مِّنْ نٰصِرِيْنَﯜ25ﯛ ڎ فَاٰمَنَ لَهٗ لُوْطٌم وَقَالَ اِنِّيْ مُهَاجِرٌ اِلٰى رَبِّيْط اِنَّهٗ هُوَ الْعَزِيْزُ الْحَكِيْمُﯜ26ﯛ وَوَهَبْنَا لَهٗٓ اِسْحٰقَ وَيَعْقُوْبَ وَجَعَلْنَا فِيْ ذُرِّيَّتِهِ النُّبُوَّةَ وَالْكِتٰبَ وَاٰتَيْنٰهُ اَجْرَهٗ فِي الدُّنْيَاج وَاِنَّهٗ فِي الْاٰخِرَةِ لَمِنَ الصّٰلِحِيْنَﯜ27ﯛ وَلُوْطًا اِذْ قَالَ لِقَوْمِهٖٓ اِنَّكُمْ لَتَاْتُوْنَ الْفَاحِشَةَز مَا سَبَقَكُمْ بِهَا مِنْ اَحَدٍ مِّنَ الْعٰلَمِيْنَﯜ28ﯛ اَئِنَّكُمْ لَتَاْتُوْنَ الرِّجَالَ وَتَقْطَعُوْنَ السَّبِيْلَ ڏ وَتَاْتُوْنَ فِيْ نَادِيْكُمُ الْمُنْكَرَط فَمَا كَانَ جَوَابَ قَوْمِهٖٓ اِلَّآ اَنْ قَالُوا ائْتِنَا بِعَذَابِ اللهِ اِنْ كُنْتَ مِنَ الصّٰدِقِيْنَﯜ29ﯛ قَالَ رَبِّ انْصُرْنِيْ عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْمُفْسِدِيْنَﯜ30ﯛع وَلَمَّا جَاۗءَتْ رُسُلُنَآ اِبْرٰهِيْمَ بِالْبُشْرٰىلا قَالُوْٓا اِنَّا مُهْلِكُوْٓ اَهْلِ هٰذِهِ الْقَرْيَةِج اِنَّ اَهْلَهَا كَانُوْا ظٰلِمِيْنَﯜ31ﯛ ښ قَالَ اِنَّ فِيْهَا لُوْطًاط قَالُوْا نَحْنُ اَعْلَمُ بِمَنْ فِيْهَاز لَنُنَجِّيَنَّهٗ وَاَهْلَهٗٓ اِلَّا امْرَاَتَهٗز كَانَتْ مِنَ الْغٰبِرِيْنَﯜ32ﯛ وَلَمَّآ اَنْ جَاۗءَتْ رُسُلُنَا لُوْطًا سِيْۗءَ بِهِمْ وَضَاقَ بِهِمْ ذَرْعًا وَّقَالُوْا لَا تَخَفْ وَلَا تَحْزَنْقف اِنَّا مُنَجُّوْكَ وَاَهْلَكَ اِلَّا امْرَاَتَكَ كَانَتْ مِنَ الْغٰبِرِيْنَﯜ33ﯛ اِنَّا مُنْزِلُوْنَ عَلٰٓى اَهْلِ هٰذِهِ الْقَرْيَةِ رِجْزًا مِّنَ السَّمَاۗءِ بِمَا كَانُوْا يَفْسُقُوْنَﯜ34ﯛ وَلَقَدْ تَّرَكْنَا مِنْهَآ اٰيَةًۢ بَيِّنَةً لِّقَوْمٍ يَّعْقِلُوْنَﯜ35ﯛ وَاِلٰى مَدْيَنَ اَخَاهُمْ شُعَيْبًالا فَقَالَ يٰقَوْمِ اعْبُدُوا اللهَ وَارْجُوا الْيَوْمَ الْاٰخِرَ وَلَا تَعْثَوْا فِي الْاَرْضِ مُفْسِدِيْنَﯜ36ﯛ فَكَذَّبُوْهُ فَاَخَذَتْهُمُ الرَّجْفَةُ فَاَصْبَحُوْا فِيْ دَارِهِمْ جٰثِمِيْنَﯜ37ﯛز وَعَادًا وَّثَمُوْدَ وَقَدْ تَّبَيَّنَ لَكُمْ مِّنْ مَّسٰكِنِهِمْوقف وَزَيَّنَ لَهُمُ الشَّيْطٰنُ اَعْمَالَهُمْ فَصَدَّهُمْ عَنِ السَّبِيْلِ وَكَانُوْا مُسْتَبْصِرِيْنَﯜ38ﯛلا وَقَارُوْنَ وَفِرْعَوْنَ وَهَامٰنَقف وَلَقَدْ جَاۗءَهُمْ مُّوْسٰى بِالْبَيِّنٰتِ فَاسْتَكْبَرُوْا فِي الْاَرْضِ وَمَا كَانُوْا سٰبِقِيْنَﯜ39ﯛ ښ فَكُلًّا اَخَذْنَا بِذَنْۢبِهٖج فَمِنْهُمْ مَّنْ اَرْسَلْنَا عَلَيْهِ حَاصِبًاج وَمِنْهُمْ مَّنْ اَخَذَتْهُ الصَّيْحَةُج وَمِنْهُمْ مَّنْ خَسَفْنَا بِهِ الْاَرْضَج وَمِنْهُمْ مَّنْ اَغْرَقْنَاج وَمَا كَانَ اللهُ لِيَظْلِمَهُمْ وَلٰكِنْ كَانُوْٓا اَنْفُسَهُمْ يَظْلِمُوْنَﯜ40ﯛ مَثَلُ الَّذِيْنَ اتَّخَذُوْا مِنْ دُوْنِ اللهِ اَوْلِيَاۗءَ كَمَثَلِ الْعَنْكَبُوْتِ ښ اِتَّخَذَتْ بَيْتًاط وَاِنَّ اَوْهَنَ الْبُيُوْتِ لَبَيْتُ الْعَنْكَبُوْتِم لَوْ كَانُوْا يَعْلَمُوْنَﯜ41ﯛ اِنَّ اللهَ يَعْلَمُ مَا يَدْعُوْنَ مِنْ دُوْنِهٖ مِنْ شَيْءٍط وَهُوَ الْعَزِيْزُ الْحَكِيْمُﯜ42ﯛ وَتِلْكَ الْاَمْثَالُ نَضْرِبُهَا لِلنَّاسِج وَمَا يَعْقِلُهَآ اِلَّا الْعٰلِمُوْنَﯜ43ﯛ خَلَقَ اللهُ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضَ بِالْحَقِّط اِنَّ فِيْ ذٰلِكَ لَاٰيَةً لِّلْمُؤْمِنِيْنَﯜ44ﯛع اُتْلُ مَآ اُوْحِيَ اِلَيْكَ مِنَ الْكِتٰبِ وَاَقِمِ الصَّلٰوةَط اِنَّ الصَّلٰوةَ تَنْهٰى عَنِ الْفَحْشَاۗءِ وَالْمُنْكَرِط وَلَذِكْرُ اللهِ اَكْبَرُط وَاللهُ يَعْلَمُ مَا تَصْنَعُوْنَﯜ45ﯛ وَلَا تُجَادِلُوْٓا اَهْلَ الْكِتٰبِ اِلَّا بِالَّتِيْ هِيَ اَحْسَنُ ڰ اِلَّا الَّذِيْنَ ظَلَمُوْا مِنْهُمْ وَقُوْلُوْٓا اٰمَنَّا بِالَّذِيْٓ اُنْزِلَ اِلَيْنَا وَاُنْزِلَ اِلَيْكُمْ وَاِلٰهُنَا وَاِلٰهُكُمْ وَاحِدٌ وَّنَحْنُ لَهٗ مُسْلِمُوْنَﯜ46ﯛ وَكَذٰلِكَ اَنْزَلْنَآ اِلَيْكَ الْكِتٰبَط فَالَّذِيْنَ اٰتَيْنٰهُمُ الْكِتٰبَ يُؤْمِنُوْنَ بِهٖج وَمِنْ هٰٓؤُلَاۗءِ مَنْ يُّؤْمِنُ بِهٖط وَمَا يَجْحَدُ بِاٰيٰتِنَآ اِلَّا الْكٰفِرُوْنَﯜ47ﯛ وَمَا كُنْتَ تَتْلُوْا مِنْ قَبْلِهٖ مِنْ كِتٰبٍ وَّلَا تَخُطُّهٗ بِيَمِيْنِكَ اِذًا لَّارْتَابَ الْمُبْطِلُوْنَﯜ48ﯛ بَلْ هُوَ اٰيٰتٌۢ بَيِّنٰتٌ فِيْ صُدُوْرِ الَّذِيْنَ اُوْتُوا الْعِلْمَط وَمَا يَجْحَدُ بِاٰيٰتِنَآ اِلَّا الظّٰلِمُوْنَﯜ49ﯛ وَقَالُوْا لَوْلَٓا اُنْزِلَ عَلَيْهِ اٰيٰتٌ مِّنْ رَّبِّهٖط قُلْ اِنَّمَا الْاٰيٰتُ عِنْدَ اللهِط وَاِنَّمَآ اَنَا نَذِيْرٌ مُّبِيْنٌﯜ50ﯛ اَوَلَمْ يَكْفِهِمْ اَنَّآ اَنْزَلْنَا عَلَيْكَ الْكِتٰبَ يُتْلٰى عَلَيْهِمْط اِنَّ فِيْ ذٰلِكَ لَرَحْمَةً وَّذِكْرٰى لِقَوْمٍ يُّؤْمِنُوْنَﯜ51ﯛع قُلْ كَفٰى بِاللهِ بَيْنِيْ وَبَيْنَكُمْ شَهِيْدًاج يَعْلَمُ مَا فِي السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضِط وَالَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا بِالْبَاطِلِ وَكَفَرُوْا بِاللهِلا اُوْلٰۗئِكَ هُمُ الْخٰسِرُوْنَﯜ52ﯛ وَيَسْتَعْجِلُوْنَكَ بِالْعَذَابِط وَلَوْلَٓا اَجَلٌ مُّسَمًّى لَّجَاۗءَهُمُ الْعَذَابُط وَلَيَاْتِيَنَّهُمْ بَغْتَةً وَّهُمْ لَا يَشْعُرُوْنَﯜ53ﯛ يَسْتَعْجِلُوْنَكَ بِالْعَذَابِط وَاِنَّ جَهَنَّمَ لَمُحِيْطَةٌۢ بِالْكٰفِرِيْنَﯜ54ﯛ يَوْمَ يَغْشٰهُمُ الْعَذَابُ مِنْ فَوْقِهِمْ وَمِنْ تَحْتِ اَرْجُلِهِمْ وَيَقُوْلُ ذُوْقُوْا مَا كُنْتُمْ تَعْمَلُوْنَﯜ55ﯛ يٰعِبَادِيَ الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْٓا اِنَّ اَرْضِيْ وَاسِعَةٌ فَاِيَّايَ فَاعْبُدُوْنِﯜ56ﯛ كُلُّ نَفْسٍ ذَاۗئِقَةُ الْمَوْتِقف ثُمَّ اِلَيْنَا تُرْجَعُوْنَﯜ57ﯛ وَالَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا وَعَمِلُوا الصّٰلِحٰتِ لَنُبَوِّئَنَّهُمْ مِّنَ الْجَنَّةِ غُرَفًا تَجْرِيْ مِنْ تَحْتِهَا الْاَنْهٰرُ خٰلِدِيْنَ فِيْهَاط نِعْمَ اَجْرُ الْعٰمِلِيْنَﯜ58ﯛ ڰ الَّذِيْنَ صَبَرُوْا وَعَلٰى رَبِّهِمْ يَتَوَكَّلُوْنَﯜ59ﯛ وَكَاَيِّنْ مِّنْ دَاۗبَّةٍ لَّا تَحْمِلُ رِزْقَهَا ڰ اللهُ يَرْزُقُهَا وَاِيَّاكُمْ ڮ وَهُوَ السَّمِيْعُ الْعَلِيْمُﯜ60ﯛ وَلَئِنْ سَاَلْتَهُمْ مَّنْ خَلَقَ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضَ وَسَخَّرَ الشَّمْسَ وَالْقَمَرَ لَيَقُوْلُنَّ اللهُج فَاَنّٰى يُؤْفَكُوْنَﯜ61ﯛ اَللهُ يَبْسُطُ الرِّزْقَ لِمَنْ يَّشَاۗءُ مِنْ عِبَادِهٖ وَيَقْدِرُ لَهٗط اِنَّ اللهَ بِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ عَلِيْمٌﯜ62ﯛ وَلَئِنْ سَاَلْتَهُمْ مَّنْ نَّزَّلَ مِنَ السَّمَاۗءِ مَاۗءً فَاَحْيَا بِهِ الْاَرْضَ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ مَوْتِهَا لَيَقُوْلُنَّ اللهُط قُلِ الْحَمْدُ لِلهِط بَلْ اَكْثَرُهُمْ لَا يَعْقِلُوْنَﯜ63ﯛع وَمَا هٰذِهِ الْحَيٰوةُ الدُّنْيَآ اِلَّا لَهْوٌ وَّلَعِبٌط وَاِنَّ الدَّارَ الْاٰخِرَةَ لَهِيَ الْحَيَوَانُم لَوْ كَانُوْا يَعْلَمُوْنَﯜ64ﯛ فَاِذَا رَكِبُوْا فِي الْفُلْكِ دَعَوُا اللهَ مُخْلِصِيْنَ لَهُ الدِّيْنَ ڬ فَلَمَّا نَجّٰهُمْ اِلَى الْبَرِّ اِذَا هُمْ يُشْرِكُوْنَﯜ65ﯛلا لِيَكْفُرُوْا بِمَآ اٰتَيْنٰهُمْ ڌ وَلِيَتَمَتَّعُوْاوقفۃ فَسَوْفَ يَعْلَمُوْنَﯜ66ﯛ اَوَلَمْ يَرَوْا اَنَّا جَعَلْنَا حَرَمًا اٰمِنًا وَّيُتَخَطَّفُ النَّاسُ مِنْ حَوْلِهِمْط اَفَبِالْبَاطِلِ يُؤْمِنُوْنَ وَبِنِعْمَةِ اللهِ يَكْفُرُوْنَﯜ67ﯛ وَمَنْ اَظْلَمُ مِمَّنِ افْتَرٰى عَلَى اللهِ كَذِبًا اَوْ كَذَّبَ بِالْحَقِّ لَمَّا جَاۗءَهٗط اَلَيْسَ فِيْ جَهَنَّمَ مَثْوًى لِّلْكٰفِرِيْنَﯜ68ﯛ وَالَّذِيْنَ جَاهَدُوْا فِيْنَا لَنَهْدِيَنَّهُمْ سُبُلَنَاط وَاِنَّ اللهَ لَمَعَ الْمُحْسِنِيْنَﯜ69ﯛع

صَدَقَ اللهُ الْعَلِیُّ الْعَظِیْمُ

29 | ANKABOOT | 69

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Aleef laam meem.

2. Kya logo ne yeh khayaal kar rakkha hai keh woh sirf is baat par chhod diye jaayege keh woh yeh kahe de keh ham imaan le aaye hai aur unka imtehaan nahi hoga.

3. Be-shak Ham ne un se pehle waalo ka bhi imtehaan liya hai aur Allah to baher-haal yeh jaanna chahta hai keh un mein kaun log sachche hai aur kaun joothay hai.

4. Kya buraayi karne waalo ka khayaal yeh hai keh Ham se agay nikal jaayege yeh bohot galat faisla kar rahe hai.

5. Jo bhi Allah ki mulaqaat ki umeed rakhta hai usey maaloom rahe keh woh muddat yaqeenan aane waali hai aur woh Khuda Samee’a bhi hai aur Aleem bhi hai.

6. Aur jisne bhi jehaad kiya hai us ne apne liye jehaad kiya hai aur Allah to saare aalameen se be-neyaaz hai.

7. Aur jo log imaan laaye aur unho ne nek aamaal kiye Ham yaqeenan unke gunaho ki parda-poshi (dhanka) karege aur unhe unke aamaal ki behtareen jaza ataa karege.

8. Aur Ham ne insaan ko maa-baap ke saath nek bartaaw karne ki nasihat ki hai aur bataaya hai keh agar woh kisi aisi shaiy ko mera shareek banaane par majboor kare jiska tumhe ilm nahi hai to khabar-daar! Unki ita’at na karna keh tum sab ki baaz-gasht Meri hi taraf hai phir Mai bataaunga keh tum log kya kar rahe thay.

9. Aur jin logo ne imaan ikhtiyaar kiya aur nek aamaal kiye Ham yaqeenan unhe nek bando mein shaamil kar lege.

10. Aur baaz log aise hai jo kehte hai keh ham Allah par imaan le aaye iske baad jab raahe Khuda mein koi takleef huwi to logo ke fitna ko azaab-e-Khuda jaisa qaraar de diya haala keh aap ke Parwardigaar ki taraf se koi madad aa jaati to fauran kahe dete keh ham to aap hi ke saath thay to kya Khuda un baato se ba-khabar nahi hai jo aalameen ke dilo mein hai.

11. Aur woh yaqeenan unhe bhi jaanna chahta hai jo sahebaan-e-imaan hai aur unhe bhi jaanna chahta hai jo munafiq hai.

12. Aur yeh kuffaar imaan laane waalo se kehte hai keh tum hamare raasteka itteba kar lo ham tumhaare gunaho ke zimme-daar hai haala keh woh kisi ke gunaho ka bojh uthaane waale nahi hai aur sara-sar joothay hai.

13. Aur unhe ek din apna bojh bhi uthaana hi padega aur uske saath un logo ka bhi aur roz-e-qayamat un se un baato ke baare mein sawaal bhi kiya jaayega jin ka yeh iftara (jooth bayaan) kiya karte thay.

14. Aur Ham ne Nooh alayhis salaam ko unki qaum ki taraf bheja aur woh unke darmiyaan pachaas saal kam ek hazaar saal (950 years) rahe phir qaum ko tufaan ne apni giraft mein le liya ke woh log zaalim thay.

15. Phir Ham ne Nooh alayhis salaam ko aur unke saath kashti waalo ko najaat de di aur usey aalameen ke liye ek nishaani qaraar de diya.

16. Aur Ibrahim ko yaad karo jab unho ne apni qaum se kaha keh Allah ki ibaadat karo aur us se daro keh isi mein tumhaare liye behtari hai agar tum kuch jaante ho.

17. Tum Khuda ko chhod kar sirf booto ki parastish karte ho aur apne paas se jooth ghadte ho. Yaad rakkho keh Khuda ke alaawa jin ki bhi parastish karte ho woh tumhaare rizq ke maalik nahi hairizq Khuda ke paas talaash karo aur usi ki ibaadat karo usi ka shukriya adaa karo keh tum sab usi ki baargah mein waapas le jaaye jaawoge.

18. Aur agar tum takzeeb karoge to tum se pehle bohot si qaume yeh kaam kar chuki hai aur Rasool ki zimme-daari to sirf waazeh taur par paighaam ka pohoncha dena hai.

19. Kya un logo ne nahi dekha keh Khuda kis tarah makhloqaat ko ijaad karta hai aur phir dobaara waapas le jaata hai yeh sab Allah ke liye bohot aasaan hai.

20. Aap kahe dijiye keh tum log zameen mein sair karo aur dekho keh Khuda ne kis tarah khilqat ka aaghaz kiya hai? Iske baad wohi aakherat mein dobaara ijaad karega be-shak wohi har shaiy par qudrat rakhne waala hai.

21. Woh jis par chahta hai azaab karta hai aur jis par chahta hai rahem karta hai aur tum sab usi ki taraf paltaaye jaawoge.

22. Aur tum zameen ya aasmaan mein usey aajiz kar sakne waale nahi ho aur uske alaawa tumhaara koi sarparast ya madad-gaar bhi nahi hai.

23. Aur jo log Khuda ki nishaaniya aur uski mulaqaat ka inkaar karte hai woh meri rehmat se maayoos hai aur unke liye dard-naak azaab hai.

24. To unki qaum ka jawaab iske alaawa kuch na tha keh inhe qatl kar do ya aag mein jala do. To Parwardigaar ne unhe us aag se najaat diladi be-shak is mein bhi sahebaan-e-imaan ke liye bohot si nishaaniya paayi jaati hai.

25. Aur Ibrahim ne kaha keh tum ne sirf zindagaani-e-duniya ki mohabbato ko barqarar rakhne ke liye Khuda ko chhod kar booto ko ikhtiyaar kar liya hai iske baad roz-e-qayamat tum mein se har ek dusre ka inkaar karega aur ek dusre par laanat karega jab-keh tum sab ka anjaam Jahannam hoga aur tumhaara koi madad-gaar na hoga.

26. Phir Loot, Ibrahim par imaan le aaye aur unho ne kaha keh mai apne rab ki taraf hijrat kar raha hu keh wohi saheb-e-izzat aur saheb-e-hikmat hai.

27. Aur Ham ne unhe Ishaaq aur Yaqoob jaisi aulaad ataa ki aur phir unki zurriyat mein kitaab aur nabuwat qaraar di aur unhe duniya mein bhi unka ajr ataa kiya aur aakherat mein bhi unka shumaar nek kirdaar logo mein hoga.

28. Aur phir Lut ko yaad karo jab unho ne apni qaum se kaha keh tum log aisi bad-kaari kar rahe ho jo saare aalam mein tum se pehle kabhi kisi ne bhi nahi ki hai.

29. Tum log mardo se jinsi taalluqaat paiyda kar rahe ho aur raaste qata’a kar rahe ho aur apni mehfilo mein buraaiyo ko anjaam de rahe ho. To unki qaum ke paas iske alaawa koi jawaab na tha keh agar tum saadeqeen mein se ho to woh azaab-e-ilaahi le aawo jiski khabar de rahe ho.

30. To Lut ne kaha Parwardigaar to is fasaad phelaane waali qaum ke muqaabl mein meri madad farma.

31. Aur jab hamare numainde farishte Ibrahim ke paas bashaarat le kar aaye aur unho ne yeh khabar soonaayi keh ham is basti waalo ko halaak karna chahte hai keh is basti ke log bade zaalim hai.

32. To Ibrahim ne kaha keh is mein to Lut bhi hai unho ne jawaab diya keh ham sab se ba-khabar hai ham unhe aur unke ghar waalo ko najaat de dege alaawa unki zauja ke keh woh peechhe rahe jaane waalo mein hai.

33. Phir jab hamare farishte Lut ke paas aaye to woh pareshaan ho gaye aur unki mehmaani ki taraf se dil-tangh hone lagey un logo ne kaha keh aap darey nahi aur pareshaan na ho ham aap ko aur aap ke ghar waalo ko bacha lege sirf aap ki zauja ko nahi keh woh peechhe rahe jaane waalo mein ho gayi hai.

34. Ham is basti par aasmaan se azaab naazil karne waale hai keh yeh log badi bad-kaari kar rahe hai.

35. Aur Ham ne is basti mein se sahebaan-e-aql-o-hosh ke liye khuli huwi nishaani baaqi rakkhi hai.

36. Aur Ham ne Madyan ki taraf unke bhai Shoaib ko bheja to unho nekaha keh logo Allah ki ibaadat karo aur roze aakherat se ummeede waabasta karo aur khabar-daar! Zameen mein fasaad na phelaate phiro.

37. To un logo ne unhe joothla diya aur nateeje mein unhe ek zalzale ne apni giraft mein le liya aur woh apne gharo mein oundhe zaano ke bal pade rahe.

38. Aur Aad-o-Samood ko yaad karo keh tum logo ke liye unke ghar bhi sare raah waazeh ho chuke hai aur shaitaan ne unke liye unke aamaal ko aaraasta kar diya tha aur unhe raastese rok diya tha haala keh woh log bohot hooshiyar thay.

39. Aur Qaroon, Firon-o-Haamaan ko bhi yaad dilaao jin ke paas Moosa khuli huwi nishaaniya le kar aaye to un logo ne zameen mein istakbaar (takabbur) se kaam liya haala keh woh ham se aaghe jaane waale nahi thay.

40. Phir Ham ne har ek ko uske gunah mein ghiraf-taar kar liya kisi par aasmaan se pattharo ki baarish kar di kisi ko ek aasmaani cheekh ne pakad liya aur kisi ko zameen mein dhansa diya aur kisi ko paani mein gharq kar (doobho) diya aur Allah kisi par zulm karne waala nahi hai bal-keh yeh log khud apne nafs par zulm kar rahe hai.

41. Aur jin logo ne Khuda ko chhod kar dusre sarparast bana liye hai unki misaal makdi (spider) jaisi hai ke us ne ghar to bana liya lekin sab se kamzor ghar makdi ka ghar hota hai agar un logo ke paas ilm-o-idraak ho.

42. Be-shak Allah khoob jaanta hai keh usey chhod kar yeh log kise pukaar rahe hai aur woh badi izzat waala aur saheb-e-hikmat hai.

43. Aur yeh misaale ham tamaam aalam-e-insaaniyat ke liye bayaan kar rahe hai lekin inhe sahebaan-e-ilm ke alaawa koi nahi samajh sakta hai.

44. Allah ne aasmaan aur zameen ko haq ke saath paiyda kiya hai aur is mein sahebaan-e-imaan ke liye uski qudrat ki nishaani paayi jaati hai.

45. Ab jiskitaab ki aap ki taraf wahi (revelation) ki gayi hai usey padh kar soonaaye aur namaaz qaayem kare ke namaaz har buraayi aur bad-kaari se rokne waali hai aur Allah ka zikr bohot badi shaiy hai aur Allah tumhaare kaar-o-baar se khoob ba-khabar hai.

46. Aur Ahle kitaab se munaazra na karo magar is andaaz se jo behtareen andaaz hai alaawa un se jo un mein se zaalim hai aur yeh kaho keh ham us par imaan laaye hai jo hamari aur tumhaari dono ki taraf naazil huwa hai aur hamara aur tumhaara Khuda ek hi hai aur ham sab usi ke ita’at guzaar hai.

47. Aur isi tarah Ham ne aap ki taraf kitaab naazil ki to jin logo ko Ham ne kitaab di hai woh is kitaab par imaan rakhte hai aur un mein se bhi baaz log imaan rakhte hai aur hamari aayaat ka inkaar kaafiro ke alaawa koi nahi karta hai.

48. Aur ay Paighambar aap is Qur’an se pehle na koi kitaab padhte thay aur na apne haath se kuch likhte thay warna yeh Ahle baatil shubh mein pad jaate.

49. Dar-haqeeqat yeh Qur’an chand khuli huwi aayato ka naam hai jo unke seeno mein mehfooz hai jinhe ilm diya gaya hai aur hamari aayaat ka inkaar zaalimo ke alaawa koi nahi karta hai.

50. Aur yeh log kehte hai keh aakhir un par Parwardigaar ki taraf se aayaat kyu’n nahi naazil hoti hai to aap kahe dijiye keh aayaat sab Allah ke paas hai aur mai to sirf waazeh taur par azaab-e-ilaahi se daraane waala hu.

51. Kya unke liye yeh kaafi nahi hai keh Ham ne aap par kitaab naazil ki hai jiski unke saamne tilaawat ki jaati hai aur yaqeenan is mein rehmat aur imaandaar qaum ke liye yaad-dahaani ka saamaan maujood hai.

52. Aap kahe dijiye keh hamare aur tumhaare darmiyaan gawahi ke liye Khuda kaafi hai jo aasmaan aur zameen ki har cheez se ba-khabar hai aur jo log baatil par imaan rakhte hai aur Allah ka inkaar karte hai woh yaqeenan khasaara uthaane waale log hai.

53. Aur yeh log azaab ki jaldi kar rahe hai haala keh agar uska waqt mo’ayyan na hota to ab tak azaab aachuka hota aur woh achaanak hi aayega jab inhe shaoor bhi na hoga.

54. Yeh azaab ki jaldi kar rahe hai haala keh Jahannam yaqeenan kaafiro ko apne ghere mein lene waala hai.

55. Jis din azaab inhe upar se aur neeche se dhaank lega aur kahega keh ab apne aamaal ka maza chakkho.

56. Mere imaandaar bando! Meri zameen bohot wasee hai lehaaza meri hi ibaadat karo.

57. Har nafs maut ka maza chakhne waala hai iske baad tum sab hamari baargah mein palta kar laaye jaawoge.

58. Aur jo log imaan laaye hai aur unho ne nek aamaal kiye hai unhe ham jannat ke jharoko (kamro) mein jagah dege jin ke neeche nehre jaari hogi aur woh hamesha un hi mein rahege keh yeh un amal karne waalo ka behtareen ajr hai.

59. Jin logo ne sabr kiya hai aur apne Parwardigaar par bharosa karte hai.

60. Aur zameen par chalne waale bohot se aise bhi hai jo apni rozi ka bojh nahi utha sakte hai lekin Khuda unhe aur tumhe sab ko rizq de raha hai woh sab ki soonne waala aur sab ke haalaat ka jaanne waala hai.

61. Aur agar aap un se poochhenge ke aasmaan-o-zameen ko kis ne paiyda kiya hai aur aftaab-o-mehtaab ko kis ne musakhkhar (stable) kiya hai to fauran Kahenge keh Allah, to yeh kidhar baheke chale ja rahe hai.

62. Allah hi jiske rizq mein chahta hai wus’at paiyda kar deta hai aur jiske rizq ko chahta hai tangh kar deta hai woh har shaiy ka khoob jaanne waala hai.

63. Aur agar aap un se poochhenge kis ne aasmaan se paani barsaaya hai aur phir zameen ko murda hone ke baad zinda kya hai? To yeh Kahenge keh Allah hi hai to phir kahe dijiye keh saari hamd Allah ke liye hai aur unki aksariyat aql istemaal nahi kar rahi hai.

64. Aur yeh zindagaani-e-duniya ek khel-tamaashe ke siwa kuch nahi hai aur aakherat ka ghar hamesha ki zindagi ka markaz hai agar yeh log kuch jaante aur samajh-te ho.

65. Phir jab yeh log kashti mein sawaar hote hai to imaan-o-aqeedeh ke poore ikhlaas ke saath Khuda ko pukaarte hai phir jab woh najaat de kar khushki tak pohoncha deta hai to fauran shirk ikhtiyaar kar lete hai.

66. Ta-keh jo kuch Ham ne ataa kiya hai uska inkaar kar de aur duniya mein maze udaaye to an-qareeb unhe is ka anjaam maaloom ho jaayega.

67. Kya un logo ne nahi dekha keh Ham ne unke waaste ek mehfooz haram bana diya hai jiske chaaro taraf log uchak liye jaate hai to kya yeh baatil par imaan rakhte hai aur Allah ki nemat ka inkaar kar dete hai.

68. Aur us se bada zaalim kaun hai jo Allah ki taraf joothi baato ki nisbat de? Ya haq ke aajaane ke baad bhi uska inkaar kar de? To kya Jahannam mein kuffaar ka thikaana nahi hai?

69. Aur jin logo ne hamare haq mein jehaad kiya hai ham unhe apne raasto ki hidaayat karege aur yaqeenan Allah husne amal waalo ke saath hai.

29 | ANKABOOT | 69

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Alif Lam Mim.

2. Do men think that they will be left alone on saying, We believe, and not be tried?

3. And certainly We tried those before them, so Allah will certainly know those who are true and He will certainly know the liars.

4. Or do they who work evil think that they will escape Us? Evil is it that they judge!

5. Whoever hopes to meet Allah, the term appointed by Allah will then most surely come; and He is the Hearing, the Knowing.

6. And whoever strives hard, he strives only for his own soul; most surely Allah is Self-sufficient, above (need of) the worlds.

7. And (as for) those who believe and do good, We will most certainly do away with their evil deeds and We will most certainly reward them the best of what they did.

8. And We have enjoined on man goodness to his parents, and if they contend with you that you should associate (others) with Me, of which you have no knowledge, do not obey them, to Me is your return, so I will inform you of what you did.

9. And (as for) those who believe and do good, We will most surely cause them to enter among the good.

10. And among men is he who says: We believe in Allah; but when he is persecuted in (the way of) Allah he thinks the persecution of men to be as the chastisement of Allah; and if there come assistance from your Lord, they would most certainly say: Surely we were with you. What! is not Allah the best knower of what is in the breasts of mankind.

11. And most certainly Allah will know those who believe and most certainly He will know the hypocrites.

12. And those who disbelieve say to those who believe: Follow our path and we will bear your wrongs. And never shall they be the bearers of any of their wrongs; most surely they are liars.

13. And most certainly they shall carry their own burdens, and other burdens with their own burdens, and most certainly they shall be questioned on the resurrection day as to what they forged.

14. And certainly We sent Nuh to his people, so he remained among them a thousand years save fifty years. And the deluge overtook them, while they were unjust.

15. So We delivered him and the inmates of the ark, and made it a sign to the nations.

16. And (We sent) Ibrahim, when he said to his people: Serve Allah and be careful of (your duty to) Him; this is best for you, if you did but know:

17. You only worship idols besides Allah and you create a lie; surely they whom you serve besides Allah do not control for you any sustenance, therefore seek the sustenance from Allah and serve Him and be grateful to Him; to Him you shall be brought back.

18. And if you reject (the truth), nations before you did indeed reject (the truth); and nothing is incumbent on the apostle but a plain delivering (of the message).

19. What! do they not consider how Allah originates the creation, then reproduces it? Surely that is easy to Allah.

20. Say: Travel in the earth and see how He makes the first creation, then Allah creates the latter creation; surely Allah has power over all things.

21. He punishes whom He pleases and has mercy on whom He pleases, and to Him you shall be turned back.

22. And you shall not escape in the earth nor in the heaven, and you have neither a protector nor a helper besides Allah.

23. And (as to) those who disbelieve in the communications of Allah and His meeting, they have despaired of My mercy, and these it is that shall have a painful punishment.

24. So naught was the answer of his people except that they said: Slay him or burn him; then Allah delivered him from the fire; most surely there are signs in this for a people who believe.

25. And he said: You have only taken for yourselves idols besides Allah by way of friendship between you in this world’s life, then on the resurrection day some of you shall deny others, and some of you shall curse others, and your abode is the fire, and you shall not have any helpers.

26. And Lut believed in Him, and he said: I am fleeing to my Lord, surely He is the Mighty, the Wise.

27. And We granted him Ishaq and Yaqoub, and caused the prophethood and the book to remain in his seed, and We gave him his reward in this world, and in the hereafter he will most surely be among the good.

28. And (We sent) Lut when he said to his people: Most surely you are guilty of an indecency which none of the nations has ever done before you;

29. What! do you come to the males and commit robbery on the highway, and you commit evil deeds in your assemblies? But nothing was the answer of his people except that they said: Bring on us Allah’s punishment, if you are one of the truthful.

30. He said: My Lord! help me against the mischievous people.

31. And when Our messengers came to Ibrahim with the good news, they said: Surely we are going to destroy the people of this town, for its people are unjust.

32. He said: Surely in it is Lut. They said: We know well who is in it; we shall certainly deliver him and his followers, except his wife; she shall be of those who remain behind.

33. And when Our messengers came to Lut he was grieved on account of them, and he felt powerless (to protect) them; and they said: Fear not, nor grieve; surely we will deliver you and your followers, except your wife; she shall be of those who remain behind.

34. Surely We will cause to come down upon the people of this town a punishment from heaven, because they transgressed.

35. And certainly We have left a clear sign of it for a people who understand.

36. And to Madyan (We sent) their brother Shuaib, so he said: O my people! serve Allah and fear the latter day and do not act corruptly in the land, making mischief.

37. But they rejected him, so a severe earthquake overtook them, and they became motionless bodies in their abode.

38. And (We destroyed) Ad and Samood, and from their dwellings (this) is apparent to you indeed; and the Shaitan made their deeds fair-seeming to them, so he kept them back from the path, though they were endowed with intelligence and skill,

39. And (We destroyed) Qaroun and Firon and Haman; and certainly Musa came to them with clear arguments, but they behaved haughtily in the land; yet they could not outstrip (Us).

40. So each We punished for his sin; of them was he on whom We sent down a violent storm, and of them was he whom the rumbling overtook, and of them was he whom We made to be swallowed up by the earth, and of them was he whom We drowned; and it did not beseem Allah that He should be unjust to them, but they were unjust to their own souls.

41. The parable of those who take guardians besides Allah is as the parable of the spider that makes for itself a house; and most surely the frailest of the houses is the spider’s house— did they but know.

42. Surely Allah knows whatever thing they call upon besides Him; and He is the Mighty, the Wise.

43. And (as for) these examples, We set them forth for men, and none understand them but the learned.

44. Allah created the heavens and the earth with truth; most surely there is a sign in this for the believers.

45. Recite that which has been revealed to you of the Book and keep up prayer; surely prayer keeps (one) away from indecency and evil, and certainly the remembrance of Allah is the greatest, and Allah knows what you do.

46. And do not dispute with the followers of the Book except by what is best, except those of them who act unjustly, and say: We believe in that which has been revealed to us and revealed to you, and our God and your God is One, and to Him do we submit.

47. And thus have We revealed the Book to you. So those whom We have given the Book believe in it, and of these there are those who believe in it, and none deny Our communications except the unbelievers.

48. And you did not recite before it any book, nor did you transcribe one with your right hand, for then could those who say untrue things have doubted.

49. Nay! these are clear communications in the breasts of those who are granted knowledge; and none deny Our communications except the unjust.

50. And they say: Why are not signs sent down upon him from his Lord? Say: The signs are only with Allah, and I am only a plain warner.

51. Is it not enough for them that We have revealed to you the Book which is recited to them? Most surely there is mercy in this and a reminder for a people who believe.

52. Say: Allah is sufficient as a witness between me and you; He knows what is in the heavens and the earth. And (as for) those who believe in the falsehood and disbelieve in Allah, these it is that are the losers.

53. And they ask you to hasten on the chastisement; and had not a term been appointed, the chastisement would certainly have come to them; and most certainly it will come to them all of a sudden while they will not perceive.

54. They ask you to hasten on the chastisement, and most surely hell encompasses the unbelievers;

55. On the day when the chastisement shall cover them from above them, and from beneath their feet; and He shall say: Taste what you did.

56. O My servants who believe! surely My earth is vast, therefore Me alone should you serve.

57. Every soul must taste of death, then to Us you shall be brought back.

58. And (as for) those who believe and do good, We will certainly give them abode in the high places in gardens beneath which rivers flow, abiding therein; how good the reward of the workers:

59. Those who are patient, and on their Lord do they rely.

60. And how many a living creature that does not carry its sustenance: Allah sustains it and yourselves; and He is the Hearing, the Knowing.

61. And if you ask them, Who created the heavens and the earth and made the sun and the moon subservient, they will certainly say, Allah. Whence are they then turned away?

62. Allah makes abundant the means of subsistence for whom He pleases of His servants, and straitens them for whom (He pleases) surely Allah is Cognizant of all things.

63. And if you ask them Who is it that sends down water from the clouds, then gives life to the earth with it after its death, they will certainly say, Allah. Say: All praise is due to Allah. Nay, most of them do not understand.

64. And this life of the world is nothing but a sport and a play; and as for the next abode, that most surely is the life— did they but know!

65. So when they ride in the ships they call upon Allah, being sincerely obedient to Him, but when He brings them safe to the land, lo! they associate others (with Him);

66. Thus they become ungrateful for what We have given them, so that they may enjoy; but they shall soon know.

67. Do they not see that We have made a sacred territory secure, while men are carried off by force from around them? Will they still believe in the falsehood and disbelieve in the favour of Allah?

68. And who is more unjust than one who forges a lie against Allah, or gives the lie to the truth when it has come to him? Will not in hell be the abode of the unbelievers?

69. And (as for) those who strive hard for Us, We will most certainly guide them in Our ways; and Allah is most surely with the doers of good.
