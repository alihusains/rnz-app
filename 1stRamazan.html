1 | MAHE RAMAZAN

NAMAZ…

1) Recite 2-rakat namaz. In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1), recite Surah An’aam (Chp-6) 1 time.
1) 2 Rakat namaz, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 1 martaba Surah An’aam (Chp-6) padhe.

2) Recite 4-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) 15-times.
2) 4 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 15 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) padhe.

1) DUA

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰہُمَّ قَدْ حَضَرَ شَھْرُ رَمَضَانَ وَقَدْ اَفْتَرَضْتَ عَلَیْنَا صِیَامَہٗ وَاَنْزَلْتَ فِیْہِ الْقُرْاٰنَ ھُدًی لِّلنَّاسِ وَبَیِّنَاتٍ مِّنَ الْھُدٰی وَالْفُرْقَانِ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اَعِنَّا عَلٰی صِیَامِہٖ وَتَقَبَّلْہُ مِنَّا وَتَسَلَّمْہُ لَنَا فِیْ یُسْرٍ مِّنْکَ وَعَافِیَۃٍ اِنَّاکَ عَلٰی کُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ قَدِیْرٌ۔
اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

2) DUA

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰهُمَّ اِنَّهٗ قَدْ دَخَلَ شَهْرُ رَمَضَانَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ رَبَّ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ الَّذِي اَنْزَلْتَ فِيْهِ الْقُرْآنَ وَجَعَلْتَهٗ بَيِّنَاتٍ مِّنَ الْهُدٰى وَالْفُرْقَانِ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ فَبَارِكْ لَنَا فِيْ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ وَاَعِنَّا عَلٰى صِيَامِهٖ وَصَلَاتِهٖ وَتَقَبَّلْهُ مِنَّا۔
اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

3) DUA

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰہُمَّ یَامَنْ یَّمْلِکُ التَّدْبِیْرَ وَھُوَعَلٰی کُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ قَدِیْرٌ۔ یَامَنْ یَّعْلَمُ خَآئِنَۃَ الْاَعْیُنِ وَمَاتُخْفِی الصُّدُوْرُ وَتُجِنُّ الضَّمِیْرُ وَہُوَاللَّطِیْفُ الْخَبِیْرُ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اجْعَلْنَا مِمَّنْ نَوٰی فَعَمِلَ وَلَاتَجْعَلْنَا مِمَّنْ شَقِیَ فَکَسَلَ وَلَا مِمَّنْ ہُوَعَلٰی غَیْرٍ عَمَلٍ یَّتَّکِلُ۔
اَللّٰھُمَّ صَحِّحْ اَبْدَآنَنَا مِنَ الْعِلَلِ وَاَعِنَّا عَلٰی مَاافْتَرَضْتَ عَلَیْنَا مِنَ الْعَمَلِ حَتّٰی یَنْقَضِیَ عَنَّا شَھْرُکَ ھٰذَا وَقَدْ اَدَّیْنَا مَفْرُوْضَکَ فِیْہِ عَلَیْنَا۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اَعِنَّا عَلٰی صِیَامِہٖ وَوَفِّفْنَا لِقِیَامِہٖ وَنَشِّطْنَا فِیْہِ لِلصَّلٰوۃِ وَلَا تَحْجُبْنَا مِنَ الْقِرَآئَۃِ وَسَھِّلْ لَنَا فِیْہِ اِیْتَآئَ الزَّکٰوۃِ۔
اَللّٰہُمَّ لَا تُسَلِّطْ عَلَیْنَا وَصَبًا وَّلَا تَعْبًا وَّلَا سَقَمًا وَّلَا عَطَبًا۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ ارْزُقْنَا الْاِفْطَارَ مِنْ رِزْقِکَ الْحَلَالِ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ سَھِّلْ لَنَا فِیْہِ مَا قَسَمْتَہٗ مِنْ رِزْقِکَ وَیَسِّرْ مَا قَدَّرْتَہٗ مِنْ اَمْرِکَ وَاجْعَلْہُ حَلَالًا طَیِّبًا نَقِیًّا مِّنَ الْاٰثَامِ خَالِصًا مِّنَ الْاٰصَارِ وَالْاَجْرَامِ۔
اَللّٰہُمَّ لَا تُطْعِمْنَا اِلَّا طَیِّبًا غَیْرَ خَبِیْثٍ وَلَا حَرَامٍ وَّاجْعَلْ رِزْقَکَ لَنَا حَلَالًا لَایَشُوْبُہٗ دَنَسٌ وَّلَا اَسْقَامٌ یَّامَنْ عِلْمُہٗ بِالسِّرِّ کَعِلْمِہٖ بِالْاِعْلَانِ یَامُتَفَضِّلًا عَلٰی عِبَادِہٖ بِالْاِحْسَانِ یَامَنْ ھُوَ عَلٰی کُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ قَدِیْرٌ وَّبِکُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ عَلِیْمٌ خَبِیْرٌ اَلْھِمْنَا ذِکْرَکَ وَجَنِّبْنَا عُسْرَکَ وَاَنِلْنَا یُسْرَکَ وَاھْدِنَا لِلرِّشَادِ وَوَفِّقْنَا لِلسَّدَادِ وَاعْصِمْنَا مِنَ الْبَلَایَا وَصُنَّا مِنَ الْاَوْزَارِ وَالْخَطَایَا۔
یَامَنْ لَّا یَغْفِرُ عَظِیْمَ الذُّنُوْبِ غَیْرُہٗ وَلَا یَکْشِفُ السُّوْٓئَ اِلَّا ھُوْ یَااَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِیْنَ وَاَکْرَمَ الْاَکْرَمِیْنَ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاَھْلِ بَیْتِہِ الطَّیِّبِیْنَ وَاجْعَلْ صِیَا مَنَا مَقْبُوْلًا وَبِالْبِرِّ وَالتَّقْوٰی مَوْصُوْلًا وَکَذٰلِکَ فَاجْعَلْ سَعْیَنَا مَشْکُوْرًا۔ وَقَیَامَنَا مَبْرُوْرًا وَّقِرَآئَتِنَا مَرْفُوْعًا۔
وَدُعَآئَنَا مَسْمُوْعًا وَّاھْدِنَا لِلْحُسْنٰی وَجَنِّبْنَا الْعُسْرٰی وَیَسِّرْنَا لِلْیُسْرٰی وَاَعْلِ لَنَا الدَّرَجَاتِ وَضَاعِفْ لَنَا الْحَسَنَاتِ وَاقْبِلْ مِنَّا الصَّوْمَ وَالصّلٰوۃَ وَاسْمَعْ مِنَّا الدَّعْوَاتِ وَاغْفِرْ لَنَا الْخَطِیَّآتِ وَتَجَاوَزْعَنَّا السَّیِّئَآتِ وَاجْعَلْنَا مِنَ الْعَامِلِیْنَ الْفَآئِزِیْنَ۔
وَلَا تَجْعَلْنَا مِنَ الْمَغْضُوْبِ عَلَیْہِمْ وَلَا الضَّآلِّیْنَ حَتّٰی یَنْقَضِیَ شَھْرُ رَمَضَانَ عَنَّا وَقَدْ قَبِلْتَ فِیْہِ صِیَآمَنَا وَقِیَامَنَا وَزَکَّیْتَ فِیْہِ اَعْمَالَنَا وَغَفَرْتَ فِیْہِ ذُنُوْبَنَا وَاَجْزَلْتَ فِیْہِ مِنْ کُلِّ خَیْرٍ نَصِیْبَنَا فَاِنَّکَ الْاِلٰہُ الْمُجِیْبُ وَالرَّبُّ الْقَرِیْبُ وَاَنْتَ بِکُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ مُحِیْطٌ۔
اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

NIGHT DUA...

1) DUA

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O ALLAH the month of Ramazan has set in. Thou has made “Fasting” obligatory on each day of this month, and in this month Thou sent down the Qur’an, a guidance to mankind. wherein are Signs to distinguish between good and evil. O ALLAH make us observe fast, respond to its calling, let it take care of our affairs, and keep us in good condition and look after our welfare and comfort through Thy consent. Verily Thou are able to do all things.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

2) DUA

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, the month of Ramazan has indeed arrived. O Allah, the Lord of the month of Ramazan in which You revealed the Holy Quran and made it a sign of guidance and criterion. O Allah, bless us all in this month of Ramazan and help us in fasting in it and that we pray in it and accept our deeds.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

3) DUA

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, O the owner of power and discretion in the universe and He is having power over everything. O one who is aware of the dishonesty of the eyes and the hidden secrets of the hearts and what is concealed in the conscience. And You are having a sharp sight and are well informed.

O Allah, make us of those who make an intention of good deeds who perform them and do not make us of those who are lazy in good deeds due to their wretchedness and evil and those who are hopeful without performing any deed.

O Allah, keep our bodies safe from diseases and illnesses and help us in that which is made obligatory on us till this month of Yours passes. And till we have performed all the obligations.

O Allah, help us to fast in it and to pray in it and make us attentive in prayers and do not keep us away from recitation of the Holy Quran and make it easy for us to pay Zakat in this month. O Allah, do make us subject to any trouble, tiredness, illness and danger.

O Allah, give us Your lawful sustenance to end the fast. O Allah, make easy for us the sustenance that You have distributed in this month and the matters that You have decreed and make that sustenance lawful, clean and pure from sins, crimes and unlawful deeds.

O Allah, do not make us eat except that which is pure and lawful and give us Your lawful sustenance which is untainted by any illness or filth O one who knows the secrets just as He knows the apparent.

O one who is kind and merciful to His servants and one who is powerful over everything. And who is cognizant of everything. Imbue our hearts with Your remembrance and save us from the poverty from Your side and bestow us with your ease and guide us to the straight path and give us the Taufeeq to remain on the right path. And keep us safe from calamities. And keep us safe from, sins and mistakes

O one except whom there is none who can forgive the great sins and who can remove the troubles. O the most merciful of the merciful ones and the most honored of the honored ones. Bless Muhammad and his pure and chaste Ahle Bayt. And accept our fasts and join them with goodness and piety.

And in the same way make our efforts thankful and make our prayers acceptable and exalt our recitation of Quran and accept our supplications and guide us to good deeds and keep us safe from poverty and make easy for us prosperity and raise our status and add to our good deeds and accept our prayers and fasts and accept our supplications and forgive our mistakes and overlook our sins and make us of the doers of good and the successful ones and do not make us of those upon whom is Your anger and those who are astray till the month of Ramazan passes from us in such a way that You have accepted in it our fasts and prayers and in which You have purified our deeds and in which You have forgiven our sins and in which You have increased every goodness for us, indeed You are the God who accepts the supplications and the most proximate Lord and You encompass everything.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

4) DUA

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Praise be to the one who granted me honor due to you, O the month of glory. O Allah, make us strong to keep fasts and to pray and make us steadfast and help us against the disbelieving people. O Allah, You are One and there is no offspring for You and You are self-sufficient and there is nothing like You and You are powerful and no one has any power on You. And You are self sufficient and I am the poor and You are the Master and I am the slave and You are the forgiver and I am the sinner and You are the merciful and I am the sinner and You are the creator and I am the creature and You are ever living and I am the mortal, I ask You for the sake of Your mercy that You forgive me and have mercy on me and overlook my mistakes, You are powerful over everything.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

5) Recite Dua to Welcome Ramazan (Sahifa Sajjadiya 44).
