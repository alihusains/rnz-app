Chp. 3:Ver. 147
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَا اغْفِرْ لَنَا ذُنُوْبَنَا وَاِسْرَافَنَا فِيْٓ اَمْرِنَا وَثَبِّتْ اَقْدَامَنَا وَانْصُرْنَا عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْكٰفِرِيْنَ۔
Our Lord! Forgive us our faults and our extravagance in our affair and make firm our feet and help us against the unbelieving people.

Khudaaya! Hamare gunaho’n ko bakhsh de, hamare umoor mein zeyadatiyo’n ko maaf farma, hamare qadmo ko sabaat (solidity) ataa farma aur kaafiro’n ke muqaable mein hamari madad farma.
