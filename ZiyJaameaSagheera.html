JAME’A SAGHIRA

This short ziyarat is common for all Imams (a.m)…

Yeh ziyarat sabhi Imams (a.m) ke liye padhi ja sakti hai…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰى اَوْلِيَآءِ اللهِ وَاَصْفِيَآئِهِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰى اُمَنَآءِ اللهِ وَاَحِبَّآئِهِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰى اَنْصَارِ اللهِ وَخُلَفَآئِهٖ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰى مَحَالِّ مَعْرِفَةِ اللهِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰى مَسَاكِنِ ذِكْرِ اللهِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰى مُظْهِرِيْ اَمْرِ اللهِ وَنَهْيِهٖ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَى الدُّعَاةِ اِلَى اللهِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَى الْمُسْتَقِرِّيْنَ فِيْ مَرْضَاةِ اللهِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَى الْمُخْلِصِيْنَ فِيْ طَاعَةِ اللهِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَى الْاَدِلَّآءِ عَلَى اللهِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَى الَّذِيْنَ مَنْ وَّالَاهُمْ فَقَدْ وَالَى اللهَ وَمَنْ عَادَاهُمْ فَقَدْ عَادَى اللهَ، وَمَنْ عَرَفَهُمْ فَقَدْ عَرَفَ اللهَ وَمَنْ جَهِلَهُمْ فَقَدْ جَهِلَ اللهَ وَمَنِ اعْتَصَمَ بِهِمْ فَقَدِ اعْتَصَمَ بِاللهِ وَمَنْ تَخَلّٰى مِنْهُمْ فَقَدْ تَخَلّٰى مِنَ اللهِ عَزَّ وَ جَلَّ وَاُشْهِدُ اللهَ اَنِّيْ سِلْمٌ لِمَنْ سَالَمْتُمْ وَحَرْبٌ لِمَنْ حَارَبْتُمْ مُؤْمِنٌ بِسِرِّكُمْ وَعَلَانِيَتِكُمْ مُفَوِّضٌ فِيْ ذَلِكَ كُلِّهٖ اِلَيْكُمْ لَعَنَ اللهُ عَدُوَّ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ مِّنَ الْجِنِّ وَالْاِنْسِ وَاَبْرَاُ اِلَى اللهِ مِنْهُمْ وَصَلَّى اللهُ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِهٖ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

JAME’A SAGHIRA

Imam Ali Naqi (a.s) ne farmaya keh: Jab bhi tum kisi bhi Imam (a.s) ki ziyarat karna chaaho to ghusl karne ke baad 2 rakat Namaaz padho phir khade ho kar Kalema-e-Shahadatain (Ash-hado an la ilaaha illal-laah wah-dahu la shareeka lahu, wa ash-hado anna Mohammadan abdohu wa Rasoolohu) zabaan par jaari karo aur phir yeh ziyarat padho…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Salaam ho Auliya-e-Khuda aur uske barguzida bando par. Salaam ho Khuda ke amaanat-daaro aur uske pyaaro par. Salaam ho Khuda ke naasiro aur uske khalifaao par. Salaam ho Khuda ki maarefat ke nishaano par. Salaam ho zikre ilaahi ke manzil-gaaho par. Salaam ho Khuda ke amr-o-nahi (amr-bil maroof wa nehi-anil munkar) ke aashkaar (waazeh) karne waalo par. Salaam ho Khuda ki taraf bulaane waalo par. Salaam ho Khuda ki razaawo mein rehne waalo par. Salaam ho Khuda ke sachche ita’at guzaaro par. Salaam ho Khuda ki taraf rehnumaayi karne waalo par. Salaam ho unpar keh jo un se mohabbat kare to Khuda us se mohabbat karta hai, aur jo un se dushmani rakkhe Khuda us se dushmani rakhta hai. Salaam ho unpar keh jo unki maarefat haasil kar le woh Khuda ko pehchaan leta hai, aur jo un se na-waaqif ho woh Khuda se na-waaqif hota hai. Jo un se ta’alluq rakkhe woh Khuda se ta’alluq rakhta hai, aur jo un se alag rahe woh Khuda se alag rahe jaata hai. Mai Khuda ko gawah banata hu keh meri sulha (peace) hai us se jisse aap ki sulha ho aur meri jangh hai us se jisse aap ki jangh ho. Mai aap ke zaahir-o-baatin par imaan rakhta hu, is baare mein khud ko aap ke supurd karta hu. Khuda laanat kare Aale Mohammad (a.s) ke dushmano par jo Jinn-o-Ins mein se hai. Mai Khuda ke saamne un se bari hu, aur Khuda rehmat kare Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal-e-Paak (a.s) par.

Iske baad Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par kasrat se (bohot ziyaada) durood aur unke dushmano par laanate bhej ne ke baad apne aur digar (dusre) momineen ke liye dua maange.

JAME’A SAGHIRA

This short ziyarat is common for all Imams (a.m)…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Peace be unto the lovers and chosen ones of God; Peace be unto the trustees and beloved ones of God; Peace be unto the auxiliaries and vice agents of God; Peace be unto the repositories of the understanding of God; Peace be unto the sanctuaries of the remembrance of God; Peace be unto the sanctuaries of the commands and prohibitions of God; Peace be unto the heralds of God; Peace be unto the dwellers in the pleasure of God; Peace be unto the sincere ones in the obedience of God; Peace be unto those who are such that he who loves them loves God; and whoever is their enemy is the enemy of God; and whoever knows them knows God; and whoever is ignorant of them is ignorant of God; and whoever clings to them clings to God; and whoever forsakes them forsakes God. I Call upon Allah as my witness that I am at peace with those who are at peace with Thee, and at war with those who are at war against Thee; I believe in Thy secrets and Thy disclosures, entrusting in this matter everything to Thee. May God curse the enemies of the Children of Muhammad from among the Jinns and men, (and from the first to the last of them). I dissociate myself from them and turn towards God. God bless Muhammad and his Children.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
