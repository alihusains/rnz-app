Chp. 40:Ver. 7
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَا وَسِعْتَ كُلَّ شَيْءٍ رَّحْمَةً وَّعِلْمًا فَاغْفِرْ لِلَّذِيْنَ تَابُوْا وَاتَّبَعُوْا سَبِيْلَكَ وَقِهِمْ عَذَابَ الْجَحِيْمِ۔
Our Lord! Thou embracest all things in mercy and knowledge, therefore grant protection to those who turn (to Thee) and follow Thy way, and save them from the punishment of the hell.

Khudaaya! Teri rehmat aur Tera ilm har shai par muheet (ghere) huwe hai lehaaza un logo’n ko bakhsh de jinho’n ne tauba ki hai aur Tere raaste ka itteba kiya hai aur unhe Jahannam ke azaab se bacha le.
