بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَلْحَمْدُ لِلهِ رَبِّ الْعٰلَمِيْنَﯜ2ﯛ اَلرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيْمِﯜ3ﯛ مٰلِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّيْنِﯜ4ﯛ اِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَاِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِيْنُﯜ5ﯛ اِهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيْمَﯜ6ﯛ صِرَاطَ الَّذِيْنَ اَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ ڏ غَيْرِ الْمَغْضُوْبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا الضَّاۗلِّيْنَﯜ7ﯛ ع

صَدَقَ اللهُ الْعَلِیُّ الْعَظِیْمُ

1 | HAMD | 7

1. Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

2. Saari taareef Allah ke liye hai jo Aalameen ka paalne waala hai.

3. Woh azeem aur daayemi rehmato waala hai

4. Roz-e-qayamat ka maalik-o-mukhtar hai.

5. Parwardigaar! Ham Teri hi ibaadat karte hai aur tujhi se madad chahte hai.

6. Hamein seedhe raaste ki hidaayat farmata rahe.

7. Jo un logo ka raasta hai jin par Tu ne nematein naazil ki hai unka raasta nahi jin par ghazab naazil huwa hai ya jo baheke huwe hai.

1 | OPENING | 7

1. In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

2. All praise is due to Allah, the Lord of the Worlds.

3. The Beneficent, the Merciful.

4. Master of the Day of Judgment.

5. Thee do we serve and Thee do we beseech for help.

6. Keep us on the right path.

7. The path of those upon whom Thou hast bestowed favors. Not (the path) of those upon whom Thy wrath is brought down, nor of those who go astray.
