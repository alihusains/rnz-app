AALE IMRAAN | 18-19

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

شَهِدَ اللهُ اَنَّهٗ لَٓا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا هُوَلا وَالْمَلٰٓائِكَةُ وَاُولُو الْعِلْمِ قَآئِمًام بِالْقِسْطِط لَٓا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا هُوَ الْعَزِيْزُ الْحَكِيْمُ۔ اِنَّ الدِّيْنَ عِنْدَ اللهِ الْاِسْلَامُقف وَمَا اخْتَلَفَ الَّذِيْنَ اُوْتُوا الْكِتٰبَ اِلَّا مِنْم بَعْدِ مَا جَآءَهُمُ الْعِلْمُ بَغْيًۢا بَيْنَهُمْط وَمَنْ يَّكْفُرْ بِاٰيٰتِ اللهِ فَاِنَّ اللهَ سَرِيْعُ الْحِسَابِ۔

ENGLISH… Allah bears witness that there is no god but He, and (so do) the angels and those possessed of knowledge, maintaining His creation with justice; there is no god but He, the Mighty, the Wise. Surely the (true) religion with Allah is Islam, and those to whom the Book had been given did not show opposition but after knowledge had come to them, out of envy among themselves; and whoever disbelieves in the communications of Allah then surely Allah is quick in reckoning.

ROMAN URDU… Allah khud gawah hai keh uske alaawa koi Khuda nahi hai malaayeka aur sahebaan-e-ilm gawah hai keh woh adl ke saath qaayem hai. Uske alaawa koi Khuda nahi hai aur woh saheb-e-izzat-o-hikmat hai. Deen, Allah ke nazdeek sirf Islaam hai aur Ahle kitaab ne ilm aane ke baad hi jaghda shuru kiya hai sirf aapas ki sharaarato ki bina par aur jo bhi ayaate ilaahi ka inkaar karega to Khuda bohot jald hisaab karne waala hai.
