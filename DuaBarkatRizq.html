BARKAT-E-RIZQ

For Sufficient Sustenance recite this dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, O Allah, O Allah, O Lord, O Lord, O Lord, O everliving, O self-subsisting, O owner of splendour and generosity, I beseech You in the name of Your great “ism azam” to give me sustenance, in abundance, lawful and pure by Your mercy, O the most merciful.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
