اَللّٰھُمَّ اَصْلِحْ عَبْدَکَ وَخَلِیفَتَکَ بِمَا اَصْلَحْتَ بِہٖ اَنْبِیَآئَکَ وَرُسُلَکَ وَحُفَّہٗ بِمَلَائِکَتِکَ وَاَیِّدْہٗ بِرُوْحِ الْقُدُسِ مِنْ عِنْدِکَ وَاسْلُکْہٗ مِنْ بَیْنِ یَدَیْہِ وَمِنْ خَلْفِہٖ رَصَدًا یَحْفَظُوْنَہٗ مِنْ کُلِّ سُوْئٍ وَّاَبْدِلْہُ مِنْ بَعْدِ خَوْفِہٖ اَمْنًا یَعْبُدُکَ لَایُشْرِکُ بِکَ شَیْئًا وَلَاتَجْعَلْ لِاَحَدٍ مِّنْ خَلْقِکَ عَلٰی وَلِیِّکَ سُلْطَانًا وَاْذَنْ لَّہٗ فِیْ جِھَادِ عَدُوِّکَ وَاجْعَلْنِیْ مِنْ اَنْصَارِہٖ اِنَّکَ عَلٰی کُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ قَدِیْرٌ۔

ALLAHUMMA
ASLEH ABDEKA

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah! Reform conditions of Your servant and caliph with those means by which You have reformed conditions of Your messengers and prophets. Surround him with the angels and support him with the Holy Spirit. Appoint protectors from the front and behind him, (those) who would guard him from all troubles and calamities.

Convert his fear into security, (so that) he may worship only You and doesn't associate anybody with You. Do not make any of Your creatures an authority over Your vicegerent. Permit him to fight against Your enemies and his enemies and include me among his helpers. Surely You possess power over everything.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
