28 | MAHE RAMAZAN

NAMAZ…

1) 6 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad Ayatul Khursi, Surah Kausar (Chp-108) aur Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) har ek 10 martaba padhe.

SHAB KI DUA…

1) Dua

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Baar-e-Ilaaha mai Tujh se sawaal karta hu, keh Tu Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur yeh chahta hu, keh mujhe narm aur Khuda se lav lagaane waala dil aur sachchi zabaan aur quwwat-e-bardaasht waala jism ataa kar aur mujhe iske sawaab mein Jannat ataa kar. Ay rahem karne waalo mein sab se zeyaada Raheem.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

2) Dua

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay raat ko hawa ke andar zakhira karne waale. Aur noor ko aasmaan mein jama karne waale. Ay aasmaan ko zameen par apni ijaazat ke bagair girne se rokne waale aur unhe zawaal se mehfooz karne waale. Ay saaheb-e-ilm o azmat. Ay maalik-e-afvo dawaam. Ay Allah, ay waaris-e-qaayenaat, ay murdo ko qabro se nikaal ne waale. Ay Allah, ay Allah, ay Allah Tere hi liye haseen asma, buland sifaat, kibriyaayi aur nemaat hai.

Mai Tujh se yeh sawaal karta hu keh Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur mere naam ko aaj ki raat nekh-bakhto mein qaraar de-de. Meri rooh shahido ke saath rahe. Aur meri nekiya buland-tareen manzilo mein rahe. Mere gunah bakhsh diye jaaye aur mujhe aisa yaqeen de-de jo mere dil mein pehwast ho (utar) jaaye aur aisa imaan ataa kar de jo shak ko dur kar de aur Tere rizq se raazi kar de.

Mujhe duniya wa aakherat mein neki ataa farma aur aatish-e-jahannum ke azaab se najaat de-de. Apna zikr, apna shukr, apni taraf raghbat o tawajjoh aur har us amr ki taufiq ataa farma. Jiski taufiq Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Mohammad (a.s) ko inaayat farmaayi hai. Tera salaam in tamaam hazraat par.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Aaj ke din nafal (mustahab) ibaadat se mujhe bohot zeyaada hissa de, aur isme mujhe masaayel-e deen yaad karne se izzat de, aur isme tamaam waseelo mein se mere waseele ko Apne huzoor qareeb-tar farma. Ay Woh zaat jise israar (force) karne waalo ka israar dusro se ghaafil nahi karta.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

28 | MAHE RAMAZAN

NAMAZ…

1) Recite 6-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Ayatul Khursi, Surah Kausar (Chp-108) and Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) 10 times each.

NIGHT DUA...
1) Dua

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, I beg You to bless Muhammad and Aale Muhammad (a.s) and that You give me a humble heart and a truthful tongue and a patient body and as its recompense, give me Paradise, O the most merciful of the merciful ones.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

2) Dua

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O He who commits the night to the custody of atmosphere; and stores the light in the skies; and through His permission (laws governing the operation of all creation) the sky (the cosmic system) prevents the earth from collision and collapse; and both are kept from total destruction, O All-knowing, O Great, O Forgiver, O Eternal, O Allah, O Possessor, O He who shall raise the dead from their graves. O Allah! O Allah! O Allah! Thine are the most beautiful names; highest and greatest are the attributes ascribed to Thee. I solicit Thee (alone) for bounties.
Send blessings on Muhammad and on the children of Muhammad and write my name, tonight, in the list of those who have found true happiness, let my soul rest in peace with those who gave their lives in Thy cause, let my good deeds earn for me an honorable place among the dwellers of Paradise, and forgive my sins; grant me sure belief which gives joy and confidence to my heart; and faith which drives away doubts from me; and let me be happy with that which has been determined; give us that which is good in this world and that which is good in the Hereafter and save us from the torment of the burning Fire; and keep me alive in a manner that I always sing Thy praise, gratefully thank Thee, call humbly and fervently on Thee, and turn repentant unto Thee; and associate me with that which keeps me attached with Muhammad and Aale Muhammad, peace be on him and peace be on them.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah increase my enjoyment and satisfaction in this month in the prayers I pray out of my own free will, kindly make haste in quick disposal of my affairs in my interest, let me be near to the “Means of Approach” (Muhammad and Aale Muhammad) who are the select best medium to gain Thy favors, O He who does not get confused on account of the ceaseless demands of the countless beseechers, but attends to each and every supplication.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
