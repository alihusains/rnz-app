SHAB KI DUA...

1) Dua

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰھُمَّ اَنْتَ اَمَرْتَ بَالدُّعَآئِ وَضَمِنْتَ الْاِجَابَۃَ فَدَعَوْنَاکَ وَنَحْنُ عِبَادُکَ وَبَنُوْا اِمَآئِکَ نَوَاصِیْنَا بِیَدِکَ وَاَنْتَ رَبُّنَا وَنَحْنُ عِبَادُکَ وَلَمْ یَسْئَلِ الْعِبَادُ مِثْلَکَ وَنَرْغَبُ اِلَیْکَ وَلَمْ یَرْغَبِ الْخَلَآئِقُ اِلٰی مِثْلِکَ یَامَوْاضِعَ شَکْوَی السَّآئِلِیْنَ وَمُنْتَھٰی حَاجَۃِ الرَّاغِبِیْنَ وَیَا ذَالْجَبَرُوْتِ وَالْمَلَکُوْتِ وَیَا ذَالسُّلْطَانِ وَالْعِزِّ یَاحَیُّ یَاقَیُّوْمُ یَابَارُّ یَارَحِیْمُ یَاحَنَّانُ یَامَنَّانُ یَابَدِیْعُ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضِ یَاذَالْجَلَالِ وَالْاِکْرَامِ یَاذَالنِّعَمِ وَالطَّوْلِ الَّذِیْ لَا یُرَامُ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَاغْفِرْلِیْ اِنَّکَ اَنْتَ الْغَفُوْرُالرَّحِیْمُ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

2) Dua

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

یَافَالِقَ الْاَصْبَاحِ وَجَاعِلَ الَّیْلِ سَکَنًا وَالشَّمْسِ وَالْقَمَرِ حُسْبَانًا. یَاعَزِیْزُ یَاعَلِیْمُ یَاذَالْمَنِّ وَالطَّوْلِ وَالْقُوَّۃِ وَالْحَوْلِ وَالْفَضْلِ وَالْاِنْعَامِ یَاذَالْجَلَالِ وَالْاِکْرَامِ۔ یَااَللہُ یَارَحْمٰنُ یَااَللہُ یَافَرْدُ یَاوِتْرُ یَااَللہُ یَاظَاہِرُ یَابَاطِنُ یَاحَیُّ لَآاِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ۔ یَااَللہُ یَااَللہُ یَااَللہُ لَکَ الْاَسْمَآئُ الْحُسْنٰی وَالْاَمْثَالُ الْعُلْیَا وَالْکِبْرِیَآئُ وَالْاٰلٰآئُ اَسْئَلُکَ اَنْ تُصَلِّیَ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَاَھْلِ بَیْتِہٖ وَاَنْ تَجْعَلَ اسْمِیْ فِیْ ھٰذِہِ الَّیْلَۃِ فِیْ السُّعَدَآئِ وَرُوْحِیْ مَعَ الشُّھَدَآئِ وَاِحْسَانِیْ فِیْ عِلِّیِّیْنَ وَاِسَآئَتِیْ مَغْفُوْرَۃً وَّاَنْ تَھَبَ لِیْ یَقِیْنًا تُبَاشِرُ بِہٖ قَلْبِیْ وَاِیْمَانًا یُذْھِبُ الشَّکَّ عَنِّیْ وَتُرْضِیَنِیْ بِمَا قَسَمْتَ لِیْ وَاٰتِنَا فِیْ الدُّنْیَا حَسَنَۃً وَّفِیْ الْاٰخِرَۃِ حَسَنَۃً وَّقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ الْحَرِیْقٍ۔ وَارْزُقْنِیْ فِیْھَا ذِکْرَکَ وَشُکْرَکَ وَالرَّغْبَۃَ اِلَیْکَ وَالْاِ نَابَۃَ وَالتَّوْبَۃَ وَالتَّوْ فِیْقَ لِمَا وَفَّقْتَ لَہٗ مُحَّمَدًا وَّاٰلَ مُحَمَّدٍ صَلَّی اللہُ عَلَیْہِ وَعَلَيْهِمُ السَّلَامُ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

NAMAZ…

1) 8 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 1 martaba koi bhi Surah padhe.

SHAB KI DUA…

1) Dua

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Baar-e-Ilaaha! Tu ne dua maang ne ka hukm diya aur usey qubool karne ki Tu ne zamaanat li, pas ham ne Tujh se dua ki aur ham log to Tere bande hai aur Tere hi bando ke farzand hai aur Tere qabza-e-qudrat mein hai.

Tu hi hamara Parwardigaar hai aur ham sab Tere na-cheez ghulam hai. Kisi insaan ne aisi zaat se sawaal nahi kiya jo Tujh se milti-julti ho (Kyu-keh Tu be-misl wa be-nazir hai) aur ham to Tujh se hi lav lagaate hai aur kabhi makhluqaat kisi aise ki taraf tawajjoh nahi karti jo Tera misl ho (is liye keh Tera koi misl hai hi nahi).

Ay saahebaan-e-haajat ke shikwa karne ki jagah aur ay tawajjoh karne waalo ki aakhri manzil, ay saaheb-e-buzurgi o iqtedaar, ay saaheb-e-ghalba o izzat, ay zinda, ay qaayem rehne waale, ay neki karne waale, ay rahem karne waale, ay maher-baan, ay ehsaan karne waale, ay aasmaano aur zameeno ki ijaad karne waale, ay saaheb-e-izzat o sharaf, ay aisi ne’mat o satwat ke maalik jinka qasd na kiya ja sakey.

Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur mere gunah maaf kar de. Yaqeenan Tu bada bakhsh ne waala aur raheem hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

2) Dua

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay daaman-e-saher ke chaak karne waale. Ay raat ko wajah-e-sukoon bana ne waale. Ay gardish-e-Shams o Qamar ke murattab karne (bana ne) waale. Ay saaheb-e-izzat o ilm. Ay saaheb-e-ehsaan o karam. Ay saaheb-e-quwwat o taaqat aur maalik-e-fazl o inaam wa jalaal o ikraam.

Ay Allah, ay zaahir, ay baatin, ay zinda Tere alaawa koi khuda nahi hai. Tamaam nek asma (naam), buland sifaat, buzurgi wa nemat Tere hi liye hai. Meri guzaarish yeh hai keh Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur mere naam ko aaj ki raat nekh-bakhto mein aur meri rooh ko shahido ke saath aur meri neki ko buland-tareen darjaat mein qaraar de.

Mere gunah bakhsh diye jaaye aur mujhe woh yaqeen ataa farma jo mere dil mein pehwast ho (utar) jaaye aur woh imaan de jo shak (doubt) ko dur kare aur maqsoom par raazi bana de. Mujhe duniya wa aakherat mein neki ataa farma aur aatish-e-jahannum ke azaab se mehfooz bana de. Apne zikr, apne shukr, apni taraf raghbat wa tawajjoh aur un tamaam umoor ki taufiq karaamat farma jinki taufiq Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Mohammad (a.s) ko karaamat farmaayi hai. Tera salaam in tamaam hazraat par.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Aaj ke din mai woh cheez maangta hu jo Tujhe pasand hai aur us cheez se panah leta hu jo Tujhe na-pasand hai, aur us mein Tujh se taufiq maangta hu ta-keh mai farma-bardaari karu aur Teri na-farmaani na karu. Ay saahilo (maangne waalo) ko bahot ataa karne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

NIGHT DUA...
1) Dua

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, You have ordered us to supplicate You and You have guaranteed its acceptance. Thus we have supplicated You and we are Your servants and the sons of Your servants and we are in Your control. And you are our Lord and we are Your servants. No man has asked from anything which is like You, because You are incomparable. And we have reposed hopes only in You and the creatures never turn to anyone who is like You, because there is no one like You. O the place where those who are in need complain. And the last station of those who become attentive. O the owner of the power and dominance, O the owner of force and greatness. O the living and the everlasting, O one who does good and one who is merciful. O the most favoring and the most generous. O the creator of the heavens and the earth. O the owner of majesty and nobility. And O the giver of bounties and favors; One who cannot be reached. Bless Muhammad and Aale Muhammad (a.s) and have mercy on me. Indeed You are the forgiving and merciful.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

2) Dua

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O He who causes the dawn to break forth, and through the (revolutions) of the sun and the moon has made available exact basis of knowledge and information, O Mighty, O All-knowing, O Owner of bounties, gifts, strength, favors, rewards, majesty and mercy, O Allah, O Beneficent, O Allah, O Unique, O Single, O Allah, O Rational Expression, O Intrinsic Fundamental, O Ever living, there is no god save Thou. O Allah! O Allah! O Allah! Thine are the most beautiful names; highest and greatest are the attributes ascribed to Thee. I solicit Thee (alone) for bounties. Send blessings on Muhammad and on the children of Muhammad and write my name, tonight, in the list of those who have found true happiness, let my soul rest in peace with those who gave their lives in Thy cause, let my good deeds earn for me an honorable place among the dwellers of Paradise, and forgive my sins; grant me sure belief which gives joy and confidence to my heart; and faith which drives away doubts from me; and let me be happy with that which has been determined; give us that which is good in this world and that which is good in the Hereafter and save us from the torment of the burning Fire; and keep me alive in a manner that I always sing Thy praise, gratefully thank Thee, call humbly and fervently on Thee, and turn repentant unto Thee; and associate me with that which keeps me attached with Muhammad and Aale Muhammad, peace be on him and peace be on them.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah I beseech Thee (to make me do) that which pleases Thee, in this month. I take refuge with Thee from that for which Thou may treat me with contempt. I beseech Thee to arrange my life in such a manner that I obey Thee and never break or tamper with Thy laws, O He who liberally gives abundantly to the beseechers.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
