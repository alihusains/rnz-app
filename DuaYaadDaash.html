سُبْحَانَ مَنْ لَّا یَعْتَدِیْ عَلٰی اَھْلِ مَمْلَکَتِہٖ سُبْحَانَ مَنْ لَّایَاخُذُ اَھْلَ الْاَرْضِ بِاَلْوَاْنِ الْعَذَاْبِ سُبْحَاْنَ الرَّؤُفِ الرَّحِیْمِ۔ اَللّٰھُمَّ اجْعَلْ لِّیْ فِیْ قَلْبِیْ نُوْرًا وَّبَصَرًا وَّفَھْمًا وَّعِلْمًا اِنَّکَ عَلٰی کُلِّ شَىْٓءٍ قَدِیْرٌ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

Yaad-daash mein hizaafe ke liye yeh dua padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Paak-o-monazzah (be-ayeb) hai woh zaat jo apne ahle-mamlekat (hukumat mein rehne waalo) par zulm-o-sitam (ziyaadati) nahi karta. Paak-o-monazzah hai woh zaat jo Ahle-zameen ko mukhtalif qism ke azaab mein mubtila nahi karta.

Paak-o-monazzah hai woh zaat jo Raoof (shafeeq)-o-Rahim hai. Ay Mabood! Tu mere dil mein noor-o-baseerat (maarefat) aur fahem (samajh)-o-ilm ataa farma. Be-shak Tu har shai par qaadir hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Glory be to Him who does not treat the citizens of His Kingdom unjustly and harshly, Glory be to Him who does not let torment and pain come suddenly upon mankind, Glory be to the Compassionate, the Merciful.

O Allah fill my heart and mind with true enlightenment, discerning intelligence, quick understanding and knowledge, Verily Thou art able to do all things.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
