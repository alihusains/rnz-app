SHAB-E-GHADEER

1) On the night of Ghadeer, it is recommended to recite 12 rakat Namaz.

1) Ghadeer ki shab mein taqid hai keh 12 rakat namaz padhe.

2) Recite Dua Shab-e-Ghadeer.

2) Dua Shab-e-Ghadeer padhe.

3) Recite salwaat as many times you can.

3) salwaat padhe jitni martaba ho sake’n.

4) Do isteghfar as much you can.

4) Isteghfar kare jitna ho sake’n.
