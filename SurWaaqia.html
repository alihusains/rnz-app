Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Jab qayamat barpa hogi.

2. Aur uske qaayem hone mein zara bhi jooth nahi hai.

3. Woh ulat-palat kar dene waali hogi.

4. Jab zameen ko zabardast jhatke lagege.

5. Aur pahaad bilkul chur-chur ho jaayege.

6. Phir zarraat (atom) ban kar muntashir ho jaayege.

7. Aur tum teen giroh (3 groups) ho jaawoge.

8. Phir daahine haath waale aur kya kehna daahine haath waalo ka.

9. Aur baaye haath waale aur kya poochhna hai baaye haath waalo ka.

10. Aur sabqat karne waale to sabqat karne waale hi hai.

11. Wohi Allah ki baargah ke muqarrab hai.

12. Nemato bhari jannato mein hoge.

13. Bohat se log agley logo mein se hoge.

14. Aur kuch aakhir daur ke hoge.

15. Moti aur Yaqoot se jadey huwe takhto par.

16. Ek dusre ke saamne takya (pellow) lagaaye baithay hoge.

17. Unke gird hamesha nau-jawaan rehne waale bachche gardish kar rahe hoge.

18. Pyaale aur tunty-waar kantar (kuze) aur sharaab ke jaam liye huwe hoge.

19. Jis se na dard-sar paiyda hoga aur na hosh-o-hawaas goom hoge.

20. Aur unki pasand ke mewe liye hoge.

21. Aur un parindo ka gosht jiski unhe khwahish hogi.

22. Aur kushadaah chasham hurre hogi.

23. Jaise sar-basta moti.

24. Yeh sab dar-haqeeqat unke aamaal ki jaza aur uska inaam hoga.

25. Wahan na koi lagwiyaat (bekaar) soonege aur na gunah ki baate.

26. Sirf har taraf salaam hi salaam hoga.

27. Aur daaheni taraf waale as-haab. Kya kehna un as-haab-e-yamin ka.

28. Be-kaante ki beir.

29. Lade guththay huwe kele (banana).

30. Phaile huwe saaye.

31. Jarne se girte huwe paani.

32. Kaseer tadaad ke mewe ke darmiyaan hoge.

33. Jin ka silsila na khatm hoga aur na un par koi rok-tok hogi.

34. Aur unche qisam ke gadde (mattresses) hoge.

35. Be-shak un hurro ko Ham ne ijaad kiya hai.

36. To unhe nat-nayi (new) (alg-alg) banaya hai.

37. Yeh kuwaariya aur aapas mein ham-joliya (friends) hogi.

38. Yeh sab as-haab-e-yamin ke liye hai.

39. Jin ka ek giroh pehle logo ka hai.

40. Aur ek giroh aakhri logo ka hai.

41. Aur baaye haath waale to unka kya poochhna hai.

42. Garam-garam hawa kholta huwa paani.

43. Kaale siyaah dhuwe (smoke) ka saaya.

44. Jo na thanda ho aur na achcha lagey.

45. Yeh wohi log hai jo pehle bohot aaraam ki zindagi guzaar rahe thay.

46. Aur bade-bade gunaho par israar (repeat) kar rahe thay.

47. Aur kehte thay keh kya jab ham mar jaayege aur khaak aur haddi ho jaayege to hamein dobaara uthaaya jaayega.

48. Kya hamare baap-dadabhi uthaaye jaayege.

49. Aap kahe dijiye keh awleen-o-aakhereen sab ke sab.

50. Ek muqarrar din ki waade-gaah (rendezvous) par jama kiye jaayege.

51. Is ke baad tum ay gumraaho aur joothlaane waalo.

52. Thoudh ke darakht ke khaane waale hoge.

53. Phir us se apne peyt (stomach) bharoge.

54. Phir us par kholta huwa paani piyoge.

55. Phir is tarah piyoge jis tarah pyaase oont peete hai.

56. Yeh qayamat ke din unki mehmaan-daari ka saamaan hoga.

57. Ham ne tum ko paiyda kiya hai to dobaara paiyda karne ki tasdeeq (confirmation) kyu’n nahi karte.

58. Kya tum ne us nutfa ko dekha hai jo rahem mein daalte ho.

59. Usey tum paiyda karte ho ya Ham paiyda karne waale hai.

60. Ham ne tumhaare darmiyaan maut ko muqaddar kar diya hai aur Ham is baat se aajiz nahi hai.

61. Keh tum jaise aur log paiyda kar de aur tumhe is aalam mein dobaara ijaad kar de jise tum jaante bhi nahi ho.

62. Aur tum pehli khilqat ko to jaante ho to phir us mein ghaur kyu’n nahi karte ho?

63. Is daane ko bhi dekha hai jo tum zameen mein botey (daal-te) ho.

64. Usey tum ugaate ho ya Ham ugaane waale hai?

65. Agar Ham chahe to usey chur-chur bana de to tum baate hi banaate rahe jaawo.

66. Keh Ham to badhe ghaate mein rahe.

67. Bal-keh ham to mehroom hi rahe gaye.

68. Kya tum ne us paani ko dekha hai jis ko tum peete ho.

69. Usey tum ne baadal se barsaaya hai ya uske barsaane waale Ham hai.

70. Agar Ham chahte to usey khaara bana dete to phir tum Hamara shukriya kyu’n nahi adaa karte ho.

71. Kya tum ne us aag ko dekha hai jise lakdi se nikaalte ho.

72. Is ke darakht ko tum ne paiyda kiya hai ya Ham uske paiyda karne waale hai?

73. Ham ne usey yaad-dahaani ka zariya aur musaafiro ke liye nafa ka saamaan qaraar diya hai.

74. Ab aap apne azeem Parwardigaar ke naam ki tasbeeh kare.

75. Aur Mai to taaro (stars) ke manaazil ki qasam kha kar kehta hu.

76. Aur tum jaante ho keh yeh qasam bohot badi qasam hai.

77. Yeh bada mohtaram Qur’an hai.

78. Jise ek poshida kitaab mein rakkha gaya hai.

79. Usey paak-o-pakeeza afraad ke alaawa koi chhu bhi nahi sakta hai.

80. Yeh Rabbul aalameen ki taraf se naazil kiya gaya hai.

81. To kya tum log is kalaam se inkaar karte ho.

82. Aur tum ne apni rozi yahi qaraar de rakkhi hai keh is ka inkaar karte raho.

83. Phir aisa kyu’n nahi hota keh jab jaan gale (gardan/neck) tak pohonch jaaye.

84. Aur tum us waqt dekhte hi rahe jaawo.

85. Aur Ham tumhaari nisbat marne waale se qareeb hai magar tum dekh nahi sakte ho.

86. Pas agar tum kisi ke dabaawo (pressure) mein nahi ho aur bilkul azaad ho.

87. To us rooh ko kyu’n nahi palta dete ho agar apni baat mein sachche ho.

88. Phir agar marne waala muqarrabin (nazdiki haasil kiye huwe) mein se hai.

89. To uske liye aasaayish, khusbhoo-daar phool aur nemato ke baaghaat hai.

90. Aur agar as-haab-e-yamin mein se hai.

91. To as-haab-e-yamin ki taraf se tumhaare liye salaam hai.

92. Aur agar joothlaane waalo aur gumraaho mein se hai.

93. To kholte huwe paani ki mehmaani hai.

94. Aur Jahannam mein jonk (daal) dene ki saza hai.

95. Yahi woh baat hai jo bilkul bar-haq aur yaqeeni hai.

96. Lehaaza apne azeem Parwardigaar ke naam ki tasbeeh karte raho.

24. A reward for what they used to do.

28. Amid thornless lote-trees,

30. And extended shade,

31. And water flowing constantly,

32. And abundant fruit,

33. Neither intercepted nor forbidden,

34. And exalted thrones.

37. Loving, equals in age,

38. For the sake of the companions of the right hand.

39. A numerous company from among the first,

41. And those of the left hand, how wretched are those of the left hand!

42. In hot wind and boiling water,

43. And the shade of black smoke,

44. Neither cool nor honorable.

45. Surely they were before that made to live in ease and plenty.

46. And they persisted in the great violation.

47. And they used to say: What! when we die and have become dust and bones, shall we then indeed be raised?

48. Or our fathers of yore?

49. Say: The first and the last,

50. Shall most surely be gathered together for the appointed hour of a known day.

51. Then shall you, O you who err and call it a lie!

52. Most surely eat of a tree of Zaqqoom,

53. And fill (your) bellies with it;

54. Then drink over it of boiling water;

55. And drink as drinks the thirsty camel.

56. This is their entertainment on the day of requital.

57. We have created you, why do you not then assent?

58. Have you considered the seed?

59. Is it you that create it or are We the creators?

60. We have ordained death among you and We are not to be overcome,

61. In order that We may bring in your place the likes of you and make you grow into what you know not.

62. And certainly you know the first growth, why do you not then mind?

63. Have you considered what you sow?

64. Is it you that cause it to grow, or are We the causers of growth?

65. If We pleased, We should have certainly made it broken down into pieces, then would you begin to lament:

66. Surely we are burdened with debt:

67. Nay! we are deprived.

68. Have you considered the water which you drink?

69. Is it you that send it down from the clouds, or are We the senders?

70. If We pleased, We would have made it salty; why do you not then give thanks?

71. Have you considered the fire which you strike?

72. Is it you that produce the trees for it, or are We the producers?

73. We have made it a reminder and an advantage for the wayfarers of the desert.

74. Therefore glorify the name of your Lord, the Great.

75. But nay! I swear by the falling of stars;

76. And most surely it is a very great oath if you only knew;

77. Most surely it is an honored Quran,

78. In a book that is protected

79. None shall touch it save the purified ones.

80. A revelation by the Lord of the worlds.

81. Do you then hold this announcement in contempt?

82. And to give (it) the lie you make your means of subsistence.

83. Why is it not then that when it (soul) comes up to the throat,

84. And you at that time look on—

85. And We are nearer to it than you, but you do not see—

86. Then why is it not— if you are not held under authority—

87. That you send it (not) back— if you are truthful?

88. Then if he is one of those drawn nigh (to Allah),

89. Then happiness and bounty and a garden of bliss.

90. And if he is one of those on the right hand,

91. Then peace to you from those on the right hand.

92. And if he is one of the rejecters, the erring ones,

93. He shall have an entertainment of boiling water,

94. And burning in hell.

95. Most surely this is a certain truth.

96. Therefore glorify the name of your Lord, the Great.
