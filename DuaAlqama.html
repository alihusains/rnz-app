ALQAMA

Recite this dua after reciting Ziyarat Ashura…
Is dua ko baad az Ziyarat Ashura padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

یَااَللہُ یَااَللہُ یَااَللہُ یَامُجِیْبَ دَعْوَۃِ الْمُضْطَرِّیْنَ یَاکَاشِفَ کُرَبِ الْمَکْرُوْبِیْنَ یَاغِیَاثَ الْمُسْتَغِیْثِیْنَ یَاصَرِیْخَ الْمُسْتَصْرِ خِیْنَ وَیَا مَنْ ھُوَ اَقْرَبُ اِلَیَّ مِنْ حَبْلِ الْوَرِیْدِ وَیَا مَنْ یَحُوْلُ بَیْنَ الْمَرْٓئِ وَقَلْبِہٖ وَیَا مَنْ ھُوَ بِالْمَنْظَرِ الْاَعْلٰی وَبِالْاُفُقِ الْمُبِیْنِ یَامَنْ ھُوَ الرَّحْمٰنُ الرَّحِیْمُ عَلَی الْعَرْشِ اسْتَوٰی وَیَا مَنْ یَّعْلُمُ خَآئِنَۃَ الْاَعْیُنِ وَمَا تُخْفِی الصُّدُوْرُ وَیَا مَنْ لَا یَخْفٰی عَلَیْہِ خَافِیَۃٌ یَّا مَنْ لَا تَشْتَبِہٗ عَلَیْہِ الْاَصْوَاتُ وَیَا مَنْ لَا تُغَلِّطُہُ الْحَاجَاتُ وَیَا مَنْ لَا یُبْرِ مُہُ الْحَاحُ الْمُلِحِّیْنَ یَامُدْرِکَ کُلِّ فَوْتٍ وَّیَا جَامِعَ کُلِّ شَمْلٍ وَّ یَا بَارِئَ النُّفُوْسِ بَعْدَ الْمَوْتِ یَامَنْ ھُوَ کُلِّ یَوْمٍ فِیْ شَانٍ یَّا قَاضِیَ الْحَاجَاتِ یَامُنَفِّسَ الْکُرُبَاتِ یَامُعْطِیَ السُّئُوْلَاتِ یَاوَلِیَّ الرَّغَبَاتِ یَاکَافِیَ الْمُھِمَّاتِ یَامَنْ یَکْفِیْ مِنْ کُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ وَّلَا یَکْفِیْ مِنْہُ شَیْٓءٍ فِی السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضِ اَسْئَلُکَ بِحَقِّ مُحَمَّدٍ خَاتِمِ النَّبِیِّیْنَ وَعَلِیٍّ اَمِیْرِ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ وَبِحَقِّ فَاطِمَۃَ بِنْتِ نَبِیِّکَ وَبِحَقِّ الْحَسَنِ وَالْحُسَیْنِ فَاِنِّیْ بِھِمْ اَتَوَجَّہٗ اِلَیْکَ فِیْ مَقَامِیْ ھٰذَا وَبِھِمْ اَتَوَسَّلُ وَبِھِمْ اَتَشَفَّعُ اِلَیْکَ وَبِحَقِّھِمْ اَسْئَلُکَ وَاُقْسِمُ وَاَعْزِمُ عَلَیْکَ وَبِالشَّانِ الَّذِیْ لَھُمْ عِنْدَکَ وَبِالْقَدْرِ الَّذِیْ لَھُمْ عِنْدَکَ وَبِالَّذِیْ فَضَّلْتَھُمْ عَلَی الْعَالَمِیْنَ وَبِاسْمِکَ الَّذِیْ جَعَلْتَہٗ عِنْدَھُمْ وَبِہٖ خَصَصْتَھُمْ دُوْنَ الْعَالَمِیْنَ وَبِہٖ اَبَنْتَھُمْ وَاَبَنْتَ فَضْلَھُمْ مِنْ فَضْلِ الْعَالَمِیْنَ حَتّٰی فَاقَ فَضْلُھُمْ فَضْلَ الْعَالَمِیْنَ جَمِیْعًا اَسْئَلُکَ اَنْ تُصَلِّیَ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاَنْ تَکْشِفَ عَنِّیْ غَمِّیْ وَ ھَمِّیْ وَ کَرْبِیْ وَ تَکْفِیَنِی الْمُھِمَّ مِنْ اُمُوْرِیْ وَتَقْضِیَ عَنِّیْ دَیْنِیْ وَتُجِیْرَ نِیْ مِنَ الْفَقْرِ وَتُجِیْرَ نِیْ مِنَ الْفَاقَۃِ وَتُغْنِیَنِیْ عَنِ الْمَسْئَلَۃِ اِلَی الْمَخْلُوْقِیْنَ وَتَکْفِیَنِیْ ھَمَّ مَنْ اَخَافُ ھَمَّہٗ وَعُسْرَ مَنْ اَخَافُ عُسْرَہٗ وَحُزُوْنَۃَ مَنْ اَخَافُ حُزُوْنَتَہٗ وَشَرَّ مَنْ اَخَافُ شَرَّہٗ وَمَکْرَ مَنْ اَخَافُ مَکْرَہٗ وَبَغْیَ مَنْ اَخْافُ بَغْیَہٗ وَجَوْرَ مَنْ اَخْافُ جَوْرَہٗ وَسُلْطَانَ مَنْ اَخْافُ سُلْطَانَہٗ وَکَیْدَ مَنْ اَخْافُ کَیْدَہٗ وَمَقْدُرَۃَ مَنْ اَخْافُ مَقْدُرَتَہٗ عَلَیَّ وَتَرُدَّنِیْ عَنِّیْ کَیْدَ الْکَیْدَۃِ وَمَکْرَ الْمَکْرَۃِ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ مَنْ اَرَادَ نِیْ فَاَرِدْہُ وَمَنْ کَادَنِیْ فَکِدْہُ وَاصْرِفْ عَنِّیْ کَیْدَہٗ وَمَکْرَہٗ وَبَاسَہٗ وَاَمَانِیَّہٗ وَاَمْنَعْہُ عَنِّیْ کَیْفَ شِئْتَ وَاَنّٰی شِئْتَ۔ اَللّٰھُمَّ اشْغُلْہُ عَنِّیْ بِفَقْرٍ لَّا تَجْبُرُہٗ وَبِبَلَآئٍ لَا تَسْتُرُہٗ وَبِفَاقَۃٍ لَّا تَسُدُّھَا وَبِسُقْمٍ لَّا تُعَافِیْہِ وَذُلٍّ لَّا تُعِزُّہٗ وَبِمَسْکَنَۃٍ لَّا تَجْبُرُھَا۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اضْرِبْ بِالذُّلِّ نَصْبَ عَیْنَیْہِ وَاَدْخِلْ عَلِیْہِ الْفَقْرَ فِیْ مَنْزِلِہٖ وَالْعِلَّۃَ وَالسُّقْمَ فِیْ بَدَنِہٖ حَتّٰی تَشْغَلَہٗ عَنِّیْ بِشُغْلٍ شَاغِلٍ لَّا فَرَاغَ لَہٗ وَاَنْسِہٖ ذِکْرِیْ کَمَا اَنْسَیْتَہٗ ذِکْرَکَ وَخُذْ عَنِّیْ بِسَمْعِہٖ وَبَصَرِہٖ وَلِسَانِہٖ وَیَدِہٖ وَرِجْلِہٖ وَقَلْبِہٖ وَجَمِیْعِ جَوَارِحِہٖ وَاَدْخِلْ عَلَیْہِ فِیْ جَمِیْعِ ذٰلِکَ السُّقْمَ وَلَا تَشْفِہٖ حَتّٰی تَجْعَلَ ذٰلِکَ لَہٗ شُغْلًا بِہٖ عَنِّیْ وَعَنْ ذِکْرِیْ وَاکْفِنِیْ یَاکَافِیْ مَالَا یَکْفِیْ سِوَاکَ فَاِنَّکَ الْکَافِیْ لَا کَافِیَ سِوَاکَ وَمُفَرِّجٌ لَّا مُفَرِّجَ سِوَاکَ وَمُغِیْثٌ لَّا مُغِیْثَ سِوَاکَ وَجَارٌ لَّا جَارَ سِوَاکَ خَابَ مَنْ کَانَ جَارُہٗ سِوَاکَ وَمُغِیْثُہٗ سِوَاکَ وَمَفْزَعُہٗ اَلٰی سِوَاکَ وَمَھْرَبُہٗ اِلٰی سِوَاکَ وَمَلْجَاہٗ اِلٰی غَیْرِکَ وَمَنْجَاہٗ مِنْ مَّخْلُوْقٍ غَیْرَکَ فَاَنْتَ ثِقَتِیْ وَرَجَآئِیْ وَمَفْزَعِیْ وَمَھْرَ بِیْ وَمَلْجَآئِیْ وَمَنْجَایَ فَبِکَ اَسْتَفْتِحُ وَبِکَ اَسْتَنْجِحُ وَبِمُحَمَّدٍ اٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ اَتَوَجَّہٗ اِلَیْکَ وَاَتَوَسَّلُ وَاَتَشَفَّعُ فَاَسْئَلُکَ یَااَللہُ یَااَللہُ یَااَللہُ فَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ وَلَکَ الشُّکْرُ وَاِلَیْکَ الْمُشْتَکٰی وَاَنْتَ الْمُسْتَعَانُ فَاسْئَلُکَ یَااللہُ یَااللہُ یَااللہُ بِحَقِّ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ اَنْ تُصَلِّیَ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاَنْ تَکْشِفَ عَنِّیْ غَمِّیْ وَھَمِّیْ وَکَرْبِیْ فِیْ مَقَامِیْ ھٰذَا کَمَا کَشَفْتَ عَنْ نَبِیِّکَ ھَمَّہٗ وَغَمَّہٗ وَکَرْبَہٗ وَکَفَیْتَہٗ ھَوْلَ عَدُوِّہٖ فَاکْشِفْ عَنِّیْ کَمَا کَشَفْتَ عَنْہُ وَفَرِّجْ عَنِّیْ کَمَا فَرَّجْتَ عَنْہُ وَاکْفِنِیْ کَمَا کَفَیْتَہٗ وَاسْرِفْ عَنِّیْ ھَوْلَ مَا اَخَافُ ھَوْلَہٗ وَمَؤُنَۃَ مَا اَخَافُ مَؤُنَتَہٗ وَھَمَّ مَا اَخَافُ ھَمَّہٗ بِلَا مَؤُنَۃٍ عَلٰی نَفْسِیْ مِنْ ذٰلِکَ وَاسْرِفْنِیْ بِقَضَآئِ حَوَآئِجِیْ وَکِفَایَۃِ مَا تَھَمَّنِیْ ھَمُّہٗ مِنْ اَمْرِ اٰخِرَتِیْ وَدُنْیَایَ یَااَمِیْرَ الْمُؤْ مِنِیْنَ وَیَا اَبَا عَبْدِ اللہِ عَلَیْکُمَا مِنِیْ سَلَامُ اللہِ اَبَدًا مَا بَقِیْتُ وَبِقِیَّ اللَّیْلُ وَالنَّھَارُ وَلَا جَعَلَہُ اللہُ اٰخِرَ الْعَھْدِ مِنْ زِیَارَتِکُمَا وَلَا فَرَّقَ اللہُ بَیْنِیْ وَبَیْنَکُمَا۔ اَللّٰھُمَّ اَحْیِنِیْ حَیْوٰۃَ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّذُرِّیَّتِہٖ وَاَمِتْنِیْ مَمَاتَھُمْ وَتَوَفَّنِیْ عَلٰی مِلَّتَھِمْ وَاحْشُرْنِیْ فِیْ زُمْرَ تِھِمْ وَلَا تُفَرِّقْ بَیْنِیْ وَبَیْنَھُمْ طَرْفَۃَ عَیْنٍ اَبَدًا فِیْ الدُّنْیَا وَالْاٰخِرَۃِ یَااَمِیْرَ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ وَیَا اَبَا عَبْدِ اللہِ اَتَیْتُکُمَا زَآئِرًا وَّمُتَوَسِّلًا اِلَی اللہِ رَبِّیْ وَرَبِّکُمَا وَمُتَوَجِّھًا اِلَیْہِ بِکُمَا وَمُسْتَشْفِعًا بِکُمَا اِلَی اللہِ تَعَالٰی فِیْ حَاجَتِیْ ھٰذِہٖ فَاشْفَعَا لِیْ فَاِنَّ لَکُمَا عِنْدَ اللہِ الْمَقَامَ الْمَحْمُوْدَ وَالْجَاہَ الْوَجِیْہَ وَالْمَنْزِلَ الرَّفِیْعِ وَالْوَسِیْلَۃَ اِنِّیْ اَنْقَلِبُ عَنْکُمَا مُنْتَظِرًا لِّتَنَجِّزُ الْحَاجَۃِ وَقَضَآئِھَا وَنَجَاحِھَا مِنَ اللہِ بِشَفَا عَتِکُمَا لِیْ اِلَی اللہِ فِیْ ذٰلِکَ فَلَا اَخِیْبُ وَلَا یَکُوْنُ مُنْقَلَبِیْ مُنْقَلَبًا خَآئِبًا خَاسِرًا بَلْ یَکُوْنُ مُنْقَلَبِیْ مُنْقَلَبًا رَّاجِحًا مُفْلِحًا مُنْجِحًا مُسْتَجَابًا بِقَضَآئِ جَمِیْعِ حَوَآئِجِیْ وَتَشَفَّعَا لِیْ اِلَی اللہِ انْقَلَبْتُ عَلٰی مَاشَآئَ اللہُ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّۃَ اِلَّا بِاللہِ مُفَوِّضًا اَمْرِیْ اِلَی اللہِ مُلْجِا ظَھْرِیْ اِلَی اللہِ مُتَوَ کِّلًا عَلَی اللہِ وَاَقُوْلُ حَسْبِیَ اللہُ وَکَفٰی سَمِعَ اللہُ لِمَنْ دَعٰی لَیْسَ وَرَآئَ اللہِ وَوَرَآئَکُمْ یَاسَادَتِیْ مُنْتَھٰی مَاشَآئَ رَبِّی کَانَ وَمَا لَمْ یَشَاْ لَمْ یَکُنْ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّۃَ اِلَّا بَاللہِ اَسْتَوْ دِعُکُمَا اللہُ وَلَا جَعَلَہُ اللہُ اٰخِرَ الْعَھْدِ مِنِّیْ اِلَیْکُمَا اِنْصَرَفْتُ یَاسَیِّدِیْ یَااَمِیْرَ الْمُؤْ مِنِیْنَ وَمَوْلَایَ وَاَنْتَ یَااَبَا عَبْدِ اللہِ یَاسَیِّدِیْ وَسَلَامِیْ عَلَیْکُمَا مُتَّصِلٌ مَّااتَّصَلَ اللَّیْلُ وَالنَّھَارُ وَاصِلٌ ذٰلِکَ اِلَیْکُمَا غَیْرُ مَحْبُوْبٍ عَنْکُمَا سَلَامِیْ اِنْشَآئَ اللہُ وَاَسْئَلُہٗ بِحَقِّکُمَا اَنْ یَّشَآئَ ذٰلِکَ وَیَفْعَلَ فَاِنَّہٗ حَمِیْدٌ مَّجِیْدٌ اِنْقَلَبْتُ یَاسَیِّدَیَّ عَنْکُمَا تَآئِبًا حَامِدًا للہِ شَاکِرًا رَاجِیًا لِّلْاِجَابَۃِ غَیْرَ اٰیِسٍ وَّلَا قَانِطٍ آئِبًا عَآئِدًا رَاجِعًا اِلٰی زِیَاتِکُمَا غَیْرَ رَاغِبٍ عَنْکُمَا وَلَا مِنْ زِیَارَتِکُمَا بَلْ رَاجِعٌ عَآئِدٌ اِنْشَآئَ اللہُ وَلَا حَوْ لَ وَلَا ALQAMA

Is dua ko baad az Ziyarat-e-Ashura padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Allah! ay Allah! ay Allah! ay be-sahara pareshan-haal logo ki dua qabool karne waale, ay mushkilo waalo ki mushkile hal karne waale, ay daad-khwaho (madad talab karne waalo) ki daad-rasi (madad) karne waale, ay faryaadiyo ki faryaad ko pohonchne waale, aur ay woh jo sheh-rag (Juglar Vein) se bhi ziyaada mere qareeb hai.
Ay woh jo insaan aur uske dil ke darmiyaan haayel ho jaata hai. Aur woh jo nazar se baalatar jagah aur roshan-tar kinaare mein hai. Ay woh jo bada maherbaan, nehaayat rahem waala, arsh par haavi hai. Ay woh jo aankho ko na dikhaayi dene waali harkat aur dilo ki baato ko jaanta hai. Ay woh jis par koi raaz poshida nahi, ay woh jis par aawaaze mushtabeh nahi hoti.
Ay woh jis ko haajato mein bhool nahi padti, ay woh jis ko maang-ne waalo ka israar tangh (bezaar) nahi karta, ay har ghum-shuda (khohe huwe) ko pa lene waale, ay bikhro ko ekattha karne waale, aur ay logo ko maut ke baad zinda karne waale, ay woh jiski har roz navi shaan hai.
Ay haajato ke pura karne waale, ay museebato ko dur karne waale, ay sawaalo ke pura karne waale, ay khwahisho par mukhtaar, ay mushkilo mein madad-gaar, ay woh jo har amr mein madad-gaar hai, aur jiske siwa zameen aur aasmaano mein koi cheez madad nahi karti.
Sawaal karta hu Tujh se nabiyo ke khaatim (aakhri Nabi) Mohammad (s.a.w.a) ke haq ke waaste aur momino ke amir Hazrat Ali-e-Murtaza (a.s) ke haq ke waaste Tere Nabi (s.a.w.a) ki dukhtar (daughter) Fatema (s.a) ke haq ke waaste, aur Hasan (a.s)-o-Husain (a.s) ke haq ke waaste. Kyu’n keh mai ne inhi ke wasile se Teri taraf rukh kiya is jagah jahan khada hu unko apna wasila banaya unhi ko Tere yahan sifaarishi banaya, aur unke haq ke waaste Tera sawaali hu usi ki qasam deta hu aur Tujh se talab karta hu.
Unki shaan ke waaste jo woh Tere yahan rakhte hai, us rutbe (martabe) ka waasta jo woh Tere huzoor rakhte hai, keh jisse Tu ne unko jahaano mein badaayi di aur Tere us naam ke waaste se jo Tu ne unke yahan qaraar diya, aur uske zariye unko jahaano mein khusu-siyat (speciality) ataa farmaayi. Unko mumtaz kiya aur unki fazilat ko jahaano mein sab se bada diya, yahan tak keh unki fazilat tamaam jahaano mein sab se ziyaada ho gayi.
Sawaal karta hu Tujh se keh Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil kar aur yeh keh dur farma de mera har ranj-o-gham har andesha (doubt), aur har dukh, aur meri madad kar har dushwaar kaam mein. Mera qarz adaa kar de, panah de mujh ko tangdasti se, bacha mujh ko na-daari (muflisi) se aur be-neyaaz kar de mujh ko logo ke aagey haath phaila ne se, aur meri madad farma us andeshe mein (aane waali balaao ki fikr) jisse mai darta hu.
Aur us tanghi mein jisse pareshan hu, us gham mein jisse ghabraata hu, us takleef mein jisse khauf khaata hu. Us buri tadbeer se jisse darta rehta hu. Us zulm se jisse sehma huwa hu, us be-ghar ho ne se jisse dara huwa hu, uske tasallut (sawaar hone) se jisse hiraasa (ghabraaya huwa) hu. Us fareb se jisse khaayef hu. Uski qudrat se jisse darta hu. Dur rakh mujh se dhoka dene waalo ke dhoke aur fareb-kaaro ke fareb ko.
Ay Ma’bood! jo mere liye jaisa qasd (iraada) kare Tu uske saath waisa qasd kar, jo mujhe dhoka de Tu usey dhoka de, aur dur kar de mujh se uske dhoke, fareb, sakhti aur uski bad-andeshi (buri soch) ko rok de, usey mujh se jis tarah Tu chahe aur jahan chahe, ay Ma’bood! Usko mera khayaal bhula de. Aise qaafile se jo dur na ho, aisi museebat se jise Tu na taale, aisi tangdasti se jise Tu na hataaye, aisi bimaari se jisse Tu na bachaaye, aisi zillat se jis mein Tu izzat na de, aur aisi be-kasi se jise Tu dur na kare.
Ay Ma’bood! Mere dushman ki khwari (zillat) ko uske saamne zaahir kar de, uske ghar mein faqr-o-faaqa ko daakhil kar de, aur uske badan mein dukh-dard aur bimaari paida kar de, yahan tak keh mujhe bhool kar usey apni hi padi ho. Keh usey buraayi ka mauqa na miley, usey meri yaad bhula de, jaise us ne Teri yaad ko bhula rakkhi hai.
Aur meri taraf se uske kaan, uski aankhe, uski zabaan, uske haath, uske pair, uska dil, aur uske tamaam aaza (parts) ko rok de, aur waarid kar de un sab par bimaari (bimaari mein mubtela kar de). Aur us se usey shifa na de, yahan tak keh bana de uske liye aisi sakhti jis mein woh pada rahe keh mujh se aur meri yaad se ghaafil ho jaaye, aur meri madad kar ay madad-gaar Tere siwa koi madad-gaar nahi.
Kyu’n keh Tu mere liye kaafi hai, Tere siwa koi kaafi nahi. Tu kashaaish (khush-haali) dene waala hai Tere siwa koi kashaaish dene waala nahi. Tu faryaad-ras (faryaad par madad ko pohonchne waala) hai Tere siwa koi faryaad-ras nahi. Tu panah dene waala hai aur koi nahi. Na-ummeed huwa woh jis ko Tu panah dene waala nahi, jiski faryaad ko Tu na pohonche phir woh kis se faryaad kare, aur Tere alawa kis ki taraf bhaghe jo usey panah de, Tere siwa kis se panah maange jo usey bachaaye (yaqeenan Tere siwa koi nahi). Tu hi mera sahara, meri ummeed ki jagah, meri faryaad-gah, mere qaraar ki jagah, meri panah-gah hai aur Tu hi mujhe najaat dene waala hai.
Pas Tujhi se najaat ka taalib hu aur kaam-yaabi chahta hu. Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) ke waaste se Teri taraf aaya aur unhe wasila banaya aur shifa’at chahta hu. Pas sawaal hai Tujh se ay Allah! ay Allah! ay Allah! Pas hamd-o-shukr Tere hi liye hai. Tujhi se shikaayat ki jaati hai aur Tu hi madad karne waala hai.
Pas sawaal karta hu Tujh se ay Allah! ay Allah! ay Allah! Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) ke waaste se, keh Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma, aur dur kar de Tu mera gham, mera andesha aur mera dukh isi jagah jahan mai khada hu jaise Tu ne dur kiya tha apne Nabi (s.a.w.a) ka andesha aur unka gham aur unki tanghi, aur dushman ke khauf se unhe najaat di thi.
Pas dur kar meri mushkil jaise unki mushkil dur ki thi. Aur kashaaish (khush-haali) de mujh ko jaise unko kashaaish di thi aur meri madad kar jaise unki madad farmaayi thi. Mera khauf dur kar jaise unka khauf dur farmaya tha, meri takleef dur kar jaise unki takleef dur farmaayi thi, aur woh andesha mita jisse darta hu ba-ghair iske keh us se mujhe koi zehmat uthani pade, mujhe palta jab keh meri haajaat puri ho jaaye aur jis amr ka andesha hai us mein meri madad kar duniya-o-aakherat ke tamaam-tar moamelaat mein.
Ay Momino ke Amir (a.s) aur ay Aba Abdillah (a.s) aap par meri taraf se Khuda ka salaam ho sada-o-daayemaana (always) jab tak zinda hu aur yeh raat-din baaqi hai. Aur Khuda meri is ziyarat ko aap dono ke liye meri aakhari ziyarat na qaraar de, aur mere aur aap ke darmiyaan kabhi judaayi na aaye. Ay Ma’bood! Mujhe Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) jaisi zindagi de aur mujhe maut aaye to unhi ki tarah. Aur mera khaatemah unhi ki ravish (raaste) par ho.
Mujhe unke zumre (group) mein mehshoor farma. Aur mere aur unke darmiyaan judaayi na daal ek pal ke liye bhi, na duniya mein aur na aakherat mein. Ay Ameeral Momineen (a.s) ay Aba Abdillah (a.s) mai aap dono ki ziyarat ko aaya keh is ko Khuda ke yahan wasila banaawu jo mera aur aap ka Rab hai, mai aap ke zariye uski taraf motawajjeh (attention) huwa hu aur aap dono ko Khuda ke yahan sifaarishi banata hu apni haajato ke liye, pas meri shifa’at kare keh aap dono Khuda ke huzoor pasandeeda maqaam, badi izzat, buland martaba, aur mohkam (mazboot) ta’alluq (rishte) rakhte hai.
Be-shak mai palat raha hu aap dono ke yahan se is intezaar aur ummeed ke saath keh meri haajat puri ho aur saari muraad bar-aaye (tamanna puri ho). Khuda ke yahan aap ki shifa’at ke zariye jo mere haq mein aap Khuda ke yahan karenge. Lehaaza mai maayoos nahi hu. Aur meri waapsi aisi waapsi nahi hai jis mein na-ummeedi aur na-kaami (failure) ho.
Bal-keh meri waapsi aisi jo behtareen faaide-mand, kaam-yaab qubool-e-dua ke haamil (dua ke qabooliyat ke saath) meri tamaam haajate puri ho ne ke saath hai. Jab-keh aap Khuda ke yahan meri sifaarishi hai. Mai palat raha hu us amr par jo Khuda chahe, aur nahi hai koi taaqat-o-quwwat siwaaye Khuda se. Mai ne apna saara mo’aamela Khuda ke supurd kar diya usi ke sahaare, aur usi Khuda ke bharose par. Mai kehta hu Khuda hi mera zimme-daar aur Wohi mere liye kaafi hai.
Yaqeenan Khuda soonta hai jo usey pukaarta hai, Mera koi thikaana nahi siwaaye Khuda ke aur aap logo ki pusht-panahi ke. Ay mere sayyed-o-sardaar! jo mera Rab chahe woh hota hai aur jo woh na chahe nahi hota. Aur nahi hai koi taaqat-o-quwwat siwaaye Khuda se. Mai aap dono ko (al-wida kehta hu aur) supurd-e-Khuda karta hu aur Khuda is ko aap dono ke yahan meri aakhri haazri qaraar na de, mai phir waapas aawu (aameen).
Ay mere Aaqa ay momino ke Amir ay mere Maula (Ali a.s) aur ay Aba Abdillah (a.s) ay mere sardaar! Mera salaam ho aap dono par motawaatir (continuously) jab tak raat-o-din baaqi hai yeh salaam aap dono ko pohonchta rahe, kabhi rukne na paaye insha-Allah. Aur mai Khuda se sawaal karta hu aap dono ke haq ke waaste se keh woh yahi chahe aur aisa hi kare. Be-shak woh hamd-o-buzurghi waala hai. Mai aap ke yahan se waapas ja raha hu.
Ay mere sayyed-o-sardaar! Khuda se tauba aur uski hamd-o-sana-o-shukr karte huwe is ummeed ke saath keh woh mere saare aamaal ko qabool kare, mujhe na-ummeed-o-maayoosi na ho. Phir dobaara aap ki ziyarat ho is ummeed ke saath na keh mooh mod ke. Insha-Allah! (agar Allah ne chaha) aur nahi hai koi taaqat-o-quwwat siwaaye Khuda se.
Ay mere sardaar! Mai be-taab (be-chain) hu aap ka aur aap ki ziyarat ka, jab-keh yeh duniya waale be-raghbat ho gaye hai aap se aur aap dono ki ziyarat karne se. Pas Khuda mujhe na-ummeed na kare us se jiski ummeed-o-aarzu rakhta hu aap ki ziyarat se. Be-shak woh nazdeek-tar hai aur qabool karne waala hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

ALQAMA

Recite this dua after reciting Ziyarat-e-Ashura…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah! O Allah! O Allah! O He who gives answer to the cries of the persecuted! O He who pulls out the grief-stricken from agony and anguish! O He who comes to help those who shed tears in helplessness! O he who brings relief to those who cry for help!
O He who is close by, nearer than my jugular vein! O He who makes peace between heart and mind; O He whom people search in the highest and noblest perspectives; in true and evident distinctions! O He who is Beneficent and Merciful in His authority! O He who knows what the furtive eyes betrays and what the secretive hearts conceal!
O He whom do not deceive those who do everything in secret! O He whom intermingled voices of the countless callers (supplicants) do not stop from giving answer to each and every one! O He whom wants and needs of the infinite needy do not put in confusion for attending to one and all separately! O he whom clamorous determined pleading of petitioners do not upset! O He who reaches and takes hold of everything that tries to escape!
O He who keeps together and brings into harmony people who know and understand! O He who examines the souls when they present themselves before Him after death! O he who, each day, has (new) distinct glory! O Fulfiller of wants and needs! O He who gives confront and respite in distress! O He who liberally satisfies those who solicit His favours!
O He who stands by and follow close upon those who humble and fervently call on Him! O He who effectively support those who make sincere efforts! O He who is equal to and can do anything instead of another in the matters concerning all things; and nothing, in the heavens and the earth, can be out of the reach of or is free from His hold.
I beseech Thee in the name of Muhammad, the last Prophet, and Ali, the commander of the faithfuls, in the name of Fatimah, the daughter of Thy Prophet, and in the name of Hasan and Husayn. I direct myself towards Thee, through them, in my address, through them I try to get a hearing, through them I put forward my case to get Thy favours, in their names I make a request to Thee; bound to Thee by oat, living a life according to Thy commands.
I carry out my pledge by every means, in the name of the love and devotion Thou has for them, and the influence and status they enjoy in every dimension of Thy will, the superior most pre-eminence in wisdom and character Thou bestowed upon them to transcend above everything in the whole universe, in the name of Thy name Thou revealed to them in trust, on account of which Thou chose them in preference over everything found in the heavens and the earths, through which Thou built their syndrome, developed to perfection their surpassing excellence in knowledge, learning and deeds, out of all the rest of the people, till their completeness surpassed the total achievement of all the people put together.
I request Thee to send blessings on Muhammad and on the children of Muhammad; (and) look into the causes of my sorrows, anxieties, burdens, and do away with them, let me be equal to, sufficiently instructed, while dealing with my important enterprises and efforts, (and) carry out and fulfil my obligations, set me free from the clutches of poverty and from the pangs of hunger, let me have enough to live upon, free from the need to turn to others.
Let me be an adequate match for him who, I am afraid, shall create trouble, thwart the schemes of the one who, I am afraid, shall treat me harshly, let him, who, I am afraid, shall vex me, come to grief, expose and rebuke him, who, I am afraid, shall slander me, layout a plan to defeat him, who, I and afraid, shall try to deceive me by stratagem, take notice and act swiftly against him, who, I am afraid, shall treat me unjustly, take me into Thy custody to keep safe from the one who, I am afraid, shall tyrannize me.
Exercise Thy supreme authority to keep him in check, who, I am afraid, shall rule over me, outwit him, who, I am afraid, shall lay traps to deceive me cunningly, make ready a fitting reply to him who, I am afraid, shall oppress me; keep off from me the obstinate deceit of the vindictive malicious, and the crafty artifice of the cunning imposter.
O my Allah put an end to the intentions of him who desires to destroy me, outwit him who makes plans, keep off from me his deceit and intrigue, his hostility and fancy, keep me aloof, refusing to have anything to do with him, in whatever manner, on any occasion, as Thou wills.
O my Allah divert his attention from me and involve him in his own over bearing anxieties which Thou will not cut down, in misfortune which Thou will not make easy to deal with, in waywardness from which Thou will not lead him back on the right path, in spiritless laziness which Thou will never change into fruitful activity, infamy and disgrace from which Thou will not allow him to rise to the surface, in broken fortune which Thou will not let get set.
O my Allah let contemptible disgrace stick like a leech on the forehead between his two eyes, expose him to emptiness in every effort he makes, let wicked inclination, distraction and inactivity cut deep into his body and soul, in order that Thou diverts his attention from, me, absorbed in his own pursuits that do not free him to employ himself at another, make him think no more of me as he has willfully neglected and forgotten Thee, take away from me and throw overboard his attention, interest, communication, influence, support, and perverted thinking every evil he commits or draws it on himself, let everything he does go waste, defective and incomplete, and do not give any letup so that he is kept fully preoccupied and gets no time to pay attention to me or think of me.
Give enough to me, O He who effectively and completely gives satisfaction. There is no one other than Thee who gives sufficiently, because Thou alone gives enough, no one else gives to the seeker’s satisfaction except Thee. Thou dispels the clouds of sorrows, no one else, save Thee can free the grief-stricken from cares. Thou comes to help, no one else, except Thee, can do a favour when called for help.
Thou art near, ready to avert the danger, no one else, save Thee, is close enough to stand by in the hour of trouble. Comes to grief he who relies upon his own friends instead of Thee; or looks for his own supporters in place of Thee; frightened, cries for help having someone else other than Thee in mind; terrorized, runs away from the danger to a place of escape outside of Thy guaranty, and takes refuge in an asylum not watched over by Thee; and tries to bring himself to a place of safety through the help of created beings, excluding Thee, the Creator, Therefore, Thou art my trust, my hope, my retreat, my refuge, my security, unto which one can run for safety, for this reason I call for thy help, and seek Thy support to carry out my affairs easily and successfully, through Muhammad and the children of Muhammad I present myself before Thee, try to draw Thy attention and ask for salvation.
So I beseech Thee, O Allah! O Allah! O Allah! Because (all) praise is for Thee (alone), and also prostration in thankfulness, thou alone listens to complaints, Thou alone is called upon for help. So I beseech Thee, O Allah! O Allah! O Allah! In the name of Muhammad and the children of Muhammad to send blessings on Muhammad and on the children of Muhammad, to put to flight the sorrows, the anxieties, the burdens, now surrounding me from all sides, just as Thou made Thy Prophet free of his sorrows, anxieties and burden, and saved him from the terror of his enemies, in such manner as Thou made him to do so, let me also do the same, let me be free of care as Thou gave him joy, heart and confidence, let me have enough and be contended as Thou gave him sufficiently.
Send away from me the terror that frightens me, the encumbrance that I find burdensome, the anxieties that wear me down, freeing me from their impact. Make me use my means freely to carry out and complete my business or employment, and do anything requisite instead of another, which may make me anxious about my affairs in this world or the hereafter.
O Ameerul-Mu’mineen! O Abaa Abdullah! So far I am live and the days and nights follow each other I invoke Allah to send blessings on you forever and ever. May Allah not make my this pledge of physical and spiritual close association with you (both) the last fulfillment, and may Allah not separate me and you (both) from one another.
O my Allah let me have a meaningful existence as Muhammad his descendants had lived, and die as they departed from this world, take to and carry out completely their creed; and raise me for the last judgment alongwith the people dedicated to them, and do not cut (us) in two (groups), even for the flash of an eyelid, ever, either in this world or in the life of the hereafter.
O Ameerul Moomineen! O Abaa Abdullah! I come nearer to (you both) to establish close physical and spiritual relationship, and thereby seek nearness to Allah, my lord Nourishes and your lord Nourisher, through you I direct myself towards Him, and request you to speak in favour of my immediate at hand needs and wants I have put before Allah.
So please speak well of me because, of course, with Allah your thoughtful judgement is highly credible, your disposition enjoys conclusive preference, your action keeps step with precise acumen, and a sure means of approach to gain His favour. I looked for and went through all possibilities and finally brought myself over to you in the hope of obtaining, speedily and successfully, fulfillment of my demands, their final settlement and favorable execution by Allah on account of your recommendations on my behalf to get Allah’s approval.
So, let not my “seeing the light” be a bitter and “fallen short” change of heart, but make my “accepting the true faith” resolution a turning point leading to preference and full satisfaction in the matter of the disposal of my wants and needs; and direct me to bring myself over to that which Allah wills and commands. (because) there is no power and no might save Allah.
I commit my case to Allah, anything neglected or taking place behind my back shall be taken care of Allah, I entrust Allah with the management of my affairs, and say: Allah is sufficient and enough, Allah pays attention to whoso makes a request to Him, there is nothing for me in that which is on the other side (in opposition) of Allah, and you, O my leaders, ultimately that which my lord Nourisher wills takes place, and happens not that which He wills not. There is no power and no might save in Allah.
Unto you (both) I have come with love and affection, motivated under Divine incentive. May Allah not make my this pledge of physical and spiritual close association with you (both) the last fulfillment. I take advantage O my leader, O Ameerul Momineen, O my friend to whom I have surrendered, body and soul, and you O Abaa Abdullah, O my leader, and pay respects with love and affection, nonstop, (till) do not carry embracing days and nights, closely embracing it, to you, if Allah so wills, without ever being separated, in your names I beseech Him, that He wills it and does it because verily it is He who alone is praised, and he brings about beautiful things.
I see the light and bring myself over to you, accepting the true faith, sorry for my earlier ignorance, thankful to Allah, always hopeful of getting His favour, never giving into despair, nor losing heart, ever ready to be near you, coming back again and again to see you, never “not longing” for your nearness, but, if Allah so wills, shall be always in your neighborhood as and everyday habit. There is no power and no might save in Allah.
O my leaders I long for you and love to be in your company though the worldly people looked at it with indifference and kept aloof from your friendly intercourse. May not Allah let me fall short of in that which I hope for and in that which I look long and attentively to establish everlasting reunion with you. Verily He is near and gives positive answer to prayers.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
