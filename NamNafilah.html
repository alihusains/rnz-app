NAFILAH

There are many Mustahab prayers which are generally called Nafilah, but more stress has been laid on the Nafilah of wajib namaz. It is as follows…

Waise to bohot se mustahab namaz hai jo nafilah kahelaati hai magar wajib namaz ki nafilah ki bohot taaqid hai aur woh is tarah se hai…

2 Rak'at before (qabl) Fajr Namaz.

8 Rak'at before (qabl) Zuhr Namaz.

8 Rak'at before (qabl) Asr Namaz.

4 Rak'at after (baad) Maghrib Namaz.

2 Rak'at after (baad) Isha Namaz (while sitting, baith kar).

As an obligatory precaution, the Nafilah for Isha prayers should be offered while sitting, and therefore its 2 Rak'ats are counted as one. But on Friday, 4 Rak'ats are added to the 16 Rak'ats of the Zuhr and the Asr Nafilah, and it is preferable that all these 20 Rak'ats are offered before the Zuhr sets in.

Ehtiyaad-e-waajib ki bina par, Nafilah-e-Isha namaz baith kar padhe, keh jo 2 rakat ek rakat ke bara-bar hai. Lekin Juma ke roz 4 rakat ka izaafa kare 16 rakat Zohar aur Asr nafila mein jis se woh 20 rakat hoti hai jise waqt-e-Zohar se pehle adaa kare.
