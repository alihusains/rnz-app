1st DAY OF MONTH

1st Day Namaz of each month…

1st Roz ki Namaz har mahine…

Recite 2 Rakat Namaz, in 1st rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1), recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) 30 times and in 2nd rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1), recite Surah Qadr (Chp.97) 30 times. After namaz the below verses of Qur’an should be recited and then Sadaqa should be given. Whoever offers this prayer each month, will win safety throughout that month.

2 Rakat namaz, jiski 1st rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 30 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) padhe aur 2nd rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 30 martaba Surah Qadr (Chp.97) padhe. Namaz ke baad niche diye huwe Qur’an ki aayate’n padhe aur phir Sadaqa de. Jo is namaz ko har mahine padhe to woh pura mahina mehfooz rahega.
