صَدَقَ اللهُ الْعَلِیُّ الْعَظِیْمُ

89 | FAJR | 30

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Qasam hai fajr ki.

2. Aur das raato ki.

3. Aur juft-o-taaq ki.

4. Aur raat ki, jab woh jaane lagey.

5. Be-shak in cheezo mein qasam hai saheb-e-aql ke liye.

6. Kya tum ne nahi dekha ke tumhaare Rab ne qaum-e-Aad ke saath kya kiya hai.

7. Sutoon waale iram (city) waale.

8. Jiska misl dusre shahero mein nahi paiyda huwa hai.

9. Aur Samood ke saath jo waadi mein patthar taraash kar makaan banaate thay.

10. Aur mikho waale Firon ke saath.

11. Jin logo ne shahero mein sar-kashi phelaayi.

12. Aur khoob fasaad kiya.

13. To phir Khuda ne un par azaab ke kodey barsa diye.

14. Be-shak tumhaara Parwardigaar zaalimo ki taak mein hai.

15. Lekin insaan ka haal yeh hai keh jab Khuda ne us ko is tarah aazmaaya keh izzat aur nemat de di to kehne laga keh mere Rab ne mujhe ba-izzat banaya hai.

16. Aur jab azmaish ke liye rozi ko tangh kar diya to kehne laga keh mere Parwardigaar ne meri tauheen ki hai.

17. Aisa harghiz nahi hai bal-keh tum yateemo ka ehteram nahi karte ho.

18. Aur logo ko miskeeno ke khaane par amaada nahi karte ho.

19. Aur meeras ke maal ko ekhatta kar ke halaal-o-haraam sab kha jaate ho.

20. Aur maal-e-duniya ko bohot dost rakhte ho.

21. Yaad rakkho keh jab zameen ko rezaa-rezaa kar diya jaayega.

22. Aur tumhaare Parwardigaar ka hukm aur farishte saf-dar-saf aa jaayege.

23. Aur Jahannam ko us din saamne laaya jaayega to insaan ko hosh aa jaayega lekin us din hosh aane ka kya faaida.

24. Insaan kahega keh kaash mein ne apni is zindagi ke liye kuch pehle bhej diya hota.

25. To us din Khuda waisa azaab karega jo kisi ne na kiya hoga.

26. Aur na is tarah kisi ne ghiraf-taar kiya hoga.

27. Ay nafs-e-mutmaeen.

28. Apne Rab ki taraf palat aa is aalam mein keh tu Us se raazi hai aur Woh tujh se raazi hai.

29. Phir mere bando mein shaamil hoja.

30. Aur meri jannat mein daakhil hoja.

89 | FAJR | 30

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. I swear by the daybreak,

2. And the ten nights,

3. And the even and the odd,

4. And the night when it departs.

5. Truly in that there is an oath for those who possess understanding.

6. Have you not considered how your Lord dealt with Ad,

7. (The people of) Aram, possessors of lofty buildings,

8. The like of which were not created in the (other) cities;

9. And (with) Samood, who hewed out the rocks in the valley,

10. And (with) Firon, the lord of hosts,

11. Who committed inordinacy in the cities,

12. So they made great mischief therein?

13. Therefore your Lord let down upon them a portion of the chastisement.

14. Most surely your Lord is watching.

15. And as for man, when his Lord tries him, then treats him with honor and makes him lead an easy life, he says: My Lord honors me.

16. But when He tries him (differently), then straitens to him his means of subsistence, he says: My Lord has disgraced me.

17. Nay! but you do not honor the orphan,

18. Nor do you urge one another to feed the poor,

19. And you eat away the heritage, devouring (everything) indiscriminately,

20. And you love wealth with exceeding love.

21. Nay! when the earth is made to crumble to pieces,

22. And your Lord comes and (also) the angels in ranks,

23. And hell is made to appear on that day. On that day shall man be mindful, and what shall being mindful (then) avail him?

24. He shall say: O! would that I had sent before for (this) my life!

25. But on that day shall no one chastise with (anything like) His chastisement,

26. And no one shall bind with (anything like) His binding.

27. O soul that art at rest!

28. Return to your Lord, well-pleased (with him), well-pleasing (Him),

29. So enter among My servants,

30. And enter into My garden.
