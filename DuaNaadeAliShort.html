NAAD-E-ALI
(Short)

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

نَادِ عَلِیًّا مَّظْھَرَ الْعَجَآئِبِ تَجِدْہٗ عَوْنًا لَّكَ فِیْ النَّوَآئِبِ كُلُّ ھَمٍّ وَّغَمٍّ سَیَنْجَلِیْ بِعَظَمَتِكَ یَااَللہُ بِنُبُوَّتِكَ یَامُحَمَّدُ بِوِلَایَتِكَ یَاعَلِیُّ یَاعَلِیُّ یَاعَلِیُّ۔ اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

NAAD-E-ALI
(Short)

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ajaaeb Khuda ke jalwo bardaar Ali ibne Abi Talib (a.s) ko jara pukaar to sahih. Tum har museebat aur balaawo mein unko apna Mushkil Kusha paawo-ge. Ay Allah Teri azmat ke waste se aur Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aap ki nabuwat ke sadqe aur Hazrat Ali (a.s) aap ki wilayat ke wasile se mere tamaam ranj-o-gham musibatein dur ho. Ya Ali! Ya Ali! Ya Ali!

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

NAAD-E-ALI
(Short)

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Call Ali, The Manifestor of Wonders Thou shalt find him a helper unto thee in thy difficulties Presently all worries and sorrows will be removed By thy Greatness O Lord By thy Apostleship o Muhammad By thy Authority O Ali, O Ali, O Ali Come to my help!

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
