2 | MAHE RAMAZAN

NAMAZ…

1) 4 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 20 martaba Surah Qadr (Chp-97) padhe.

SHAB KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay pehlo aur pechhlo ke Khuda, ay maujood rehne waalo ke Khuda aur ay guzar jaane waalo ke Khuda, ay saat (7) aasmaano aur un mein basne waalo ke Khuda, ay subh ki paw (twilight) phaad ne waale, ay raat ko baais-e-sukoon qaraar dene waale.

Ay chaand aur suraj ko auqaat (waqt) shumaari ka zariya bana ne waale. Tere hi liye hamd hai aur Tere hi liye shukr hai aur Tu hi karam o ehsaan ka maalik hai aur Tu hi taaqat o quwwat waala aur Tu hi akela be-neyaaz wa be-aiyb hai.

Ay mere Aqa o Maula mai Tujh se Teri buzurgi aur Tere kamaal-e-husn-e-sifaat ke wasile se sawaal karta hu, keh Tu Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur mere gunaho ko bakhsh de aur mujh par rahem kar aur mujh ko meri khataawo se dar-guzar farma, yaqeenan Tu bakhsh ne waala aur rahem karne waala hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Aaj ke din mujhe Apni razaawo ke qareeb kar de, aaj ke din mujhe Apni naraazghi aur Apni sazaawo se bachaaye rakh aur aaj ke din mujhe Apni aayaat padhne ki taufiq de Apni rehmat se ay sab se zeyaada rahem karne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

2 | MAHE RAMAZAN

NAMAZ…

1) Recite 4-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Qadr (Chp-97) 20 times.

NIGHT DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O the God of the formers and the laters and O the God of those who remain and the God of those who pass away. O Lord of the seven heavens and whatever is in them. O one who breaks the day and One who made the night as the time of repose and One who made the Sun and the Moon as a way of calculating the time. Praise is for You and thankfulness is for You. And only You are the beneficent and the giver of bounties. You are the One, the Unique, the self-sufficient. I ask you for the sake of Your majesty O my Chief and for the sake of Your beauty O my Master that You bless Muhammad and Aale Muhammad (a.s) and forgive me and have mercy on me and overlook my faults. Indeed You are the forgiving and merciful.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah let me come nearer to Thy benevolence in this month; let me be far off from Thy displeasure and contempt; let me grow used to the recitation of Thy verses (in the Holy Quran), through Thy mercy, O the Most Merciful.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
