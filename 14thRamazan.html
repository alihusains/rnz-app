14 | MAHE RAMAZAN

NAMAZ…

1) Perform Ghusl.
1) Ghusl kare.

2) Recite 4-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) 25 times.

2) 4 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 25 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) padhe.


3) Recite 6-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Zilzaal (Chp-99) 30 time.

3) 6 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 30 martaba Surah Zilzaal (Chp-99) padhe.


4) Recite 4 rakats namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp۔1) recite Surah Yasin (Chp۔36), Surah Mulk (Chp۔67) and Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112).

4) 4 Rakat namaz padhe, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 1 martaba Surah Yasin (Chp-36), Surah Mulk (Chp۔67) and Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) padhe


5) Recite Dua-e-Mujeer.
4) Dua-e-Mujeer padhe.

SHAB KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay tamaam auliya ke naasir o madad-gaar, ay tamaam zabardasto se zeyaada zabardast, ay tamaam guzishta logo ke Khuda Tu ne mujhe khalq kiya haala keh mai kuchh bhi na tha. Tu ne hi mujhe apni itaa’at karne ka hukm diya to mai ne bhi ba-qadr apni bardaasht se, ay mere Aaqa Teri itaa’at ki.

Pas agar Teri itaa’at mein meri taraf se kuchh susti ya bhul-chuk ho gayi, to ay mere Aaqa! Tu mujh par apna fazl kar aur meri un ummeedo ko, jo Tujh se waabasta hai qata (alag) na kar.

Aur mujh par apni rehmat se ehsaan kar aur mujh ko aur Nabi-e-Rehmat Hazrat Mohammad bin Abdullah (s.a.w.a) ko yekja (ek saath) kar de (mujhe Jannat mein manzil-e-Rasool tak pohoncha de) aur mere gunaho ko bakhsh de, yaqeenan Tu gunaho ko maaf karne waala rahim hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Aaj ke din lagzisho par meri ghiraft na farma, isme meri khataawo aur fuzool baato se dar-guzar kar, aur aaj mujh ko mushkilo aur museebato ka nishaana qaraar na de be-waaste Apni izzat ke. Ay musalmaano ki izzat.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

NIGHT DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O the helper of all the Awliya, O one who is most powerful of the powerful ones, O the God of the formers, You have created me whereas I was nothing and You ordered me obedience and I also my Chief, obeyed You according to my capacity. Thus if there had been some sloth or forgetfulness in Your obedience, have mercy on me and do not cut off my hopes that I have reposed in Thee, my Chief. Bless me with Your mercy and join me with the Prophet of mercy, Muhammad bin Abdullah, may Allah bless him and his progeny and forgive me. And you are the one who accepts repentance and one who is merciful.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah do not let indecision and confusion take hold of me, in this month, nor errors and lapses get the better of me, nor let misfortune and disaster make me its target, through Thy power, O He who makes powerful those who willingly submit to Him.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
