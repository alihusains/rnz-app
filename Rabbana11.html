Chp. 3:Ver. 53
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَآ اٰمَنَّا بِمَآ اَنْزَلْتَ وَاتَّبَعْنَا الرَّسُوْلَ فَاكْتُبْنَا مَعَ الشّٰهِدِيْنَ۔
Our Lord! we believe in what Thou hast revealed and we follow the apostle, so write us down with those who bear witness.

Parwardigaar! Ham un tamaam baato’n par imaan le aaye jo Tu ne naazil ki hai aur Tere Rasool ka itteba kiya lehaaza hamara naam apne Rasool ke gawaho’n mein darj kar le.
