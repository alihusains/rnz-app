SAAFFAAT |180-182

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

سُبْحٰنَ رَبِّكَ رَبِّ الْعِزَّةِ عَمَّا يَصِفُوْنَج۔ وَسَلٰمٌ عَلَى الْمُرْسَلِيْنَج۔ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلهِ رَبِّ الْعٰلَمِيْنَ۔

ENGLISH… Glory be to your Lord, the Lord of Honor, above what they describe. And peace be on the apostles. And all praise is due to Allah, the Lord of the worlds.

ROMAN URDU… Aap ka Parwardigaar jo maalik-e-izzat bhi hai unke bayanaat se paak-o-pakeeza hai. Aur hamara salaam tamaam mursaleen par hai. Aur saari taareef us Allah ke liye hai jo Aalameen ka Parwardigaar hai.
