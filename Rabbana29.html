Chp. 20:Ver. 145
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَآ اِنَّنَا نَخَافُ اَنْ يَّفْرُطَ عَلَيْنَآ اَوْ اَنْ يَّطْغٰى
O our Lord! Surely we fear that he may hasten to do evil to us or that he may become inordinate.

Parwardigaar! Hamein yeh khauf hai keh kahi woh ham par ziyadati na kare ya aur sarkash (rebellious) na ho jaaye.
