The Whispered Prayer of the Complainers (Shakeen)…
Shikwa karne waalo ki Munajaat…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اِلٰهِىْ اِلَيْكَ اَشْكُوْ نَفْسًۢا بِالسُّوْ ٓءِ اَمَّارَةً وَاِلَى الْخَطِيْٓئَةِ مُبَادِرَةً وَبِمَعَاصِيْكَ مُوْلَعَةً وَ لِسَخَطِكَ مُتَعَرِّضَةً۔ تَسْلُكُ بِىْ مَسَالِكَ الْمَهَالِكِ وَتَجْعَلُنِىْ عِنْدَكَ اَهْوَنَ هَالِكٍ كَثِيْرَةَ الْعِلَلِ طَوِيْلَةَ الْاَمَلِ اِنْ مَسَّهَا الشَّرُّ تَجْزَعُ وَاِنْ مَسَّهَا الْخَيْرُ تَمْنَعُ مَيَّالَةً اِلَى اللَّعَبِ وَاللَّهْوِ مَمْلُوَّةًۢ بِالْغَفْلَةِ وَالسَّهْوِ تُسْرِعُ بِىْ اِلَى الْحَوْبَةِ وَتُسَوِّفُنِىْ بِالتَّوْبَةِ۔

اِلٰهِىْٓ اَشْكُوْ اِلَيْكَ عَدُوًّا يُضِلُّنِىْ وَشَيْطَانًا يُغْوِنِىْ۔ قَدْ مَلَاَ بِالْوَسْوَاسِ صَدْرِىْ وَاَحَاطَتْ هَوَاجِسُهٗ بِقَلْبِىْ۔ يُعَاضِدُ لِىَ الْهَوٰى وَيُزَيِّـنُ لِىْ حُبَّ الدُّنْيَا وَيَحُوْلُ بَيْنِىْ وَبَيْنَ الطَّاعَةِ وَالزُّلْفٰى۔

اِلٰهِىْٓ اِلَيْكَ اَشْكُوْ قَلْبًا قَاسِيًا مَّعَ الْوَسْوَاسِ مُتَقَلِّبًا وَبِالرَّيْنِ وَالطَّبْعِ مُتَلَبِّسًا وَعَيْنًا عَنِ الْبُكَآءِ مِنْ خَوْفِكَ جَامِدَةً وَاِلٰى مَا يَسُرُّهَا طَامِحَةً۔

اِلٰهِىْ لَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ اِلَّا بِقُدْرَتِكَ وَلَا نَجَاةَ لِىْ مِنْ مَكَارِهِ الدُّنْيَا اِلَّا بِعِصْمَتِكَ۔ فَاَسْئَلُكَ بِبَلَاغَةِ حِكْمَتِكَ وَنَفَاذِ مَشِيَّتِكَ اَنْ لَّا تَجْعَلَنِىْ لِغَيْرِجُوْدِكَ مُتَعَرِّضًا وَلَا تُصَيِّرَنِىْ لِلْفِتَنِ غَرَضًا۔

وَكُنْ بِىْ عَلَى الْاَعْدَآءِ نَاصِرًا وَّعَلَى الْمَخَازِىْ وَالْعُيُوْبِ سَاتِرًا وَّمِنَ الْبَلَآءِ وَاقِيًا وَعَنِ الْمَعَاصِىْ عَاصِمًۢا بِرَافَتِكَ وَرَحْمَتِكَ يَآاَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِيْنَ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

Shakeen
(Complainers)

Shikwa karne waalo ki Munajaat…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Mai Tujh se apne nafs ki shikaayat (complain) karta hu jo buraayi par uksaane waala aur Khata ki taraf jald badhne waala hai. Woh Teri na-farmani ka shayeq (shauq rakhne waala) aur Teri naraazghi se takkar leta hai (naraazghi ka saamna karta hai). Woh mujhe tabaahi ki raaho’n par le jaata hai aur usne mujhe Tere saamne zaleel aur bad-haal (khasta-haal) bana diya hai. Yeh bada bahaana-saaz aur lambi ummeedo waala hai. Agar usey takleef ho to faryaad karta hai, aur agar aaraam pohonche to chup rehta hai. Woh khel-tamaashe ki taraf zeyaada maayel aur ghaflat o bhool-chuk se bhaara pada hai. Mujhe tezi se gunah ki taraf le jaata hai aur tauba karne mein taakhir (der) karta hai.

Mere Mabood! Mai Tujh se shikaayat karta hu us dushman (shaitaan) ki jo gumraah karta hai aur Shaitaan (iblis) ki jo behkaata hai. Usne mere seeneh (chest) ko boore khayaalo se bhar diya, uski khwahisho (chaahato) ne mere dil ko gher liya hai. Buri khwaahisho ki takmil mein madad karta hai aur duniya ki mohabbat ko achchha bana kar dikhaata hai. Woh mere aur Teri bandagi aur taqarrub ke darmiyaan haayel ho gaya hai.

Ay Mabood! Mai Tujh se dil ki sakhti ki shikaayat karta hu aur musalsal (continuously) waswaso ki shikaayat karta hu. Jo zang o teerghi se aaluda hai. Us aankh ki shikaayat karta hu jo Tere khauf mein girya nahi karti aur jo cheez achchhi laghe ussey khush hai.

Ay Mabood! Nahi hai mere liye koi chaara (taaqat) o quwat magar jo Teri qudrat se milti hai. Mai bachh nahi sakta duniya ki buraaiyo se magar sirf Teri nighah-daasht (security) se.

Pas Tujh se sawaal karta hu Teri ghehri (deep) hikmat aur Teri puri hone waali marzi ke waaste se, keh mujhe Apni bakhshish ke siwa kisi taraf na jaane de. Aur mujhe fitno ka hadaf (target) na banne de. Aur dushmano ke muqaabil mera madad-gaar ban, mere aibo’n aur ruswaaiyo ki parda-poshi farma. Mujh se museebatein dur kar. Aur gunaho se bachaaye rakh, Apni rehmat o nawaazish se, Ay sab se zeyaada rahem karne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

The Whispered Prayer of the Complainers (Shakeen)…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

My God, to Thee I complain of a soul commanding to evil, rushing to offenses, eager to disobey Thee, and exposing itself to Thy anger. It takes me on the roads of disasters, it makes me the easiest of perishers before Thee; many its pretexts, drawn out its expectations; when evil touches it, it is anxious, when good touches it, grudging; inclining to sport and diversion, full of heedlessness and inattention, it hurries me to misdeeds and makes me delay repentance.

My God, I complain to Thee of an enemy who misguides me and a satan who leads me astray. He has filled my breast with tempting thoughts, and his suggestions have encompassed my heart. He supports caprice against me, embellishes for me the love of this world, and separates me from obedience and proximity!

My God, to Thee I complain of a heart that is hard, turned this way and that by tempting thoughts, clothed in rust and the seal, and of an eye too indifferent to weep in fear of Thee and eagerly seeking that which gladdens it!

My God, there is no force and no strength except in Thy power, and no deliverance for me from the detested things of this world save through Thy preservation. So I ask Thee by Thy far-reaching wisdom and Thy penetrating will not to let me expose myself to other than Thy munificence and not to turn me into a target for trials! Be for me a helper against enemies, a coverer of shameful things and faults, a protector against afflictions, a preserver against acts of disobedience! By Thy clemency and mercy, O Most Merciful of the merciful.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
