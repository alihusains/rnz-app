SHAB EID-UL-FITR

It is narrated from the Messenger of Allah (s.a.w.a) that one who keeps awake this night, his heart will not die on the day that all hearts are dead due to fear.
Allah ke Rasool (s.a.w.a) se waarid hai, keh jo shakhs Shab-e-Eid jaagta rahega, to uska dil murda nahi hoga us din jab sab dil murda ho jaayege khauf ki bina par.

1) Two Ghusls: One at the time of sunset and the other at dawn.
1) 2 Ghusl: Ek Maghrib se pehle aur dusra Subhe Sadiq.

2) One should recite below Takbeers after Maghrib, Isha, Fajr and Eid prayers…
2) Is Takbeer ko Maghrib, Isha, Fajr aur Eid ki namaaz ke baad padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللہُ اَکْبَرُ اَللہُ اَکْبَرُ لَااِلٰہَ اِلَّااللہُ وَاللہُ اَکْبَرُ۔ اَللہُ اَکْبَرُ وَلِلہِ الْحَمْدُ۔ اَللہُ اَکْبَرُ عَلٰی مَا ھٰدَانَا وَلَہُ الشُّکْرُ عَلٰی مَا اَوْلٰینَا۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

3) Dua after the Maghrib Namaz and Nafila…
3) Namaz Maghrib aur Nafila ke baad yeh dua padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

یَاذَالْمَنِّ وَالطَّوْلِ یَاذَالْجُوْدِ یَامُصْطَفِیَ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّنَاصِرَہٗ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔ وَاغْفِرْلِیْ کُلَّ ذَنْۢبٍ اَحْصَیْتَہٗ وَھُوَعِنْدَکَ فِیْ کِتَابٍۢ مُبِیْن۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

After that go into Sajdah recite 100 times and then ask for your needs from Allah(s.w.t)…
Uske baad Sajdeh mein jaaye aur 100 martaba kahe. Phir Allah (s.w.t) se apni haajat talab kare…

اَتُوْبُ اِلَی اللہِ۔

4) Dua to be recited in Sajdah after Maghrib Namaz…
4) Namaz Maghrib ke baad Sajdeh mein yeh dua padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

يَاذَا الْحَوْلِ يَاذَا الطَّوْلِ يَامُصْطَفِيَ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّنَاصِرَهٗ صَلِّ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَاغْفِرْ لِيْ كُلَّ ذَنْۢبٍ اَذْنَبْتُهٗ وَنَسَيْتُهٗ اَنَا وَهُوَ عِنْدَكَ فِيْ كِتَابٍ مُّبِيْنٍ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

After that go into Sajdah recite 100 times and then ask for your needs from Allah (s.w.t)…
Uske baad Sajdeh mein jaaye aur 100 martaba kahe. Phir Allah (s.w.t) se apni haajat talab kare…

اَتُوْبُ اِلَی اللہِ۔

5) 10 Martaba padhe…

یَادَآئِمَ الْفَضْلِ عَلٰی الْبَرِیّۃِ یَابَاسِطَ الْیَدَیْنِ بِالْعَطِیَّۃِ یَاصَاحِبَ الْمَوَاھِبِ السَّنِیَّۃِ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِہٖ خَیْرَ الْوَرٰی سَجِیَّۃً وَّاغْفِرْلَنَا یَاذَالْعُلٰی فِیْ ھٰذِہِ الْعَشِیَّۃِ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

6) Recite 14-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Ayatul Khursi 1 time.
6) 14 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 1 martaba Ayatul Khursi padhe.

7) Imam Ali (a.s) used to recite 2-rakat namaz. In 1st rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) 1000 times Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112). In 2nd rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) 1 time Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112). After namaz he used to go into Sajdah and say 100 time…
7) Imam Ali (a.s) 2-rakat namaz adaa karte thay. 1st rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 1000 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112). Aur 2nd rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 1 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112). Namaz ke baad woh Sajdeh mein ja kar 100 martaba yeh zikr padhte thay…

اَتُوْبُ اِلَی اللہِ۔

Thereafter he used to recite the following Dua…
Uske ke baad woh is dua ko padhte thay…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

یَاذَالْمَنِّ وَالْجُوْدِ یَاذَالْمَنِّ وَالطَّوْلِ یَامُصْطَفِیَ مُحَمَّدٍصَلَّی اللهُ عَلَیْہِ وَاٰلِہٖ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِہٖ وَافْعَلْ بِیْ کَذَاکَذَا۔

Thereafter he invocated Allah (s.w.t) for his needs.
Phir woh Allah (s.w.t) se apni haajat talab karte thay.

SHAB EID-UL-FITR

Allah ke Rasool (s.a.w.a) se waarid hai, keh jo shakhs Shab-e-Eid jaagta rahega, to uska dil murda nahi hoga us din jab sab dil murda ho jaayege khauf ki bina par.

1) 2 Ghusl: pehla Maghrib se pehle aur dusra Subhe Sadiq.

2) Is Takbeer ko Maghrib, Isha, Fajr aur Eid ki namaaz ke baad padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Allah bada hai. Allah bada hai. Nahi hai koi Khuda siwa Allah ke. Allah bada hai. Allah bada hai. Saari taarif Allah ke liye hai. Allah bada hai keh Us ne hamari rehnumaayi ki hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

3) Namaz Maghrib aur Nafila ke baad yeh dua padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay saaheb-e-ehsaan o karam, ay maalik-e-jud o ataa. Ay paigambar ko muntakhab karne waale aur unki madad karne waale. Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur hamare un tamaam gunaho ko bakhsh de jinka Tu ne shumaar kar rakkha hai aur Tere paas kitab-e-Mubin mein mehfooz hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

Uske baad Sajdeh mein jaaye aur 100 martaba kahe. Phir Allah (s.w.t) se apni haajat talab kare…

Mai Khuda ki bargah mein tauba karta hu.

4) Namaz Maghrib ke baad Sajdeh mein yeh dua padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay harkat dene waale, ay sakhawat waale, ay Hazrat Mohammad Mustafa (s.a.w.a) ko muntakhab karne waale aur unke haami, rehmat naazil farma Sarkar-e-Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Mohammad (a.s) par aur mere woh sabhi gunah bakhsh de jo mai ne kiye aur unhe bhul gaya hu keh mai aur woh gunah Teri roshan kitab mein darj-shuda hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

Uske baad Sajdeh mein jaaye aur 100 martaba kahe…

Tauba karta hu pesh-e-Khuda.

Phir Allah (s.w.t) se apni haajat talab kare.

5) 10 Martaba padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.
