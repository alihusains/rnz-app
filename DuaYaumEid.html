YAUM-UL-EID
Supplication to be recited on the Day of Eid…
Is Dua ko Eid ke din padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

يَامَنْ يَّرْحَمُ مَنْ لَّا يَرْحَمُهُ الْعِبَادُ وَيَا مَنْ يَّقْبَلُ مَنْ لَّا تَقْبَلُهُ الْبِلَادُ۔ وَيَا مَنْ لَّا يَحْتَقِرُ اَهْلَ الْحَاجَةِ اِلَيْهِ۔ وَيَا مَنْ لَّا يُخَيِّبُ الْمُلِحِّيْنَ عَلَيْهِ۔ وَيَا مَنْ لَّا يَجْبَهُ بِالرَّدِّ اَهْلَ الدَّالَّةِ عَلَيْهِ۔ وَيَا مَنْ يَّجْتَبِيْ صَغِيْرَ مَا يُتْحَفُ بِهٖ، وَيَشْكُرُ يَسِيْرَ مَا يُعْمَلُ لَهٗ۔ وَيَا مَنْ يَّشْكُرُ عَلَى الْقَلِيْلِ وَيُجَازِيْ بِالْجَلِيْلِ وَيَا مَنْ يَّدْنُوْ اِلٰى مَنْ دَنَا مِنْهُ۔ وَيَا مَنْ يَّدْعُوْ اِلٰى نَفْسِهٖ مَنْ اَدْبَرَ عَنْهُ۔ وَيَا مَنْ لَّا يُغَيِّرُ النِّعْمَةَ، وَلَا يُبَادِرُ بِالنَّقِمَةِ۔ وَيَا مَنْ يُّثْمِرُ الْحَسَنَةَ حَتّٰى يُنْمِيَهَا، وَيَتَجَاوَزُ عَنِ السَّيِّئَةِ حَتّٰى يُعَفِّيَهَا۔ اِنْصَرَفَتِ الْاٰمَالُ دُوْنَ مَدٰى كَرَمِكَ بِالْحَاجَاتِ، وَامْتَلَاَتْ بِفَيْضِ جُوْدِكَ اَوْعِيَةُ الطَّلِبَاتِ، وَتَفَسَّخَتْ دُوْنَ بُلُوْغِ نَعْتِكَ الصِّفَاتُ، فَلَكَ الْعُلُوُّ الْاَعْلٰى فَوْقَ كُلِّ عَالٍ، وَالْجَلَالُ الْاَمْجَدُ فَوْقَ كُلِّ جَلَالٍ۔ كُلُّ جَلِيْلٍ عِنْدَكَ صَغِيْرٌ، وَكُلُّ شَرِيْفٍ فِيْ جَنْبِ شَرَفِكَ حَقِيْرٌ، خَابَ الْوَافِدُوْنَ عَلٰى غَيْرِكَ، وَخَسِرَ الْمُتَعَرِّضُوْنَ اِلَّا لَكَ، وَضَاعَ الْمُلِمُّوْنَ اِلَّا بِكَ، وَاجْدَبَ الْمُنْتَجِعُوْنَ اِلَّا مَنِ انْتَجَعَ فَضْلَكَ بَابُكَ مَفْتُوْحٌ لِلرَّاغِبِيْنَ، وَجُوْدُكَ مُبَاحٌ لِلسَّآئِلِيْنَ، وَاِغَاثَتُكَ قَرِيْبَةٌ مِّنَ الْمُسْتَغِيْثِيْنَ۔ لَا يَخِيْبُ مِنْكَ الْامِلُوْنَ، وَلَا يَيْاسُ مِنْ عَطَآئِكَ الْمُتَعَرِّضُوْنَ، وَلَا يَشْقٰى بِنَقِمَتِكَ الْمُسْتَغْفِرُوْنَ۔ رِزْقُكَ مَبْسُوْطٌ لِمَنْ عَصَاكَ، وَحِلْمُكَ مُعْتَرِضٌ لِمَنْ نَاوَاكَ، عَادَتُكَ الْاِحْسَانُ اِلَى الْمُسِيْئِيْنَ، وَسُنَّتُكَ الْاَبْقَاءُ عَلَى الْمُعْتَدِيْنَ حَتّٰى لَقَدْ غَرَّتْهُمْ اِنَاتُكَ عَنِ الرُّجُوْعِ، وَصَدَّهُمْ اِمْهَالُكَ عَنِ النُّزُوْعِ۔ وَاِنَّمَا تَانَّيْتَ بِهِمْ لِيَفِيْئُوًا اِلٰى اَمْرِكَ، وَامْهَلْتَهُمْ ثِقَةًۢ بِدَوَامِ مُلْكِكَ، فَمَنْ كَانَ مِنْ اَهْلِ السَّعَادَةِ خَتَمْتَ لَهٗ بِهَا، وَمَنْ كَانَ مِنْ اَهْلِ الشَّقَاوَةِ خَذَلْتَهٗ لَهَا۔ كُلُّهُمْ صَآئِرُوْنَ ، اِلٰى حُكْمِكَ، وَاُمُوْرُهُمْ اَئِلَةٌ اِلٰى اَمْرِكَ، لَمْ يَهِنْ عَلٰى طُوْلِ مُدَّتِهِمْ سُلْطَانُكَ، وَلَمْ يَدْحَضْ لِتَرْكِ مُعَاجَلَتِهِمْ بُرْهَانُكَ۔ حُجَّتُكَ قَآئِمَةٌ لَا تُدْحَضُ، وَسُلْطَانُكَ ثَابِتٌ لَا يَزُوْلُ، فَالْوَيْلُ الدَّآئِمُ لِمَنْ جَنَحَ عَنْكَ، وَالْخَيْبَةُ الْخَاذِلَةُ لِمَنْ خَابَ مِنْكَ، وَالشَّقَآءُ الْاَشْقٰى لِمَنِ اغْتَرَّ بِكَ۔ مَا اَكْثَرَ تَصَرُّفَهٗ فِيْ عَذَابِكَ، وَمَا اَطْوَلَ تَرَدُّدَهٗ فِيْ عِقَابِكَ، وَمَا اَبْعَدَ غَايَتَهٗ مِنَ الْفَرَجِ، وَمَا اَقْنَطَهُ مِنْ سُهُوْلَةِ الْمَخْرَجِ عَدْلًا مِّنْ قَضَآئِكَ لَا تَجُوْرُ فِيْهِ، وَاِنْصَافًا مِنْ حُكْمِكَ لَا تَحِيْفُ عَلَيْهِ۔ فَقَدْ ظَاهَرْتَ الْحُجَجَ، وَابْلَيْتَ الْاعْذَارَ، وَقَدْ تَقَدَّمْتَ بِالْوَعِيْدِ، وَتَلَطَّفْتَ فِي التَّرْغِيْبِ، وَضَرَبْتَ الْاِمْثَالَ، وَاطَلْتَ الْامْهَالَ، وَاَخَّرْتَ وَاَنْتَ مُسْتَطِيْعٌ لِلْمُعَاجَلَةِ، وَتَانَّيْتَ وَاَنْتَ مَلِيْٓ‏ءٌۢ بِالْمُبَادَرَةِ لَمْ تَكُنْ اَنَاتُكَ عَجْزًا، وَلَا اَمْهَالُكَ وَهْنًا، وَلَا اَمْسَاكُكَ غَفْلَةً، وَلَا اِنْتِظَارُكَ مُدَارَاةً، بَلْ لِتَكُوْنَ حُجَّتُكَ اَبْلَغَ، وَكَرَمُكَ اَكْمَلَ، وَاِحْسَانُكَ اَوْفٰى، وَنِعْمَتُكَ اَتَمَّ، كُلُّ ذٰلِكَ كَانَ وَلَمْ تَزَلْ ، وَهُوَ كَآئِنٌ وَّلَا تَزَالُ۔ حُجَّتُكَ اَجَلُّ مِنْ اَنْ تُوْصَفَ بِكُلِّهَا، وَمَجْدُكَ اَرْفَعُ مِنْ اَنْ يُّحَدَّ بِكُنْهِهٖ، وَنِعْمَتُكَ اَكْثَرُ مِنْ اَنْ تُحْصٰى بِاَسْرِهَا، وَاِحْسَانُكَ اَكْثَرُ مِنْ اَنْ تُشْكَرَ عَلٰى اَقَلِّهٖ وَقَدْ قَصَّرَ بِيَ السُّكُوْتُ عَنْ تَحْمِيْدِكَ، وَفَهَّهَنِيَ الْاِمْسَاكُ عَنْ تَمْجِيْدِكَ، وَقُصَارَايَ الْاِقْرَارُ بِالْحُسُوْرِ، لَا رَغْبَةً۔ يَااِلٰهِيْ بَلْ عَجْزًا۔ فَهَا اَنَا ذَا اَؤُمُّكَ بِالْوِفَادَةِ، وَاَسْاَلُكَ حُسْنَ الرِّفَادَةِ، فَصَلِّ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِهٖ، وَاَسْمَعْ نَجْوَايَ، وَاَسْتَجِبْ دُعَآئِيْ، وَلَا تَخْتِمْ يَوْمِيْ بِخَيْبَتِيْ، وَلَا تَجْبَهْنِيْ بِالرَّدِّ فِيْ مَسْاَلَتِيْ، وَاَكْرِمْ مِنْ عِنْدِكَ مُنْصَرَفِي، وَاِلَيْكَ مُنْقَلَبِيْ، اِنَّكَ غَيْرُ ضَآئِقٍۢ بِمَا تُرِيْدُ، وَلَا عَاجِزٍ عَمَّا تُسْاَلُ، وَاَنْتَ عَلٰى كُلِّ شَيْٓ‏ءٍ قَدِيْرٌ، وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ اِلَّا بِاللهِ الْعَلِيِّ الْعَظِيْمِ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

YAUM-UL-EID

Is Dua ko Juma aur Eid-ul-Fitr ke din padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay woh jo aise shakhs par rahem karta hai, jis par bande rahem nahi karte. Ay woh jo aise (gunah-gaar) ko qabool karta hai jise koi zameen ke kisi bhi hisse mein gunaho’n ki wajah se qabool nahi karta. Ay woh jo Apne haajat-mand ko haqeer nahi samajhta. Ay woh jo gid-gidaane waale ko khaali haath nahi pherta (radd nahi karta). Ay woh jo beja (fuzool) maang karne waalo’n ko bhi nahi thukraata. Ay woh jo chote se chote tohfe ko bhi pyaar ki nazaro’n se dekhta hai aur jo maamuli se maamuli amal Uske liye baja laaya gaya ho uski bhi jaza deta hai.

Ay woh jo Usse qareeb ho Woh usse qareeb hota hai aur jo Usse mooh modhe usey bhi Apni taraf bulaata hai. Ay woh jo nemat ko badalta nahi na saza dene mein jaldi karta hai. Ay woh jo nekiyo’n ko badha-ta hai aur gunaho’n ko maaf karta hai ta-keh unhe nabood kar de, ummeede’n Teri karam ki hado’n ko chuhne se pehle hi kaamyaab-o-kaamraan ho kar palat aaye aur talab, aarzoo, maang ke pyaale (mug) Tere faiz-o-karam se chalak uthay aur achchaaiya’n Tere kamaal-e-zaat ki manzil tak pohonchne se phail gayi.

Is liye keh buland se buland tareen aur buzurg se buzurg tareen azmat Tere liye makhsoos hai. Har buzurgi Teri buzurgi ke saamne choti hai aur har saaheb-e-sharaf Tere sharaf ke saamne haqeer hai. Jisne Tere alaawa kisi aur ki taraf rukh kiya woh na-kaam huwa. Jinho ne Tere siwa dusro’n se talab kiya woh nuksaan mein rahe. Jinho ne Tere siwa dusro’n ke yahan manzil ki woh tabah huwe. Jo Tere fazl-o-karam ke bajaaye dusro’n se rizq-o-nemat ke talab-gaar huwe woh qahet (famine) aur museebat mein phas gaye.

Tera darwaaza talab karne waalo’n ke liye khula hai aur Tera jud-o-karam saahilo’n ke liye aam hai. Tu fariyaad karne waalo’n ki fariyaad ko jaldi pohonchta hai. Ummeed-waar Tujh se mehroom nahi rehte aur maangne waale Teri ataa-o-bakhshish se kabhi maayus nahi hote aur maghferat chahne waalo’n par Tera azaab kabhi naazil nahi hota. Tera dastarkhwan-e-nemat unke liye bhi bichha huwa hai jo Teri na-farmaani karte hai aur Tera sabr, burdbaari unke bhi aade aati (faaide-mand) hai jo Tujh se dushmani rakhte hai.

Buro’n se neki karna Tera kaam aur sarkasho’n par, gunah-gaaro’n par maher-baani lutf-o-karam karna Tera tarika hai. Yahan tak-keh narmi aur hilmaane unhe (haq ki taraf) rujoo hone se ghaafil kar diya, aur Teri di huwi mohallat ne gunaho’n se bachne se rok diya haala’n keh Tu ne unse narmi is liye ki thi keh woh Tere farmaan ki taraf laut aaye aur mohallat is liye di thi keh Tujhe apne tasallut aur hukumat ki taaqat-o-quwwat aur hameshgi ke upar bharosa tha keh jab chahe unhe Apni giraft mein le sakta tha.

Ab jo khush-naseeb tha uska khaatema bhi khush-naseebi par kiya aur jo be-naseeb tha woh na-kaam huwa, (woh khush-naseeb ho ya bad-naseeb) sab ke sab Teri taraf palat ne waale hai, sab ke sab Teri bargah mein haazir hone waale hai, aur unka faisla Tere hukm se waabasta hai, unki lambhi-chaudi mohalat ki mudad se Teri daleel-o-hujjat mein kamzori nahi aati jaise us shakhs ki daleel kamzor ho jaati hai jo apne haq ke haasil karne mein deri (late) kare aur fauri giraft ko nazar-andaaz karne se Teri hujjat-o-burhaan baatil qaraar nahi paate.

Yeh kaha jaaye keh agar Uske paas unke khilaaf daleel burhaan hoti to mohalat kyu’n deta, Teri hujjat bar-qaraar hai jo baatil nahi ho sakti, Teri daleel mohkam aur mazboot hai jo khatm nahi ho sakti, lehaaza hamesha ka hasrat-o-gham us shakhs ke liye hai jo Tujh se mooh modh le aur ruswa karne waali ruswaayi uske liye hai jo Tere yahan se mehroom rahe, aur bad-tareen bad-bakhti uske liye hai jisne Teri (chashm-poshi) se dhoka khaaya.

Aisa shakhs kis qadar Tere azaab mein ulat-ta palat-ta rahega aur kitne lambhe zamaane tak Tere azaab mein ghumta rahega, uski rehaayi aur azaadi ka marhala kitna dur hai, aur aasaani ke saath najaat haasil karne ke liye kitna maayoos hoga, aur yeh Tera faisla adl-o-insaaf ki wajah se hai jis mein zarra baraabar zulm ka naam-o-nishan nahi aur Tera yeh hukm insaaf ke saath hai jis mein zarra baraabar zulm-o-ziyadati nahi.

Is liye keh Tu ne baar-baar ek ke baad ek daleele’n qaayem aur kaabil-e-qabool hujjate’n zaahir kar di aur phele se daraane waali cheezo’n ke zariye se aagah kar diya aur lutf-o-maher-baani se (aakherat ki) yaad dilaayi aur tarah-tarah ki misaale bayaan ki, mohalat ki mudad bada di aur azaab mein deri se kaam liya, haala’n ki Tu fauri giraft yaani jaldi se faisla karne ka ikhteyaar rakhta tha, phir bhi narmi se kaam liya jab keh Tu jaldi karne par qudrat rakhta tha.

Yeh Teri narmi kisi aajeziyo’n pareshani ki bina par nahi aur mohalat dena kisi kamzori ki wajah se nahi aur azaab mein deri karna kisi ghaflat aur be-khabri ki wajah se nahi, bal-keh yeh taakhir-e-narmi Tere lutf-o-karam ki bina par thi bal-keh yeh sab is liye tha keh Teri hujjat har tarah se puri ho jaaye.

Tera karam kaamil, Tera ehsaan bohot ziyada, Teri nemate’n mukammal, yeh tamaam cheeze’n thi aur rahegi aur Tu hamesha se hai aur Tu hamesha rahega, Teri hujjat aur daleele’n isse baala-tar hai keh uske tamaam gosho’n ko, ishaaro’n ko puri tarah se bayaan kiya ja sake’n, aur Teri izzat-o-buzurgi se bhi buland-tar hai keh uski haqeeqat ki hade’n qaayem ki jaaye’n aur Teri nemate’n usse bhi ziyada hai keh unka shumaar ho sake’n, aur Tere ehsaan isse kahin ziyada-tar hai keh un mein se chote se chote ehsaan par bhi Tera shukriya adaa kiya ja sake’n.

Mai Teri hamd-o-sana taarif-o-tausif se aajiz aur laachar hu, khaamoshi ne Teri baar-baar hamd-o-sana se mujhe kamzor kar diya hai aur rukaawato’n ne Teri taarif-o-tausif se mujhe gungga kar diya hai. Is sil-sile mein meri taaqat-o-tawaanayi ki hadd (limit) yeh hai keh apni be-basi aur laachaari ka eteraaf karu, yeh be-raghbati aur be-tawajjohi ki wajah se nahi hai.

Ay mere Mabood! yeh sab kamzori aur laachari na-tawaani ki wajah se hai, achha to mai ab Teri bargah mein haazir hone ka iraada karta hu aur Tujh se achchi madad ki khwaaish karta hu, to Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma, meri raaz-o-neyaaz ki baato’n ko soon, meri dua’o ko qabooliyat ka sharaf ataa kar, mere din ko na-kaami ke saath khatm na kar, mere sawaal mein mujhe khaali haath na pher, aur apni bargah se palat ne aur phir palat ke aane ko izzat-o-ehteraam se qareeb-tar kar.

Is liye keh Tujhe Tere iraade mein koi dushwaari nahi hoti aur jo cheez Tujh se talab ki jaaye uske dene se Tu aajiz nahi hota, aur Tu har cheez par qaadir hai, Tu to qaadir-e-mutlaq hai, koi quwwat-o-taaqat nahi siwaye Allah ke sahare ki keh jo buland martaba wa azeem hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

YAUM-UL-EID

Supplication to be recited on the Day of Eid…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O He who has mercy upon him toward whom the servants show no mercy!

O He who accepts him whom the cities will not accept!

O He who looks not down upon those who have need of Him!

O He who disappoints not those who implore Him!

O He who slaps not the brow of the people of boldness toward Him with rejection!

O He who collects the little that is given to Him and shows gratitude for the paltry that is done for Him!

O He who shows gratitude for the small and rewards with the great!

O He who comes close to him who comes close to Him!

O He who invites to Himself him who turns his back on Him!

O He who changes not favour196 and rushes not to vengeance!

O He who causes the good deed to bear fruit so that He may make it grow, and overlooks the evil deed so that He may efface it!

Hopes turn back with needs fulfilled short of the extent of Thy generosity, the cups of requests fill up with the overflow of Thy munificence, and attributes fall apart without reaching Thy description. For to Thee belongs the highest highness above everything high, and the most glorious majesty beyond every majesty!

Everything majestic before Thee is small, everything eminent beside Thy eminence vile! Those who reach other than Thee are disappointed, those who present themselves to other than Thee have lost, those who stay with other than Thee have perished, and those who retreat - except those who retreat to Thy bounty are desolate!

Thy door is open to the beseechers, Thy munificence free to the askers, Thy help near to the help-seekers!

The expectant are not disappointed by Thee, those who present themselves despair not of Thy bestowal, the forgiveness-seekers become not wretched through Thy vengeance!

Thy provision is spread among those who disobey Thee, Thy clemency presents itself to those hostile toward Thee, Thy habit is beneficence toward the evildoers, and Thy wont is to spare the transgressors, so much so that Thy lack of haste deludes them from returning, and Thy disregard bars them from desisting!

Thou actest without haste toward them so that they will come back to Thy command and Thou disregardest them confident in the permanence of Thy kingdom, so Thou sealest him who is worthy of it with felicity, and Thou abandonest him who is worthy of it to wretchedness!

All of them come home to Thy decree, their affairs revert to Thy command; Thy authority grows not feeble through their drawn out term, Thy proof is not refuted by the failure to hurry after them.

Thy argument is established, never refuted, Thy authority fixed, never removed. Permanent woe belongs to him who inclines away from Thee, forsaking disappointment to him who is disappointed by Thee, and the most wretched wretchedness to him who is deluded about Thee!

How much he will move about in Thy chastisement! How long he will frequent Thy punishment! How far his utmost end from relief! How he will despair of an easy exit! [All of this] as justice from Thy decree (Thou art not unjust in it!), and equity from Thy judgement (Thou dost not act wrongfully against him!).

Thou supported the arguments, tested the excuses, began with threats, showed gentleness with encouragement, struck similitudes, made long the respite, delayed, while Thou art able to hurry, and acted without haste, while Thou art full of quick accomplishment!

Not because of incapacity is Thy slowness, feebleness Thy giving respite, heedlessness Thy showing restraint, dissemblance Thy waiting! But that Thy argument be more conclusive, Thy generosity more perfect, Thy beneficence more exhaustive, Thy favour more complete! All of this has been and always was, is and ever will be.

Thy argument is greater than that its totality be described, Thy glory more elevated than that it be limited in its core, Thy favour more abundant than that its entirety be counted, Thy beneficence more abundant than that thanks be given for its least amount!

Speechlessness has made me fall short of praising Thee, restraint has made me powerless to glorify Thee, and the most I can do is admit to inability, not out of desire, my God, but out of incapacity.

So here I am: I repair to Thee by coming forward, and I ask from Thee good support So bless Muhammad and his Household, hear my whispered words, grant my supplication, seal not my day with disappointment, slap not my brow by rejecting my request, and make noble my coming from Thee and my going back to Thee! Surely Thou art not constrained by what Thou desirest, nor incapable of what Thou art asked! Thou art powerful over everything, and 'There is no force and no strength save in God, the All-high, the All-mighty!'

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
