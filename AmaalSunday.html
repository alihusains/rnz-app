SUNDAY

Sunday has been assigned to Imam Ali (a.s) and Fatema Zahra (s.a).
Sunday Imam Ali (a.s) aur Fatema Zahra (s.a) se mansub hai.

NAQSH…
One who looks at this Naqsh on Sunday will be safe from Hell fire, work to be made easier, to be held in high esteem and enemies to be destroyed.

Jo shakhs is Naqsh ko baroz Sunday dekhe to woh Jahannam ki aag se mehfuz rahega, kaam ki aasaani, buland darjaat aur dushmano ki naboodi hogi.

ZIKR…
Recite 1000 times…
1000 Martaba padhe…

یَاذَالْجَلَالِ وَالْاِکْرَامِ

YA ZAL JALAALE WAL IKRAAM
O! The Lord of Glory and Honor.
Ay Jalaalat aur Karam Waale.

NAMAZ…
Recite 4-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite Surah Mulk (Chp.67) 1 time each.
4 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 1 martaba Surah Mulk (Chp.67) padhe.

DUA…
Recite Sunday Dua.
Sunday ki Dua padhe.

ZIYARAT…
Recite Sunday Ziyarat.
Sunday ki Ziyarat padhe.
