MUBAHILA

The event of Mubahila took place on 24th Zilhijjah in the 9th year of Hijra. A deputation of 14 Christian (najran) scholars came to discuss the truth of Islam with the Holy Prophet (s.a.w.a). Arguments were put forward by both sides. Several days went by. The Christians did not listen to reason. Then Allah (s.w.t) revealed the following verses…

"Verily, the likeness of Isa /Jesus with Allah is as the likeness of Adam. He created him of dust, then He said unto him:” Be, and he was” (This is) the truth from your Lord (O Muhammad), so be not of those who doubt. And whoso disputes with you concerning him after the knowledge which has come unto you, say (unto them): “Come we will summon our sons and you your sons, and our women and you your women, and our ‘selfs’ and your ‘selfs’, and then will pray humbly (to the Lord) and invoke the curse of Allah upon those who lie. (Qur’an 3:59-61)

The Holy Prophet took with him Hassan & Hussain (for sons), Fatima (as women) & Ali as (Self/Nafs) (a.s) thus proving the exalted position of the 5 personalities who represented Islam as Truthful. Upon seeing them the Najran accepted defeat & did not enter into the encounter.

Waaqeya-e-Mubahila 24th Zilhijjah 9th Hijri mein huwa. 14 Isaiyo’n (najraan) ke ulama’o ka ek giroh Islam ke mutaliq Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) se ghuftagu karne aaye thay. Dono taraf se daleele’n pesh ki gayi aur kuch din guzar gaye. Jab Isaiyo’n ne haq ko tasleem nahi kiya, to Allah (s.w.t) ne yeh aayate’n naazil ki…

Esa alayhis salaam ki misaal Allah ke nazdeek Aadam alayhis salaam jaisi hai keh inhe mitti se paiyda kiya aur phir kaha hoja aur woh ho gaya. Haq tumhaare Parwardigaar ki taraf se aa chuka hai lehaaza khabar-daar! Ab tumhaara shumaar shak karne waalo’n mein na hona chaahiye. Paighambar ilm ke aa jaaane ke baad jo log tum se kat-hujjati (argue) kare un se kahe dijiye keh aawo ham log apne-apne farzand, apni-apni aurto’n aur apne-apne nafso’n ko bulaaye aur phir Khuda ki baargah mein dua kare aur jootho’n par Khuda ki laanat qaraar de. (Qur’an 3:59-61)

Yeh aayato’n ke naazil hone par Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) ne Imam Hasan (a.s) aur Imam Husain (a.s) ko (farzand), Bibi Fatema (s.a) ko (aurto’n) aur Imam Ali (a.s) ko (nafso’n) ke taur par le gaye, is waaqiye se yeh waazeh hai keh Islam ki sachchayi aur haqeeqat ki numayendegi karne ke liye Allah (s.w.t) ne in 5 aala shaksiyat ko muntakhab kiya. In 5 azeem shaksiyat ko dekhte hi un Isaiyo’n ne apni haar maan li aur mubahila (debate) karne se mana kar diya.

1) Have Ghusl.

1) Ghusl karo.

2) Observe fast and remove sadaqa.

2) Roza rakkhe aur sadaqa nikaale.

3) Offer 2 rakat namaz before Zohar, in each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1), recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112), Ayatul Kursi (2:255-257) and Surah Qadr (Chp.97) 10 times each.

3) 2 Rakat namaz Zohar se pehle padhe, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad Surah Iklaas (Chp.112), Ayatul Kursi (2:255-257) aur Surah Qadr (Chp.97) har ek 10 martaba padhe.

4) Recite Dua-e-Mubahila.

4) Dua Dua-e-Mubahila padhe.
