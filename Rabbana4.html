Chp. 2:Ver. 250
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَآ اَفْرِغْ عَلَيْنَا صَبْرًا وَّثَبِّتْ اَقْدَامَنَا وَانْصُرْنَا عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْكٰفِرِيْنَ۔
Our Lord, pour down upon us patience, and make our steps firm and assist us against the unbelieving people.

Khudaaya hamein be-panah sabr ataa farma hamare qadmo ko sabaat (solidity) de aur hamein kaafiro’n ke muqaable mein nusrat ataa farma.
