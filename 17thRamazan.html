SHAB KI DUA...

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Baar-e-Ilaaha! Ramazan ka mahina hai keh jis mein Tu ne Qur’an naazil kiya aur Tu ne is mah (month) mein aabaadi-e-Masajid aur dua’o aur rozo aur namaazo ka hukm diya aur Tu ne qubooliyat-e-dua ki zamaanat di (is mah mein to ham ne bhi ba-qadre imkaan (jitna ho sakey) koshish ki aur Tu ne hamaari madad ki.

Pas bakhsh de is mahine mein hamare gunaho ko aur is Mahe Ramazan ko hamari zindagi ka aakhri Ramazan na qaraar dena, bakhsh de haamari khataawo ko.

Yaqeenan! Tu hi hamara Parwardigaar hai, rahem farma ham par yaqeenan Tu hi hamara Aaqa hai aur ham ko un logo mein se qaraar de, keh jo Teri khushnoodi-e-maghferat ki taraf palat kar jaane waale hai. Yaqeenan Tu hi ba-izzat o ba-azmat hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

NIGHT DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, this is the month of Ramazan, in which You have revealed the Quran and ordered the building of Masjids. And ordered supplication and fasting and prayers and guaranteed to us their acceptance. So we also struggled as per our ability and You also helped us. So forgive us in it and do not make it to be our last Ramazan and forgive us. Indeed You are our Lord; have mercy on us. Indeed You are our Chief and make us of those who are going to turn to Your forgiveness and pleasure. Indeed You are the most majestic and the greatest.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah guide me to do good deeds, in this month; takes a favorable decision in the matter of my needs and desires, O He who is such that there is no need to draw His attention by making a request or by giving information, (because) He is aware of that which every human beings longs for secretly in his or her heart; send blessing on Muhammad and on his pure and purified children.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
