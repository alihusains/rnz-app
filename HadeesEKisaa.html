HADEES-E-KISA

Awalim al-Ulum through an authentic chain of authority that Jabir ibn Abdullah al-Ansari said that he heard Fatimah the daughter of the Holy Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him and his Household, saying…
Hadeese Kisa mustanad aur mohtabar-tareen hadeeso mein se hai jo keh Ahle Bayt (a.s) ki shaan mein hai. Har khushi ke mauqe-o-monasebaat par khaas kar shabe Jum’a momineen ko jama kar ke padhne ki taaqeed hai. Isey khuzoo o khushoo ke saath padh kar jo bhi dili-muraad maangi jaaye be-fazle Ahle-Bayt (a.s) woh puri ho jaati hai…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

عَنْ جَابِرِ ابْنِ عَبْدُ اللہِ الْاَنْصَارِیْ عَنْ فَاطِمَۃَ الزَّاھْرَآءِ عَلَیْھَا السَّلَامُ بِنْتِ رَسُوْلِ اللہِ صَلَّی اللہُ عَلَیْہِ وَاٰلِہٖ وَسلَّمَ۔ قَالَ سَمِعْتُ فَاطِمَۃَ اَنَّھَا قَالَتْ دَخَلَ عَلَیَّ اَبِیْ رَسُوْلُ اللہِ فِیْ بَعْضِ الْاَیَّامِ۔

فَقَالَ السَّلَامُ عَلَیْكِ یَافَاطِمَۃُ فَقُلْتُ عَلَیْكَ السَّلَامُ۔ قَالَ اِنِّیْٓ اَجِدُ فِیْ بَدَنِیْ ضُعْفًا فَقُلْتُ لَہٗ اُعِیْذُكَ بِاللہِ یَااَبَتَاہُ مِنَ الضُّعْفِ۔ فَقَالَ یَافَاطِمَۃُ اِیْتِیْنِیْ بِالْكِسَآءِ الْیَمَانِیْ فَغَطِّیْنِیْ بِہٖ فَاَتَیْتُہٗ بِہٖ وَصِرْتُ اَنْظُرُ اِلَیْہِ وَاِذَا وَجْھَہٗ یَتَلَالَؤُ كَاَنَّہُ الْبَدْرُ فِیْ لَیْلَۃِ تَمَامِہٖ وَ كَمَالِہٖ (صَلَوَاتَ)۔ فَمَا كَانَتْ اِلَّا سَاعَۃً وَّاِذَا بِوَلَدِیَ الْحَسَنِ قَدْ اَقْبَلَ۔

وَقَالَ السَّلَامُ عَلَیْكِ یَااُمَّاہٗ فَقُلْتُ وَعَلَیْكَ السَّلَامُ یَا قُرَّۃَ عَیْنِیْ وَثَمَرَۃَ فُؤَادِیْ۔ فَقَالَ یَااُمَّاہٗ اِنِّیْٓ اَشُمُّ عِنْدَكِ رَآئِحَۃً طَیِّبَۃً كَاَنَّھَا رَآئِحَۃُ جَدِّیْ رَسُوْلِ اللہِ فَقُلْتُ نَعَمْ اِنَّ جَدَّكَ تَحْتَ الْكِسَآءِ فَاَقْبَلَ الْحَسَنُ نَحْوَ الْكِسَآءِ۔

وَ قَالَ السَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاجَدَّاہٗ یَارَسُوْلَ اللہِ اَ تَاْذَنُ لِیْ اَنْ اَدْخُلَ مَعَكَ تَحْتَ الْكِسَآءِ فَقَالَ وَعَلَیكَ السَّلَامُ یَاوَلَدِیْ وَیَا صَاحِبَ حَوْضِیْ قَدْ اَذِنْتُ لَكَ فَدَخَلَ مَعَہٗ تَحَتَ الْكِسَآءِ (صَلَوَاتَ)۔

فَمَا كَانَتْ اِلَّا سَاعَۃً وَّاِذَا بِوَلَدِیَ الْحُسَیْنِ قَدْ اَقْبَلَ وَقَالَ السَّلَامُ عَلَیْكِ یَااُمَّاہٗ فَقُلْتُ وَعَلَیْكَ السَّلَامُ یَاوَلَدِیْ وَیَا قُرَّۃَ عَیْنِیْ وَثَمَرْۃَ فُؤَادِیْ۔ فَقَالَ لِیْ یَا اُمَّاہُ اِنِّیْٓ اَشَمُّ عِنْدَكِ رَآئِحَۃً طَیِّبَۃً كَاَنَّھَا رَآئِحَۃُ جَدِّیْ رَسُوْلِ اللہِ صَلَّی اللہُ عَلَیہِ وَاَلِہٖ فَقُلْتُ نَعَمْ اِنَّ جَدَّكَ وَاَخَاكَ تَحَتَ الْكِسَآءِ فَدَنَی الْحُسَیْنُ نَحْوَ الْكِسَآءِ وَقَالَ السَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاجَدَّاہٗ۔

اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَامَنِ اخْتَارَہُ اللہُ اَتَاْذَنُ لِیْٓ اَنْ اَكُوْنَ مَعْكُمَا تَحَتَ الْكِسَآءِ۔ فَقَالَ عَلَیْكَ السَّلَامُ یَاوَلَدِیْ وَیَا شَافِعَ اُمَّتِیْ قَدْ اَذِنْتُ لَكَ فَدَخَلَ مَعَھُمَا تَحْتَ الْكِسَآءِ (صَلَوَاتَ)۔

فَاَقْبَلَ عِنْدَ ذٰلِكَ اَبُو الْحَسَنِ عَلِیُّ بْنُ اَبِیْطَالِبٍ۔ وَقَالَ السَّلَامُ عَلَیْكِ یَابِنْتَ رَسُوْلِ اللہِ فَقُلْتُ وَعَلَیْكَ السَّلَامُ یَااَبَا الْحَسَنِ وَیَااَمِیْرَ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ فَقَالَ یَافَاطِمَۃُ اِنِّیْ اَشُمُّ عِنْدَكِ رَآئِحَۃً طَیِّبَۃً كَاَنَّھَا رَآئِحَۃُ اَخِیْ وَابْنِ عَمِّیْ رَسُوْلِ اللہِ فَقُلْتُ نَعَمْ ھَا ھُوَ مَعَ وَلَدَیْكَ تَحْتَ الْكِسَآءِ فَاَقْبَلَ عَلِیٌّ نَحْوَ الْكِسَآءِ وَقَالَ السَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَارَسُوْلَ اللہِ اَتَاْذَنُ لِیْ اَنْ اَكُوْنَ مَعَكُمْ تَحْتَ الْكِسَآءِ۔

وَقَالَ لَہٗ وَعَلَیْكَ السَّلَامُ یَااَخِیْ یَاوَصِیِّیْ وَخَلِیْفَتِیْ وَصَاحِبَ لِوَآئِیْ قَدْ اَذِنْتُ لَكَ فَدَخَلَ عَلِیٌّ تَحَتَ الْكِسَآء (صَلَوَاتَ)۔

ثُمَّ اَتَیْتُ نَحْوَ الْكِسَآءِ وَقُلْتُ السَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَااَبَتَاہُ یَارَسُوْلَ اللہِ اَتَاذَنُ لِیْ اَنْ اَكُوْنَ مَعَكُمْ تَحْتَ الْكِسَآءِ فَقَال وَعَلَیْكِ السَّلَامُ یَابِنْتِیْ وَیَا بَضْعَتِیْ قَدْ اَذِنْتُ لَكِ فَدَخَلْتُ تَحْتَ الْكِسَآءِ (صَلَوَاتَ)۔

فَلَمَّا اكْتَمَلْنَا جَمِیْعًا تَحْتَ الْكِسَآءِ اَخَذَ اَبِیْ رَسُوْلُ اللہِ بِطَرَفَیِ الْكِسَآءِ وَاَوْمَیَٔ بِیَدِہِ الْیُمْنٰی اِلَی السَّمَآءِ وَقَالَ۔ اَللّٰھُمَّ اِنَّ ھٰؤُلَآءِ اَھْلُ بَیْتِیْ وَخَاصَّتِیْ وَحَآمَّتِیْ لَحْمُھُمْ لَحْمِیْ وَدَمُھُمْ دَمِیْ یُؤْلِمُنِیْ مَا یُؤْلِمُھُمْ وَیَحْزُنُنِیْ مَا یُحْزُنُھُمْ اَنَا حَرْبٌ لِّمَنْ حَارَبَھُمْ وَسِلْمٌ لِّمَنْسَالَمَھُمْ وَعَدُوٌّ لِّمَنْ عَادَاھُمْ وَمُحِبٌّ لِّمَنْ اَحَبَّھُمْ اِنَّھُمْ مِنِّیْ وَاَنَا مِنْھُمْ فَاجْعَلْ صَلَوٰتِكَ وَبَرَكَاتِكَ وَرَحْمَتَكَ وَغُفْرَانَكَ وَرِضْوَانَكَ عَلَیَّ وَعَلَیْھِمْ وَاَذْھِبْ عَنْھُمُ الرِّجْسَ وَطَھِّرْھُمْ تَطْھِیْرًا (صَلَوَاتَ)۔

فَقَالَ اللہُ عَزَّ وَ جَلَّ یَا مَلَآئِكَتِیْ وَیَاسُكَّانَ سَمٰوٰتِیْ اِنِّیْ مَا خَلَقْتُ سَمَآءً مَّبْنِیَّۃً وَّلَا اَرْضًا مَّدْحِیَّۃً وَّلَا قَمَرًا مُّنِیْرًا وَّلَا شَمْسًا مُّضِیٓئَۃً وَّلَا فَلَكًا یَّدُوْرُ وَلَا بَحْرًا یَّجْرِیْ وَلَا فُلْكًا یَّسْرِیْٓ اِلَّا فِیْ مَحَبَّۃِ ھٰؤُلَآءِ الْخَمْسَۃِ الَّذِیْنَ ھُمْ تَحْتَ الْكِسَآءِ۔ فَقَالَ الْاَمِیْنُ جَبْرَآئِیْلُ یَارَبِّ وَمَنْ تَحْتَ الْكِسَآءِ فَقَالَ عَزَّ وَجَلَّ ھُمْ اَھْلُ بَیْتِ النُّبُوَّۃِ وَمَعْدِنُ الرِّسَالَۃِ ھُمْ فَاطِمَۃُ وَاَبُوْھَا وَبَعْلُھَا وَبَنُوْھَا (صَلَوَاتَ)۔

فَقَالَ جَبْرَآئِیْلُ یَارَبِّ اَتَاذَنُ لِیْٓ اَنْ اَھْبِطَ اِلَی الْاَرْضِ لِاَكُوْنَ مَعَھُمْ سَادِسًا فَقَالَ اللہُ نَعَمْ قَدْ اَذِنْتُ لَكَ فَھَبَطَ الْاَمِیْنُ جَبْرَآئِیْلُ وَقَالَ السَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَارَسُوْلَ اللہِ اَلْعَلِیُّ الْاَعْلٰی یُقْرِئُكَ السَّلَامُ وَیَخُصُّكَ بِالتَّحِیَّۃِ وَالْاِكْرَامِ وَیَقُوْلُ لَكَ وَعِزَّتِیْ وَجَلَآلِیْ اِنِّیْ مَا خَلَقْتُ سَمَآءً مَّبْنِیَّۃً وَّلَا اَرْضًا مَّدْحِیَّۃً وَّلَا قَمَرًا مُّنِیْرًا وَّلَا شَمْسًا مُّضِٓیْئَۃً وَّلَا فَلَكًا یَّدُوْرُ وَلَا بَحْرًا یَّجْرِیْ وَلَا فُلْكًا یَّسْرِیْ اِلَّا لِاَجْلِكُمْ وَمَحَبَّتِكُمْ وَقَدْ اَذِنَ لِیْ اَنْ اَدْخُلَ مَعَكُمْ فَھَلْ تَاْذَنُ لِیْ یَارَسُوْلَ اللہِ فَقَالَ رَسُوْلُ اللہِ وَعَلَیْكَ السَّلَامُ یَااَمِیْنَ وَحْیِ اللہِ اِنَّہٗ نَعَمْ قَدْ اَذِنْتُ لَكَ فَدَخَلَ جَبْرَآئِیْلُ مَعَنَا تَحَتَ الْكِسَآءِ (صَلَوَاتَ)۔

فَقَالَ لِاَبِیْ اِنَّ اللہَ قَدْ اَوْحٰٓی اِلَیْكُمْ یَقُوْلُ اِنَّمَا یُرِیْدُ اللہُ لِیُذْھِبَ عَنْكُمُ الرِّجْسَ اَھْلَ الْبَیْتِ وَیُطَھِّرَكُمْ تَطْھِیْرًا (صَلَوَاتَ)۔

فَقَالَ عَلِیٌّ لِاَبِیْ یَارَسُوْلَ اللہِ اَخْبِرْنِیْ مَا لِجُلُوْسِنَا ھٰذَا تَحْتَ الْكِسَآءِ مِنَ الْفَضْلِ عِنْدَ اللہِ فَقَالَ النَّبِیُّ وَالَّذِیْ بَعَثَنِیْ بِالْحَقِّ نَبِیًّا وَّاصْطَفَانِیْ بِالرِّسَالَۃِ نَجِیًّا مَّا ذُكِرَ خَبَرُنَا ھٰذَا فِیْ مَحْفِلٍ مِّنْ مَحَافِلِ اَھْلِ الْاَرْضِ وَفِیْہِ جَمْعٌ مِّنْ شِیْعَتِنَا وَمُحِبِّیْنَا اِلَّا وَنَزَلَتْ عَلَیْھِمُ الرَّحْمَۃُ وَحَفَّتْ بِھِمُ الْمَلَآئِكَۃُ وَاسْتَغْفَرَتْ لَھُمْ اِلٰی اَنْ یَّتَفَرَّقُوْا فَقَالَ عَلِیٌّ اِذًا وَّاللہِ فُزْنَا وَفَازَ شِیْعَتُنَا وَرَبِّ الْكَعْبَۃِ۔

فَقَالَ النَّبِیُّ ثَانِیًا یَاعَلِیُّ وَالَّذِیْ بَعَثَنِیْ بِالْحَقِّ نَبِیًّا وَّاصْطَفَانِیْ بِالرِّسَالَۃِ نَجِیًّا مَّا ذُكِرَ خَبَرُنَا ھٰذَا فِیْ مَحْفِلٍ مِّنْ مَحَافِلِ اَھْلِ الْاَرْضِ وَفِیْہِ جَمْعٌ مِّنْ شِیْعَتِنَا وَمُحِبِّیْنَا وَفِیْھِمْ مَھْمُوْمٌ اِلَّا وَفَرَّجَ اللہُ ھَمَّہٗ وَلَا مَغْمُوْمٌ اِلَّا وَكَشَفَ اللہُ غَمَّہٗ وَلَا طَالِبُ حَاجَۃٍ اِلَّا وَقَضَی اللہُ حَاجَتَہٗ۔ فَقَالَ عَلِیٌّ اِذًا وَّاللہِ فُزْنَا وَسُعِدْنَا وَكَذٰلِكَ شِیْعَتُنَا فَازُوْ وَسُعِدُوْا فِی الدُّنْیَا وَالْاٰخِرَۃِ وَرَبِّ الْكَعْبَۃِ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

HADEES-E-KISA

Hadees-e-Kisa mustanad aur mohtabar-tareen hadeeso mein se hai jo keh Ahle Bayt (a.s) ki shaan mein hai. Har khushi ke mauqe-o-monasebaat par khaas kar shabe Jum’a momineen ko jama kar ke padhne ki taaqeed hai. Isey khuzoo-o-khushoo ke saath padh kar jo bhi dili-muraad maangi jaaye be-fazle Ahle-Bayt (a.s) woh puri ho jaati hai. Awwal wa aakhir 3-baar Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par durood bheje. Insha-Allah aap ko khushi miley-gi!

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Jabir ibne Abdullah-e-Ansari Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) ki sahab-zaadi Hazrat Fatema Zahra (s.a) se riwaayat karte hai keh unho-ne farmaya: Ek din baba-jaan Janabe Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) mere ghar tashreef laaye aur farmaane lage! Fatema tum par salaam ho. Mai ne jawaab diya baba-jaan aap par bhi salaam ho. Phir aap ne farmaya: mai apne jism mein zof (kamzori) si mehsoos kar raha hu. Mai ne kaha: baba-jaan Khuda na kare jo aap kamzor ho.

Phir aap ne farmaya: Fatema mujhe ek yemani chaadar la kar udha do. Mai yemani chaadar le aayi, aur woh mai ne baba-jaan ko udha di. Mai dekh rahi thi ke Aap (s.a.w.a) ka chehra chaudhawi raat ke chaand ki tarah chamak raha hai.

Ek sa’at (abhi thodi der) nahi guzri thi keh mere bete Hasan (a.s) aa gaye aur bole: Ammi jaan aap par salaam ho. Mai ne jawaab diya mere bete, meri aankh ke taare (star), mere dil ke tukde tum par bhi salaam ho. Phir woh kehne lage: Ammi jaan mujhe ghar mein nana-jaan ki khushbu aa rahi hai. Mai ne kaha’n haa’n. Tumhare nana-jaan chaadar o’dhe huwe hai.

Uspar Hasan (a.s) chaadar ki taraf bade aur kehne lage: nana-jaan ay Khuda ke Rasool (s.a.w.a) aap par salaam ho. Kya mujhe ijaazat (permission) hai keh mai bhi aap ke paas chaadar mein aa jaau? Aap (s.a.w.a) ne jawaab diya: mere bete, mere hauz ke maalik, tum par bhi salaam ho. Tumhe ijaazat hai. Pas Hasan (a.s) aap ke paas chaadar mein daakhil ho gaye.

Ek sa’at (abhi thodi der) nahi guzri thi keh mere bete Husain (a.s) aa gaye aur kehne lage: Ammi jaan aap par salaam ho. Mai ne jawaab diya. Mere bete, meri aankh ke taare, mere lakhte jigar tum par bhi salaam ho. Phir woh mujh se kehne lage: Ammi jaan mujhe ghar mein nana-jaan ki khushbu aa rahi hai. Mai ne kaha’n haa’n. Tumhare nana-jaan aur bhai-jaan dono chaadar ke andar hai.

Uspar Husain (a.s) chaadar ke qareeb gaye aur bole: nana-jaan ay Khuda ke mukhtaar (choone huwe) Nabi (s.a.w.a) aap par salaam ho. Kya aap ki ijaazat (permission) hai keh mai bhi aap dono ke paas chaadar mein aa jaau? Aap (s.a.w.a) ne farmaya: mere bete, meri ummat ko bakhshwaane waale tum par bhi salaam ho. Tumhe ijaazat hai. Pas Husain (a.s) bhi un dono ke paas chaadar mein daakhil ho gaye.

Uske baad Ali ibne Abi Taalib (a.s) aa gaye aur bole: Rasool (s.a.w.a) ki beti Tum par salaam ho. Mai ne jawaab diya. Abul Hasan (a.s) ay momino ke Ameer, aap par bhi salaam ho. Phir woh kehne lage: Fatema! mujhe ghar mein apne bhai aur ibne Amm (cousin) ki khushbu aa rahi hai. Mai ne jawaab diya haa’n: woh aap ke dono beto ke saath chaadar ke andar hai.

Phir Hazrat Ali (a.s) chaadar ke qareeb gaye, aur bole: ay Khuda ke Rasool (s.a.w.a) aap par salaam ho. Kya mujhe bhi ijaazat hai ke mai aap ke paas chaadar mein aa jaau? Baba-jaan ne un se farmaya: Tum par bhi salaam ho mere bhai, mere wasi, mere ja-nasheen, mere alambar-daar tumhe ijaazat hai. Pas Hazrat Ali (a.s) bhi chaadar mein daakhil ho gaye.

Phir mai chaadar ke qareeb gayi aur mai ne kaha: Salaam ho aap par baba-jaan. Ay Khuda ke Rasool (s.a.w.a) kya mai bhi aap ke paas chaadar mein aa jaau? baba-jaan ne farmaya: Tum par bhi salaam ho meri beti, mere para-e-jigar tumhe bhi ijaazat hai. Chunan-che mai bhi chaadar mein daakhil ho gayi.

Ab jo ham sab chaadar mein ekattha (jama) ho gaye to baba-jaan ne chaadar ke dono kinare pakde aur daaye haath (right hand) se aasmaan ki taraf ishara karte huwe farmaya: ay Ma’bood! yeh mere Ahle-bayt (a.s) mere khaas log aur mere haami (himayat karne waale) hai.

Inka gosht mera gosht hai. Aur inka khoon mera khoon hai. Jo inhey satata hai woh mujhe takleef deta hai. Jo inhey ranjeeda karta hai woh mujhe ranj (dukh) deta hai. Jo in se ladega mai bhi us se ladunga. Jo in se sulho (peace) rakkhega mai bhi us se sulho rakkhu-ga. Jo in se dushmani karega mai bhi usko apna dushman samjhohunga. Aur jo in se dosti rakkhega mai bhi usko apna dost jaanuga. Kyu’n keh yeh mujh se hai aur mai in se hu.

Ay Ma’bood! Tu apni salwaat, barakaat, rehmat, bakhshish aur khushnoodi mere aur inke liye qaraar de, in se najaasat ko dur rakh aur inhe nehaayat paak-o-pakeeza rakh jis tarah paak-o-pakeeza rakhne ka haq hai.

Phir Khuda-e-buzurgh-o-bartar ne farmaya: ay farishto, ay aasmaan ke rehne waalo, mai ne yeh mazboot aasmaan, yeh phaili huwi zameen, roshan chaand, chamakta suraj, ghoomte sayyare, (planets) chalak-te samandar, tairti kashti, yeh saari cheeze sirf inhi paanch afraad ke khaatir aur inhi ki mohabbat mein paida ki hai jo chaadar ke andar hai.

Uspar Jibraeel-e-Ameen (a.s) ne poonchha: ay Parwardigaar! yeh chaadar ke andar kaun log hai? Khuda wande Aalam ne farmaya: yeh Nabi (s.a.w.a) ke Ahle-bayt aur Risaalat ka khazeena hai. Yeh Fatema (s.a) unke baba, unke shauhar, aur unke do bete hai. Jibraeel ne arz kiya: ay Parwardigaar! Mujhe bhi ijaazat de, keh mai zameen par utru aur un mein shaamil ho kar chhatta fard (6th person) ban jaau, Khuda ne farmaya: tumhe ijaazat hai.

Pas Jibraeel-e-Ameen zameen par utre aur kehne lage: ay Khuda ke Rasool (s.a.w.a) aap par salaam ho. Khuda-e-buzurgh-o-bartar aap ko salaam kehta hai, Aap ko durood aur ehteraam se sarfaraaz farmata hai, aur aap se kehta hai keh Meri izzat aur buzurghi ki qasam mai ne yeh mazboot aasmaan, yeh phaili huwi zameen, roshan chaand, chamakta suraj, ghoomte sayyare, (planets) chalakte samandar, tairti kashti (boat), yeh saari cheeze sirf tum logo ke khaatir aur tumhari hi mohabbat mein paida ki hai. Aur Khuda ne mujhe ijaazat de di hai keh mai bhi chaadar ke andar aap ke paas aa jaau.

Ay Khuda ke Rasool (s.a.w.a) kya aap bhi mujhe ijaazat dete hai? Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) ne farmaya: ay Khuda ki wahi ke ameen tum par bhi salaam ho, haa’n! tumhe bhi ijaazat hai. Phir Jibraeel (a.s) bhi ham logo ke saath chaadar mein aa gaye. Uske baad Jibraeel (a.s) ne mere baba-jaan se kaha: Khuda aap logo ko wahibhejta hai aur kehta hai keh: “Be-shak Khuda ne yeh iraada kar liya hai keh aap Ahle-bayt (a.s) se najaasat ko dur rakkhe aur aap logo ko paak-o-pakeeza rakkhe jis tarah paak-o-pakeeza rakhne ka haq hai” (salwaat).

Tab Hazrat Ali (a.s) ne baba-jaan se kaha: Mujhe bataaiye keh is chaadar ke andar aa jaana Khuda ke nazdeek kya fazeelat rakhta hai? Uspar Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) ne farmaya: Us zaat ki qasam jisne mujhe Nabi banaya, aur logo ki najaat ki khatir mujhe Risaalat ke liye muntakhab kiya. Ahle-zameen ki jis mehfil mein hamaare Shi’a aur dost-daar (dosti rakhne waale) ekatthe (gether) hoge, aur hamaari yeh hadees bayaan kare (padhe), to unpar Khuda ki rehmat naazil hogi.

Farishte unko halqe mein le lege. Aur jab tak woh log mehfil se rukhsat nahi ho jaayege, unke gunaho ki bakhshish ke liye dua karte rahenge. Uspar Hazrat Ali (a.s) ne kaha: Khuda ki qasam phir to ham kaam-yaab huwe. Aur hamaare Shi’a bhi kaam-yaab ho gaye!

Phir Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) ne farmaya: ay Ali (a.s) us zaat ki qasam jisne mujhe sachcha Nabi banaya aur logo ki najaat ke khaatir mujhe risaalat ke liye muntakhab (appoint) kiya ahle-zameen ki jis mehfil mein hamaare Shi’a aur dost-daar (dosti rakhne waale) ikatthe (gether) hoge, aur hamaari yeh hadees bayaan karenge (padhenge), us mein jo koi dukhi hoga Khuda uska dukh dur karega.

Jo gham-zadah (gham-gheen) hoga usko gham se chhutkaara dega. Aur jo koi haajat-mand hoga Khuda uski haajat puri karega. Tab Hazrat Ali (a.s) ne farmaya: Khuda ki qasam phir to ham ne kaam-yaabi aur barkat-o-saadat pa li, aur Kaabe ke Rab ki qasam usi tarah hamaare Shi’a bhi kaam-yaab huwe, aur duniya-o-aakherat mein khush-naseeb thaiyre.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

HADEES-E-KISA

Awalim al-Ulum through an authentic chain of authority that Jabir ibn Abdullah al-Ansari said that he heard Fatimah the daughter of the Holy Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him and his Household, saying…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

One day my father, the Prophet of Allah, came to my house and said to me: "Peace be on you, Fatimah" I replied: "And upon you be peace" Then he said: "I feel weakness in my body" I said: "Allah protects you from weakness, father" He said: "Fatimah, please bring Yemeni cloak and cover me with it" So, I brought the Yemeni cloak and covered him with it. Then, I looked at him and saw that his face was shining like a full moon with its glory and splendor.

After a while, my son Hasan came in and said: "Peace be on you, mother" I replied: "And upon you peace, O light of my eyes, and the delight of my heart" He then said: "Mother! I smell a fragrance so sweet and so pure as that of my grandfather, the Prophet of Allah". I replied: "Yes. Your grandfather is lying underneath the cloak" Hasan went near the cloak and said: "Peace be on you, my grandfather, the Prophet of Allah; May I enter the cloak with you?" He replied: "And upon you peace, my son and the master of my fountain (Kauthar), you are given the permission to enter" So, Hasan entered the cloak with him.

After a while, my Husain came in and said: "Peace be on you, mother" I replied: "And upon you peace, O light of my eyes, and the delight of my heart" He then said: "Mother! I smell a sweet fragrance like that of my grandfather, the Prophet of Allah" I replied: "Yes. Your grandfather and your brother are lying underneath the cloak" Husain stepped towards the cloak and said: "Peace be on you, my grandfather, the Chosen of Allah; May I enter the cloak with you?" He replied: "And upon you peace, my son and interceder of my followers, you are given the permission to enter" So, Husain entered the cloak with them.

After a while, Abul Hasan, Ali bin Abi Talib came in and said: "Peace be on you, O daughter of the Prophet of Allah" I replied: "And upon you peace, O father of Hasan, and the Commander of the faithful" He then said: "Fatimah! I smell a sweet fragrance like that of my brother, (my cousin), the Prophet of Allah" I replied: "Yes. He is underneath the cloak with your two sons" So, Ali went near the cloak and said: "Peace be on you, Prophet of Allah; May I enter the cloak with you?" He replied: "And upon you peace, my brother, my legatee, my successor, and my standard bearer, you are given the permission to enter" So, Ali entered the cloak with them.

Then I stepped forward and said: "Peace be on you, my father, O Prophet of Allah; May I enter the cloak with you?" He replied: "And upon you peace, my daughter, O part of my self; you are given the permission to enter" So, I entered the cloak with them.

Getting together underneath the cloak, my father, the Prophet of Allah, held the two ends of the cloak and raised his right hand towards the heavens and prayed: "O Allah, these are the people of my Household (Ahlul-Bayt). They are my confidants and my supporters. Their flesh is my flesh and their blood is my blood. Whoever hurts them, hurts me too. Whoever displeases them, displeased me too.

I am at war with those at war with them. I am at peace with those at peace with them. I am the enemies of their enemies and I am the friend of their friends. They are from me and I am from them.

O Allah! Bestow Your Blessings, Benevolence, Forgiveness and Your pleasure upon me and upon them. And remove impurity from them and keep them thoroughly pure".

Then the Lord, Almighty Allah said: "O My angels! O Residents of My Heavens, verily, I have not created the erected Sky, the stretched earth, the illuminated moon, the bright sun, the rotating planets, the flowing seas and the sailing ships, but for the love of these Five lying underneath the cloak" Gabriel, the trusted angel, asked: "Who are under the cloak?" The Almighty answered: "They are the Household of the Prophet and the assets of Prophethood. They are: Fatimah, her father, her husband and her two sons".

Gabriel said: "O Lord, May I fly to earth to be the sixth of them?" Allah replied: "Yes. You are given the permission" Gabriel, the trusted, landed near them and said: "Peace be on you, O Prophet of Allah. The All-Highest conveys His peace on you and His greetings and says: "By My Honor and Glory, O My angels! O Residents of My Heavens, verily, I have not created the erected Sky, the stretched earth, the illuminated moon, the bright sun, the rotating planets, the flowing seas and the sailing ships, but for your sake and love".

God has given me permission to enter the cloak with you. May I join you, O Prophet of Allah?" The Prophet replied: "And peace be on you, O trusted bearer of Allah's Revelations! you are granted the permission" So, Gabriel entered the cloak with us and said to my father: "Allah sends His Revelations to you" Verily Allah's desire is to remove blemish from you, O People of Household (Ahlul-Bayt) and purify you with a perfect purification".

Then Ali said to my father: "O Prophet of Allah, tell me: What significance has Allah reserved from His grace for this gathering of ours under this cloak?" The Prophet replied: "I swear by He who rightfully appointed me a Prophet and selected me with prophethood as a saviour: this episode of ours will not be recounted in any gathering from the gatherings of the people of the earth where a group of our shias and lovers are present except that there will descend upon them mercy! And angels will encircle them asking Allah the remission of their sins until the assembly disperses!".

So Ali said: "In that case, We have attained success, and our Shias have attained success, by the Lord of the Ka'bah!" Then the Prophet replied a second time: "O Ali! I swear by He who rightfully appointed me a Prophet and selected me with prophethood as a saviour: this episode of ours will not be recounted in any gathering from the gatherings of the people of the earth where a group of our shias and lovers are present and there be a person present full of grief except that Allah will remove his grief!

And no person in distress except that will dispel his distress! And no seeker of any wish except that Allah grants his wish! So Ali stated: "In that case, by Allah! We have attained success and felicity, and our followers also have attained success and felicity, in this world and the hereafter, by the Lord of the Ka'bah!"

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
