WAHESHAT

The day on which the Mayyit is buried, 2-rakat Namaz with the above niyyat has to be recited after Namaz-e-Isha. In the 1st rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite once Ayatul Khursi (2:255-257). And in the 2nd rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite 10-times Surah Qadr (Chp.97). Then complete the Namaz as usual and say that the sawaab of this namaz is for the Marhoom (take the name of the buried one)

Yeh 2 rakat Namaz, mayyit ki qabr ki pehli shab mein adaa kare. 1st rakat mein Surah Hamd (Chp.1) ke baad 1 martaba Ayatul Khursi (2:255-257) padhe aur 2nd rakat mein Surah Hamd (Chp.1) ke baad 10 martaba Surah Qadr (Chp.97) padhe aur namaz-e-subh ki tarah tamaam kare. Phir namaz tamaam kare aam namazo ki tarah aur kahe iska namaz ka sawaab us Marhoom (yahan dafn hone waale ka naam le) ko miley.
