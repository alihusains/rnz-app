بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

يٰٓاَيُّهَا الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْٓا اَوْفُوْا بِالْعُقُوْدِ ڛ اُحِلَّتْ لَكُمْ بَهِيْمَةُ الْاَنْعَامِ اِلَّا مَا يُتْلٰى عَلَيْكُمْ غَيْرَ مُحِلِّي الصَّيْدِ وَاَنْتُمْ حُرُمٌط اِنَّ اللهَ يَحْكُمُ مَا يُرِيْدُﯜ1ﯛ يٰٓاَيُّهَا الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا لَا تُحِلُّوْا شَعَاۗئِرَ اللهِ وَلَا الشَّهْرَ الْحَرَامَ وَلَا الْهَدْيَ وَلَا الْقَلَۗائِدَ وَلَۗا اٰۗمِّيْنَ الْبَيْتَ الْحَرَامَ يَبْتَغُوْنَ فَضْلًا مِّنْ رَّبِّهِمْ وَرِضْوَانًاط وَاِذَا حَلَلْتُمْ فَاصْطَادُوْاط وَلَا يَجْرِمَنَّكُمْ شَنَاٰنُ قَوْمٍ اَنْ صَدُّوْكُمْ عَنِ الْمَسْجِدِ الْحَرَامِ اَنْ تَعْتَدُوْام وَتَعَاوَنُوْا عَلَى الْبِرِّ وَالتَّقْوٰىص وَلَا تَعَاوَنُوْا عَلَى الْاِثْمِ وَالْاُدْوَانِص وَاتَّقُوا اللهَ ط اِنَّ اللهَ شَدِيْدُ الْعِقَابِﯜ2ﯛ حُرِّمَتْ عَلَيْكُمُ الْمَيْتَةُ وَالدَّمُ وَلَحْمُ الْخِنْزِيْرِ وَمَآ اُهِلَّ لِغَيْرِ اللهِ بِهٖ وَالْمُنْخَنِقَةُ وَالْمَوْقُوْذَةُ وَالْمُتَرَدِّيَةُ وَالنَّطِيْحَةُ وَمَآ اَكَلَ السَّبُعُ اِلَّا مَا ذَكَّيْتُمْقف وَمَا ذُبِحَ عَلَى النُّصُبِ وَاَنْ تَسْتَقْسِمُوْا بِالْاَزْلَامِط ذٰلِكُمْ فِسْقٌط اَلْيَوْمَ يَئِسَ الَّذِيْنَ كَفَرُوْا مِنْ دِيْنِكُمْ فَلَا تَخْشَوْهُمْ وَاخْشَوْنِط اَلْيَوْمَ اَكْمَلْتُ لَكُمْ دِيْنَكُمْ وَاَتْمَمْتُ عَلَيْكُمْ نِعْمَتِيْ وَرَضِيْتُ لَكُمُ الْاِسْلَامَ دِينًاط فَمَنِ اضْطُرَّ فِيْ مَخْمَصَةٍ غَيْرَ مُتَجَانِفٍ لِاِثْمٍلا فَاِنَّ اللهَ غَفُوْرٌ رَّحِيْمٌﯜ3ﯛ يَسْاَلُوْنَكَ مَاذَٓا اُحِلَّ لَهُمْط قُلْ اُحِلَّ لَكُمُ الطَّيِّبٰتُلا وَمَا عَلَّمْتُمْ مِّنَ الْجَوَارِحِ مُكَلِّبِيْنَ تُعَلِّمُوْنَهُنَّ مِمَّا عَلَّمَكُمُ اللهُ فَكُلُوْا مِمَّآ اَمْسَكْنَ عَلَيْكُمْ وَاذْكُرُوْا اسْمَ اللهِ عَلَيْهِص وَاتَّقُوا اللهَط اِنَّ اللهَ سَرِيْعُ الْحِسَابِﯜ4ﯛ اَلْيَوْمَ اُحِلَّ لَكُمُ الطَّيِّبٰتُط وَطَعَامُ الَّذِيْنَ اُوْتُوا الْكِتٰبَ حِلٌّ لَّكُمْص وَطَعَامُكُمْ حِلٌّ لَّهُمْز وَالْمُحْصَنٰتُ مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنٰتِ وَالْمُحْصَنٰتُ مِنَ الَّذِيْنَ اُوْتُوا الْكِتٰبَ مِنْ قَبْلِكُمْ اِذَآ اٰتَيْتُمُوْهُنَّ اُجُوْرَهُنَّ مُحْصِنِيْنَ غَيْرَ مُسٰفِحِيْنَ وَلَا مُتَّخِذِيْٓ اَخْدَانٍط وَمَنْ يَّكْفُرْ بِالْاِيْمَانِ فَقَدْ حَبِطَ عَمَلُهٗز وَهُوَ فِي الْاٰخِرَةِ مِنَ الْخٰسِرِيْنَﯜ5ﯛ يٰٓاَيُّهَا الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْٓا اِذَا قُمْتُمْ اِلَى الصَّلٰوةِ فَاغْسِلُوْا وُجُوْهَكُمْ وَاَيْدِيَكُمْ اِلَى الْمَرَافِقِ وَامْسَحُوْا بِرُءُوْسِكُمْ وَاَرْجُلَكُمْ اِلَى الْكَعْبَيْنِط وَاِنْ كُنْتُمْ جُنُبًا فَاطَّهَّرُوْاط وَاِنْ كُنْتُمْ مَّرْضٰٓى اَوْ عَلٰى سَفَرٍ اَوْ جَاۗءَ اَحَدٌ مِّنْكُمْ مِّنَ الْغَاۗئِطِ اَوْ لٰمَسْتُمُ النِّسَاۗءَ فَلَمْ تَجِدُوْا مَاۗءً فَتَيَمَّمُوْا صَعِيْدًا طَيِّبًا فَامْسَحُوْا بِوُجُوْهِكُمْ وَاَيْدِيْكُمْ مِّنْهُط مَا يُرِيْدُ اللهُ لِيَجْعَلَ عَلَيْكُمْ مِّنْ حَرَجٍ وَّلٰكِنْ يُّرِيْدُ لِيُطَهِّرَكُمْ وَلِيُتِمَّ نِعْمَتَهٗ عَلَيْكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُوْنَﯜ6ﯛ وَاذْكُرُوْا نِعْمَةَ اللهِ عَلَيْكُمْ وَمِيْثَاقَهُ الَّذِيْ وَاثَقَكُمْ بِهٖٓلا اِذْ قُلْتُمْ سَمِعْنَا وَاَطَعْنَاز وَاتَّقُوا اللهَ ط اِنَّ اللهَ عَلِيْمٌۢ بِذَاتِ الصُّدُوْرِﯜ7ﯛ يٰٓاَيُّهَا الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا كُوْنُوْا قَوّٰمِيْنَ لِلهِ شُهَدَاۗءَ بِالْقِسْطِز وَلَا يَجْرِمَنَّكُمْ شَنَاٰنُ قَوْمٍ عَلٰٓى اَلَّا تَعْدِلُوْاط اِعْدِلُوْاقف هُوَ اَقْرَبُ لِلتَّقْوٰىز وَاتَّقُوا اللهَط اِنَّ اللهَ خَبِيْرٌۢ بِمَا تَعْمَلُوْنَﯜ8ﯛ وَعَدَ اللهُ الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا وَعَمِلُوا الصّٰلِحٰتِلا لَهُمْ مَّغْفِرَةٌ وَّاَجْرٌ عَظِيْمٌﯜ9ﯛ وَالَّذِيْنَ كَفَرُوْا وَكَذَّبُوْا بِاٰيٰتِنَآ اُوْلٰۗئِكَ اَصْحٰبُ الْجَحِيْمِﯜ10ﯛ يٰٓاَيُّهَا الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوا اذْكُرُوْا نِعْمَتَ اللهِ عَلَيْكُمْ اِذْ هَمَّ قَوْمٌ اَنْ يَّبْسُطُوْٓا اِلَيْكُمْ اَيْدِيَهُمْ فَكَفَّ اَيْدِيَهُمْ عَنْكُمْج وَاتَّقُوا اللهَط وَعَلَى اللهِ فَلْيَتَوَكَّلِ الْمُؤْمِنُوْنَﯜ11ﯛ وَلَقَدْ اَخَذَ اللهُ مِيْثَاقَ بَنِيْٓ اِسْرَاۗئِيْلَج وَبَعَثْنَا مِنْهُمُ اثْنَيْ عَشَرَ نَقِيْبًاط وَقَالَ اللهُ اِنِّي مَعَكُمْط لَئِنْ اَقَمْتُمُ الصَّلٰوةَ وَاٰتَيْتُمُ الزَّكٰوةَ وَاٰمَنْتُمْ بِرُسُلِيْ وَعَزَّرْتُمُوْهُمْ وَاَقْرَضْتُمُ اللهَ قَرْضًا حَسَنًا لَّاُكَفِّرَنَّ عَنْكُمْ سَيِّاٰتِكُمْ وَلَاُدْخِلَنَّكُمْ جَنّٰتٍ تَجْرِيْ مِنْ تَحْتِهَا الْاَنْهٰرُج فَمَنْ كَفَرَ بَعْدَ ذٰلِكَ مِنْكُمْ فَقَدْ ضَلَّ سَوَاۗءَ السَّبِيْلِﯜ12ﯛ فَبِمَا نَقْضِهِمْ مِّيْثَاقَهُمْ لَعَنّٰهُمْ وَجَعَلْنَا قُلُوْبَهُمْ قٰسِيَةًج يُحَرِّفُوْنَ الْكَلِمَ عَنْ مَّوَاضِعِهٖلا وَنَسُوْا حَظًّا مِّمَّا ذُكِّرُوْا بِهٖج وَلَا تَزَالُ تَطَّلِعُ عَلٰى خَاۗئِنَةٍ مِّنْهُمْ اِلَّا قَلِيْلًا مِّنْهُمْ فَاعْفُ عَنْهُمْ وَاصْفَحْط اِنَّ اللهَ يُحِبُّ الْمُحْسِنِيْنَﯜ13ﯛ وَمِنَ الَّذِيْنَ قَالُوْٓا اِنَّا نَصٰرٰٓى اَخَذْنَا مِيْثَاقَهُمْ فَنَسُوْا حَظًّا مِّمَّا ذُكِّرُوْا بِهٖص فَاَغْرَيْنَا بَيْنَهُمُ الْعَدَاوَةَ وَالْبَغْضَاۗءَ اِلٰى يَوْمِ الْقِيٰمَةِط وَسَوْفَ يُنَبِّئُهُمُ اللهُ بِمَا كَانُوْا يَصْنَعُوْنَﯜ14ﯛ يٰٓاَهْلَ الْكِتٰبِ قَدْ جَاۗءَكُمْ رَسُوْلُنَا يُبَيِّنُ لَكُمْ كَثِيْرًا مِّمَّا كُنْتُمْ تُخْفُوْنَ مِنَ الْكِتٰبِ وَيَعْفُوْ عَنْ كَثِيْرٍڛ قَدْ جَاۗءَكُمْ مِّنَ اللهِ نُوْرٌ وَّكِتٰبٌ مُّبِيْنٌﯜ15ﯛ يَّهْدِيْ بِهِ اللهُ مَنِ اتَّبَعَ رِضْوَانَهٗ سُبُلَ السَّلٰمِ وَيُخْرِجُهُمْ مِّنَ الظُّلُمٰتِ اِلَى النُّوْرِ بِاِذْنِهٖ وَيَهْدِيْهِمْ اِلٰى صِرَاطٍ مُّسْتَقِيْمٍﯜ16ﯛ لَقَدْ كَفَرَ الَّذِيْنَ قَالُوْٓا اِنَّ اللهَ هُوَ الْمَسِيْحُ ابْنُ مَرْيَمَط قُلْ فَمَنْ يَّمْلِكُ مِنَ اللهِ شَيْئًا اِنْ اَرَادَ اَنْ يُّهْلِكَ الْمَسِيْحَ ابْنَ مَرْيَمَ وَاُمَّهٗ وَمَنْ فِي الْاَرْضِ جَمِيْعًاط وَلِلهِ مُلْكُ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضِ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَاط يَخْلُقُ مَا يَشَاۗءُط وَاللهُ عَلٰى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيْرٌﯜ17ﯛ وَقَالَتِ الْيَهُوْدُ وَالنَّصٰرٰى نَحْنُ اَبْنٰۗؤُ اللهِ وَاَحِبَّاۗؤُهٗط قُلْ فَلِمَ يُعَذِّبُكُمْ بِذُنُوْبِكُمْط بَلْ اَنْتُمْ بَشَرٌ مِّمَّنْ خَلَقَط يَغْفِرُ لِمَنْ يَّشَاۗءُ وَيُعَذِّبُ مَنْ يَّشَاۗءُط وَلِلهِ مُلْكُ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضِ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَاز وَاِلَيْهِ الْمَصِيْرُﯜ18ﯛ يٰٓاَهْلَ الْكِتٰبِ قَدْ جَاۗءَكُمْ رَسُوْلُنَا يُبَيِّنُ لَكُمْ عَلٰى فَتْرَةٍ مِّنَ الرُّسُلِ اَنْ تَقُوْلُوْا مَا جَاۗءَنَا مِنْۢ بَشِيْرٍ وَّلَا نَذِيْرٍز فَقَدْ جَاۗءَكُمْ بَشِيْرٌ وَّنَذِيْرٌط وَاللهُ عَلٰى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيْرٌﯜ19ﯛ وَاِذْ قَالَ مُوْسٰى لِقَوْمِهٖ يٰقَوْمِ اذْكُرُوْا نِعْمَةَ اللهِ عَلَيْكُمْ اِذْ جَعَلَ فِيْكُمْ اَنْبِۢيَاۗءَ وَجَعَلَكُمْ مُّلُوْكًا ڰ وَاٰتٰكُمْ مَّا لَمْ يُؤْتِ اَحَدًا مِّنَ الْعٰلَمِيْنَﯜ20ﯛ يٰقَوْمِ ادْخُلُوا الْاَرْضَ الْمُقَدَّسَةَ الَّتِيْ كَتَبَ اللهُ لَكُمْ وَلَا تَرْتَدُّوْا عَلٰٓى اَدْبَارِكُمْ فَتَنْقَلِبُوْا خٰسِرِيْنَﯜ21ﯛ قَالُوْا يٰمُوْسٰٓى اِنَّ فِيْهَا قَوْمًا جَبَّارِيْنَ ڰوَاِنَّا لَنْ نَّدْخُلَهَا حَتّٰى يَخْرُجُوْا مِنْهَاج فَاِنْ يَخْرُجُوْا مِنْهَا فَاِنَّا دٰخِلُوْنَﯜ22ﯛ قَالَ رَجُلٰنِ مِنَ الَّذِيْنَ يَخَافُوْنَ اَنْعَمَ اللهُ عَلَيْهِمَا ادْخُلُوْا عَلَيْهِمُ الْبَابَج فَاِذَا دَخَلْتُمُوْهُ فَاِنَّكُمْ غٰلِبُوْنَ ڬ وَعَلَى اللهِ فَتَوَكَّلُوْٓا اِنْ كُنْتُمْ مُؤْمِنِيْنَﯜ23ﯛ قَالُوْا الْمُتَّقِيْنَﯜ27ﯛ لَئِنْۢ بَسَطْتَّ اِلَيَّ يَدَكَ لِتَقْتُلَنِيْ مَآ اَنَا بِبَاسِطٍ يَّدِيَ اِلَيْكَ لِاَقْتُلَكَج اِنِّيْٓ اَخَافُ اللهَ رَبَّ الْعٰلَمِيْنَﯜ28ﯛ اِنِّيْٓ اُرِيْدُ اَنْ تَبُوْءَ بِاِثْمِيْ وَاِثْمِكَ فَتَكُوْنَ مِنْ اَصْحٰبِ النَّارِج وَذٰلِكَ جَزٰۗءُ الظّٰلِمِيْنَﯜ29ﯛ فَطَوَّعَتْ لَهٗ نَفْسُهٗ قَتْلَ اَخِيْهِ فَقَتَلَهٗ فَاَصْبَحَ مِنَ الْخٰسِرِيْنَﯜ30ﯛ فَبَعَثَ اللهُ غُرَابًا يَّبْحَثُ فِي الْاَرْضِ لِيُرِيَهٗ كَيْفَ يُوَارِيْ سَوْءَةَ اَخِيْهِط قَالَ يٰوَيْلَیٰٓی اَعَجَزْتُ اَنْ اَكُوْنَ مِثْلَ هٰذَا الْغُرَابِ فَاُوَارِيَ سَوْءَةَ اَخِیْج فَاَصْبَحَ مِنَ النّٰدِمِيْنَﯜ31ﯛ مِنْ اَجْلِ ذٰلِكَ ڔكَتَبْنَا عَلٰى بَنِيْٓ اِسْرَاۗئِيلَ اَنَّهٗ مَنْ قَتَلَ نَفْسًۢا بِغَيْرِ نَفْسٍ اَوْ فَسَادٍ فِي الْاَرْضِ فَكَاَنَّمَآ قَتَلَ النَّاسَ جَمِيْعًاط وَمَنْ اَحْيَاهَا فَكَاَنَّمَآ اَحْيَا النَّاسَ جَمِيْعًاط وَلَقَدْ جَاۗءَتْهُمْ رُسُلُنَا بِالْبَيِّنٰتِز ثُمَّ اِنَّ كَثِيْرًا مِّنْهُمْ بَعْدَ ذٰلِكَ فِي الْاَرْضِ لَمُسْرِفُوْنَﯜ32ﯛ اِنَّمَا جَزٰۗءؤُ الَّذِيْنَ يُحَارِبُوْنَ اللهَ وَرَسُوْلَهٗ وَيَسْعَوْنَ فِي الْاَرْضِ فَسَادًا اَنْ يُّقَتَّلُوْٓا اَوْ يُصَلَّبُوْٓا اَوْ تُقَطَّعَ اَيْدِيْهِمْ وَاَرْجُلُهُمْ مِّنْ خِلَافٍ اَوْ يُنْفَوْا مِنَ الْاَرْضِط ذٰلِكَ لَهُمْ خِزْيٌ فِي الدُّنْيَا وَلَهُمْ فِي الْاٰخِرَةِ عَذَابٌ عَظِيْمٌﯜ33ﯛ اِلَّا الَّذِيْنَ تَابُوْا مِنْ قَبْلِ اَنْ تَقْدِرُوْا عَلَيْهِمْج فَاعْلَمُوْٓا اَنَّ اللهَ غَفُوْرٌ مَّا فِي الْاَرْضِ جَمِيْعًا وَّمِثْلَهٗ مَعَهٗ لِيَفْتَدُوْا بِهٖ مِنْ عَذَابِ يَوْمِ الْقِيٰمَةِ مَا تُقُبِّلَ مِنْهُمْج وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ اَلِيْمٌﯜ36ﯛ يُرِيْدُوْنَ اَنْ يَّخْرُجُوْا مِنَ النَّارِ وَمَا هُمْ بِخٰرِجِيْنَ مِنْهَاز وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ مُّقِيْمٌﯜ37ﯛ وَالسَّارِقُ وَالسَّارِقَةُ فَاقْطَعُوْٓا اَيْدِيَهُمَا جَزَاۗءًۢ بِمَا كَسَبَا نَكَالًا مِّنَ اللهِط وَاللهُ عَزِيْزٌ حَكِيْمٌﯜ38ﯛ فَمَنْ تَابَ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ ظُلْمِهٖ وَاَصْلَحَ فَاِنَّ اللهَ يَتُوْبُ عَلَيْهِط اِنَّ اللهَ غَفُوْرٌ رَّحِيْمٌﯜ39ﯛ اَلَمْ تَعْلَمْ اَنَّ اللهَ لَهٗ مُلْكُ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضِط يُعَذِّبُ مَنْ يَّشَاۗءُ وَيَغْفِرُ لِمَنْ يَّشَاۗءُط وَاللهُ عَلٰى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيْرٌﯜ40ﯛ يٰٓاَيُّهَا الرَّسُوْلُ لَا يَحْزُنْكَ الَّذِيْنَ يُسَارِعُوْنَ فِي الْكُفْرِ مِنَ الَّذِيْنَ قَالُوْٓا اٰمَنَّا اَنْ يُّطَهِّرَ قُلُوْبَهُمْط لَهُمْ فِي الدُّنْيَا خِزْيٌ ښ وَّلَهُمْ فِي الْاٰخِرَةِ عَذَابٌ عَظِيْمٌﯜ41ﯛ سَمّٰعُوْنَ لِلْكَذِبِ اَكّٰلُوْنَ لِلسُّحْتِط فَاِنْ جَاۗءُوْكَ فَاحْكُمْ بَيْنَهُمْ اَوْ اَعْرِضْ عَنْهُمْج وَاِنْ تُعْرِضْ عَنْهُمْ فَلَنْ يَّضُرُّوْكَ شَيْئًاط وَاِنْ حَكَمْتَ فَاحْكُمْ بَيْنَهُمْ بِالْقِسْطِط اِنَّ اللهَ يُحِبُّ الْمُقْسِطِيْنَﯜ42ﯛ وَكَيْفَ يُحَكِّمُوْنَكَ وَعِنْدَهُمُ التَّوْرٰةُ فِيْهَا حُكْمُ اللهِ ثُمَّ يَتَوَلَّوْنَ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ ذٰلِكَط وَمَآ اُوْلٰۗئِكَ بِالْمُؤْمِنِيْنَﯜ43ﯛ اِنَّآ اَنْزَلْنَا التَّوْرٰةَ فِيْهَا هُدًى وَّنُوْرٌج يَحْكُمُ بِهَا يَعْقِلُوْنَﯜ58ﯛ قُلْ يٰٓاَهْلَ الْكِتٰبِ هَلْ تَنْقِمُوْنَ مِنَّآ اِلَّآ اَنْ اٰمَنَّا بِاللهِ وَمَآ اُنْزِلَ اِلَيْنَا وَمَآ اُنْزِلَ مِنْ قَبْلُلا وَاَنَّ اَكْثَرَكُمْ فٰسِقُوْنَﯜ59ﯛ قُلْ هَلْ اُنَبِّئُكُمْ بِشَرٍّ مِّنْ ذٰلِكَ مَثُوْبَةً عِنْدَ اللهِط مَنْ لَّعَنَهُ اللهُ وَغَضِبَ عَلَيْهِ وَجَعَلَ مِنْهُمُ الْقِرَدَةَ وَالْخَنَازِيْرَ وَعَبَدَ الطَّاغُوْتَط اُوْلٰۗئِكَ شَرٌّ مَّكَانًا يُنْفِقُ كَيْفَ يَشَاۗءُط وَلَيَزِيْدَنَّ كَثِيْرًا مِّنْهُمْ مَّآ اُنْزِلَ اِلَيْكَ مِنْ رَّبِّكَ طُغْيَانًا وَّكُفْرًاط وَاَلْقَيْنَا بَيْنَهُمُ الْعَدَاوَةَ وَالْبَغْضَاۗءَ اِلٰى يَوْمِ الْقِيٰمَةِط كُلَّمَآ اَوْقَدُوْا نَارًا لِّلْحَرْبِ اَطْفَاَهَا اللهُلا وَيَسْعَوْنَ فِي الْاَرْضِ فَسَادًاط وَاللهُ لَا يُحِبُّ الْمُفْسِدِيْنَﯜ64ﯛ وَلَوْ اَنَّ اَهْلَ الْكِتٰبِ اٰمَنُوْا رَّبِّكَط وَاِنْ لَّمْ تَفْعَلْ فَمَا بَلَّغْتَ رِسَالَتَهٗط وَاللهُ يَعْصِمُكَ مِنَ النَّاسِط اِنَّ اللهَ لَا يَهْدِي الْقَوْمَ الْكٰفِرِيْنَﯜ67ﯛ قُلْ يٰٓاَهْلَ الْكِتٰبِ لَسْتُمْ عَلٰى شَيْءٍ حَتّٰى 5 | MAAIDA | 120

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Imaan waalo apne ahed-o-paimaan aur mamlaat ki pa-bandi karo tumhaare liye tamaam chopaaye halaal kar diye gaye hai alaawa unke jo tumhe padh kar soonaye ja rahe hai. Magar haale ehraam mein shikaar ko halaal mat samajh lena. Be-shak Allah jo chahta hukm deta hai.

2. Imaan waalo! khabar-daar! Khuda ki nishaaniya ki hurmat ko zaaya na karna aur na mohtaram mahine. qurbaani ke jaanwar aur jin jaanwaro ke gale (gardan/neck) mein pattee bandh diye gayi hai aur jo log khana-e-Khuda ka iraada karne waale hai aur farze Parwardigaar aur raza-e-ilaahi ke talab-gaar hai Unki hurmat ki khilaaf warzi na karna aur jab ehraam khatm ho jaaye to shikaar karo aur khabar-daar! Kisi qaum ki adaawat faqat is baat par keh us ne tumhe masjidul haraam se rok diya hai tumhe zulm par amaada na kar de neki aur taqwa par ek dusre ki madad karo aur gunah aur ta’adi (had se guzar jane) par aapas mein taawoon na karna aur Allah se darte rehna ke uska azaab bohot sakht hai.

3. Tumhare upar haraam kar diya gaya hai murdaar khoon soowwar ka gosht aur jo jaanwar ghair-e-Khuda ke naam par zibah kiya jaaye aur munkhaka (gala ghot kar maara jaaye) aur mawquza (tadpa ke maara gaya) aur mutradiya (bulandiyo se phaik kar maara gaya) aur nateeha (dusre haiwaan se lada ke mare) aur jis ko darinde kha jaaye magar yeh ke tum khud zibah kar lo aur jo nisaab par zibah kiya jaaye aur jiski teero ke zariye qura andazi karo keh yeh sab fisq hai aur kuffaar tumhaare deen se maayoos ho gaye hai lehaaza tum un se na daro aur mujh se daro aaj mai ne tumhaare liye deen ko kaamil kar diya hai aur apni nemato ko tamaam kar diya hai aur tumhaare liye deen-e-Islaam ko pasandeedah bana diya hai lehaaza jo shakhs bhook mein majboor ho jaaye aur gunah ki taraf maael na ho to Khuda bada bakhshne waala maher-baan hai.

4. Paighambar yeh tum se sawaal karte hai keh unke liye kya halaal kiya gaya hai to kahe dijiye keh tumhaare liye tamaam pakeeza cheeze halaal hai aur jo kuch tum ne shikaari kutto ko sikha rakkha hai aur Khudai taaleem mein se kuch unke hawaale kar diya hai to jo kuch woh pakad ke laaye usey khaalo aur us par naam-e-Khuda zaroor lo aur Allah se daro keh woh bohot jald hisaab karne waala hai.

5. Aaj tumhaare liye tamaam pakeeza cheeze halaal kar di gayi hai aur Ahle kitaab ka ta’aam (khaana) bhi tumhaare liye halaal hai aur tumhaara ta’aam unke liye halaal hai aur Ahle imaan ki azaad aur paak daaman aurte’n ya unki azaad aurte’n jinko tum se pehle kitaab di gayi hai tumhaare liye halaal hai ba-sharte keh tum unki ujrat de do pakeezgi ke saath na khullam-khulla zina ki ujrat ke taur par aur na poshida taur par dosti ke andaaz se aur jo bhi imaan se inkaar karega uske aamaal yaqeenan barbaad ho jaayege aur woh aakherat mein ghaata (nuksaan) uthaane waalo mein hoga.

6. Imaan waalo jab bhi namaaz ke liye utho to pehle apne chehro ko aur koniyo (L-bow) tak apne haatho ko dho (wash) aur apne sar aur gate (ankles) tak pairo ka masaah karo aur agar janaabat ki haalat mein ho to ghusl karo aur agar mareez ho ya safar ke aalam mein ho ya paikhana waghera nikal aaya hai ya aurto’n ko ba-ham lams (jima) kiya hai aur paani na miley to paak mitti se tayammum kar lo. Is tarah keh apne chehre aur haatho ka masaah kar lo keh Khuda tumhaare liye kisi tarah ki zehmat nahi chahta. Bal-keh yeg chahta hai keh tumhe paak-o-pakeeza bana de aur tum par apni nemat ko tamaam kar de shaayad tum is tarah uske shukr-guzaar bande ban jaawo.

7. Aur apne upar Allah ki nemat aur uske us ahed ko yaad karo jo us ne tum se liya hai jab tum ne yeh kaha keh ham ne soon liya aur ita’at ki aur khabar-daar! Allah se darte raho keh Allah dil ke raazo ka bhi jaanne waala hai.

8. Imaan waalo Khuda ke liye qayaam karne waale aur insaaf ke saath gawahi dene waale bano aur khabar-daar! Kisi qaum ki adaawat tumhe is baat par amaada na kar de ke insaaf ko tark kar do insaaf karo keh yahi taqwa se qareeb-tar hai aur Allah se darte raho keh Allah tumhaare aamaal se khoob ba-khabar hai.

9. Allah ne sahebaan-e-imaan aur amal-e-saaleh waalo se waada kiya hai keh unke liye maghferat aur ajre azeem hai.

10. Aur jin logo ne kufr ikhtiyaar kiya aur hamari aayaat ki takzeeb (joothlaaye) ki woh sab ke sab jahannami hai.

11. Imaan waalo! Apne upar Allah ki us nemat ko yaad karo keh jab ek qaum ne tumhaari taraf haath badhaane ka iraada kiya to Khuda ne unke haatho ko tum tak pohonchne se rok diya aur Allah se darte raho keh sahebaan-e-imaan Allah hi par bharosa karte hai.

12. Aur be-shak Allah ne bani Israeel se ahed liya aur ham ne un mein se baara naqeeb (sarparast/leader) bheje aur Khuda ne yeh bhi kahe diya keh ham tumhaare saath hai agar tum ne namaaz qaayem ki aur zakaat adaa ki aur hamare Rasoolo par imaan le aaye aur unki madad ki aur Khuda ki raah mein karz-e-hasna diya to ham yaqeenan tumhaari buraaiyo se dar-guzar karege aur tumhe un baaghaat mein daakhil karege jin ke neeche nehre jaari hogi phir jo iske baad kufr karega to woh bohot bure raaste ki taraf bahek gaya hai.

13. Phir unki ahed shikani ki bina par Ham ne un par laanat ki aur unke dilo ko sakht bana diya woh hamare kalemaat ko unki jagah se hata dete hai aur unho ne hamari yaad-dahaani ka aksar hissa faraamosh kar diya hai aur tum unki khayaanato par baraabar muttala (agah) hote rahoge alaawa chand afraad ke lehaaza un se dar-guzar karo aur unki taraf se kinaara-kashi karo keh Allah ehsaan karne waalo ko dost rakhta hai.

14. Aur jin logo ka kehna hai keh ham nasrani hai Ham ne un se bhi ahed liya aur unho ne bhi hamari nasihat (Injeel) ke ek hisse ko faraamosh kar diya to Ham ne bator saza unke darmiyaan adaawat aur bughz ko qayamat tak keh liye raasta de diya aur an-qareeb Khuda unhe un baato se ba-khabar karega jo woh anjaam de rahe thay.

15. Ay ahle kitaab tumhaare paas hamara Rasool aa chuka hai jo un mein se bohot si baato ki wazaahat kar raha hai jin ko tum kitaab-e-Khuda mein se chhupa rahe thay aur bohot si baato se dar-guzar bhi karta hai tumhaare paas Khuda ki taraf se noor aur roshan kitaab aa chuki hai.

16. Jiske zariye Khuda apni khushnudi ka itteba karne waalo ko salaamti ke raasto ki hidaayat karta hai aur unhe taarikiyo se nikaal kar apne hukm se noor ki taraf le aata hai aur unhe siraat-e-mustaqeem ki hidaayat karta hai.

17. Yaqeenan woh log kaafir hai jin ka kehna yeh hai keh Maseeh alayhis salaam ibne Maryam hi Allah hai. Paighambar aap un se kahe keh phir Khuda ke muqaable mein kaun kis amr ka saheb-e-ikhtiyaar hoga agar woh Maseeh alayhis salaam bin Maryam aur unki maa aur saare ahle zameen ko maar daalna chahe aur Allah hi ke liye zameen-o-aasmaan aur unke darmiyaan ki kul hukumat hai. Woh jise bhi chahta hai paiyda karta hai aur woh har shaiy par qudrat rakhne waala hai.

18. Aur yahoodiyo ne yeh kehna shuru kar diya keh ham Allah ke farzand aur uske dost hai to Paighambar aap kahe dijiye keh phir Khuda tum par tumhaare gunaho ki bina par azaab kyu’n karta hai? Bal-keh tum uski makhluqaat mein se bashar ho aur woh jis ko chahta hai bakhsh deta hai aur jis par chahta hai azaab karta hai aur uske liye zameen-o-aasmaan aur unke darmiyaan ki kul qaayenaat hai aur usi ki taraf sab ki baaz-gasht (wapsi) hai.

19. Ay Ahle kitaab tumhaare paas Rasoolo ke ek waqfe ke baad hamara yeh Rasool aaya hai keh tum yeh na kaho ke hamare paas koi basheer-o-nazeer nahi aaya tha to lo yeh basheer-o-nazeer aa gaya hai aur Khuda har shaiy par qaadir hai.

20. Aur us waqt ko yaad karo jab Moosa alayhis salaam ne apni qaum se kaha keh ay qaum apne upar Allah ki nemat ko yaad karo jab us ne tum mein ambiya qaraar diye aur tumhe badshah banaya aur tumhe woh sab-kuch de diya jo aalameen mein kisi ko nahi diya tha.

21. Aur ay qaum us arze muqaddas mein daakhil ho jaawo jise Allah ne tumhaare liye likh diya hai aur maidaan se ulte paau na palat jaawo keh ulte khasaare (nuksaan) waalo mein se ho jaawoge.

22. Un logo ne kaha keh Moosa alayhis salaam wahan to jaabiro (zaleemo) ki qaum abaad hai aur ham us waqt tak daakhil na hoge jab tak woh wahan se nikal na jaaye phir agar woh nikal gaye to ham yaqeenan daakhil ho jaayege.

23. Magar do insaan jinhe Khuda ka khauf tha aur un par Khuda ne fazl-o-karam kiya tha unho ne kaha ke unke darwaaze se daakhil ho jaawo. Agar tum darwaaze mein daakhil ho gaye to yaqeenan ghaalib aa jaawoge aur Allah par bharosa karo agar tum sahebaan-e-imaan ho.

24. Un logo ne kaha ke Moosa alayhis salaam ham harghiz wahan daakhil na hoge jab tak woh log wahan hai. Aap apne Parwardigaar ke saath ja kar jangh kijiye ham yahan baithay huwe hai.

25. Moosa alayhis salaam ne kaha Parwardigaar mai sirf apni zaat aur apne bhai ka ikhtiyaar rakhta hu lehaaza hamare aur is faasiq qaum ke darmiyaan judaayi paiyda kar de.

26. Irshaad huwa keh ab un par chaalees (40) saal haraam kar diye gaye keh yeh zameen mein chakkar lagaate rahege lehaaza tum is faasiq qaum par afsos na karo.

27. Aur Paighambar alayhis salaam aap un ko Aadam alayhis salaam ke dono farzando ka sachcha qissa padh kar soonaayiye keh jab dono ne qurbaani di aur ek ki qurbaani qabool ho gayi aur dusre ki na huwi to us ne kaha ke mai tujhe qatl kar dunga to dusre ne jawaab diya ke mera kya qasoor hai Khuda sirf sahebaan-e-taqwa ke aamaal ko qabool karta hai.

28. Agar aap meri taraf qatl ke liye haath badhayenge bhi to mai aap ki taraf harghiz haath na badhaunga keh mai aalameen ke paalne waale Khuda se darta ho.

29. Mai chahta hu keh aap mere aur apne dono ke gunaho ka markaz ban jaaye aur Jahannamiyo mein shaamil ho jaaiye ke zaalemeen ki yahi saza hai.

30. Phir uske nafs ne usey bhai ke qatl par amaada kar diya aur us ne usey qatl kar diya aur woh khasaare (nuksaan) waalo mein shaamil ho gaya.

31. Phir Khuda ne ek kawwa (crow) bheja jo zameen khod raha tha keh usey dikhlaaye ke bhai ki laash ko kis tarah chhupaayega to us ne kaha keh afsos mai is kawwe ke jaisa bhi na ho saka keh apne bhai ki laash ko zameen mein chhupa deta aur is tarah woh naademeen aur pasheman logo mein shaamil ho gaya.

32. Isi bina par ham ne bani Israeel par likh diya keh jo shakhs kisi nafs ko kisi nafs ke badle ya ruye zameen mein fasaad ki saza ke alaawa qatl kar daalega us ne goya saare insaano ko qatl kar diya aur jisne ek nafs ko zindagi de di us ne goya saare insaano ko zindagi de di aur bani Israeel ke paas hamare Rasool khuli huwi nishaaniya le kar aaye magar iske baad bhi un mein ke aksar log zameen mein zeyaadatiya karne waale hi rahe.

33. Bas Khuda-o-Rasool se jangh karne waale aur zameen mein fasaad karne waalo ki saza yahi hai keh unhe qatl kar diya jaaye ya suli par chadha diya jaaye ya unke haath aur pair mukhtalif simt se qataa kar diye jaaye ya unhe arze watan se nikaal baahar kiya jaaye. Yeh unke liye duniya mein ruswaai hai aur unke liye aakherat mein azaab-e-azeem hai.

34. Alaawa un logo ke jo tumhaare qaabu mein aane se pehle hi tauba kar le to samajh lo keh Khuda bada bakhshne waala maher-baan hai.

35. Imaan waalo Allah se daro aur us tak pohonchne ka waseela talaash karo aur uski raah mein jehaad karo ke shaayad is tarah kaam-yaab ho jaawo.

36. Yaqeenan jin logo ne kufr ikhtiyaar kiya agar unke paas saari zameen ka sarmaya ho aur utna hi aur shaamil kar de ke roz-e-qayamat ke azaab ka badla ho jaaye to yeh muawza (badla) qabool na kiya jaayega aur unke liye dard-naak azaab hoga.

37. Yeh log chahte hai Jahannam se nikal jaaye haala keh yeh nikalne waale nahi hai aur unke liye ek mustaqil (permanent) azaab hai.

38. Chor mard aur chor aurat dono ke haath kaat do, keh yeh unke liye badla aur Khuda ki taraf se ek saza hai aur Khuda saheb-e-izzat bhi hai aur saheb-e-hikmat bhi hai.

39. Phir zulm ke baad jo shakhs tauba kar le aur apni islaah kar le to Khuda uski tauba qabool kar lega, keh Allah bada bakhshne waala aur maher-baan hai.

40. Kya tum ko nahi maaloom ke zameen-o-aasmaan ka mulk sirf Khuda hi ke liye hai woh jis par chahta hai azaab karta hai aur jis ko chahta hai bakhsh deta hai aur Allah har shaiy par qaadir-o-mukhtaar hai.

41. Ay Rasool! Imaan ka daawa karne waalo mein jo log kufr ki taraf jald baazi se badh rahe hai unki harkaat se aap ranjida na ho. Yeh sirf zabaan se imaan ka naam lete hai aur unke dil momin nahi hai aur yahoodiyo mein se bhi baaz aise hai jo joothi baate soonte hai aur dusri qaum waale jo aap ke paas haazir nahi huwe unhe soonaate hai yeh kalemaat ko unki jagah se hata dete hai aur logo se kehte hai keh agar Paighambar ki taraf se yahi diya jaaye to le lena aur agar yeh na diya jaaye to parhez karna aur jiske fitne ke baare mein Khuda iraada kar le uske baare mein aap ka koi ikhtiyaar nahi hai yeh woh afraad hai jin ke baare mein Khuda ne yeh iraada hi nahi kiya ke zabardasti unke dilo ko paak kar dega. Unke liye duniya mein bhi ruswaai hai aur aakherat mein bhi azaab-e-azeem hai.

42. Yeh jooth ke soonne waale aur haraam ke khaane waale hai lehaaza agar aap ke paas muqadma le kar aaye to aap ko ikhtiyaar hai ke faisla kar de ya earaz kar le (munh moud le) keh agar earaz (munh moud le) bhi kar lege to yeh aap ko koi nuksaan na pohoncha sakayge lekin agar faisla hi kare to insaaf ke saath kare keh Khuda insaaf karne waalo ko dost rakhta hai.

43. Aur yeh kis tarah aap se faisla karaayege jab-keh unke paas Taurait maujood hai jis mein hukm-e-Khuda bhi hai aur yeh iske baad bhi mooh pher lete hai aur phir imaan laane waale nahi hai.

44. Be-shak ham ne Taurait ko naazil kiya hai jis mein hidaayat aur noor hai aur iske zariye ita’at guzaar ambiya yahoodiyo ke liye faisla karte hai aur Allah waale aur olma-e-yahood is cheez se faisla karte hai jiska kitaab-e-Khuda mein un ko muhaafiz banaya gaya hai aur jiske yeh gawah bhi hai lehaaza tum un logo se na daro sirf ham se daro aur khabar-daar! Thodi si qeemat ke liye hamari aayaat ka kaar-o-baar na karna aur jo bhi hamare naazil kiye huwe qaanoon ke mutaabiq faisla na karega woh sab kaafir shumaar hoge.

45. Aur ham ne Taurait mein yeh bhi likh diya hai ke jaan ka badla jaan aur aankh ka badla aankh aur naak ka badla naak aur kaan ka badla kaan aur daant ka badla daant hai aur zakhmo ka bhi badla liya jaayega ab agar koi shakhs maaf kar de to yeh uske gunaho ka bhi kaffaara ho jaayega aur jo bhi Khuda ke naazil kardah hukm ke khilaaf faisla karega woh zaalimo mein se shumaar hoga.

46. Aur ham ne un hi ambiya ke naqsh-e-qadam par Isa alayhis salaam bin Maryam ko chala diya jo apne saamne ki Taurait ki tasdeeq (confirmation) karne waale thay aur ham ne unhe Injeel de di jis mein hidaayat aur noor tha aur woh apne saamne ki Tauraitki tasdeeq (confirmation) karne waali aur hidaayat thi aur sahebaan-e-taqwa ke liye saamaane nasihat thi.

47. Ahle injeel ko chaahiye ke Khuda ne jo hukm naazil kiya hai uske mutaabiq faisla kare keh jo bhi tanzile (hukme) Khuda ke mutaabiq faisla na karega woh faasiqo mein shumaar hoga.

48. Aur ay Paighambar ham ne aap ki taraf kitaab naazil ki hai jo apne pehle ki Taurait aur Injeel ki tasdeeq (confirmation) aur muhaafiz hai lehaaza aap unke darmiyaan tanzile Khuda ke mutaabiq faisla kare aur Khuda ki taraf se aaye huwe haq se alag ho kar unki khwahishaat ka itteba na kare. Ham ne sab ke liye alag-alag sharaaet aur raasta muqarrar kar diya hai aur Khuda chahta to sab ko ek hi ummat bana deta lekin woh apne diye huwe qaanoon se tumhaari azmaish karna chahta hai lehaaza tum sab nekiyo ki taraf sabqat karo keh tum sab ki baaz-gasht (wapsi) Allah hi ki taraf hai. Wahan Woh tumhe un tamaam baato se ba-khabar karega jin mein tum ikhtelaaf kar rahe thay.

49. Aur Paighambar aap unke darmiyaan tanzile Khuda ke mutaabiq faisla kare aur unki khwahishaat ka itteba na kare aur is baat se bachte rahe keh yeh baaz ehkaam-e-ilaahi se munharif kar (bhatka) de. Phir agar yeh khud munharif ho (bhatak) jaaye to yaad rakhna keh Khuda un ko unke baaz gunaho ki museebat mein mubtala karna chahta hai aur insaano ki aksariyat faasiq aur daira-e-ita’at se khaarij hai.

50. Kya yeh log jaaheliyat ka hukm chahte hai jab-keh sahebaan-e-yaqeen ke liye Allah ke faisla se behtar kis ka faisla ho sakta hai.

51. Imaan waalo! yahoodiyo aur Isaaiyo ko apna dost aur sarparast na banaawo ke yeh khud aapas mein ek dusre ke dost hai aur tum mein se koi unhe dost banaayega to un hi mein shumaar ho jaayega be-shak Allah zaalim qaum ki hidaayat nahi karta hai.

52. Paighambar aap dekhenge keh jin ke dilo mein nifaaq ki bimaari hai woh doud-doud kar unki taraf ja rahe hai aur yeh uzr (excuse) bayaan karte hai keh hamein gardish-e zamaane ka khauf hai. Pas an-qareeb Khuda apni taraf se fatah ya koi dusra amr le aayega to yeh apne dil ke chhupaaye huwe raaz par pasheman ho jaayege.

53. Aur sahebaan-e-imaan Kahenge keh kya yahi log hai jinho ne naam-e-Khuda par sakht-tareen qasme khaai thi ke ham tumhaare saath hai to ab unke aamaal barbaad ho gaye aur yeh khasaare (nuksaan) waale ho gaye.

54. Imaan waalo! Tum mein se jo bhi apne deen se palat jaayega, to an-qareeb Khuda ek qaum ko le aayega jo uski mehboob aur us se mohabbat karne waali, momenin ke saamne khaak-saar (naram mizaaj) aur kufr ke saamne saheb-e-izzat, raahe Khuda mein jehaad karne waali aur kisi malaamat karne waale ki malaamat ki parwah na karne waali hogi. Yeh fazle Khuda hai woh jise chahta hai ataa karta hai aur woh saheb-e-wus’at aur aleem-o-dana bhi hai.

55. Imaan waalo bas tumhaara wali Allah hai aur uska Rasool aur woh sahebaan-e-imaan jo namaaz qaayem karte hai aur haalate ruku mein zakaat dete hai.

56. Aur jo bhi Allah, Rasool aur sahebaan-e-imaan ko apna sarparast banaayega to Allah ki hi jamaat ghaalib aane waali hai.

57. Imaan waalo khabar-daar! Ahle kitaab mein jin logo ne tumhaare deen ko mazaaq aur tamaasha bana liya hai aur digar kuffaar ko bhi apna wali aur sarparast na banaana aur Allah se darte rehna agar tum waaqeyi saheb-e-imaan ho.

58. Aur jab tum namaaz ke liye azaan dete ho to yeh us ko mazaaq aur khel bana lete hai is liye keh yeh bilkul be-aql qaum hai.

59. Paighambar aap kahe dijiye keh ay Ahle kitaab kya tum ham se sirf is baat par naraaz ho keh ham Allah aur us ne jo kuch hamari taraf ya ham se pehle naazil kiya hai un sab par imaan rakhte hai aur tumhaari aksariyat faasiq aur na-farmaan hai.

60. Kya ham tum ko Khuda ke nazdeek ek manzil ke etebaar se is se badtar aib ki khabar de ke jis par Khuda ne laanat aur ghazab naazil kiya hai aur un mein se baaz ko bandar aur suwwar bana diya hai aur jisne bhi taaghoot ki ibaadat ki hai woh jagah ke etebaar se bad-tareen aur seedhe raasta se intehaai bahe-ka huwa hai.

61. Aur yeh jab tumhaare paas aate hai to kehte hai keh ham imaan le aaye hai. haala keh yeh kufr hi ke saath daakhil huwe hai aur isi ko le kar nikle hai aur Khuda un sab baato ko khoob jaanta hai, jinko yeh chhupaaye huwe hai.

62. Un mein se bohot so’n ko aap dekhenge keh woh gunah aur zulm aur haram-khori mein daudte-phirte hai aur yeh bad-tareen kaam anjaam de rahe hai.

63. Aakhir unhe Allah waale aur olama unke jooth bolne aur haraam khaane se kyu’n nahi mana karte? yeh yaqeenan bohot bura kar rahe hai.

64. Aur yahoodi kehte hai keh Khuda ke haath bandhe huwe hai jab-keh asl mein un hi ke haath bandhe huwe hai aur yeh apne qaul ki bina par mal’oon hai aur Khuda ke dono haath khule huwe hai aur woh jis tarah chahta hai kharch karta hai aur jo kuch aap par Parwardigaar ki taraf se naazil huwa hai uska inkaar un mein se bohot so’n ke kufr aur unki sar-kashi ko our badha dega aur ham ne unke darmiyaan qayamat tak keh liye adaawat aur bughz paiyda kar diya hai keh jab bhi jangh ki aag bhadkaana chahenge Khuda bujha dega aur yeh zameen mein fasaad ki koshish kar rahe hai aur Khuda mufseedo (fasaadi) ko dost nahi rakhta.

65. Aur agar Ahle kitaab imaan le aate aur ham se darte rehte to ham unke gunaho ko maaf kar dete aur unhe nemato ke baaghaat mein daakhil kar de te.

66. Aur agar yeh log Taurait-o-Injeel aur jo kuch unki taraf Parwardigaar ki taraf se naazil kiya gaya hai sab ko qaayem karte to apne upar aur qadmo ke neeche se rizq-e-Khuda haasil karte, un mein se ek qaum miyana-ro hai aur zeyada hissa log bad-tareen aamaal anjaam de rahe hai.

67. Ay Paighambar! Aap us hukm ko pohoncha de jo aap ke Parwardigaar ki taraf se naazil kiya gaya hai aur agar aap ne yeh na kiya to goya uske paighaam ko nahi pohonchaya aur Khuda aap ko logo ke shar se mehfooz rakkhega keh Allah kaafiro ki hidaayat nahi karta hai.

68. Kahe dijiye keh ay ahle kitaab tumhaara koi mazhab nahi hai jab tak Taurait-o-Injeel aur jo kuch Parwardigaar ki taraf se naazil huwa hai usey qaayem na karo aur jo kuch aap ke paas Parwardigaar ki taraf se naazil huwa hai woh unki kaseer tadaad ki sar-kashi aur kufr mein izaafah kar dega to aap kaafiro ke haal par ranjida na ho.

69. Be-shak jo log saheb-e-imaan hai ya yahoodi aur sitaara parast aur isaayi hai un mein se jo bhi Allah aur aakherat par waaqeyi imaan laayega aur amal-e-saaleh karega uske liye na khauf hai aur na usey huzn (gham) hoga.

70. Ham ne bani Israeel se ahed liya hai aur unki taraf bohot se Rasool beje hai lekin jab unke paas koi Rasool unki khwahish ke khilaaf hukm le aaya to unho ne ek jamaat ki takzeeb ki (juthlaaya) aur ek giroh ko qatl kar dete hai.

71. Aur un logo ne khayaal kiya keh us mein koi kharaabi na hogi isi liye yeh haqayiq se andhe aur behre ho gaye hai. Iske baad Khuda ne unki tauba qabool kar-li lekin phir bhi aksariyat andhi behri ho gayi aur Khuda unke tamaam aamaal par gehri nighah rakhta hai.

72. Yaqeenan woh log kaafir hai jin ka kehna hai keh Allah hi Maseeh alayhis salaam bin Maryam hai. Jab-keh khud Maseeh ka kehna hai keh ay bani Israeel apne aur mere Parwardigaar ki ibaadat karo. Jo koi uska shareek qaraar dega us par Khuda ne jannat haraam kar di hai aur uska anjaam Jahannam hai aur zaaleemo ka koi madad-gaar na hoga.

73. Yaqeenan woh log kaafir hai jin ka kehna yeh hai keh Allah teen mein ka teesra hai. haala keh Allah ke alaawa koi Khuda nahi hai aur agar yeh log apne qaul se baaz na aayege to un mein se kufr ikhtiyaar karne waalo par dard-naak azaab naazil ho jaayega.

74. Yeh log Khuda ki baargah mein tauba aur isteghfaar kyu’n nahi karte jab-keh Allah bohot bakhshne waala aur maher-baan hai.

75. Maseeh alayhis salaam bin Maryam kuch nahi hai sirf hamare Rasool hai, jin se pehle bohot se Rasool guzar chuke hai aur unki maa siddiqa thi aur woh dono khaana khaaya karte thay. Dekho ham apni nishaaniya ko kis tarah waazeh kar ke bayaan karte hai aur phir dekho keh yeh log kis tarah baheke ja rahe hai.

76. Paighambar aap un se kahiye keh kya tum Allah ko chhod kar unki ibaadat karte ho jo tumhaare liye nafa aur nuksaan ke maalik bhi nahi hai aur Khuda soonne waala bhi hai aur jaanne waala bhi hai.

77. Paighambar! Aap kahe dijiye keh ay ahle kitaab apne deen mein na-haq gulloow (had se aage badhne) se kaam na lo aur is qaum ki khwahishaat ka itteba na karo jo pehle se gumraah ho chuki hai aur bohot se logo ko gumraah bhi kar chuki hai aur seedhe raasta se bahek chuki hai.

78. Bani Israeel mein se kufr ikhtiyaar karne waalo par Janab-e-Dawood alayhis salaam aur Janab-e-Isa alayhis salaam ki zabaan se laanat ki ja chuki hai keh un logo ne na-farmaani ki aur hamesha hadd se tajaawuz kiya karte thay.

79. Unho ne jo buraayi bhi ki hai us se baaz nahi aate thay aur bad-tareen kaam kiya karte thay.

80. Un mein se aksar ko aap dekhenge keh yeh kuffaar se dosti karte hai. Unho ne apne nafs ke liye jo saamaan pehle se faraaham kiya hai woh bohot bura saamaan hai jis par Khuda un se naraaz hai aur woh azaab mein hamesha rehne waale hai.

81. Agar yeh log Allah aur nabi Moosa alayhis salaam aur jo kuch un par naazil huwa hai sab par imaan rakhte to harghiz kuffaar ko dost na banaate lekin un mein se bohot se log faasiq aur na-farmaan hai.

82. Aap dekhenge ke sahebaan-e-imaan se sab se zeyada adaawat rakhne waale yahoodi aur mushriq hai aur unki mohabbat se sab se zeyada qareeb woh log hai jo kehte hai keh ham nasrani hai. Yeh is liye hai keh un mein bohot se qasees (aaleem) aur raahib (dunya ko tark karne waale) paaye jaate hai aur yeh mutaqabbir aur buraayi karne waale nahi hai.

83. Aur jab us kalaam ko soonte hai jo Rasool par naazil huwa hai to tum dekhte ho keh unki aankho se be-saakhta (be-qaboo) ansoo jaari ho jaate hai keh unho ne haq ko pehchaan liya hai aur kehte hai keh Parwardigaar ham imaan le aaye hai lehaaza hamara naam bhi tasdeeq (confirmation) karne waalo mein darj (enroll) kar le.

84. Bhala ham Allah aur apne paas aane waale haq par kis tarah imaan na laayege jab-keh hamari khwahish hai keh Parwardigaar hamein nek kirdaar bando mein shaamil kar le.

85. To is qaul ki bina par Parwardigaar ne unhe woh baaghaat de diye jin ke neeche nehre bahe rahi hai aur woh unhi mein hamesha rehne waale hai keh yahi nek kirdaar logo ki jaza hai.

86. Aur jin logo ne kufr ikhtiyaar kar liya aur hamari aayaat ko joothla diya to woh log jahannami hai.

87. Imaan waalo jin cheezo ko Khuda ne tumhaare liye halaal kiya hai unhe haraam na banaawo aur hadd se tajaawuz na karo keh Khuda tajaawuz karne waalo ko dost nahi rakhta hai.

88. Aur jo us ne rizqe halaal-o-pakeeza diya hai us ko khaawo aur us Khuda se darte raho jis par imaan rakhne waale ho.

89. Khuda tum se be-maqsad qasme khaane par muakhza (hisaab kitaab) nahi karta hai lekin jin qasmo ki girah dil ne bandh li hai unki mukhaalefat ka kaffaara das miskeeno ke liye ousaat (medium) darje ka khaana hai jo apne ghar waalo ko khilaate ho ya unka kapda ya ek ghulam ki azaadi hai. Phir agar yeh sab na-mumkin ho to teen roze rakkho. Keh yeh tumhaari qasmo ka kaffaara hai jab bhi tum qasam kha kar uski mukhaalefat karo. Apni qasmo ka tahaffuz karo keh Khuda is tarah apni aayaat ko waazeh kar ke bayaan karta hai keh shaayad tum uske shukr-guzaar bande ban jaawo.

90. Imaan waalo! Sharaab, jooa, booth (statue), pansa (lottery) yeh sab gande shaitaani aamaal hai lehaaza un se parhez karo ta-keh kaam-yaabi haasil kar sako.

91. Shaitaan to bas yahi chahta hai keh sharaab aur jooe ke baare mein tumhaare darmiyaan bughz aur adaawat paiyda kar de aur tumhe yaade Khuda aur namaaz se rok de to kya tum waaqeyi ruk jaawoge.

92. Aur dekho Allah ki ita’at karo aur Rasool ki ita’at karo aur na-farmaani se bachte raho warna agar tum ne rugardaani ki to jaan lo ke Rasool ki zimme-daari sirf waazeh paighaam ka pohoncha dena hai.

93. Jo log imaan laaye aur unho ne nek aamaal kiye unke liye us mein koi harj nahi hai jo kuch kha-pi chuke hai, jab keh woh muttaqi ban gaye aur imaan le aaye aur nek aamaal kiye aur parhez kiya aur imaan le aaye aur phir parhez kiya aur nek amal kiya aur nek aamaal karne waalo hi ko dost rakhte hai.

94. Imaan waalo! Allah un shikaaro ke zariye tumhaara imtehaan zaroor lega jin tak tumhaare haath aur naize pohonch jaate hai ta-keh woh yeh dekhe keh is se logo ke gaa-e-baana (invisible) mein bhi kaun Kaun darta hai phir jo iske baad bhi zayadti karega uske liye dard-naak azaab hai.

95. Imaan waalo haale ehraam mein shikaar ko na maaro aur jo tum mein qasdan aisa karega uski saza unhi jaanwaro ke baraabar hai jinhe qatl kiya hai jiska faisla tum mein se do aadil afraad kare aur is qurbaani ko kaabe tak jaana chaahiye ya masaakeen ke khaane ki shakl mein kaffaara diya jaaye ya uske baraabar roze rakkhe jaaye ta-keh apne kaam ke anjaam ka maza chakhe. Allah ne guzishta mamlaat ko maaf kar diya hai lekin ab jo dobaara shikaar karega to us se inteqaam lega aur woh sab par ghaalib aane waala aur badla lene waala hai.

96. Tumhare liye daryai jaanwar ka shikaar karna aur uska khaana halaal kar diya gaya hai keh tumhaare liye aur qaaflo ke liye faaide ka zariya hai aur tumhaare liye khushki (zameen) ka shikaar haraam kar diya gaya hai jab tak haalat ehraam mein raho aur us Khuda se darte raho jiski baargah mein haazir hona hai.

97. Allah ne Kaabe ko jo baitul-haram hai aur mohtaram mahine ko aur qurbaani ke aam jaanwaro ko aur jin jaanwaro ke gale (gardan/neck) mein patta daal diya gaya hai sab ko logo ke qayaam-o-salaah ka zariya qaraar diya hai ta-keh tumhe yeh maaloom rahe ke Allah zameen-o-aasmaan ki har shaiy se ba-khabar hai aur woh qaayenaat ki har shaiy ka jaanne waala hai.

98. Yaad rakkho Khuda sakht azaab karne waala bhi hai aur Ghafoor-o-Raheem bhi hai.

99. Aur Rasool par tableegh ke alaawa koi zimme-daari nahi hai aur Allah se jin baato ka tum izhaar karte ho ya chhupaate ho sab se ba-khabar hai.

100. Paighambar! Aap kahe dijiye keh khabees aur pakeeza baraabar nahi ho sakte, chahe khabees ki kasrat (zyaada/more) achchi hi kyu’n na laghe sahebaan-e-aql Allah se darte raho shaayad ke tum is tarah kaam-yaab ho jaawo.

101. Imaan waalo un cheezo ke baare mein sawaal na karo jo tum par zaahir ho jaaye to tumhe buri lage aur agar nuzool-e-Qur’an ke waqt daryaaft kar loge to zaahir bhi kar di jaayega aur ab tak ki baato ko Allah ne maaf kar diya hai keh woh bada bakhshne waala aur bardaasht karne waala hai.

102. Tum se pehle waali qaumo ne bhi aise hi sawaalaat kiye aur iske baad phir kaafir ho gaye.

103. Allah ne baheera, saaeba, vaseela aur haam (yeh 4 khaas sifaat ke haiwaan thay jiske baare mein jaahil arabo mein khaas qaanoon tha jis) ka koi qaanoon nahi banaya yeh jo log kaafir ho gaye hai woh Allah par jootha bohtaan bandhte hai aur un mein ke aksar be-aql hai.

104. Aur jab un se kaha jaata hai keh Khuda ke naazil kiye huwe ehkaam aur uske Rasool ki taraf aawo to kehte hai keh hamare liye wohi kaafi hai jis par ham ne apne abaa-o-ajdaad ko paaya hai chahe unke abaa-o-ajdaad na kuch samajh-te ho aur na kisi tarah ki hidaayat karte ho.

105. Imaan waalo apne nafs ki fikr karo agar tum hidaayat yaafta rahe to gumraaho ki gumraahi tumhe koi nuksaan nahi pohoncha sakti. Tum sab ki baaz-gasht Khuda ki taraf hai phir woh tumhe tumhaare aamaal se ba-khabar karega.

106. Imaan waalo! Jab tum mein se kisi ki maut saamne aa jaaye to gawahi ka khayaal rakhna ke wasiyat ke waqt do aadil gawah tum mein se ho ya phir tumhaare ghair mein se ho agar tum safar ki haalat mein ho aur wahi maut ki museebat naazil ho jaaye un dono ko namaaz ke baad rok kar rakkho phir agar tumhe shak ho to yeh Khuda ki qasam khaaye ke ham is gawahi se kisi tarah ka faaida nahi chahte hai chahe qaraabat-daar hi ka maamla kyu’n na ho aur na khudai shahadat ko chhupaate hai keh is tarah ham yaqeenan gunah-gaaron mein shumaar ho jaayege.

107. Phir agar maaloom ho jaaye keh woh dono gunah ke haq-daar ho gaye hai to unki jagah do dusre aadmi khade hoge un afraad mein se jo mayyat se qareeb-tar hai aur woh qasam khaayege ke hamari shahadat unki shahadat se zeyada sahih hai aur ham ne kisi tarah ki zayadti nahi ki hai keh is tarah zaalemeen mein shumaar ho jaayege.

108. Yeh behtareen tareeqa hai ke shahadat ko sahih tareeqe se pesh kare. Ya us baat se darey keh kahi hamari gawahi dusro ki gawahi ke baad radd na kar di jaaye aur Allah se darte raho aur uski soono keh Woh faasiq qaum ki hidaayat nahi karta hai.

109. Jis din Khuda tamaam mursaleen ko jama kar ke sawaal karega keh tumhe qaum ki taraf se tableegh ka kya jawaab mila to woh Kahenge ke ham kya bataaye Tu khud hi ghaib ka jaanne waala hai.

110. Us waqt ko yaad karo jab Allah ne Isa ibne Maryam se kaha ke meri woh nemato ko yaad karo jo mai ne aap ko aur aap ki waaledah ko ataa ki hai aur roohul-quds ke zariye taaeed ki aur gehwaare mein aur jawaani mai logo se baate karte thay aur us zamaane ko bhi yaad karo jab ham ne aap ko kitaab, hikmat, Taurait aur Injeel ko sikhaaya aur us waqt ko bhi yaad karo jab hamare hukm se mitti se parindeh ki shaklo-surat banaate thay aur us mein phoonk te thay our hamare hukm se woh parinda ban jaata tha. Aur maadar zaad andhe aur kodh waale ko mere hukm se shafa dete thay aur murdo ko mere hukm se zinda kiya karte thay aur us waqt ko yaad karo jab bani Israeel ke sharr se aap ko bachaya jab-keh aap uske liye waazeh daleel bhi pesh kar chuke thay lekin un logo ne jo kufr ikhtiyaar kar chuke thay kaha keh yeh sab khule jadoo (magic) ke siwa kuch nahi hai.

111. Aur jab ham ne hawarieen ki taraf ilhaam kiya keh ham par aur hamare Rasoolo par imaan le aawo to unho ne kaha keh ham imaan le aaye aur tu gawah rehna ke ham musalmaan hai.

112. Aur jab hawarieen ne kaha keh ay Isa ibne Maryam kya tumhaare rab mein yeh taaqat bhi hai ke hamare upar aasmaan se dastarkhwan (khaana) naazil kar de to unho ne jawaab diya ke tum agar momin ho to Allah se daro.

113. Un logo ne kaha keh ham chahte hai keh ham us mein se khaaye aur itminaan-e-qalb paiyda kare aur yeh jaan le ke aap ne ham se sachh kaha hai aur ham khud bhi iske gawaho mein shaamil ho jaaye.

114. Isa ibne Maryam ne kaha Khudaaya! Parwardigaar! hamare upar aasmaan se dustarkhwaan naazil kar de ke hamare awwal-o-aakhir ke liye eid ho jaaye aur Teri qudrat ki nishaani ban jaaye aur hamein rizq de keh tu behtareen rizq dene waala hai.

115. Parwardigaar ne kaha ke ham naazil to kar rahe hai lekin iske baad jo tum mein se inkaar karega us par aisa azaab naazil karege keh aalameen mein kisi par nahi kiya hai.

116. Aur jab Allah ne kaha ke ay Isa ibne Maryam kya tum ne logo se yeh kahe diya hai ke Allah ko chhod ke mujhe aur meri maa ko Khuda maan lo to Isa ne arz ki keh Teri zaat be-neyaaz hai mai aisi baat kaise kahunga jiska mujhe koi haq nahi hai aur agar mai ne kaha tha to Tujhe to maaloom hi hai keh tu mere dil ka haal jaanta hai aur mai Tere asaraar (raaz/secret) nahi jaanta ho. Tu to ghaib ka jaanne waala bhi hai.

117. Mai ne un se sirf wohi kaha hai jiska to ne hukm diya tha keh mere aur apne Parwardigaar ki ibaadat karo aur mai jab tak unke darmiyaan raha unka gawah aur nigra raha. Phir jab Tu ne mujhe utha liya to Tu unka nigheh-baan hai aur Tu har shaiy ka gawah aur nigra hai.

118. Agar Tu un par azaab karega to woh Tere hi bande hai aur agar maaf kar dega to Tu saheb-e-izzat bhi hai aur saheb-e-hikmat bhi hai.

119. Allah ne kaha keh yeh qayamat ka din hai jab saadeqeen ko unka sachh faaida pohonchaayega ke unke liye baaghaat hoge jin ke neeche nehre jaari hogi aur woh un mein hamesha hamesha rahege. Khuda un se raazi hoga aur woh Khuda se aur yahi ek azeem kaam-yaabi hai.

120. Allah ke liye zameen-o-aasmaan aur unke darmiyaan ki kul hukumat hai aur woh har shaiy par qudrat rakhne waala hai.

5 | MAAIDA | 120

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. O you who believe! fulfill the obligations. The cattle quadrupeds are allowed to you except that which is recited to you, not violating the prohibition against game when you are entering upon the performance of the pilgrimage; surely Allah orders what He desires.

2. O you who believe! do not violate the signs appointed by Allah nor the sacred month, nor (interfere with) the offerings, nor the sacrificial animals with garlands, nor those going to the sacred house seeking the grace and pleasure of their Lord; and when you are free from the obligations of the pilgrimage, then hunt, and let not hatred of a people— because they hindered you from the Sacred Masjid— incite you to exceed the limits, and help one another in goodness and piety, and do not help one another in sin and aggression; and be careful of (your duty to) Allah; surely Allah is severe in requiting (evil).

3. Forbidden to you is that which dies of itself, and blood, and flesh of swine, and that on which any other name than that of Allah has been invoked, and the strangled (animal) and that beaten to death, and that killed by a fall and that killed by being smitten with the horn, and that which wild beasts have eaten, except what you slaughter, and what is sacrificed on stones set up (for idols) and that you divide by the arrows; that is a transgression. This day have those who disbelieve despaired of your religion, so fear them not, and fear Me. This day have I perfected for you your religion and completed My favor on you and chosen for you Islam as a religion; but whoever is compelled by hunger, not inclining willfully to sin, then surely Allah is Forgiving, Merciful.

4. They ask you as to what is allowed to them. Say: The good things are allowed to you, and what you have taught the beasts and birds of prey, training them to hunt— you teach them of what Allah has taught you— so eat of that which they catch for you and mention the name of Allah over it; and be careful of (your duty to) Allah; surely Allah is swift in reckoning.

5. This day (all) the good things are allowed to you; and the food of those who have been given the Book is lawful for you and your food is lawful for them; and the chaste from among the believing women and the chaste from among those who have been given the Book before you (are lawful for you); when you have given them their dowries, taking (them) in marriage, not fornicating nor taking them for paramours in secret; and whoever denies faith, his work indeed is of no account, and in the hereafter he shall be one of the losers.

6. O you who believe! when you rise up to prayer, wash your faces and your hands as far as the elbows, and wipe your heads and your feet to the ankles; and if you are under an obligation to perform a total ablution, then wash (yourselves) and if you are sick or on a journey, or one of you come from the privy, or you have touched the women, and you cannot find water, betake yourselves to pure earth and wipe your faces and your hands therewith, Allah does not desire to put on you any difficulty, but He wishes to purify you and that He may complete His favor on you, so that you may be grateful.

7. And remember the favor of Allah on you and His covenant with which He bound you firmly, when you said: We have heard and we obey, and be careful of (your duty to) Allah, surely Allah knows what is in the breasts.

8. O you who believe! Be upright for Allah, bearers of witness with justice, and let not hatred of a people incite you not to act equitably; act equitably, that is nearer to piety, and be careful of (your duty to) Allah; surely Allah is Aware of what you do.

9. Allah has promised to those who believe and do good deeds (that) they shall have forgiveness and a mighty reward.

10. And (as for) those who disbelieve and reject our communications, these are the companions of the flame.

11. O you who believe! remember Allah’s favor on you when a people had determined to stretch forth their hands towards you, but He withheld their hands from you, and be careful of (your duty to) Allah; and on Allah let the believers rely.

12. And certainly Allah made a covenant with the children of Israel, and We raised up among them twelve chieftains; and Allah said: Surely I am with you; if you keep up prayer and pay the poor-rate and believe in My apostles and assist them and offer to Allah a goodly gift, I will most certainly cover your evil deeds, and I will most certainly cause you to enter into gardens beneath which rivers flow, but whoever disbelieves from among you after that, he indeed shall lose the right way.

13. But on account of their breaking their covenant We cursed them and made their hearts hard; they altered the words from their places and they neglected a portion of what they were reminded of; and you shall always discover treachery in them excepting a few of them; so pardon them and turn away; surely Allah loves those who do good (to others).

14. And with those who say, We are Christians, We made a covenant, but they neglected a portion of what they were reminded of, therefore We excited among them enmity and hatred to the day of resurrection; and Allah will inform them of what they did.

15. O followers of the Book! indeed Our Apostle has come to you making clear to you much of what you concealed of the Book and passing over much; indeed, there has come to you light and a clear Book from Allah;

16. With it Allah guides him who will follow His pleasure into the ways of safety and brings them out of utter darkness into light by His will and guides them to the right path.

17. Certainly they disbelieve who say: Surely, Allah— He is the Messiah, son of Marium. Say: Who then could control anything as against Allah when He wished to destroy the Messiah son of Marium and his mother and all those on the earth? And Allah’s is the kingdom of the heavens and the earth and what is between them; He creates what He pleases; and Allah has power over all things.

18. And the Jews and the Christians say: We are the sons of Allah and His beloved ones. Say: Why does He then chastise you for your faults? Nay, you are mortals from among those whom He has created, He forgives whom He pleases and chastises whom He pleases; and Allah’s is the kingdom of the heavens and the earth and what is between them, and to Him is the eventual coming.

19. O followers of the Book! indeed Our Apostle has come to you explaining to you after a cessation of the (mission of the) apostles, lest you say: There came not to us a giver of good news or a warner, so indeed there has come to you a giver of good news and a warner; and Allah has power over all things.

20. And when Musa said to his people: O my people! remember the favor of Allah upon you when He raised prophets among you and made you kings and gave you what He had not given to any other among the nations.

21. O my people! enter the holy land which Allah has prescribed for you and turn not on your backs for then you will turn back losers.

22. They said: O Musa! surely there is a strong race in it, and we will on no account enter it until they go out from it, so if they go out from it, then surely we will enter.

23. Two men of those who feared, upon both of whom Allah had bestowed a favor, said: Enter upon them by the gate, for when you have entered it you shall surely be victorious, and on Allah should you rely if you are believers.

32. 34. Except those who repent before you have them in your power; so know that Allah is Forgiving, Merciful.

O Apostle! let not those grieve you who strive together in hastening to unbelief from among those who say with their mouths: We believe, and their hearts do not believe, and from among those who are Jews; they are listeners for the sake of a lie, listeners for another people who have not come to you; they alter the words from their places, saying: If you are given this, take it, and if you are not given this, be cautious; and as for him whose temptation Allah desires, you cannot control anything for him with Allah. 42. 44. 45. And We prescribed to them in it that life is for life, and eye for eye, and nose for nose, and ear for ear, and tooth for tooth, and (that there is) reprisal in wounds; but he who foregoes it, it shall be an expiation for him; and whoever did not judge by what Allah revealed, those are they that are the unjust.

48. And We have revealed to you the Book with the truth, verifying what is before it of the Book and a guardian over it, therefore judge between them by what Allah has revealed, and do not follow their low desires (to turn away) from the truth that has come to you; for every one of you did We appoint a law and a way, and if Allah had pleased He would have made you (all) a single people, but that He might try you in what He gave you, therefore strive with one another to hasten to virtuous deeds; to Allah is your return, of all (of you), so He will let you know that in which you differed;

49. And that you should judge between them by what Allah has revealed, and do not follow their low desires, and be cautious of them, lest they seduce you from part of what Allah has revealed to you; but if they turn back, then know that Allah desires to afflict them on account of some of their faults; and most surely many of the people are transgressors.

50. Is it then the judgment of (the times of) ignorance that they desire? And who is better than Allah to judge for a people who are sure?

O you who believe! whoever from among you turns back from his religion, then Allah will bring a people, He shall love them and they shall love Him, lowly before the believers, mighty against the unbelievers, they shall strive hard in Allah’s way and shall not fear the censure of any censurer; this is Allah’s grace, He gives it to whom He pleases, and Allah is Ample-giving, Knowing.

Nay, both His hands are spread out, He expends as He pleases; and what has been revealed to you from your Lord will certainly make many of them increase in inordinacy and unbelief; and We have put enmity and hatred among them till the day of resurrection; whenever they kindle a fire for war Allah puts it out, and they strive to make mischief in the land; and Allah does not love the mischief-makers.

Say: O followers of the Book! you follow no good till you keep up the Taurat and the Injeel and that which is revealed to you from your Lord; and surely that which has been revealed to you from your Lord shall make many of them increase in inordinacy and unbelief; grieve not therefore for the unbelieving people.

Surely whoever associates (others) with Allah, then Allah has forbidden to him the garden, and his abode is the fire; and there shall be no helpers for the unjust.

Certainly you will find the most violent of people in enmity for those who believe (to be) the Jews and those who are polytheists, and you will certainly find the nearest in friendship to those who believe (to be) those who say: We are Christians; this is because there are priests and monks among them and because they do not behave proudly.
