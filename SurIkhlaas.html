Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Ay Rasool!Kahe dijiye keh woh Allah ek hai.

2. Allah bar-haq aur be-neyaaz hai.

3. Uski na koi aulaad hai aur na waalid.

4. Aur na uska koi kufu aur hamsar hai.

112 | IKHLAAS | 4

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Say: He, Allah, is One.

2. Allah is He on Whom all depend.

3. He begets not, nor is He begotten.

4. And none is like Him.
