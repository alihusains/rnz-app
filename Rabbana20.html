Chp. 7:Ver. 23
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَا ظَلَمْنَآ اَنْفُسَنَاسكتۃ وَاِنْ لَّمْ تَغْفِرْ لَنَا وَتَرْحَمْنَا لَنَكُوْنَنَّ مِنَ الْخٰسِرِيْنَ۔
Our Lord! We have been unjust to ourselves, and if Thou forgive us not, and have (not) mercy on us, we shall certainly be of the losers.

Parwardigaar! Ham ne apne nafs par zulm kiya hai ab agar Tu maaf na karega aur rahem na karega to ham khasaare (nuqsaan) uthaane waalo’n mein ho jaayege.
