90 | BALAD | 20

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Mai is shaher ki qasam khaata hu.

2. Aur tum isi shaher mein to rehte ho.

3. Aur tumhaare baap Aadam alayhis salaam aur unki aulaad ki qasam.

4. Ham ne insaan ko mashaqqat mein rehne waala banaya hai.

5. Kya uska khayaal yeh hai keh us par koi qaabu na paa sakayga.

6. Keh woh kehta hai keh mai ne be-tahaasha sarf kiya hai.

7. Kya uska khayaal hai keh us ko kisi ne nahi dekha hai.

8. Kya Ham ne uske liye do aankhe nahi qaraar di hai.

9. Aur zabaan aur do hont (lips) bhi.

10. Aur Ham ne usey dono raasto ki hidaayat di hai.

11. Phir woh ghaati (kinaare) par se kyu’n nahi guzra.

12. Aur tum kya jaano yeh ghaati kya hai.

13. Kisi gardan ka azaad karaana.

14. Ya bhook ke din mein khaana khilaana.

15. Kisi qarabat-daar yateem ko.

16. Ya khaakh-saar miskeen ko.

17. Phir woh un logo mein shaamil ho jaata jo imaan laaye aur unho ne sabr aur marhamat ki ek dusre ko nasihat ki.

18. Yahi log khoosh-naseebi waale hai.

19. Aur jin logo ne Hamari aayaat se inkaar kiya hai woh bad-bakhti waale hai.

20. Inhe aag mein daal kar usey har taraf se bandh kar diya jaayega.

90 | BALAD | 20

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Nay! I swear by this city.

2. And you shall be made free from obligation in this city—

3. And the begetter and whom he begot.

4. Certainly We have created man to be in distress.

5. Does he think that no one has power over him?

6. He shall say: I have wasted much wealth.

7. Does he think that no one sees him?

8. Have We not given him two eyes,

9. And a tongue and two lips,

10. And pointed out to him the two conspicuous ways?

11. But he would not attempt the uphill road,

12. And what will make you comprehend what the uphill road is?

13. (It is) the setting free of a slave,

14. Or the giving of food in a day of hunger

15. To an orphan, having relationship,

16. Or to the poor man lying in the dust.

17. Then he is of those who believe and charge one another to show patience, and charge one another to show compassion.

18. These are the people of the right hand.

19. And (as for) those who disbelieve in our communications, they are the people of the left hand.

20. On them is fire closed over.
