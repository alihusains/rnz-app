109 | KAAFIROON | 6

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Aap kahe dijiye keh ay kaafiro!

2. Mai un Khudawo ki ibaadat nahi kar sakta jin ki tum pooja karte ho.

3. Aur na tum mere Khuda ki ibaadat karne waale ho.

4. Aur na mai tumhaare maaboodo ki pooja karne waala hu.

5. Aur na tum mere maabood ke ibaadat guzaar ho.

6. Tumhare liye tumhaara deen hai aur mere liye mera deen hai.

109 | KAAFIROON | 6

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Say: O unbelievers!

2. I do not serve that which you serve,

3. Nor do you serve Him Whom I serve:

4. Nor am I going to serve that which you serve,

5. Nor are you going to serve Him Whom I serve:

6. You shall have your religion and I shall have my religion.
