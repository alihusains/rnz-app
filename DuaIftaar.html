IFTAAR

Is dua ko Iftaar karne se pehle padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Allah! Tere liye roza rakhta hu, Tere diye huwe rizq se iftaar karta hu, aur Tujhi par bharosa karta hu. (ay hamaare Parwardigaar! Tu hamaare aamaal ko qabool farma. Be-shak Tu sab kuchh soonne aur jaanne waala hai. Aameen!)

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

Iftar se pehle Surah Qadr (Chp.97) padhe.

IFTAAR

Recite this dua before breaking the fast…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O my Allah, for Thee, I fast, and with the food Thou gives me I break the fast, and I rely on Thee. In the name of Allah, O Allah, we fast, and with the food Thou gives us we break the fast, an obligation we fulfill, and Thou art Hearer, Knower.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

Recite Surah Qadr (Chp.97) just before Iftar.
