SHAB KI DUA...

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Hamd hai Usi Allah ke liye keh jisne ham ko is mahine ke zariye buzurg kiya aur is mahine mein hamare liye Qur’an naazil kiya aur ham ko uske (is mahine mein Qur’an ke) haq se aagah kiya aur hamd hai Allah ki basirat-e-deeni inaayat farmaane par.

Pas Tujhe apni zaat ki nooraaniyat ka waasta, ay hamare Khuda aur ay hamare pehle buzurgo ke Khuda ham ko tauba ki is mahine mein taufiq inaayat kar aur ham ko hamare haal par na chod dena aur hamare us husn-zan, keh jo Tere saath waabasta hai uske khilaaf na kar. Yaqeenan Tu zabardast o jaleel hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Aaj ke din mujh ko is mahine ki sehriyo ki barkato se aagah (alert) kar isme mere dil ko uski roshniyo se chamka de, aur us mein mere aaza’a (parts of body) ko is mah (month) ke ahkaam par amal karne ki taraf laga de, be-waaste Apne noor ke. Ay pehchaan rakhne waalo ke dilo’n ko roshan karne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

NAMAZ…

1) Recite 4-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Kausar (Chp-108) 25 times.

NIGHT DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Praise be to Allah, Who honored us with this month who revealed in it the Holy Quran and made us recognize His right. And praise be to Allah, who gave the religious insight. Thus for the sake of the effulgence of Your face, O our God and the God of our foremost ancestors. Give us the taufeeq for repentance in this month and do not leave us to our devices and do not act against the good expectation we have with regard to You. Indeed You the most majestic and the most powerful.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah keep me awake in this month to find and get the blessings of its mornings; enlighten my heart with the rays of its bright lights; let me (all parts of my body) yield to follow its tradition, in the name of Thy Light, O the Light of the hearts and minds of those who know.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
