KAMILA

The Holy Prophet (s.a.w.a.) is reported to have said: As for anyone who, before midday on Friday recite a 4 rakat namaz, with the unit of 2, in each rakat, Surah Fateha (Chp.1) 10 times, Surah Naas (Chp.114), Surah Falaq (Chp.113), Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112), Surah Kafiroon (Chp.109), Ayat al-Kursi (2:255-257) 10 times each, (And, according to another narration, Surah Qadr (Chp.97), Ayat Aale Imran (3:18-19) 10 times each) and, after completing namaz, repeat the following zikr 100 times each…

Hazrat Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) ne taaqid ki hai keh, Juma ke Zohr se pehle 4 rakat namaz, 2x2 karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) 10 martaba, Surah Naas (Chp.114), Surah Falaq (Chp.113), Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112), Surah Kafiroon (Chp.109) aur Ayat al-Kursi (2:255-257) har ek 10 martaba padhe, (Aur ek riwaayat ke mutaabik, Surah Qadr (Chp.97), Ayat Aale Imran (3:18-19) bhi 10 martaba padhe) aur namaz ke baad har ek Zikr ko 100 martaba padhe…
