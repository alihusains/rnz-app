TASBEEH-3

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

لَااِلٰہَ اِلَّااللہُ قَبْلَ کُلِّ اَحَدٍ وَّلَااِلٰہَ اِلَّااللہُ بَعْدَ کُلِّ اَحَدٍ وَّلَااِلٰہَ اِلَّا اللہُ مَعَ کُلِّ اَحَدٍ وَّلَااِلٰہَ اِلَّااللہُ یَبْقٰی رَبُّنَا وَیَفْنٰی کُلُّ اَحَدٍ وَّلَااِلٰہَ اِلَّا اللہُ تَسْبِیْحًا یَّفْضُلُ تَسْبِیْحَ الْمُسَبِّحِیْنَ فَضْلًا کَثِیْرًا قَبْلَ کُلِّ اَحَدٍ۔

وَلَااِلٰہَ اِلَّا اللہُ تَسْبِیْحًا یَّفْضُلُ تَسْبِیْحَ الْمُسَبِّحِیْنَ فَضْلًا کَثِیْرًاۢ بَعْدَ کُلِّ اَحَدٍ وَّلَااِلٰہَ اِلَّا اللہُ تَسْبِیْحًا یَّفْضُلُ تَسْبِیْحَ الْمُسَبِّحِیْنَ فَضْلًا کَثِیْرًا لِّرَبِّنَاالْبَا قِیْ وَیَفْنٰی کُلُّ اَحَدٍ۔

وَلَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّااللہُ تَسْبِیْحًا لَّا یُحْصٰی وَلَا یُدْرٰی وَلَا یُنْسٰی وَلَا یَبْلٰی وَلَایَفْنٰی وَلَیْسَ لَہٗ مُنْتَھٰی وَلَااِلٰہَ اِلَّا اللہُ تَسْبِیْحًا یَّدُوْمُ بِدَوَامِہٖ وَیَبْقٰی بِبَقَآئِہٖ فِیْ سَنِی الْعَالَمِیْنَ وَشُھُوْرِ الدُّھُوْرِ وَاَیَّامِ الدُّنْیَا وَسَاعَاتِ اللَّیْلِ وَالنَّھَارِ۔

لَااِلٰہَ اِلَّا اللہُ اَبَدَ الْاَبَدِ وَمَعَ الْاَبَدِ مِمَّا لَا یُحْصِیْہِ الْعَدَدُ وَلَا یُفْنِیْہِ الْاَمَدُ وَلَا یُقطَعُہُ الْاَبَدُ وَتَبَارَکَ اللہُ اَحْسَنُ الْخَالِقِیْنَ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

TASBEEH -3

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Nahi koi ma’bood siwaaye Allah ke jo har cheez se pehle hai. Nahi koi ma’bood siwaaye Allah ke jo har cheez ke baad hai. Nahi koi ma’bood siwaaye Allah ke jo har cheez ke saath hai.

Nahi koi ma’bood siwaaye Allah ke jo hamara Rab hai aur hamesha baqi rehne waala hai jab-keh har shaiy phana ho jaane waali hai. Nahi koi ma’bood siwaaye Allah ke jo tasbeeh karne waalo ki tasbeeh se bohot bohot baala-o-aala (buland-o-bartar) hai har cheez se pehle.

Nahi koi ma’bood siwaaye Allah ke jo tasbeeh karne waalo ki tasbeeh se bohot bohot baala-o-aala (buland-o-bartar) hai har cheez ke baad. Nahi koi ma’bood siwaaye Allah ke jo tasbeeh karne waalo ki tasbeeh se bohot bohot baala-o-aala (buland-o-bartar) hai har cheez ke saath.

Nahi koi ma’bood siwaaye Allah ke, hamara Rab hamesha baqi rehne waala hai jab-keh har cheez phana ho jaane waali hai. Nahi koi ma’bood siwaaye Allah ke, jo shumaar (ghinti) mein nahi aata. Aur na samajh mein aata hai na faramosh hota hai (na bhulaya jaata hai) na woh purana hota hai na phana (khatm) aur uski koi inteha nahi hai.

Nahi koi ma’bood siwaaye Allah ke, jo (apne bal-boote par) jaari-o-saari hai, hamesha se hai aur hamesha rahe-ga jahaano ke barso (years) aur har ek mahino, duniya ke tamaam dino aur raat-din ki ghadiyo (saa’ato) ke saath hamesha se hai aur hamesha baaqi rahe-ga.

Nahi koi ma’bood siwaaye Allah ke, jo (apne bal-boote par) hamesha se hai aur hamesha rahe-ga. Keh jiski zaat ka andaaza nahi kiya ja sakta. Woh zamaana guzarne se phana nahi hota aur na zamaana kabhi us se munqate (juda) ho sakta hai. Aur ba-barkat hai woh Khuda jo behtareen khaaliq hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

TASBEEH -3

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

There is no god save Allah, before everyone (accepts His single Divinity). There is no god save Allah, after everyone (accepts His single Divinity). There is no god save Allah, when everyone (accepts His single Divinity). There is no god save Allah, Everlasting Forever is our Lord-Nourisher, and everyone passes away.

There is no god save Allah, the glorification of the glorifiers exalts His glory, immeasurably exalted is His glory, before everyone (exalts Him). There is no god save Allah, the glorification of the glorifiers exalts His glory, immeasurably exalted is His glory, after everyone (exalts Him).

There is no god save Allah, the glorification of the glorifiers exalts His glory, immeasurably exalted is the glory of our Everlasting Lord-Nourisher, and everyone passes away. There is no god save Allah, (His) glory is, immeasurable, unperceivable, unforgetable, unperishing, indestructible and there is no end to it.

There is no god save Allah, everlasting duration of His glory makes His glorification eternal, continuous existence of His glory keeps His glorification alive for ever, throughout the cosmic ages, months all through the centuries, days passing by in this world, and every moment of the nights and days.

There is no god save Allah, never-ending acceptance of His single Divinity along with his ever eternal single Divinity, which, neither can be counted in addition, nor can be terminated into an end, nor can ever be discontinued, be praised and hallowed is Allah, the Best Creator.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
