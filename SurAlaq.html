Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Us Khuda ka naam le kar padho jisne paiyda kiya hai.

2. Us ne insaan ko jamey huwe khoon se paiyda kiya hai.

3. Padho aur tumhaara Parwardigaar bada kareem hai.

4. Jisne qalam ke zariye taaleem di hai.

5. Aur insaan ko woh sab-kuch bata diya hai jo usey nahi maaloom tha.

6. Be-shakinsaan sar-kashi karta hai.

7. Keh apne ko be-neyaaz khayaal karta hai.

8. Be-shak aap ke Rab ki taraf waapsi hai.

9. Kya tum ne us shakhs ko dekha hai jo mana karta hai.

10. Banda-e-Khuda ko jab woh namaaz padhta hai.

11. Kya tum ne dekha keh agar woh banda hidaayat par ho.

12. Ya taqwa ka hukm de to rokna kaisa hai.

13. Kya tum ne dekha keh agar us kaafir ne joothlaaya aur mooh pher liya.

14. To kya tumhe nahi maaloom keh Allah dekh raha hai.

15. Yaad rakkho agar woh rokne se baaz na aaya to Ham peshaani ke baal pakad kar ghasitege.

16. Joothay aur khata-kaar ki peshaani ke baal.

17. Phir woh apne ham-nasheeno ko bulaaye.

18. Ham bhi apne jallaad farishto ko bulaate hai.

19. Dekho tum harghiz uski ita’at na karna aur sajdah kar ke qurb-e-Khuda haasil karo.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Read in the name of your Lord Who created.

2. He created man from a clot.

3. Read and your Lord is Most Honorable,

4. Who taught (to write) with the pen

5. Taught man what he knew not.

6. Nay! man is most surely inordinate,

7. Because he sees himself free from want.

8. Surely to your Lord is the return.

9. Have you seen him who forbids

10. A servant when he prays?

11. Have you considered if he were on the right way,

12. Or enjoined guarding (against evil)?

13. Have you considered if he gives the lie to the truth and turns (his) back?

14. Does he not know that Allah does see?

15. Nay! if he desist not, We would certainly smite his forehead,

16. A lying, sinful forehead.

17 Then let him summon his council,

18. We too would summon the braves of the army.

19. Nay! obey him not, and make obeisance and draw nigh (to Allah).
