LAILATUL QADR

It is related from Imam Sadiq (a.s.) that: When Shab-e-Qadr arrives a caller calls out from the seventh heaven that Allah has forgiven every one who comes for the Ziarat of Imam Husain (a.s)…

Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) se waarid huwa hai keh jab Shab-e-Qadr aati hai to saatwe aasmaan (7th Sky) par ek munaadi (announcer) neda (aawaaz) deta hai keh Khuda wande Aalam ne har aise shakhs ko bakhsh diya jo Husain Ibne Ali (a.s) ki ziyarat ke liye aaya hai.

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ رَسُوْلِ اللہِ. اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ اَمِیْرِ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ. اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ الصِّدِّیْقَۃِ الطَّاھِرَۃِ فَاطِمَۃَ سَیِّدَۃِ نِسَآءِ الْعَالَمِیْنَ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَامَوْلَایَ یَااَبَا عَبْدِ اللہِ وَرَحْمَۃُ اللہِ وَبَرَكَاتُهٗ۔ اَشْھَدُ اَنَّكَ قَدْ اَقَمْتَ الصَّلٰوۃَ وَاٰتَیْتَ الزَّكٰوۃَ وَاَمَرْتَ بِالْمَعْرِوْفِ وَنَھَیْتَ عَنِ الْمُنْكَرِ وَتَلَوْتَ الْكِتَّابَ حَقَّ تِلَاوَتِهٖ وَجَاھَدْتَ فِی اللہِ حَقَّ جِھَادِهٖ وَصَبَرْتَ عَلَی الْاَذٰی فِیْ جَنْبِهٖ مُحْتَسِبًا حَتّٰی اَتٰیكَ الْیَقِیْنُ اَشْھَدُ اَنَّ الَّذِیْنَ خَالَفُوْكَ وَحَارَبُوْكَ وَالَّذِیْنَ خَذَلُوْكَ وَالَّذِیْنَ قَتَلُوْكَ مَلْعُوْنُوْنَ عَلٰی لِسَانِ النَّبِیِّ الْاُمِّیِّ وَقَدْ خَابَ مَنِ افْتَرٰی لَعَنَ اللہُ الظَّالِمِیْنَ لَكُمْ مِنَ الْاَوَّلِیْنَ وَالْاٰخِرِیْنَ وَضَاعَفَ عَلَیْھِمُ الْعَذَابَ الْاَلِیْمَ اَتَیْتُكَ یَامَوْلَایَ یَابْنَ رَسُوْلِ اللہِ زَآئِرًا عَارِفًا بِحَقِّكَ مُوَالِیًا لِاَوْلِیَآئِكَ مُعَادِیًا لِاَعْدَآئِكَ مُسْتَبْصِرًا بِالْھُدَی الَّذِیْ اَنْتَ عَلَیْهِ عَارِفًا بِضَلَالَۃِ مَنْ خَالَفَكَ فَاشْفَعْ لِیْ عِنْدَ رَبِّكَ۔

On the head side say…
Sar ki tarah khade ho kar kahe…

اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاحُجَّۃَ اللہِ فِیْ اَرْضِهٖ وَسَمَآئِهٖ صَلَّی اللہُ عَلٰی رُوْحِكَ الطَّیِّبِ وَجَسَدِكَ الطَّاھِرِ وَعَلَیْكَ السَّلَامُ یَامَوْلَایَ وَرَحْمَۃُ اللہِ وَ بَرَكَاتُهٗ۔

Then go towards the head and pray 2 rakats namaz, then recite more 2 rakats and go to the footside and recite the Ziarat of Hazrat Ali Akbar (a.s)…
Phir sar ki taraf 2 rakat namaz, aur phir se 2 rakat namaz adaa kare aur paayti ki taraf aa kar Ali Akbar (a.s) ki ziyarat padhe…

اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَامَوْلَایَ وَابْنَ مَوْلَایَ وَرَحْمَۃُ اللہِ وَ بَرَكَاتُهٗ وَلَعَنَ اللہُ مَنْ ظَلَمَكَ وَلَعَنَ اللہُ مَنْ قَتَلَكَ وَضَاعَفَ عَلَیْھِمُ الْعَذَابَ الْاَلِیْمَ۔

Pray for whatever you desire. Then perform the Ziarat of the martyrs in such a way that from the footside you bend towards the Qibla and say…
Apni haajaat talab kare. Phir Shohada-e-Karbala ki ziyarat is tarah khade ho kar padhe ke paayti ki taraf se qible ki taraf mood (turn) ho jaaye aur kahe…

اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكُمْ اَیُّھَا الصِّدِّیْقُوْنَ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكُمْ اَیُّھَا الشُّھَدَآءُ الصَّابِرُوْنَ۔ اَشْھَدُ اَنَّكُمْ جَاھَدْتُمْ فِیْ سَبِیْلِ اللہِ وَصَبَرْتُمْ عَلَی الْاَذٰی فِیْ جَنْبِ اللہِ وَنَصَحْتُمْ لِلهِ وَلِرَسُوْلِهٖ حَتّٰی اَتٰی كُمُ الْیَقِیْنُ اَشْھَدُ اَنَّكُمْ اَحْیَآءٌ عِنْدَ رَبِّكُمْ تُرْزَقُوْنَ فَجَزَاكُمُ اللہُ عَنِ الْاِسْلَامِ وَاَھْلِهٖ اَفْضَلَ جَزَآءِ الْمُحْسِنِیْنَ وَجَمَعَ بَیْنَنَا وَبَیْنَكُمْ فِیْ مَحَلِّ النَّعِیْمِ۔

Recite the Ziarat of Hazrat Abbas (a.s) in his shrine and if one is at a distance one should imagine it…
Hazrat Abbas (a.s) ki ziyarat padhe unke haram mein ja kar aur agar dur se padhte ho to tasawwur kare…

اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ اَمِیْرِ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ اَیُّھَا الْعَبْدُ الصَّالِحُ الْمُطِیْعُ لِلهِ وَ لِرَسُوْلِهٖ۔ اَشْھَدُ اَنَّكَ قَدْ جَاھَدْتَ وَنَصَحْتَ وَصَبَرْتَ حَتّٰی اَتٰیكَ الْیَقِیْنُ لَعَنَ اللہُ الظَّالِمِیْنَ لَكُمْ مِنَ الْاَوَّلِیْنَ وَالْاٰخِرِیْنَ وَاَلْحَقَھُمْ بِدَرَكِ الْجَحِیْم۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

LAILATUL QADR

Imam Husain (a.s)

Imam Mohammad Taqi (a.s) se marvi (riwaayat ki gayi) hai keh jo shakhs Shabe Qadr mein Imam Husain (a.s) ki ziyarat kare to 24 hazaar (24000) paighambar aur farishte jo Allah (s.w.t) ki ijaazat le kar ziyarat karne ke liye aaye hai woh us shakhs ke saath musaafeha karte hai.

Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) se waarid huwa hai keh jab Shab-e-Qadr aati hai to saatwe aasmaan (7th Sky) par ek munaadi (announcer) neda (aawaaz) deta hai keh Khuda wande Aalam ne har aise shakhs ko bakhsh diya jo Husain Ibne Ali (a.s) ki ziyarat ke liye aaya hai.

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Salaam ho aap par ay Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) ke farzand. Salaam ho aap par ay Ameerul Momineen (a.s) ke farzand. Salaam ho aap par ay Fatematuz Zahra (s.a) ke farzand jo bohot hi sachchi, pakeeza aur tamaam jahaano ki aurto’n ki sardaar hai. Aap par salaam ho ay mere Maula ay Aba Abdillah. Khuda ki rehmat ho aap par aur uski barkate naazil ho aap par. Mai gawaahi deta hu keh aap ne namaaz qaayem ki aur zakaat di. Aap ne logo ko nek kaamo ka hukm diya aur burey kaamo se mana kiya (roka). Aur aap ne Kitaab-e-Khuda (Qur’an) ki is tarah tilaawat ki jo tilaawat ka haq hai. Aur raahe Khuda mein woh jehaad kiya jo jehaad ka haq hai. Aur aap ne Khuda ke liye (Khuda ki khushnoodi ke khaatir) dukho (museebato) par sabr kiya ummeed-e-ajr mein, yahan tak keh aap daraja-e-shahadat par faayez huwe. Mai gawaahi deta hu keh jinho ne aap ki mukhaalefat ki aur aap se jangh ki aur aap ka saath na diya aur jinho ne aap ko qatl kiya woh sab maloon qaraar diye gaye Nabi-ul-Ummi ke zabaan se, yaqeenan woh na-kaam raha jisne jhoota daawa kiya, Khuda ki laanat ho un par jinho ne aap par zulm kiya hai (chahe woh) awwaleen mein se ho ya aakhereen mein se, aur dugna (double) ho unpar darad-naak azaab. Mai aaya (haazir huwa) aap ke yahan ay mere aaqa-o-maula ay farzande Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) aap ki ziyarat karne aap ke haq ko pehchaante huwe. Aap ke dosto se dosti aur aap ke dushmano se dushmani rakhte huwe. Us raaste ko duroost aur sahih hidaayat ka raasta jaante huwe jis par aap chale, aur usey gumrah samajhte huwe jisne aap se mukhaalefat ki. Pas apne Parwardigaar ke yahan hamaare liye sifaarish farma de.

Phir (sirhaane (head) ki taraf ja kar) qabr se lipat jaaye aur apne rukhsaar ko Zareeh-e-Mubarak par rakh kar kahe…

Salaam ho aap par ay Hujjate Khuda uski zameen aur uske aasmaan mein. Khuda rehmat kare aap ke taiyyab-o-taahir ruh par aur aap ke jism-e-paak (pakeeza jism) par. Aur aap par salaam ho ay mere aaqa-o-maula, aur Khuda ki rehmat-o-barkate naazil ho aap par.

Iske baad 2 rakat namaaz padh kar Imam (a.s) ko hadiya kare.
Phir qabre mubarak ki paayenti (leg) ki taraf jaaye aur Hazrate Ali Akbar (a.s) ki ziyarat padhe…

Salaam ho aap par ay mere Maula aur mere Maula ke farzand. Khuda ki rehmat aur uski barkate naazil ho aap par. Khuda laanat kare uspar jisne aap par zulm kiya aur Khuda laanat kare uspar jisne aap ko qatl kiya aur unki shadeed dard-naak azaab mein kahi guna (multiple) izaafa kare. (Phir Khuda se jo chahe dua maange)

Iske baad Tamaam shohada ki ziyarat kare…

Salaam ho aap sab par ay sachcho. Salaam ho aap sab par ay shaheedo jinho ne bohot sabr kiya. Mai gawaahi deta hu keh yaqeenan aap logo ne Khuda ki raah mein jehaad kiya aur Khuda ki khushnoodi ke khaatir dukh-dard aur takleefo par sabr kiya, aap logo ne Khuda wa Rasool (s.a.w.a) se khuloos barta yahan tak keh duniya se guzar gaye. Mai gawaahi deta hu ke zaroor aap log apne Parwardigaar ke yahan zinda hai aur rizq paate hai. Pas Khuda aap logo ko behtareen jaza de. Islam aur ahle Islam ki taraf se behtareen jaza jo neku-kaaro (nek kaam karne waalo) ke liye hai. Aur ek-ja (ekhattha) kare hamein aur aap ko nemato se bhaari Jannat ke mahal aur makaano mein.

Iske baad Hazrat Abbas (a.s) ki zari ke paas ja kar unki ziyarat padhe…

Salaam ho aap par ay Ameerul Momineen (a.s) ke farzand. Salaam ho aap par ay Abde-Saaleh (banda-e-khush-kirdaar) Khuda aur uske Rasool (s.a.w.a) ke ita’at guzaar. Mai gawaahi deta hu keh ba-tahqeeq aap ne jehaad kiya aur khair-khwahi (bhalaayi) ki aur sabr se kaam liya yahan tak keh aap duniya se chal basey, Khuda ki laanat ho unpar jinho ne aap par zulm dhaaya (chahe woh) awwaleen mein se ya aakhereen mein se aur Khuda unko Jahannam ke nichle daraje mein phenke (aameen).

LAILATUL QADR

It is related from Imam Sadiq (a.s.) that: When Shab-e-Qadr arrives a caller calls out from the seventh heaven that Allah has forgiven every one who comes for the Ziarat of Imam Husain (a.s.)…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Peace be upon you, O son of Allah’s Messenger. Peace be upon you, O son of the Commander of the Faithful. Peace be upon you, O son of the veracious, immaculate lady Fatimah the Doyenne of the women of the world. Peace be upon you, O my master Abu Abdullah. So be upon you Allah’s mercy and blessings. I bear witness that you performed the prayers, defrayed the poor-rate, enjoined the right, forbade the wrong, recited the Book (i.e. the Holy Qur'an) as exactly as it should be recited, strove for the sake of Allah as exactly as striving must be, and faced all troubles steadfastly for His sake, seeking his reward, until death came upon you. I also bear witness that those who dissented you, those who fought against you, those who let you down, and those who slew you (all those) were accursed directly by the Meccan Prophet. And he who forges lies indeed fails to attain his desire. May Allah curse those who wronged you from amongst the past and the present generations. May Allah also double for them the painful torment. O my master and son of Allah’s Messenger, I have come to you visiting you, acknowledging the standing that you enjoy, declaring my loyalty to those who are loyal to you, renouncing your enemies, seeking to follow the true guidance that you are following, and recognizing the deviation of those who oppose you; so, (please) intercede for me before your Lord.

On the head side say…

Peace be upon you, O Argument of Allah in His lands and heavens. May Allah bless your immaculate spirit and your pure body. Peace and Allah’s mercy and blessings be upon you, O my master.

Then go towards the head and pray 2 rakats namaz, then recite more 2 rakats and go to the footside and recite the Ziarat of Hazrat Ali Akbar (a.s)…

Peace be upon you, O my master and son of my master. So be upon you Allah’s mercy and blessings. May Allah curse those who wronged you. May Allah curse those who slew you. May Allah double the painful chastisement for them.

Pray for whatever you desire. Then perform the Ziarat of the martyrs in such a way that from the footside you bend towards the Qibla and say…

Peace be upon you, O veracious ones. Peace be upon you, O steadfast martyrs. I bear witness that you strove in the way of Allah, acted patiently against harm for the sake of Allah, and did sincerely for the sake of Allah and His Messenger until death came upon you. I bear witness that you are alive and are being provided sustenance from your Lord. May Allah reward you with the best reward of the good-doers on behalf of Islam and its people and may He join us to you in the abode of bliss.

Recite the Ziarat of Hazrat Abbas (a.s) in his shrine and if one is at a distance one should imagine it…

Peace be upon you, O son of the Commander of the Faithful. Peace be upon you, O righteous servant (of Allah) and obedient to Allah and to His Messenger. I bear witness that you strove (in the way of Allah), acted sincerely, and tolerated (harm) until death came upon you. May Allah curse those who wronged you from the past and the present generations and may He send them to the lowest layer of the blazing Hell.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
