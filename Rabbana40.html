Chp. 66:Ver. 8
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَآ اَتْمِمْ لَنَا نُوْرَنَا وَاغْفِرْ لَنَاج اِنَّكَ عَلٰى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيْرٌ۔
Our Lord! Make perfect for us our light, and grant us protection, surely Thou hast power over all things.

Khudaaya! Hamare liye hamare noor ko mukammal kar de aur hamein bakhsh de keh Tu yaqeenan har shai par qudrat rakhne waala hai.
