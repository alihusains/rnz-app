GENERAL TAQIBAAT
1) Immediately after your obligatory namaz recite this dua…
1) Waajib namaz ke fauran baad yeh dua padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

لَا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا اللهُ اِلٰهًا وَّاحِدًا وَّنَحْنُ لَهٗ مُسْلِمُوْنَ لَا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا اللهُ وَلَا نَعْبُدُ اِلَّا اِيَّاهُ مُخْلِصِيْنَ لَهُ الدِّيْنَ وَلَوْ كَرِهَ الْمُشْرِكُوْنَ لَا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا اللهُ رَبُّنَا وَرَبُّ آبَآئِنَا الْاَوَّلِيْنَ لَا اِلٰهَ اِلَا اللهُ وَحْدَهٗ وَحْدَهٗ وَحْدَهٗ اَنْجَزَ وَعْدَهٗ وَنَصَرَ عَبْدَهٗ وَاَعَزَّ جُنْدَهٗ وَهَزَمَ الْاَحْزَابَ وَحْدَهٗ فَلَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ يُحْيِىْ وَيُمِيْتُ وَيُمِيْتُ وَيُحْيِىْ وَهُوَ حَىُّ لَايَمُوْتُ بِيَدِهِ الْخَيْرُ وَهُوَ عَلٰی كُلِّ شَىْٓءٍ قَدِيْرٌ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلیٰ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

2) Then, say the following…
2) Phir kahe…

اَسْتَغْفِرُ اللهَ الَّذِىْ لَا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا هُوَ الْحَىُّ الْقَيُّوْمُ وَاَتُوْبُ اِلَيْهِ۔

3) Then, say the following…
3) Aur phir kahe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰهُمَّ اهْدِنِىْ مِنْ عِنْدِكَ وَاَفِضْ عَلَىَّ مِنْ فَضْلِكَ وَانْشُرْ عَلَىَّ مِنْ رَّحْمَتِكَ وَاَنْزِلْ عَلَىَّ مِنْ بَرَكَاتِكَ سُبْحَانَكَ لَا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ اِغْفِرْ لِىْ ذُنُوْبِىْ كُلَّهَا جَمِيْعًا فَاِنَّهٗ لَا يَغْفِرُ الذُّنُوْبَ كُلَّهَا جَمِيْعًا اِلَّا اَنْتَ۔ اَللّٰهُمَّ اِنّىْ اَسْئَلُكَ مِنْ كُلِّ خَيْرٍ اَحَاطَ بِهٖ عِلْمُكَ وَاَعُوْذُ بِكَ مِنْ كُلِّ شَرٍّ اَحَاطَ بِهٖ عِلْمُكَ۔ اَللّٰهُمَّ اِنّى اَسْئَلُكَ عَافِيَتَكَ فِىْ اُمُوْرِىْ كُلِّهَا وَاَعُوْذُ بِكَ مِنْ خِزْىِ الدُّنْيَا وَعَذَابِ الْاٰخِرَةِ وَاَعُوْذُ بِوَجْهِكَ الْكَرِيْمِ وَعِزَّتِكَ الَّتِىْ لَا تُرَامُ وَقُدْرَتِكَ الَّتِىْ لَا يَمْتَنِعُ مِنْهَا شَىْٓءٌ مِنْ شَرِّ الدُّنْيَا وَلْاٰخِرَةِ وَمِنْ شَرِّ الْاَوْجَاعِ كُلِّهَا وَمِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ دَابَّةٍ اَنْتَ آخِذٌ بِنَاصِيَتِهَا اِنَّ رَبّىْ عَلٰى صِرَاطٍ مُّسْتَقِيْمٍ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ اِلَّا بِاللهِ الْعَلِىِّ الْعَظِيْمِ تَوَّكَلْتُ عَلَى الْحَىِّ الَّذِىْ لَا يَمُوْتُ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلهِ الَّذِىْ لَمْ يَتَّخِذْ وَلَدًا وَّلَمْ يَكُنْ لَّهٗ شَرِيْكٌ فِى الْمُلْكِ وَلَمْ يَكُنْ لَّهٗ وَلِىُّ مِنَ الذُّلِّ وَكَبِّرْهُ تَكْبِيْرًا۔
اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلیٰ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

4) Then, recite Tasbeeh-e-Zahra before you move from your namaz-place, repeat the following zikr 10 times…
4) Phir Tasbeeh-e-Zahra (s.a) padhe iske pehle ke aap apni namaz ki jagah se utthe, yeh zikr 10 martaba padhe…

اَشْهَدُ اَنْ لَّا اِلٰهَ اِلَا اللهُ وَحْدَهٗ لَا شَرِيْكَ لَهٗ اِلٰهًا وَّاحِدًا اَحَدْا فَرْدًا صَمَدًا لَمْ يَتَّخِذْ صَاحِبَةً وَلَا وَلَدًا۔

5) Then, say the following dua…
5) Phir yeh dua padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

سُبْحَانَ اللهِ كُلَّمَا سَبَّحَ اللهَ شَىْٓءٌ وَّكَمَا يُحِبُّ اللهُ اَنْ يُّسَبَّحَ وَكَمَا هُوَ اَهْلُهٗ وَكَمَا يَنْبَغِىْ لِكَرَمِ وَجْهِهٖ وَعِزِّ جَلَالِهٖ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلهِ كُلَّمَا حَمِدَ اللهَ شَىْٓءٌ وَّكَمَا يُحِبُّ اللهُ اَنْ يُّحْمَدَ وَكَمَا هُوَ اَهْلُهٗ وَكَمَا يَنْبَغِىْ لِكَرَمِ وَجْهِهٖ وَعِزِّ جَلَالِهٖ وَلَا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا اللهُ كُلَّمَا هَلَّلَ اللّهَ شَىْٓءٌ وَّكَمَا يُحِبُّ اللهُ اَنْ يُّهَلَّلَ وَكَمَا هُوَ اَهْلُهٗ وَكَمَا يَنْبَغِىْ لِكَرَمِ وَجْهِهٖ وَعِزِّ جَلَالِهٖ۔ وَاللّهُ اَكْبَرُ كُلَّمَا كَبَّرَ اللهَ شَىْٓءٌ وَّكَمَا يُحِبُّ اللهُ اَنْ يُّكَبَّرَ وَكَمَا هُوَ اَهْلُهٗ وَكَمَا يَنْبَغِىْ لِكَرَمِ وَجْهِهٖ وَعِزِّ جَلَالِهٖ سُبْحَانَ اللهِ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلهِ وَلَا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا اللهُ وَاللهُ اَكْبَرُ عَلٰى كُلِّ نِعْمَةٍ اَنْعَمَ بِهَا عَلَىَّ وَعَلٰى كُلِّ اَحَدٍ مِّنْ خَلْقِهٖ مِمَّنْ كَانَ اَوْ يَكُوْنُ اِلٰى يَوْمِ الْقِيْامَةِ۔ اَللّٰهُمَّ اِنّىْ اَسْئَلُكَ اَنْ تُصَلِّىَ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاَسْئَلُكَ مِنْ خَيْرِ مَا اَرْجُوْ وَخَيْرِ مَا لَا اَرْجُوْ وَاَعُوْذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا اَحْذَرُ وَمِنْ شَرِّ مَا لَا اَحْذَرُ۔
اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلیٰ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

6) Then, recite the following Quranic texts…
6) Phir yeh Ayaat-e-Qur’an padhe…
a) Surah Fateha (Chp۔1)
b) Ayat al-Kursi (2:255-257)
c) Ayat-e-Shahadah (3:18-19)
d) Ayat-e-Mulk (3:26-27)
e) Ayat-e-Sakharah (7:54-56)
f) Ayat-e-Surah Saaffaat (37:180-182) 3 times (3 martaba)…
7) Then, repeat the following zikr 3 times…
7) Phir yeh zikr 3 martaba padhe…

اَللّٰهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاجْعَلْ لِىْ مِنْ اَمْرِىْ فَرَجَا وَمَخْرَجَا وَارْزُقْنِىْ مِنْ حَيْثُ اَحْتَسِبُ وَمِنْ حَيْثُ لَا اَحْتَسِبُ۔

8) Then, hold your beard with your right hand, raise your left hand towards the sky, and repeat the following zikr 7 times…
8) Phir apni daadi (beard) seedhe haath (right hand) se pakde aur apna ulta haath (left hand) aasmaan ki taraf buland karke 7 martaba yeh zikr padhe…

يَا رَبَّ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ صَلِّ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّعَجِّلْ فَرَجَ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

9) Be on the same situation, repeat the following zikr 3 times…
9) Usi kaifiyat (position) mein 3 martaba yeh zikr padhe…

يَاذَا الْجَلَالِ وَالَاِكْرَامِ صَلِّ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّارْحَمْنِىْ وَاَجِرْنِىْ مِنَ النَّارِ۔

10) Recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp 112) 12 times and say the following dua…
10) Surah Ikhlaas (Chp 112) 12 martaba padhe aur phir yeh dua padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰهُمَّ اِنّىْ اَسْئَلُكَ بِاسْمِكَ الْمَكْنُوْنِ الْمَخْزُوْنِ الطَّاهِرِ الطُّهْرِ الْمُبَارَكِ وَاَسْئَلُكَ بِاسْمِكَ الْعَظِيْمِ وَسُلْطَانِكَ الْقَدِيْمِ يَاوَاهِبَ الْعَطَايَا وَيَامُطْلِقَ الْاُسْارٰى وَيَا فَكَّاكَ الرِّقَابِ مِنَ النَّارِ اَسْئَلُكَ اَنْ تُصَلِّىَ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاَنْ تُعْتِقَ رَقَبَتِىْ مِنَ النَّارِ وَاَنْ تُخْرِجَنِىْ مِنَ الدُّنْيَا سَالِمًا وَّ )اَنْ( تُدْخِلَنِى الْجَنَّةَ آمِنًا وَّاَنْ تَجْعَلَ دُعَآئِىْ اَوَّلَهٗ فَلَاحًا وَّاَوْسَطَهٗ نَجَاحًا وَّآخِرَهٗ صَلَاحًا اِنَّكَ اَنْتَ عَلَّامُ الْغُيُوْبِ۔
اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلیٰ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

11) It is advisably to recite this zikr after the obligatory namaz…
11) Yeh zikr ko waajib namaz ke baad padhne ki taaqid hai…

يَامَنْ لَّا يَشْغَلُهٗ سَمْعٌ عَنْ سَمْعٍ وَّيَا مَنْ لَّا يُغَلِّطُهُ السَّآئِلُوْنَ وَيَا مَنْ لَّايُبْرِمُهٗ اِلْحَاحُ الْمُلِحِّيْنَ اَذِقْنِىْ بَرْدَ عَفْوِكَ وَحَلَاوَةَ رَحْمَتِكَ وَمَغْفِرَتِكَ۔

12) Before you start your namaz, recite this dua to get concentration in namaz…
12) Jab namaz shuru karne ka iraada ho to namaz mein dil lagne ke liye yeh dua padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰھُمَّ اِنِّیْ اُقَدِّمُ اِلَیْکَ مُحَمَّدًا ﷺ بَیْنَ یَدَیْ حَاجَتِیْ وَاَتَوَجَّہٗ بِہٖ اِلَیْکَ فَاجْعَلْنِیْ بِہٖ وَجِیْھًا عِنْدَکَ فِی الدُّنْیَا وَالْآخِرَۃِ مِنَ الْمُقَرَّبِیْنَ وَاجْعَلْ صَلٰاتِیْ بِہٖ مَقْبُوْلَۃً وَّذَنْبِیْ بِہٖ مَغْفُوْرَۃً وَّدُعَآئِیْ بِہٖ مُسْتَجَابًا اِنَّکَ اَنْتَ اَرْحَمُ الرّٰحِمِیْنَ۔
اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلیٰ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

13) You may also say this dua…
13) Yeh dua bhi padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

لَا حَوْلَ وَ لَا قُوَّۃَ اِلَّا بِاللہِ تَوَکَّلْتُ عَلَی الْحَیِّ الَّذِیْ لَا یَمُوْتُ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلہِ الَّذِیْ لَمْ یَتَّخِذْ وَلَدًا وَّلَمْ یَکُنْ لَّہٗ شَرِیْکٌ فِیْ الْمُلْکِ وَلَمْ یَکُنْ لَّہٗ وَلِیٌّ مِّنَ الذُّلِّ وَکَبِّرْہُ تَکْبِیْرًا۔ سُبْحَانَ اللہِ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلہِ وَلَآ اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اللہُ وَاللہُ اَکْبَرُ وَلِلہِ الْحَمْدُ۔
اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلیٰ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

14) Dua for acceptence of Namaz.
14) Qubooliyat-e-Namaz ki dua.
15) Dua for sharp memory.
15) Haafeza (yaad-daash) tez hone ki dua.
16) It is advisable to repeat the following dua 3 times after obligatory namaz…
16) Waajib namaz ke baad yeh dua 3 martaba padhne ki taaqid hai…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اُعِيْذُ نَفْسِىْ وَدِيْنِىْ وَاَهْلِىْ وَمَالِىْ وَوَلَدِىْ وَاِخْوَانِىْ فِىْ دِيْنِىْ وَمَا رَزَقَنِىْ رَبّىْ وَخَوَاتِيْمَ عَمَلِىْ وَمَنْ يَّعْنِيْنِىْ اَمْرُهٗ بِاللهِ الْوَاحِدِ الْاَحَدِ الصَّمَدِ الَّذِىْ لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُوْلَدْ وَلَمْ يَكُنْ لَّهٗ كُفُوًا اَحَدٌ وَّبِرَبِّ الْفَلَقِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ وَمِنْ شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ اِذَا وَقَبَ وَمِنْ شَرِّ النَّفَّاثَاتِ فِى الْعُقَدِ وَمِنْ شَرِّ حَاسِدٍ اِذَا حَسَدَ وَبِرَبِّ النَّاسِ مَلِكِ النَّاسِ اِلٰهِ النَّاسِ مِنْ شَرِّ الْوَسْوَاسِ الْخَنَّاسِ الَّذِىْ يُوَسْوِسُ فِىْ صُدُوْرِ النَّاسِ مِنَ الْجِنَّةِ وَالنَّاسِ۔
اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلیٰ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

GENERAL TAQIBAAT

1) Waajib namaz ke fauran baad yeh dua padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Khuda ke siwa koi Mabood nahi jo Mabood-e-yagana hai aur ham uske farma-bardaar hai. Khuda ke siwa koi Mabood nahi ham bas usi ki ibaadat karte hai. Usi ke deen ke saath mukhlis hai chahe mushrekin ko na-gawaar ho.

Nahi koi Mabood siwaaye Khuda ke jo hamara aur hamare aaba-o-ajdaad (father and forefathers) ka Rab hai. Khuda ke siwa koi Mabood nahi jo yakta hai, yakta hai, yakta hai. Usne apna waada pura kiya, apne bande ki nusrat farmaayi, uske lashkar ko ghaalib kiya aur akele hi jhund (dushmano) ko maar bhagaya.

Pas mulk usi ka aur hamd usi ke liye hai, wohi zinda karta aur maut deta hai, wahi maut deta aur zinda karta hai. Woh zinda hai, khair (bhalaayi) usi ke haath mein hai aur woh har cheez par qaadir hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

2) Phir kahe…

Mai bakhshish chahta hu us Khuda se jiske siwa koi Mabood nahi. Woh zinda-o-painda hai aur mai usi ke huzoor tauba karta hu.

3) Aur phir kahe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Allah! Apni jaanib se meri rehnumaayi farma. Mujh par apna fazl-o-karam farma. Mujhe apni rehmat ke saaye taley (saaye mein) rakh aur mujh par apni barkate’n naazil farma. Paak hai Teri zaat nahi koi Mabood siwaaye Tere mere saare ke saare gunah bakhsh de keh Tere siwa koi saare gunah maaf nahi kar sakta.

Ay Allah! Mai Tujh se har khair ka talab-gaar hu keh Tera ilm jisko ghere huwe hai aur har shar se Teri panah chahta hu jis par Tera ilm mohit (ghera huwa) hai. Ay Allah! Mai apne tamaam umoor (affairs) mein Tujh se aafiyat talab karta hu, mai duniya ki ruswaayi aur aakherat ke azaab se Teri panah chahta hu. Mai Teri zaat-e-karim aur maqaam-e-buland keh jis tak kisi ki rasaayi (pohonch) nahi aur Teri qudrat keh jiske saamne koi cheez thairti (rukti) nahi.

Inke zariye duniya-o-aakherat ke shar, tamaam dardo’n ki aziyat (takleef) aur har haiwaan (animal) ke shar se panah chahta hu jo Tere qabza-e-qudrat mein hai. Be-shak mere Rab ka raasta mustaqeem (seedha) hai aur harkat-o-quwwat bas Khudaaye azeem-o-bartar hi se milti hai aur mera bharosa us zinda (Khuda) par hai jiske liye maut nahi.

Hamd us Khuda ke liye hai jisne na kisi ko farzand banaya na koi uske mulk mein shareek hai aur na uski aajezi ke sabab koi uska madad-gaar hai aur tum uski badaayi ka izhaar karo.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

5) Phir yeh dua padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Paak hai Khuda jab bhi koi cheez Khuda ki tasbih kare, keh jaisi tasbih ko woh pasand karta hai aur jaisi tasbih ka woh ahel hai aur jaisi tasbih uski zaat-e-karim aur jalaalat-e-shaan ke layaq hai.

Hamd Khuda hi ke liye hai jab bhi koi cheez uski hamd kare keh, jaisi hamd ko woh pasand karta hai aur jaisi hamd ka woh ahel hai keh jo uski zaat-e-karim aur izzat-o-jalaal ke laayaq hai.

Nahi koi Mabood siwaaye Khuda ke jab bhi koi cheez uska zikr kare, jaise zikr ko Khuda pasand karta hai keh woh uska ahel hai aur jo zikr uski buzurgi aur izzat-o-jalaal ke shaayaan-e-shaan hai.

Khuda busurg-tar hai jab bhi koi cheez uski buzurgi bayaan kare, jaisi buzurgi ko woh pasand karta hai jiska ahel hai aur jo buzurg-daasht uski buland shaan aur izzat-o-jalaal ke laayaq hai.

Paak hai Khuda aur hamd usi ke liye hai aur nahi koi Mabood siwaaye Khuda ke aur Khuda buzurg-tar hai har nemat par jo usne mujhe di aur jo guzri huwi makhluq par huwi aur ta-qayaamat aane waali makhluq par hoti rahegi.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

19) Hifz-e-Imaan ki dua.

GENERAL TAQIBAAT

1) Immediately after your obligatory namaz recite this dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

There is no god save Allah, the one true God. And, unto Him we have surrendered. There is no god save Allah. We do not worship but Him (alone), making the religion pure, for Him (only), however much idolaters may be averse. There is no god save Allah; our Lord, and Lord of our fathers of old.

There is no god save Allah, and no other, Allah, and no other, Allah, and no other, (who) fulfilled his promise, helped his servant, loved dearly and backed up his troop, and put host of clans to flight, therefore to Him belongs the kingdom (sovereignty), unto Him (alone) belongs the praise. He gives life and causes death, puts to death and brings to life, He is ever living, dies not, in His hand ss the good; and He is able to do all things.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

2) Then, say the following…

I seek forgiveness of Allah, (who is) “There is no god save Allah” the ever living, the everlasting and unto Him I turn repentant.

3) Then, say the following…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O my Allah make me find the right way under thy guidance, single me out for bestowing upon me Thy bounties, spread out and make me accessible for me Thy mercy, let Thy blessings come down and put up with me.

Glory be to Thee, there is no god save Allah, blot out my errors and faults, one and all, altogether, because no one can cover them up, except Thee. O my Allah, I ask Thee for everything good there is that Thy wisdom surrounds; I take refuge with Thee from every evil that Thy wisdom surrounds. O my Allah! I ask Thee for success and safety, in all matters concerning me.

I seek Thy shelter from misery and disgrace I come across in this world, and the trouble I may have to face on the day of Judgment; I take shelter under Thy kind and compassionate, equitable ways and means, and Thy glorious majesty that does not fix terms (predestination), and Thy omnipotence that does not arbitrarily prevent things from (obeying the operation of) evil of this world and hereafter (if they chose to be in the thick of it); also it is true in connection with the evil of hunger and the diseases and evil of (all type of) violence.

Thou leads, uncovering the evil, certainly my Lord, to the right path. There is no stratagem, and no strength save in Allah, the highest high. I commit my affairs to the charge of that ever living who does not die.

(All) praise is for Allah, who did not choose for himself a son; there is no partner (or associate) in His kingdom (sovereignty); nor He makes friends with (any) mean and low individual, and say “Allahu Akbar”.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

4) Then, recite Tasbeeh-e-Zahra before you move from your namaz-place, repeat the following zikr 10 times…

I bear witness that witness that there is no God save Allah, alone, He has no partner, Lord God, Unique, One, Single, the eternally Besought of all. He has chosen no consort, nor has He any son.

5) Then, say the following dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Glory be to Allah, as often as often as and each time when something glorifies Allah, and just as loves Allah that which glorifies Him with (the words) “Subh’aanallaahi” as He (alone) is fit for and is worthy of, since it is for the sake of His kind disposition, and glory of His Majesty.

(All) praise is for Allah, as often as and each time when something praises Allah, and just as loves Allah that which praises Him with (the worlds) “Alhamdulillaah” as He (alone) is it fit for and is worthy of, since it is for the sake of His kind disposition, and glory of His Majesty.
