COMMON AMAAL

Common Amaal for 19th 21st 23rd Shab-e-Qadr
19th 21st 23rd ke Mushtareka Amaal Shab-e-Qadr

Merits of Shab-e-Qadr Nights…

One must remain awake on all the three (19th, 21st, 23rd) nights and spend whole night in worship and try to pray and supplicate as much as possible so that we are able to get the excellence of Shab-e-Qadr۔ All affairs of the year are destined on Shab-e-Qadr. The Amaal of these 3 nights are of 2 types: Some are to be performed on all the 3 nights and some of them are specific to a particular night. Amaal to be performed on all 3 nights.

Shab-e-Qadr ki fazilat…

Shabe Qadr aisi azeem raat hai keh aam raate iski fazeelat ko nahi pohonch sakte. Kyu’n keh is raat ka amal hazaar mahino ke amal se behtar hai, isi raat taqdeer likkhi jaati hai aur Ruhul Ameen jo malaayeka mein sab se azeem hai woh isi raat Parwardigaar ke hukm se farishto ke saath zameen par naazil hote hai. Yeh malaayeka Imam-e-Asr (a.t.f.s) ki khidmat mein haazir hote hai aur har kisi ke muqaddar mein jo kuchh bhi likkha jaata hai uski tafseel Hazrat ki khidmat mein pesh karte hai.

1) Perform Ghusl before praying Namaz-e-Maghrebain.
1) Ghusl kare aur Namaaz-e-Maghrib usi ghusl ke saath adaa kare.

2) Recite 2 rakats namaz, in each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp۔1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp۔112) 7 times۔ After the prayer, recite 70 times…
2) 2-Rakat Namaaz baja laaye jiski har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp۔1) ke baad 7-martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp۔112) padhe, aur namaaz ke baad 70-martaba kahe…

اَسْتَغْفِرُ اللهَ رَبِّيْ وَاَتُوْبُ اِلَيْهِ۔

3) Amaal-e-Qur’an: It is Mustahab to hold the open Quran and recite the following dua…
3) Aamaal-e-Qur’an ko anjaam de. Qur’an-e-Kareem ko khol kar apne saamne rakkhe aur kahe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰہُمَّ اِنِّیْ اَسْئَلُکَ بِکِتَابِکَ الْمُنْزَلِ وَمَا فِیْہِ وَ فِیْہِ اسْمُکَ الْاَکْبَرُ وَاَسْمَآؤُکَ الْحُسْنٰی وَمَا یُخَافُ وَ یُرْجٰی اَنْ تَجْعَلَنِیْ مِنْ عُتَقَآئِکَ مِنَ النَّارِ۔

After that supplicate for your needs.
Uske baad jo haajat chahe talab kare.

Then one should place the Quran on his head and say…
Phir Qur’an-e-paak ko apne sar par rakkhe aur kahe…

اَللّٰھُمَّ بِحَقِّ ھٰذَالْقُرْاٰنِ وَبِحَقِّ مَنْ اَرْسَلْتَہٗ بِہٖ وَبِحَقِّ کُلِّ مُؤْمِنٍ مَدَحْتَہٗ فِیْہِ وَبِحَقِّکَ عَلَیْہِمْ فَلَا اَحَدَ اَعْرَفُ بِحَقِّکَ مِنْکَ۔

Then repeat 10 times each…
Phir das-das (10-times each) martaba kahe…

بِکَ یَااَللہُ۔

بِمُحَمَّدٍ۔

بِعَلِیٍّ۔

بِفَاطِمَۃَ۔

بِالْحَسَنِ۔

بِالْحُسَیْنِ۔

بِعَلِیِّ بْنِ الْحُسَیْنِ۔

بِمُحَمَّدِ بْنِ عَلِیٍّ۔

بِجَعْفَرِ بْنِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

بِمُوْسَی بْنِ جَعْفَرٍ۔

بِعَلِیِّ بْنِ مُوْسٰی۔

بِمُحَمَّدِ بْنِ عَلِیٍّ۔

بِعَلِیِّ بْنِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

بِالْحَسَنِ بْنِ عَلِیٍّ۔

بِالْحُجَّۃِ الْقَآئِمِ عَلَیْہِ السَّلَامُ۔

After that supplicate for your needs.
Phir apni haajat talab kare.

4) Recite 100 rakats namaz in units of 2 just like Namaz-e-Fajr, but if one has his obligatory namaz due, then he should recite his qaza namaz starting with Zuhr and ending with Fajr.
4) 100 Rakat namaaz, 2x2 rakat kare Namaz-e-Subh ki tarah padhe. Agar kisi ki qaza namaz ho to woh Namaz-e-Zohar se shuru kare aur Namaz-e-Fajr pe khatm karte huwe anjaam de.

5) Receite Dua Jawshan Kabir.
5) Dua Jawshan Kabir padhe.

6) Receite Dua Jawshan Saghir.
6) Dua Jawshan Saghir padhe.

7) Recite Salawat as many time as you can.
7) Jitni martaba ho sakey Salawat padhe.

8) Seek forgiveness on behalf of ones parents, relatives and believers; those who are alive as well as those who have departed۔ One must also supplicate for the wellbeing of the world and the Hereafter, especially the reappearance of Imam Zamana (a.t.f.s).
8) Apne (marhoom ya zinda) ghar waale, aziz, dost aur imaani bhaiyo ki taraf se is shab mein zaroor maafi talab kare. Duniya aur Akherat ki kaamiyaabi aur khusoosan Imam-e-Zamana (a.t.f.s) ke zahoor ki dua ko faramosh nahi kare.

9) Recite Ziyarat-e-Lailatul Qadr.
9) Ziyarat-e-Lailatul Qadr padhe.
