SHAB-E-JUM’A

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰهُمَّ مَنْ تَعَبَّاَ وَتَهَيَّاَ وَاَعَدَّ وَاسْتَعَدَّ لِوِفَادَةٍ اِلٰى مَخْلُوْقٍ رَّجَآءَ رِفْدِهٖ وَطَلَبَ نَآئِلِهٖ وَجَآئِزَتِهٖ فَاِلَيْكَ يَارَبِّ تَعْبِيَتِيْ وَاسْتِعْدَادِيْ رَجَآءَ عَفْوِكَ وَطَلَبَ نَآئِلِكَ وَجَآئِزَتِكَ فَلَا تُخَيِّبْ دُعَآئِيْ۔

يَامَنْ لَا يَخِيْبُ عَلَيْهِ سَآئِلٌ (اَلسَّائِلُ‏) وَلَا يَنْقُصُهٗ نَآئِلٌ فَاِنِّيْ لَمْ آتِكَ ثِقَةً بِعَمَلٍ صَالِحٍ عَمِلْتُهٗ وَلَا لِوِفَادَةِ مَخْلُوقٍ رَّجَوْتُهٗ اَتَيْتُكَ مُقِرًّا عَلٰى نَفْسِيْ بِالْاِسَآءَةِ وَالظُّلْمِ، مُعْتَرِفًا بِاَنْ لَا حُجَّةَ لِيْ وَلَا عُذْرَ اَتَيْتُكَ اَرْجُوْ عَظِيْمَ عَفْوِكَ الَّذِيْ عَفَوْتَ (عَلَوْتَ) بِهٖ (عَلٰى) عَنِ الْخَاطِئِيْنَ (الْخَطَّآئِيْنَ) فَلَمْ يَمْنَعْكَ طُوْلُ عُكُوْفِهِمْ عَلٰى عَظِيْمِ الْجُرْمِ اَنْ عُدْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ بِالرَّحْمَةِ فَيَا مَنْ رَحْمَتُهٗ وَاسِعَةٌ وَّعَفْوُهُ عَظِيْمٌ۔

يَاعَظِيْمُ يَاعَظِيْمُ يَاعَظِيْمُ لَا يَرُدُّ غَضَبَكَ اِلَّا حِلْمُكَ وَلَا يُنْجِيْ مِنْ سَخَطِكَ اِلَّا التَّضَرُّعُ اِلَيْكَ فَهَبْ لِيْ يَااِلٰهِيْ فَرَجًا بِالْقُدْرَةِ الَّتِيْ تُحْيِيْ بِهَا مَيْتَ الْبِلَادِ، وَلَا تُهْلِكْنِيْ غَمًّا حَتّٰى تَسْتَجِيْبَ لِيْ وَتُعَرِّفَنِي الْاِجَابَةَ فِيْ دُعَآئِيْ وَاَذِقْنِيْ طَعْمَ الْعَافِيَةِ اِلٰى مُنْتَهٰى اَجَلِيْ وَلَا تُشْمِتْ بِيْ عَدُوِّيْ وَلَا تُسَلِّطْهُ عَلَيَّ وَلَا تُمَكِّنْهُ مِنْ عُنُقِيْ۔

اَللّٰهُمَّ (اِلَهِيْ‏) اِنْ وَضَعْتَنِيْ فَمَنْ ذَا الَّذِيْ يَرْفَعُنِيْ وَاِنْ رَفَعْتَنِيْ فَمَنْ ذَا الَّذِيْ يَضَعُنِيْ وَاِنْ اَهْلَكْتَنِيْ فَمَنْ ذَا الَّذِيْ يَعْرِضُ لَكَ فِيْ عَبْدِكَ اَوْ يَسْاَلُكَ عَنْ اَمْرِهٖ، وَقَدْ عَلِمْتُ اَنَّهٗ لَيْسَ فِيْ حُكْمِكَ ظُلْمٌ وَّلَا فِيْ نَقِمَتِكَ عَجَلَةٌ وَّاِنَّمَا يَعْجَلُ مَنْ يَّخَافُ الْفَوْتَ وَاِنَّمَا يَحْتَاجُ اِلَى الظُّلْمِ الضَّعِيْفُ وَقَدْ تَعَالَيْتَ يَااِلٰهِيْ عَنْ ذَلِكَ عُلُوًّا كَبِيْرًا۔

اَللَّهُمَّ اِنِّي اَعُوْذُ بِكَ فَاَعِذْنِيْ وَاَسْتَجِيْرُ بِكَ فَاَجِرْنِيْ وَاَسْتَرْزِقُكَ فَارْزُقْنِيْ وَاَتَوَكَّلُ عَلَيْكَ فَاكْفِنِيْ وَاَسْتَنْصِرُكَ عَلٰى عَدُوِّيْ (عَدُوِّكَ) فَانْصُرْنِيْ وَاَسْتَعِيْنُ بِكَ فَاَعِنِّيْ وَاَسْتَغْفِرُكَ يَااِلٰهِيْ فَاغْفِرْ لِيْ آمِيْنَ آمِيْنَ آمِيْنَ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

SHAB-E-JUM’A

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Khudaaya! Jo bhi ataa wa bakhshish ke liye makhluq ki taraf jaane ko amaadah aur mustayid ho (to be ready and alert), uski ummeed usi ki daad-o-hash (ataa wa bakhshish) par lagi hoti hai. (jis bande ke paas woh jaata hai) to ay mere Parwardigaar: meri aamadgi wa taiyyari Tere afvo wa dar-guzar (moaafi-o-uzr-khwahi) Teri bakhshish aur Tere in’aam ke husool (achievement) ki ummeed par hai.

Pas meri dua ko (radd kar ke) maayoos na kar ay woh zaat jisse koi saahil na-ummeed nahi hota. Kisi shaiy ka haasil karna uski ataa ko kam nahi kar sakta hai. Pas mai ne jo amal-e-saaleh (nek kaam) kiya uske bharose par Teri janaab (baargah) mein nahi aaya aur na hi makhluq ke den (ataa-o-karam) ki ummeed rakhta hu.

Mai to apni buraaiyo aur (apne nafs par) zulm ka iqraar karte huwe Teri baargah mein haazir huwa hu aur etraaf (accept) karta hu keh mai koi hujjat (daleel) aur uzr (excuse) nahi rakhta hu. Mai Tere huzoor afve-azeem (badi bakhshish) ki ummeed le kar aaya hu jisse Tu khata-kaaro ko maaf farmata hai keh unke bade gunaho ka tasalsul (non-stop) Tujhe un par rehmat karne se baaz nahi rakh sakta (nahi rokta) to ay woh zaat jiski rehmat aam aur afvo-o-bakhshish azeem hai.

Ay Khuda-e-Azeem, ay Khuda-e-Azeem, ay Khuda-e-Azeem. Tera ghazab Tere hi hilm (bardaasht) se palat sakta hai aur Teri naraazgi Tere Huzoor nalaa wa faryaad (gid-gidaane) se hi dur ho sakti hai to ay mere Khuda mujhe apni qudrat se kashaaish (khush-haali) ataa kar jisse Tu ujde huwe shahro (cities) ko aabaad karta hai mujhe gham-gheeni mein (dukho ke zariye) halaaq na kar, yahan tak keh Tu meri dua ko qabool kar le, aur dua ki qabooliyat se mujhe aagah farma (jaan kar) de, mujhe aakhir-e-dam (aakhri saans) tak sehat wa aafiyat se rakh. Aur mere dushman ko meri buri haalat par khush na ho ne de, aur usey mujh par tasallut aur ikhtiyaar na de.

Ay Parwardigaar! Agar Tu mujhe ghira de to kaun mujhe uthaane waala hai? Aur agar Tu mujhe buland kare to kaun hai jo mujhe past kar (ghira) sakta hai. Agar Tu mujhe halaaq kare to kaun Tere bande se mutaalliq (related) Tujhe kuchh kahe sakta hai uske mutaalliq sawaal kar sakta hai (kaun hai jo mere baare mein Tujh se poonchhne jaayega). Be-shak mai jaanta hu keh Tere faisle mein zulm nahi aur Tere azaab mein jaldi nahi. Aur be-shak jaldi woh karta hai jise waqt nikal jaane ka darr ho, aur zulm woh karta hai jo kamzor ho (quwwate bardaasht na rakhta ho).

Aur ay mere Mabood! Tu in baato se bohot buland-o-baala aur bohot bada hai. Ay Ma’bood! Mai Teri panah chahta hu Tu mujhe panah de. Tere nazdeek aata hu Tu mujhe nazdeek kar le, Tujh se rozi maangta hu mujhe rozi de, Tujh par bharosa karta hu Tu meri kifalat (parwarish) farma. Apne dushman ke khilaaf Tujh se madad chahta hu aur nusrat (madad) ka taalib hu Tu meri madad farma. Aur ay mere Ma’bood! Tujh se bakhshish ka taalib hu Tu mujhe bakhsh de, Aameen-Aameen-Aameen. (Ya Rabbal Aalameen).

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

SHAB-E-JUM’A

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, One may call up, prepare, ready oneself, and stand prepared for purpose of coming to a creature in the hope of gaining his aid and asking for his gift and present. As for myself, to You O my Lord I make my preparations and readiness hoping for Your pardon and asking for Your gift and present. So, (please) do not disappoint my entreaty.

O He Who never disappoints His beseechers and Whose prizes never decrease His ample-giving! I have not come to You with confidence of a righteous deed that I have done nor have I had any hope in a grant of any creature. Rather, I have come to You professing myself to be evildoer and transgressor and confessing that I lack any pretext or excuse.

I have come to You hoping for Your great pardon due to which You have pardoned the wrongdoers. Hence, their long-lasting retirement to their grave offenses has not prevented You from turning to them with mercy. O He Whose mercy is huge and Whose pardon is great;

O All-great, O All-great, O All-great; nothing can repel Your rage except Your forbearance and nothing can save from Your wrath except imploring earnestly to You.

So, my God, (please) grant me a relief in the name of the power through which You restore to life the derelict lands, and do not cause me to perish out of grief until You respond to me and show me signs of Your response to my prayers. (please) Make me taste the flavor of wellbeing up to the end of my lifetime, do not make my enemies gloat over me, do not give them a hand over me, and do not make them control over me.

O Allah, If You humiliate me, who can then exalt me? And if You exalt me, who can then humiliate me? If You annihilate me, who can then interfere before You concerning the affair of me Your slave or who can intercede to ask You about it?

I have already known for sure that Your decrees are devoid of injustice and Your punishment does not count on immediateness. Verily, immediateness is the work of him who fears lest he may miss, and only do the weak need injustice. As for You, O my God, You are too exalted to be so Exalted and Great You are beyond all measure!

O Allah, I take refuge in You; so, (please) have protection upon me! I call for Your safekeeping; so, (please) keep me under Your security! I appeal for Your sustenance; so, (please) provide me with sustenance!

I put trust in You; so, (please) keep me under Your supervision! I call for Your aid against my enemy; so, (please) grant me support! I seek Your assistance; so, (please) assist me! I ask for Your forgiveness; so, (please) forgive me. So, Respond! Respond! Respond!

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
