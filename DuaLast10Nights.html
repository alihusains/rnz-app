LAST 10 NIGHTS

Duas for the last 10 nights…
It is narrated from Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) that one must recite the following duas on each of the last ten nights:
Aakhari 10 raato mein padhne ki Duas …Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) se riwaayat hai keh Mahe Ramazan ke aakhari 10 shabo mein yeh duaaye padhe:


Dua - 1

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَعُوْذُ بِجَلَالِ وَجْہِکَ الْکَرِیْمِ اَنْ یَّنْقَضِیَ عَنِّیْ شَہْرُ رَمَضَانَ اَوْ یَطْلُعَ الْفَجْرُ مِنْ لَیْلَتِیْ ہٰذِہٖ وَلَکَ قِبَلِیْ ذَنْبٌ اَوْ تَبِعَۃٌ تُعَذِّبُنِیْ عَلَیْہِ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

Dua - 2

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰهُمَّ اِنَّكَ قُلْتَ فِيْ كِتَابِكَ الْمُنْزَلِ شَهْرُ رَمَضَانَ الَّذِيْ اُنْزِلَ فِيْهِ الْقُرْآنُ هُدًى لِّلنَّاسِ وَبَيِّنَاتٍ مِّنَ الْهُدٰى وَالْفُرْقَانِ فَعَظَّمْتَ حُرْمَةَ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ بِمَا اَنْزَلْتَ فِيْهِ مِنَ الْقُرْآنِ وَخَصَصْتَهٗ بِلَيْلَةِ الْقَدْرِ وَجَعَلْتَهَا خَيْرًا مِّنْ اَلْفِ شَهْرٍ۔

اَللّٰهُمَّ وَهٰذِهِ اَيَّامُ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ قَدِ انْقَضَتْ وَلَيَالِيْهِ قَدْ تَصَرَّمَتْ وَقَدْ صِرْتُ۔ يَااِلٰهِيْ مِنْهُ اِلٰى مَا اَنْتَ اَعْلَمُ بِهٖ مِنِّيْ وَاَحْصٰى لِعَدَدِهٖ مِنَ الْخَلْقِ اَجْمَعِيْنَ فَاَسْاَلُكَ بِمَا سَئَلُكَ بِهٖ مَلَآئِكَتُكَ الْمُقَرَّبُوْنَ وَاَنْبِيَاؤُكَ الْمُرْسَلُوْنَ وَعِبَادُكَ الصَّالِحُوْنَ اَنْ تُصَلِّيَ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔ وَاَنْ تَفُكَّ رَقَبَتِيْ مِنَ النَّارِ وَتُدْخِلَنِي الْجَنَّةَ بِرَحْمَتِكَ وَاَنْ تَتَفَضَّلَ عَلَيَّ بِعَفْوِكَ وَكَرَمِكَ وَتَتَقَبَّلَ وَتَسْتَجِيْبَ دُعَآئِيْ وَتَمُنَّ عَلَيَّ بِالْاَمْنِ يَوْمَ الْخَوْفِ مِنْ كُلِّ هَوْلٍ اَعْدَدْتَهٗ لِيَوْمِ الْقِيَامَةِ۔

اِلٰهِي وَاَعُوْذُ بِوَجْهِكَ الْكَرِيْمِ وَبِجَلَالِكَ الْعَظِيْمِ اَنْ يَّنْقَضِيَ اَيَّامُ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ وَلَيَالِيْهِ وَلَكَ قِبَلِيْ تَبِعَةٌ اَوْ ذَنْبٌ تُؤَاخِذُنِيْ بِهٖ اَوْ خَطِيْئَةٌ تُرِيْدُ اَنْ تَقْتَصَّهَا مِنِّيْ لَمْ تَغْفِرْهَا لِيْ۔

سَيِّدِيْ سَيِّدِيْ سَيِّدِيْ اَسْاَلُكَ يَالَا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ اِذْ لَا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ اِنْ كُنْتَ رَضِيْتَ عَنِّيْ فِيْ هٰذَا الشَّهْرِ فَازْدَدْ عَنِّيْ رِضًا وَّاِنْ لَّمْ تَكُنْ رَضِيْتَ عَنِّيْ فَمِنَ الْآنَ فَارْضَ عَنِّيْ يَااَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِيْنَ۔ يَا اللهُ يَااَحَدُ يَاصَمَدُ يَامَنْ لَّمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُوْلَدْ وَلَمْ يَكُنْ لَّهٗ كُفُوًا اَحَدٌ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

Dua - 3

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰھُمَّ اَدِّ عَنَّا حَقَّ مَا مَضٰی مِنْ شَھْرِ رَمَضَانَ وَاغْفِرْ لَنَا تَقْصِیْرَنَا فِیْہِ وَتَسَلَّمْہُ مِنَّا مَقْبُوْلًا۔ وَلَا تُؤَاخِذْنَا بِاِسْرَافِنَا عَلٰی اَنْفُسِنَا، وَاجْعَلْنَا مِنَ الْمَرْحُوْمِیْنَ وَلَا تَجْعَلْنَا مِنَ الْمَحْرُوْمِیْنَ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

Dua - 4

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

يَامُلَيِّنَ الْحَدِيْدِ لِدَاوُدَ۔ يَاكَاشِفَ الضُّرِّ وَالْكُرَبِ الْعِظَامِ عَنْ اَيُّوْبَ۔ اَيْ مُفَرِّجَ هَمِّ يَعْقُوْبَ۔ اَيْ مُنَفِّسَ غَمِّ يُوْسُفَ۔ صَلِّ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ كَمَا اَنْتَ اَهْلُهٗ اَنْ تُصَلِّيَ عَلَيْهِمْ اَجْمَعِيْنَ وَافْعَلْ بِيْ مَا اَنْتَ اَهْلُهٗ وَلَا تَفْعَلْ بِيْ مَا اَنَا اَهْلُهٗ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

LAST 10 NIGHTS

Aakhari 10 raato mein padhne ki Dua’o …

Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) se riwaayat hai keh Mahe Ramazan ke aakhari 10 shabo mein yeh duaaye padhe:

1) Dua

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Allah!Teri zaate kareem ki panah leta hu isse keh jab mera Mahe Ramazan ikhtetaam-pazeer (khatm) ho ya jab meri is raat ki fajr tulu’a kare (subh ho) to mere zimme koi gunah ya uspar mera giraft (moaakheza) baaqi na ho jis par mujhe azaab de.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

2) Dua

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Ma’bood! Tu ne apni naazil-karda kitaab (Qur’an) mein yeh farmaya hai keh Ramazan woh mahina hai jis mein Qur’an-e-Kareem naazil kiya gaya jo logo ke liye hidaayat hai aur us mein hidaayat ki daleele aur haq wa baatil ka imtiyaz (farq bayaan kiya gaya) hai. Pas Tu ne Mahe Ramazan ko isse buzurghi di, is mein Qur’an-e-Kareem ka nuzool farmaya aur usey shabe qadr ke liye khaas kiya, aur is raat ko hazaar mahino se behtar qaraar diya.

Ay Ma’bood! Yeh Mahe Ramazanul Mubarak ke din hai keh jo guzre ja rahe hai aur iski raate hai jo beet (guzar) rahi hai. Ay mere Allah un guzre shab-o-roz mein meri jo haajat rahi Tu usey mujh se ziyaada jaanta hai aur tamaam logo se badh kar Tu uska hisaab rakhta hai, lehaaza mai us wasile se sawaal karta hu jisse Tere moqarrib farishte sawaal karte hai aur Tere bheje huwe Ambiya (a.s) aur Tere nek bande sawaal karte hai keh Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur yeh keh mujhe Jahannam ki aag se rehaayi (freedom) ataa farma.

Aur apni rehmat se mujhe Jannat mein daakhil farma. Neez yeh keh mujh par apne dar-guzar (maghferat) aur ehsaan se fazl kar. Mere qurb haasil karne (ki koshish) ko qabool farma aur meri dua ko qabooliyat bakhsh. Aur mujh par ehsaan karte huwe us khauf ke din ki har dehshat (darr) se mehfuz rakh. Jo Tu ne roze qayamat ke liye taiyyaar ki huwi hai.

Ay Allah! Mai panah leta hu Teri zaat-e-kareem aur Tere buzurg-tar jalaal ki isse keh jab Mahe Ramazanul Mubarak ke din aur raate guzar jaaye to mere zimme koi jawaabdehi (responsibility) ho ya koi gunah ho jis par meri giraft (pakad) kare ya koi lagzish ho Tu mujhe jiski saza dena chahta ho aur uski moaafi na di ho.

Mere maalik, mere aaqa, mere sardaar! Mai sawaal karta hu ay woh keh nahi koi ma’bood magar Tu hi hai. Agar Tu is mahine mein mujh se raazi ho gaya hai to mere liye apni khushnoodi mein izaafa farma. Aur agar Tu mujh se raazi nahi huwa to is ghadi (lamha) mujh se raazi ho ja. Ay sab se ziyaada rahem karne waale.

Ay Allah! ay ek-o-tanha, ay be-neyaaz. Ay woh jisne na jnaa (gave birth) hai aur na jnaa gaya hai. Aur na koi uska hamsar (life-partner) hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

3) Dua

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Mahe Ramazan ka jo haq hamari taraf rahe gaya ho woh hamari jaanib se adaa kar de hamara yeh qasoor maaf farma aur is ham se pura qabool farma is mah (month) mein ham ne apne nafs par jo ziyaadati ki us par hamein na pakad aur hamein un logo’n mein rakh jin par rahem ho chuka aur hamein na-kaam logo’n mein qaraar na de.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

4) Dua

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Hazrat-e-Dawood (a.s) ke liye lohe (Iron) ko narm karne waale. Ay Hazrat-e-Ayub (a.s) ke dukh aur takleef ko hata dene waale. Ay Hazrat-e-Yaqoob (a.s) ki be-taabi dur karne waale. Ay Hazrat-e-Yousuf (a.s) ka ranj mita dene waale. Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma. Jaisa keh Tu uska ahel hai keh un sab par apni taraf se rehmat naazil farma aur mere saath woh sulook kar jo Tere shayaan-e-shaan hai. Aur woh sulook na kar jiska mai sazawaar hu.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

LAST 10 NIGHTS

Duas for the last 10 nights…
It is narrated from Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) that one must recite the following duas on each of the last ten nights:

Dua -1

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

I seek refuge in the majesty of Your noble visage that so that this month of Ramazan passes in such a way or that the morning breaks tomorrow in such a condition that a sin or a recompense remains in my account that You will punish me for it.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

Dua -2

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah Thou has said in Thy revealed Book that in the month of Ramazan Thou had sent down the Holy Quran, a guidance to mankind; (in it) there are Signs unto the Right Path which distinguish between evil and good; and thus Thou made great and rendered important the sanctity of this month of Ramazan and distinguished it with the “Laylatul Qadr”, which has been declared better than a thousand months.

O Allah these days of the month of Ramazan have gone by and nights are at the end, and how I have been attached to them and that which I tried to accomplish is known to Thee more than me, and Thou knows better than anyone about the quality and quantity of the preparations, so I beseech Thee through it, Just as Thy favorite Angels, Thy appointed Prophets and Thy pious servants had beseeched Thee, to send blessings on Muhammad and on the children of Muhammad, and draw me apart from the drift that leads to Hellfire and kindly show me into Paradise, give preference to sparing dispensation and treat me with kindness and generosity, give access to my approach for coming nearer, listen to my prayers, and be merciful and keep me safe from every frightful threat put in readiness for the Day of Judgment, a day everyone will feel unsafe therein.

O my Allah, I take refuge with Thy loving and kind disposition and Thy matchless majesty lest the days and nights of the month of Ramazan slip off and I am left alone to face Thee, liable to be punished, and sinful, for which Thou may take suitable action, and wrongdoings for which Thou may decide to penalize me in retribution, and thereupon find me unfit for lenient treatment, O Lord Master, Lord Master, Lord Master.

I beseech Thee O “there is no god save Thou.” If “there is no god save Thou” is happy with me in this month, then which goodwill is more satisfying; and if Thou art not satisfied with me, then who can please me better than Thou, O Most Merciful. O Allah, O Single, O Everlasting Independent, O He who begets not, nor was begotten; and there is none comparable unto Him.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

Dua -3

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, absolve us from the rights of the portion of the month of Ramazan that has passed and forgive us for whatever shortcoming we might have had in it. Accept it from us and do not hold us accountable for the excesses that we have done to our souls and include us among those who are washed in Your mercy and do make us from the sinners.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

Dua -4

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O He who melted the iron for Dawood, peace be on him! O He who relieved Ayyoob, peace be on him, from affliction and pain! O He who dispelled the sorrows of Yaqoob, peace be on him! O He who put the sad Yusuf, peace be on him, at ease! Send blessings on Muhammad and on the children of Muhammad; and in a manner befitting Thee, bless all of them; and so also likewise settle my affairs, and do not do that which I deserve.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
