Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Jab Khuda ki madad aur fatah ki manzil aa jaayegi.

2. Aur aap dekhenge keh log deen-e-Khuda mein fauj-dar-fauj daakhil ho rahe hai.

3. To apne Rab ki hamd ki tasbeeh kare aur us se isteghfaar kare keh woh bohot zeyada tauba qabool karne waala hai.

110 | NASR | 3

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. When there comes the help of Allah and the victory,

2. And you see men entering the religion of Allah in companies,

3. Then celebrate the praise of your Lord, and ask His forgiveness; surely He is oft-returning (to mercy).
