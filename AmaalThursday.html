THURSDAY

Thursday has been assigned to Imam Hasan Askari (a.s).
Thursday Imam Hasan Askari (a.s) se mansub hai.

NAQSH…
One who looks at this Naqsh on Thursday will receive love and respect, success in work, prosperity throughout life and eternal peace and happiness.

Jo shakhs is Naqsh ko baroz Thursday dekhe to woh tamaam logo’n ki nazar mein aziz o mohtaram hoga aur usey kaamiyaabi haasil hogi aur duniya wa aakherat mein Khushi aur sukoon haasil hoga.

ZIKR…
Recite 1000 times…
1000 Martaba padhe…

لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اللہُ الْمَلِکُ الْحَقُّ الْمُبِیْن

LA ILAAHA ILLALLAAHUL MALEKUL HAQQUL MOBEEN
There is no power except Allah, the King and the Manifest Truth.
Nahi hai koi Mabood siwaay Allah ke, jo Maalik aur waaze Haq hai.

NAMAZ…
Recite 10-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) 10 times.
10 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 10 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) padhe.

DUA…
Recite Thursday Dua.
Thursday ki Dua padhe.

ZIYARAT…
Recite Thursday Ziyarat.
Thursday ki Ziyarat padhe.
