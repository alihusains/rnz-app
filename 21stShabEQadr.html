21ST SHAB-E-QADR

1) Ziyarat Amirul Momineen (a.s) padhe.

2) Sau (100) martaba kahe…

Astaghferullaha Rabi wa atoobo ilai-he.
Moaafi (bakhshish) chahta hu Allah se jo mera Rab hai aur uske huzoor tauba karta hu.

3) 100 Martaba padhe…

Allahummal-an qatalata Ameeril Momineen.
Ay Ma’bood! laanat bhej Ameerul Momineen ke qaatilo par.

4) Aakhri 10 shab ki dua’o mein se 21st Shab ki Dua padhe.

Imam Ali (a.s) ki Aakhri Wasiyyat
(apne bachcho aur Shiyo ke liye)

Jab Aap (a.s) ko Ibne Muljim (laanatullah) zarbat laga chuka to aap ne Hasan (a.s) wa Husain (a.s) se farmaya…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Main Tum dono ko wasiyyat karta hu keh Allah se darte rehna, duniya ke khwahishmand na hona agar-che woh (duniya) tumhare pichhe lage, aur duniya ki kisi aisi cheez par na kudh-na (na milne par afsos na karna) jo tum se rok li jaaye. Jo kehna haq aur haq ke liye kehna, aur jo karna sawaab ke liye karna, zaalim se dushmani aur mazloom ke madad-gaar bane rehna.

Main tum ko apni tamaam aulaad ko, apne kunbe (khaandaan) ko aur jin-jin tak mera yeh navishta (wasiyyat-nama) pohonche, sab ko wasiyyat karta hu keh: Allah se darte rehna, apne moamelaat duroost aur aapas ke taalluqaat (relationship) ko suljaaye (maintain) rakhna. Kyu’n keh mai ne tumhare nana Rasoolallah (s.a.w.a) ko farmate sunahai keh aapas ki ranjish (dispute) ko mitana aam namaaz-o-roze se afzal hai.

(Dekho) Yateemo ke baare mein Allah se darte rehna, unke kaam-kaaj ka khayaal rakhna ta-keh faaqeh (bhooke marne) ki naubat na aaye, aur tumhari maujoodgi (presence) mein woh tabaah-o-barbaad na ho jaaye.

Apne ham-saayo (padosiyo) ke baare mein Allah se darte rehna kyu’n keh unke baare mein tumhare Nabi (s.a.w.a) ne baraabar hidaayat ki hai, aur aap is hadd tak unke liye sifaarish karte rahe keh ham logo ko yeh gumaan ho ne laga keh aap unhe bhi virsa (warsa) dilaaye ghe.

Qur’an ke baare mein Allah se darte rehna aisa na ho keh dusre uspar amal karne mein tum par sabqat le jaaye (aaghe badh jaaye).

Namaaz ke baare mein Allah se darna kyu’n keh woh tumhare deen ka sutoon (pilar of religion) hai. Apne Parwardigaar ke ghar ke baare mein Allah se darna apne jeete-ji usey khaali na chhodna kyu’n keh agar yeh khaali chhod diya gaya to phir (azaab se) mohlat na paaonge.

Jaan, maal aur zabaan se raahe Khuda mein jihaad karne ke baare mein Allah ko bhool na jaana. Aur Tum par laazim hai keh aapas mein mil-jul kar rehna aur ek-dusre ki madad karte rehna. Aur khabar-daar ek-dusre ki taraf se peeth pherne aur taalluqaat todne se parhez karna. Neki ka hukm dene aur buraayi se rokne mein kabhi qadam peechhe na hatana. Warna bad-kirdaar log tum par musallat ho jaayege, phir tum dua maango-ge to qabool nahi hogi.

Phir irshaad farmaya…

Ay Abdul Muttaalib ke beto! Aisa na ho ne paaye keh tum Ameerul Momineen (a.s) qatl ho gaye ya qatl kar diye gaye ke naare lagate huwe musalmaano ke khoon se apne haatho ko rang do. Dekho mere badle mein sirf mera qaatil hi qatl kiya jaaye, aur dekho agar mai is zarbat se mar jaau to uske (ibne Muljim ke) ek zarb (zarbat) ke badle mein sirf ek hi zarb lagaana aur uske haath-paiyr na kaatna. Kyu’n keh mai ne Rasoolallah (s.a.w.a) ko farmate huwe sunahai keh khabar-daar kisi ke bhi haath-paiyr na kaato chahe woh kaatne waala Kutta (dog) hi kyu’n na ho.

The Last Will of Imam Ali ibn Abi Talib (AS)

Amir Al-Momeneen Imam Ali ibn Abi Talib (a.s)’s last will to his sons Imam Hasan (a.s) and Imam Hussain (a.s) after the attempt on his life by a stab from Ibn Muljam (l.a)…

My advice to you is to be conscious of Allah and steadfast in your religion. Do not yearn for the world, and do not be seduced by it. Do not resent anything you have missed in it. Proclaim the truth; work for the next world. Oppose the oppressor and support the oppressed.

I advise you, and all my children, my relatives, and whosoever receives this message, to be conscious of Allah, to remove your differences, and to strengthen your ties. I heard your grandfather, peace be upon him, say: "Reconciliation of your differences is more worthy than all prayers and all fasting."

Fear Allah in matters concerning orphans. Attend to their nutrition and do not forget their interests in the middle of yours.

Fear Allah in your relations with your neighbors. Your Prophet often recommended them to you, so much so that we thought he would give them a share in inheritance.

Remain attached to the Quran. Nobody should surpass you in being intent on it, or more sincere in implementing it. Fear Allah in relation to your prayers. It is the pillar of your religion.

Fear Allah in relation to His House; do not abandon it as long as you live. It you should do that you would abandon your dignity.

Persist in jihad in the cause of Allah, with your money, your souls, and your tongue.

Maintain communication and exchange of opinion among yourselves. Beware of disunity and enmity. Do not desist from promoting good deeds and cautioning against bad ones. Should you do that, the worst among you would be your leaders, and you will call upon Allah without response.

O Children of Abdul Muttalib! Do not shed the blood of Muslims under the banner: The Imam has been assassinated! Only the assassin should be condemned to death. If I die of this stab of his, kill him with one similar stroke. Do not mutilate him! I have heard the Prophet, peace be upon him, say: "Mutilate not even a rabid dog."
