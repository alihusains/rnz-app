30 | MAHE RAMAZAN

NAMAZ…

1) Perform Ghusl

2) Recite 12-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) 20 times and after namaz recite salwaat 100 times.

3) Recite 10-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) 10 times and in Ruku & Sujood recite 10 times Tasbihaat Arba…

All glory be to Allah; all praise be to Allah; there is no god save Allah; Allah is the Greatest.

After completing 10 rakat namaz recite 1000 times…

I seek the forgiveness of Allah, my Lord, and I repent before Him.

And then go in prostration and recite the following dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Ever-alive, O Eternal, O Owner of majesty and kindness, O He who is kind enough to have compassion for one and all in this world and in the Hereafter, and to have mercy upon His servants in both the worlds, O the Most Merciful, O God of the formers, forgive our wrongdoings, and accept our obligatory and optional prayers and fasts.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

4) Recite Ziyarat Imam Husain (a.s)

5) Recite Surah Anaam (Chp.6).

6) Recite Surah Kahf (Chp.18).

7) Recite Surah Yasin (Chp.36).

8) Recite 100 times…

I seek the forgiveness of Allah and I repent before Him.

NIGHT DUA...
1) Dua

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, the Lord of the month of Ramazan and the revealer of Quran. And this month of Ramazan has bid farewell. O Lord, I seek Your refuge from the eventuality that this night should pass away and there is still a sin in my account, which You have not forgiven or there is something for which You have not rewarded me yet. Due to which You will punish me when I am presented in Your court. But that You forgive it through Your generosity and forgiveness, O the most merciful of the merciful ones. O Allah, bless Muhammad and Aale Muhammad (a.s). Indeed You are the praised and the favoring.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

2) Dua

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Praise be to Allah, there is no associate with Him. Praise be to Allah just as His loving and kind disposition portrays Him, and His majesty magnifies Him- praise of which He alone is worthy of, O He who is free from all aspects of the created beings, O Light, O Light of purity, O Owner of glory, O He unto whom praise and prayers are directed, O Beneficent, O He who treats with mercy, O Allah, O All-knowing, O Greatest, O Allah, O Subtle, O Majestic, O Allah, O Hearer, O Seer.
O Allah! O Allah! O Allah! Thine are the most beautiful names; highest and greatest are the attributes ascribed to Thee. I solicit Thee (alone) for bounties. Send blessings on Muhammad and on the children of Muhammad and write my name, tonight, in the list of those who have found true happiness, let my soul rest in peace with those who gave their lives in Thy cause, let my good deeds earn for me an honorable place among the dwellers of Paradise, and forgive my sins; grant me sure belief which gives joy and confidence to my heart; and faith which drives away doubts from me; and let me be happy with that which has been determined; give us that which is good in this world and that which is good in the Hereafter and save us from the torment of the burning Fire; and keep me alive in a manner that I always sing Thy praise, gratefully thank Thee, call humbly and fervently on Thee, and turn repentant unto Thee; and associate me with that which keeps me attached with Muhammad and Aale Muhammad, peace be on him and peace be on them.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
