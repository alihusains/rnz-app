KHATM-E-QUR’AN
Upon Completing the Recitation of Qur’an

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰھُمَّ اِنَّکَ اَعَنْتَنِیْ عَلٰی خَتْمِ کِتَابِکَ الَّذِیْ اَنْزَلْتَہٗ نُوْرًا وَّجَعَلْتَہٗ مُھَیْمِنًا عَلٰی کُلِّ کِتَابٍ اَنْزَلْتَہٗ وَفَضَّلْتَہٗ عَلٰی کُلِّ حَدِیْثٍ قَصَصْتَہٗ وَفُرْقَانًا فَرَقْتَ بِہٖ بَیْنَ حَلَالِکَ وَحَرَامِکَ وَقُرْاٰنًا اَعْرَبْتَ بِہٖ عَنْ شَرَآئِعِ اَحْکَامِکَ وَکِتَابًا فَصَّلْتَہٗ لِعِبَادِکَ تَفْصِیْلًا وَّوَحْیًا اَنْزَلْتَہٗ عَلٰی نَبِیِّکَ مُحَمَّدٍ صَلَوٰاتُکَ عَلَیْہِ وَاٰلِہٖ تَنْزِیْلًا وَّجَعَلْتَہٗ نُوْرًا نَھْتَدِیْ مِنْ ظُلَمِ الضَّلَالَۃِ وَالْجِھَالَۃِ بِاِتِّبَاعِہٖ، وَشِفَآئً لِّمَنْ اَنْصَتَ بِفَھَمِ التَّصْدِیْقِ اِلَی اسْتِمَاعِہٖ وَ مِیْزَانَ قِسْطٍ لَّا یَحِیْفُ عَنِ الْحَقِّ لِسَانُہٗ وَنُوْرَ ھُدًی لَّا یَطْفَا عَنِ الشَّاھِدِیْنَ بُرْھَانُہٗ وَعَلَمَ نَجَاۃٍ لَّا یَضِلُّ مَنْ اَمَّ قَصْدَ سُنَّتِہٖ وَلَا تَنَالُ اَیْدِی الْھَلَکَاتِ مَنْ تَعَلَّقَ بِعُرْوَۃِ عِصْمَتِہٖ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ فَاِذْ اَفَدْتَنَا الْمَعُوْنَۃَ عَلٰی تِلَاوَتِہٖ وَسَھَّلْتَ جَوَاسِیَ اَلْسِنَتِنَا بِحُسْنِ عِبَارَتِہٖ فَاجْعَلْنَا مِمَّنْ یَّرْعَاہٗ حَقَّ رِعَایَتِہٖ وَیَدِیْنُ لَکَ بِاِعْتِقَادِ التَّسْلِیْمِ لِمُحْکَمِ اٰیَاتِہٖ وَیَفْزَعُ اِلَی الْاِقْرَارِ بِمُتَشَابِھِہٖ وَمُوْضِحَاتِ بَیِّنَاتِہٖ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ اِنَّکَ اَنْزَلْتَہٗ عَلٰی نَبِیِّکَ مُحَمَّدٍ صَلَّی اللہُ عَلَیْہِ وَاٰلِہٖ مُجْمَلًا وَّاَلْھَمْتَہٗ عِلْمَ عَجَآئِبِہٖ مُکَمَّلًا وَّوَرَّثْتَنَا عِلْمَہٗ مُفَسَّرًا وَّفَضَّلْتَنَا عَلٰی مَنْ جَھِلَ عِلْمَہٗ وَقَوَّیْتَنَا عَلَیْہِ لِتَرْفَعَنَا فَوْقَ مَنْ لَّمْ یُطِقْ حَمْلَہٗ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ نَکَمَا جَعَلْتَ قُلُوْبَنَا لَہٗ حَمَلَۃً وَّعَرَّفْتَنَا بِرَحْمَتِکَ شَرَفَہٗ وَفَضْلَہٗ فَصَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدِ نِالْخَطِیْبِ بِہٖ وَعَلٰی اٰلِہِ الْخُزَّانِ لَہٗ وَاجْعَلْنَا مِمَّنْ یَّعْتَرِفُ بِاَنَّہٗ مِنْ عِنْدِکَ حَتّٰی لَا یُعَارِضْنَا الشَّکُّ فِیْ تَصْدِیْقِہٖ وَلَا یَخْتَلِجَنَا الزَّیْغُ عَنْ قَصْدِ طَرِیْقِہٖ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَاٰلِہٖ وَاجْعَلْنَا مِمَّنْ یَّعْتَصِمُ بِحَبْلِہٖ وَیَاوِیْ مِنَ الْمُتَشَابِھَاتِ اِلٰی حِرْزِ مَعْقِلِہٖ وَیَسْکُنُ فِیْ ظِلِّ جَنَاحِہٖ وَیَھْتَدِیْ بِضَوْئِ صَبَاحِہٖ وَیَقْتَدِیْ بِتَبَلُّجِ اِسْفَارِہٖ وَیَسْتَصْبِحُ بِمِصْبَاحِہٖ وَلَا یَلْتَمِسُ الْھُدٰی فِیْ غَیْرِہٖ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ وَکَمَا نَصَبْتَ بِہٖ مُحَمَّدًا عَلَمًا لِّلدَّلَالَۃِ عَلَیْکَ وَاَنْھَجْتَ بِاٰلِہٖ سُبُلَ الرِّضَا اِلَیْکَ فَصَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِہٖ وَاجْعَلِ الْقُرْاٰنَ وَسِیْلَۃًلَّنَا اِلٰی اَشْرَفِ مَنَازِلِ الْکَرَامَۃِ وَسُلَّمًا نَّعْرُجُ فِیْہِ اِلٰی مَحَلِّ السَّلَامَۃِ وَسَبَبًا نُّجْزٰی بِہِ النَّجَاۃَ فِیْ عَرْصَۃِ الْقِیٰمَۃِ وَذَرِیْعَۃً نَقْدَمُ بِھَا عَلٰی نَعِیْمِ دَارِالْمُقَامَۃِ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمّدٍ وَّ اٰلِہٖ وَاحْطُطْ بِالْقُرْاٰنِ عَنَّا ثِقْلَ الْاَوْزَارِ وَھَبْ لَنَا حُسْنَ شَمَآئِلِ الْاَبْرَارِ وَاقْفُ بِنَا اٰثَارَ الَّذِیْنَ قَامُوْا لَکَ بِہٖ اٰنَآئَ اللَّیْلَ وَاَطْرَافَ النَّھَارَ حَتّٰی تُطَھِّرْنَا مِنْ کُلِّ دَنَسٍۢ بِتَطْھِیْرِہٖ وَتَقْفُوَبِنَا اٰثَارَالَّذِیْنَ اسْتَضَآؤُا بِنُوْرِہٖ وَلَمْ یُلْھِھِمِ الْاَمَلُ عَنِ الْعَمَلِ فَیَقْطَعَھُمْ بِخُدَعِ غُرُوْرِہٖ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمّدٍ وَّاٰلِہٖ وَاجْعَلِ الْقُرْاٰنَ لَنَا فِیْ ظُلْمِ اللَّیَالِیْ مُوْنِسًا وَّمِنْ نَزَغَاتِ الشَّیْطَانِ وَخَطَرَاتِ الْوَسَاوِسِ حَارِسًا وَّلِاَقْدَامِنَا عَنْ نَقْلِھِا اِلَی الْمُعَاصِیْ حَابِسًا وَّلِاَ لْسِنَتِنَا عَنِ الْخَوْضِ فِی الْبَاطِلِ مِنْ غَیْرِ مَا اٰفَۃٍ مُّخْرِسًا وَّلِجَوَارِحِنَا عَنِ اقْتَرَافِ الْاٰثَامِ زَاجِرًا وَّلِمَا طَوَتِ الْغَفْلَۃُ عَنَّا مِنْ تَصَفُّحِ الْاِعْتِبَارِنَاشِرًا حَتّٰی تُوْصِلَ اِلٰی قُلُوْبِنَا فَھُمْ عَجَآئِبِہٖ وَزَوَاجِرَ اَمْثَالِہِ الَّتِیْ ضَعْفَتِ الْجِبَالُ الرَّوَاسِیْ عَلٰی صَلَابَتِھَا عَنِ احْتِمَالِہٖ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمّدٍ وَّاٰلِہٖ وَاَدِمْ بِالْقُرْاٰنِ صَلَاحَ ظَاہِرِنَا وَاحْجُبْ بِہٖ خَطَرَاتِ الْوَسَاوِسِ عَنْ صِحَّۃِ ضَمَآئِرِنَا وَاغْسِلْ بِہٖ دَرَنَ قُلُوْبِنَا وَعَلَآئِقَ اَوْزَارِنَا وَاجْمَعْ بِہٖ مُنْتَشَرَ اُمُوْرِنَا وَاَرْوِبِہٖ فِیْ مَوْقِفِ الْعَرْضِ عَلَیْکَ ظَمَاھَوَاجِرِنَا وَاکْسُنَابِہٖ حُلَلَ الْاَمَانِ یَوْمَ الْفَزَعِ الْاَکْبَرِ فِیْ نُشُوْرِنَا۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمّدٍ وَّاٰلِہٖ وَاجْبُرْ بِالْقُرْاٰنِ خَلَّتَنَا مِنْ عَدَمِ الْاَمْلَاقِ وَسُقْ اِلَیْنَا بِہٖ رَغَدَالْعَیْشِ وَخِصْبَ سَعَۃِ الْاَرْزَاقِ وَجَنِّبْنَا بِہِ الضَّرَآئِبَ الْمَذْمُوْمَۃِ وَمَدَانِی الْاَخْلَاقِ وَاعْصِمْنَا بِہٖ مِنْ ھُوَّۃِ الْکُفْرِ وَدَوَاعِیَ النِّفَاقِ حَتّٰی یَکُوْنَ لَنَا فِی الْقِیٰمَۃِ اِلٰی رِضْوَانِکَ وَجِنَانِکَ قَآئِدًا وَّلَنَا فِی الدُّنْیَا عَنْ سُخْطَکَ وَتَعَدِّیْ حُدُوْدِکَ ذَآئِدًا وَّلَمَّا عِنْدَکَ بِتَحْلِیْلِ حَلَالِہٖ وَتَحْرِیْمِ حَرَامِہٖ شَاھِدًا۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمّدٍ وَّ اٰلِہٖ وَھَوِّنْ بِالْقُرْاٰنِ عِنْدَ الْمَوْتِ عَلٰی اَنْفُسِنَا کَرْبَ السِّیَاقِ وَجَھْدَ الْاَنِیْنِ وَتَرَادُفَ الْحَشَارِجِ اِذَا بَلَغَتِ النَّفُوْسُ التَّرَاقِیَ وَقِیْلَ مَنْ رَّاقٍ وَّتَجَلّٰی مَلَکُ الْمَوْتِ لِقَبْضِھَا مِنْ حُجُبِ الْغُیُوْبِ وَرَمَاھَا عَنْ قَوْسِ الْمَنَایَا بِاَسْہُمِ وَحْشَۃِ الْفِرَاقِ وَدَافَ لَھَا مِنْ ذُعَافِ الْمَوْتِ کَاسًا مَسْمُوْمَۃَ الْمَذَاقِ وَدَنَامِنَّا اِلَی الْاٰخِرَۃِ رَحِیْلٌ وَّانْطِلَاقٌ وَّصَارَتِ الْاَعْمَالُ قَلَآئِدَ فِی الْاَعْنَاقِ وَکَانَتِ الْقُبُوْرِ ھِیَ الْمَاوٰی اِلٰی مِیْقَاتِ یَوْمِ التَّلَاقِ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمّدٍ وَّاٰلِہٖ وَبَارِکْ لَنَا فِیْ حُلُوْلِ دَارِالْبَلٰی وَطُوْلِ الْمَقَامَۃِ بَیْنَ اِطْبَاقِ الثَّرٰی وَاجْعَلِ الْقُبُوْرَ بَعْدَفِرَاقِ الدُّنْیَا خَیْرَ مَنَازِلَنَا وَاَفْسَحْ لَنَا بِرَحْمَتِکَ فِیْ ضِیْقِ مَلَاحِدِنَا وَلَا تَفْضَحْنَا فِیْ حَاضِرِی الْقِیٰمَۃِ بِمُوْبِقَاتِ اٰثَامِنَا وَارْحَمْ بِالْقُراٰنِ فِیْ مَوْقِفِ الْعَرْضِ عَلَیْکَ ذُلَّ مَقَامِنَا وَثَبِّتْ بِہٖ عِنْدَ اضْطِرَابِ جِسْرِ جَھَنَّمَ یَوْمَ الْمَجَازِ عَلَیْھَا زَلَلَ اَقْدَامِنَا وَنَوِّرْبِہٖ قَبْلَ الْبَعْثِ سُدَفَ قُبُوْرِنَا وَنَجِّنَا بِہٖ مِنْ کُلِّ کَرْبٍ یَّوْمَ الْقِیٰمَۃِ وَشَدَآئِدِ اَھْوَالِ یَوْمِ الطَّامَّۃِ وَبَیِّضْ وُجُوْہَنَا یَوْمَ تَسْوَدُّ وُجُوْہُ الظَّلَمَۃِ فِیْ یَوْمِ الْحَسْرَۃِ وَالنِّدَامَۃِ وَاجْعَلْ لَنَا فِیْ صُدُوْرِ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنِ وُدًّا وَّلَا تَجْعَلِ الْحَیٰوۃَ عَلَیْنَا نَکَدًا۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمّدٍ عَبْدِکَ وَرَسُوْلِکَ کَمَابَلَّغَ رِسَالَتَکَ وَصَدَعَ بِاَمْرِکَ وَنَصَحَ لِعِبَادِکَ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ اجْعَلْ نَبِیِّنَا صَلَوَاتُکَ عَلَیْہِ وَعَلٰی اٰلِہٖ یَوْمَ الْقِیٰمَۃِ اَقْرَبَ النَّبِیِّیْنَ مِنْکَ مَجْلِسًا وَّاَمْکَنَھُمْ مِنْکَ شَفَاعَۃً وَّاَجَلَّھُمْ عِنْدَکَ قَدْرًا وَّاَوْجَھَھُمْ عِنْدَکَ جَاھًا۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمّدٍ وَّشَرِّفْ بُنْیَانَہٗ وَعَظِّمْ بُرْھَانَہٗ وَثَقِّلْ مِیْزَانَہٗ وَتَقَبَّلْ شَفَاعَتَہٗ وَقَرِّبْ وَسِیْلَتَہٗ وَبَیِّضْ وَجْھَہٗ وَاَتِمَّ نُوْرَہٗ وَارْفَعْ دَرَجَتَہٗ وَاَحْیِنَا عَلٰی سُنَّتِہٖ وَتَوَفَّنَا عَلٰی مِلَّتِہٖ وَخُذْبِنَا مِنْھَاجَہٗ وَاَسْئَلُکَ بِنَاسَبِیْلَہٗ وَاجْعَلْنَا مِنْ اَھْلِ طَاعَتِہٖ وَاحْشُرْنَا فِیْ زُمْرَتِہٖ وَاَوْرِدْنَا حَوْضَہٗ وَاسْقِنَا بِکَاسِہٖ۔

وَاَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمّدٍ وَّاٰلِہٖ صَلَوٰاۃً تُبَلِّغُہٗ بِھَا اَفْضَلَ مَا یَامُلُ مِنْ خَیْرِکَ وَفَضْلِکَ وَکَرَامَتِکَ اِنَّکَ ذُوْرَحْمَۃٍ وَّاسِعَۃٍ وَّفَضْلٍ کَرِیْمٍ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ اجْزِہٖ بِمَا بَلَّغَ مِنْ رِسَالَاتِکَ وَاَدّٰی مِنْ اٰیَاتِکَ وَنَصَحَ لِعِبَادِکَ وَجَاھَدَ فِیْ سَبِیْلِکَ اَفْضَلَ مَا جَزَیْتَ اَحَدًا مِّنْ مَلَآئِکَتِکَ الْمُقَرَّبِیْنَ وَاَنْبِیَآئِکَ الْمُرْسَلِیْنَ الْمُصْطَفَیْنَ وَالسَّلَامُ عَلَیْہِ وَعَلٰی اٰلِہِ الطَّیِّبِیْنَ الطَّاھِرِیْنَ وَرَحْمَۃُ اللہِ وَبَرَکَاتُہٗ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

KHATM-E-QUR’AN

Qur’an Majeed ke muqammal tilaawat ke baad yeh dua padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Baare-ilaaha! (ay Allah) Tu ne apni kitaab (ki tilaawat) ke khatm karne par meri madad farmaayi, woh kitaab jise Tu ne noor bana kar utaara. Aur tamaam aasmaani kitaabo par usey gawah banaya aur har us kalaam par jise Tu ne bayaan farmaya usey faasil (farq karne waala) qaraar diya jiske zariye halaal-o-haraam ko alag-alag kar diya, woh Qur’an jiske zariye shariyat ke ahkaam waazeh kiye, woh kitaab jise Tu ne apne bando ke liye sharha-o-tafseel (in detail) bayaan kiya aur woh wahi-e-aasmaani jise apne Paighambar (s.a.w.a) par naazil farmaya.

Jise woh noor banaya jiski pairawi se ham gumraahi-o-jehaalat ki taarikiyo mein hidaayat haasil kar sakte hai. Aur us shakhs ke liye usey shifa qaraar diya jo uspar e’teqaad (bharosa) rakhte huwe usey samajh-na chahe aur khaamoshi ke saath (sukoon se) usey soone, aur woh adl-o-insaaf ka taraazu banaya jiska kaanta haq se idhar-udhar nahi hota, aur woh noore hidaayat qaraar diya jiski daleel wa burhaan (proof) ki roshni (Tawheed wa Nabuwat ki) gawaahi dene waalo ke liye bujhti nahi.

Aur woh najaat ka nishaan banaya keh jo iske seedhe tareeqe par chalne ka iraada kare woh kabhi gumrah nahi hota. Aur jo iski rassi ke bandhan se waabasta ho woh (khauf-o-faqr aur azaab ki) halaakato ki dast-ras (pohonch) se baahar ho jaata hai.

Baare-ihaaha! (ay Allah) jab-keh Tu ne iski tilaawat ke silsile mein hamein madad pohonchayi, aur uske husne adaayegi (perfect pronounce) ke liye hamaari zabaan ki girhe (knot) khol di. To phir hamein un logo mein se qaraar de jo iski puri tarah se hifaazat wa nigheh-daasht (nighraani) karte ho. Aur uski mohkam aayato ke etraaf-o-tasleem ki pokhtagi (mazbooti) ke saath Teri ita’at karte ho. Aur mota-shaabeh (similar) aayato roshan wa waazeh daleelo ke iqraar ke saaye mein panah lete ho.

Ay Allah! Tu ne usey apne paighambar Mohammad-e-Mustafa (s.a.w.a) par ijmaal (ishaaratan) ke taur par utaara aur uske ajaayeb wa asraar (raazo) ka pura-pura ilm unhe ilqa (ataa) kiya, aur uske ilm-e-tafseeli ka hamein (Ayimma a.s ko) waaris qaraar diya, aur jo uska ilm nahi rakhte unpar hamein fazeelat di, aur uske moqtaziyaat (taqaaze) par amal karne ki quwwat bakhshi ta-keh jo uske haqaayeq ke mothammil (bearer) nahi ho sakte unpar hamaari fouqiyat (bade hone) wa bartari saabit kar de.

Ay Allah! Jis tarah Tu ne hamaare dilo ko Qur’an ka haamir (bearer) banaya aur apni rehmat se uske (Qur’an ke) fazl-o-sharaf se aagah kiya. You’n hi Mohammad (s.a.w.a) par jo Qur’an ke khutba-khwa (aayaato ki tafsilaat ko waazeh karne waale) aur unki Aal par jo Qur’an ke khazeena-daar hai (uloome Qur’an jin ke paas hai) unpar rehmat naazil farma, aur hamein un logo mein se qaraar de jo yeh iqraar karte hai keh yeh sab kuchh Teri jaanib se hai ta-keh uski tasdeeq (verification) mein hamein shak wa shubha (doubt) la-haq na ho. Aur uske seedhe raaste se ru-gardaani (deny) ka khayaal bhi na aane paaye.

Ay Allah! Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur hamein un logo mein se qaraar de jo unki rassi se waabast (thaame huwe) aur moshtabeh umoor (doubtful affairs) mein uski mohkam (mazboot) panahgah ka sahara lete hai. Aur uske paro (feathers) ke zere-saaya (saaye ke nichhe) manzil talaash karte hai, uski subh-e-darakhsha’n (sitaaro) ki roshni se hidaayat lete hai, aur uske noor ki roshni ki pairawi karte hai, aur uske chiraag se chiraag jalaate hai (uske bataaye aur dikhaaye huwe raaste par chalte hai), aur uske alawa kisi se hidaayat ke taalib nahi hote.

Baare-ilaaha! (ay Allah) jis tarah Tu ne is Qur’an ke zariye Mohammad wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) ko apni taraf rehnumaayi ka nishaan banaya (alaamat qaraar diya) hai aur unki Aal (a.s) ke zariye apni reza wa khushnoodi ki raahe (raaste) aashkaara (waazeh) ki hai you’n hi Mohammad wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur hamaare liye Qur’an ko izzat-o-buzurghi ki buland paaya (peak) manzilo tak pohonchne ka wasila aur salaamati ke maqaam tak buland ho ne ka zeena (stair) aur maidaane Hashr mein najaat ko jaza mein paane ka sabab (reason) aur Mahalle-Qeyaam (Jannat) ki nemato tak pohonchne ka zariya qaraar de.

Ay Allah! Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur Qur’an ke zariye gunaho ka bhaari bojh hamaare sar se utaar de. Aur neku-kaaro ke achchhe khasaayel (sifat) wa aadate (habits) hamein marhamat (ataa) farma. Aur un logo ke naqshe-qadam par chala jo Tere liye raat ke lamho aur subh-o-shaam ki saa’ato (waqto) mein usey (Qur’an ko) apna dastoorul-amal (life style) banaate hai ta-keh uski tatheer (pakeezgi) ke wasile se Tu hamein har aaloodgi (gunaho mein mulawwis hone) se paak kar de, aur un logo ke naqsh-e-qadam par chala jinho ne uske noor se roshni haasil ki hai aur ummeedo ne unhe amal se ghaafil nahi ho ne diya keh unhe apne qareeb ki ranginiya (chamk-dhamak) tabah kar de.

Ay Allah! Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur Qur’an ko raat ki taarikiyo (andhero) mein hamara munis (saathi) aur shaitaan ke mufseedo (fasaadi) aur dil mein guzarne waale was-waso se nigheh-baani karne aur hamaari qadmo ko na-farmaaniyo ki taraf badhne se rok dene waala, aur hamaari zabaano ko baatil kalaam (be-haq baato) se ba-ghair kisi maraz (bimaari) ke gungh (dumb) kar dene waala aur hamaare aaza (parts of body) ko irtekaab-e-gunah (gunah karne) se baaz rakhne waala (rokne waala) aur hamaari ghaflat wa madhoshi ne jis daftar-e-ibrat aur nasihat-aamez cheezo ko rok (stop) kar rakkha hai usey phaila ne waala qaraar de, ta-keh uske ajaayeb wa raazo ki haqeeqato aur uski muntabeh (alert) karne waali misaalo (examples) ko keh jinhe uthaane se pahaado (mountains) apne istehkaam (mazbooti) ke ba-wajood aajeez aa chuke hai hamaare dilo mein utaar de.

Ay Allah! Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur Qur’an ke zariye hamaare zaahir ko hamesha selaah (mashwarey) wa rushd (jaankaari) se araasta rakh aur hamaari zameer ki fitri salaamati se galat tasawwuraat ki dacha-andaazi (interference) ko rok de, aur hamaare dilo ki kasaafato (gandagiyo) aur gunaho ki aaludgiyo ko dho de, aur iske (Qur’an) ke zariye hamaare bhikhre umoor samet (jamaa kar) de, aur maidaan-e-Hashr mein hamaari jhulasti (jalti) huwi dhoop ki tapish wa tashnagi (pyaas) ko bujha de, aur sakht khauf wa hiraas (darr) ke din jab qabro se uthay to hamein amn-o-aafiyat ke jaame (libaas) pehna de.

Ay Allah! Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur Qur’an ke zariye faqr-o-faaqa aur ehtiyaaj (requirement) ki wajah se hamaari khastagi wa badhaali ka tadaaruk (bhar-paayi) farma. Aur zindagi ki kashayish aur feraagh-rozi (rozi mein wus’at) aur aasoodgi (khush-haali) ka rukh hamaare jaanib pher (mod) de. Aur buri aadaat (habits) aur past akhlaaq (bad character) se hamein dur kar de, aur kufr ke ga-dhe mein girne aur nefaaq-anghez (munaafiqat waali) cheezo se bacha le, ta-keh woh hamein qayamat mein Teri khushnoodi aur Jannat ki taraf badhaane waala aur duniya mein Teri naraazgi aur hudood shikani (limit cross) se rokne waala ho, aur is amr par gawah ho keh jo cheez Tere nazdeek halaal thi usey halaal jaana aur jo haraam thi usey haraam samjha.

Ay Allah! Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur is Qur’an ke wasile se maut ke waqt haalate nez’a ki aziyato, karaahne ki sakhtiyo aur jaan-kani ki lagataar hichkiyo ko (ruh nikalne ki takleef) ham par aasaan farma jab-keh jaan galey tak pohonch jaaye, aur kaha’n jaaye keh koi jhaad-phoonk (ilaaj) karne waala hai (jo takleef se kuchh najaat de) aur Malakul-Maut ghaib ke parde cheer kar qabze-ruh ke liye saamne aaye aur maut ki kamaan mein, feraaq (duniya se judaayi) ki dehshat (khauf) ke teer jod kar apne nishaana ki zad par rakh le, aur maut ke zaherile (poisonious) jaam mein zaher-e-halaahal (halaak kar dene waali zaher) ghol de, aur aakherat ki taraf hamara chal-chalaao aur safar qareeb ho jaaye, aur hamaare aamaal hamaari gardan ka tauq ban jaaye. Aur qabr roze hashr ki saa’at (ghadi) tak aaraam-gah qaraar paaye.

Ay Allah! Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur puraana wa boseeda ghar mein utaar-ne aur mitti ki taho mein (neeche) muddato pade rehne ko hamaare liye aasaan aur mubarak karna aur duniya se mooh mod-ne ke baad qabr ko hamara achchha ghar banana. Aur apni rehmat se hamaare liye qabr ki tanghi ko kushada kar dena aur hashr ke aam majme mein aur unke saamne hamaare mohlik gunaho ki wajah se hamein ruswa wa zaleel na karna, aur aamaal ke pesh ho ne ke maqaam par hamein zillat wa ruswaayi se bachana.

Us-waqt ham par rahem karna jis din Jahannam ke Pool par se guzarna hoga to uske ladkhada ne ke waqt hamaare dagmaagate huwe qadmo ko jama dena aur qayamat ke din hamein uske zariye (Qur’an ke zariye) har ranj-o-gham aur roze mahshar ki sakht haulnaakiyo (khauf-naak manzar) se najaat dena. Aur jab-keh hasrat-o-nedaamat ke din zaalimo ke chehre siyaah hoge hamaare chehro ko noorani karna, aur momineen ke dilo mein hamaari mohabbat paida kar de, aur zindagi ko hamaare liye dushwaar guzaar na bana.

Ay Allah! Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma keh unho-ne Tera paigaam pohonchaya, Teri shariyat ko waazeh taur par pesh kiya aur Tere bando ko waaz-o-naseehat ki. Ay Allah! hamaare Nabi (s.a.w.a) ko qayamat ke din tamaam nabiyo se (manzelat ke lehaaz se) moqarrab (qareeb-tar) aur shifa’at ke lehaaz se bartar, qadr-o-manzilat ke etebaar se buzurg-tar aur jaah-o-hasham ke etebaar se mumtaaz-tar (excellent) qaraar de.

Ay Allah! Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur unki izzat-o-sharaf ko buland farma. Unki daleel wa burhaan ko azeem aur unke mizaan-e-amal ko bhaari kar de, unki shifa’at qabool farma. Aur unki manzilat ko apne se qareeb kar. Unke chehre ko roshan, unke noor ko kaamil, aur unke daraje ko buland farma. Aur hamein unhi ke usoolo niza-e-hayaat par zinda rakh, aur unhi ke deen par maut de, aur unhi ki shah-e-raah (ravish-o-raah) par gaamzan kar (chala) aur unhi ke raaste par chalne ki taufeeq de, aur hamein unke farma-bardaaro mein se qaraar de, aur unke zumre mein mehshoor kar, aur unke Hawz-e-Kausar par utaar, aur unke sagar se seraab farma.

Ay Allah! Mohammad aur unki Aal (a.s) par aisi rehmat naazil farma jiske zariye unhe behtareen neki, fazl aur izzat tak pohoncha de jiske woh haq-daar hai. Is liye keh Tu wasee rehmat aur azeem fazl-o-ehsaan ka maalik hai, ay Allah unho-ne jo Tere paighamaat ki tableegh ki, Teri aayto ko pohonchaya, Tere bando ko waaz-o-naseehat ki aur Teri raah mein jehaad kiya. In sab ki unhe jaza de jo har us jaza se behtar ho jo Tu ne moqarrab (qareeb-tar) farishto aur barguzeeda mursal nabiyo ko ataa ki ho.

Un par aur unki paak-o-pakeeza Aal (a.s) par durood wa salaam ho aur Allah-taala ki rehmate aur barkate unke shaamil-e-haal ho.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

KHATM-E-QUR’AN

Upon Completing the Recitation of Qur’an recite this dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, certainly through Your help I have completed the recitation of Your book, You sent down as a light (elucidating wisdom). (It is) a corroboration and amelioration of that which had been revealed in the earlier scriptures, (but) it is superior in style and substance, therefore You preferred it over every narrative You have narrated; it teaches to distinguish between good and evil, bifurcating the lawful and the unlawful; (it is) a Quran, explaining and analysing the framework and substance of Your commands; (it is) a sacred document in the hands of Your servants, fully and thoroughly made clear in detail, (it is) a divine abiding revelation, communicated to Your prophet Muhammad (Your blessings be on him and on his children); (it is) a true basis of knowledge and education, by following which we steer clear of disorderly lawlessness and stupid ignorance; (it is) a remedy for those who after receiving the information, believe in its truthfulness through reason and understanding, and act upon it in letter and spirit; (it is) a demonstration of just rulers and methods, nothing comes out from it to depart from the truth; (it is) a source of decisive guidance, never grow dim or wither away its clear signs for those who look for guidance; (it is) a milestone showing the direction of salvation and everlasting happiness; whoso decides to pursue this route never goes astray, and whoso holds fast its clasping handhold never falls into the clutches of destructive calamities.

O Allah just as You helped us to attain proficiency in its recitation, and made the pronunciation easy for our tongues to manifest the beauty of its style, let us be among those who guard themselves against errors and abstain from that which has been declared unlawful in it, yield to You with positive acceptance of its decisive verses and make every effort to affirm and attend carefully to the consimilar therein as well as its clearly visible signs (decisive verses).

O Allah verily You revealed it in totality to Your prophet Muhammad, (blessings of Allah be on him and on his children), through divine communication laid bare every aspect of its extraordinary system of knowledge before him, relied upon us to explain and expound its knowledge, preferred us over those who are unaware of its meaningful wisdom enabled us to claim the right to defend it by placing us in the higher regions of learning over those who do not have the ability to shoulder this responsibility.

O Allah for the reason that You filled our hearts and minds with its import (meaning and tendency), and through Your mercy gave us a thorough awareness of its glory and completeness, we beseech You to send blessings on Muhammad, who preached its contents, and on his children who are its very active expert advocates, and let us be among those who know and make known that certainly it (the whole) is from You (their Lord), till there remains no distracting question whatsoever about its authenticity, and there is no hurdle or obstacle which could serve our march on the path shown therein.

O Allah send blessings on Muhammad and on his children, and let us be among those who hold fast to the cable (cord) of Allah, and take shelter under (refer to and rely upon) its decisive verses, based upon reason and logic to cope with its consimilar verses, stay for good under its extensive and streching out shade (dwell on and be engrossed in its reassuring invulnerability), find the right way in the twinkling of its awakening light; and proceed steadily in the same way, keeping track of its lit up campaign and exploration, light up the inner light and the power of vision by its enlightening bright source which brings to light every aspect of knowledge, and do not seek and inquire for guidance from anything other than this book.

O Allah because You set up Muhammad to stand upright, through Your book, as a decisive sign to point out convincingly unto You, and made accessible the road unto Your pleasure for only those who come through the path (medium) of his children, so then send blessings on Muhammad and on his children, and (for us) make the Qur’an a means to reach the heights of refinement and high-mindedness, a ladder to climb into the higher regions of blissful peace and harmony, a conviction to lie us up with ever-lasting happiness after we go through the ordeal of test and trial on the day of requital, a recommendation to advance our arrival in the eternal land of comfort and enjoyment.

O Allah send blessings on Muhammad and on his children, and, through the Quran, take off the load of troublesome burden which weigh heavy on our backs let us take to heart the refined natural disposition of those who, pleased and satisfied, did good in secret (even when alone, not to show off) and let us follow in their footsteps the tracks they kept to day in and day out, till we are cleansed of all (the dirt off) immorality through its orderliness, let us walk behind and follow the track of those who found the right path in the light of its guidance, and did not waste active energy by passing away time in whimsical day dreaming, lest its infatuation might drown them deep into nothingness.

O Allah send blessings on Muhammad and on his children, and let the Qur’an be our comforting escort in the darkness of the nights, because it will watch over us and ward off the mischief of Shaitan (the devil) and stop us from falling into the pitfalls of unscrupulous temptations, hold us back from taking steps into the regions of corruption, keep a rein on our tongue as if dump and mute (without a malady) at the time it rushes hastily to stir up falsehood, prevent our limbs, by giving a timely warning, from committing crimes make us see clearly that which carelessness folds up to obscure our analytical power of review, (to such an extent) that our hearts and minds get at the root of its extraordinary wisdom and pay careful attention to the warnings its similies and parables point out-which are so penetrating (sharp and astute) that even the strongest columns of towering mountains were too weak to carry.

O Allah send blessings on Muhammad and on his children, and make us follow the examples given in the Qur’an to do good in our day to day life, and by using it as a shield keep our conscience clear from the onslaught of the diabolical suggestions of the devil; wash clean our hearts to get rid of pollution and the coagulated blood of immorality, collect ourselves to regulate our wayward and wasteful mode of living; quench our thirst (find ourselves mercifully treated) when we will be presented before You (for accountability), alone and seperated, longing for (Your mercy); and at the time of resurrection (raising of the dead from the graves), a frightening day of terror, dress ourselves in the garments of peace (vie in the glory of divine “protection”.)

O Allah send blessings on Muhammad and on his children, and, through the Quran, cement the gaps (break of continuity) created in our lives due to lack of adequate means, bless us with everything that is good to live a pleasant life and make our efforts (for procuring the means of livelihood) fruitful, keep us aloof from contemptible mannerism and egotistic show-off, keep us safe from falling into the abyss of willful obstinate waywardness (kufr) and vicissitudes of hypocrisy, so that on the day of judgement it leads us on unto Your sanctuary, having obtained Your permission, (because) here, in this world, it keeps off Your displeasure by preventing us from overstepping the boundaries laid down by You, and see that we do as a duty only that which it has made lawful, and abstain from that which it has declared unlawful.

O Allah send blessings on Muhammad and on his children, and, through the Qur’an, make easy for us (to suffer) the agony of lying in the last gasps at the time of death, out of breath, the final assault of the death-rattle when the breath of life comes up to the throat and men say. “Who is the spellbinder?” (Then) the angel of death comes out from behind the unknown to take away the breath of life, shooting death-dealing arrows (striking the fatal blow) to throw (us) in the expanse of wild loneliness spraying into the air the bitter deadly poison (but) made palatable; (and thereafter) our departure, taking us nearer to the end, begins our deeds hanging around our necks all along the journey, lying still in the graves till the appointed day (of judgement) to meet (You) takes place.

O Allah send blessings on Muhammad and on his children, and let our underground sojourn (the long wait) deep inside the layers of the earth be a good omen for receiving Your blessings, let our stay in the graves, after leaving the world, be a comforting halt, make enough room for us inside the narrow walls of our buried abodes, do not disclose the infamy of our shameful guilt before the people on the day of judgement, in the name of the Qur’an have mercy on us when we, in a hopeless situation, present ourselves before You; help us to overcome the crisis we shall encounter while passing over the bridge, on the day of requital, without our steps faltering thereon to land us in hell, and light up our graves to remove darkness before we are brought to life again, save us from the agony of the day of resurrection and the acute anguish of the day of disaster; let it be a day of regretful anxiety and remorseful repentance for the oppressors, their faces sullied and tarnished, while our faces shinning bright, (and in this world) let there be love and tenderness in the hearts of the believers for us, (so that) our lives do not become hard and miserable.

O Allah send blessings on Muhammad, Your beloved servant and messenger, commensurate (equal in measure and extent) to that which he did to spread Your message, to carry out Your commands and to give correct advice to Your servants as a sincere friend.

O Allah let our Holy Prophet (Your blessings be on him and on his children) occupy the nearest possible nearness close to You in preference to all other prophets, on the day of judgement, and give them (Muhammad and Aale Muhammad) permission and influence to speak in favour of those whom they find fit for recommendation, hold them high in Your estimation as the worthiest and most substantial group, let them reach the zenith (highest point) of glory.

O Allah send blessings on Muhammad and on his children of Muhammad, (let) his foundation tower over and dominate, (let) his arguments make a deep and everlasting impression, (let) his judgement and conclusions rely upon reason and equity, (let) his intercession receive Your approval and sanction, (let) his influence gain Your closest attention, (let) his style and manners vie (shine bright) in grace and beauty, (let) his light light-up the whole universe, (let) his status reach the climax; and let us have a meaningful existence in the light of his ‘way of life” (sunnah), (till) we depart from here disciplined in his creed, trained in and well-versed with his ways and means.

I beseech You to make us follow his path, and let us be among those who obey him raise us on the day of resurrection in the group of his disciples, let us present ourselves to him at his cistern (pool of Kawthar) and drink until we are drunk. O Allah send blessings on Muhammad and on his children, in a way, they expect it to reach them through Your generosity, liberality and kindness. Verily You are the owner of boundless mercy and surpassing generosity.

O Allah compensate him for what he did to make known Your message, to convey Your signs (verses of the Quran), to give sound council to Your servants as a sincere friend, and to strive in Your cause leaving no stone unturned- (it should be) a compensation You have not allowed yet to any of Your favourite angels and prophets chosen as Your messengers, peace be on him and on his chaste and pure children, and also mercy and blessings of Allah be for them.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
