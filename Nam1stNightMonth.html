1st NIGHT OF MONTH

1st Night Namaz of each month…

Har mahine ki 1st Shab ki Namaz…

Recite 2 Rakat Namaz, in each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1), recite Surah An’aam (Chp.6) 1 time. One may then pray Almighty Allah to save him from any terror or pain and to make him live throughout that month peacefully and safely.

4) 2 Rakat namaz, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 1 martaba Surah An’aam (Chp.6)> padhe. Namaz ke baad Allah (s.w.t) se dua kare keh har aafat o dard se mehfooz rakkhe aur pura mahina sukoon aur salaamati se guzar jaaye.
