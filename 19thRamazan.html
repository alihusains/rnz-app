SHAB KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay woh jo har cheez se pehle maujood tha, phir har ek cheez ko paida kiya. Tu woh hai jo baaqi rahe-ga aur har cheez phana ho jaayegi, ay woh jiski maanind koi cheez nahi, ay woh jo na buland aasmaano mein hai aur na pistareen zameeno mein hai aur na unke upar aur na unke neeche hai aur na darmiyaan mein hai. Woh Mabood jiske siwa koi ma’bood nahi Tere liye hamd-o-sana hai, woh hamd keh koi usey shumaar (count) na kar sa-ke siwaaye Tere.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Aaj ke din is mah (month) ki barkato’n mein mera hissa badha de, uski bhalaaiyo ke liye mera raasta aasaan farma, aur meri nekiyo ki qubooliyat se mujhe mahroom na kar, Ay waazeh wo roshan haq ki taraf hidaayat karne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

NIGHT DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O One who was there before everything. Then He created everything. Then He will be there forever, whereas everything else will be destroyed. O one except whom there is no deity either in the high heavens or in the deep earths or upon them or in them or between them. Numerous praises are for You, whose counting only You can do and none else.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah multiply my share in this month on account of its abundant blessings, make easy and smooth my means and ways to reach its mercy and bounties, and do not prevent me from getting its good, O He who guides unto the clear truth.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
