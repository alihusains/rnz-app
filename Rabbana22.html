Chp. 7:Ver. 89
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَا افْتَحْ بَيْنَنَا وَبَيْنَ قَوْمِنَا بِالْحَقِّ وَاَنْتَ خَيْرُ الْفٰتِحِيْنَ۔
Our Lord! Decide between us and our people with truth; and Thou art the best of deciders.

Khudaaya! Tu hamare aur hamari qaum ke darmiyaan haq se faisla farma de keh Tu behtareen faisla karne waala hai.
