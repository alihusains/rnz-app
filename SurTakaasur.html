Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Tumhe baahami muqaable kasrat-e-maal-o-aulaad ne ghaafil bana diya.

2. Yahan tak keh tum ne qabro se mulaqaat kar-li.

3. Dekho tumhe an-qareeb maaloom ho jaayega.

4. Aur phir khoob maaloom ho jaayega.

5. Dekho agar tumhe yaqeeni ilm ho jaata.

6. Keh tum Jahannam ko zaroor dekhoge.

7. Phir usey apni aankho dekhe yaqeen ki tarah dekhoge.

8. Aur phir tum se us din nemat ke baare mein sawaal kiya jaayega.

102 | TAKAASUR | 8

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Abundance diverts you,

2. Until you come to the graves.

3. Nay! you shall soon know,

4. Nay! Nay! you shall soon know.

5. Nay! if you had known with a certain knowledge,

6. You should most certainly have seen the hell;

7. Then you shall most certainly see it with the eye of certainty;

8. Then on that day you shall most certainly be questioned about the boons.
