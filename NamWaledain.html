HADIYA-E-WALEDAIN

Gift to your parents

You can gift this 2 rakat namaz to your parents (Alive or Dead). In 1st rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite the below dua 10 times…
Yeh 2 rakat namaz apne waledain (Zinda ya Marhoom) ke liye hadiya kar sakte hai. 1st Rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad yeh dua 10 martaba padhe…

رَبِّ اغْفِرْلِىْ وَلِوَالِدَىَّ وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِيْنَ يَوْمَ يَقُوْمُ الْحِسَابُ۔

In 2nd rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite the below dua 10 times…
2nd Rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad yeh dua 10 martaba padhe.

رَبِّ اغْفِرْلِىْ وَلِوَالِدَىَّ وَلِمَنْ دَخَلَ بَيْتِىَ مُؤْمِنًا وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِيْنَ وَالْمُؤْمِنَاتِ۔

After completing namaz recite the following zikr 10 times…
Baad az namaz yeh zikr 10 martaba padhe.

رَبِّ ارْحَمْهُمَا كَمَا رَبَّيَانِىْ صَغِيْرًا۔
