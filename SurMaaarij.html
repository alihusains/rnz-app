Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Ek maangne waale ne waaqe hone waale azaab ka sawaal kiya.

2. Jiska kaafiro ke haq mein koi dafa karne waala nahi hai.

3. Yeh bulandiyo waale Khuda ki taraf se hai.

4. Jiski taraf farishte aur roohul-ameen buland hote hai Us ek din mein jiski miqdaar pachaas hazaar saal ke baraabar hai.

5. Lehaaza aap behtareen sabr se kaam le.

6. Yeh log usey dur samajh rahe hai.

7. Aur Ham usey qareeb hi dekh rahe hai.

8. Jis din aasmaan pigle huwe tambe (liquid coper) ke maanind ho jaayega.

9. Aur pahaad dhake huwe oon (covered cotton) jaise.

10. Aur koi hamdard kisi hamdard ka pursaane haal na hoga.

11. Woh sab ek dusre ko dikh laaye jaayege to mujreem chahega keh kaash aaj ke din ke azaab ke badle uski aulaad ko le liya jaaye.

12. Aur biwi aur bhai ko.

13. Aur us kunbe (family) ko jis mein woh rehta tha.

14. Aur ruye-zameen ki saari makhluqaat ko aur usey najaat de di jaaye.

15. Harghiz nahi yeh aatish-e-Jahannam hai.

16. Khaal utaar dene waali.

17. Un sab ko aawaaz de rahi hai jo mooh pher kar jaane waale thay.

18. Aur jinho ne maal jama kar ke bandh kar rakkha tha.

19. Be-shak insaan bada laalchi hai.

20. Jab takleef pohonch jaati hai to faryaadi ban jaata hai.

21. Aur jab maal mil jaata hai to bakheel (kanjoos) ho jaata hai.

22. Alaawa un namaaziyo ke.

23. Jo apni namaazo ki pa-bandi karne waale hai.

24. Aur jin ke amwaal mein ek muqarrara haq mo’ayyan hai.

25. Maangne waale ke liye aur na-maangne waale ke liye.

26. Aur jo log roz-e-qayamat ki tasdeeq (confirmation) karne waale hai.

27. Aur jo apne Parwardigaar ke azaab se darr ne waale hai.

28. Be-shak azaab-e-Parwardigaar be khauf rehne waali cheez nahi hai.

29. Aur jo apni sharam-gaaho ki hifaazat karne waale hai.

30. Alaawa apni biwiyo aur kanizo ke, keh is par malaamat nahi ki jaati hai.

31. Phir jo iske alaawa ka khawaahish-mand ho woh hadd se guzar jaane waala hai.

32. Aur jo apni amaanato aur ahed ka khayaal rakhne waale hai.

33. Aur jo apni gawaahiyo par qaayem rehne waale hai.

34. Aur jo apni namaazo ka khayaal rakhne waale hai.

35. Yahi log jannat mein ba-izzat tareeqe se rehne waale hai.

36. Phir un kaafiro ko kya ho gaya hai keh aap ki taraf bhaaghe chale aa rahe hai.

37. Daahe-baaye se giroh-dar-giroh.

38. Kya un mein se har ek ki tama (khwaahish) yeh hai keh usey jannatun-naeem mein daakhil kar diya jaaye.

39. Hargiz nahi inhe to maaloom hai keh Ham ne inhe kis cheez se paiyda kiya hai.

40. Mai tamaam mashriq-o-maghrib ke Parwardigaar ki qasam kha kar kehta hu keh Ham qudrat rakhne waale hai.

41. Is baat par keh unke badle un se behtar afraad le aaye aur Ham aajiz nahi hai.

42. Lehaaza inhe chhod dijiye yeh apne baatil mein doobhe rahe aur khel-tamaasha karte rahe yahan tak keh us din se mulaqaat kare jiska waada kiya gaya hai.

43. Jis din yeh sab qabro se tezi ke saath niklege jis tarah kisi parcham ki taraf bhaaghe ja rahe ho.

44. Unki nighahe juki hogi aur zillat un par chhai hogi aur yahi woh din hoga jiska un se waada kiya gaya hai.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. One demanding, demanded the chastisement which must befall

2. The unbelievers— there is none to avert it—

3. From Allah, the Lord of the ways of Ascent.

4. To Him ascend the angels and the Spirit in a day the measure of which is fifty thousand years.

5. Therefore endure with a goodly patience.

6. Surely they think it to be far off,

7. And We see it nigh.

8. On the day when the heaven shall be as molten copper

9. And the mountains shall be as tufts of wool

10. And friend shall not ask of friend

11. (Though) they shall be made to see each other. The guilty one would fain redeem himself from the chastisement of that day by (sacrificing) his children,

12. And his wife and his brother

13. And the nearest of his kinsfolk who gave him shelter,

14. And all those that are in the earth, (wishing) then (that) this might deliver him.

15. By no means! Surely it is a flaming fire

16. Dragging by the head,

17. It shall claim him who turned and fled (from truth),

18. And amasses (wealth) then shuts it up.

19. Surely man is created of a hasty temperament;

20. Being greatly grieved when evil afflicts him,

21. And miserly when good befalls him,

22. Except those who pray,

23. Those who are constant at their prayer,

24. And those in whose wealth there is a fixed portion.

25. For him who begs and for him who is denied (good),

26. And those who accept the truth of the judgment day,

27. And those who are fearful of the chastisement of their Lord—

28. Surely the chastisement of their Lord is (a thing) not to be felt secure of—

29. And those who guard their private parts,

30. Except in the case of their wives or those whom their right hands possess— for these surely are not to be blamed,

31. But he who seeks to go beyond this, these it is that go beyond the limits—

32. And those who are faithful to their trusts and their covenant

33. And those who are upright in their testimonies,

34. And those who keep a guard on their prayer,

35. Those shall be in gardens, honored.

36. But what is the matter with those who disbelieve that they hasten on around you,

37. On the right hand and on the left, in sundry parties?

38. Does every man of them desire that he should be made to enter the garden of bliss?

39. By no means! Surely We have created them of what they know.

40. But nay! I swear by the Lord of the Easts and the Wests that We are certainly able

41. To bring instead (others) better than them, and We shall not be overcome.

42. Therefore leave them alone to go on with the false discourses and to sport until they come face to face with that day of theirs with which they are threatened;

43. The day on which they shall come forth from their graves in haste, as if they were hastening on to a goal,

44. Their eyes cast down; disgrace shall overtake them; that is the day which they were threatened with.
