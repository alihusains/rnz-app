MAAREFAT

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰھُمَّ عَرِّفْنِیْ نَفْسَکَ‘ فَاِنَّاکَ اِنْ لَّمْ تُعَرِّ فْنِیْ نَفْسَکَ لَمْ اَعْرَفْ نَبِیَّکَ۔ اَللّٰھُمَّ عَرِّفْنِیْ نَبِیَّکَ‘ فَاِنَّاکَ اِنْ لَّمْ تُعَرِّ فْنِیْ نَبِیَّکَ لَمْ اَعْرَ فْ حُجَّتَکَ۔ اَللّٰھُمَّ عَرِّفْنِیْ حُجَّتَکَ فَاِنَّاکَ اِنْ لَمْ تُعَرِّ فْنِیْ حُجَّتَکَ‘ ضَلَلْتُ عَنْ دِیْنِیْ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

MAAREFAT

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Ma’bood! Tu mujhe apni maarefat ataa farma, pas (isme koi shak nahi keh) agar Tu ne mujhe apni maarefat na di to mai Tere Rasool (s.a.w.a) ko nahi pehchaan.

Ay Ma’bood! Mujhe apne Rasool (s.a.w.a) ki maarefat ataa farma, pas (isme koi shak nahi keh) agar Tu ne mujhe apne Rasool (s.a.w.a) ki maarefat ataa na ki to mai Tere Hujjat ko nahi pehchaan pawoonga.

Ay Ma’bood! Mujhe apne Hujjat ki maarefat ataa farma, pas be-shak agar Tu ne mujhe apne Hujjat ki maarefat na karaayi to mai apne deen se bhatak (dur ho) jaaunga.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

MAAREFAT

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah introduce Yourself to me For, if you do not introduce Yourself to me, I will not recognize Your Prophet (s.a.w.a).

O Allah make me recognize Your Prophet (s.a.w.a) because if I do not recognize Your Prophet (s.a.w.a), I will not recognize Your Proof [Imam (as)].

O Allah make me recognize Your Proof [Imam (a.s)] because if you do not make me recognize Your Proof, I will deviate from my religion.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
