Chp. 2:Ver. 286
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَا وَلَا تُحَمِّلْنَا مَا لَا طَاقَةَ لَنَا بِهٖج وَاعْفُ عَنَّاوقفۃ وَاغْفِرْ لَنَاوقفۃ وَارْحَمْنَاوقفۃ اَنْتَ مَوْلٰنَا فَانْصُرْنَا عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْكٰفِرِيْنَ۔
Our Lord do not impose upon us that which we have not the strength to bear; and pardon us and grant us protection and have mercy on us, Thou art our Patron, so help us against the unbelieving people.

Parwardigaar! Ham par woh baar na daalna jiski ham mein taaqat na ho. Hamein maaf kar dena, hamein bakhsh dena, ham par rahem karna Tu hamara maula aur maalik hai ab kaafiro’n ke muqaable mein hamari madad farma.
