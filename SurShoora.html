بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

حٰمۗﯜ1ﯛج عۗسۗقۗﯜ2ﯛ كَذٰلِكَ يُوْحِيْٓ اِلَيْكَ وَاِلَى الَّذِيْنَ مِنْ قَبْلِكَلا اللهُ الْعَزِيْزُ الْحَكِيْمُﯜ3ﯛ لَهٗ مَا فِي السَّمٰوٰتِ وَمَا فِي الْاَرْضِط وَهُوَ الْعَلِيُّ الْعَظِيْمُﯜ4ﯛ تَكَادُ السَّمٰوٰتُ يَتَفَطَّرْنَ مِنْ فَوْقِهِنَّ وَالْمَلٰۗئِكَةُ يُسَبِّحُوْنَ بِحَمْدِ رَبِّهِمْ وَيَسْتَغْفِرُوْنَ لِمَنْ فِي الْاَرْضِط اَلَٓا اِنَّ اللهَ هُوَ الْغَفُوْرُ الرَّحِيْمُﯜ5ﯛ وَالَّذِيْنَ اتَّخَذُوْا مِنْ دُوْنِهٖٓ اَوْلِيَاۗءَ اللهُ حَفِيْظٌ عَلَيْهِمْ ڮ وَمَآ اَنْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ بِوَكِيْلٍﯜ6ﯛ وَكَذٰلِكَ اَوْحَيْنَآ اِلَيْكَ قُرْاٰنًا عَرَبِيًّا لِّتُنْذِرَ اُمَّ الْقُرٰى وَمَنْ حَوْلَهَا وَتُنْذِرَ يَوْمَ الْجَمْعِ لَا رَيْبَ فِيْهِط فَرِيْقٌ فِي الْجَنَّةِ وَفَرِيْقٌ فِي السَّعِيْرِﯜ7ﯛ وَلَوْ شَاۗءَ اللهُ لَجَعَلَهُمْ اُمَّةً وَّاحِدَةً وَّلٰكِنْ يُّدْخِلُ مَنْ يَّشَاۗءُ فِيْ رَحْمَتِهٖط وَالظّٰلِمُوْنَ مَا لَهُمْ مِّنْ وَّلِيٍّ وَّلَا نَصِيْرٍﯜ8ﯛ اَمِ اتَّخَذُوْا مِنْ دُوْنِهٖٓ اَوْلِيَاۗءَج فَاللهُ هُوَ الْوَلِيُّ وَهُوَ يُحْيِ الْمَوْتٰىز وَهُوَ عَلٰى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيْرٌﯜ9ﯛع وَمَا اخْتَلَفْتُمْ فِيْهِ مِنْ شَيْءٍ فَحُكْمُهٗٓ اِلَى اللهِط ذٰلِكُمُ اللهُ رَبِّيْ عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ وَاِلَيْهِ اُنِيْبُﯜ10ﯛ فَاطِرُ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضِط جَعَلَ لَكُمْ مِّنْ اَنْفُسِكُمْ اَزْوَاجًا وَّمِنَ الْاَنْعَامِ اَزْوَاجًاج يَذْرَؤُكُمْ فِيْهِط لَيْسَ كَمِثْلِهٖ شَيْءٌج وَهُوَ السَّمِيْعُ الْبَصِيْرُﯜ11ﯛ لَهٗ مَقَالِيْدُ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضِج يَبْسُطُ الرِّزْقَ لِمَنْ يَّشَاۗءُ وَيَقْدِرُط اِنَّهٗ بِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ عَلِيْمٌﯜ12ﯛ شَرَعَ لَكُمْ مِّنَ الدِّيْنِ مَا وَصّٰى بِهٖ نُوْحًا وَّالَّذِيْٓ اَوْحَيْنَآ اِلَيْكَ وَمَا وَصَّيْنَا بِهٖٓ اِبْرٰهِيْمَ وَمُوْسٰى وَعِيْسٰٓى اَنْ اَقِيْمُوا الدِّيْنَ وَلَا تَتَفَرَّقُوْا فِيْهِط كَبُرَ عَلَى الْمُشْرِكِيْنَ مَا تَدْعُوْهُمْ اِلَيْهِط اَللهُ يَجْتَبِيْٓ اِلَيْهِ مَنْ يَّشَاۗءُ وَيَهْدِيْٓ اِلَيْهِ مَنْ يُّنِيْبُﯜ13ﯛط وَمَا تَفَرَّقُوْٓا اِلَّا مِنْۢ بَعْدِ مَا جَاۗءَهُمُ الْعِلْمُ بَغْيًۢا بَيْنَهُمْط وَلَوْلَا كَلِمَةٌ سَبَقَتْ مِنْ رَّبِّكَ اِلٰٓى اَجَلٍ مُّسَمًّى لَّقُضِيَ بَيْنَهُمْط وَاِنَّ الَّذِيْنَ اُوْرِثُوا الْكِتٰبَ مِنْۢ بَعْدِهِمْ لَفِيْ شَكٍّ مِّنْهُ مُرِيْبٍﯜ14ﯛ فَلِذٰلِكَ فَادْعُج وَاسْتَقِمْ كَمَآ اُمِرْتَج وَلَا تَتَّبِعْ اَهْوَاۗءَهُمْج وَقُلْ اٰمَنْتُ بِمَآ اَنْزَلَ اللهُ مِنْ كِتٰبٍج وَاُمِرْتُ لِاَعْدِلَ بَيْنَكُمُط اَللهُ رَبُّنَا وَرَبُّكُمْط لَنَآ اَعْمَالُنَا وَلَكُمْ اَعْمَالُكُمْط لَا حُجَّةَ بَيْنَنَا وَبَيْنَكُمُط اَللهُ يَجْمَعُ بَيْنَنَاج وَاِلَيْهِ الْمَصِيْرُﯜ15ﯛ وَالَّذِيْنَ يُحَاۗجُّوْنَ فِي اللهِ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ مَا اسْتُجِيْبَ لَهٗ حُجَّتُهُمْ دَاحِضَةٌ عِنْدَ رَبِّهِمْ وَعَلَيْهِمْ غَضَبٌ وَّلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ شَدِيْدٌﯜ16ﯛ اَللهُ الَّذِيْٓ اَنْزَلَ الْكِتٰبَ بِالْحَقِّ وَالْمِيْزَانَط وَمَا يُدْرِيْكَ لَعَلَّ السَّاعَةَ قَرِيْبٌﯜ17ﯛ يَسْتَعْجِلُ بِهَا الَّذِيْنَ لَا يُؤْمِنُوْنَ بِهَاج وَالَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا مُشْفِقُوْنَ مِنْهَالا وَيَعْلَمُوْنَ اَنَّهَا الْحَقُّط اَلَٓا اِنَّ الَّذِيْنَ يُمَارُوْنَ فِي السَّاعَةِ لَفِيْ ضَلٰلٍۢ بَعِيْدٍﯜ18ﯛ اَللهُ لَطِيْفٌۢ بِعِبَادِهٖ يَرْزُقُ مَنْ يَّشَاۗءُج وَهُوَ الْقَوِيُّ الْعَزِيْزُﯜ19ﯛ مَنْ كَانَ يُرِيْدُ حَرْثَ الْاٰخِرَةِ نَزِدْ لَهٗ فِيْ حَرْثِهٖج وَمَنْ كَانَ يُرِيْدُ حَرْثَ الدُّنْيَا نُؤْتِهِ مِنْهَالا وَمَا لَهٗ فِي الْاٰخِرَةِ مِنْ نَّصِيْبٍﯜ20ﯛ اَمْ لَهُمْ شُرَكٰۗؤُ شَرَعُوْا لَهُمْ مِّنَ الدِّيْنِ مَا لَمْ يَاْذَنْۢ بِهِ اللهُط وَلَوْلَا كَلِمَةُ الْفَصْلِ لَقُضِيَ بَيْنَهُمْط وَاِنَّ الظّٰلِمِيْنَ لَهُمْ عَذَابٌ اَلِيْمٌﯜ21ﯛ تَرَى الظّٰلِمِيْنَ مُشْفِقِيْنَ مِمَّا كَسَبُوْا وَهُوَ وَاقِعٌۢ بِهِمْط وَالَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا وَعَمِلُوا الصّٰلِحٰتِ فِيْ رَوْضٰتِ الْجَنّٰتِج لَهُمْ مَّا يَشَاۗءُوْنَ عِنْدَ رَبِّهِمْط ذٰلِكَ هُوَ الْفَضْلُ الْكَبِيْرُﯜ22ﯛ ذٰلِكَ الَّذِيْ يُبَشِّرُ اللهُ عِبَادَهُ الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا وَعَمِلُوا الصّٰلِحٰتِط قُلْ لَّا اَسْئَلُكُمْ عَلَيْهِ اَجْرًا اِلَّا الْمَوَدَّةَ فِي الْقُرْبٰىط وَمَنْ يَّقْتَرِفْ حَسَنَةً نَّزِدْ لَهٗ فِيْهَا حُسْنًاط اِنَّ اللهَ غَفُوْرٌ شَكُوْرٌﯜ23ﯛ اَمْ يَقُوْلُوْنَ افْتَرٰى عَلَى اللهِ كَذِبًاج فَاِنْ يَّشَاِ اللهُ يَخْتِمْ عَلٰى قَلْبِكَط وَيَمْحُ اللهُ الْبَاطِلَ وَيُحِقُّ الْحَقَّ بِكَلِمٰتِهٖط اِنَّهٗ عَلِيْمٌۢ بِذَاتِ الصُّدُوْرِﯜ24ﯛ وَهُوَ الَّذِيْ يَقْبَلُ التَّوْبَةَ عَنْ عِبَادِهٖ وَيَعْفُوْ عَنِ السَّيِّاٰتِ وَيَعْلَمُ مَا تَفْعَلُوْنَﯜ25ﯛلا وَيَسْتَجِيْبُ الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا وَعَمِلُوا الصّٰلِحٰتِ وَيَزِيْدُهُمْ مِّنْ فَضْلِهٖط وَالْكٰفِرُوْنَ لَهُمْ عَذَابٌ شَدِيْدٌﯜ26ﯛ وَلَوْ بَسَطَ اللهُ الرِّزْقَ لِعِبَادِهٖ لَبَغَوْا فِي الْاَرْضِ وَلٰكِنْ يُّنَزِّلُ بِقَدَرٍ مَّا يَشَاۗءُط اِنَّهٗ بِعِبَادِهٖ خَبِيْرٌۢ بَصِيْرٌﯜ27ﯛ وَهُوَ الَّذِيْ يُنَزِّلُ الْغَيْثَ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ مَا قَنَطُوْا وَيَنْشُرُ رَحْمَتَهٗط وَهُوَ الْوَلِيُّ الْحَمِيْدُﯜ28ﯛ وَمِنْ اٰيٰتِهٖ خَلْقُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْاَرْضِ وَمَا بَثَّ فِيْهِمَا مِنْ دَاۗبَّةٍط وَهُوَ عَلٰى جَمْعِهِمْ اِذَا يَشَاۗءُ قَدِيْرٌﯜ29ﯛع وَمَآ اَصَابَكُمْ مِّنْ مُّصِيْبَةٍ فَبِمَا كَسَبَتْ اَيْدِيْكُمْ وَيَعْفُوْ عَنْ كَثِيْرٍﯜ30ﯛط وَمَآ اَنْتُمْ بِمُعْجِزِيْنَ فِي الْاَرْضِج وَمَا لَكُمْ مِّنْ دُوْنِ اللهِ مِنْ وَّلِيٍّ وَّلَا نَصِيْرٍﯜ31ﯛ وَمِنْ اٰيٰتِهِ الْجَوَارِ فِي الْبَحْرِ كَالْاَعْلَامِﯜ32ﯛ اِنْ يَّشَاْ يُسْكِنِ الرِّيْحَ فَيَظْلَلْنَ رَوَاكِدَ عَلٰى ظَهْرِهٖط اِنَّ فِيْ ذٰلِكَ لَاٰيٰتٍ لِّكُلِّ صَبَّارٍ شَكُوْرٍﯜ33ﯛ اَوْ يُوْبِقْهُنَّ بِمَا كَسَبُوْا وَيَعْفُ عَنْ كَثِيْرٍﯜ34ﯛلا وَّيَعْلَمَ الَّذِيْنَ يُجَادِلُوْنَ فِيْٓ اٰيٰتِنَاط مَا لَهُمْ مِّنْ مَّحِيْصٍﯜ35ﯛ فَمَآ اُوْتِيْتُمْ مِّنْ شَيْءٍ فَمَتَاعُ الْحَيٰوةِ الدُّنْيَاج وَمَا عِنْدَ اللهِ خَيْرٌ وَّاَبْقٰى لِلَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا وَعَلٰى رَبِّهِمْ يَتَوَكَّلُوْنَﯜ36ﯛج وَالَّذِيْنَ يَجْتَنِبُوْنَ كَبٰۗئِرَ الْاِثْمِ وَالْفَوَاحِشَ وَاِذَا مَا غَضِبُوْا هُمْ يَغْفِرُوْنَﯜ37ﯛج وَالَّذِيْنَ اسْتَجَابُوْا لِرَبِّهِمْ وَاَقَامُوا الصَّلٰوةَص وَاَمْرُهُمْ شُوْرٰى بَيْنَهُمْص وَمِمَّا رَزَقْنٰهُمْ يُنْفِقُوْنَﯜ38ﯛج وَالَّذِيْنَ اِذَآ اَصَابَهُمُ الْبَغْيُ هُمْ يَنْتَصِرُوْنَﯜ39ﯛ وَجَزٰۗؤُ سَيِّئَةٍ سَيِّئَةٌ مِّثْلُهَاج فَمَنْ عَفَا وَاَصْلَحَ فَاَجْرُهٗ عَلَى اللهِط اِنَّهٗ لَا يُحِبُّ الظّٰلِمِيْنَﯜ40ﯛ وَلَمَنِ انْتَصَرَ بَعْدَ ظُلْمِهٖ فَاُوْلٰۗئِكَ مَا عَلَيْهِمْ مِّنْ سَبِيْلٍﯜ41ﯛ اِنَّمَا السَّبِيْلُ عَلَى الَّذِيْنَ يَظْلِمُوْنَ النَّاسَ وَيَبْغُوْنَ فِي الْاَرْضِ بِغَيْرِ الْحَقِّط اُوْلٰۗئِكَ لَهُمْ عَذَابٌ اَلِيْمٌﯜ42ﯛ وَلَمَنْ صَبَرَ وَغَفَرَ اِنَّ ذٰلِكَ لَمِنْ عَزْمِ الْاُمُوْرِﯜ43ﯛع وَمَنْ يُّضْلِلِ اللهُ فَمَا لَهٗ مِنْ وَّلِيٍّ مِّنْۢ بَعْدِهٖط وَتَرَى الظّٰلِمِيْنَ لَمَّا رَاَوُا الْعَذَابَ يَقُوْلُوْنَ هَلْ اِلٰى مَرَدٍّ مِّنْ سَبِيْلٍﯜ44ﯛج وَتَرٰهُمْ يُعْرَضُوْنَ عَلَيْهَا خٰشِعِيْنَ مِنَ الذُّلِّ يَنْظُرُوْنَ مِنْ طَرْفٍ خَفِيٍّط وَقَالَ الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْٓا اِنَّ الْخٰسِرِيْنَ الَّذِيْنَ خَسِرُوْٓا اَنْفُسَهُمْ وَاَهْلِيْهِمْ يَوْمَ الْقِيٰمَةِط اَلَٓا اِنَّ الظّٰلِمِيْنَ فِيْ عَذَابٍ مُّقِيْمٍﯜ45ﯛ وَمَا كَانَ لَهُمْ مِّنْ اَوْلِيَاۗءَ يَنْصُرُوْنَهُمْ مِّنْ دُوْنِ اللهِط وَمَنْ يُّضْلِلِ اللهُ فَمَا لَهٗ مِنْ سَبِيْلٍﯜ46ﯛط Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Ha meem.

2. Ayn sin qaaf.

3. Isi tarah Khudaaye aziz-o-hakeem aap ki taraf aur aap se pehle waalo ki taraf wahi (revelation) bhejta rehta hai.

4. Zameen-o-aasmaan mein jo kuch bhi hai sab usi ki milkiyat hai aur wohi Khudaaye buzurag-o-bartar hai.

5. Qareeb hai keh aasmaan uski haibat se upar se shigaafta ho jaaye (phat jaaye) aur malaayeka bhi apne Parwardigaar ki hamd ki tasbeeh kar rahe hai aur zameen waalo ke haq mein isteghfaar kar rahe hai aagah ho jaawo keh Khuda hi woh hai jo bohot bakhshne waala aur maher-baan hai.

6. Aur jin logo ne uske alaawa sarparast bana liye hai Allah un sab ke haalaat ka nigra hai aur Paighambar aap kisi ke zimme-daar nahi hai.

7. Aur Ham ne isi tarah aap ki taraf arabi zabaan mein Qur’an ki wahi (revelation) bheji ta-keh aap Macca aur uske atraaf waalo ko daraaiye aur us din se daraaiye jis din sab ko jama kiya jaayega aur us mein kisi shak ki gunjaaish nahi hai. Us din ek giroh jannat mein hoga aur ek Jahannam mein hoga.

8. Aur agar Khuda chahta to sab ko ek qaum bana deta lekin woh jise chahta hai usi ko apni rehmat mein daakhil karta hai aur zaalemeen ke liye na koi sarparast hai aur na madad-gaar.

9. Kya un logo ne us ko chhod kar apne liye sarparast banaaye hai jab-keh Wohi sab ka sarparast hai aur Wohi murdo ko zinda karta hai aur Woh har shaiy par qudrat rakhne waala hai.

10. Aur tum jis cheez mein bhi ikhtelaaf karoge uska faisla Allah ke haatho mein hai. Wohi mera Parwardigaar hai aur Usi par mera bharosa hai aur Usi ki taraf mai rujoo karta hu.

11. Woh aasmaano aur zameen ka paiyda karne waala hai us ne tumhaare nufoos mein se bhi joda (pair) banaya hai aur jaanwaro mein se bhi jode (pair) banaaye hai Woh tum ko usi jode (pair) ke zariye duniya mein phaila raha hai. Uske jaisa koi nahi hai woh sab ki soonne waala aur har cheez ka dekhne waala hai.

12. Aasmaan-o-zameen ki tamaam kunjiya (keys) usi ke ikhtiyaar mein hai Woh jiske liye chahta hai uske rizq mein wus’at paiyda kar deta hai aur jiske liye chahta hai tanghi paiyda kar deta hai Woh har shaiy ka khoob jaanne waala hai.

13. Us ne tumhaare liye deen mein woh raasta muqarrar kiya hai jiski nasihat Nooh ko ki hai aur jiski wahi (revelation) Paighambar tumhaari taraf bhi ki hai aur jiski nasihat Ibrahim alayhis salaam, Moosa alayhis salaam aur Isa alayhis salaam ko bhi ki hai. Keh deen ko qaayem karo aur is mein tafarqa (dispute) na paiyda hone paaye mushreqeen ko woh baat sakht-gira guzarti hai jiski tum unhe daawat de rahe ho Allah jis ko chahta hai apni baargah ke liye chunn leta hai aur jo Uski taraf rujoo karta hai usey hidaayat de deta hai.

14. Aur un logo ne aapas mein tafarqa (dispute) usi waqt paiyda kiya hai jab unke paas ilm aa chuka tha aur yeh sirf aapas ki dushmani ki bina par tha aur agar Parwardigaar ki taraf se ek baat pehle se ek muddat ke liye tai na ho gayi hoti to ab tak unke darmiyaan faisla ho chuka hota aur be-shak jin logo ko unke baad kitaab ka waaris banaya gaya hai woh uski taraf se sakht shak mein pade huwe hai.

15. Lehaaza aap usi ke liye daawat de aur is tarah isteqaamat se kaam le jis tarah aap ko hukm diya gaya hai aur unki khwahishaat ka itteba na kare aur yeh kahe keh mera imaan is kitaab par hai jo Khuda ne naazil ki hai aur mujhe hukm diya gaya hai keh tumhaare darmiyaan insaaf karu Allah hamara aur tumhaara dono ka Parwardigaar hai hamare aamaal hamare liye hai aur tumhaare aamaal tumhaare liye hamare aur tumhaare darmiyaan koi bahes (arguement) nahi hai Allah ham sab ko ek din jama karega aur usi ki taraf sab ki baaz-gasht hai.

16. Aur jo log uske maan liye jaane ke baad Khuda ke baare mein jaghda karte hai unki daleel bilkul mohmal (faaltu) aur lagw (bekaar) hai aur un par Allah ka ghazab hai aur unke liye shadeed qisam ka azaab hai.

17. Allah hi woh hai jisne haq ke saath kitaab aur mizaan ko naazil kiya hai aur aap ko kya maaloom keh shaayad qayamat qareeb hi ho.

18. Uski jaldi sirf woh log karte hai jin ka us par imaan nahi hai. Warna jo imaan waale hai woh to us se khauf-zadah hi rehte hai aur yeh jaante hai keh woh baher-haal bar-haq hai. Hoshiyaar! Keh jo log qayamat ke baare mein shak karte hai woh gumraahi mein bohot dur tak chale gaye hai.

19. Allah apne bando par maher-baan hai woh jis ko bhi chahta hai rizq ataa karta hai aur woh saheb-e-quwwat bhi hai aur saheb-e-izzat bhi hai.

20. Jo insaan aakherat ki kheti chahta hai Ham uske liye izaafah kar dete hai aur jo duniya ki kheti ka talab-gaar hai usey isi mein se ataa kar dete hai aur phir aakherat mein uska koi hissa nahi hai.

21. Kya unke liye aise shorka bhi hai jinho ne deen ke woh raaste muqarrar kiye hai jin ki Khuda ne ijaazat bhi nahi di hai aur agar faisle ke din ka waada na ho gaya hota to ab tak unke darmiyaan faisla kar diya gaya hota aur yaqeenan zaalimo ke liye bada dard-naak azaab hai.

22. Aap dekhenge keh zaalemeen apne kartoot ke azaab se khauf-zadah hai aur woh un par baher-haal naazil hone waala hai aur jo log imaan le aaye aur unho ne nek aamaal kiye hai woh jannat ke baaghaat mein rahege aur unke liye Parwardigaar ki baargah mein woh tamaam cheeze hai jin ke woh khawaahish-mand hoge aur yeh ek bohot bada fazl-e-Parwardigaar hai.

23. Yahi woh fazl-e-azeem hai jiski bashaarat Parwardigaar apne bando ko deta hai jinho ne imaan ikhtiyaar kiya hai aur nek aamaal kiye hai to aap kahe dijiye keh mai tum se is tableegh-e-risaalat ka koi ajr nahi chahta alaawa uske, keh mere aqraba (karaabat-daaro) se mohabbat karo aur jo shakhs bhi koi neki haasil karega Ham uski neki mein izaafah kar dege keh be-shak Allah bohot zeyada bakhshne waala aur qadr-daan hai.

24. Kya un logo ka kehna yeh hai keh Rasool, Allah par joothay bohtaan baandh-ta hai jab-keh Khuda chahe to tumhaare qalb par bhi mohar laga sakta hai aur Khuda to baatil ko mita deta hai aur haq ko apne kalemaat ke zariye saabit aur paayedaar bana deta hai. Yaqeenan woh dilo ke raazo ka jaanne waala hai.

25. Aur wohi woh hai jo apne bando ki tauba ko qabool karta hai aur unki buraaiyo ko maaf karta hai aur woh tumhaare aamaal se khoob ba-khabar hai.

26. Aur jo log imaan laaye aur unho ne nek aamaal kiye wohi daawat-e-ilaahi ko qabool karte hai aur Khuda apne fazl-o-karam se unke ajr mein izaafah kar deta hai aur kaafereen ke liye to bada sakht azaab rakkha gaya hai.

27. Aur agar Khuda tamaam bando ke liye rizq ko wasee kar deta hai to yeh log zameen mein bagaawat kar dete magar woh apni mashiyat ke mutaabiq moayyana miqdaar mein naazil karta hai keh woh apne bando se khoob ba-khabar hai aur unke haalaat ka dekhne waala hai.

28. Wohi Woh hai jo unke maayoos ho jaane ke baad baarish ko naazil karta hai aur apni rehmat ko muntashir karta hai aur Wohi qaabil-e-taareef, maalik aur sarparast hai.

29. Aur Uski nishaaniya mein se zameen-o-aasmaan ki khilqat aur unke andar chalne waale tamaam jaan-daar hai aur woh jab chahe un sab ko jama kar lene par qudrat rakhne waala hai.

30. Aur tum tak jo museebat bhi pohonchti hai woh tumhaare haatho ki kamaai hai aur woh bohot si baato ko maaf bhi kar deta hai.

31. Aur tum zameen mein Khuda ko aajiz nahi kar sakte ho aur tumhaare liye uske alaawa koi sarparast aur madad-gaar bhi nahi hai.

32. Aur uski nishaaniya mein se samandar mein chalne waale baad-baani jahaaz bhi hai jo pahaad ki tarah buland hai.

33. Woh agar chahe to hawa ko saakin (khaamosh) bana de to sab satha-e-aab (surface) par jam kar rahe jaaye be-shak is haqeeqat mein sabr-o-shukr karne waalo ke liye bohot si nishaaniya paayi jaati hai.

34. Ya Woh unhe unke aamaal ki binapar halaak hi kar de aur Woh bohot si baato ko maaf bhi kar deta hai.

35. Aur hamari aayato mein jaghda karne waalo ko maaloom hojaana chaahiye keh unke liye koi chhut-kaara nahi hai.

36. Pas tum ko jo kuch bhi diya gaya hai woh zindagaani-e-duniya ka chain (sukoon) hai aur bas aur jo kuch Allah ki baargah mein hai woh khair aur baqi rehne waala hai un logo ke liye jo imaan rakhte hai aur apne Parwardigaar par bharosa karte hai.

37. Aur bade-bade gunaho aur fohash (abuse) baato se parhez karte hai aur jab gussa aa jaata hai to maaf kar dete hai.

38. Aur jo apne rab ki baat ko qabool karte hai aur namaaz qaayem karte hai aur aapas ke mamlaat mein mashwara karte hai aur hamare rizq mein se hamari raah mein kharch karte hai.

39. Aur jb un par koi zulm hota hai to uska badla le lete hai.

40. Aur har buraayi ka badla uske jaisa hota hai phir jo maaf kar de aur islaah kar de uska ajr Allah ke zimme hai woh yaqeenan zaalimo ko dost nahi rakhta hai.

41. Aur jo shakhs bhi zulm ke baad badla le uske upar koi ilzaam nahi hai.

42. Ilzaam un logo par hai jo logo par zulm karte hai aur zameen mein na-haq zeyadatiya phaila-te hai un hi logo ke liye dard-naak azaab hai.

43. Aur yaqeenan jo sabr kare aur maaf kar de to uska yeh amal bade housle ka kaam hai.

44. Aur jis ko Khuda gumraahi mein chhod de uske liye uske baad koi waali aur sarparast nahi hai aur tum dekhoge keh zaalemeen azaab ko dekhne ke baad yeh Kahenge keh kya wapsi ka koi raasta nikal sakta hai.

45. Aur tum unhe dekhoge keh woh Jahannam ke saamne pesh kiye jaayege to zillat se unke sar jhuke huwe hoge aur woh kin aankhiyo se dekhte bhi jaayege aur sahebaan-e-imaan Kahenge keh ghaate waale wohi afraad hai jinho ne apne nafs aur apne ahel ko qayamat ke din ghaate mein mubtala kar diya hai. Aagah ho jaao! Keh zaalimo ko baher-haal daayemi azaab mein rehna padega.

46. Aur unke liye aise sarparast bhi na hoge jo Khuda se hatt kar unki madad kar sakay aur jis ko Khuda gumraahi mein chhod de uske liye koi raasta nahi hai.

47. Tum log apne Parwardigaar ki baat ko qabool karo qabl iske keh woh din aa jaaye jo palat ne waala nahi hai us din tumhaare liye koi panah-gaah bhi na hogi aur azaab ka inkaar karne ki himmat bhi na hogi.

48. Ab bhi agar yeh log etaraaz kare to Ham ne aap ko unka nigheh-baan bana kar nahi bheja hai aap ka farz sirf paighaam pohoncha dena tha aur bas. Aur Ham jab kisi insaan ko rehmat ka maza chakhaate hai to woh akad jaata hai aur jab uske aamaal hi ke nateeje mein koi buraayi pohonch jaati hai to bohot zeyada na-shukri karne waala ban jaata hai.

49. Be-shak aasmaan-o-zameen ka ikhtiyaar sirf Allah ke haatho mein hai Woh jo kuch chahta hai paiyda karta hai jis ko chahta hai betiya ataa karta hai aur jis ko chahta hai bete ataa karta hai.

50. Ya phir bete aur betiya dono ko jama kar deta hai aur jis ko chahta hai baanjh (janm na de sakne wala) hi bana deta hai yaqeenan woh saheb-e-ilm bhi hai aur saheb-e-qudrat-o-ikhtiyaar bhi hai.

51. Aur kisi insaan ke liye yeh baat nahi hai keh Allah us se kalaam kare magar yeh keh wahi (revelation) kar de ya pas-e-parda (behind the curtain)se baat kar le ya koi numainda farishta bhej de aur phir woh uski ijaazat se jo woh chahta hai woh paighaam pohonchade keh woh yaqeenan buland-o-baala aur saheb-e-hikmat hai.

52. Aur isi tarah Ham ne aap ki taraf apne hukm se rooh (qur’an) ki wahi (revelation) ki hai aap ko nahi maaloom tha keh kitaab kya hai aur imaan kin cheezo ka naam hai. Lekin Ham ne usey ek noor qaraar diya hai jiske zariye apne bando mein jise chahte hai usey hidaayat de dete hai aur be-shak aap logo ko seedhe raaste ki hidaayat kar rahe hai.

53. Us Khuda ka raasta jiske ikhtiyaar mein zameen-o-aasmaan ki tamaam cheeze hai aur yaqeenan usi ki taraf tamaam umoor ki baaz-gasht hai.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Ha Mim.

2. Ain Sin Qaf.

3. Thus does Allah, the Mighty, the Wise, reveal to you, and (thus He revealed) to those before you.

4. His is what is in the heavens and what is in the earth, and He is the High, the Great.

5. The heavens may almost rend asunder from above them and the angels sing the praise of their Lord and ask forgiveness for those on earth; now surely Allah is the Forgiving, the Merciful.

6. And (as for) those who take guardians besides Him, Allah watches over them, and you have not charge over them.

7. And thus have We revealed to you an Arabic Quran, that you may warn the mother city and those around it, and that you may give warning of the day of gathering together wherein is no doubt; a party shall be in the garden and (another) party in the burning fire.

8. And if Allah had pleased He would surely have made them a single community, but He makes whom He pleases enter into His mercy, and the unjust it is that shall have no guardian or helper.

9. Or have they taken guardians besides Him? But Allah is the Guardian, and He gives life to the dead, and He has power over all things.

10. And in whatever thing you disagree, the judgment thereof is (in) Allah’s (hand); that is Allah, my Lord, on Him do I rely and to Him do I turn time after time.

11. The Originator of the heavens and the earth; He made mates for you from among yourselves, and mates of the cattle too, multiplying you thereby; nothing like a likeness of Him; and He is the Hearing, the Seeing.

12. His are the treasures of the heavens and the earth; He makes ample and straitens the means of subsistence for whom He pleases; surely He is Cognizant of all things.

13. He has made plain to you of the religion what He enjoined upon Nuh and that which We have revealed to you and that which We enjoined upon Ibrahim and Musa and Isa that keep to obedience and be not divided therein; hard to the unbelievers is that which you call them to; Allah chooses for Himself whom He pleases, and guides to Himself him who turns (to Him), frequently.

14. And they did not become divided until after knowledge had come to them out of envy among themselves; and had not a word gone forth from your Lord till an appointed term, certainly judgment would have been given between them; and those who were made to inherit the Book after them are most surely in disquieting doubt concerning it.

15. To this then go on inviting, and go on steadfastly on the right way as you are commanded, and do not follow their low desires, and say: I believe in what Allah has revealed of the Book, and I am commanded to do justice between you: Allah is our Lord and your Lord; we shall have our deeds and you shall have your deeds; no plea need there be (now) between us and you: Allah will gather us together, and to Him is the return.

16. And (as for) those who dispute about Allah after that obedience has been rendered to Him, their plea is null with their Lord, and upon them is wrath, and for them is severe punishment.

17. Allah it is Who revealed the Book with truth, and the balance, and what shall make you know that haply the hour be nigh?

18. Those who do not believe in it would hasten it on, and those who believe are in fear from it, and they know that it is the truth. Now most surely those who dispute obstinately concerning the hour are in a great error.

19. Allah is Benignant to His servants; He gives sustenance to whom He pleases, and He is the Strong, the Mighty.

20. Whoever desires the gain of the hereafter, We will give him more of that again; and whoever desires the gain of this world, We give him of it, and in the hereafter he has no portion.

21. Or have they associates who have prescribed for them any religion that Allah does not sanction? And were it not for the word of judgment, decision would have certainly been given between them; and surely the unjust shall have a painful punishment.

22. You will see the unjust fearing on account of what they have earned, and it must befall them; and those who believe and do good shall be in the meadows of the gardens; they shall have what they please with their Lord: that is the great grace.

23. That is of which Allah gives the good news to His servants, (to) those who believe and do good deeds. Say: I do not ask of you any reward for it but love for my near relatives; and whoever earns good, We give him more of good therein; surely Allah is Forgiving, Grateful.

24. Or do they say: He has forged a lie against Allah? But if Allah pleased, He would seal your heart; and Allah will blot out the falsehood and confirm the truth with His words; surely He is Cognizant of what is in the breasts.

25. And He it is Who accepts repentance from His servants and pardons the evil deeds and He knows what you do;

26. And He answers those who believe and do good deeds, and gives them more out of His grace; and (as for) the unbelievers, they shall have a severe punishment.

27. And if Allah should amplify the provision for His servants they would certainly revolt in the earth; but He sends it down according to a measure as He pleases; surely He is Aware of, Seeing, His servants.

28. And He it is Who sends down the rain after they have despaired, and He unfolds His mercy; and He is the Guardian, the Praised One.

29. And one of His signs is the creation of the heavens and the earth and what He has spread forth in both of them of living beings; and when He pleases He is all-powerful to gather them together.

30. And whatever affliction befalls you, it is on account of what your hands have wrought, and (yet) He pardons most (of your faults).

31. And you cannot escape in the earth, and you shall not have a guardian or a helper besides Allah.

32. And among His signs are the ships in the sea like mountains.

33. If He pleases, He causes the wind to become still so that they lie motionless on its back; most surely there are signs in this for every patient, grateful one,

34. Or He may make them founder for what they have earned, and (even then) pardon most;

35. And (that) those who dispute about Our communications may know; there is no place of refuge for them.

36. So whatever thing you are given, that is only a provision of this world’s life, and what is with Allah is better and more lasting for those who believe and rely on their Lord.

37. And those who shun the great sins and indecencies, and whenever they are angry they forgive.

38. And those who respond to their Lord and keep up prayer, and their rule is to take counsel among themselves, and who spend out of what We have given them.

39. And those who, when great wrong afflicts them, defend themselves.

40. And the recompense of evil is punishment like it, but whoever forgives and amends, he shall have his reward from Allah; surely He does not love the unjust.

41. And whoever defends himself after his being oppressed, these it is against whom there is no way (to blame).

42. The way (to blame) is only against those who oppress men and revolt in the earth unjustly; these shall have a painful punishment.

43. And whoever is patient and forgiving, these most surely are actions due to courage.

44. And whomsoever Allah makes err, he has no guardian after Him; and you shall see the unjust, when they see the punishment, saying: Is there any way to return?

45. And you shall see them brought before it humbling themselves because of the abasements, looking with a faint glance. And those who believe shall say: Surely the losers are they who have lost themselves and their followers on the resurrection day. Now surely the iniquitous shall remain in lasting chastisement.

46. And they shall have no friends to help them besides Allah; and whomsoever Allah makes err, he shall have no way.

47. Hearken to your Lord before there comes the day from Allah for which there shall be no averting; you shall have no refuge on that day, nor shall it be yours to make a denial.

48. But if they turn aside, We have not sent you as a watcher over them; on you is only to deliver (the message); and surely when We make man taste mercy from Us, he rejoices thereat; and if an evil afflicts them on account of what their hands have already done, then surely man is ungrateful.

49. Allah’s is the kingdom of the heavens and the earth; He creates what He pleases; He grants to whom He pleases daughters and grants to whom He pleases sons.

50. Or He makes them of both sorts, male and female; and He makes whom He pleases barren; surely He is the Knowing, the Powerful.

51. And it is not for any mortal that Allah should speak to him except by revelation or from behind a veil, or by sending a messenger and revealing by His permission what He pleases, Surely He is High, wise.

52. And thus did We reveal to you an inspired book by Our command. You did not know what the Book was, nor (what) the faith (was), but We made it a light, guiding thereby whom We please of Our servants; and most surely you show the way to the right path:

53. The path of Allah, Whose is whatsoever is in the heavens and whatsoever is in the earth; now surely to Allah do all affairs eventually come.
