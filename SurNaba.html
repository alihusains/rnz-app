Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Yeh log aapas mein kis cheez ke baare mein sawaal kar rahe hai.

2. Bohat badi khabar ke baare mein.

3. Jiske baare mein un mein ikhtelaaf hai.

4. Kuch nahi an-qareeb inhe maaloom ho jaayega.

5. Aur khoob maaloom ho jaayega.

6. Kya Ham ne zameen ka farsh nahi banaya hai.

7. Aur pahaado ki meekhe (khile/nails) nahi nasb ki hai.

8. Aur Ham hi ne tum ko joda banaya hai.

9. Aur tumhaari neend ko aaraam ka saamaan qaraar diya hai.

10. Aur raat ko parda-posh banaya hai.

11. Aur din ko waqt-e-ma’aash (roz-gaar) qaraar diya hai.

12. Aur tumhaare saro par saat mazboot aasmaan banaaye hai.

13. Aur ek bhadakta huwa chiraag banaya hai.

14. Aur baadalo mein se musla-dhaar paani barsaaya hai.

15. Ta-keh uske zariye daane aur ghaas baraamad kare.

16. Aur ghane-ghane baaghaat paiyda kare.

17. Be-shak faisle ka din mo’ayyan hai.

18. Jis din soor phoonka jaayega aur tum sab fauj-dar-fauj aawoge.

19. Aur aasmaan ke raaste khol diye jaayege aur darwaaze ban jaayege.

20. Aur pahaado ko jagah se harkat de di jaayega aur woh ret (sand) jaise ho jaayege.

21. Be-shak Jahannam unki ghaat (intezaar) mein hai.

22. Woh sarkasho ka aakhri thikaana hai.

23. Is mein woh muddato rahege.

24. Na thandak ka maza chakh sakayge aur na kisi peene ki cheez ka.

25. Alaawa kholte (garam) paani aur peep ke.

26. Yeh unke aamaal ka mukammal badla hai.

27. Yeh log hisaab-o-kitaab ki umeed hi nahi rakhte thay.

28. Aur unho ne hamari aayaat ki ba-qaaeda takzeeb ki hai.

29. Aur Ham ne har shaiy ko apni kitaab mein jama kar liya hai.

30. Ab tum apne aamaal ka maza chakkho aur Ham azaab ke alaawa koi izaafah nahi kar sakte.

31. Be-shak sahebaan-e-taqwa ke liye kaam-yaabi ki manzil hai.

32. Baaghaat hai aur angoor.

33. Nokhaiz doshizaaye (kuwari) hai aur sab hamsin (same age).

34. Aur chhalakte huwe paimaane.

35. Wahan na koi lagw baat soonege na gunah.

36. Yeh tumhaare Rab ki taraf se hisaab ki huwi ataa hai aur tumhaare aamaal ki jaza.

37. Woh aasmaan-o-zameen aur unke maa-bain (darmiyaan) ka Parwardigaar Rehmaan hai jiske saamne kisi ko baat karne ka yaara (taaqat) nahi hai.

38. Jis din Roohul quds aur malaayeka saf-basta (row) khade hoge aur koi baat bhi na kar sakega alaawa uske jise Rehmaan ijaazat de-de aur theek-theek baat kare.

39. Yahi bar-haq din hai to jiska jee chahe apne Rab ki taraf thikaana bana le.

40. Ham ne tum ko ek qareebi azaab se daraaya hai jis din insaan apne kiye dhare ko dekhega aur kaafir kahega keh ay kaash! Mai khaak ho gaya hota.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Of what do they ask one another?

2. About the great event,

3. About which they differ?

4. Nay! they shall soon come to know.

5. Nay! Nay! they shall soon know.

6. Have We not made the earth an even expanse?

7. And the mountains as projections (thereon)?

8. And We created you in pairs,

9. And We made your sleep to be rest (to you),

10. And We made the night to be a covering,

11. And We made the day for seeking livelihood.

12. And We made above you seven strong ones,

13. And We made a shining lamp,

14. And We send down from the clouds water pouring forth abundantly,

15. That We may bring forth thereby corn and herbs,

16. And gardens dense and luxuriant.

17. Surely the day of decision is (a day) appointed:

18. The day on which the trumpet shall be blown so you shall come forth in hosts,

19. And the heaven shall be opened so that it shall be all openings,

20. And the mountains shall be moved off so that they shall remain a mere semblance.

21. Surely hell lies in wait,

22. A place of resort for the inordinate,

23. Living therein for ages.

24. They shall not taste therein cool nor drink

25. But boiling and intensely cold water,

26. Requital corresponding.

27. Surely they feared not the account,

28. And called Our communications a lie, giving the lie (to the truth).

29. And We have recorded everything in a book,

30. So taste! for We will not add to you aught but chastisement.

31. Surely for those who guard (against evil) is achievement,

32. Gardens and vineyards,

33. And those showing freshness of youth, equals in age,

34. And a pure cup.

35. They shall not hear therein any vain words nor lying.

36. A reward from your Lord, a gift according to a reckoning:

37. The Lord of the heavens and the earth and what is between them, the Beneficent God, they shall not be able to address Him.

38. The day on which the spirit and the angels shall stand in ranks; they shall not speak except he whom the Beneficent God permits and who speaks the right thing.

39. That is the sure day, so whoever desires may take refuge with his Lord.

40. Surely We have warned you of a chastisement near at hand: the day when man shall see what his two hands have sent before, and the unbeliever shall say: O! would that I were dust!
