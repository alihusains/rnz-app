Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Jab chaadar aftaab ko lapet diya jaayega.

2. Jab taare gir padege.

3. Jab pahaad harkat mein aa jaayege.

4. Jab an-qareeb janne (janam dene) waali oontniya moattal (bekaar) kar di jaayegi.

5. Jab jaanwaro ko ekhatta kiya jaayega.

6. Jab dariya bhadak uthayge.

7. Jab rooho ko jismo se jood diya jaayega. (7)

8. Aur jab zinda dar-gowr (dafanaayi) ladkiyo ke baare mein sawaal kiya jaayega.

9. Keh unhe kis gunah mein maara gaya hai.

10. Aur jab naama-e-aamaal muntashir kar diye jaayege.

11. Aur jab aasmaan ka chhilka (cover) utaar diya jaayega.

12. Aur jab Jahannam ki aag bhadka di jaayegi.

13. Aur jab jannat qareeb-tar kar di jaayegi.

14. Tab har nafs ko maaloom hoga keh us ne kya haazir kiya hai.

15. To Mai un sitaro ki qasam khaata hu jo palat jaane waale hai.

16. Chalne waale aur chhup jaane waale hai.

17. Aur raat ki qasam jab khatm hone ko aaye.

18. Aur subah ki qasam jab saans lene lagey.

19. Be-shak yeh ek moazziz farishte ka bayaan hai.

20. Woh saheb-e-quwwat hai aur saheb-e-arsh ki baargah ka makeen (rehne wala) hai.

21. Woh wahan qaabile ita’at aur phir amaanat-daar hai.

22. Aur tumhaara saathi Paighambar diwaana nahi hai.

23. Aur us ne farishte ko buland ufuq (aasmaan) par dekha hai.

24. Aur woh ghaib ke baare mein bakheel nahi hai.

25. Aur yeh Qur’an kisi shaitaan-e-rajim ka qaul nahi hai.

26. To tum kidhar chale ja rahe ho.

27. Yeh sirf aalameen ke liye ek nasihat ka saamaan hai.

28. Jo tum mein se seedha hona chahe.

29. Aur tum log kuch nahi chaah sakte magar yeh keh aalameen ka Parwardigaar Khuda chahe.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. When the sun is covered,

2. And when the stars darken,

3. And when the mountains are made to pass away,

4. And when the camels are left untended,

5. And when the wild animals are made to go forth,

6. And when the seas are set on fire,

7. And when souls are united,

8. And when the female infant buried alive is asked

9. For what sin she was killed,

10. And when the books are spread,

11. And when the heaven has its covering removed,

12. And when the hell is kindled up,

13. And when the garden is brought nigh,

14. Every soul shall (then) know what it has prepared.

15. But nay! I swear by the stars,

16. That run their course (and) hide themselves,

17. And the night when it departs,

18. And the morning when it brightens,

19. Most surely it is the Word of an honored messenger,

20. The processor of strength, having an honorable place with the Lord of the Dominion,

21. One (to be) obeyed, and faithful in trust.

22. And your companion is not gone mad.

23. And of a truth he saw himself on the clear horizon.

24. Nor of the unseen is he a tenacious concealer.

25. Nor is it the word of the cursed Shaitan,

26. Whither then will you go?

27. It is naught but a reminder for the nations,

28. For him among you who pleases to go straight.

29. And you do not please except that Allah please, the Lord of the worlds.
