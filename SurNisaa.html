بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

يٰٓاَيُّهَا النَّاسُ اتَّقُوْا رَبَّكُمُ الَّذِيْ خَلَقَكُمْ مِّنْ نَّفْسٍ وَّاحِدَةٍ وَّخَلَقَ مِنْهَا زَوْجَهَا وَبَثَّ مِنْهُمَا رِجَالًا كَثِيْرًا وَّنِسَآءًج وَاتَّقُوا اللهَ الَّذِيْ تَسَآءَلُوْنَ بِهٖ وَالْاَرْحَامَط اِنَّ اللهَ كَانَ عَلَيْكُمْ رَقِيْبًاﯜ1ﯛ وَاٰتُوا الْيَتٰمٰٓى اَمْوَالَهُمْ وَلَا تَتَبَدَّلُوا الْخَبِيْثَ بِالطَّيِّبِص وَلَا تَاْكُلُوْٓا اَمْوَالَهُمْ اِلٰى اَمْوَالِكُمْط اِنَّهٗ كَانَ حُوْبًا كَبِيْرًاﯜ2ﯛ وَاِنْ خِفْتُمْ اَلَّا تُقْسِطُوْا فِي الْيَتٰمٰى فَانْكِحُوْا مَا طَابَ لَكُمْ مِّنَ النِّسَآءِ مَثْنٰى وَثُلٰثَ وَرُبٰعَج فَاِنْ خِفْتُمْ اَلَّا تَعْدِلُوْا فَوَاحِدَةً اَوْ مَا مَلَكَتْ اَيْمَانُكُمْط ذٰلِكَ اَدْنٰٓى اَلَّا تَعُوْلُوْاﯜ3ﯛ وَاٰتُوا النِّسَآءَ صَدُقٰتِهِنَّ نِحْلَةًط فَاِنْ طِبْنَ لَكُمْ عَنْ شَيْءٍ مِّنْهُ نَفْسًا فَكُلُوْهُ هَنِٓيْئًا مَّرِٓيْئًاﯜ4ﯛ وَلَا تُؤْتُوا السُّفَهَآءَ اَمْوَالَكُمُ الَّتِيْ جَعَلَ اللهُ لَكُمْ قِيٰمًا وَّارْزُقُوْهُمْ فِيْهَا وَاكْسُوْهُمْ وَقُوْلُوْا لَهُمْ قَوْلًا مَّعْرُوْفًاﯜ5ﯛ وَابْتَلُوا الْيَتٰمٰى حَتّٰٓى اِذَا بَلَغُوا النِّكَاحَج فَاِنْ اٰنَسْتُمْ مِّنْهُمْ رُشْدًا فَادْفَعُوْٓا اِلَيْهِمْ اَمْوَالَهُمْج وَلَا تَاْكُلُوْهَآ اِسْرَافًا وَّبِدَارًا اَنْ يَّكْبَرُوْاط وَمَنْ كَانَ غَنِيًّا فَلْيَسْتَعْفِفْج وَمَنْ كَانَ فَقِيْرًا فَلْيَاْكُلْ بِالْمَعْرُوْفِط فَاِذَا دَفَعْتُمْ اِلَيْهِمْ اَمْوَالَهُمْ فَاَشْهِدُوْا عَلَيْهِمْط وَكَفٰى بِاللهِ حَسِيْبًاﯜ6ﯛ لِلرِّجَالِ نَصِيْبٌ مِّمَّا تَرَكَ الْوَالِدٰنِ وَالْاَقْرَبُوْنَص وَلِلنِّسَآءِ نَصِيْبٌ مِّمَّا تَرَكَ الْوَالِدٰنِ وَالْاَقْرَبُوْنَ مِمَّا قَلَّ مِنْهُ اَوْ كَثُرَط نَصِيْبًا مَّفْرُوْضًاﯜ7ﯛ وَاِذَا حَضَرَ الْقِسْمَةَ اُولُو الْقُرْبٰى وَالْيَتٰمٰى وَالْمَسٰكِيْنُ فَارْزُقُوْهُمْ مِّنْهُ وَقُوْلُوْا لَهُمْ قَوْلًا مَّعْرُوْفًاﯜ8ﯛ وَلْيَخْشَ الَّذِيْنَ لَوْ تَرَكُوْا مِنْ خَلْفِهِمْ ذُرِّيَّةً ضِعٰفًا خَافُوْا عَلَيْهِمْ فَلْيَتَّقُوا اللهَ وَلْيَقُوْلُوْا قَوْلًا سَدِيْدًاﯜ9ﯛ اِنَّ الَّذِيْنَ يَاْكُلُوْنَ اَمْوَالَ الْيَتٰمٰى ظُلْمًا اِنَّمَا يَاْكُلُوْنَ فِيْ بُطُوْنِهِمْ نَارًاط وَسَيَصْلَوْنَ سَعِيْرًاﯜ10ﯛ يُوْصِيْكُمُ اللهُ فِيْٓ اَوْلَادِكُمْق لِلذَّكَرِ مِثْلُ حَظِّ الْاُنْثَيَيْنِج فَاِنْ كُنَّ نِسَآءً فَوْقَ اثْنَتَيْنِ فَلَهُنَّ ثُلُثَا مَا تَرَكَج وَاِنْ كَانَتْ وَاحِدَةً فَلَهَا النِّصْفُط وَلِاَبَوَيْهِ لِكُلِّ وَاحِدٍ مِّنْهُمَا السُّدُسُ مِمَّا تَرَكَ اِنْ كَانَ لَهٗ وَلَدٌج فَاِنْ لَّمْ يَكُنْ لَّهٗ وَلَدٌ وَّوَرِثَهٗٓ اَبَوٰهُ فَلِاُمِّهِ الثُّلُثُج فَاِنْ كَانَ لَهٗٓ اِخْوَةٌ فَلِاُمِّهِ السُّدُسُ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ وَصِيَّةٍ يُوْصِيْ بِهَآ اَوْ دَيْنٍط اٰبَآؤُكُمْ وَاَبْنَآؤُكُمْ لَا تَدْرُوْنَ اَيُّهُمْ اَقْرَبُ لَكُمْ نَفْعًاط فَرِيْضَةً مِّنَ اللهِط اِنَّ اللهَ كَانَ عَلِيْمًا حَكِيْمًاﯜ11ﯛ وَلَكُمْ نِصْفُ مَا تَرَكَ اَزْوَاجُكُمْ اِنْ لَّمْ يَكُنْ لَّهُنَّ وَلَدٌج فَاِنْ كَانَ لَهُنَّ وَلَدٌ فَلَكُمُ الرُّبُعُ مِمَّا تَرَكْنَ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ وَصِيَّةٍ يُّوْصِيْنَ بِهَآ اَوْ دَيْنٍط وَلَهُنَّ الرُّبُعُ مِمَّا تَرَكْتُمْ اِنْ لَّمْ يَكُنْ لَّكُمْ وَلَدٌج فَاِنْ كَانَ لَكُمْ وَلَدٌ فَلَهُنَّ الثُّمُنُ مِمَّا تَرَكْتُمْ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ وَصِيَّةٍ تُوْصُوْنَ بِهَآ اَوْ دَيْنٍط وَاِنْ كَانَ رَجُلٌ يُّوْرَثُ كَلٰلَةً اَوِ امْرَاَةٌ وَّلَهٗٓ اَخٌ اَوْ اُخْتٌ فَلِكُلِّ وَاحِدٍ مِّنْهُمَا السُّدُسُج فَاِنْ كَانُوْٓا اَكْثَرَ مِنْ ذٰلِكَ فَهُمْ شُرَكَآءُ فِي الثُّلُثِ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ وَصِيَّةٍ يُّوْصٰى بِهَآ اَوْ دَيْنٍلا غَيْرَ مُضَآرٍّج وَصِيَّةً مِّنَ اللهِط وَاللهُ عَلِيْمٌ حَلِيْمٌﯜ12ﯛ تِلْكَ حُدُوْدُ اللهِط وَمَنْ يُّطِعِ اللهَ وَرَسُوْلَهٗ يُدْخِلْهُ جَنّٰتٍ تَجْرِيْ مِنْ تَحْتِهَا الْاَنْهٰرُ خٰلِدِيْنَ فِيهَاط وَذٰلِكَ الْفَوْزُ الْعَظِيْمُﯜ13ﯛ وَمَنْ يَّعْصِ اللهَ وَرَسُوْلَهٗ وَيَتَعَدَّ حُدُوْدَهٗ يُدْخِلْهُ نَارًا خَالِدًا فِيْهَاص وَلَهٗ عَذَابٌ مُّهِيْنٌﯜ14ﯛ وَالَّاتِي يَاْتِيْنَ الْفَاحِشَةَ مِنْ نِّسَآئِكُمْ فَاسْتَشْهِدُوْا عَلَيْهِنَّ اَرْبَعَةً مِّنْكُمْج فَاِنْ شَهِدُوْا فَاَمْسِكُوْهُنَّ فِي الْبُيُوْتِ حَتّٰى يَتَوَفّٰهُنَّ الْمَوْتُ اَوْ يَجْعَلَ اللهُ لَهُنَّ سَبِيْلًاﯜ15ﯛ وَالَّذٰنِ يَاْتِيٰنِهَا مِنْكُمْ فَاٰذُوْهُمَاج فَاِنْ تَابَا وَاَصْلَحَا فَاَعْرِضُوْا عَنْهُمَاط اِنَّ اللهَ كَانَ تَوَّابًا رَّحِيْمًاﯜ16ﯛ اِنَّمَا التَّوْبَةُ عَلَى اللهِ لِلَّذِيْنَ يَعْمَلُوْنَ السُّوْٓءَ بِجَهَالَةٍ ثُمَّ يَتُوْبُوْنَ مِنْ قَرِيْبٍ فَاُوْلٰۗئِكَ يَتُوْبُ اللهُ عَلَيْهِمْط وَكَانَ اللهُ عَلِيْمًا حَكِيْمًاﯜ17ﯛ وَلَيْسَتِ التَّوْبَةُ لِلَّذِيْنَ يَعْمَلُوْنَ السَّيِّاٰتِج حَتّٰٓى اِذَا حَضَرَ اَحَدَهُمُ الْمَوْتُ قَالَ اِنِّي تُبْتُ الْئٰنَ وَلَا الَّذِيْنَ يَمُوْتُوْنَ وَهُمْ كُفَّارٌط اُوْلٰۗئِكَ اَعْتَدْنَا لَهُمْ عَذَابًا اَلِيْمًاﯜ18ﯛ يٰٓاَيُّهَا الَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا لَا يَحِلُّ لَكُمْ اَنْ تَرِثُوا النِّسَآءَ كَرْهًاط وَلَا تَعْضُلُوْهُنَّ لِتَذْهَبُوْا بِبَعْضِ مَآ اٰتَيْتُمُوْهُنَّ اِلَّآ اَنْ يَّاْتِيْنَ بِفَاحِشَةٍ مُّبَيِّنَةٍج وَعَاشِرُوْهُنَّ بِالْمَعْرُوْفِج فَاِنْ كَرِهْتُمُوْهُنَّ فَعَسٰٓى اَنْ تَكْرَهُوْا شَيْئًا وَّيَجْعَلَ اللهُ فِيْهِ خَيْرًا كَثِيْرًاﯜ19ﯛ وَاِنْ اَرَدْتُّمُ اسْتِبْدَالَ زَوْجٍ مَّكَانَ زَوْجٍ لا وَّاٰتَيْتُمْ اِحْدٰهُنَّ قِنْطَارًا فَلَا تَاْخُذُوْا مِنْهُ شَيْئًاط اَتَاْخُذُوْنَهٗ بُهْتَانًا وَّاِثْمًا مُّبِيْنًاﯜ20ﯛ وَكَيْفَ تَاْخُذُوْنَهٗ وَقَدْ اَفْضٰى بَعْضُكُمْ اِلٰى بَعْضٍ وَّاَخَذْنَ مِنْكُمْ مِّيْثَاقًا غَلِيْظًاﯜ21ﯛ وَلَا تَنْكِحُوْا مَا نَكَحَ اٰبَآؤُكُمْ مِّنَ النِّسَآءِ اِلَّا مَا قَدْ سَلَفَط اللّٰتِيْٓ اَرْضَعْنَكُمْ وَاَخَوٰتُكُمْ مِّنَ الرَّضَاعَةِ وَاُمَّهٰتُ نِسَآئِكُمْ وَرَبَآئِبُكُمُ اللّٰتِيْ فِيْ حُجُوْرِكُمْ مِّنْ نِسَآئِكُمُ اللّٰتِيْ دَخَلْتُمْ بِهِنَّز فَاِنْ لَّمْ تَكُوْنُوْا دَخَلْتُمْ بِهِنَّ فَلَا جُنَاحَ عَلَيْكُمْز وَحَلَٓائِلُ اَبْنَآئِكُمُ الَّذِيْنَ مِنْ اَصْلَابِكُمْلا وَاَنْ تَجْمَعُوْا بَيْنَ الْاُخْتَيْنِ اِلَّا مَا قَدْ سَلَفَط اِنَّ اللهَ كَانَ مُّحْصِنِيْنَ غَيْرَ مُسٰفِحِيْنَط فَمَا اسْتَمْتَعْتُمْ بِهٖ مِنْهُنَّ فَاٰتُوْهُنَّ اُجُوْرَهُنَّ فَرِيْضَةًط وَلَا جُنَاحَ عَلَيْكُمْ فِيْمَا تَرٰضَيْتُمْ بِهٖ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ الْفَرِيْضَةِط اِنَّ اللهَ كَانَ عَلِيْمًا حَكِيْمًاﯜ24ﯛ وَمَنْ لَّمْ يَسْتَطِعْ مِنْكُمْ طَوْلًا اَنْ يَّنْكِحَ الْمُحْصَنٰتِ الْمُؤْمِنٰتِ فَمِنْ مَّا مَلَكَتْ اَيْمَانُكُمْ مِّنْ فَتَيٰتِكُمُ الْمُؤْمِنٰتِط وَاللهُ حَرَجًا مِّمَّا قَضَيْتَ وَيُسَلِّمُوْا تَسْلِيْمًاﯜ65ﯛ وَلَوْ اَنَّا كَتَبْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ اَنِ اقْتُلُوْٓا اَنْفُسَكُمْ اَوِ اخْرُجُوْا مِنْ دِيَارِكُمْ مَّا فَعَلُوْهُ اِلَّا قَلِيْلٌ مِّنْهُمْط مُّسْتَقِيْمًاﯜ68ﯛ وَمَنْ يُّطِعِ اللهَ وَالرَّسُوْلَ فَاُوْلٰۗئِكَ مَعَ الَّذِيْنَ اَنْعَمَ اللهُ عَلَيْهِمْ مِّنَ النَّبِيّٖنَ وَالصِّدِّيْقِيْنَ وَالشُّهَدَآءِ وَالصّٰلِحِيْنَج وَحَسُنَ مِنْكُمْ لَمَنْ لَّيُبَطِّئَنَّ فَاِنْ اَصَابَتْكُمْ مُّصِيْبَةٌ قَالَ قَدْ اَنْعَمَ اللهُ عَلَيَّ اِذْ لَمْ اَكُنْ مَّعَهُمْ شَهِيْدًاﯜ72ﯛ وَلَئِنْ اَصَابَكُمْ فَضْلٌ مِّنَ اللهِ لَيَقُوْلَنَّ كَاَنْ لَّمْ تَكُنْۢ بَيْنَكُمْ وَبَيْنَهٗ مَوَدَّةٌ يّٰلَيْتَنِيْ كُنْتُ مَعَهُمْ فَاَفُوْزَ فَوْزًا عَظِيْمًاﯜ73ﯛ فَلْيُقَاتِلْ فِيْ سَبِيْلِ اللهِ الَّذِيْنَ يَشْرُوْنَ الْحَيٰوةَ الدُّنْيَا الرِّجَالِ وَالنِّسَآءِ وَالْوِلْدَانِ الَّذِيْنَ يَقُوْلُوْنَ رَبَّنَآ اَخْرِجْنَا مِنْ هٰذِهِ الْقَرْيَةِ الظَّالِمِ اَهْلُهَاج وَاجْعَلْ لَّنَا مِنْ لَّدُنْكَ وَلِيًّا ڌ وَّاجْعَلْ لَّنَا مِنْ لَّدُنْكَ نَصِيْرًاﯜ75ﯛ اَلَّذِيْنَ اٰمَنُوْا يُقَاتِلُوْنَ فِيْ سَبِيْلِ اللهِج وَالَّذِيْنَ كَفَرُوْا يُقَاتِلُوْنَ فِيْ سَبِيْلِ الطَّاغُوْتِ فَقَاتِلُوْٓا اَوْلِيَآءَ الشَّيْطٰنِج اِنَّ كَيْدَ مِّنْهُمْ يَخْشَوْنَ النَّاسَ كَخَشْيَةِ اللهِ اَوْ اَشَدَّ خَشْيَةًج وَقَالُوْا رَبَّنَا لِمَ كَتَبْتَ عَلَيْنَا الْقِتَالَج لَوْلَٓا اَخَّرْتَنَآ اِلٰٓى اَجَلٍ قَرِيْبٍط قُلْ مَتَاعُ الدُّنْيَا حَدِيْثًاﯜ78ﯛ مَآ اَصَابَكَ مِنْ حَسَنَةٍ فَمِنَ اللهِز وَمَآ اَصَابَكَ مِنْ سَيِّئَةٍ فَمِنْ نَفْسِكَط وَاَرْسَلْنٰكَ لِلنَّاسِ رَسُوْلًاط وَكَفٰى بِاللهِ شَهِيْدًاﯜ79ﯛ مَنْ يُّطِعِ الرَّسُوْلَ صَدَقَ اللهُ الْعَلِیُّ الْعَظِیْمُ

4 | NISAA | 176

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Us Parwardigaar se daro jisne tum sab ko ek nafs se paiyda kiya hai aur uska joda bhi usi ki jins se paiyda kiya hai aur phir dono se ba-kasrat mard wa aurat duniya mein phaila diye hai aur us Khuda se bhi daro jiske zariye ek dusre se sawaal karte ho aur qaraabat-daaro ki be-talluqi se bhi. Allah tum sab ke aamaal ka nighraah hai.

2. Aur yateemo ko unka maal de do aur unke maal ko apne maal se na badlo aur unke maal ko apne maal ke saath mila kar na kha jaawo keh yeh gunah-e-kabeera hai.

3. Aur agar yateemo ke baare mein insaaf na kar sakne ka khatra hai to jo aurte’n tumhe pasand hai do. Teen chaar un se nikah kar lo aur agar un mein bhi insaaf na kar sakne ka khatra hai to sirf ek... Yaa jo kaneeze tumhaare haath ki milkiyat hai, yeh baat insaaf se tajaawuz na karne se qareeb-tar hai.

4. Aurto’n ko unka maher ataa kar do phir agar woh khusi khusi tumhe dena chahe to shouq se khaalo.

5. Aur na-samajh logo ko unke woh amwaal jin ko tumhaare liye qayaam ka zariya banaya gaya hai na do. Us mein unke khaane kapde ka intezaam kar do aur un se munaasib ghuftagu karo.

6. Aur yateemo ka imtehaan lo aur jab woh nikah ke qaabil ho jaaye to agar un mein Rasheed (samajh-daar) hone ka ehsaas karo to unke amwaal unke hawaale kar do aur zyada’ti ke saath ya is khauf se keh kahi woh bade na ho jaaye jaldi jaldi na kha jaawo... Aur tum mein jo ghani hai woh unke maal se parhez kare aur jo faqeer hai woh bhi sirf be-qadre munaasib khaaye phir jab unke amwaal unke hawaale karo to gawah bana lo aur Khuda to hisaab ke liye khud hi kaafi hai.

7. Mardo ke liye unke waaledain aur aqraba ke tarkah (varse) mein ek hissa hai aur aurto’n ke liye bhi unke waaledain aur aqraba ke tarkah (waarse) mein se ek hissa hai woh maal bohot ho ya thoda yeh hissa bator fareeza hai.

8. Aur agar taqseem ke waqt digar qaraabat-daar, aytam, masaakin bhi aa jaaye to unhe bhi us mein se bator rizq de do aur un se narm aur munaasib ghuftagu karo.

9. Aur un logo ko is baat se darna chaahiye ke agar woh khud apne baad zaeef-o-na-tawa aulaad chhod jaate to kis qadr pareshan hote lehaaza Khuda se darey aur seedhi-seedhi ghuftagu kare.

10. Jo log zaalimaana andaaz se yateemo ka maal kha jaate hai woh dar-haqeeqat apne peyt (stomach) mein aag bhar rahe hai aur an-qareeb waasile Jahannam hoge.

11. Allah tumhaari aulaad ke baare mein tumhe hidaayat farmaata hai, ek ladke ka hissa do ladkiyo ke hisse ke baraabar hai, pas agar ladkiya do se zaada ho to tarke ka do tehaayi (2/3) unka haq hai aur agar sirf ek ladki hai to nisf (aadha) uska hai aur mayyat ki aulaad hone ki surat mein waaledain mein se har ek ko tarke ka chhatta (1/6) hissa milega aur agar mayyat ki aulaad na ho bal-keh sirf maa-baap uske waaris ho to uski maa ko teesra (1/3rd) hissa milega, pas agar mayyat ke bhai ho to maa ko chhatta (1/6) hissa milega, yeh taqseem mayyat ki wasiyat par amal karne aur iske karz ki adaaygi ke baad hogi, tumhe nahi maaloom tumhaare waaledain aur tumhaari aulaad mein faaidey ke hawaale se kaun tumhaare zeyada qareeb hai, yeh hisse Allah ke muqarrar kardah hai, yaqeenan Allah bada jaanne waala, ba-hikmat hai.

12. Aur tumhe apni biwiyo ke tarke (varse) mein se agar unki aulaad na ho nisf hissa milega aur agar unki aulaad ho to unke tarke mein se chothai (1/4) tumhaara hoga, yeh taqseem mayyat ki wasiyat par amal karne aur karz adaa karne ke baad hogi aur tumhaari aulaad na ho to unhe tumhaare tarke mein se chothai (1/4) milega aur agar tumhaari aulaad ho to unhe tumhaare tarke mein se aathwa (1/8) hissa milega, yeh taqseem tumhaari wasiyat par amal karne aur karz adaa karne ke baad hogi aur agar koi mard ya aurat be aulad ho aur waaledain bhi zinda na ho aur uska ek bhai ya ek bahen ho to bhai aur bahen mein se har ek ko chhatta (1/6) hissa milega, pas agar bahen-bhai ek se zeyada ho to sab ek tehaayi (1/3) hisse mein shareek hoge, yeh taqseem wasiyat par amal karne aur karz adaa karne ke baad hogi, be-sharte ke zarrar rasan (nuksan deh) na ho, yeh nasihat Allah ki taraf se hai aur Allah bada daana, burdbaar hai.

13. Yeh sab ilaahi hudood hai aur jo Allah-o-Rasool ki ita’at karega Khuda usey un jannato mein daakhil karega jin ke neeche nehre jaari hogi aur woh un mein hamesha rahege aur dar-haqeeqat yahi sab se badi kaam-yaabi hai.

14. Aur jo Khuda-o-Rasool ki na-farmaani karega aur uske hudood se tajaawuz kar jaayega Khuda usey Jahannam mein daakhil kar dega aur woh wahi hamesha rahega aur uske liye ruswa-kun azaab hai.

15. Aur tumhaari aurto’n mein se jo aurte’n bad-kaari kare un par apno mein se chaar gawaho ki gawahi lo aur jab gawahi de-de to unhe gharo mein bandh kar do. Yahan tak keh maut aa jaaye ya Khuda unke liye koi raasta muqarrar kar de.

16. Aur tum mein se jo aadmi bad-kaari kare unhe aziyat do phir agar tauba kar le aur apne haal ki islaah kar le to un se aarz karo ke Khuda bohot tauba qabool karne waala aur maher-baan hai.

17. Tauba Khuda ke zimme sirf un logo ke liye hai jo jehaalat ki bina par buraayi karte hai aur phir fauran tauba kar lete hai keh Khuda unki tauba ko qabool kar leta hai woh Aleem-o-dana bhi hai aur saheb-e-hikmat bhi.

18. Aur tauba un logo ke liye nahi hai jo pehle buraaiya karte hai aur phir jab maut saamne aa jaati hai to kehte hai keh ab ham ne tauba kar-li aur na unke liye hai jo haal-e-kufr mein marjate hai keh unke liye ham ne bada dard-naak azaab mohayya (tayyar) kar rakkha hai.

19. Ay imaan waalo! tumhaare liye jaayez nahi hai ke jabran aurto’n ke waaris ban jaawo aur khabar-daar! unhe mana bhi na karo ke jo kuch un ko de diya hai uska kuch hissa le lo magar yeh keh waazeh taur par bad-kaari kare aur unke saath nek bartaaw karo ab agar tum unhe na-pasand bhi karte ho to ho sakta hai ke tum kisi cheez ko na-pasand karte ho aur Khuda usi mein khair-e-kaseer qaraar de-de.

20. Aur agar tum ek zauja ki jagah par dusri zauja ko laana chaaho aur ek ko maale kaseer bhi de chuke ho to khabar-daar! Us mein se kuch waapas na lena. Kya tum us maal ko bohtaan aur khule gunah ke taur par lena chahte ho.

21. Aur aakhir kis tarah tum maal ko waapas loge jab-keh ek dusre se mut-tasil ho chuka hai aur un aurto’n ne tum se bohot sakht qism ka ahed liya hai.

22. Aur khabar-daar! Jin aurto’n se tumhaare baap-dada ne nikah kiya hai un se nikah na karna magar woh jo ab tak ho chuka hai... yeh khuli huwi buraayi aur Parwardigaar ka ghazab aur bad-tareen raasta hai.

23. Tumhare upar tumhaari maae, betiya, bahene, phuphiya, khaalaae, bhatijiya, bhaanjiya, woh maae jinho ne tum ko doodh pilaaya hai, tumhaari razaayi (doodh shareek) bahene, tumhaari biwiyo ki maae, tumhaari parwardah aurte’n jo tumhaari aagosh mein hai aur un aurto’n ki aulaad, jin se tum ne dukhool (jimaa) kiya hai, haa’n agar dukhool nahi kiya hai to koi harj nahi hai aur tumhaare farzando ki biwiya jo farzand tumhaare sulb se hai aur do baheno ka ek saath jama karna sab haraam kar diya gaya hai alaawa uske jo is se pehle ho chuka hai keh Khuda bohot bakhshne waala aur maher-baan hai.

24. Aur tum par haram hai shaadi-shuda aurte’n. Alaawa unke jo tumhaari kanize ban jaaye. Yeh Khuda ka khula huwa qaanoon hai aur un sab aurto’n ke alaawa tumhaare liye halaal hai keh apne amwaal ke zariye aurto’n se rishta paiyda karo iffat-o-paak daamani ke saath safah-o-zina ke saath nahi pas jo bhi un aurto’n se tamat-to kare unki ujrat unhe bator fareeza de-de aur fareeze ke baad aapas mein raza-mandi ho jaaye to koi harj nahi hai be-shak Allah Aleem bhi hai aur Hakeem bhi hai.

25. Aur jiske paas is qadr maali wus’at nahi hai keh momin azaad aurto’n se nikah kare to woh momeena kaneez aurat se aqd kar le Khuda tumhaare imaan se ba-khabar hai tum sab ek dusre se ho. Un kanizo se unke ahel ki ijaazat se aqd karo aur unhe unki munaasib ujrat (maher) de do. Un kanizo se aqd karo jo afifa (pakiza) aur paak daaman ho na ke khullam-khulla zina kaar ho aur na chori chhupe dosti karne waali hoon. Phir jab aqd mein aa gayi to agar zina kare to unke liye azaad aurto’n ke nisf ke baraabar saza hai yeh kanizo se aqd unke liye hai jo be-sabri ka khatra rakhte ho warna sabr karo to tumhaare haq mein behtar hai aur Allah Ghafoor-o-Raheem hai.

26. Woh chahta hai keh tumhaare liye waazeh ehkaam bayaan kar de aur tumhe tum se pehle waalo ke tareeqa-e-kaar ki hidaayat kar de aur tumhaari tauba qabool kar le aur woh khoob jaanne waala aur saheb-e-hikmat hai.

27. Khuda chahta hai keh tumhaari tauba qabool kare aur khwahishaat ki pairwi karne waale chahte hai keh tumhe bilkul hi raah-e-haq se dur kar de.

28. Khuda chahta hai keh tumhaare liye takhfeef (kami) ka saamaan kar de aur insaan to kamzor hi paiyda kiya gaya hai.

29. Ay imaan waalo! Aapas mein ek dusre ke maal ko na-haq tareeqe se na kha jaaya karo... Magar yeh keh bahami raza-mandi se moamlaat ho aur khabar-daar! Apne nafs ko qatl na karo Allah tumhaare haal par bohot maher-baan hai.

30. Aur jo aisa iqdaam hudood se tajaawuz aur zulm ke unwaan se karega ham an-qareeb usey Jahannam mein daal dege aur Allah ke liye yeh kaam bohot aasaan hai.

31. Agar tum bade-bade gunaho se jin se tumhe roka gaya hai parhez kar loge to ham dusre gunaho ki parda-poshi kar dege aur tumhe ba-izzat manzil tak pohoncha dege.

32. Aur khabar-daar! Jo Khuda ne baaz afraad ko baaz se kuch zeyada diya hai uski tamanna aur aarzoo na karna mardo ke liye woh hissa hai jo unho ne kamaaya hai aur aurto’n ke liye woh hissa hai jo unho ne haasil kiya hai Allah se uske fazl ka sawaal karo keh woh be-shak har shaiy ka jaanne waala hai.

33. Aur jo kuch maa-baap ya aqraba ne chhoda hai ham ne sab ke liye wali-o-waaris muqarrar kar diye hai aur jin se tum ne ahed-o-paimaan kiya hai unka hissa bhi unhe de do. Be-shak Allah har shaiy par gawah aur nigra hai.

34. Mard aurto’n ke haakim aur nigra hai un fazilato ki bina par jo Khuda ne baaz ko baaz par di hai aur is bina par keh unho ne aurto’n par apna maal kharch kiya hai pas nek aurte’n woh hi hai jo shauharo (husband) ki ita’at karne waali aur unki ghaibat mein un cheezo ki hifaazat karne waali hai jin ki Khuda ne hifaazat chaahi hai aur jin aurto’n ki na-farmaani ka khatra hai unhe maw-eza karo unhe khwaab gaah mein alag kar do aur maaro aur phir ita’at karne lage to koi zayadti ki raah talaash na karo keh Khuda bohot buland aur buzurag hai.

35. Aur agar dono ke darmiyaan ikhtelaaf ka andesha hai to ek hakam (faisla karne wala) mard ki taraf se aur ek aurat waalo mein se bhejo. Phir woh dono islaah chahenge to Khuda unke darmiyaan hamahaangi paiyda kar dega be-shak Allah Aleem bhi hai aur Khabir bhi.

36. Aur Allah ki ibaadat karo aur kisi shaiy ko uska shareek na banaawo aur waaledain ke saath nek bartaaw karo aur qarabat-daaro ke saath aur yateemo, miskeeno, qareeb ke hamsaaya, dur ke hamsaaya, pehlu nasheen, musaafir ghurbat-zada, ghulam-o-kaneez sab ke saath nek bartaaw karo ke Allah maghroor aur mutaqabbir logo ko pasand nahi karta.

37. Jo khud bhi bukhl (kanjoosi) karte hai aur dusro ko bhi bukhl ka hukm dete hai aur jo kuch Khuda ne apne fazl-o-karam se ataa kiya hai is par parda daalte hai aur ham ne kaafiro ke waaste ruswa-kun azaab mohayya (tayyar) kar rakkha hai.

38. Aur jo log apne amwaal ko logo ko dikhaane ke liye kharch karte hai aur Allah aur aakherat par imaan nahi rakhte hai unhe maaloom hona chaahiye ke jiska shaitaan saathi ho jaaye woh bad-tareen saathi hai.

39. Un ka kya nuksaan hai agar yeh Allah aur aakherat par imaan le aaye aur jo Allah ne bator rizq diya hai usey uski raah mein kharch kare aur Allah har ek ko khoob jaanta hai.

40. Allah kisi par zarra baraabar zulm nahi karta. Insaan ke paas neki hoti hai to usey dugna kar deta hai aur apne paas se ajr azeem ataa karta hai.

41. Us waqt kya hoga jab ham har ummat ko uske gawah ke saath bulaayege aur Paighambar aap ko un sab ka gawah bana kar bulaayege.

42. Us din jin logo ne kufr ikhtiyaar kiya hai aur Rasool ki na-farmaani ki hai yeh khwahish karege ke ay kaash unke upar se zameen baraabar kar di jaati aur woh Khuda se kisi baat ko nahi chhupa sakte hai.

43. Imaan waalo khabar daar nashe ki haalat mein namaaz ke qareeb bhi na jaana jab tak yeh hosh na aa jaaye ke tum kya kahe rahe ho aur janaabat ki haalat mein bhi, magar yeh keh raaste se guzar rahe ho. Jab tak ghusl na kar lo aur agar bimaar ho ya safar ki haalat mein ho aur kisi ka paikhana nikal aaye, ya aurto’n se bahami jinsi rabt qaayem ho jaaye aur paani na miley to paak mitti se tayammum kar lo is tarah keh apne chehro aur haatho par masah kar lo be-shak Khuda bohot maaf karne waala aur bakhshne waala hai.

44. Kya tumne un logo ko nahi dekha hai jinhe kitaab ka thoda sa hissa de diya gaya hai keh woh gumraahi ka sauda karte hai aur chahte hai keh tum bhi raaste se bahek jaawo.

45. Aur Allah tumhaare dushmano ko khoob jaanta hai aur woh tumhaari sar-parasti aur madad ke liye kaafi hai.

46. Yahoodiyo mein woh log bhi hai jo kalemaate ilaahi ko unki jagah se hata dete hai aur kehte hai keh ham ne baat sooni aur na-farmaani ki aur tum bhi soono magar tumhaari baat na sooni jaayegi. Yeh sab zabaan ki tod-marod aur deen mein tana-zani (taunt) ki bina par hota hai haala keh agar yeh log yeh kehte keh ham ne soona aur ita’at ki aap bhi soone aur nazre karam kijiye to unke haq mein behtar aur munaasib tha lekin Khuda ne unke kufr ki bina par un par laanat ki hai to yeh imaan na laayege magar bohot qaleel tadaad mein.

47. Ay woh log jinhe kitaab di gayi hai! hamare naazil kiye huwe Qur’an par imaan le aawo jo tumhaari kitaabo ki tasdeeq (confirmation) karne waala hai qabl is ke, keh ham tumhaare chehro ko bigaad kar pusht ki taraf pher de ya un par is tarah laanat kare jis tarah ham ne as-haabe sabt (saturday waalo) par laanat ki hai aur Allah ka hukm baher-haal nafiz hai.

48. Allah is baat ko maaf nahi kar sakta ke uska shareek qaraar diya jaaye aur iske alaawa jis ko chahe bakhsh sakta hai aur jo bhi uska shareek banaayega us ne bohot bada gunah kiya hai.

49. Kya tum ne un logo ko nahi dekha jo apne nafs ki pakeezgi ka izhaar karte hai... haala keh Allah jis ko chahta hai pakeeza banata hai aur bando par dhaage ke baraabar bhi zulm nahi hota.

50. Dekho to unho ne kis tarah Khuda par khullam-khulla ilzaam lagaya hai aur yahi unke khullam-khulla gunah ke liye kaafi hai.

51. Kya tum ne nahi dekha keh jin logo ko kitaab ka kuch hissa de diya gaya woh shaitaan aur booto par imaan rakhte hai aur kufr ko bhi bataate hai keh yeh log imaan waalo se zeyada seedhe raaste par hai.

52. Yahi woh log hai jin par Khuda ne laanat ki hai aur jis par Khuda laanat kar de, aap phir uska koi madad-gaar na paayege.

53. Kya mulke duniya mein unka bhi koi hissa hai keh logo ko bhoosi (zarra) baraabar bhi nahi dena chahte hai.

54. Ya woh un logo se hasad karte hai jinhe Khuda ne apne fazl-o-karam se bohot kuch ataa kiya hai to phir ham ne aale Ibrahim alayhis salaam ko kitaab-o-hikmat aur mulk-e-azeem sab-kuch ataa kya hai.

55. Phir un mein se baaz un cheezo par imaan le aaye aur baaz ne inkaar kar diya aur un logo ke liye dehakta (bhadakta) huwa Jahannam hi kaafi hai.

56. Be-shak jin logo ne hamari aayato ka inkaar kiya hai ham unhe aag mein bhon dege aur jab ek khaal pak jaayega to dusri badal dege ta-keh azaab ka maza chahkte rahe Khuda sab par ghaalib aur saheb-e-hikmat hai.

57. Aur jo log imaan le aaye aur unho ne nek amal kiye ham an-qareeb unhe jannato mein daakhil karege jin ke neeche nehre jaari hogi aur woh un hi mein hamesha rahege unke liye wahan pakeeza biwiya hogi aur unhe ghani-chao (shades) mein rakkha jaayega.

58. Be-shak Allah tumhe hukm deta hai ke amaanato ko unke ahel tak pohoncha do aur jab koi faisla karo to insaaf ke saath karo Allah tumhe behtareen nasihat karta hai be-shak Allah sami’a bhi hai aur baseer bhi.

59. Imaan waalo Allah ki ita’at karo Rasool aur sahebaan-e-amr ki ita’at karo jo tum hi mein se hai phir agar aapas mein kisi baat mein ikhtelaaf ho jaaye to usey Khuda aur Rasool ki taraf palta do agar tum Allah aur roze aakherat par imaan rakhne waale ho. Yahi tumhaare haq mein khair aur anjaam ke etebaar se behtareen baat hai.

60. Kya aap ne un logo ko nahi dekha jin ka khayaal yeh hai keh woh aap par aur aap ke pehle naazil hone waali cheezo par imaan le aaye hai aur phir yeh chahte hai keh sarkash (rebellious) logo ke paas faisla karaaye jab-keh unhe hukm diya gaya hai ke taaghoot ka inkaar kare aur shaitaan to yahi chahta hai keh unhe gumraahi mein dur tak kheench kar le jaaye.

61. Aur jab un se kaha jaata hai ke hukm-e-Khuda aur uske Rasool ki taraf aawo to tum munafeqeen ko dekhoge keh woh shiddat se inkaar kar dete hai.

62. Pas us waqt kya hoga jab un par unke aamaal ki bina par museebat naazil hogi aur woh aap ke paas aa kar Khuda ki qasam khaayege ke hamara maqsad sirf neki karna aur ittehaad paiyda karna tha

63. Yahi woh log hai jin ke dil ka haal Khuda khoob jaanta hai lehaaza aap un se kinaara-kash rahe unhe nasihat kare aur unke dil par asar karne waali mahal mauqa ke mutaabiq baat kare.

64. Aur ham ne kisi Rasool ko bhi nahi bheja hai magar sirf is liye ke hukm-e-Khuda se uski ita’at ki jaaye aur kaash jab un logo ne apne nafs par zulm kiya tha to aap ke paas aate aur khud bhi apne gunaho ke liye isteghfaar karte aur Rasool bhi unke haq mein isteghfaar karta to yeh Khuda ko bada hi tauba qabool karne waala aur maher-baan paate.

65. Pas aap ke Parwardigaar ki qasam keh yeh harghiz saheb-e-imaan na ban sakayge jab tak aap ko apne ikhtelaafaat mein hakam na banaaye aur phir jab aap faisla kar de to apne dil mein kisi tarah ki tanghi ka ehsaas na kare aur aap ke faisle ke saamne saraapa tasleem (submission) ho jaaye.

66. Aur agar ham un munafeqeen par farz kar dete keh apne ko qatl kar daale ya ghar chhod kar nikal jaaye to chand afraad ke alaawa koi amal na karta haala keh agar yeh is nasihat par amal karte to unke haq mein behtar hi hota aur un ko zeyada sabaat haasil hota.

67. Aur ham unhe apni taraf se ajre azeem bhi ataa karte.

68. Aur unhe seedhe raasta ki hidaayat bhi kar dete.

69. Aur jo bhi Allah aur Rasool ki ita’at karega woh un logo ke saath rahega jin par Khuda ne nemate naazil ki hai ambiya, siddiqeen, shohada aur saaleheen aur yahi behtareen rofaqaa (dost) hai.

70. Yeh Allah ki taraf se fazl-o-karam hai aur Khuda har ek ke haalaat ke ilm ke liye kaafi hai.

71. Imaan waalo apne tahaffuz (hifazat) ka saamaan sambhaal lo aur jamaat jamaat ya ekhatta, jaisa mauqa ho sab nikal pado.

72. Tum mein aise log bhi ghoos gaye hai jo logo ko rokenge aur agar tum par koi museebat aa gayi to Kahenge ke Khuda unhe ham par ehsaan kiya keh ham unke saath haazir nahi thay.

73. Aur agar tumhe khudai fazl-o-karam mil gaya to is tarah jaise tumhaare unke darmiyaan kabhi dosti hi nahi thi kehne lagege ke kaash ham bhi unke saath hote aur kaam-yaabi ki azeem manzil par faaez ho jaate.

74. Ab zarurat hai keh raahe Khuda mein woh log jehaad kare jo zindagaani-e-duniya ko aakherat ke ewaz bech daalte hai aur jo bhi raahe Khuda mein jehaad karega woh qatl ho jaaye ya ghaalib aa jaaye dono surato mein ham usey ajre azeem ataa karege.

75. Aur aakhir tumhe kya ho gaya hai keh tum Allah ki raah mein aur un kamzor mardo, aurto’n aur bachcho ke liye jehaad nahi karte ho jinhe kamzor bana kar rakkha gaya hai aur jo baraabar dua karte hai keh Khudaaya hamein is (basti) se najaat de-de jiske baashinde zaalim hai aur hamare liye koi sarparast aur apni taraf se madad-gaar qaraar de-de.

76. Imaan waale hamesha Allah ki raah mein jehaad karte hai aur jo kaafir hai woh hamesha taaghoot ki raah mein ladte hai lehaaza tum shaitaan ke saathiyo se jehaad karo be-shak shaitaan ka makr bohot kamzor hota hai.

77. Kya tum ne un logo ko nahi dekha jin se kaha gaya tha keh haath roke rakkho aur namaaz qaayem karo, zakaat adaa karo (to be-chain ho gaye) aur jab jehaad waajib kar diya gaya to ek giroh logo (dushmano) se is qadr darta tha jaise Khuda se darta ho ya us se bhi kuch zeyada aur yeh kehte hai keh Khudaaya itni jaldi kyu’n jehaad waajib kar diya kaash thodi muddat tak aur taal diya jaata Paighambar aap kahe dijiye keh duniya ka sarmaya bohot thoda hai aur aakherat sahebaan-e-taqwa ke liye behtareen jagah hai aur tum par dhaagha baraabar bhi zulm nahi kiya jaayega.

78. Tum jahan bhi rahoge maut tumhe paalegi chahe mustahkam (mazboot) qilo (fort) mein kyu’n na bandh ho jaawo. Un logo ka haal yeh hai ke achche haalaat paiyda hote hai to kehte hai keh yeh Khuda ki taraf se hai aur museebat aati hai to kehte hai keh yeh aap ki taraf se hai to aap kahe dijiye keh sab Khuda ki taraf se hai phir aakhir is qaum ko kya ho gaya hai keh yeh koi baat samajh-ti hi nahi hai.

79. Tum tak jo bhi achhaayi aur kaam-yaabi pohonchi hai woh Allah ki taraf se hai aur jo bhi buraayi pohonchi hai woh khud tumhaari taraf se hai... Aur ay Paighambar ham ne aap ko logo ke liye Rasool banaya hai aur Khuda gawahi ke liye kaafi hai.

80. Jo Rasool ki ita’at karega us ne Allah ki ita’at ki aur jo mooh moud lega to ham ne aap ko uska zimme-daar bana kar nahi bheja hai.

81. Aur yeh log pehle ita’at ki baat karte hai. Phir jab aap ke paas se bahaar nikaalte hai to ek giroh apne qaul ke khilaaf tadbeere karta hai aur Khuda unki un baato ko likh raha hai. Aap un se earaaz kare (unke haal par chhor de) aur Khuda par bharosa kare aur Khuda is zimme-daari ke liye kaafi hai.

82. Kya yeh log Qur’an mein ghaur-o-fikr nahi karte hai keh agar woh ghair-e-Khuda ki taraf se hota to is mein bada ikhtelaaf hota.

83. Aur jab unke paas aman ya khauf ki khabar aati hai to fauran nashr kar dete hai haala keh agar Rasool aur sahebaan-e-amr ki taraf palta dete to un se istefaada karne waale haqeeqat haal ka ilm paiyda kar lete aur agar tum logo par Khuda ka fazl aur uski rehmat na hoti to chand afraad ke alaawa sab shaitaan ka itteba kar lete.

84. Ab aap raahe Khuda mein jehaad kare aur aap apne nafs ke alaawa dusro ke mukallaf (zimmedar) nahi hai aur momenin ko jehaad par amaada kare. An-qareeb Khuda kufr ke shar ko rok dega aur Allah intehaai taaqat waala aur sakht saza dene waala hai.

85. Jo shakhs achchi sifarish karega usey uska hissa milega aur jo buri sifarish karega usey us mein se hissa milega aur Allah har shaiy par iqtedaar rakhne waala hai.

86. Aur jab tum logo ko koi tohfa (salaam) pesh kiya jaaye to us se behtar ya kam se kam waisa hi waapas karo keh be-shak Allah har shaiy ka hisaab karne waala hai.

87. Allah woh Khuda hai jiske alaawa koi maabood nahi hai woh tum sab ko roz-e-qayamat jama karega aur is mein koi shak nahi hai aur Allah se zeyada sachchi baat kaun karne waala hai.

88. Aakhir tumhe kiya ho gaya hai keh munafeqeen ke baare mein do giroh ho gaye ho jab-keh Allah ne unke aamaal ki bina par unhe ulat diya hai kya tum usey hidaayat dena chahte ho Khuda ne jise gumraahi par chhod diya hai haala keh jise Khuda gumraahi mein chhod de uske liye tum koi raasta nahi nikaal sakte.

89. Yeh munafeqeen chahte hai keh tum bhi unki tarah kaafir ho jaawo aur sab baraabar ho jaaye to khabar-daar! Tum unhe apna dost na banaana jab tak raahe Khuda mein hijrat na kare phir yeh inheraaf kare to inhe ghiraf-taar kar lo aur jahan pa jaawo qatl kar do aur khabar daar un mein se kisi ko apna dost aur madad-gaar na banana.

90. Alaawa unke jo kisi aisi qaum se mil jaaye jin ke aur tumhaare darmiyaan mohaaida (agreement) ho ya woh tumhaare paas dil-tangh ho kar aa jaaye keh na tum se jangh karege aur na apni qaum se aur agar Khuda chahta to un ko tumhaare upar musallat kar deta aur woh tum se bhi jangh karte lehaaza agar tum se alag rahe aur jangh na kare aur sulh ka paighaam de to Khuda ne tumhaare liye unke upar koi raah nahi qaraar di hai.

91. An-qareeb tum ek aur jamaat ko paawoge jo chahte hai keh tum se bhi mehfooz rahe aur apni qaum se bhi. Yeh jab bhi fitna ki taraf bulaaye jaate hai ulte us mein ondhe mooh gir padte hai lehaaza yeh agar tum se alag na ho aur sulh ka paighaam na de aur haath na rokey to unhe ghiraf-taar kar lo aur jahan paao qatl kar do yahi woh hai keh jin par tumhe khula ghalba ataa kiya gaya hai.

92. Aur kisi momin ko yeh haq nahi hai keh woh kisi momin ko qatl kar de magar galti se aur jo galti se qatl kar de usey chaahiye ke ek ghulam azaad kare aur maqtool (slain) ke waariso ko diyyat de magar yeh ke woh maaf kar de phir agar maqtool (slain) aisi qaum se hai jo tumhaari dushman hai aur (ittafaq se) khud momin hai to sirf ghulam azaad karna hoga aur agar aisi qaum ka fard hai jiska tum se mohaaida (agreement) hai to uske ahel ko diyyat dena padegi aur ek momin ghulam azaad karna hoga aur ghulam na miley to do mah (2 months) ke musalsal roze rakhna hoge yahi Allah ki taraf se tauba ka raasta hai aur Allah sab ki niyyato se ba-khabar hai aur apne ehkaam mein saheb-e-hikamat bhi hai.

93. Aur jo bhi kisi momin ko qasdan (jaan-bujh kar) qatl kar dega uski jaza Jahannam hai usi mein hamesha rehna hai aur us par Khuda ka ghazab bhi hai aur Khuda laanat bhi karta hai aur us ne iske liye azaab-e-azeem bhi mohayya (tayyar) kar rakkha hai.

94. Imaan waalo jab tum raahe Khuda mein jehaad ke liye safar karo to pehle tehqeeq kar lo aur khabar-daar! Jo Islaam ki pesh-kaash (izhaar) kare us se yeh na kehna ke tu momin nahi hai ke is tarah tum zindagaani-e-duniya ka chand roza sarmaya chahte ho aur Khuda ke paas ba-kasrat fawaaeed paaye jaate hai aakhir tum bhi to pehle aise hi kaafir thay Khuda ne tum par ehsaan kiya keh tumhaare Islaam ko qabool kar liya (aur dil cheerne ki shart nahi lagaayi) to ab tum bhi iqdaam se pehle tahqeeq karo ke Khuda tumhaare aamaal se khoob ba-khabar hai.

95. Andhe bimaar aur maazoor afraad ke alaawa ghar baithay rehne waale sahebaan-e-imaan harghiz un logo ke baraabar nahi ho sakte jo raahe Khuda mein apne jaan-o-maal se jehaad karne waale hai. Allah ne apne maal aur jaan se jehaad karne waalo ko baithay rehne waalo par imtiyaz inaayat kiye hai aur har ek se neki ka waada kiya hai aur mujahedeen ko baithay rehne waalo ke muqaable mein ajre azeem ataa kiya hai.

96. Uski taraf se darajaat, maghferat aur rehmat hai aur woh bada bakhshne waala aur maher-baan hai.

97. Jin logo ko malaayeka ne is haal mein uthaaya keh woh apne nafs par zulm karne waale thay un se poochha keh tum kis haal mein thay? Unho ne kaha keh ham zameen mein kamzor bana diye gaye thay. Malaayeka ne kaha ke kya zameene Khuda waseea nahi thi ke tum hijrat kar jaate un logo ka thikaana Jahannam hai aur woh bad-tareen manzil hai.

98. Alaawa un kamzor mardo, aurto’n aur bachcho ke jin ke ikhtiyaar mein koi tadbeer na thi aur woh koi raasta na nikaal sakte thay.

99. Yahi woh log hai jinko an-qareeb Khuda maaf kar dega keh woh bada maaf karne waala aur bakhshne waala hai.

100. Aur jo bhi raahe Khuda mein hijrat karega woh zameen mein bohot se thikaane aur wus’at paayega aur jo apne ghar se Khuda-o-Rasool ki taraf hijrat ke iraade se niklega uske baad usey maut bhi aa jaayega to uska ajr Allah ke zimme hai aur Allah bada bakhshne waala maher-baan hai.

101. Aur jab tum zameen mein safar karo to tumhaare liye koi harj nahi hai ke apni namaaze qasr kar do agar tumhe kuffaar ke hamla kar dene ka khauf hai ke kuffaar tumhaare liye khule huwe dushman hai.

102. Aur jab aap mujahedeen ke darmiyaan ho aur unke liye namaaz qaayem kare to unki ek jamaat aap ke saath namaaz padhe aur apne aslaha (arms) saath rakkhe iske baad jab yeh sajdah kar chuke to yeh pusht-panah ban jaaye aur dusri jamaat jisne namaaz nahi padhi hai woh aa kar shareek-e-namaaz ho jaaye aur apne aslaha aur bachao ke saamaan apne saath rakkhe kuffaar ki khwahish yahi hai keh tum apne saaz-o-saamaan aur aslaha se ghaafil ho jaawo to yeh yak-baragi (achanak) hamla kar de... Haa’n agar baarish ya bimaari ki wajah se aslaha na utha sakte ho to koi harj nahi hai keh aslaha rakh do lekin bachao ka saamaan saath rakkho. Allah ne kufr karne waalo ke liye ruswa-kun azaab mohayya (tayyar) kar rakkha hai.

103. Is ke baad jab yeh namaaz tamaam ho jaaye to khade, baithe, leyte hamesha Khuda ko yaad karte raho aur jab itminaan haasil ho jaaye to ba-qaida namaaz qaayem karo ke namaaz sahebaan-e-imaan ke liye ek waqte mo’ayyan ke saath fareeza hai.

104. Aur khabar-daar! dushmano ka peecha karne mein susti se kaam na lena keh agar tumhe koi bhi ranj pohonchana hai to tumhaari tarah kufr ko bhi takleef pohonchti hai aur tum Allah se woh ummeede rakhte ho jo inhe haasil nahi hai aur Allah har ek ki niyat ka jaanne waala aur saheb-e-hikmat hai.

105. Hum ne aap ki taraf yeh bar-haq kitaab naazil ki hai keh logo ke darmiyaan hukm-e-Khuda ke mutaabiq faisla kare aur khayaanat-kaaro ke taraf-daar na bane.

106. Aur Allah se isteghfaar kijiye keh Allah bada Ghafoor-o-Raheem hai.

107. Aur khabar-daar! Jo log khud apne nafs se khayaanat karte hai unki taraf se difa na kijiyega keh Khuda khayaanat-kaar mujreemo ko harghiz dost nahi rakhta hai.

108. Yeh log insaano ki nazro se apne ko chhupaate hai aur Khuda se nahi chhup sakte hai jab-keh woh us waqt bhi unke saath rehta hai jab woh na-pasandeedah baato ki saazish karte hai aur Khuda unke tamaam aamaal ka ehaata (cover) kiye huwe hai.

109. Hoshiyar! Us waqt tum ne zindagaani-e-duniya mein unki taraf se jaghda shuru bhi kar diya to ab roz-e-qayamat unki taraf se Allah se kaun jaghda karega aur kaun unka vakil aur taraf-daar banega.

110. Aur jo bhi kisi ke saath buraayi karega ya apne nafs par zulm karega uske baad isteghfaar karega to Khuda ko Ghafoor aur Raheem paayega.

111. Aur jo qasdan (jaan-bujh kar) gunah karta hai woh apne hi khilaaf karta hai aur Khuda sab ka jaanne waala hai aur saheb-e-hikmat bhi hai.

112. Aur jo shakhs bhi koi galti ya gunah kar ke dusre be-gunah ke sar daal deta hai woh bohot badhe bohtaan aur khule gunah ka zimme-daar hota hai.

113. Agar aap par fazle Khuda aur rehmate Parwardigaar ka saaya na hota to unki ek jamaat ne aap ko behkaane ka iraada kar liya tha aur yeh apne alaawa kisi ko gumraah nahi kar sakte aur aap ko koi takleef nahi pohoncha sakte aur Allah ne aap par kitaab aur hikmat naazil ki hai aur aap ko un tamaam baato ka ilm de diya hai jin ka ilm na tha aur aap par Khuda ka bohot bada fazl hai.

114. Un logo ki aksar raaz ki baato mein koi khair nahi hai magar woh shakhs jo kisi sadqa, kaar-e-khair ya logo ke darmiyaan islaah ka hukm de aur jo bhi yeh saare kaam raza-e-ilaahi ki talab mein anjaam dega ham usey ajre azeem ataa karege.

115. Aur jo shakhs bhi hidaayat ke waazeh ho jaane ke baad Rasool se ikhtelaaf karega aur momenin ke raaste ke alaawa koi dusra raasta ikhtiyaar karega usey ham udhar hi pher dege jidhar woh phir gaye hai aur Jahannam mein jhonk dege jo bad-tareen thikaana hai.

116. Khuda is baat ko maaf nahi kar sakta keh uska shareek qaraar diya jaaye aur uske alaawa jis ko chahe bakhsh sakta hai aur jo Khuda ka shareek qaraar dega woh gumraahi mein bohot dur tak chala gaya hai.

117. Yeh log Khuda ko chhod kar bas aurto’n ki parastish karte hai aur sarkash (rebellious) shaitaan ko aawaaz dete hai.

118. Jis par Khuda ki laanat hai aur us ne Khuda se bhi kahe diya keh mai Tere bando mein se ek muqarrar hissa zaroor le lu ga.

4 | NISAA | 176

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. O people! be careful of (your duty to) your Lord, Who created you from a single being and created its mate of the same (kind) and spread from these two, many men and women; and be careful of (your duty to) Allah, by Whom you demand one of another (your rights), and (to) the ties of relationship; surely Allah ever watches over you.

2. And give to the orphans their property, and do not substitute worthless (things) for (their) good (ones), and do not devour their property (as an addition) to your own property; this is surely a great crime.

3. And if you fear that you cannot act equitably towards orphans, then marry such women as seem good to you, two and three and four; but if you fear that you will not do justice (between them), then (marry) only one or what your right hands possess; this is more proper, that you may not deviate from the right course.

4. And give women their dowries as a free gift, but if they of themselves be pleased to give up to you a portion of it, then eat it with enjoyment and with wholesome result.

5. And do not give away your property which Allah has made for you a (means of) support to the weak of understanding, and maintain them out of (the profits of) it, and clothe them and speak to them words of honest advice.

6. And test the orphans until they attain puberty; then if you find in them maturity of intellect, make over to them their property, and do not consume it extravagantly and hastily, lest they attain to full age; and whoever is rich, let him abstain altogether, and whoever is poor, let him eat reasonably; then when you make over to them their property, call witnesses in their presence; and Allah is enough as a Reckoner.

7. Men shall have a portion of what the parents and the near relatives leave, and women shall have a portion of what the parents and the near relatives leave, whether there is little or much of it; a stated portion.

8. And when there are present at the division the relatives and the orphans and the needy, give them (something) out of it and speak to them kind words.

9. And let those fear who, should they leave behind them weakly offspring, would fear on their account, so let them be careful of (their duty to) Allah, and let them speak right words.

10. (As for) those who swallow the property of the orphans unjustly, surely they only swallow fire into their bellies and they shall enter burning fire.

11. Allah enjoins you concerning your children: The male shall have the equal of the portion of two females; then if they are more than two females, they shall have two-thirds of what the deceased has left, and if there is one, she shall have the half; and as for his parents, each of them shall have the sixth of what he has left if he has a child, but if he has no child and (only) his two parents inherit him, then his mother shall have the third; but if he has brothers, then his mother shall have the sixth after (the payment of) a bequest he may have bequeathed or a debt; your parents and your children, you know not which of them is the nearer to you in usefulness; this is an ordinance from Allah: Surely Allah is Knowing, Wise.

12. And you shall have half of what your wives leave if they have no child, but if they have a child, then you shall have a fourth of what they leave after (payment of) any bequest they may have bequeathed or a debt; and they shall have the fourth of what you leave if you have no child, but if you have a child then they shall have the eighth of what you leave after (payment of) a bequest you may have bequeathed or a debt; and if a man or a woman leaves property to be inherited by neither parents nor offspring, and he (or she) has a brother or a sister, then each of them two shall have the sixth, but if they are more than that, they shall be sharers in the third after (payment of) any bequest that may have been bequeathed or a debt that does not harm (others); this is an ordinance from Allah: and Allah is Knowing, Forbearing.

13. These are Allah’s limits, and whoever obeys Allah and His Apostle, He will cause him to enter gardens beneath which rivers flow, to abide in them; and this is the great achievement.

14. And whoever disobeys Allah and His Apostle and goes beyond His limits, He will cause him to enter fire to abide in it, and he shall have an abasing chastisement.

15. And as for those who are guilty of an indecency from among your women, call to witnesses against them four (witnesses) from among you; then if they bear witness, confine them to the houses until death takes them away or Allah opens some way for them.

16. And as for the two who are guilty of indecency from among you, give them both a punishment; then if they repent and amend, turn aside from them; surely Allah is Oft-returning (to mercy), the Merciful.

17. Repentance with Allah is only for those who do evil in ignorance, then turn (to Allah) soon, so these it is to whom Allah turns (mercifully), and Allah is ever Knowing, Wise.

18. And repentance is not for those who go on doing evil deeds, until when death comes to one of them, he says: Surely now I repent; nor (for) those who die while they are unbelievers. These are they for whom We have prepared a painful chastisement.

19. O you who believe! it is not lawful for you that you should take women as heritage against (their) will, and do not straiten them in order that you may take part of what you have given them, unless they are guilty of manifest indecency, and treat them kindly; then if you hate them, it may be that you dislike a thing while Allah has placed abundant good in it.

20. And if you wish to have (one) wife in place of another and you have given one of them a heap of gold, then take not from it anything; would you take it by slandering (her) and (doing her) manifest wrong?

21. And how can you take it when one of you has already gone in to the other and they have made with you a firm covenant?

22. And marry not woman whom your fathers married, except what has already passed; this surely is indecent and hateful, and it is an evil way.

23. Forbidden to you are your mothers and your daughters and your sisters and your paternal aunts and your maternal aunts and brothers’ daughters and sisters’ daughters and your mothers that have suckled you and your foster-sisters and mothers of your wives and your step-daughters who are in your guardianship, (born) of your wives to whom you have gone in, but if you have not gone in to them, there is no blame on you (in marrying them), and the wives of your sons who are of your own loins and that you should have two sisters together, except what has already passed; surely Allah is Forgiving, Merciful.

24. And all married women except those whom your right hands possess (this is) Allah’s ordinance to you, and lawful for you are (all women) besides those, provided that you seek (them) with your property, taking (them) in marriage not committing fornication. Then as to those whom you profit by, give them their dowries as appointed; and there is no blame on you about what you mutually agree after what is appointed; surely Allah is Knowing, Wise.

25. And whoever among you has not within his power ampleness of means to marry free believing women, then (he may marry) of those whom your right hands possess from among your believing maidens; and Allah knows best your faith: you are (sprung) the one from the other; so marry them with the permission of their masters, and give them their dowries justly, they being chaste, not fornicating, nor receiving paramours; and when they are taken in marriage, then if they are guilty of indecency, they shall suffer half the punishment which is (inflicted) upon free women. This is for him among you who fears falling into evil; and that you abstain is better for you, and Allah is Forgiving, Merciful.

26. Allah desires to explain to you, and to guide you into the ways of those before you, and to turn to you (mercifully), and Allah is Knowing, Wise.

27. And Allah desires that He should turn to you (mercifully), and those who follow (their) lusts desire that you should deviate (with) a great deviation.

28. Allah desires that He should make light your burdens, and man is created weak.

29. O you who believe! do not devour your property among yourselves falsely, except that it be trading by your mutual consent; and do not kill your people; surely Allah is Merciful to you.

30. And whoever does this aggressively and unjustly, We will soon cast him into fire; and this is easy to Allah.

31. If you shun the great sins which you are forbidden, We will do away with your small sins and cause you to enter an honorable place of entering.

32. And do not covet that by which Allah has made some of you excel others; men shall have the benefit of what they earn and women shall have the benefit of what they earn; and ask Allah of His grace; surely Allah knows all things.

33. And to every one We have appointed heirs of what parents and near relatives leave; and as to those with whom your rights hands have ratified agreements, give them their portion; surely Allah is a witness over all things.

34. Men are the maintainers of women because Allah has made some of them to excel others and because they spend out of their property; the good women are therefore obedient, guarding the unseen as Allah has guarded; and (as to) those on whose part you fear desertion, admonish them, and leave them alone in the sleeping-places and beat them; then if they obey you, do not seek a way against them; surely Allah is High, Great.
