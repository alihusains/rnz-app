بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اِلٰہِیْ عَظُمَ الْبَلَآئُ وَبَرِحَ الْخَفَآئُ، وَانْکَشَفَ الْغِطآئُ وَانْقَطَعَ الرَّجآئُ وَضآقَتِ الْارْضُ وَمُنِعَتِ السَّمآئُ، وَاَنْتَ الْمُسْتَعآنُ وَاِلَیْکَ الْمُشْتَکٰی وَعَلَیْکَ الْمُعَوَّلُ فِی الشِّدَّۃِ وَالرَّخآئِ۔ اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ اُوْلِی الْاَمْرِ الَّذِیْنَ فَرَضْتَ عَلَیْنَا طَاعَتَھُمْ وَعَرَّفْتَنَا بِذٰلِکَ مَنْزِلَتَھُمْ فَفَرِّجْ عَنَّا بِحَقِّهِمْ فَرَجًا عَاجِلًا قَرِیْبًا کَلَمْحِ الْبَصَرِ اَوْھُوَ اَقْرَبُ یَامُحَمَّدُ یَاعَلِیُّ، یَاعَلِیُّ یَامُحَمَّدُ، اِکْفِیَانِیْ فَاِنَّکُمَا کَافِیَانِ، وَانْصُرَانِیْ فَاِنَّکُمَا نَاصِرَانِ یَامَوْلَانَا یَاصَاحِبَ الزَّمَانِ، اَلْغَوْثَ اَلْغَوْثَ اَلْغَوْثَ اَدْرِکْنِیْ اَدْرِکْنِیْ اَدْرِکْنِیْ اَلسَّاعَۃَ اَلسَّاعَۃَ اَلسَّاعَۃَ، اَلْعَجَلَ اَلْعَجَلَ اَلْعَجَلَ، یَآاَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِیْنَ بِحَقِّ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِہِ الطَّاھِرِیْنَ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

FARAJ
ILLAHI AZOMAL

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Mere Ma’bood! Museebat badh gayi hai, chhupi huwi baat khul gayi hai, parda phaash (open) ho gaya hai, ummeed toot gayi hai, zameen tangh ho gayi hai, aur aasmaan ne rukaawat daal di hai, Ab Tu hi madad-gaar hai aur Tujh se hi shikaayat ho sakti hai aur tanghi-o-aasaani mein sirf Tu hi sahara (madadgaar) ban sakta hai.

Ay Ma’bood! Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma jo sahebaane Amr hai. Wahi hai jin ki ita’at Tu ne ham par farz (waajib) ki hai aur is tarah hamein unke martabe ki pehchaan karaayi hai. Pas unke sadqe mein hamein aasoodgi (takleefo se najaat) ataa farma, jald-tar, nazdeek-tar (jaldi aur bohot jaldi) goya palak jhapakne (aankh jhapakne ki miqdaar) ya us se bhi pehle.

Ya Mohammado (s.a.w.a) Ya Ali (a.s) Ya Alio (a.s) Ya Mohammad (s.a.w.a) meri sarprasti (guidance) farmaiye, keh aap dono hi kaafi hai. Meri madad farmaiye keh aap dono hi mere madad-gaar hai. Ay hamaare aaqa, ay Sahebaz Zamaan (a.t.f.s) faryaad hai, faryaad hai, faryaad hai. Meri madad kijiye, meri madad kijiye, meri madad kijiye, isi waqt, isi lamhe, isi ghadi, jald-tar, jald-az-jald, jaldi-se-jaldi.

Ay sab se ziyaada rahem karne waale Tujhe waasta hai Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unke pakeeza-tareen Aal ka (Aameen).

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

FARAJ
ILLAHI AZOMAL

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

My Allah, irretrievable is our plight, our helplessness causes pain and trouble, (now our) urgency has been laid bare (before Thee), (all) hopes have been cut off, the (plentiful) earth has shrunk (with very little to spare) for us the heavenly blessings have been withheld, we call upon Thee for help, we refer our grief and sorrow to Thee, we have full faith in Thee, in the time of distress as well as in good fortune.

O Allah, send blessings on Muhammad and on the progeny of Muhammad, the Ulil Amr (authority) obedience to whom has been made obligatory, through which their high status has been made known. Therefore let there be joy after sorrow for us for their sake, right away, in the twinkle of an eye more rapidly.

O Muhammad O Ali, O Ali O Muhammad, Give me enough because both of you provide sufficiently and help me because both of you help and protect. O Our Master O the Authority of our times (Saahibaz Zamaan) Help! Help! Help! Come to my help, Come to my help, Come to my help, in this hour, in this hour, in this hour, Be quick, Be quick, Be quick, O the most Merciful! For the sake of Muhammad and his pure progeny.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
