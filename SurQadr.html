Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Be-shak Ham ne usey shab-e-qadr mein naazil kiya hai.

2. Aur aap kya jaane yeh shab-e-qadr kya cheez hai.

3. Shab-e-qadr hazaar mahino se behtar hai.

4. Us mein malaayeka aur roohul quds izne Khuda ke saath tamaam umoor ko le kar naazil hote hai.

5. Yeh raat tulu-e-fajr tak salaamti hi salaamti hai.

97 | QADR | 5

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Surely We revealed it on the grand night.

2. And what will make you comprehend what the grand night is?

3. The grand night is better than a thousand months.

4. The angels and the Spirit descend in it by the permission of their Lord for every affair,

5. Peace! it is till the break of the morning.
