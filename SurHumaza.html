104 | HUMAZA | 9

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Tabaahi aur barbaadi hai har taana-zan aur chugal-khoor ke liye.

2. Jisne maal ko jama kiya aur khoob uska hisaab rakkha.

3. Us ka khayaal tha keh yeh maal usey hamesha baaqi rakkhega.

4. Hargiz nahi usey yaqeenan haata mein daal diya jaayega.

5. Aur tum kya jaano ke hatam kya shaiy hai.

6. Yeh Allah ki bhadkaayi huwi aag hai.

7. Jo dilo tak chadh-ti (aar paar) jaayegi.

8. Woh aag unke upar gira di jaayegi.

9. Lambe-lambe sutoono (pillars) ke saath.

104 | HUMAZA | 9

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Woe to every slanderer, defamer,

2. Who amasses wealth and considers it a provision (against mishap);

3. He thinks that his wealth will make him immortal.

4. Nay! he shall most certainly be hurled into the crushing disaster,

5. And what will make you realize what the crushing disaster is?

6. It is the fire kindled by Allah,

7. Which rises above the hearts.

8. Surely it shall be closed over upon them,

9. In extended columns.
