AZAAN-IQAMAH

Azaan and Iqamah is only for obligatory namaz and not for mustahab namaz. It is recommended that Azaan be pronounced in the right ear of the child, and Iqamah in its left ear, on the day it is born or before the umbilical cord is cast off.

Azaan aur Iqamah sirf waajib namazo’n ke liye hai aur mustahab namazo’n ke liye nahi hai. Taqeed hai ke bachche ki wiladat par, bachche ke daaye’n kaan (right ear) mein Azaan aur baaye’n kaan (left ear) mein Iqamah de.
