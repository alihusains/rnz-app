SEMAAT

Shaykh Kafami says that this dua contains Ismi Azam (the great name of Allah). Imam Baqir (a.s) advised the faithfuls to recite this dua as it brings the downfall of the enemies of Ahlul Bayt. Imam Sadiq (a.s) said that Allah (s.w.t) taught this dua to prophet Musa (a.s) to overcome his enemies. This dua is particularly recited in the closing hours before sunset on Friday. Also recite these words just before sunset…

سُبْحَانَكَ لَا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ يَاحَنَّانُ يَامَنَّانُ يَابَدِيْعَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْاَرْضِ يَاذَا الْجَلَالِ وَالْاِكْرَامِ۔

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰہُمَّ اِنِّیْ اَسْئَلُکَ بِاسْمِکَ الْعَظِیْمِ الْاَعْظْمِ الْاَعَزِّ الْاَجَلِّ الْاَکْرَمِ الَّذِیْٓ اِذَا دُعِیْتَ بِہٖ عَلٰی مَغَالِقِ اَبْوَابِ السَّمَآئِ لِلْفَتْحِ بِالرَّحْمَۃِ انْفَتَحْتَ وَاِذَا دُعِیْتَ بِہٖ عَلٰی مَضَآئِیْقِ اَبْوَابِ الْاَرْضِ لِلْفَرَجِ انْفَرَجَتْ وَاِذَا دُعِیْتَ بِہٖ عَلَی الْعُسْرِ لِلْیُسْرِ تَیَسَّرَتْ وَاِذَا دُعِیْتَ بِہٖ عَلَی الْاَمْوَاتِ لِلنُّشُوْرِ انْتَشَرَتْ وَاِذَا دُعِیْتَ بِہٖ عَلٰی کَشْفِ الْبَاسَآئِ وَالضَّرَّآئِ انْکَشَفَتْ وَبِجَلَالِ وَجْھِکَ الْکَرِیْمِ اَکْرَمِ الْوُجُوْہِ وَاَعَزِّ الْوُجُوْہِ الَّذِیْ عَنَتْ لَہُ الْوُجُوْہُ وَخَضَعَتْ لَہُ الرِّقَابُ وَخَشَعَتْ لَہٗ الْاَصْوَاتُ وَوَجِلَتْ لَہُ الْقُلُوْبُ مِنْ مَخَافَتِکَ وَبِقُوَّتِکَ الَّتِیْ بِھَا تُمْسِکُ السَّمَآئَ اَنْ تَقَعَ عَلَی الْاَرْضِ اِلَّا بِاِذْنِکَ وَتُمْسِکُ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضَ اَنْ تَزُوْلَا وَبِمَشِیَّتِکَ الَّتِیْ دَانَ لَھَا الْعَالَمُوْنَ وَبِکَلِمَتِکَ الَّتِیْ خَلَقْتَ بِھَا السَّمٰوٰاتِ وَالْاَرْضَ وَبِحِکْمَتِکَ الَّتِیْ صَنَعَتَ بِھَا الْعَجَٓائِبَ وَخَلَقْتَ بِھَا الظُّلْمَۃَ وَجَعَلْتَھَا لَیْلًا وَّجَعَلْتَ اللَّیْلَ سَکَنًا وَّخَلَقْتَ بِھَا النُّوْرَ وَجَعَلْتَہٗ نَھَارًا وَّجَعَلْتَ النَّھَارَ نُشُوْرًا مُّبْصِرًا وَّخَلَقْتَ بِھَا الشَّمْسَ وَجَعَلْتَ الشَّمْسَ ضِیَآئً وَّخَلَقْتَ بِھَا الْقَمَرَ وَجَعَلْتَ الْقَمَرَ نُوْرًا وَّخَلَقْتَ بِھَا الْکَوَاکِبَ وَجَعَلْتَھَا نُجُوْمًا وَّبُرُوْجًا وَّمَصَابِیْحَ وَزِیْنَۃً وَّرُجُوْمًا وَّجَعَلْتَ لَہَا مَشَارِقَ وَمَغَارِبَ وَجَعَلْتَ لَہَا مَطَالِعَ وَمَجَادِیَ وَجَعَلْتَ لَھَا فَلَکًا وَّمَسَابِحَ وَقَدَّرْ تَھَا فِیْ السَّمَآئِ مَنَازِلَ فَاَحْسَنْتَ تَقْدِیْرَھَا وَصَوَّرْتَھَا فَاَحْسَنْتَ تَصْوِیْرَھَا وَاَحْصَیْتَہَا بِاَسْمَآئِکَ اِحْصَآئً وَّدَبَّرْتَہَا بِحِکْمَتِکَ تَدْبِیْرًا وَّاَحْسَنْتَ تَدْبِیْرَھَا وَسَخَّرْتَھَا بِسُلْطَانِ اللَّیْلِ وَسُلْطَانِ النَّھَارِ وَالسَّاعَاتِ وَعَدَدَ السِّنِیْنَ وَالْحِسَابِ وَجَعَلْتَ رُوْیَتَھَا لِجَمِیْعِ النَّاسِ مَرْیًٔ وَّاحِدً وَّاَسْئَلُکَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ بِمَجْدِکَ الَّذِیْ کَلَّمْتَ بِہٖ عَبْدَکَ وَرَسُوْلَکَ مُوْسَی بْنَ عِمْرَانَ عَلَیْہِ السَّلَامُ فِی الْمُقَدَّ سِیْنَ فَوْقَ اِحْسَاسِ الْکَرُّوْبِیْنَ فَوْقَ غَمَآئِمِ النُّوْرِ فَوْقَ تَابُوْتِ الشَّھَادَۃِ فِیْ عَمُوْدِ النَّارِ وَفِیْ طُوْرِ سَیْنَآئَ وَفِیْ جَبَلِ حُوْرِیْثَ فِی الْوَادِ الْمُقَدَّسِ فِی الْبُقْعَۃِ الْمُبَارَکَۃِ مِنْ جَانِبِ الطُّوْرِ الْاَیْمَنِ مِنَ الشَّجَرَۃِ وَفِیْ اَرْضِ مِصْرَ بِتِسْعِ اٰیَاتٍ بَیِّنَاتٍ وَّیَوْمَ فَرَقْتَ لِبَنِیْٓ اِسْرَٓائِیْلَ الْبَحْرَ وَفِی الْمُنْبَجِسَاتِ الَّتِیْ صَنَعْتَ بِھَا الْعَجَآیِٔبَ فِیْ بَحْرِ سُوْفٍ وَّعَقَدْتَ مَآئَ الْبَحْرِ فِیْ قَلْبِ الْغَمْرِ کَالْحِجَارَۃِ وَجَاوَزْتَ بِبَنِیْٓ اِسْرَآئِیْلَ الْبَحْرَ وَتَمَّتْ کَلِمَتُکَ الْحُسْنٰی عَلَیْھِمْ بِمَا صَبَرُوْا وَاَوْرَثْتَھُمْ مَشَارِقَ الْاَرْضِ وَمَغَارِبَھَا الَّتِیْ بَارَکْتَ فِیْہَا لِلْعٰلَمِیْنَ وَاَغْرَقْتَ فِرْعَوْنَ وَجُنُوْدَہٗ وَمَرَاکِبَہٗ فِی الْیَمِّ وَبِاسْمِکَ الْعَظِیْمِ الْاَعْظَمِ الْاَعَزِّ الْاَجَلِّ الْاَ کْرَمِ وَبِمَجْدِکَ الَّذِیْ تَجَلَّیْتَ بِہٖ لِمُوْسٰی کَلِیْمِکَ عَلَیْہِ السَّلَامُ فِیْ طُوْرِ سَیْنَآئَ وَلِاِبْرَاھِیْمَ عَلَیْہِ السَّلَامُ خَلِیْلِکَ مِنْ قَبْلُ فِیْ مَسْجِدِ الْخَیْفِ وَلِاِسْحٰقَ صَفِیِّکَ عَلَیْہِ السَّلَامُ فِیْ بِئْرِ شِیَعٍ وَّلِیَعْقُوْبَ نَبِیِّکَ عَلَیْہِ السَّلَامُ فِیْ بَیْتِ اِیْلٍ وَّاَوْفَیْتَ لِاِبْرَاھِیْمَ عَلَیْہِ السَّلَامُ بِمِیْثَاقِکَ وَلِاِسْحٰقَ بِحَلَفِکَ وَلِیَعْقُوْبَ بِشَھَادَتِکَ وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ بِوَعْدِکَ وَلِلدَّاعِیْنَ بِاَسْمَآئِکَ فَاَجَبْتَ وَبِمَجْدِکَ الَّذِیْ ظَھَرَ لِمُوْسَی بْنِ عِمْرَانَ عَلَیْہِ السَّلَامُ عَلٰی قُبَّۃِ الرُّمَّانِ وَبِاٰیٰاتِکَ الَّتِیْ وَقَعَتْ عَلٰی اَرْضِ مِصْرَ بِمَجْدِ الْعِزَّۃِ وَالْغَلَبَۃِ بِاٰیٰاتٍ عَزِیْزَۃٍ وَّبِسُلْطَانِ الْقُوَّۃِ وَبِعِزَّۃِ الْقُدْرَۃِ وَبِشَانِ الْکَلِمَۃِ التَّامَّۃِ وَبِکَلِمَاتِکَ الَّتِیْ تَفَضَّلْتَ بِھَا عَلٰی اَھْلِ السَّمٰوٰاتِ وَالْاَرْضِ وَاَھْلِ الدُّنْیَا وَاَھْلِ الْاٰخِرَۃِ وَبِرَحْمَتِکَ الَّتِیْ مَنَنْتَ بِھَا عَلٰی جَمِیْعِ خَلْقِکَ وَبِاسْتِطَاعَتِکَ الَّتِیْ اَقَمْتَ بِھَا عَلَی الْعَالَمِیْنَ وَبِنُوْرِکَ الَّذِیْ قَدْ خَرَّ مِنْ فَزَعِہٖ طُوْرُ سَیْنَآئَ وَبِعِلْمِکَ وَجَلَالِکَ وَکِبْرِیَآئِکَ وَعِزَّتِکَ وَجَبَرُوْتِکَ الَّتِیْ لَمْ تَسْتَقِلَّھَا الْاَرْضُ وَانْخَفَضَتْ لَھَا السَّمٰوٰاتُ وَانْزَجَرَ لَھَا الْعُمْقُ الْاَکْبَرُ وَرَکَدَتْ لَھَا الْبِحَارُ وَالْاَنْہَارُ وَخَضَعَتْ لَہَا الْجِبَالُ وَسَکَنَتْ لَہَا الْاَرْضُ بِمَنَاکِبِھَا وَاسْتَسْلَمَتْ لَھَا الْخَلَٓائِقُ کُلُّھَا وَخَفَقَتْ لَہَا الرِّیَاحُ فِیْ جَرَیَانِہَا وَخَمِدَتْ لَھَا النِّیْرَانُ فِیْ اَوْطَانِھَا وَبِسُلْطَانِکَ الَّذِیْ عُرِفَتْ لَکَ بِہِ الْغَلَبَۃُ دَھْرَ الدُّھُوْرِ وَحُمِدْتَ بِہٖ فِیْ السَّمٰوٰاتِ وَالْاَرَضِیْنَ وَبِکَلِمَتِکَ کَلِمَۃِ الصِّدْقِ الَّتِیْ سَبَقَتْ لِاَبِیْنَا اٰدَمَ عَلَیْہِ السَّلَامُ ذُرِّیَّتِہٖ بِالرَّحْمَۃِ وَاَسْئَلُکَ بِکَلِمَتِکَ الَّتِیْ غَلَبَتْ کُلَّ شَیْٓءٍ وَّبِنُوْرِ وَجْھِکَ الَّذِیْ تَجَلَّیْتَ بِہٖ لِلْجَبَلِ فَجَعَلَہٗ دَکًّا وَّخَرَّ مُوْسٰی صَعِقًا وَّبِمَجْدِکَ الَّذِیْ ظَھَرَ عَلٰی طُوْرِ سَیْنَآئَ فَکَلَّمْتَ بِہٖ عَبْدَکَ وَرَسُوْلَکَ مُوْسَی بْنَ عِمْرَانَ وَبِطَلْعَتِکَ فِیْ سَاعِیْرَ وَظُہُوْرِکَ فِیْ جَبَلِ فَارَانَ بِرَبَوَاتِ الْمُقَدَّسِیْنَ وَجُنُوْدِ الْمَلَٓائِکَۃِ الصَّآفِّیْنَ وَخُشُوْعِ الْمَلَآئِکَۃِ الْمُسَبِّحِیْنَ وَبِبَرَکَتَکَ الَّتِیْ بَارَکْتَ فِیْہَا عَلٰی اِبْرَاھِیْمَ خَلِیْلِکَ عَلَیْہِ السَّلَامُ فِیْ اُمَّۃِ مُحَمَّدٍ صَلَّی اللہُ عَلَیْہِ وَاٰلِہٖ وَبَارَکْتَ لِاِسْحٰقَ صَفِیِّکَ فِیْ اُمَّۃِ عِیْسٰی عَلَیْہِمَا السَّلَامُ وَبَارَکْتَ لِیَعْقُوْبَ اِسْرَآئِیْلِکَ فِیْ اُمَّۃِ مُوْسٰی عَلَیْھِمَا السَّلَامُ وَبَارَکْتَ لِحَبِیْبِکَ مُحَمَّدٍ صَلَّی اللہُ عَلَیْہِ وَاٰلِہٖ فِیْ عِتْرَتِہٖ وَذُرِّیَّتِہٖ وَاُمَّتِہٖ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ وَکَمَا غِبْنَا عَنْ ذٰلِکَ وَلَمْ نَشْھَدْہُ وَاٰمَنَّا بِہٖ وَلَمْ نَرَہٗ صِدْقًا وَّعَدْلًا اَنْ تُصَلِّیَ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاَنْ تُبَارِکَ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّتَرَحَّمَ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّ اٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ کَاَفْضَلِ مَا صَلَّیْتَ وَبَارَکْتَ وَتَرَحَّمْتَ عَلٰی اِبْرَاھِیْمَ وَاٰلِ اِبْرَاھِیْمَ اِنَّکَ حَمِیْدٌ مَّجِیْدٌ فَعَّالٌ لِّمَا تُرِیْدُ وَاَنْتَ عَلٰی کُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ قَدِیْرٌ۔

You may now beseech Almighty Allah to grant you your personal requests. Then, say the following…
Ab aap Khudawande Aalam se apni haajat talab kare aur phir is tarah kahe…
اَللّٰہُمَّ بِحَقِّ ھٰذَا الدُّعَآئِ وَبِحَقِّ ھٰذِہِ الْاَسْمَآئِ الَّتِیْ لَا یَعْلَمُ تَفْسِیْرَ ھَا وَلَا یَعْلَمُ بَاطِنَھَا غَیْرُکَ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّافْعَلْ بِیْ مَآ اَنْتَ اَھْلُہٗ وَلَا تَفْعَلْ بِیْ مَآ اَنَا اَھْلُہٗ وَاغْفِرْلِیْ مِنْ ذُنُوْبِیْ مَآ تَقَدَّمَ مِنْہَا وَمَا تَاخَّرَ وَوَسِّعْ عَلَیَّ مِنْ حَلَالِ رِزْقِکَ وَاکْفِنِیْ مَؤْنَۃَ اِنْسَانِ سَوْئٍ وَّجَارِ سَوْئٍ وَّقَرِیْنِ سَوْئٍ وَّسُلْطَانِ سَوْئٍ اِنَّکَ عَلٰی مَا تَشَآئُ قَدِیْرٌ وَّبِکُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ عَلِیْمٌ اٰمِیْنَ رَبَّ الْعَالَمِیْنَ۔

SEMAAT

Is dua ko Imam Baqir-o-Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) se riwaayat ki gayi hai. Yeh dua Juma ke din aakhari waqt mein padhna mustahab hai. Mushkilaat aur dushmano se najaat paane ke liye yeh behtareen dua hai…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Ma’bood! Mai Tere azeem, badi azmat waale, bade roshan, badi izzat waale naam ke zariye sawaal karta hu keh jab aasmaan ke bandh darwaaze rehmat ke liye kholna chahe to Tujhe is naam se pukaare to khul jaate hai. Aur jab zameen ke tangh raaste kholne ke liye Tujhe is naam se pukaara jaaye to woh kushada (chaude/broad) ho jaate hai.

Aur jab sakhti ke waqt aasaani ke liye is naam se pukare to aasaani ho jaati hai. Aur jab murdo ko uthaane ke liye Tujhe is naam se pukare to woh uth khade hote hai. Aur jab tanghiya-o-sakhtiya dur karne ke liye Tujhe is naam se pukare to woh dur ho jaati hai. Aur sawaali ho Teri zaate kareem ke jalaal ke zariye jo sab se buzurg zaat hai.

Sab se moazziz (izzat-daar) zaat hai, keh jiske aagey chehre jhukte hai aur uske saamne gardane kham hoti (jhuk jaati) hai. Uske huzoor aawaaze kaapti hai aur jiske khauf se dilo mein larza (kap-kapaahat) taari ho jaati hai. Aur sawaal karta hu Tere us quwwat ke zariye jisse Tu ne aasmaan ko zameen par girne se rok rakkha hai. Magar yeh keh jab Tu usey hukm de.

Aur is aasmaan aur zameen ko roka huwa hai keh khisakh (jagah se hat) na jaaye. Aur Teri us mashiyyat (iraade) ke zariye sawaali hu aalameen (sari jahan) jiske mutthee (fist) mein hai. Tere un kalemaat ke waaste se sawaali hu jisse Tu ne Aasmaano aur zameeno ko paida kiya. Teri us hikmat ke waaste se jisse Tu ne ajaayeb (ajib-o-garib/uniqe) ko banaya aur jisse Tu ne taariki ko khalq kiya aur usey raat (night) qaraar diya aur usey aaraam ke liye makhsoos kiya.

Aur apni hikmat se Tu ne roshni paida ki aur usey din (day) ka naam diya aur din ko jaag uthne (kaam-kaaj) aur dekhne ke liye banaya. Aur Tu ne us se suraj ko paida kiya aur suraj ko roshan kiya. Aur Tu ne us se chaand ko paida kiya aur chaand ko chamak-daar banaya. Aur Tu ne us se sitaaro ko paida kiya unhe bohot bada banaya, unke burj (planet) banaaye aur unhe chiraag banaya aur zeenat banaya, sakht banaya.

Tu ne unke liye mashriq aur maghrib banaaye, Tu ne unke chamakne aur chalne ki raahe banaaye. Tu ne unke liye falak (aasmaan/sky) aur sair (ghumne) ki jagah banaayi. Aur aasmaan mein unki manzile moqarrar ki. Pas Tu ne unka behtareen andaaza thairaya. Aur Tu ne unhe shakl ataa ki kya hi achchhi shakl di. Aur unhe apne naamo ke saath puri tarah shumaar kiya. Aur apni hikmat se unka ek nizam (system) qaayem kiya. Aur khoob tadbeer (phikr/pondering) farmaayi.

Aur raat ke arse (period) aur din ki muddat ke liye mu’ti (obedient) banaya. Aur saato’n (hours) aur saalo (years) ke hisaab ka zariya banaya, aur sab logo ke liye unko dekhna yaksa (ek jaisa) kar diya. Aur sawaal karta hu Tujh se ay Allah Teri us buzurghi ke zariye jisse Tu ne apne bande aur Rasool Hazrat Musa (a.s) se kalaam farmaya. Paak logo mein jo farishto ki samajh se baala (uncha).

Noor ke baadalo se buland, taboot-e-shahadat se uncha, jo aag ke sutoon mein Toore Seina mein Kohe Huris (mount Huris) mein, Waadiye moqaddas (sacred valley) mein, barkat waali zameen mein, Toore Ayman ki taraf ek darakht se jo sar zameen-e-Misr (Egypt) mein paida huwa. Sawaal karta hu nau roshan mojezo (miracles) ke waaste se, aur us din ke waaste se keh jis din Tu ne Bani Israeel ke liye dariya mein raasta banaya.

Aur un chashmo mein jo patthar se jaari huwe keh jin ke zariye Tu ne ajeeb mojezaat (miracle) ko Dariya-e-Soof mein zaahir kiya. Aur Tu ne dariya ke paani ko bhawar (whril) ke darmiyaan pattharo ke maanind jakad ke rakh diya. Aur Tu ne Bani Israeel ko dariya se guzaar diya. Aur unke baare mein Tera behtareen mo’aheda (agreement) pura huwa. Jab unho-ne sabr kiya aur Tu ne unko zameen ke mashriqo aur maghribo ka maalik banaya. Jin mein Tu ne aalameen ke liye barkate rakkhi hai.

Aur Tu ne Firon aur uske lashkar ko aur unki sawariyo ko Dariya-e-Neel mein garq (dubo) diya. Aur sawaali hu be-waasta Tere naam ke jo buland-tar izzat waala, roshan buzurghi waala hai. Aur be-waasta Teri shaan keh jo Tu ne apne kaleem Musa (a.s) ke liye Toore Seina (mount Seina) mein zaahir ki. Aur us se pehle apne khaleel Ibrahim (a.s) ke liye Masjid-e-Kheef mein aur apne barguzidah (banda-e-khaas) Isaaq (a.s) ke liye Shee ke Kuwe (well) mein zaahir ki.

Aur apne mehboob Yaqoob (a.s) ke liye Bait-e-Eel mein zaahir ki. Aur Tu ne Ibrahim (a.s) se apna ahed-o-paimaan pura kiya. Aur Isaaq (a.s) ke liye apni qasam puri ki. Aur Yaqoob (a.s) ke liye apni shahadat (gawahi) zaahir ki. Aur momineen se apna mehboob wafa kiya. Aur jinho ne Tere naamo ke zariye duaaye ki unhe qabool kiya. Aur sawaali hu be-waasta Teri us shaan ke jo Qubbaye Romaan (Anaar ke teele) par Musa (a.s) ke liye zaahir huwi. Aur be-waasta Tere mojezo (miracles) ke jo Mulk-e-Misr mein Teri shaan-o-izzat aur ghalaba se, izzat waali nishaaniyo se, ghaalib quwwat se, qudrat ki bulandi aur pura ho ne waale qaul ki shaan se rehnuma huwe aur Tere un kalemaat se jin ke zariye Tu ne Aasmaano aur zameen ke rehne waalo aur ahle duniya-o-ahle aakherat par ehsaan kiya.

Aur sawaali hu Teri us rehmat ke zariye jisse Tu ne apni saari makhluq par karam kiya. Sawaali hu Teri us tawanaayi (taaqat) ke waaste se jisse Tu ne ahle aalam (duniya waalo) ko qaayem rakkha. Sawaali hu be-waasta Tere us Noor keh jiske khauf se Toore Seina (mount Seina) chakna-choor huwa.

Sawaali hu Tere us ilm-o-jalaalat aur Teri badaayi-o-izzat aur Tere Jabrut (Almighty) ke waaste se jis ko zameen bardaasht na kar saki. Aur aasmaan aajeez ho gaye, aur us se zameen ki gehraaiya kap-kapa gayi. Jiske aagey samandar aur nehre ruk gayi. Pahaad uske liye jhuk gaye, aur zameen uske liye apne sutoono (pillars) par thaheyr gayi. Aur uske saamne saari makhluq sar-nagoo (sajdah-rez) ho gayi. Apni jagoh par chalte huwaye uske saamne pareshan ho gayi. Uske liye aag apne maqaam par bujh gayi.

Sawaali hu be-waasta Teri us hukumat ke jiske zariye hamesha Tere ghalbe ki pehchaan hoti hai aur aasmaano aur zameeno mein us se Teri hamd hoti hai. Sawaali hu be-waasta Tere us sachche qaul keh jo Tu ne hamaare baap Hazrat Aadam (a.s) aur unki aulaad ke liye rehmat ke saath farmaya hai.

Sawaali hu be-waasta Tere us kalemaat keh jo tamaam cheezo par ghaalib hai. Sawaali hu be-waasta Teri zaat ke us noor keh jiska jalwa Tu ne pahaad (Kohe-Toor) par zaahir kiya to woh tukde-tukde ho gaya aur Musa (a.s) be-hosh (unconscious) ho kar gir pade.

Sawaali hu be-waasta Teri us buzurghi keh jo Toore-Seina par zaahir huwi to, Tu apne bande aur apne Rasool Hazrat Musa bin Imraan (a.s) se ham-kalaam huwa. Sawaali hu be-waasta Teri Nooraniyyat ke zariye Janabe Isa (a.s) ki munajaat ki jagah mein aur Tere Noor ke zahoor ke zariye Kohe Faraan (Mount Faraan) mein buland-o-moqaddas maqamaat mein safeyn baandhe huwe Malaayeka ki fauj ke zariye aur Tasbeeh-khwa Malaayeka ke khushoo ke zariye.

Sawaali hu be-waasta Teri un barakaat ke zariye jin se Tu ne barkat ataa ki apne Khaleel Ibrahim (a.s) ko Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) ki ummat mein barkat di aur apne barguzeeda (banda-e-khaas) Isaaq (a.s) ko Hazrat Isa (a.s) ki ummat mein barkat di, aur barkat di Tu ne apne khaas bande Yaqoob (a.s) ko Hazrat Musa (a.s) ki ummat mein, aur barkat di Tu ne apne Habeeb Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) ko unki itrat, zurriyat, aur unki ummat mein.

Khudaaya jaisa keh ham unke ahed (zamaane) mein maujood na thay, aur ham ne unhe dekha nahi, aur unpar sachchayi aur haqqaaniyyat ke saath sahih tareeqe se Imaan laaye. Ham chahte hai keh Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat farma. Aur Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par barkat naazil farma. Aur Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par shafqat (rahem) farma jis tarah Tu ne behtareen rehmat aur barkat aur shafqat Ibrahim (a.s) wa Aale Ibrahim (a.s) par farmaayi thi. Be-shak Tu hamd aur shaan waala hai. Jo chahe so karne waala hai, aur Tu har cheez par qudrat rakhta hai.

Ab apni haajat talab kare aur kahe…

Ay Ma’bood! Is dua ke waaste aur un naamo ke waaste se keh jin ki tafseer Tere siwa koi nahi jaanta. Aur jin ki haqeeqat se siwaaye Tere koi aagah nahi. Tu Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat farma. Aur mujh se woh sulook kar jo Tere shayaane-shaan hai. Na keh woh sulook jiska mai mustahaq hu.

Aur mere gunaho mein se jo mai ne pehle kiye aur jo baad mein, bakhsh de aur apna rizqe halaal mere liye kushada kar de, aur mujhe burey insaan, burey ham-saaye (padosi) burey saathi aur burey haakim ki aziyat se bachaaye rakh. Be-shak Tu jo chahe woh karne par qaadir hai aur har cheez ka ilm rakhta hai. Aameen ya Rabbal aalameen.

Uske baad jo haajat ho uska zikr kare aur kahe…

(Ay Ma’bood) Tu har cheez par qudrat rakhta hai. Ay Allah! ay mohabbat karne waale ay ehsaan karne waale ay aasmaan aur zameen ke paida karne waale ay jalaalat aur buzurghi waale ay sab se ziyaada rahem karne waale. Ay Ma’bood! Is dua ke waaste aur un naamo ke waaste se keh jin ki tafseer Tere siwa koi nahi jaanta. Aur jin ki haqeeqat se siwaaye Tere koi aagah nahi.

Tu Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat farma. Aur mujh se woh sulook kar jo Tere shayaane-shaan hai. Na keh woh sulook jaisa (jiska) mai mustahaq hu. Aur mere gunaho mein se jo mai ne pehle kiye aur jo baad mein, bakhsh de aur apna rizqe halaal mere liye kushada kar de, aur mujhe burey insaan, burey ham-saaye (padosi) burey saathi aur burey haakim ki aziyat se bachaaye rakh. Be-shak Tu jo chahe woh karne par qaadir hai aur har cheez ka ilm rakhta hai. Aameen ya Rabbal aalameen.

Phir yeh dua padhe…

Ay Ma’bood! Is dua ke waaste se aur un naamo ke waaste se keh jin ki tafseer aur taahweel (wazhaat/detail) aur jin ke zaahir-o-baatin ko Tere siwa koi nahi jaanta. Tu Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat farma. Aur mujhe duniya aur aakherat ki bhalai ataa farma de. Aur mujh se woh sulook kar jo Tere shayaane-shaan hai na keh woh sulook jiska mai mustahaq hu. Aur meri taraf se falaan ……..ibne, falaan……… se badla le.

Yahan khaali jagah apne aur Ahle Bayt (a.s) ke dushmano ka naam le aur kahe…

Aur mere gunaho mein se jo mai ne pehle kiye aur jo baad mein kiye maaf kar de, aur mere maa-baap aur saare momeneen-o-mominaat ke gunah bakhsh de. Aur apna rizqe halaal mere liye kushada (ziyaada) kar de, aur mujhe burey insaan, burey ham-saaye (padosi), burey saathi aur burey haakim, burey din burey waqt ki aziyat se bachaaye rakh.

Aur meri taraf se badla le us se jisne mujhe dhoka diya aur jisne mujh par zulm kiya aur jo zulm ka iraada rakhta hai. Mere ahel, meri aulaad, mere bhaiyo aur mere ham-saayo ke liye jo momineen aur mominaat mein se hai (unke dushmano se) inteqaam le. Be-shak Tu jo kuchh chahta us par qudrat rakhta hai aur har cheez se waaqif hai. Aameen ya Rabbal aalameen.

Phir kahe…

Ay Ma’bood! Is dua ke waaste se gharib momineen aur mominaat ko maal-o-daulat ataa farma. Bimaar momineen aur mominaat ko tanduroosti aur sehat ataa farma, aur zinda momineen aur mominaat par lutf-o-karam farma aur murda momineen aur mominaat par bakhshish-o-rehmat farma, aur musaafir momineen aur mominaat ko salaamati-o-rizq ke saath unhe gharo tak pohoncha.

Apni rehmat se ay sab se ziyaada rahem karne waale, aur hamaare sardaar Khatemun Nabiyyin Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) par rehmate Khuda ho aur unki pakeeza Aal (a.s) par salaam ho, bohot ziyaada salaam.

Ay Ma’bood! Mai sawaal karta hu be-waaste is dua ki hurmat ke, aur un naamo ke zariye jo is mein mazkoor (zikr) nahi aur us tafseer-o-tadbeer ke waaste se sawaal karta hu jiska siwaaye Tere koi ehaata (hisaab) nahi kar sakta keh Tu mere liye aisa aur aisa kar (Yahan apni haajate bayaan kare).

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

SEMAAT

Shaykh Kafami says that this dua contains ismi azam (the great name of Allah). Imam Baqir (a.s) advised the faithfuls to recite this dua as it brings the downfall of the enemies of Ahlul Bayt. Imam Sadiq (a.s) said that Allah (s.w.t) taught this dua to prophet Musa (a.s) to overcome his enemies. This dua is particularly recited in the closing hours before sunset on Friday. Also recite these words just before sunset…

Glory be to You! There is no god save You! O All-tender! O All-bestower! O Fashioner of the heavens and the earth! O Lord of Majesty and Honor.

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Allah! I beseech Thee through Thy Name, the most great, the most majestic, the most magnificent and the most noble.

If Thou art requested therewith to open the closed doors of the sky these will open with Thy mercy. If Thou art requested thereby to remove the narrowness of the doors of the heaven, they will be opened wide. If Thou art requested thereby to make easy the difficulty, it will be easy.

If Thou art requested thereby to restore the dead to life, they will wise from their graves. If Thou art requested thereby to remove misery and adversity, they will be removed. I beseech Thee through the Majesty of Thy person, the Most Generous and the Most Honoured One, before Whom everybody bows down in submission and all necks bend down in reverence and through fear of Whom all voices become humbled and all hearts tremble.

And I beseech Thee through Thy Might, which prevents the sky from falling on the earth except with Thy permission, and which controls all the heavens and the earth so closely that they cannot move away from their positions; and I beseech Thee through they Will, which the whole world obeys, and through Thy Word.

By which Thou hath created the heaven and the earth, and through Thy Wisdom, with which Thou hath created wonderful, and with which Thou created Darkness and made it Night, and made night the time of quiet and rest; and with which Thou created Light and made it Day, and made day the time of movement and observation; and with which Thou hath created the Sun, and made the sun of light.

And with which Thou hath created the Moon, and made the moon of light; and with which Thou hath created heavenly bodies, and made them fixed stars and planets, and lamps for guidance at night, and as a means of decoration, and missiles for driving away the devils, and hath created for them east and west, and hath created for them the places of ascendancy and orbits, and created for them a firmament and sphere, and fixed for them stages in the heave - and how beautiful the stages - and shaped the stars - and how beautiful the shapes - and made them stable through Thy Name; and Thy Wisdom Thou designed a system for them - and what a good system, and controlled the heavenly bodies through the king of the night and the king of day, and through hours, making them a means for the reckoning of years and counting, making appearance of heavenly bodies regular and universal.

And I beseech Thee, O Allah, through Thy Glory, with which Thou addressed Thy slave and Thy Messenger, Moses son of 'Imran peace be on him, while he was in the company of angels - which address even the favourite cherubin could not ever hear, above the clouds of light, above the box of evidence, with in the pillar of fire on Mount Sinai, and on Mount Hurith in the Holy Vale in the sacred tract of land to the right of Taurus Mountain-through a tree; and again addressed him on the soil of Egypt, after showing nine clear signs and on the day Thou split the sea for the Children of Israel, and caused springs to flow from a stone, thereby displaying wonders of Thy might in a deep sea; and on the day when Thou solidified the water of the sea in the midst of the storm, and caused the Children of Israel to pass by it, and fulfilled Thy excellent promises made to them because of their calmly endurance, and made them the master of the East and the West in the earth, wherein there are blessings for all the worlds; and Thou drowned Pharaoh and his armies and boats in the water.

And (I beseech Thee) through Thy name, which is the most great, the most majestic, and the most holy; and through Thy glory, with which Thou revealed light to Moses, Thy addressee, peace be on him, on Mount Sinal; and earlier radiated the light for Abraham, Thy sincere friend peace be on him, in the mosque of al-Khayf; and for Isaac, Thy chosen Prophet, peace be on him, in Bi-ri shiay', and for Thy Prophet Jacob, peace be on him, in Bayt iil and through Thy Glory, with which Thou fulfilled Thy promise to Abraham, peace be on him; and through Thy Oath in favour of Isaac; and through Thy witnessing favouring Jacob; and through the fulfillments of Thy promise to the faithful: and through Thy Names with which Thou accepted the prayers of supplications; and through Thy Glory, which appeared to Moses, son of Imran, in Qubbat al-Rumman; and through Thy Signs, which appeared on the soil of Egypt with great might and victory, with powerful signs and with the display of full command and great power; and with the affair of the perfect word; and with Thy words with which Thou doth a favor for the people of the skies and the earth and the people of here and the hereafter; and through Thy Mercy with which Thou bestowth upon all Thy creatures: and through thy Power with which Thou dominateth the worlds; and (I beseech Thee) through Thy Light, through fear of which Mount Sinai collapsed, through Thy Knowledge, and Thy Greatness and Majesty, and Thy Unique Magnificence and Thy honour, and (through) Thy might, which the earth could not bear, and, for which the heavens and earth bent down, and the great depths restrained themselves, and the seas and rivers became stable, and to which the mountains made submission, and due to which the earth became firm on its shoulders, and to which all creatures submitted, and due to which the winds breathed, and the flaming fires were extinguished in their places.

And I beseech Thee through Thy might, through which Thou hath been known to be All-powerful for ever and ever, and for which Thou hath been praised in the heavens and the earth; and I beseech Thee through Thy word, the Word of Truth, which became a blessing for our father Adam and his generation; and I beseech Thee through Thy word, by which Thou overcame everything; and I beseech thee through the light of Thy Face, which when it flased on Mount Sinai crushed it to pieces, whereby Moses fainted and fell down, and through Thy Glory, which appeared on Mount Sinai, and through which Thou addressed Thy slave and Thy Mesenger; Moses, son of 'Imran, and through Thy appearance in Sa'ir and on Mount Faran near Rabawat, which are clean and holy places, when troops of heavenly angels were in rows, and with the sumissiveness of the angels praising Thee, and with Thy blessings with which Thou honoured Abraham, Thy sincere friend, peace be upon him, among the followers of Muhammad, Thy blessings be upon him and his Progeny and Thou honoured Isaac, Thy chosen prophet, among the followers of Jesus, peace be on them both, and honoured Jacob, Thy favoured slave, by including him among the followers of Moses, peace be on them both, and honoured Thy intimate friend Muhammad, may the blessings of Allah be upon him and his Progeny, and peace be upon his offspring and seed, and his people.

O Allah! Just as we believe in the turth and justice of these manifestations of Thy glory, without having been present at the time, and without having been eyewitnesses. So we beseech Thee to send Thy blessings upon Muhammad and the Progeny of Muhammad, and honour Muhammad and the Progeny of Muhamamd, and honour Muhammad and the Progeny of Muhammad and have mercy on Muhammad and the Progeny of Muhammad, with the blessings, honour, and mercy Thou sent and bestowed on and meted out to Abraham and the posterity of Abraham. Surely Thou art Praiseworthy, Glorious, the Doer of what Thou willeth. Thou art Powerful over all things.

You may now beseech Almighty Allah to grant you your personal requests. Then, say the following…

O Allah! For the sake of this prayer, and for the sake of these Names which no one can interpret, the inner meaning of which no one other than Thyself knows. Send Thy blessing upon Muhammad and the Progeny of Muhammad; and give me the good of here and the hereafter; and do to me what suits Thee and do not do to me what I deserve and exact vengeance from…(enemy of progeny of Holy Prophet (s.a.w.a.), and forgive my sins past and future, and forgive my parents and all faithful men and women, and increase Thy lawful sustenance for me, and save me from the help of wicked men, wicked neighbours, wicked rulers, wicked companions, and evil days and hours, and exact vengeance for me on everyone who hatches secret plots against me, and on everyone who oppresses me and seeks to do injustice to me or my wife or my children or my brothers or my neighbours or my relations among faithful men and women. Verily Thou art able to do whatever Thou wileth, and Thou knowest everything. Amen, O Lord of the worlds.

O Allah! For the sake of this prayer, grant wealth and affluence to indigent faithful men and women, and recovery and sound health to the sick faithful men and women, and bliss and clemency to the living faithful men and women, and remit the sins of, and have mercy on, the deceased faithful men and women, and arrange the return home of all faithful travellers with safety and gain. Through Thy mercy, O Most Merciful of all the merciful. May Allah's blessings and peace in abundant measure be upon our master Muhammad, the last of the prophets, and his Progeny, the purified.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

You may now mention your personal needs.
