ROZ-E-JUMA

Day of Friday

Holy Prophet (s.a.w.a) said, “Verily, Friday is the master of days. On Fridays, Almighty Allah doubles up the rewards (for good deeds), erases, forgivingly, the punishments (for evildoings), raises the ranks (of faith), responds to prayers, relieves from misfortunes, and fulfills the grand requests.

Hazrat Rasool Khuda (s.a.w.a) ne farmaya, Yaqeenan Juma dino ka sardar hai. Juma ke din Allah (s.w.t) (amaale khair ke) sawab ko dugna, gunaho’n ki maghferat, imaan ki bulandi, Dua’o ki qabooliyat, pareshani se najaat aur nek khwahishaat ko pura karta hai.

1) In the 1st rakat of Namaz-e-Fajr, it is recommended to recite Surah Juma (Chp.62) and, in the 2nd rakat, Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112).

1) Namaz-e-Fajr ki 1st rakat mein Surah Juma (Chp.62) aur 2nd rakat mein Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) padhe.

2) Before saying anything, it is recommended to say the following supplicatory prayer after the Namaz-e-Fajr so that all sins committed throughout the week will be forgiven…

2) Fauran Namaz-e-Fajr ke baad bagair kuchh kiye is dua ko padhe ta-keh poore hafte mein jo gunah huwa hai uski maafi ho jaaye…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
اَللّٰهُمَّ مَا قُلْتُ فِيْ جُمُعَتِيْ هٰذِهٖ مِنْ قَوْلٍ اَوْ حَلَفْتُ فِيْهَا مِنْ حَلْفٍ اَوْ نَذَرْتُ فِيْهَا مِنْ نَذْرٍ فَمَشِيَّتُكَ بَيْنَ يَدَيْ ذٰلِكَ كُلِّهٖ فَمَا شِئْتَ مِنْهُ اَنْ يَّكُوْنَ كَانَ وَمَا لَمْ تَشَاْئَ مِنْهُ لَمْ يَكُنْ۔ اَللّٰهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِيْ وَتَجَاوَزْ عَنِّيْ۔ اَللّٰهُمَّ مَنْ صَلَّيْتَ عَلَيْهِ فَصَلَوَاتِيْ عَلَيْهِ وَمَنْ لَّعَنْتَ فَلَعْنَتِيْ عَلَيْهِ۔
اَللّٰهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

3) It is highly recommended to recite this dua after Namaz-e-Fajr on Fridays…

3) Is dua ko Namaz-e-Fajr ke baad padhne ki khaas taaqid hai…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
اَللّٰهُمَّ اِنِّيْ تَعَمَّدْتُ اِلَيْكَ بِحَاجَتِيْ وَاَنْزَلْتُ اِلَيْكَ الْيَوْمَ فَقْرِيْ وَفَاقَتِيْ وَمَسْكَنَتِيْ فَاَنَا لِمَغْفِرَتِكَ اَرْجٰى مِنِّيْ لِعَمَلِيْ وَلَمَغْفِرَتُكَ وَرَحْمَتُكَ اَوْسَعُ مِنْ ذُنُوْبِيْ فَتَوَلَّ قَضَآءَ كُلِّ حَاجَةٍ لِّيْ بِقُدْرَتِكَ عَلَيْهَا وَتَيْسِيْرِ (تَيَسُّرِ) ذٰلِكَ عَلَيْكَ وَلِفَقْرِيْ اِلَيْكَ فَاِنِّيْ لَمْ اُصِبْ خَيْرًا قَطُّ اِلَا مِنْكَ وَلَمْ يَصْرِفْ عَنِّيْ سُوْٓءَا قَطُّ اَحَدٌ سِوَاكَ وَلَسْتُ (لَيْسَ) اَرْجُوْ لِآخِرَتِيْ وَدُنْيَايَ وَلَا لِيَوْمِ فَقْرِيْ يَوْمَ يُفْرِدُنِي النَّاسُ فِيْ حُفْرَتِيْ وَاُفْضِيْ اِلَيْكَ بِذَنْبِيْ سِوَاكَ۔

4) Recite Dua-e-Ahad after Namaz-e-Fajr on Fridays.

4) Juma ki Namaz-e-Fajr ke baad Dua-e-Ahad padhe.

5) Recite this salwaat at least 10 times after Namaz-e-Fajr and Namaz Zohr on Friday…

5) Juma ke din, Namaz-e-Fajr aur Namaz Zohr ke baad kam-as-kam 10 martaba yeh salwaat padhe…

اَللّٰهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّعَجِّلْ فَرَجَهُمْ۔

6) It is recommended to recite Surah Rehman (Chp.55) after Namaz-e-Fajr on Fridays and to say, “None of Your favors do I deny,” wherever the verses, “Then which of the favors of your Lord will ye deny?” are recited.

6) Juma ke din Namaz-e-Fajr ke baad Surah Rehman (Chp.55) padhe aur jab bhi yeh ayaat “Phir tum dono apne Rab ki kin-kin neymato’n ko juthlaawoge.” Padhe to uske baad fauran yeh padhe “Teri koi bhi neymato’n ko nahi juthlaata.”

لَا بِشَیْ ئٍ مِّنْ اٰلَآٰ ئِكَ رَبِّ اُكَذِّبُ۔

7) It is highly recommended to recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) 100 times after Namaz-e-Fajr on Fridays.

7) Juma ko Namaz-e-Fajr ke baad Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) 100 martaba padhne ki khaas taaqid hai.

8) It is recommended to repeat Surah Kafiroon (Chp.109) 10 times before sunrise and then pray Almighty Allah so that all prayers will be answered.

8) Taaqid hai keh Tulu-e-Aaftaab se pehle Surah Kafiroon (Chp.109) 10 martaba padhe aur Allah (s.w.t) se dua’e kare ta-keh mustjaab ho.

9) It is also highly recommended to recite Surah Nisa (Chp.4), Hud (Chp.11), Kahf (Chp.18), Saaffaat (Chp.37), Ahqaf (Chp.46) and Mu’minun (Chp.23) after Namaz-e-Fajr.

9) Khaas taaqid hai keh Juma ko Namaz-e-Fajr ke baad Surah Nisa (Chp.4), Hud (Chp.11), Kahf (Chp.18), Saaffaat (Chp.37), Ahqaf (Chp.46) aur Mu’minun (Chp.23) padhe.

10) It has been narrated that Imam Sajjad (a.s) used to repeat reciting Ayat (2:255) from early morning up to midday on Fridays. When he accomplished the obligatory namaz, he would recite Surah Qadr (Chp.97) frequently.

10) Imam Sajjad (a.s) se riwaayat hai keh Juma ko Fajr se Zohr tak Ayat (2:255) ko musalsal padha kare. Jab waajib namaz padhe to uske baad Surah Qadr (Chp.97) musalsal padhe.

اَللهٗ لَٓا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا هُوَج اَلْحَيُّ الْقَيُّوْمُج لَا تَاْخُذُهٗ سِنَةٌ وَّلَا نَوْمٌط لَهٗ مَا فِي السَّمٰوٰتِ وَمَا فِي الْاَرْضِط مَنْ ذَا الَّذِيْ يَشْفَعُ عِنْدَهٗٓ اِلَّا بِاِذْنِهٖ ط يَعْلَمُ مَا بَيْنَ اَيْدِيْهِمْ وَمَا خَلْفَهُمْج وَلَا يُحِيْطُوْنَ بِشَيْٓءٍ مِّنْ عِلْمِهٖٓ اِلَّا بِمَا شَآءَج وَسِعَ كُرْسِيُّهُ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضَج وَلَا يَئُوْدُهٗ حِفْظُهُمَاج وَهُوَ الْعَلِيُّ الْعَظِيْمُ۔

11) It is highly recommended to perform Ghusl (Spiritual bath) before midday. The Holy Prophet (s.a.w.a) is reported to have instructed Imam Ali (a.s) by saying, “O Ali; wash yourself every Friday even if you have to purchase water with your daily provisions and have nothing to eat. Verily, no voluntary act is better than this.”

11) Juma ke Zohr se pehle Ghusl ki khaas taaqid hai. Hazrat Rasool Khuda (s.a.w.a) ne Imam Ali (a.s) se farmaya, Ay Ali Juma ke din ghusl karo je-chaaye iske liye tumhe paani kharidna hi kyu’n na pade jab ki tumhare pass khaane ko kuch bhi nahi ho, koi aur amal isse behtar nahi hai.

Imam Sadiq (a.s) is also reported to have said: Whoever washes himself on Fridays and, meanwhile, says the following zikr will be pure (from sin) throughout that week…

Imam Sadiq (a.s) se riwaayat hai keh, jo koi Juma ke din Ghusl karne ke dauraan yeh zikr padhe to woh apne saare gunaho’n se paak ho jaayega jo us ne us hafte mein kiye hai…

اَشْهَدُ اَنْ لَّا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا اللهُ وَحْدَهٗ لَا شَرِيْكَ لَهٗ وَاَشْهَدُ اَنَّ مُحَمَّدًا عَبْدُهٗ وَرَسُوْلُهٗ۔ اَللّٰهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاجْعَلْنِيْ مِنَ التَّوَّابِيْنَ وَاجْعَلْنِيْ مِنَ الْمُتَطَهِّرِيْنَ۔

12) It is also recommended to cut the mustache neatly and trim the nails starting with the little finger of the left hand and end with that of the right hand. The same thing is applicable to the nails of the feet. These acts bring about expansion in sustenance, cancellation of sins up to the next Friday, and security from insanity and leprosy. ROZ-E-JUMA

Hazrat Rasool Khuda (s.a.w.a) ne farmaya, Yaqeenan Juma dino ka sardar hai. Juma ke din Allah (s.w.t) (amaale khair ke) sawab ko dugna, gunaho’n ki maghferat, imaan ki bulandi, Dua’o ki qabooliyat, pareshani se najaat aur nek khwahishaat ko pura karta hai.

1) Namaz-e-Fajr ki 1st rakat mein Surah Juma (Chp.62) aur 2nd rakat mein Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) padhe.

2) Fauran Namaz-e-Fajr ke baad bagair kuchh kiye is dua ko padhe ta-keh poore hafte mein jo gunah huwa hai uski maafi ho jaaye…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Mai ne apne is Juma ke roz (din) jo baat kahi ya us mein kisi baat par qasm khaayi hai ya kisi tarah ki nazr o mannat maani hai to yeh sab kuch Teri marzi ke taabe hai. Pas in mein jiske pura hone mein Teri maslehat hai usey pura farma aur jise Tu na chahe pura na kar. Ay Mabood! Mujhe bakhsh de aur dar-guzar farma, ay Mabood! Jis par Tu rehmat kare mai us par rehmat ka taalib hu aur jis par Tu laanat kare mai bhi laanat karta hu.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par.

3) Is dua ko Namaz-e-Fajr ke baad padhne ki khaas taaqid hai…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Apni haajat le kar Tere huzoor aaya hu aur aaj ke din Teri jaanib mai apne faqr o faaqa aur be-chaaragi ke saath haazir hu. To mai apne amal ke bajaaye Teri bakhshish par nazar rakhta hu aur Teri bakhshish o rehmat mere gunaho’n se ziyada wus’at rakhti hai.

Pas meri tamaam haajaat puri farma keh Tu aisa karne ki qudrat rakhta hai aur yeh Tere liye bohot hi aasaan hai aur meri mohtaji ke munaasib hai, khyu’n keh mai Teri taufiq ke bagair koi neki nahi kar sakta aur siwaye Tere mujhe buraayi se bachane waala koi nahi hai aur mai apni duniya o aakherat ke baare mein siwaye Tere kisi se ummeed nahi rakhta aur na us din jab log mujhe qabr mein akela chhod jaayenge mai apni be-chaargi aur apne gunaho’n mein Tere siwa kisi ko kaar-saaz nahi samajhta.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par.

4) Juma ki Namaz-e-Fajr ke baad Dua-e-Ahad padhe.

5) Juma ke din, Namaz-e-Fajr aur Namaz Zohr ke baad kam-as-kam 10 martaba yeh salwaat padhe…

Ay Mabood! Rehmat farma Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par aur unke zuhoor mein taajil (jaldi) farma.

6) Juma ke din Namaz-e-Fajr ke baad Surah Rehman (Chp.55) padhe aur jab bhi yeh ayaat “Phir tum dono apne Rab ki kin-kin neymato’n ko juthlaawoge.” Padhe to uske baad fauran yeh padhe “Teri koi bhi neymato’n ko nahi juthlaata.”

7) Juma ko Namaz-e-Fajr ke baad Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) 100 martaba padhne ki khaas taaqid hai.

8) Taaqid hai keh Tulu-e-Aaftaab se pehle Surah Kafiroon (Chp.109) 10 martaba padhe aur Allah (s.w.t) se dua’e kare ta-keh mustjaab ho.

9) Khaas taaqid hai keh Juma ko Namaz-e-Fajr ke baad Surah Nisa (Chp.4), Hud (Chp.11), Kahf (Chp.18), Saaffaat (Chp.37), Ahqaf (Chp.46) aur Mu’minun (Chp.23) padhe.

10) Imam Sajjad (a.s) se riwaayat hai keh Juma ko Fajr se Zohr tak Ayat (2:255) ko musalsal padha kare. Jab waajib namaz padhe to uske baad Surah Qadr (Chp.97) musalsal padhe.

(Ayat 2:255): Allah jiske alaawa koi Khuda nahi hai zinda bhi hai aur Usi se kul qayenaat qaayem hai Usey na neend aati hai na ongh aasmaano aur zameen mein jo kuch bhi hai sab Usi ka hai. Kaun hai jo Uski bargah mein Uski ijazat ke bagair sifarish kar sake. Woh jo kuch unke saamne hai aur jo pas-e-pusht (peeche) hai sab ko jaanta hai aur yeh Uske ilm ka ek hisse ka bhi ehaata (cover) nahi kar sakte magar Woh jis qadr chahe. Uski khursi-e-ilm o iqtedaar zameen o aasmaan se wasee-tar hai aur Usey unke tahaffuz mein koi takleef bhi nahi hoti woh aali martaba bhi hai aur saheb-e-azmat bhi.

11) Juma ke Zohr se pehle Ghusl ki khaas taaqid hai. Hazrat Rasool Khuda (s.a.w.a) ne Imam Ali (a.s) se farmaya, Ay Ali Juma ke din ghusl karo je-chaaye iske liye tumhe paani kharidna hi kyu’n na pade jab ki tumhare pass khaane ko kuch bhi nahi ho, koi aur amal isse behtar nahi hai.

Imam Sadiq (a.s) se riwaayat hai keh, jo koi Juma ke din Ghusl karne ke dauraan yeh zikr padhe to woh apne saare gunaho’n se paak ho jaayega jo us ne us hafte mein kiye hai…

Mai gawahi deta hu keh siwaye Khuda ke koi Mabood nahi jo yekta wa la-saani (Us jaisa nahi) hai aur gawahi deta hu keh Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) uske bande aur Rasool hai. Ay Mabood! Rehmat farmata rahe Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Muhammad (a.s) par aur mujhe tauba karne waalo’n aur paakeeza logo mein se qaraar de.

12) Taaqid hai keh Juma ke din Mooch kaate (baarik kare) aur Naakhun ko bhi kaate (taraashe’n) is tarteeb se keh daahe haath (left hand) ki aakhri ungli se shuru kare aur baahe haath (right hand) ki aakhri ungle par tamaam kare. Isi tarteeb se paiyro’n (legs) ke naakhun bhi kaate. Yeh amal anjaam dene se rizq mein hizaafa, agle Juma tak gunaho’n ki maafi aur bimaariyo’n se mehfuz. Taaqid hai keh yeh amal karte waqt is zikr ko padhe…

Khuda ke naam aur uski zaat ke zikr aur Muhammad (s.a.w.a) ki ravish par ibteda (shuru) karta hu.

13) Taaqid hai keh behtareen libaas pehne aur Khushbu lagaaye.

14) Shab-e-Juma aur Yaum-e-Juma ko Sadaqa dene ki taaqid hai.

15) Imam Sadiq (a.s) se riwaayat hai keh, “Waai ho us Musalman par jo Juma ko deeni umoor haasil nahi karta.”

16) Juma ko 100 martaba Salwaat, Isteghfaar aur Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) padhe...

Mai apne Rab se taalib-e-maghferat hu aur uske huzoor tauba karta hu.

17) Imam Baqir (a.s) se waarid hai keh Juma ko apne Marhumeen ki qabr par jaawo, keh woh jaante hai keh kaun unki qabr par aata hai aur us se woh khush hote hai.

18) Dua-e-Nudbah padhne ki taaqid hai.

19) Fajr aur Zohr ke darmiyaan, Namaz Jafar-e-Tayyar padhe.

20) Namaz-e-Kamilah padhe.

21) Imam Ali (a.s) farmaate hai, agar mumkeen ho to Juma ko 10 rakat namaz 2x2 karke, jiske har Ruku aur Sujood mein 100 maratab yeh zikr padhe…

Ba-barkat hai Allah ki zaat aur hamd usi ke liye hai.

22) Imam Sadiq (a.s) ne farmaya, jo Juma ko 2 rakat namaz padhe jiski har ek rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1), Surah Ibrahim (Chp.14) aur Surah Hijr (Chp.15) 1 martaba padhe to woh ghurbat, paagalpan aur haadso’n se hamesha mehfuz rahega.

23) Juma ko 14 Masumeen (a.s) ki Namaz padhe.

24) Is Zikr ko padhe…

Nahi koi Mabood siwaye Allah ke aur Allah buzurg o bar-tar hai aur Allah ki zaat paak hai aur hamd hai us Khuda ke liye jisne kisi ko apna beta nahi banaya aur na koi uski hukumat mein uska saathi hai, na woh kamzor hai keh koi uska haami (madad-gaar) ho aur tum uski badaayi khub bayaan karo.

Phir is tarah se kahe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay kaamil neymato’n waale, ay museebato’n ko dur karne waale, ay jaan-daaro’n ke paida karne waale, ay buland himmato’n waale, ay taarikiya (andhero’n) ko dur karne waale, ay ataa o bakhshish ke maalik, ay dard o gham ko dur karne waale, ay taarikiyo’n mein khauf-zada logo’n ka saath dene waale, ay woh aalim jisne ilm haasil nahi kiya, rehmat farma Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Muhammad (a.s) par aur mujh se woh bartaaw kar jo Tere shaayaan-e-shaan hai, ay woh jiska naam dawa hai, jiska zikr shifa hai, jiski ita’at tawang-giri hai us par rahem farma jiski poonji bas ummeed aur jiska hatyaar (arms) girya hai. Tu paak hai, nahi koi mabood siwaye Tere, ay maher-baani karne waale, ay ehsaan karne waale, ay aasmaano aur zameen ke ijaad karne waale, ay jalaalato’n aur buzurgi ke maalik.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par.

25) Taaqid hai keh Juma ki Namaz-e-Zohr mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 1st rakat mein 1 martaba Surah Juma (Chp.62) aur 2nd rakat mein Surah Munafiqun (Chp.63) padhe. 27) Taaqid hai keh is Zikr ko Namaz-e-Fajr ya Namaz-e-Zohr ke baad padhe…

Ay Mabood! Qaraar de apni rehmat, apne maalyeka ki rehmat, apne rasoolo’n ki rehmat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Muhammad (a.s) par.

Ay Mabood! Rehmat farma Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par aur unke Zuhoor mein taajil (jaldi) farma

Ay Mabood! Mujhe Jannat waalo’n mein se qaraar de jisko barkat ne pur kiya huwa hai, jisko aabaad karne waale farishte aur unke saath hamare nabi Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur hamare baap Ibrahim (a.s) hai.

ROZ-E-JUMA

Day of Friday

Holy Prophet (s.a.w.a) said, “Verily, Friday is the master of days. On Fridays, Almighty Allah doubles up the rewards (for good deeds), erases, forgivingly, the punishments (for evildoings), raises the ranks (of faith), responds to prayers, relieves from misfortunes, and fulfills the grand requests.

1) In the 1st rakat of Namaz-e-Fajr, it is recommended to recite Surah Juma (Chp.62) and, in the 2nd rakat, Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112).

2) Before saying anything, it is recommended to say the following supplicatory prayer after the Namaz-e-Fajr so that all sins committed throughout the week will be forgiven…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, whatever saying I have uttered this week, whatever oath I have taken during it, and whatever vow I have made during it, verily, it is Your will that is before all that. Hence, only that which You will to have effect will come to pass and whatever You do not will to have effect will never come to pass. O Allah, (please) forgive me and overlook my offenses. O Allah, whosoever You bless, then I invoke Your blessings upon him and whosoever You curse, then I invoke Your curse upon him.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

3) It is highly recommended to recite this dua after Namaz-e-Fajr on Fridays…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, I am resorting to You with my needs, and I am putting in Your presence my poverty, my indigence, and my deprivation. Verily, I hope for Your forgiveness more than I rely on my deeds. Indeed, Your forgiveness and Your mercy are more inclusive than my sins are. So, (please) embark on settling all my needs out of Your power over them, out of the easiness for You to do that, and out of my urgent need for that. Verily, I have not gained any good thing save from You and none could ever repel evil from me save You. I hope for none to stand for me in my affairs of this world and the Next World and on the day of my poverty, when people will leave me alone in my hole (i.e. grave) and I will then confess of my sins before You, except You.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

4) Recite Dua-e-Ahad after Namaz-e-Fajr on Fridays.

5) Recite this salwaat at least 10 times after Namaz-e-Fajr and Namaz Zohr on Friday…

O Allah, bless Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad and hasten their Relief.

6) It is recommended to recite Surah Rehman (Chp.55) after Namaz-e-Fajr on Fridays and to say, “None of Your favors do I deny,” wherever the verses, “Then which of the favors of your Lord will ye deny?” are recited.

7) It is highly recommended to recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) 100 times after Namaz-e-Fajr on Fridays.

8) It is recommended to repeat Surah Kafiroon (Chp.109) 10 times before sunrise and then pray Almighty Allah so that all prayers will be answered.

9) It is also highly recommended to recite Surah Nisa (Chp.4), Hud (Chp.11), Kahf (Chp.18), Saaffaat (Chp.37), Ahqaf (Chp.46) and Mu’minun (Chp.23) after Namaz-e-Fajr.

10) It has been narrated that Imam Sajjad (a.s) used to repeat reciting Ayat (2:255) from early morning up to midday on Fridays. When he accomplished the obligatory namaz, he would recite Surah Qadr (Chp.97) frequently.

(Ayat 2:255):

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Allah is He besides Whom there is no god, the Ever living, the Self-subsisting by Whom all subsist; slumber does not overtake Him nor sleep; whatever is in the heavens and whatever is in the earth is His; who is he that can intercede with Him but by His permission? He knows what is before them and what is behind them, and they cannot comprehend anything out of His knowledge except what He pleases, His knowledge extends over the heavens and the earth, and the preservation of them both tires Him not, and He is the Most High, the Great.

11) It is highly recommended to perform Ghusl (Spiritual bath) before midday. The Holy Prophet (s.a.w.a) is reported to have instructed Imam Ali (a.s) by saying, “O Ali; wash yourself every Friday even if you have to purchase water with your daily provisions and have nothing to eat. Verily, no voluntary act is better than this.”

Imam Sadiq (a.s) is also reported to have said: Whoever washes himself on Fridays and, meanwhile, says the following zikr will be pure (from sin) throughout that week…

I bear witness that there is no god except Allah, the One, (there is) no associate with Him; and I testify that Muhammad is His servant and His Messenger. O Allah, send blessings to Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad, and make me of those who turn to You constantly and those who keep themselves pure and clean.

12) It is also recommended to cut the mustache neatly and trim the nails starting with the little finger of the left hand and end with that of the right hand. The same thing is applicable to the nails of the feet. These acts bring about expansion in sustenance, cancellation of sins up to the next Friday, and security from insanity and leprosy. It is recommended to say the following zikr…

In the Name of Allah (I begin); and by (the command of) Allah and according to the tradition of Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad (I do this).

13) It is recommended to put on the best of one’s clothes and apply perfume.

14) It is recommended to give alms (Sadaqa), Friday night and Friday.

15) Imam Sadiq (a.s) is reported as saying, “Woe to a Muslim who does not dedicate Fridays to learning the affairs of his religion.”

16) Recite Salwaat, Isteghfar and Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) 100 times on Friday...

I pray the forgiveness of Allah, my Lord, and I turn repentant to Him.

17) Imam Baqir (a.s) is reported as saying, “Visit the graves of your relatives on Fridays, for they know about those who visit their graves and will be happy for that.”

18) It is recommended to recite Dua-e-Nudbah.

19) Recite Namaz Jafar-e-Tayyar between Fajr and Zohr.

20) Recite Namaz-e-Kamilah.

21) Imam Ali (a.s) said: If possible, offer on Fridays 10 rakat namaz with the unit of 2 rakat. Recite the below zikr 100 times in Ruku and Sujood…

All glory be to Allah and with the Praising of Him.

22) Imam Sadiq (a.s) said, “One who recites 2 rakat namaz on Friday, in each rakat Surah Fateha (Chp.1), Surah Ibrahim (Chp.14) and Surah Hijr (Chp.15) once will be saved from poverty, insanity, and misfortunes forever.

AMAAL OF ASR ON FRIDAY

1) It has been narrated that the best hours of Friday are after the Asr namaz. Accordingly, it is recommended to repeat the following salwaat 100 times each…

a) O Allah, send blessings to Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad and hasten their Relief.

b) The blessings of Allah, His angels, His prophets, His messengers, and all of His creatures be upon Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad. And peace be upon him and upon them and upon their souls and bodies. So be Allah’s mercy and blessings.

2) Recite the following salwaat 7 or 10 times…

O Allah, bless Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad, the Successors and the pleased, with the best of Your blessings, and confer upon them with the best of your favors. And peace be upon them and upon their souls and bodies; and Allah’s mercy and blessings be upon them.

3) Recite the following salwaat 7 times…

O Allah, bless Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad the Successors and the pleased with the best of Your blessings, and confer upon them with the best of your favors. And peace be upon him and them and Allah’s mercy and blessings be also upon them.

4) It is recommended to repeat the following Isteghfaar 70 times…

I pray the forgiveness of Allah and I repent before Him.

5) It is recommended to recite Surah Qadr (Chp.97) 100 times.

6) Recite Dua-e-Ashraat.

7) It is recommended to recite zikr before the sunset on Friday…

Glory be to You! There is no god save You! O All-tender! O All-bestower! O Fashioner of the heavens and the earth! O Lord of Majesty and Honor.

8) It is also recommended to recite Dua-e-Semaat.

9) It is recommended to recite the Durood Abul Hasan Isfahani.
