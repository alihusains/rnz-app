103 | ASR | 3

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Qasam hai asr ki.

2. Be-shak insaan khasaare mein hai.

3. Alaawa un logo ke jo imaan laaye aur unho ne nek aamaal kiye aur ek dusre ko haq aur sabr ki wasiyat-o-nasihat ki.

103 | ASR | 3

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. I swear by the time,

2. Most surely man is in loss,

3. Except those who believe and do good, and enjoin on each other truth, and enjoin on each other patience.
