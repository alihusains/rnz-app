ROZ-E-ARAFAH

Imam Zainul Aabedeen (a.s) ne Arafa ke din ek saayel ki aawaaz sooni jo logo’n se khairaat maang raha tha, aap ne farmaya: Waaye ho tujh par (afsos hai tere haal par) keh aaj ke din bhi tu gair-e-Khuda se sawaal kar raha hai, haala’n keh aaj to yeh ummeed hai keh maawo (mothers) ke pet ke bachche bhi Khuda ke lutf o karam se maala-maal ho kar saeed o khushbakht ho jaaye. Imam Husain (a.s) ki ziyarat kare. Iska sawab 1000 Hajj o Umrah aur 1000 jihad jitna bal-keh us se bhi ziyada hai.

1) Roza rakkhe, siwaye iske keh amaal karne mein mushkil pesh aati ho.

4) 4 Rakat nama, 2x2 rakat kare ke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 50 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) padhe.

5) Yeh dua padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Paak hai woh zaat jiska arsh aasmaan mein hai. Paak hai woh zaat jiska hukm zameen mein naafiz hai. Paak hai woh zaat jiska faisla qabro’n mein naafiz hai. Paak hai woh zaat jiska dariya (river) mein raasta hai. Paak hai woh zaat jo Jahannam par ikhteyaar rakhta hai.

Paak hai woh zaat Jannat mein jiski rehmat hai. Paak hai woh zaat qayamat mein jiska adl hai. Paak hai woh zaat jisne aasmaan ko buland kiya. Paak hai woh zaat jisne zameen bichhaayi. Paak hai woh zaat jis se panah o najaat nahi magar usi ke yahan se.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par.

Woh zinda karta aur maut deta hai aur wahi maut dene ke baad dubaara (phir se) zinda karega. Woh aisa zinda hai jise maut nahi usi ke haath mein (ham sab ki) bhalaayi hai aur woh har cheez par qudrat rakhta hai.

b) Bakhshish chahta hu us Allah se ke jiske siwa koi Mabood nahi. Zinda o paayenda (hamesha rehne waala) hai aur mai uske huzoor tauba karta hu.

c) Ay Allah.

d) Ay bade Maher-baan.

e) Ay Rahem waale.

f) Ay aasmaano aur zameen ke paida karne waale.

g) Ay jalaalat o buzurgi ke maalik.

h) Ay zinda ay paayenda.

i) Ay mohabbat waale ay ehsaan karne waale.

j) Ay woh keh Tere siwa koi Mabood nahi.

k) Aameen.

Ay woh jiski misl (like) koi cheez nahi hai, aur sab kuch sunne aur dekhne waala hai. Sawaal karta hu Tujh se keh Muhammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Muhammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma.

Pas qayamat mein mujhe unke didaar se mehroom na rakhna, aur unki sohbat (saath rehna) naseeb karna, neez (also) mujhe unke deen par maut de, unke Hawze Kausar mein se mujhe seraab karna. Jo aisa seraab kar dene waala khush-maza o shreen ho keh uske baad mai kabhi pyasa na hu, be-shak Tu har cheez par qudrat rakhta hai.

Ay Allah! Be-shak mai imaan laya hu Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) par aur unhe dekha nahi. Pas Jannat mein mujhe unki pehchaan kara dena. Ay Allah! pohoncha de Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) ko meri taraf se bohot bohot aadaab o salaam.

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Glory be to He whose Arsh is in the heavens! Glory be to He whose Will operates in the earth! Glory be to He who examines (the dead) in the graves! Glory be to He who surfaced a path over the ocean! Glory be to He who has the total control over the Fire! Glory be to He who surrounds the Paradise with (His) mercy! Glory be to He who will do justice on the Day of Judgment! Glory be to He who raised the sky! Glory be to He who spread the earth! Glory be to He from whom there is no running away unless (one takes refuge) with Him!

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

6) Then, repeat the following 100 times:

Glory be to Allah. (All) praise be to Allah. There is no god save Allah. Allah is the Greatest.

7) Then recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112), Ayatul Kursi (2:255-257) and salawaat 100 times each.

8) Then, repeat the following zikr 10 times each:

a) There is no god save Allah, the Only One. He has no partner. Sovereignty belongs to Him. (All) Praise belongs to Him. He gives life, puts to death, makes dead, and brings to life. He is Ever-Alive, never non-existent, in His hand is the good. He is able to do all things.

b) I ask forgiveness of Allah. There is no god save He, the Ever-Alive.

c) O Allah!

d) O Beneficent!

e) O Merciful!

f) O Creator of the heavens and the earth.

g) O Owner of might and majesty.

h) O Ever-Alive! O Eternal!

i) O Giver! O Bestower!

j) O “There is no god save Thou”!

k) So be it.

9) Then, say the following dua:

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah I beseech Thee. O He who is nearer than my jugular vein. O He who intervenes between the body and the soul. O He who is in the heavenly heights; and true horizons. O He who is Beneficent on the established A’rsh. O He like unto whom there is nothing and He is Hearing, Seeing. I ask Thee to send blessing on Muhammad and on the children of Muhammad.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

Now pray for your needs.

10) Then recite this dua.

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah! O the Most Generous who gives more than all the givers! O the Best (eternally) Besought of all those who are beseeched! O the Most Merciful of all the merciful! O Allah send blessing on Muhammad and on his children in the earlier times, and send blessings on Muhammad and on his children in the later times, send blessings on Muhammad and on his children, (in the midst of those) living in a satisfying state of the highest degree, send blessings on Muhammad and on his children in the midst of the Messengers.

O Allah give to Muhammad and his children (all) rights of being “the means of approach” (unto Thee), and abundant merits, distinctions, high ranks, and most important privileges.

O Allah I have accepted the “truth of Muhammad” (Allah’s blessings be on him and on his children), though I have not met him, therefore let it not be that I am deprived of his vision on the Day of Judgment, and let my life (in the Hereafter be spent in his company, make me follow his creed, let me quench my thirst from his cistern (Kawthar), thoroughly, in plenty, gliding the wholesome water pleasantly down the throat, never ever to be thirsty again.

Verily, Thou art able to do all things. O Allah I have accepted the “truth of Muhammad” (Allah’s blessings on him and on his children), though I have not met him, therefore, let me remain aware of his true aspects in the Paradise. O Allah convey to Muhammad (Allah’s blessings be on him and on his children) my much compliments and salutations.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

24) At the last hour of the ‘Arafat Day, it is recommended to say the following…

O my Lord, my sins do not harm You nor will Your forgiveness to me reduce Your Grandeur. So, (please do) grant me that which does not reduce Your Grandeur and forgive me that which does not harm You.

25) It is also recommended to say the following…

O Allah, (please) do not deprive me of the good that You have because of the evil that I have. If You do not have mercy upon me due to my fatigue and tiredness, (please) then do not deprive me of the reward of those whom are afflicted with misfortunes.
