Chp. 25:Ver. 74
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَا هَبْ لَنَا مِنْ اَزْوَاجِنَا وَذُرِّيّٰتِنَا قُرَّةَ اَعْيُنٍ وَّاجْعَلْنَا لِلْمُتَّقِيْنَ اِمَامًا۔
O our Lord! Grant us in our wives and our offspring the joy of our eyes, and make us guides to those who guard (against evil).

Khudaaya! Hamein hamaari azwaaj aur aulaad ki taraf se khunki chashm (raahat) ataa farma aur hamein saheh-e-baan taqwa ka peshwa bana de.
