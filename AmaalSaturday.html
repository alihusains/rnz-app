SATURDAY

Saturday has been assigned to Prophet Muhammad (s.a.w.a).
Saturday Hazrat Rasool Khuda (s.a.w.a) se mansub hai.

NAQSH…
One who looks at this Naqsh on Saturday will be safe under divine protection, no fear from oppressors and be safe from accidental or unexpected advent death.

Jo shakhs is Naqsh ko baroz Saturday dekhe to woh balaawo’n aur aafato’n se hifaazat-e-Khuda-wande Taala mein mehfooz rahega aur baadshaho’n aur amiro ki haibat se bachega aur haadse waali aur achaanak maut se mehfooz rahega.

ZIKR…
Recite 1000 times…
1000 Martaba padhe…

یَارَبَّ الْعَالَمِیْنَ

YA RABBAL AALAMEEN
O! Lord of the Worlds.
Ay Aalameen ke Paalne Waale.

NAMAZ…
Recite 4-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) and Ayatul Kursi 1 time each.
4 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 1 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) aur Ayatul Kursi padhe.

DUA…
Recite Saturday Dua.
Saturday ki Dua padhe.

ZIYARAT…
Recite Saturday Ziyarat.
Saturday ki Ziyarat padhe.
