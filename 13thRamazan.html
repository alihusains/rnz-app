3) 2 Rakat namaz padhe, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp۔1) ke baad 1 martaba Surah Yasin (Chp۔36), Surah Mulk (Chp۔67) and Surah Ikhlaas (Chp۔112) padhe.

4) Dua-e-Mujeer padhe.

SHAB KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay aasmaano par tasallut (qabza) aur zameeno par iqtedaar rakhne waale, ay woh zaat keh jiske qabze mein aasmaano aur zameeno ki hukm-raani hai. Tu jo gunaho ko bakhsh ne waala hai bahot waaqif, bada bakhsh ne waala, burdbaar, raheem, be-neyaaz aur tanha hai.

Tu aisa hai keh Tera koi shabi (replica) nahi hai aur na kisi ko haq-e-wilaayat o hukumat Tujh par haasil hai. Tu hi buland o bartar aur saaheb-e-iqtedaar o qaadir hai aur Tu hi tauba qubool karne waala maherbaan hai.

Mai Tujh se sawaal karta hu keh Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unke Aal (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur mere gunah bakhsh de aur mujh par rahem kar, yaqeenan Tu tamaam rahem karne waalo mein sab se zeyaada rahem waala hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Aaj ke din mujh ko mail-kuchail (gandaghi) se paak wo saaf rakh, isme mujhe muqaddar-shuda (qismat mein likkhe huwe) mushkilaat par sabr ataa farma, aur aaj Apni madad ke saath mujh ko parhezgaar aur neku-kaaro ke saath rehne ki taufiq de. Ay bechaaro ke aankho ki thandak.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

NIGHT DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O the powerful one of the heavens and the powerful of the earths and one for whom is the kingdom of the heavens and the kingdom of the earths and the one who forgives the sins and the Hearing the knowing the forgiving the forbearing the merciful the needless the unique who has no likeness and no one is having authority on You. You are the Highest of the High and the most powerful of the powerful. And you are the one who accepts repentance and one who is merciful. I beg You to bless Muhammad and his progeny. And that You forgive me and have mercy on me. Indeed You are the most merciful of the merciful ones.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah remove from me, in this month, the impurities of foul immorality and tendency to turn from good with disgust; let me be calm and composed in the face of events and accidents that have to happen; let me attach myself to piety and make friends with the pious, through Thy assistance, O the Apple of the eye of the docile.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
