MONDAY

Monday has been assigned to Imam Hasan (a.s) and Imam Husain (a.s).
Monday Imam Hasan (a.s) aur Imam Husain (a.s) se mansub hai.

NAQSH…
One who looks at this Naqsh on Monday his prayers will be accepted, to be safe from troubles, frustrations and losses, and be known among the people.

Jo shakhs is Naqsh ko baroz Monday dekhe to uski dua qabool hogi, mushkilaat, ghabraahat aur nuksaan se mefooz rahega aur woh logo’n mein mehboob hoga.

ZIKR…
Recite 1000 times…
1000 Martaba padhe…

یَاقَاضِیَ الْحَاجَاتِ

YA QAAZEYAL HAAJAAT
O! Bestower of Desires.
Ay haajato’n ke pura karne waale

NAMAZ…
Recite 10-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) 10 times.
10 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 10 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) padhe.

DUA…
Recite Monday Dua.
Monday ki Dua padhe.

ZIYARAT…
Recite Monday Ziyarat.
Monday ki Ziyarat padhe.
