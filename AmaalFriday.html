FRIDAY

Friday has been assigned to Imam Mahdi (a.t.f.s).
Friday Imam Mahdi (a.t.f.s) se mansub hai.

NAQSH…
One who looks at this Naqsh on Friday his enemies will turn into friends, legitimate desire to be fulfilled, to be safe from misfortune and to get respect and honour.

Jo shakhs is Naqsh ko baroz Friday dekhe to uske dushman dost ho jaayenge, jitni muraade’n hai uski woh puri hogi aur logo’n ki nigah mein mohtaram ho jaayega aur aafat-o-bala se mehfooz rahega.

ZIKR…
Recite 1000 times…
1000 Martaba padhe…

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَجِّلْ فَرَجَھُمْ

ALLAHUMMA SWALLE ALAA MUHAMMADIW WA AALE MUHAMMAD WA AJJIL FARAJAHUM
O Allah send blessings on Muhammad and on the children of Muhammad and make his appearance early.
Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par aur unke zahur mein taajil (jaldi) farma.

NAMAZ…
Recite 4-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite last 2 Verses of Surah Mulk (Chp.67) and Surah Ha-Mim (Chp.41) 1 time each.
4 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 1 martaba Surah Mulk (Chp.67) ki aakhri 2 aayat aur Surah Ha-Mim (Chp.41) padhe.

DUA…
Recite Friday Dua.
Friday ki Dua padhe.

ZIYARAT…
Recite Friday Ziyarat.
Friday ki Ziyarat padhe.
