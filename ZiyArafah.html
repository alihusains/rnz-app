ARAFAH

Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) says: One who perform ghusl and wear clean cloths and recite this Ziyarat of Imam Hussain (a.s) on the day of Arafah. Allah (s.w.t) will reward him the sawaab of accepted 1000 Hajj, 1000 Umrah and 1000 Jehad which he has performed with Prophet or Imam. Then he said Allah (s.w.t) will look with mercy on the zayereen of Karbala before he turn towards the hujjaj on the day of arafah.

Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) farmate hai: jo shaks roze Arafah Imam Husain (a.s) ki maarefat ke saath ziyarat kare to uske naama-e-aamaal mein 1000 Hajj, 1000 Umrah-e-maqbula aur 1000 Jehaad ka sawaab tahreer (registered) kiya jaata hai. Jo us ne kisi Nabi-e-Mursal (a.s) ya Imam-e-Aadil (a.s) ke saath kiye ho. Phir farmaya ke Khudaaye-taala roze Arafah ahle Arafaat ki taraf nazar karne se pehle zaayereen-e-qabre Imam Husain (a.s) par nazr-e-rehmat karta hai. Pehle Imam Husain (a.s) ki ziyarat ki niyyat se ghusl baja laaye, Phir paak-saaf libaas pahen kar qabre Imam Husain (a.s) ki taraf rukh kar ke bohot hi sanjeedgi aur adab-o-ehteraam ke saat yeh ziyarat padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللہُ اَكْبَرُ كَبِیْرًا وَّالْحَمْدُ لِلہِ كَثِیْرًا وَّسُبْحَانَ اللہِ بُكْرَۃً وَّاَصِیْلًا وَّالْحَمْدُ لِلہِ الَّذِیْ ہَدَانَا لِہٰذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لِنَہْتَدِیَ لَوْلَا اَنْ ہَدَانَا اللہُ لَقَدْ جَآئَتْ رُسُلُ رَبِّنَا بِالْحَقِّ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰی رَسُوْلِ اللہِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰی اَمِیْرِ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰی فَاطِمَۃَ الزَّہْرَآئِ سَیِّدَۃِ نِسَآئِ الْعَالَمِیْنَ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَی الْحَسَنِ وَالْحُسَیْنِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰی عَلِیِّ بْنِ الْحُسَیْنِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدِ بْنِ عَلِیٍّ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰی جَعْفَرِ بْنِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدِ بْنِ عَلِیٍّ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰی عَلِیِّ بْنِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَی الْحَسَنِ بْنِ عَلِیٍّ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَی الْخَلَفِ الصَّالِح الْمُنْتَظَرِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَااَبَا عَبْدِ اللہِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ رَسُوْلِ اللہِ عَبْدُكَ وَابْنُ عَبْدِكَ وَابْنُ اَمَتِكَ الْمُوَالِیْ لِوَلِیِّكَ الْمُعَادِیْ لِعَدُوِّكَ اسْتَجَارَ بِمَشْہَدِكَ وَتَقَرَّبَ اِلَی اللہِ بِقَصْدِكَ۔ اَلْحَمْدُ لِلہِ الَّذِیْ ہَدَانِیْ لِوِلَایَتِكَ وَخَصَّنِیْ بِزِیَارَتِكَ وَسَہَّلَ لِیْ قَصْدَكَ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاوَارِثَ اٰدَمَ صِفْوَۃِ اللہِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاوَارِثَ نُوْحٍ نَبِیِّ اللہِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاوَارِثَ اِبْرَاھِیْمَ خَلِیْلِ اللہِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاوَارِثَ مُوْسیٰ كَلِیْمِ اللہِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاوَارِثَ عِیْسٰی رُوْحِ اللہِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاوَارِثَ مُحَمَّدٍ حَبِیْبِ اللہِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاوَارِثَ اَمِیْرِ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاوَارِثَ فَاطِمَۃَ الزَّھْرَآئِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ مُحَمَّدِ نِالْمُصْطَفٰی۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ عَلِیِّ نِالْمُرْتَضٰی۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ فَاطِمَۃَ الزَّھْرَآئِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ خَدِیْجَۃَ الْكُبْرٰی۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاثَارَ اللہِ وَابْنَ ثَارِہٖ وَالْوِتْرَ الْمَوْتُوْرَ۔ اَشْھَدُ اَنَّكَ قَدْ اَقَمْتَ الصَّلٰوۃَ وَاٰتَیْتَ الزَّكٰوۃَ وَاَمَرْتَ بِالْمَعْرُوْفِ وَنَھَیْتَ عَنِ الْمُنْكَرِ وَاَطَعْتَ اللہَ حَتّٰی اَتٰكَ الْیَقِیْنُ۔ فَلَعَنَ اللہُ اُمَّۃً قَتَلَتْكَ وَلَعَنَ اللہُ اُمَّۃً ظَلَمَتْكَ وَلَعَنَ اللہُ اُمَّۃً سَمِعَتْ بِذٰلِكَ فَرَضِیَتْ بِہٖ۔ یَامَوْلَایَ یَااَبَا عَبْدِ اللہِ۔ اُشْھِدُ اللہَ وَمَلَآئِكَتَہٗ وَاَنْبِیَآئَہٗ وَرُسُلَہٗ اَنِّیْ بِكُمْ مُّؤْمِنٌ وَّبِاِیَابِكُمْ مُّوْقِنٌ بِشَرَایِعِ دِنِیْ وَخَوَاتِیْمِ عَمَلِیْ وَمُنْقَلَبِیْ اِلٰی رَبِّیْ فَصَلَوَاتُ اللہِ عَلَیْكُمْ وَعَلٰی اَرْوَاحِكُمْ وَعَلٰی اَجْسَادِكُمْ وَعَلٰی شَاھِدِكُمْ وَعَلٰی غَآئِبِكُمْ وَظَاھِرِكُمْ وَبَاطِنِكُمْ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ خَاتَمِ النَّبِیِّیْنَ وَابْنَ سَیِّدِ الْوَصِیِّیْنَ وَابْنَ اِمَامِ الْمُتَّقِیْنَ وَابْنَ قَآئِدِ الْغُرِّ الْمُحَجَّلِیْنَ اِلٰی جَنَّاتِ النَّعِیْمِ وَكَیْفَ لَا تَكُوْنُ كَذٰلِكَ وَاَنْتَ بَابُ الْھُدٰی وَاِمَامِ التُّقٰی وَالْعُرْوَۃُ الْوُثْقٰی وَالْحُجَّۃُ عَلٰی اَھْلِ الدُّنْیَا وَخَامِسُ اَھْلِ اَصْحَابِ الْكِسَآئِ غَذَتْكَ یَدُ الرَّحْمَۃِ وَرَضِعْتَ مِنْ ثَدْیِ الْاِیْمَانِ وَرُبِّیْتَ فِیْ حِجْرِ الْاِسْلَامِ فَالنَّفْسُ غَیْرُ رَاضِیَۃٍ بِفِرَاقِكَ وَلَا شَآكَّۃٍ فِیْ حَیٰوتِكَ صَلَوَاتُ اللہِ عَلَیْكَ وَعَلٰی اٰبَآئِكَ وَاَبْنَآئِكَ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَاصَرِیْعَ الْعَبْرَۃِ السَّاكِبَۃِ وَقَرِیْنَ الْمُصِیْبَۃِ الرَّاتِبَۃِ۔ لَعَنَ اللہُ اُمَّۃً نِاسْتَحَلَّتْ مِنْكَ الْمَحَارِمَ فَقُتِلْتَ صَلَّی اللہُ عَلَیْكَ مَقْھُوْرًا وَاَصْبَحَ رَسُوْلُ اللہِ صَلَّی اللہُ عَلَیْہِ وَ اٰلِہٖ بِكَ مَوْتُوْرًا وَّاَصْبَحَ كِتَابُ اللہِ بِفَقْدِكَ مَھْجُوْرًا۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ وَعَلٰی جَدِّكَ وَاَبِیْكَ وَاُمِّكَ وَاَخِیْكَ وَعَلَی الْاَئِمَّۃِ مِنْۢ بَنِیْكَ وَعَلَی الْمُسْتَشْھَدِیْنَ مَعَكَ وَعَلَی الْمَلَآئِكَۃِ الْحَآفِّیْنَ بِقَبْرِكَ وَالشَّاھِدِیْنَ لِزُّوَّارِكَ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ بِالْقَبُوْلِ عَلٰی دُعَآئِ شِیْعَتِكَ وَالسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ وَرَحْمَۃُ اللہِ وَ بَرَكَاتُہٗ۔ بِاَبِیْ اَنْتَ وَاُمِّیْ یَابْنَ رَسُوْلِ اللہِ۔ بِاَبِیْ اَنْتَ وَاُمِّیْ یَااَبَا عَبْدِ اللہِ لَقَدْ عَظُمَتِ الرَّزِیَّۃُ وَجَلَّتِ الْمُصِیْبَۃُ بِكَ عَلَیْنَا وَعَلٰی جَمِیْعِ اَھْلِ السَّمٰوَاتِ وَالْاَرْضِ۔ فَلَعَنَ اللہُ اُمَّۃً اَسْرَجَتْ وَاَلْجَمَتْ وَتَھَیَّاَتْ لِقِتَالِكَ یَامَوْلَایَ یَااَبَا عَبْدِ اللہِ قَصَدْتُ حَرَمَكَ وَاَتَیْتُ مَشْھَدَكَ اَسْئَلُ اللہَ بِالشَّاْنِ الَّذِیْ لَكَ عِنْدَہٗ وَبِالْمَحَلِّ الَّذِیْ لَكَ لَدَیْہِ اَنْ یُّصَلِّیَ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاَنْ یَّجْعَلَنِیْ مَعَكُمْ فِی الدُّنْیَا وَالْاٰخِرَۃِ بِمَنِّہٖ وَجُوْدِہٖ وَكَرَمِہٖ۔

Kiss the shrine and recite 2 rakats namaz above the head and then recite…
Zari-e-Mubarak ko chume aur 2 rakat namaz sar ke paas khade ho kar adaa kare aur phir is tarah padhe…

اَللّٰہُمَّ اِنِّیْ صَلَّیْتُ وَرَكَعْتُ وَسَجَدْتُ لَكَ وَحْدَكَ لَا شَرِیْكَ لَكَ لِاَنَّ الصَّلَاۃَ وَالرُّكُوْعَ وَالسُّجُوْدَ لَا تَكُوْنُ اِلَّا لَكَ لِاَنَّكَ اَنْتَ اللہُ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاَبْلِغْہُمْ عَنِّیْ اَفْضَلَ الْتَّحِیَّۃِ وَالسَّلَامِ وَارْدُدْ عَلَیَّ مِنْہُمُ التَّحِیَّۃَ وَالسَّلَام۔َ اَللّٰہُمَّ وَہَاتَانِ الرَّكْعَتَانِ ہَدِیَّۃٌ مِّنِّیْ اِلٰی مَوْلَایَ وَسَیِّدِیْ وَاِمَامِیْ حُسَیْنِ بْنِ عَلِیٍّ عَلَیْہِمَا السَّلَامُ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّتَقَبَّلْ ذٰلِكَ مِنِّیْ وَاجْزِنِیْ عَلٰی ذٰلِكَ اَفْضَلَ اَمَلِیْ وَرَجَآئِیْ فِیْكَ وَفِیْ وَلِیِّكَ یَااَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِیْنَ۔

Then he should get up and go to the foot of Imam Husain (a.s) and recite the Ziarat of Janab Ali Akbar (a.s)and recite the following…
Phir Imam Hussain (a.s) ki paayti (foot) ki taraf khade ho kar Janab Ali Akbar (a.s) ki ziyarat padhe…

اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ رَسُوْلِ اللہِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ نَبِیِّ اللہِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ اَمِیْرِ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ الْحُسِیْنِ الشَّھِیْدِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ اَیُّھَا الشَّھِیْدُ بْنُ الشَّھِیْدِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ اَیُّھَا الْمَظْلُوْمُ ابْنُ الْمَظْلُوْمِ۔ لَعَنَ اللہُ اُمَّۃً قَتَلَتْكَ وَلَعَنَ اللہُ اُمَّۃً ظَلَمَتْكَ وَلَعَنَ اللہُ اُمَّۃً سَمِعَتْ بِذٰلِكَ فَرَضِیَتْ بِہٖ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَامَوْلَایَ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَا وَلِیَّ اللہِ وَابْنَ وَلِیِّہٖ لَقَدْ عَظُمَتِ الْمُصِیْبَۃُ وَجَلَّتِ الرَّزِیَّۃُ بِكَ عَلَیْنَا وَعَلٰی جَمِیْعِ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ۔ فَلَعَنَ اللہُ اُمَّۃً قَتَلَتْكَ وَاَبْرَئُ اِلَی اللہِ وَاِلَیْكَ مِنْھُمْ فِی الدُّنْیَا وَالْاٰخِرَۃِ۔

After this recite the Ziarat of the martyrs of Kerbala…

ARAFAH

Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) farmate hai: jo shaks roze Arafah Imam Husain (a.s) ki maarefat ke saath ziyarat kare to uske naama-e-aamaal mein 1000 Hajj, 1000 Umrah-e-maqbula aur 1000 Jehaad ka sawaab tahreer (registered) kiya jaata hai. Jo us ne kisi Nabi-e-Mursal (a.s) ya Imam-e-Aadil (a.s) ke saath kiye ho. Phir farmaya ke Khudaaye-taala roze Arafah ahle Arafaat ki taraf nazar karne se pehle zaayereen-e-qabre Imam Husain (a.s) par nazr-e-rehmat karta hai. Aur is ziyarat ki kaifiyat you’n bayaan ki gayi hai keh: Pehle Imam Husain (a.s) ki ziyarat ki niyyat se ghusl baja laaye, Phir paak-saaf libaas pahen kar qabre Imam Husain (a.s) ki taraf rukh kar ke bohot hi sanjeedgi aur adab-o-ehteraam ke saat yeh ziyarat padhe.

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Khuda buzurgh-tar hai buzurghi ke saath, aur hamd hai Khuda ke liye jo paak-o-monazzah (pakeeza) hai, har subh aur shaam hamd-o-sana hai us Khuda ke liye jisne is taraf hamaari rehnumaayi ki. Yaqeenan ham raah-e-hidaayat na pa sakte agar Khuda hamaari rehnumaayi na farmata. Aur hamaare Rab ke sabhi Rasool (s) haq ke saath aaye hai. Salaam ho Khuda ke Rasool (s.a.w.a) par. Salaam ho momino ke Amir par. Salaam ho Fatematuz Zahra (s.a) par. Jo saare jahan ki aurto’n ki sardaar hai. Salaam ho Hazrat Imam Hasan (a.s)-o-Imam Husain (a.s) par. Salaam ho Hazrat Ali ibnil Husain Zainul Abedeen (a.s) par. Salaam ho Hazrat Mohammad ibne Ali Al-Baqir (a.s) par. Salaam ho Hazrat Jafar ibne Mohammad As-Sadiq (a.s) par. Salaam ho Hazrat Moosa ibne Jafar Al-Kazim (a.s) par. Salaam ho Hazrat Ali ibne Musa Ar-Reza (a.s) par. Salaam ho Hazrat Mohammad ibne Ali At-Taqi Al-Jawaad (a.s) par. Salaam ho Hazrat Ali ibne Mohammad An-Naqi Al-Haadi (a.s) par. Salaam ho Hazrat Hasan ibne Ali Al-Askari (a.s) par. Salaam ho un khalafus-swoleh Hazrat Hujjat ibnil Hasan (a.t.f.s) par jo muntazar hai. Aap par salaam ho ay Aba Abdillah. Aap par salaam ho ay Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) ke farzand. Aap ka yeh ghulaam jo aap ke ghulaam aur aap ke kaneez ka beta hai. Aap ke dost se dosti rakhne waala aur aap ke dushman se dushmani rakhne waala, panah leta hai aap ke rauze ki aur aap ki taraf aane mein Khuda ka qurb (nazdeeki) chahta hai. Hamd hai Khuda ke liye jisne mujhe aap ki wilaayat ka raasta dikhaya. Mujhe aap ki ziyarat ke liye makhsoos kiya. Aur aap ki taraf aane mein sahulate faraaham ki.

Phir Hazrat Imam Husain (a.s) ke rauza-e-moqaddesa mein daakhil ho kar aap ke sirhaane (near head) khade ho kar is tarah ziyarat padhe:

Aap par salaam ho ay Aadam (a.s) ke waaris jo Khuda ke pasandeeda hai. Aap par salaam ho ay Nooh (a.s) ke waaris jo Khuda ke Nabi hai. Aap par salaam ho ay Ibrahim (a.s) ke waaris jo Khuda ke khaleel hai. Aap par salaam ho ay Moosa (a.s) ke waaris jo Khuda ke kaleem hai. Aap par salaam ho ay Isa (a.s) ke waaris jo Khuda ki ruh hai. Aap par salaam ho ay Mohammad-e-Mustafa (s.a.w.a) ke waaris jo Khuda ke mehboob hai. Aap par salaam ho ay Ameerul momineen Hazrat Ali ibne Abi Taalib (a.s) ke waaris. Salaam ho aap par ay Fatema Zahra (s.a) ke waaris. Salaam ho aap par ay Hazrat Mohammad Mustafa (s.a.w.a) ke farzand. Salaam ho Aap par ay Hazrat Ali-e-Murtaza (a.s) ke farzand. Aap par salaam ho ay Fatema Zahra (s.a) ke farzand. Aap par salaam ho ay Khadeejatul Kubra (a.s) ke farzand. Aap par salaam ho ay Qurbaan-e-Khuda aur Qurbaan-e-Khuda ke farzand. Yahan tak keh shaheed ho gaye. Pas Khuda laanat kare us giroh (group) par jisne aap ko qatl kiya. Khuda laanat kare us giroh (group) par jisne aap par zulm kiya. Aur Khuda laanat kare us giroh (group) par jisne yeh waaqeya sunato us par khush huwe. Ay mere aaqa ay Aba Abdillah (a.s) mai gawah banata hu Khuda ko uske farishto ko uske nabiyo ko aur rasoolo ko ispar ke mai aap par aur aap ki raj’at (waapsi) par imaan rakhta hu aur yaqeen rakhta hu ahkaam-e-deen par apne amal ki jaza ka aur Khuda ki taraf apni waapsi par. Pas Khuda ki rehmate ho aap par, aap ki ruho par, aap ke jismo par, aur aap mein se haazir par, aur aap ke ghaib par, aap ke zaahir par aur aap ke baatin par rehmate naazil ho. Salaam ho aap par ay Khaatimul Ambiya (s.a.w.a) ke farzand. Ausiya ke sardaar ke farzand par. Parhez-gaaro ke Imam ke farzand. Roshan chehre waalo ke peshwa ke farzand. nemato waali Jannat mein daakhil ho ne tak (lagataar) salaam ho. Aur aisa kyu’n na ho jab-keh aap hidaayat ka darwaaza hai, parhez-gaaro ke Imam hai. Khuda ki mazboot rassi hai, ahle duniya par Khuda ki hujjat hai, aur aap ahle kisa ke paanchwe (5th) fard hai. Aap ne daste rehmat se ghiza khaayi, sar-chashma-e-imaan se doodh piya. Aur aap ne Islam ki aagosh mein parwarish paayi, pas yeh dil aap ki judaayi mein na-khush hai, aur isey aap ke zinda ho ne mein koi shubha (doubt) nahi. Khuda ki rehmate ho aap par, aap ke buzurgaan par, aur aap ke farzando par. Salaam ho aap par ay woh shaheed jis par aansoo (tears) bahaye gaye, aur jis par museebat pe museebat aati rahi. Khuda laanat kare us giroh (group) par jisne aap ki hurmat ka khayaal na rakkha. Pas Khuda ki rehmat ho aap par keh aap zulm-o-sitam se shaheed kiye gaye. Rasool-e-Arabi (s.a.w.a) aap ke khoon ka badla lene ke daawe-daar bane. Aur aap ki shahadat se Kitaab-e-Khuda matrook ho gayi (logo ne Khuda ki kitaab ko chhod diya). Aap par salaam ho aur aap ke nana par aap ke waalid par aap ke waaleda par aur aap ke bhai par salaam ho, aap ki aulaad mein Aimma (a.s) par salaam ho. Aap ke ham-raah (saath) shaheed ho ne waalo par salaam ho. Un farishto par salaam ho jo aap ki qabr ke ird-gird rehte hai aur aap ke zaahiro ke gawah hai aur aap se mohabbat karne waalo ki dua’o ki qabooliyat ke liye aameen kehte hai aur salaam ho aap par aur Khuda ki rehmat ho aur uski barkat ho. Qurbaan aap par mere maa-baap ay Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a) ke farzand. qurbaan aap par mere maa-baap ay Aba Abdillah (a.s). Yaqeenan aap ki sogh-waari aur aap ki museebat bohot bhaari (azeem) hai ham par aur un sab par jo aasmaano aur zameeno mein rehte hai. Pas Khuda ki laanat ho us giroh (group) par jisne (ghode ko) zeen kasa aur lagaam di aur aap se ladne ke liye ghode taiyyaar kiye. Ay mere aaqa, ay Aba Abdillah (a.s) mai ne aap ke haram ka iraada kiya aur aap ke mazaar par haazir huwa. Sawaal karta hu Khuda se aap ke shaan ke wasile se jo uske yahan hai aur aap ke martabe ke waaste se jo uske huzoor hai. Yeh keh Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil kare, aur yeh keh mujhe aap ke saath rakkhe duniya-o-aakherat mein apne ehsaan, bakhshish aur ataa se kaam lete huwe. (Aameen)

Mai gawaahi deta hu keh aap ne Khuda aur uske Rasool (s.a.w.a) aur apne bhai ki badi khair-khwahi ki. To aap kya hi achchhe fida-kaar bhai hai.

Pas Khuda ki laanat ho aap ko qatl karne waalo par. Khuda ki laanat ho uspar jisne aap par zulm kiya. Aur Khuda ki laanat ho aap ki hurmato ko zaaye karne waalo par. Aur laanat ho aap ke qatl se Islam ki be-hurmati karne waalo par. Pas woh achchhe bhai hai, sabr waale, jehaad karne waale, himayat karne waale, aur nusrat karne waale aur woh bhai hai jo apne bhai ka difa (defence) karne waale hai. Khuda ki ita’at mein haazir ho ne waale, aur us cheez ki taraf badhne waale hai jisse dusro ne mooh moda woh hai bohot bada sawaab aur behtareen taareef. Pas Khuda aap ko aap ke buzurgo ke maqaam par pohonchaye, nemato bhaari Jannat mein keh woh (Allah) qaabil-e-taareef aur nehaayat izzat waala hai.

Phir zareeh-e-mubarak se lipat kar kahe…

Ay Ma’bood! Tere saamne haazir huwa hu, Tere pyaaro ki ziyarat ke liye chalke aaya hu, Teri taraf se sawaab ki khwahish mein, Teri taraf se maghferat ki aarzoo aur Teri maherbaani ki ummeed mein. Pas sawaal karta hu Tujh se keh Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat farma aur unke wasile se mere rizq mein izaafa farma, meri zindagi ko pur-sukoon, meri ziyarat ko qubool, aur mere gunaho ko maaf farma de, aur unke waaste se mujhe kaam-yaab-o-kaamraan lauta. Qubool-e-dua ke saath keh jis behtareen surat mein Tu ne unke zaahiro aur unki taraf aane waalo mein se kisi ko lautaaya. Apni rehmat ke saath ay sab se ziyaada rahem karne waale. Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

Phir zareeh-e-mubarak par bosa de aur 2-rakat namaaz-e-ziyarat adaa kare aur uska sawaab Maula Abul Fazlil Abbas (a.s) ko hadiya kare.

ARAFAH

Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) says: One who perform ghusl and wear clean cloths and recite this ziyarat of Imam Hussain (a.s) on the day of Arafah. Allah (s.w.t) will reward him the sawaab of accepted 1000 Hajj, 1000 Umrah and 1000 Jehad which he has performed with Prophet or Imam. Then he said Allah (s.w.t) will look with mercy on the zayereen of Karbala before he turn towards the hujjaj on the day of arafah.

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Allah is the Most Great of all. All praise be to Allah very much. Glory be to Allah in mornings and evenings. All praise be to Allah Who has guided us to this. We would not have been guided if Allah had not guided us. The messengers of our Lord have indeed come with the truth. Peace be upon the Messenger of Allah, peace of Allah be upon him and his Household. Peace be upon the Commander of the Faithful. Peace be upon Fatimah the luminous and the doyenne of all women of the world. Peace be upon al-Hasan and al-Husayn. Peace be upon Ali the son of al-Husayn. Peace be upon Muhammad the son of Ali. Peace be upon Ja`far the son of Muhammad. Peace be upon Musa the son of Ja`far. Peace be upon Ali the son of Musa. Peace be upon Muhammad the son of Ali. Peace be upon Ali the son of Muhammad. Peace be upon al-Hasan the son of Ali. Peace be upon the virtuous Successor and Awaited. Peace be upon you, O Abu Abdullah. Peace be upon you, O son of Allah’s Messenger. I the servant of you and the son of your servant and your bondwoman, who is loyal to those who are loyal to you and enemy of those who are your enemies am seeking the shelter of your shrine and seeking nearness to Allah through directing to you. All praise be to Allah Who guided me to be loyal to you, Who conferred upon me with my visiting you, and Who made easy for me to direct to you.

You may then enter the shrine, stop next to the side of the Imam’s head, and say the following words…

Peace be upon you, O inheritor of Adam the chosen by Allah. Peace be upon you, O inheritor of Noah the prophet of Allah. Peace be upon you, O inheritor of Abraham the friend of Allah. Peace be upon you, O inheritor of Moses the spoken by Allah. Peace be upon you, O inheritor of Jesus the spirit of Allah. Peace be upon you, O inheritor of Muhammad the most beloved by Allah. Peace be upon you, O inheritor of the Commander of the Faithful. Peace be upon you, O inheritor of Fatimah the luminous. Peace be upon you, O son of Muhammad the well-chosen prophet. Peace be upon you, O son of Ali the well-pleased. Peace be upon you, O son of Fatimah the luminous. Peace be upon you, O son of Khadijah the grand lady. Peace be upon you, O vengeance of Allah and son of His vengeance and the unavenged sanctity. I bear witness that you performed the prayers, defrayed the poor-rates, enjoined the right, forbade the wrong, and obeyed Allah until death came upon you. May Allah curse the people who slew you. May Allah curse the people who wronged you. May Allah curse the people who were informed about that but they approved it. O my master! O Abu Abdullah! I call Allah, His angels, His Prophets, and His Messengers to witness that I believe in you and in your Return and I have faith in the laws of my religion and in the sealing deeds of mine, and in my final return to my Lord. Allah’s blessings be upon you all, upon your souls, upon your bodies, upon the present and the absent (of your group), and upon the manifest and the hidden (of your affairs). Peace be upon you, O son of the Seal of Prophets, son of the chief of the Prophets’ successors, son of the leader of the pious, and son of the white-forehead, marked believers. How can that you be anything other than this while you are the door to the true guidance, the guide to piety, the firmest handle, the argument against the inhabitants of this world, and the fifth of the People of the Cloak. You were nourished by the hand of mercy, breast-fed by the bosom of faith, and brought up in the lap of Islam. So, my soul is definitely displeased by the way you left this world and it has no doubt that you are alive. Allah’s blessings be upon you, your ascendants, and your descendants. Peace be upon you, O object of the pouring tears and match of the astounding misfortune. May Allah curse the people who violated your sanctities and infringed the holiness of Islam. So, you may Allah’s blessings be upon you were killed overwhelmed, the Messenger of Allah, Allah’s blessings be upon him and his Household, became cut off because of loss of you, and the Book of Allah became deserted due to losing you. Peace be upon you, your grandfather, your father, your mother, your brother, the Imams from your descendants, those who were martyred with you, and the angels who are surrounding your tomb, witnessing for your visitors, and praying for the acceptance of the prayers of your adherents. Peace and Allah’s mercy and blessings be upon you. May my father and mother be ransoms for you, O son of Allah’s Messenger. May my father and mother be ransoms for you, O Abu Abdullah. So unbearable is the misfortune and so horrendous is the calamity of you for us and for all the inhabitants of the heavens and the earth. May Allah curse the people who put saddles and reins on their animals and readied themselves for fighting against you. O my master! O Abu Abdullah! I have directed to your sanctuary and I have come to your shrine asking Almighty Allah in the name of the standing that you enjoy with Him and in the name of the position that you occupy with him to send blessings upon Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad and to include me with your group in this world and in the Hereafter, out of His favor, magnanimity, and generosity.

Kiss the shrine and recite 2 rakats namaz above the head and then recite…

O Allah, I have offered a prayer, genuflected, and prostrated myself for Your sake purely without setting any partner with You, since all prayers, genuflections, and prostrations suit none except You. Because You are Allah; there in no god save You. O Allah, (please) send blessings upon Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad, convey to them my best greetings and compliments, and convey to me their replies to my greetings and compliments. O Allah, these two units of prayer are present from me to my master, chief, and leader, al-Husayn the son of Ali, peace be upon both of them. O Allah, send blessings upon Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad and accept that from me and reward me for it in the best rewarding that I anticipate and hope from You and from Your saint. O most merciful of all those who show mercy!

Then he should get up and go to the foot of Imam Husain (a.s) and recite the Ziarat of Janab Ali Akbar (a.s)and recite the following…

Peace be upon you, O son of Allah’s Messenger. Peace be upon you, O son of Allah’s Prophet. Peace be upon you, O son of the Commander of the Faithful. Peace be upon you, O son of al-Husayn the martyr. Peace be upon you, O martyr and son of the martyr. Peace be upon you, O persecuted and son of the persecuted. May Allah curse the people who slew you. May Allah curse the people who wronged you. May Allah curse the people who were informed of that and they approved it. Peace be upon you, O my master. Peace be upon you, O Allah’s friend and son of His friend. Unbearable is the misfortune and so horrendous is the calamity of you for us and for all faithful believers. May Allah curse the people who slew you. I release myself in the presence of Allah and you from these people in this world and in the Hereafter.

After this recite the Ziarat of the martyrs of Kerbala…

Peace be upon you, O Allah’s friends and beloved by him. Peace be upon you, O choices of Allah and His favorites. Peace be upon you, O supporters of Allah’s Religion, supporters of His Prophet, supporters of the Commander of the Faithful, and supporters of Fatimah the doyenne of the women of the world. Peace be upon you, O supporters of Ab£-Muhammad al-Hasan the sincere saint. Peace be upon you, O supporters of Abu Abdullah al-Husayn the wronged martyr. Blessings of Allah be upon them all. May my father and mother be your ransoms. Pure are you, and pure is the soil in which you were buried. And, by Allah, great is your triumph. I wish I were with you so that I would have won with you in the gardens of Paradise with the martyrs and the righteous. Excellent is the companionship of these. Peace and Allah’s mercy and blessings be upon you all.

Then he should go at the head side of Imam Husain (a.s) and pray for his family members and believers as much as possible…

Then he should go to the shrine of Hazrat Abbas (a.s) and recite as follows…

Peace be upon you, O Abul-Fazl al-Abbas son of the Commander of the Faithful. Peace be upon you, O son of the chief of the Prophets’ successors. Peace be upon you, O son of the foremost in accepting Islam, the leading in having faith in it, the most perfect in carrying out the laws of Allah’s religion, and the most careful for Islam. I bear witness that you have acted sincerely for the sake of Allah, His Messenger, and your brother. So, how excellent sacrificing brother you are! May Allah curse the people who slew you. May Allah curse the people who wronged you. May Allah curse the people who violated your sanctities and infringed the holiness of Islam when they killed you. Most excellent steadfast, striver brother, defender, supporter, one who stood up for his brother, one who responded to the obedience to his Lord, and one who desired for what others deemed unworthy; namely, the abundant reward and the excellent praise you are. May Allah attach you to the rank of your ancestors in the Abode of Bliss.

Then he should throw himself on the grave and say…

O Allah, I am presenting myself before You and I am intending to visit your intimate servants, for I desire for gaining Your reward and I hope for Your forgiveness and abundant benevolence. So, I beseech You to send blessings upon Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad, and, (I beseech You) in their names, to make my sustenance unstoppable, my life peaceful, my pilgrimage admissible, my life serene, and my sins forgiven. And (I beseech You to) make me return from them with success and prosperity, having my prayers granted, with the best things with which any one of his visitors and comers may return; in the name of Your mercy, O most merciful of all those who show mercy.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

Then recite 2 rakat namaz-e-ziyarat and pray for your needs.
