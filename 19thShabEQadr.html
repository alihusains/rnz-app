19TH SHAB-E-QADR

1) Recite 100 times…

Astaghferullaha Rabi wa atoobo ilai-he.

I seek the forgiveness of Allah, my Lord, and I repent before Him.

2) Recite 100 times…

Allahummal-an qatalata Ameeril Momineen.

O Allah! Curse the killers of the Commander of the Believers (Imam Ali (a.s)).

3) Recite Dua no.5 from the supplications of every night.
