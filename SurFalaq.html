113 | FALAQ | 5

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Kahe dijiye keh mai subh ke maalik ki panah chahta hu.

2. Tamaam makhluqaat ke shar se.

3. Aur andheri raat ke shar se jab uska andhera chha jaaye.

4. Aur gando (knot) par phoonk-ne waaliyo ke shar se.

5. Aur har hasad karne waale ke shar se jab bhi woh hasad kare.

113 | FALAQ | 5

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of the dawn,

2. From the evil of what He has created,

3. And from the evil of the utterly dark night when it comes,

4. And from the evil of those who blow on knots,

5. And from the evil of the envious when he envies.
