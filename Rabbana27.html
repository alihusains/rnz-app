Chp. 14:Ver. 41
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَا اغْفِرْ لِيْ وَلِوَالِدَيَّ وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِيْنَ يَوْمَ يَقُوْمُ الْحِسَابُ۔
O our Lord! Grant me protection and my parents and the believers on the day when the reckoning shall come to pass!

Parwardigaar! Mujhe aur mere waaledain ko aur tamaam momenin ko us din bakhsh dena jis din hisaab qaayem hoga.
