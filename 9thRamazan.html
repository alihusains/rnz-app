1) 6 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 7 martaba Aayat Al-Khursi padhe. Namaz ke baad 50 martaba Salawat padhe.

SHAB KI DUA...

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Aaqa, ay Parwardigaar, ay saaheb-e-jalaalat aur buzurgi, ay woh saaheb-e-arsh, keh jis ko kabhi neend nahi aati aur aisi izzat ka maalik jis ka qasd (iraada) nahi kiya ja sakta.

Ay umoor ko anjaam dene waale, ay bimaar dilo ki tashaffi (shafa) karne waale mere umoor mein Tu khushaadgi ataa kar aur Tu apni ummeedo ko mere qalb mein daal de, ta-keh Tere siwa kisi aur se mai ummeede na baandhu.

Ay mere aaqa Tujh par mai bharosa karta hu aur Teri hi taraf rujoo karta hu aur Teri hi taraf baaz-gasht hai. Mai sawaal karta hu Tujh se ay tamaam ma’boodo ke Ma’bood aur ay zabardasto ke zabardast aur ay sab bado ke bade.

Ay aise, keh jo tawakkul kare uski kifaayat kare aur jo tawakkul karne waale ke liye kaafi ho aur uske umoor dilwa de, mai ne Tujh hi par tawakkul kiya ay Khuda pas meri kifaayat kar aur Teri hi taraf rujoo kiya pas rahem kar mujh par aur Teri hi taraf baaz-gasht hai pas bakhsh de mere gunaho ko.

Aur us roz, keh jis roz kuchh chehre siya (black) honge aur kuchh safed (white) honge mera chehra siya (black) na karna, Tu saaheb-e-izzat o hikmat hai. Khuda-wand rehmat naazil farma Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Mohammad (a.s) par aur rahem kar mujh par aur dar-guzar farma mujh se, yaqeenan Tu bakhsh ne waala aur rahem karne waala hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Aaj ke din mujhe Apni wasee rehmat mein se bohot zeyaada hissa de, us mein mujh ko Apni roshan dalaayel ki hidaayat farma, aur meri mehaar (nakaal) pakad kar mujhe Apni har su phaile huwe razaao ki taraf le ja Apni mohabbat se. Ay shauq (wo tamanna) rakhne waalo ki aarzu.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

9 | MAHE RAMAZAN

NAMAZ…

1) Recite 6-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Aayat Al-Khursi 7 times. After Namaz recite Salawat 50 times.

NIGHT DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Chief, O Lord, O one with majesty and nobility. O the owner of the throne who is not smitten by sleep and O the powerful one who cannot be reached. O the one who decides the affairs, O the curer of the sick hearts, give me ease in my affairs and put Your hopes in my heart so that I do not repose hopes in anyone else. My Chief, I rely upon You alone and to You alone I refer and the return is to You alone. I ask You O the deity of all deities and the most forceful of the forceful ones and the greatest of the great. O one who is such that if one relies on You, You suffice him. And one who is sufficient for one who relies on Him. And who completes His affairs. I have relied on You alone, O Allah, suffice me. And I have referred to You only; so have mercy on me. And the return is to You alone, so forgive my sins on the day when some faces will be dark. Indeed You are Powerful and Wise. O Allah, bless Muhammad and Aale Muhammad (a.s.) and have mercy on me and overlook my faults. Indeed You are the forgiving and merciful.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah give me, in this month, a very large share from Thy boundless mercy, let me keep to the right way that leads on to Thy evident clear Signs (Muhammad and Aale Muhammad (a.s)), and for the sake of Thy love and kindness direct any behavior, from every angle, unto that which please Thee, O the Ultimate Hope of those who eagerly desire fulfillment.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
