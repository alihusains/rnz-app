بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اِلٰهِىْ اَسْكَنْتَنَا دَارًا حَفَرَتْ لَنَا حُفَرَ مَكْرِهَا وَعَلَّقَتْنَا بِاَيْدِى الْمَنَايَا فِىْ حَبَآئِلِ غَدْرِهَا فَاِلَيْكَ نَلْتَجِءُ مِنْ مَكَآئِدِ خُدَ عِهَا وَبِكَ نَعْتَصِمُ مِنَ الْاِغْتِرَارِ بِزَخَارِفِ زِيْنَتِهَا فَاِنَّهَا الْمُهْلِكَةُ طُلَّابَهَا الْمُتْلِفَةُ حَلّٰالَهَا الْمَحْشُوَّةُ بِالْاٰفَاتِ الْمَشْحُوْنَةُ بِالنَّكَبَاتِ۔

اِلٰهِىْ فَزَهِّدْنَا فِيْهَا وَسَلِّمْنَا مِنْهَا بِتَوْفِيْقِكَ وَعِصْمَتِكَ وَانْزَعْ عَنَّا جَلَابِيْبَ مُخَالَفَتِكَ وَتَوَّلَ اُمُوْرَنَا بِحُسْنِ كـِفَايَتِكَ وَاَوْفِرْ مَزِيْدَنَا مِنْ سَعَةِ رَحْمَتِكَ وَاَجْمِلْ صِلَاتِنَا مِنْ فَيْضِ مَوَاهِبِكَ وَاَغْرِسْ فِىْ اَفْئِدَتِنَا اَشْجَارَ مَحَبَّتِكَ وَاَتْمِمْ لَنَا اَنْوَارَ مَعْرِفَتِكَ وَاَذِقْنَا حَلَاوَةَ عَفْوِكَ وَ لَذَّةَ مَغْفِرَتِكَ وَ اَقْرِرْ اَعْيُنَنَا يَوْمَ لِقَآئِكَ بِرُؤْيَتِكَ وَاَخْرِجْ حُبَّ الدُّنْيَا مِنْ قُلُوْبِنَا كَمَا فَعَلْتَ بِالصَّالِحِيْنَ مِنْ صَفْوَتِكَ وَالْاَبْرَارِ مِنْ خَآصَّتِكَ بِرَحْمَتِكَ يَآاَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِيْنَ وَيَا اَكْرَمَ الْاَكْرَمِيْنَ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

Zahedeen
(Abstainers)

Zaahido ki Munajaat…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Mere Mabood! Tu ne Hamein aise ghar mein rakkha jisko fareb ke kodaal ne (dhoke ne) hamare liye khoda hai. Aur Tu ne hamein aarzuwo ke haatho uske fareb ki rassio mein jakad diya hai. Pas ham is duniya ke farebiyo aur makkaariyo se Teri panah chaahte hai. Aur ham uski jhooti zeenato ke dhoko (cheatings) se bachne ke liye Tera mazboot daaman pakad-te hai. Keh yeh (duniya) apne talab-gaaro ko halaaq karne waali aur yahan aane waalo ko tabaah karne waali aafato se bhaari bad-haaliyo se pur hai.

Mere Mabood! Hamein us mein zohod (paarsaayi) ataa farma aur Apni madad aur hifaazat ke saath ussey bachaaye rakh. Apni mukhaalefat ki chaadaro ko ham se juda kar de. Apni behtareen kifaayat se hamare umoor ki sarprasti farma, hamare liye Apni wasee rehmat faraawaan aur zeyaada kar de, apni ataawo ke faiz se hamein behtareen jazaaye de. Hamare dilo’n mein Apni ulfat (mohabbat) ke darakht laga de, hamein Apni maarefat ke sabhi anwaar ataa kar de, aur Apne afvo (darguzari) ki shirini aur bakhshish ki lazzat ka zaayeqa chakha. Apni mulaaqaat ke din Apne Jamaal se hamari aankhe thandi farma, aur hamare dilo’n se duniya ki mohabbat nikaal de. Jaisa keh Tune Apne makhsoos neku-kaaro aur Apne chune huwe khush-kirdaar logo’n ke liye kiya hai. Tujhe waasta Teri rehmat ka ay sab se zeyaada rahem karne waale, aur ay sab se zeyaada karam karne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

The Whispered Prayer of the Abstainers (Zahedeen)…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

My God, Thou hast settled us in an abode which has dug for us pits of deception, and Thou hast fastened us by the hands of death in the snares of that abode's treachery! In Thee we seek asylum from the tricks of its guile, and to Thee we hold fast, lest we be deluded by the glitter of its ornaments! It destroys its pursuers and ruins its settlers, it is stuffed with blights and loaded with calamities.

My God, induce us to renounce it and keep us safe from it by Thy giving success and Thy preservation from sin. Strip from us the robes of opposing Thee, attend to our affairs through Thy good sufficiency, amplify our increase from the boundless plenty of Thy mercy, be liberal in our gifts from the overflow of Thy grants, plant in our hearts the trees of Thy love, complete for us the lights of Thy knowledge, give us to taste the sweetness of Thy pardon and the pleasure of Thy forgiveness, gladden our eyes on the day of meeting Thee with the vision of Thee, dislodge the love of this world from our spirits, just as Thou hast done for the righteous, Thy selected friends, and for the pious, those whom Thou hast singled out! O Most Merciful of the merciful, O Most Generous of the most generous!

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
