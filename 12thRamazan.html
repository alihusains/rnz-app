SHAB KI DUA...

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Khudaaya Tu hi buland o bartar hai, Tere hi liye aisi hamd o sataaish saza-waar hai jo kabi bhi phanah nahi hogi bal-keh hamesha baaqi rehne waali hai aur Tu hi zinda aur burd-baar hai.

Mai Tujh se Teri buzurg martaba zaat ki nooraaniyat o jalaalat ke sahaare se woh jalaalat jis ka qasd (iraada) nahi kiya ja sakta aur Tere us ghalba o iqtedaar ke zariye se keh jo maghloob nahi ho sakta.

Sawaal karta hu keh Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur mere gunah bakhsh de aur mujh par rahem farma, yaqeenan Tu tamaam rahem karne waalo mein sab se zeyaada raheem hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Aaj ke din mujhe parde aur paak-daamani se zeenat de, isme mujhe qana’at aur khuddaari ke libaas se dhaanp de, isme mujhe adl wo insaaf par aamadah (ready) farma. Aur isme mujhe Apni panah ke saath un cheezo’n se aman de jin se darta hu. Ay darne waalo ki panah-gah.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

12 | MAHE RAMAZAN

NAMAZ…

1) Recite 8-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Qadr (Chp-97) 30 times.

NIGHT DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, You are the High and the Great. Praise is only for You; such a praise which will never be destroyed. And You are the living, the forbearing. I beg You for the sake of the effulgence of Your noble face and for the sake of the majesty of Your face which is unreachable and Your power which cannot be suppressed that You bless Muhammad and Aale Muhammad (a.s) and that You forgive me and have mercy on me and overlook my mistakes. Indeed You are the forgiving and merciful.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, in this month, beautify me with the trimming and frills of modesty and chastity, let contentment and (self) satisfaction be my daily wear, let me adhere to justice and keep me safe from all that which terrify me, through Thy support, O He who gives support to the frightened.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
