15 | MAHE RAMAZAN

NAMAZ…

1) Perform Ghusl.
1) Ghusl kare.

2) Recite 4-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In 1st & 2nd Rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) 100 times. And in 3rd & 4th Rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) 50 times.

2) 4 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, 1st & 2nd Rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 100 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) padhe. Aur 3rd aur 4th Rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 50 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) padhe.


3) Recite 6 rakats namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp۔1) recite Surah Yasin (Chp۔36), Surah Mulk (Chp۔67) and Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112).

3) 6 Rakat namaz padhe, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 1 martaba Surah Yasin (Chp-36), Surah Mulk (Chp۔67) and Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) padhe


4) Recite 100-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) 10 times.

4) 100 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 10 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) padhe.


5) Recite Dua-e-Mujeer.
4) Dua-e-Mujeer padhe.

6) Recite the Ziyarat of Imam Hasan (a.s).
6) Ziyarat-e-Imam Hasan (a.s) padhe.

7) Recite the Ziyarat of Imam Husain (a.s).
7) Ziyarat-e-Imam Husain (a.s) padhe.

SHAB KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay mere Aaqa Tu hi haqeeqi maher-baan hai, ay mere Maula Tu hi ehsaan karne waala hai, ay Aaqa Tu hi kareem hai, ay Maula Tu hi bakhsh ne waala hai, ay Aaqa Tu hi burdbaar hai, ay Maula Tu hi bahot ataa karne waala hai, ay Aaqa Tu hi izzat waala hai, ay Aaqa Tu hi nazdeek hai.

Ay Aaqa bas Tu hi yekta hai, ay Maula Tu hi to ghaalib hai, ay Aaqa Tu be-neyaaz hai, ay Maula Tu hi izzat-daar hai. Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur mujh se dar-guzar farma, Tu hi sab se buzurg o azeem hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

NIGHT DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O my Chief, You are the one who is truly merciful. O my master, You are the one who bestows gifts. O my Chief, You are the noble. O my master, You are forgiving. O my Chief, You are the forbearing. O my master, You are the giver. O my Chief, You are the powerful. O my master, You are the Near. O my Chief, You are the One. O my master, You are the forceful. O my Chief, You are the needless. O my master, You are the powerful. Bless Muhammad and his progeny, forgive me and have mercy on me and overlook my mistakes. Indeed You are the most majestic and the most great.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah keep me in this month, in submissiveness the repentant unpretentious have recourse to and benefit by; instill in my heart the repentance of those who humble themselves before Thee to obtain Thy protection; in the name of Thy sanctuary, O the refuge of the terrified.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
