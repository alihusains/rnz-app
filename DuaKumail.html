KUMAIYL
According to Allama Majlisi (a.r) Kumayl called at the house of Imam Ali, and requested him to acquaint him with Prophet Khizr's Du'a. Imam Ali asked Kumayl to sit down, record and memorise the dua which Imam Ali dictated to Kumayl. Imam Ali then advised Kumayl to recite this dua on the eve of every Friday, or once a month or at least once in every year so that, Allah may protect thee from the evils of the enemies and the plots contrived by impostors…
Yeh Janabe Khizr (a.s) ki dua’o mein se ek dua hai. Jise Maula-e-Qayenaat Hazrat Ali (a.s) ne apne sahabi Kumail bin Ziyaad (a.r) ko taleem di thi aur farmaya tha: ay Kumail! agar tum is dua ko har shab-e-Juma’a ek baar ya har mahine mein ek baar ya saal mein ek martaba ya phir saari zindagi mein sirf ek martaba padh lo to Khuda se qurbat, takmeel-e-haajaat, mushkilaat ka hal, gunaho ki maghferat, rozi mein wus’at (izaafa), aur dushmano se najaat paane ka behtareen zariya hai…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰہُمَّ اِنِّیْٓ اَسْئَلُکَ بِرَحْمَتِکَ الَّتِیْ وَسِعَتْ کُلَّ شَیْٓءٍ وَّبِقُوَّتِکَ الَّتِیْ قَھَرْتَ بِھَا کُلَّ شَیْٓءٍ وَخَضَعَ لَھَا کُلُّ شَیْٓءٍ وَّذَلَّ لَھَا کُلُّ شَیْٓءٍ وَّبِجَبَرُوْتِکَ الَّتِیْ غَلَبْتَ بِھَا کُلَّ شَیْٓءٍ وَّبِعِزَّتِکَ الَّتِیْ لَا یَقُوْمُ لَھَا شَیْءٌ وَّبِعَظَمَتِکَ الَّتِی مَلَاتْ کُلَّ شَیْٓءٍ وَّبِسُلْطَانِکَ الَّذِیْ عَلَا کُلَّ شَیْٓءٍ وَّبِوَجْھِکَ الْبَاقِیْ بَعْدَفَنَآئِ کُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ وَبِاَسْمَآئِکَ الَّتِیْ مَلَاتْ اَرْکَانَ کُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ وَّبِعِلْمِکَ الَّذِیْ اَحَاطَ بِکُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ وَّبِنُوْرِ وَجْھِکَ الَّذِیْ اَضَآئَ لَہٗ کُلُّ شَیْٓءٍ ۔

یَانُوْرُ یَاقُدُّوْسُ یَااَوَّلَ الْاَوَّلِیْنَ وَیَا آخِرَ الْآخِرِیْنَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِیَ الذُّنُوْبَ الَّتِیْ تَھْتِکُ الْعِصَمَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اغْفِرْلِیَ الذُّنُوْبَ الَّتِیْ تُنْزِلُ النِّقَمَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِیَ الذُّنُوْبَ الَّتِی تُغَیِّرُ النِّعَمَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِیَ الذُّنُوْبَ الَّتِیْ تَحْبِسُ الدُّعَآئَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِیَ الذُّنُوْبَ الَّتِیْ تَقْطَعُ الرَّجَآئَ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ اغْفِرْلِیَ الذُّنُوْبَ الَّتِیْ تُنْزِلُ الْبَلَآئَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِیْ کُلَّ ذَنْبٍ اَذْنَبْتُہٗ وَکُلَّ خَطِیْئَۃٍ اَخْطَاْتُھَا۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اِنِّیْٓ اَتَقَرَّبُ اِلَیْکَ بِذِکْرِکَ وَاَسْتَشْفِعُ بِکَ اِلٰی نَفْسِکَ وَاَسْئَلُکَ بِجُوْدِکَ اَنْ تُدْنِیَنِیْ مِنْ قُرْبِکَ وَاَنْ تُوزِ عَنِیْ شُکْرَکَ وَاَنْ تُلْھِمَنِیْ ذِکْرَکَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اِنِّیْٓ اَسْئَلُکَ سُؤَالَ خَاضِعٍ مُّتَذَلِّلٍ خَاشِعٍ مُّتَضَرِّعٍ اَنْ تُسَامِحَنِیْ وَتَرْحَمَنِیْ وَتَجْعَلَنِیْ بِقِسْمِکَ رَاضِیًا قَانِعًا وَفِیْ جَمِیْعِ الْاَ حْوَالِ مُتَوَاضِعًا۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ وَاَسْئَلُکَ سُؤَالَ مَنِ اشْتَدَّتْ فَاقَتُہٗ وَاَنْزَلَ بِکَ عِنْدَ الشَّدَآئِدِ حَاجَتَہٗ وَعَظُمَ فِیْمَا عِنْدَکَ رَغْبَتُہٗ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ عَظُمَ سُلْطَانُکَ وَعَلَا مَکَانُکَ وَخَفِیَ مَکْرُکَ وَظَھَرَ اَمْرُکَ وَغَلَبَ قَھْرُکَ وَجَرَتْ قُدْرَتُکَ وَلَا یُمْکِنُ الْفِرَارُ مِنْ حُکُوْمَتِکَ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ لَا اَجِدُ لِذُنُوْبِیْ غَافِرًا وَلَالِقَبَآ ئِحِیْ سَاتِرًا وَلَا لِشَیْٓءٍ مِّنْ عَمَلِیَ الْقَبِیْحِ بِالْحَسَنِ مُبَدِّلًا غَیْرَکَ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّآ اَنْتَ سُبْحَانَکَ وَبِحَمْدِکَ ظَلَمْتُ نَفْسِیْ وَتَجَرَّاتُ بِجَھْلِیْ وَسَکَنْتُ اِلٰی قَدِیْمِ ذِکْرِکَ لِیْ وَمَنِّکَ عَلَیَّ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ مَوْلَایَ کَمْ مِّنْ قَبِیْحٍ سَتَرْتَہٗ وَکَمْ مِّنْ فَادِحٍ مِّنَ الْبَلَآئِ اَقَلْتَہٗ وَکَمْ مِّنْ عِثَارٍ وَّ قَیْتَہٗ وَکَمْ مِّنْ مَّکْرُوْہٍ دَفَعْتَہٗ وَکَمْ مِنْ ثَنَآئٍ جَمِیْلٍ لَّسْتُ اَھْلًا لَّہٗ نَشَرْتَہٗ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ عَظُمَ بَلَآئِیْ وَاَفْرَطَ بِیْ سُوْٓئُ حَالِیْ وَقَصُرَتْ بِیْٓ اَعْمَالِیْ وَقَعَدَتْ بِیْٓ اَغْلَالِیْ وَحَبَسَنِیْ عَنْ نَفْعِیْ بُعْدُ اٰمَالِی وَخَدَعَتْنِی الدُّنْیَا بِغُرُوْرِھَا وَنَفْسِیْ بِخِیَانَتِھَا وَمِطَالِیْ یَاسَیِّدِیْ فَاسْئَلُکَ بِعِزَّتِکَ اَنْ لَّایَحْجُبَ عَنْکَ دُعَآئِیْ سُوْٓئُ عَمَلِیْ وَفِعَالِیْ وَلَاتَفْضَحْنِیْ بِخَفِیِّ مَا اطَّلَعْتَ عَلَیْہِ مِنْ سِرِّیْ وَلَاتُعَاجِلْنِیْ بِالْعُقُوْبَۃِ عَلٰی مَا عَمِلْتُہٗ فِیْ خَلَوَاتِیْ مِنْ سُوْٓئِ فِعْلِی وَاِسَآئَ تِیْ وَدَوَامِ تَفْرِیْطِیْ وَجَھَالَتِیْ وَکَثرَۃِ شَھَوَاتِی وَغَفْلَتِیْ وَکُنِ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ بِعِزَّتِکَ لِیْ فِیْ کُلِّ الْاَحْوَالِ رَؤُفًا وَّعَلَیَّ فِیْ جَمِیْعِ الْاُمُوْرِ عَطُوْ فًا اِلٰھِیْ وَمَوْلَایَ وَ رَبِّیْ مَنْ لِیْ غَیْرُکَ اَسْئَلُہٗ کَشْفَ ضُرِّیْ وَالنَّظَرَفِیْ اَمْرِیْ اِلٰھِیْ وَمَوْلَایَ اَجْرَیْتَ عَلَیَّ حُکْمًانِاتَّبَعْتُ فِیْہِ ھَوَیٰ نَفْسِیْ وَلَمْ اَحْتَرِسْ فِیْہِ مِنْ تَزْیِیْنِ عَدُوِّیْ فَغَرَّنِیْ بَمَا اَھْوَیٰ وَاَسْعَدَہٗ عَلٰی ذَلِکَ الْقَضَآئُ فَتَجَاوَزْتُ بِمَا جَرٰی عَلَیَّ مِنْ ذٰلِکَ بَعْضَ حُدُوْدِکَ وَخَالَفْتُ بَعْضَ اَوَامِرِکَ۔

فَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ عَلَیَّ فِیْ جَمِیْعِ ذٰلِکَ وَلَاحُجَّۃَ لِیْ فِیْمَا جَرٰی عَلَیَّ فِیْہِ قَضَآؤُکَ وَاَلْزَمَنِیْ حُکْمُکَ وَبَلَآؤُکَ وَقَدْ اَتَیْتُکَ یَااِلٰہِیْ بَعْدَ تَقْصِیْرِیْ وَاِسْرَافِیْ عَلٰی نَفْسِیْ مُعْتَذِرًا نَادِمًا مُنْکَسِرًا مُسْتَقِیْلًا مُسْتَغْفِرًا مُنِیْبًا مُّقِرًّا مُّذْعِنًا مُعْتَرِفًا لَا اَجِدُ مَفَرًّا مِّمَّا کَانَ مِنِّیْ وَلَا مَفْزَعًا اَتَوَجَّہٗ اِلَیْہِ فِیْ اَمْرِیْ غَیْرَ قَبُوْلِکَ عُذْرِیْ وَاِدْخَالِکَ اِیَّایَ فِیْ سَعَۃِ مِّن رَّحْمَتِکَ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ فَاقْبَلْ عُذْرِیْ وَارْحَمْ شِدَّۃَ ضُرِّی وَفُکَّنِیْ مِنْ شَدِّ وَثَا قِیْ یَارَبِّ ارْحَمْ ضَعْفَ بَدَنِیْ وَرِقَّۃَ جِلْدِیْ وَدِقَّۃَ عَظْمِیْ یَامَنْ بَدَاَ خَلْقِیْ وَذِکْرِیْ وَتَرْبِیَتِیْ وَبِرِّیْ وَتَغْذِیَتِیْ ھَبْنِیْ لِاِبْتِدَآئِ کَرَمِکَ وَسَالِفِ بِرِّکَ بِیْ۔

یَااِلٰھِیْ وَسَیِّدِیْ وَرَبِّیْ اَتُرَاکَ مُعَذِّبِیْ بِنَارِکَ بَعْدَ تَوْحِیْدِکَ وَبَعْدَ مَاانْطَوٰی عَلَیْہِ قَلْبِیْ مِنْ مَّعْرِفَتِکَ وَلَھِجَ بِہٖ لِسَانِیْ مِنْ ذِکْرِکَ وَاعْتَقَدَہٗ ضَمِیْرِیْ مِنْ حُبِّکَ وَبَعْدَ صِدْقِ اعْتِرَافِیْ وَدُعَآئِیْ خَاضِعًا لِرُبُوْ بِیَّتِکَ ھَیْھَاتَ اَنْتَ اَکْرَمُ مِنْ اَنْ تُضَیِّعَ مَنْ رَّبَّیْتَہٗ اَوْتُبَعِّدَ مَنْ اَدْنَیْتَہٗ اَوْ تُشَرِّدَ مَنْ آوَیْتَہٗ اَوْتُسَلِّمَ اِلَی الْبَلَآئِ مَنْ کَفَیْتَہٗ وَرَحِمْتَہٗ وَلَیْتَ شِعْرِیْ۔

یَاسَیِّدِیْ وَاِلٰھِیْ وَمَوْلَایَ اَتُسَلِّطُ النَّارَ عَلٰی وُجُوْہٍ خَرَّتْ لِعَظَمَتِکَ سَاجِدَۃً وَّعَلٰی اَلْسُنٍ نَطَقَتْ بِتَوْحِیْدِکَ صَادِقَۃً وَّبِشُکْرِکَ مَادِحَۃً وَّعَلٰی قُلُوْبٍ نِاعْتَرَفَتْ بِاِلٰہِیَّتِکَ مُحَقِّقَۃً وَّعَلٰی ضَمَآئِرَ حَوَتْ مِنَ الْعِلْمِ بِکَ حَتّٰی صَارَتْ خَاشِعَۃً وَّعَلٰی جَوَارِحَ سَعَتْ اِلٰی اَوْطَانِ تَعَبُّدِکَ طَآئِعَۃً وَاَشَارَتْ بِاِسْتِغْفَارِکَ مُذْعِنَۃً مَا ھٰکَذَاالظَّنُّ بِکَ وَلَا اُحْبِرْنَا بِفَضْلِکَ عَنْکَ یَاکَرِیْمُ۔

یَارَبِّ وَاَنْتَ تَعْلَمُ ضَعْفِیْ عَنْ قَلِیْلٍ مِّنْ بَلَآئِ الدُّنْیَا وَعُقُوْبَاتِھَا وَمَایَجْرِیٓ فِیْھَا مِنَ الْمَکَاَرِہٖ عَلٰی اَھْلِھَا عَلٰی اَنَّ ذٰلِکَ بَلَآئٌ وَّمَکْرُوْہٌ قَلِیْلٌ مَّکْثُہٗ یَسِیْرٌ بَقَآؤُہٗ قَصِیْرٌ مُّدَّتُہٗ فَکَیْفَ احْتِمَالِیْ لِبَلَآئِ اَلْآخِرَۃِ وَجَلِیْلِ وُقُوْعِ الْمَکَارِہٖ فِیْھَا وَھُوَبَلَآئٌ تَطُوْلُ مُدَّتُہٗ وَیَدُوْمُ مَقَامُہٗ وَلَایُخَفَّفُ عَنْ اَھْلِہٖ لِاَنَّہٗ لَایَکُوْنُ اِلَّاعَنْ غَضَبِکَ وَانْتِقَامِکَ وَسَخَطِکَ وَھٰذَا مَالَا تَقُوْمُ لَہٗ السَّمٰوٰتُ وَالْاَرْضُ یَاسَیِّدِیْ فَکَیْفَ لِیْ وَاَنَا عَبْدُکَ الضَّعِیْفُ الذَّلِیْلُ الْحَقِیْرُ الْمِسْکِیْنُ الْمُسْتَکِیْنُ۔

یَا اِلٰھِیْ وَرَبِّیْ وَسَیِّدِیْ وَمَوْلَایَ لِاَیِّ الْاُ مُوْرِ اِلَیْکَ اَشْکُوْا وَلِمَا مِنْھَا اَضِجُّ وَاَبْکِیْ لِاَلِیْمِ الْعَذَاَبِ وَشِدَّتِہٖ اَمْ لِطُوْلِ الْبَلَآئِ وَمُدَّتِہٖ فَلَئِنْ صَیَّرْتَنِیْ فِیْ الْعُقُوْبَاتِ مَعَ اَعْدَآئِکَ وَجَمَعْتَ بَیْنِیْ وَبَیْنَ اَھْلِ بَلَآئِکَ وَفَرَّقْتَ بَیْنِیْ وَبَیْنَ اَحِبَّآئِکَ وَاَوْلِیَآئِکَ فَھَبْنِیْ۔

یَااِلٰھِیْ وَسَیِّدِیْ وَمَوْلَایَ وَرَبِّیْ صَبَرْتُ عَلٰی عَذَا بِکَ فَکَیْفَ اَصْبِرُ عَلٰی فِرَاقِکَ وَھَبْنِیْ صَبَرْتُ عَلٰی حَرِّنَارِکَ فَکَیْفَ اَصْبِرُ عَنِ النَّظَرِ اِلٰی کَرَامَتِکَ اَمْ کَیْفَ اَسْکُنُ فِیْ النَّارِ وَرَجَآئِیْ عَفْوُکَ فَبِعِزَّتِکَ۔

یَاسَیِّدِیْ وَمَوْلَایَ اُقْسِمُ صَادِقًا لَّئِنْ تَرَکْتَنِیْ نَاطِقًا لَاضِجَّنَّ اِلَیْکَ بَیْنَ اَھْلِھَا ضَجِیْجَ الْاٰمِلِیْنَ وَلْاَصْرُخَنَّ اِلَیْکَ صُرَاخَ الْمُسْتَصْرِخِیْنَ وَلْاَ بْکِیَنَّ عَلَیْکَ بُکَآئَ الْفَاقِدِیْنَ وَلْاُ نَادِیَنَّکَ اَیْنَ کُنْتَ یَاوَلِیَّ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ یَاغَایَۃَ اٰمَالِ الْعَارِفِیْنَ۔

یَاغِیَاثَ الْمُستَغِیْثِیْنَ یَاحَبِیْبَ قُلُوْبِ الصَّادِقِیْنَ وَیَا اِلٰہَ الْعَالَمِیْنَ اَفَتُرَاکَ سُبْحَانَکَ یَااِلٰھِیْ وَبِحَمْدِکَ تَسْمَعُ فِیْھَا صَوْتَ عَبْدٍ مُّسْلِمٍ سُجِنَ فِیْھَا بِمُخَاَلَفَتِہٖ وَذَاقَ طَعْمَ عَذَا بِھَا بَمَعْصِیَتِہٖ وَحُبِسَ بَیْنَ اَطْبَاقِھَا بِجُرْمِہٖ وَجَرِیْرَتِہٖ وَھُوَ یَضِجُّ اِلَیْکَ ضَجِیْجَ مُؤَمِّلٍ لِرَحْمَتِکَ وَیُنَادِیْکَ بِلِسَانِ اَھْلِ تَوْحِیْدِکَ وَیَتَوَسَّلُ اِلَیْکَ بِرُبُوْبِیَّتِکَ۔

یَامَوْلَایَ فَکَیْفَ یَبْقٰی فِی الْعَذَابِ وَھُوَ یَرْجُوْ مَا سَلَفَ مِنْ حِلْمِکَ وَرَافَتِکَ وَرَحْمَتِکَ اَمْ کَیْفَ تُوْلِمُہٗ النَّارُ وَھُوَ یَامُلُ فَضْلَکَ وَرَحْمَتَکَ اَمْ کَیْفَ یُحْرِقُہٗ لَھِیْبُہَا وَاَنْتَ تَسْمَعُ صَوْتَہٗ وَتَرَیٰ مَکَاَنَہٗ اَمْ کَیْفَ یَشْتَمِلُ عَلَیْہِ زَفِیْرُھَا وَاَنْتَ تَعْلَمُ ضَعْفَہٗ اَمْ کَیْفَ یَتَقَلْقَلُ بَیْنَ اَطْبَاقِھَا وَاَنْتَ تَعْلَمُ صِدْقَہٗ اَمْ کَیْفَ تَزْجُرُہٗ زَبَانِیَتُھَا وَھُوَ یُنَادِیْکَ۔

یَارَبَّہٗ اَمْ کَیْفَ یَرْجُوْ فَضْلَکَ فِیْ عِتْقِہٖ مِنْھَا فَتَتْرُ کُہٗ فِیْھَا ھَیْھَاتَ مَاذٰلِکَ الظَّنُّ بِکَ وَلَا الْمَعْرُوْفُ مِنْ فَضْلِکَ وَلَا مُشْبِہٌ لِّمَا عَامَلْتَ بِہِ الْمُوَحِّدِیْنَ مِنْ بِرِّکَ وَاِحْسَانِکَ فَبِالْیَقِیْنِ اَقْطَعُ لَوْ لَا مَا حَکَمْتَ بِہٖ مِنْ تَعْذِیْبِ جَاحِدِیْکَ وَقَضَیْتَ بِہٖ مِنْ اِخْلَادِ مُعَانِدِیْکَ لَجَعَلْتَ النَّارَ کُلَّہَا بَرْدًا وَّسَلَامًا وَّمَا کَانَ لِاَحَدٍ فِیْھَا مَقَرًّا وَّلَا مُقَامًا لٰکِنَّکَ تَقَدَّسَتْ اَسْمَآؤُکَ اَقْسَمْتَ اَنْ تَمْلَاھَا مِنَ الْکَافِرِیْنَ مِنَ الْجِنَّۃِ وَالنَّاسِ اَجْمَعِیْنَ۔

وَاَنْ تُخَلِّدَ فِیْھَا الْمُعَانِدِیْنَ وَاَنْتَ جَلَّ ثَنَآؤُکَ قُلْتَ مُبْتَدِئًا وَّتَطَوَّلْتَ بِالْاِنْعَامِ مُتَکَرِّمًا اَفَمَنْ کَانَ مُؤْمِنًا کَمَنْ کَانَ فَاسِقًا لَا یَسْتَوُوْنَ اِلٰھِیْ وَسَیِّدِیْ فَاَسْئَلُکَ بِالْقُدْرَۃِ الَّتِیْ قَدَّرْتَھَا وَبِالْقَضِیَّۃِ الَّتِیْ حَتَمْتَھَا وَحَکَمْتَھَا وَغَلَبْتَ مَنْ عَلَیْہِ اَجْرَیْتَھَا اَنْ تَھَبَ لِیْ فِیْ ھٰذِہِ اللَّیْلَۃِ وَفِیْ ھٰذِہِ السَّاعَۃِ کُلَّ جُرْمٍ اَجْرَمْتُہٗ وَکُلَّ ذَنْبٍ اَذْنَبْتُہٗ وَکُلَّ قَبِیْحٍ اَسْرَرْتُہٗ وَکُلَّ جَھْلٍ عَمِلْتُہٗ کَتَمْتُہٗ اَوْ اَعْلَنْتُہٗ اَخْفَیْتُہٗ اَوْ اَظْھَرْتُہٗ وَکُلَّ سَیِّئَۃٍ اَمَرْتَ بِاِثْبَاتِھَا الْکِرَامَ الْکَاتِبِیْنَ۔

اَلَّذِیْنَ وَکَّلْتَہُمْ بِحِفْظِ مَا یَکُوْنُ مِّنِیْ وَجَعَلْتَھُمْ شُھُوْدًا عَلَیَّ مَعَ جَوَارِ حِیْ وَکُنْتَ اَنْتَ الرَّقِیْبَ عَلَیَّ مِنْ وَّرَآئِھِمْ وَالشَّاھِدَ لِمَاخَفِیَ عَنْھُمْ وَبِرَحْمَتِکَ اَخْفَیْتَہٗ وَبِفَضْلِکَ سَتَرْتَہٗ وَاَنْ تُوَفِّرَ حَظِّیْ مِنْ کُلِّ خَیْرٍ اَنْزَلْتَہٗ اَوْ اِحْسَانٍ فَضَّلْتَہٗ اَوْ بِرٍّنَشَرْتَہٗ اَوْ رِزْقٍ بَسَطْتَہٗ اَوْ ذَنْبٍ تَغْفِرُہٗ اَوْخَطَآئٍ تَسْتُرُہٗ۔

یَارَبِّ یَارَبِّ یَارَبِّ یَااِلٰھِیْ وَسَیِّدِیْ وَمَوْلَایَ وَمَالِکَ رِقِّیْ یَامَنْ بِیَدِہٖ نَاصِیَتِیْ یَاعَلِیْمًا بِضُرِّیْ وَمَسْکَنَتِیْ یَاخَبِیْرًا بِفَقْرِیْ وَفَاقَتِیْ یَارَبِّ یَارَبِّ یَارَبِّ اَسْئَلُکَ بِحَقِّکَ وَقُدْسِکَ وَاَعْظَمِ صِفَاتِکَ وَاَسْمَآئِکَ اَنْ تَجْعَلَ اَوْقَاتِیْ فِی اللَّیْلِ وَالنَّھَارِ بِذِکْرِکَ مَعْمُوْرَۃً وَّبِخِدْ مَتِکَ مَوْصُوْلَۃً وَّاَعْمَالِیْ عِنْدَکَ مَقْبُوْلَۃً حَتّٰی تَکُوْنَ اَعْمَالِیْ وَاَوْرَادِیْ کُلُّھَا وِرْدًا وَّاحِدًا وَّحٰالِیْ فِیْ خِدْمَتِکَ سَرْمَدًا۔

یَاسَیِّدِیْ یَامَنْ عَلَیْہِ مُعَوَّلِیْ یَامَنْ اِلَیْہِ شَکَوْتُ اَحْوَالِیْ یَارَبِّ یَارَبِّ یَارَبِّ قَوِّ عَلٰی خِدْمَتِکَ جَوَارِحِیْ وَاشْدُدْ عَلَی الْعَزِیْمَۃِ جَوَانِحِیْ وَھَبْ لِیَ الْجِدِّ فِیْ خَشْیِتَکَ وَالدَّوَامَ فِیْ الْاِتِّصَالِ بِخِدْمَتِکَ حَتّٰی اَسْرَحَ اِلَیْکَ فِیْ مَیَادِیْنِ السَّابِقِیْنَ وَاُسْرِعَ اِلَیْکَ فِی الْمُبَادِرِیْنَ وَاَشْتَاقَ اِلٰی قُرْبِکَ فِیْ الْمُشْتَاقِیْنَ وَاَدْنُوَ مِنْکَ دُنُوَّ الْمُخْلِصِیْنَ وَاَخافَکَ مَخَافَۃَ الْمُوْقِنِیْنَ وَاَجْتَمِعَ فِیْ جِوَارِکَ مَعَ الْمُؤْمْنِیْنَ۔

اَللّٰہُمَّ وَمَنْ اَرَادَنِیْ بِسُوْئٍ فَاَرِدْہُ وَمَنْ کَادَنِیْ فَکِدْہُ وَاجْعَلْنِیْ مِنْ اَحْسَنِ عَبِیْدِکَ نَصِیْبًا عِنْدَکَ وَاَقْرَبِھِمْ مَّنْزِلَۃً مِّنْکَ وَاَخَصِّہِمْ زُلْفَۃً لَدَیْکَ فَاِنَّہٗ لَا یُنَالُ ذٰلِکَ اِلَّا بِفَضْلِکَ وَجُدْلِیْ بِجُوْدِکَ وَاعْطِفْ عَلَیَّ بِمَجْدِکَ وَاحْفَظْنِیْ بِرَحْمَتِکَ وَاجْعَلْ لِّسَانِیْ بِذِکْرِکَ لَھِجًا وَّقَلْبِیْ بِحُبِّکَ مُتَیَّمًا وَّمُنَّ عَلَیَّ بِحُسْنِ اِجَابَتِکَ وَاَقِلْنِیْ عَثْرَتِیْ وَاغْفِرْلِیْ زَلَّتِیْ فَاِنَّکَ قَضَیْتَ عَلٰی عِبَادِکَ بِعِبَادَتِکَ وَاَمَرْتَھُمْ بِدُعَآئِکَ وَضَمِنْتَ لَھُمُ الْاِجَابَۃَ فَاِلَیْکَ۔

یَارَبِّ نَصَبْتُ وَجْھِیْ وَاِلَیْکَ یَارَبِّ مَدَدْتُ یَدِیْ فَبِعِزَّتِکَ اسْتَجِبْ لِیْ دُعَآئِیْ وَبَلِّغْنِیْ مُنَایَ وَلَا تَقْطَعْ مِنْ فَضْلِکَ رَجَآئِیْ وَاکْفِنِیْ شَرَّالْجِنِّ وَالْاِنْسِ مِنْ اَعْدَآئِیْ یَاسَرِیْعَ الرِّضَا اِغْفِرْ لِمَنْ لَا یَمْلِکُ اِلَّا الدُّعَآئَ فَاِنَّکَ فَعَّالٌ لِّمَا تَشَآئُ یَامَنِ اسْمُہٗ دَوَآئٌ وَّذِکْرُہٗ شِفآئٌ وَّطَاعَتُہٗ غِنًی اِرْحَمْ مَن رَّاسُ مَالِہِ الرَّ جَآئُ وَسِلَاحُہٗ الْبُکَآئُ۔

یَاسَابِغَ النِّعَمِ یَادَافِعَ النِّقَمِ یَانُوْرَالْمُسْتَوْ حِشِیْنَ فِی الظُّلَمِ یَاعَالِمًا لَایُعَلَّمُ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّافْعَلْ بِیْ مَآ اَنْتَ اَھْلُہٗ وَصَلَّ اللہُ عَلٰی رَسُوْلِہٖ وَالْاَئِمَّۃِ الْمَیَا مِیْنَ مِنْ اٰلِہٖ وَسَلَّمَ تَسْلِیْمًا کَثِیْرًا۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

KUMAIYL

Yeh Janabe Khizr (a.s) ki dua’o mein se ek dua hai. Jise Maula-e-Qayenaat Hazrat Ali (a.s) ne apne sahabi Kumail bin Ziyaad (a.r) ko taleem di thi aur farmaya tha: ay Kumail! agar tum is dua ko har shab-e-Juma’a ek baar ya har mahine mein ek baar ya saal mein ek martaba ya phir saari zindagi mein sirf ek martaba padh lo to Khuda se qurbat, takmeel-e-haajaat, mushkilaat ka hal, gunaho ki maghferat, rozi mein wus’at (izaafa), aur dushmano se najaat paane ka behtareen zariya hai.

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Ma’bood! Mai Tujh se sawaal karta hu. Teri rehmat ke waaste se jo har shaiy ko ghere huwe hai. Teri quwwat ke zariye jisse Tu ne har shaiy ko daba rakkha hai. Aur uske saamne har shaiy jhuki huwi aur har cheez zer hai. Aur jabrut (zabar-dast taaqat) ke zariye jisse Tu har shaiy par ghaalib hai. Teri izzat ke zariye jiske aagey koi cheez thaherti nahi, Teri azmat ke zariye jisne har cheez ko pur kar diya, Teri saltanat ke zariye jo har cheez se buland hai, Teri zaat ke waaste se jo har cheez ki phana ke baad baqi rahegi, aur sawaal karta hu Tere naamo ke zariye jinho ne har cheez ke ajza (parts) ko pur kar rakkha hai, Tere ilm ke zariye jisne har cheez ko gher rakkha hai. Aur Teri zaat ke noor ke zariye jisse har cheez roshan huwi hai.

Ya Nooro Ya Quddus. Ay awwaleen mein sab se awwal, aur ay aakhereen mein sab se aakhir. Ay Ma’bood! Mere un gunaho ko maaf kar de jo parda phaash (zaleel) karte hai, Khudaaya mere un gunaho ko maaf kar de jin se azaab naazil hota hai, Khudaaya mere un gunaho ko maaf kar de jin se nemate zaayel (barbaad) hoti hai.

Ay Ma’bood! Mere un gunaho ko maaf farma jo dua ko (qabooliyat se) rok lete hai. Ay Allah mere un gunaho ko bakhsh de jin se balaaye (aafat) naazil hoti hai. Ay Khuda mera har woh gunah maaf farma jo mai ne kiye hai. Aur har lagzish (khata) ko dar-guzar (ignore) kar jo mujh se huwi hai.

Ay Allah! Mai Tere zikr ke zariye Tera taqarrub (nazdeeki) chahta hu, aur Teri zaat ko Tere huzoor apna sifaarishi (vakil) banata hu, Tere karam ke waaste se sawaal karta hu keh mujhe apna qurb ataa farma. Aur taufeeq de keh Tera shukr adaa karu aur meri zabaan par apna zikr jaari farma.

Ay Allah mai sawaal karta hu jhuke huwe, girey huwe, darey huwe ki tarah keh mujh se chashm-poshi farma. Mujh par rahem kar aur mujhe apni taqdeer par raazi-o-qaane (mutmaeen/satisfied) aur har qism (tarah) ke haalaat mein narm-khoo (motawaaze) rehne waala bana de.

Ay Allah! Mai Tujh se sawaal karta hu us shakhs ki tarah jo sakht tanghi mein ho, sakhtiyo mein pada huwa apni haajat le kar Tere paas aaya hu, aur jo kuchh Tere paas hai us mein ziyaada raghbat (dil-chaspi/interest) rakhta hu.

Ay Allah! Teri azeem saltanat aur Tera maqaam buland hai. Teri tadbeer poshida (chhupi huwi/hidden) aur Tera amr zaahir hai. Tera qaheyr (azaab) ghaalib Teri qudrat kaar-gar hai. Aur Teri hukumat se faraar mumkin nahi, Khuda-wanda mai Tere siwa kisi ko nahi paata jo mere gunah bakhshne waala, meri buraaiyo ko chhupaane waala aur mere burey amal ko neki mein badal dene waala ho.

Tere siwa koi ma’bood nahi, Tu paak hai, aur hamd Tere hi liye hai, mai ne apne nafs par zulm kiya. Apni jehaalat ki wajah se jur’at ki, aur mai ne Teri qadeem yaad-aawori (yaad-dahaani) aur apne liye Teri bakhshish par bharosa kiya hai.

Ay Allah mere maula kitne hi gunaho ki Tu ne parda-poshi ki aur kitni hi sakht balaawo se mujhe bacha liya. Kitni hi lagzishe (khataaye) maaf farmaayi. Aur kitni hi buraaiya mujh se dur ki. Tu ne meri kitni hi taarife aam ki jinka mai hargiz ahel (laayak) na tha.

Ay Ma’bood! Meri museebat azeem hai, bad-haali kuchh ziyaada hi badh chuki hai, mere aamaal bohot kam hai, gunaho ki zanjeer ne mujhe jakad liya hai, lambi aarzuwo (khwahisho/desires) ne mujhe apna qaidi bana rakkha hai, duniya ne dhoke-baazi se aur nafs ne jarayem aur heela-saazi (makkaari) se mujh ko fareb (dhoka) diya hai.

Ay mere Aaqa! Mai Teri izzat ka waasta de kar sawaal karta hu keh meri bad-aamaali-o-bad-kirdaari meri dua ko Tujh se na roke. Aur Tu mujhe mere poshida kaamo se ruswa na kare, jin mein Tu mere raaz ko jaanta hai. Aur mujhe uspar saza dene mein jaldi na kar jo mai ne khalwat (tanhaayi) mein galat kaam kiye. Hamesha mai ne buraayi ki, kotaahi ki, us mein meri na-daani (jehaalat), khwahisho ki kasrat aur ghaflat bhi hai. Aur ay mere Allah Tujhe apni izzat ka waasta mere liye har haal mein maher-baan rahe. Aur tamaam umoor mein mujh par inaayat farma.

Mere Ma’bood, mere Rab! Tere siwa mera kaun hai jisse sawaal karu keh meri takleef dur kar de, aur mere mo’aamle (affairs) par nazar rakkhe. Mere ma’bood aur mere maula Tu ne mere liye hukm saadir farmaya. Lekin mai ne us mein apne khwahish ka kaha’n maana aur mai dushman ke fareb-kaari (dhoke) se bachh na saka us ne meri khwahisho mein dhoka diya aur waqt ne uska saath diya. Pas Tu ne jo hukm saadir kiya (diya) mai ne us mein Teri baaz (kuch) hudood ko toda (na-farmaani kiya) aur Tere baaz ahkaam ki mukhaalefat ki, pas is mo’aamle mein mujh par laazim hai Teri hamd baja laana, aur mere paas koi hujjat nahi. Us mein jo faisla Tu ne mere liye kiya hai aur mere liye Tera hukm aur Teri aazmaaish laazim hai.

Aur ay Allah! Mai Tere huzoor mein aaya hu, jab keh mai ne kotaahi (la-parwaayi) ki aur apne nafs par zeyadti ki hai. Mai uzr-khwah-o-pashemaan (excuses & guilty), haara huwa, moaafi ka taalib, bakhshish ka sawaali, Taayeb (Tauba karne waala), gunaho ka iqraari, sar jhukaye iqbaale (iqraare) jurm karta hu, jo kuchh mujh se huwa na us se faraar ki raah hai aur na koi jaaye-panah keh apne mo’aamle mein uski taraf tawajjoh karu, siwaaye iske keh Tu mera uzr (excuse) qabool kar aur mujhe apni wasee-tar rehmat mein daakhil kar le.

Ay Ma’bood! Bas mera uzr (excuse) qabool farma, meri sakht takleef par rahem kar, aur bhaari mushkil se rehaayi (chutkara) de. Ay Parwardigaar! Mere kamzor badan, naazuk jild (skin), aur baarik haddiyo par rahem farma. Ay woh zaat jisne meri khilqat, mera zikr, meri parwarish, meri neki aur meri ghiza (food) ka pehle hi intezaam kiya. Apne ibtadayee karam aur guzishta neki ke tahet mujhe maaf farma.

Ay mere Ma’bood, mere Aaqa-o-mere Rab! Kya mai yeh samjhu keh Tu mujhe apni aag ka azaab dega jab keh Teri Tauheed ka mai motarif (iqraar karnewaala) hu, uske saath mera dil Teri maarefat se labrez (bhara huwa) hai, aur meri zabaan Tere zikr mein lagi huwi hai, mera zameer Teri mohabbat se juda huwa (attached) hai, aur apne gunaho ke sachche etraaf aur Teri rububiyat (shaan) ke aagey meri aajezaana (kamzor) pukaar ke baad bhi Tu mujhe azaab dega? Hargiz nahi. Tu buland hai isse keh jise paala ho usey zaaye (barbaad) kare ya jise qareeb kiya ho usey dur kare, ya jise panah di ho usey chhod de, ya jiski sar-parasti ki ho aur us par maherbaani ki ho usey museebat ke hawaale kar de, ay kaash mai jaanta.

Ay mere Aaqa mere ma’bood aur mere Maula! Keh kya Tu un chehro ko aag mein daalega jo Teri azmat ke saamne sajdeh mein pade hai, aur un zabaano ko jo Teri Tauheed ke bayaan mein sachchi hai, aur shukr ke saath Teri taarif karti hai. Aur un dilo ko jo tehqeeq ke saath Tujhe ma’bood maante hai, aur unke zameero ko jo Teri maarefat se pur ho kar Tujh se khaayef (dare huwe) hai, Tu unhe aag mein daalega? Aur un aaza (parts) ko jo farma-bardaari se Teri ibaadat-gaaho ki taraf daudte hai. Aur yaqeen ke saath Teri maghferat ke taalib hai (Tu unhe aag mein daale?) Teri zaat se aisa gumaan nahi. Na yeh Tere fazl ke munaasib hai.

Ay Kareem! ay Parwardigaar! Duniya ki mukhtasar takleefo aur museebato ke muqaabil Tu meri na-tawaani (kamzori) ko jaanta hai aur ahle-duniya (duniya waalo) par jo tanghiya (dushwariya) aati hai (mai unhe bardaasht nahi kar sakta) agar-che us tanghi wa sakhti ka thaheraaw (period) aur baqa ka waqt thoda aur uski muddat mukhtasar hai, to phir kyu’n-kar mai aakherat ki mushkilo ko jhel sakoonga jo keh badi sakht hai aur woh aisi takleefe hai jin ki muddat tulaani aur baqa daayemi hai, aur un mein se kisi mein kami nahi hogi. Kyu’n keh woh Tere ghazab, Tere inteqaam aur Teri naraazgi se aati hai aur yeh woh sakhtiya hai jin ke saamne zameen-o-aasmaan bhi khade nahi rahe sakte. To ay mere Aaqa mujh par kya guzregi jab keh mai Tera kamzor, past, be-haisiyat, be-sarmaya aur be-bas banda hu.

Ay mere Aaqa-o-Maula! ay mere Parwardigaar! Mai kin-kin baato ka Tujh se shikaayat karu aur kis-kis ke liye naala-o-faryaad karu? Dard-naak azaab aur uski sakhti ke liye, Ya tulaani (lambi) museebat aur uski muddat ki zeyadti ke liye. Pas agar Tu ne mujhe azaab-o-eqaab mein apne dushmano ke saath rakkha aur mujhe aur apne azaabo ko ekhattha kar diya. Aur mere aur apne dosto aur mehboobo mein duri daal di to ay mere Ma’bood, ay mere aaqa-o-maula ay Parwardigaar! Tu hi bata keh agar mai Tere azaab par sabr kar bhi lu magar Tujh se duri par kaise sabr karunga? Aur mujhe bata keh mai ne Teri aag ki ta-pish (gharmi/heat) par sabr kar bhi liya to Tere karam se kis tarah chashm-poshi (ignore) kar sakoonga. Ya kaise aag mein pada rahuga. Jab keh mai Tere afvo-o-bakhshish ka ummeed-waar hu.

Pas qasam hai Teri izzat ki ay mere Aaqa-o-Maula! Sachchi qasam kha kar kehta hu keh agar Tu ne mujhe chhod diya aur meri goyaayi (bolne ki taaqat) baaqi rehne di to mai ahle-naar (Jahannamiyo) ke darmiyaan Tere huzoor faryaad karunga aarzu-mando ki tarah. Aur Tere saamne naala-o-shikwa karunga jaise madad ke talab-gaar karte hai. Tere firaaq (judaayi) mein you’n girya (aah-o-buka) karunga jaise na-ummeed ho ne waale girya-o-foga karte hai, aur Tujhe sada deta (pukaarta) rahuga. Kaha’n hai Tu ay momino ke madad-gaar. Ay aareefo ke ummeedo ke markaz. Ay be-chaaro ki daad-rasi (madad) karne waale!

(Yaa gheyaasal mustaghiseen) Ay sachche logo ke dost. Ay Aalameen ke Ma’bood! Kya mai Tujhe dekhta hu Tu Paak hai usse. Ay mere Allah apni hamd ke saath keh Tu wahan se banda-e-Muslim ki aawaaz soon raha hai jo na-farmaani ki wajah se dozakh mein hai. Apni buraayi ki wajah se azaab ka zayeka chakh raha hai. Aur apne jurm, gunah par jahannam ke tabqo (stages) ke beech-o-beech bandh hai. Woh Tere saamne girya kar raha hai. Teri rehmat ke ummeed-waar ki tarah. Aur ahle-tauheed ki zabaan mein Tujhe pukaar raha hai aur Tere huzoor Teri rububiyat (shaan) ko wasila bana raha hai.

Ay mere Maula! Pas kis tarah woh azaab mein rahe-ga, jab keh woh Tere guzishta hilm (bardaasht) ka ummeed-waar hai. Ya phir aag kyu’n-kar usey takleef degi jab keh woh Tere fazl aur rehmat ki ummeed rakhta hai. Ya aag ke sholay kaise usko jalaayenge? Jab keh Tu uski aawaaz soon raha hai. Aur uske maqaam ko dekh raha hai. Ya kaise aag ke sholay usey gherenge jab keh Tu uski na-tawaani-o-be-basi ko jaanta hai. Ya kaise woh jahannam ke tabqo mein pareshan rahe-ga, jab keh Tu uski sachchayi se waaqif hai. Ya kaise jahannam ke farishte usey jhidkenge jab keh woh Tujhe pukaar raha hai.

Ay mere Rab! Yeh kaise mumkin hai keh woh khalaasi (jaan chhudane) mein Tere fazl ka ummeed-waar ho aur Tu usey jahannam mein rehne de, hargiz nahi! Tere baare mein yeh gumaan nahi ho sakta, na Tere fazl ka aisa taaruf (pehchaan) hai. Na yeh Tauheed parasto par Tere ehsaan-o-karam ke mutabiq hai. Pas mai yaqeen rakhta hu keh agar Tu ne apne dushmano ko aag ka azaab dene ka hukm na diya hota aur apne mukhaalifo ko hamesha us (aag) mein rakhne ka faisla na kiya hota to zaroor Tu aag ko thandi aur aaraam-bakhsh bana deta. Aur kisi ko bhi aag mein jagah aur thikaana na diya jaata. Lekin Tu ne apne pakeeza naamo ki qasam khaai hai keh jahannam ko tamaam kaafiro se bhar dega. Jinno aur Insaano mein se. Aur yeh mukhalefeen hamesha us mein rahenge. Aur Tu badi taarif waala hai, Tu ne fazl-o-karam karte huwe bela saabeqa yeh farmaya keh: kya woh shakhs jo momin hai woh faasiq (khulle-aam gunah karnewaala) jaisa ho sakta hai? Yeh dono baraabar nahi.

Mere ma’bood mere Aaqa! Mai Teri qudrat jise Tu ne tawana (taaqat-war) kiya aur Tera farmaan jise Tu ne yaqeeni-o-mohkam banaya, aur Tu ghaalib hai uspar jis par usey jaari kare, uske waaste se sawaal karta hu bakhsh de is shab (raat) mein aur isi waqt mere tamaam woh jurm jo mai ne kiye, tamaam woh gunah jo mujh se sarzad huwe, woh sab buraaiya jo mai ne chhupaaye hai, jo na-daaniya mai ne jahel ki wajah se ki hai, alal-elaan (khulle-aam) ya posheeda (chhupi) rakkhi ho, ya zaahir ki hai. Aur meri buraaiya (bed deeds) jin ke likhne ka Tu ne Keraamal Kaatebeen (moazziz farishto) ko hukm diya hai. Jinhe Tu ne moqarrar (appoint) kiya hai keh jo kuchh mai karu usey mehfuz (record) kare, aur unko mere aaza (parts of body) ke saath mujh par gawah banaya. Aur unke alawa khud Tu bhi mujh par nazar rakkhe huwe hai aur us-baat ka gawah hai jo un se (farishto se) posheeda (chhupa huwa) hai.

Haala keh Tu ne apni rehmat se usey chhupaaya, aur apne fazl se uspar parda daala woh maaf farma. Aur mere liye bohot ziyaada hissa qaraar de har us khair mein jise Tu ne naazil kiya. Ya har us ehsaan mein jo Tu ne kiya. Ya har neki mein jise Tu ne phailaaya, Rizq mein jise Tu ne wasee kiya (jis mein Tu ne wus’at diya) Ya gunah mein jise Tu ne maaf kiya (bakhsh diya) Ya galti mein jise Tu ne chhupaaya.

Ya Rabbe Ya Rabbe Ya Rab (ay Parwardigaar, ay Paalne waale, ay Rabbe jahan). Ay mere Ma’bood, mere aaqa-o-maula aur meri jaan ke Maalik. Ay woh jiske haath mein meri lagaam (control) hai. Ay meri tanghi-o-be-chaargi se waaqif. Ay meri na-daari (muflisi)-o-tangdasti se ba-khabar. Ya Rabbe Ya Rabbe Ya Rab (ay Parwardigaar, ay Paalne waale, ay Rabbe jahan).

Mai Tujh se Tere haq hone, Teri pakeezgi, Teri azeem sifaat (qualities) aur asma (naamo) ka waasta de kar sawaal karta hu keh mere raat-din ke awqaat (waqt) apne zikr se aabaad kar. Aur musalsal apni huzoori mein rakh. Aur mere aamaal ko apni janaab mein qabooliyat ataa farma. Yahan tak keh mere tamaam aamaal aur azkaar Tere huzoor vird qaraar paaye. Aur mera yeh haal Teri baargah mein hamesha qaayem rahe. Ay mere Aaqa ay woh jis par mera takya (bharosa) hai. Ay jisse mai apne haalaat ki tanghi bayaan karta hu.

Ya Rabbe Ya Rabbe Ya Rab (ay Parwardigaar, ay Paalne waale, ay Rabbe jahan). Mere zaaheri aaza (parts) ko apni huzoori mein qawi (strong) aur mere baateni (poshida) iraado ko mohkam-o-mazboot bana de. Aur mujhe taufeeq de keh Tujh se darne ki koshish karu. Aur Teri huzoori mein hameshgi paida karu ta-keh Teri baargah mein saabeqeen (pehle waalo) ki raaho par chal padu. Aur Teri taraf jaane waalo se aagey nikal jaau. Tere qurb (nazdeeki) ka shauq rakhne waalo mein ziyaada shauq waala ban jaau. Tere khaalis bando ki tarah Tere qareeb ho jaau ahle-yaqeen ki maanind Tujh se daru, aur Tere aastana (dar) par momino ke saath haazir rahu.

Ay Ma’bood! jo mere liye buraayi ka iraada kare Tu uske liye waisa hi kar. Jo mere saath makr-o-fareb kare Tu uske saath bhi waisa hi kar. Mujhe apne un khaas bando mein qaraar de. Jo naseeb mein behtar hai, jo manzilat mein Tere qareeb hai. Jo Tere huzoor taqarrub mein makhsoos hai. Kyu’n keh Tere fazl ke ba-ghair yeh darajaat nahi mil sakte. Ba-waaste apne karam ke mujh par karam kar, Ba-zariye apni buzurghi ke mujh par tawajjoh farma. Ba-wajeh apni rehmat ke meri hifaazat kar. Meri zabaan ko apne zikr mein goya farma. Aur mere dil ko apna aseer-e-mohabbat bana de. Meri dua ba-khoobi qabool farma, mujh par ehsaan farma. Mera gunah maaf kar de, aur meri khata bakhsh de. Kyu’n keh Tu ne bando par ibaadat farz (waajib) ki hai. Aur unhe dua maang-ne ka hukm diya, aur qabooliyat ki zamaanat (guarantee) di.

Pas ay Parwardigaar! Mai apna rukh Teri taraf kar raha hu. Aur Tere aagey haath phaila raha hu. Tu apni izzat ke tufaiyl se meri dua qabool farma. Meri tamannaaye puri kar de, aur apne fazl se lagi meri ummeed na tod. Mere dushman jo Jinno aur Insaano mein se hai unke sharr se meri kifaayat kar. Ay jald raazi ho ne waale (Ya Saree Ar Reza). Usey (mujhe) bakhsh de, jo dua ke siwa kuchh nahi rakhta. Be-shak Tu jo chahe karne waala hai.

Ay woh jiska naam dawa aur jiska zikr shifa. Aur jiski ita’at daulat-mandi hai, rahem farma us par jiska sarmaya sirf ummeed hai. Aur jiska hathiyaar girya (rona) hai. Ay nemate puri karne waale, ay sakhtiya dur karne waale, ay taarikiyo (andhero) mein darne waalo ke liye Noor. Ay woh Aalim jise padhaya nahi gaya. Mohammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Mohammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma. Aur mujh se woh sulook kar jiska Tu ahel hai (na keh mai). Khuda apne Rasool par aur ba-barkat Aimma par salaam bhejta hai, bohot ziyaada salaam-o-tahaiyaat (compliments) jo unki Aal mein se hai.

KUMAIYL

According to Allama Majlisi (a.r) Kumayl had attended an assembly in the Mosque at Basra which was addressed by Imam Ali in the course of which the night of the 15th of Shaban was mentioned. Imam Ali said-"Whosoever keeps awake in devoutness on this night and recites the Du'a of Prophet Khizr, undoubtedly that person's supplication will be responded to and granted. When the assembly at the Mosque had dispersed, Kumayl called at the house where Imam Ali was staying, and requested him to acquaint him with Prophet Khizr's dua. Imam Ali asked Kumayl to sit down, record and memorise the dua which Imam Ali dictated to Kumayl. Imam Ali then advised Kumayl to recite this dua on the eve of every Friday, or once a month or at least once in every year so that, Allah may protect thee from the evils of the enemies and the plots contrived by impostors…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah! I beseech Thee by Thy mercy which encompasses all things And by Thy power by which Thou over-cometh all things and submit to it all things and humble before it all things And by Thy might by which Thou hast conquered all things And by Thy majesty against which nothing can stand up. And by Thy grandeur which prevails upon all things And by Thy authority which is exercised over all things And by Thy own self that shall endure forever after all things have vanished And by Thy Names which manifest Thy power over all things And by Thy knowledge which pervades all things And by the light of Thy countenance which illuminates everything.

O Thou who art the light! O Thou who art the most holy! O Thou who existed before the foremost! O Thou who shall exist after the last! O Allah! Forgive me my such sins as would affront my continency O Allah! Forgive me my such sins as would bring down calamity.

O Allah! Forgive me my such sins as would change divine favours (into disfavours (O Allah! Forgive me my such sins as would hinder my supplication O Allah! Forgive me such sins as bring down misfortunes (or afflictions) O Allah! Forgive my such sins as would suppress hope.

O Allah! Forgive every sin that I have committed and every error that I have erred O Allah! I endeavor to draw myself nigh to Thee through Thy invocation And I pray to Thee to intercede on my behalf And I entreat Thee by Thy benevolence to draw me nearer to Thee And grant me that I should be grateful to Thee and inspire me to remember and to invoke Thee.

O Allah! I entreat Thee begging Thee submissively, humbly and awestricken To treat me with clemency and mercy, and to make me pleased and contented with what Thou hast allotted to me And cause me to be modest and unassuming in all circumstances.

O Allah! I beg Thee as one who is passing through extreme privation and who supplicates his needs to Thee and his hope has been greatly raised by that which is with Thee. O Allah! Great is Thy kingdom and exalted is Thy greatness Thy plan is secret, Thy authority is manifest, Thy might is victorious and subduing and Thy power is prevalent throughout and it is not possible to escape from Thy dominion.

O Allah! Except Thee I do not find any one able to pardon my sins nor to conceal my loathsome acts Nor have I anyone except Thee to change my evil deeds into virtues There is no god but Thou glory and praise be to Thee I have made my own soul to suffer I had the audacity (to sin) by my ignorance Relying upon my past remembrance of Thee and Thy grace towards me.

O Allah! My Lord! How many of my loathsome acts hast Thou screened (from public gaze) How many of my grievous afflictions (distresses) hast Thou reduced in severity And how many of my stumbling hast Thou protected, how many of my detestable acts has Thou averted, and how many of my undeserving praises hast Thou spread abroad!

O Allah! My trials and sufferings have increased and my evilness has worsened, my good deeds have diminished and my yokes (of misdeeds) have become firm And remote hopes restrain me to profit (by good deeds) and the world has deceived me with its allurements and my own self has been affected by treachery and procrastination.

Therefore, my Lord! I implore Thee by Thy greatness not to let my sins and my misdeeds shut out access to my prayers from reaching Thy realm and not to disgrace me by exposing those (hidden ones) of which Thou hast knowledge nor to hasten my retribution for those vices and misdeeds committed by me in secret which were due to evil mindedness, ignorance, excessive lustfulness and my negligence.

O Allah! I beg Thee by Thy greatness to be compassionate to me in all circumstances and well disposed towards me in all matters My God! My Nourisher! Have I anyone except Thee from whom I can seek the dislodging of my evils and understanding of my problems?

My God! My Master! Thou decreed a law for me but instead I obeyed my own low desires And I did not guard myself against the allurements of my enemy He deceived me with vain hopes whereby I was led astray and fate helped him in that respect Thus I transgressed some of its limits set for me by Thee and I disobeyed some of Thy commandments; Thou hast therefore a (just) cause against me in all those matters and I have no plea against Thy judgment passed against me I have therefore become (justifiably) liable to Thy judgment and afflictions.

But now I have turned Thee, my Lord, after being guilty of omissions and transgressions against my soul, apologetically, repentantly, broken heartedly, entreating earnestly for forgiveness, yieldingly confessing (to my guilt) as I can find no escape from that which was done by me and having no refuge to which I could turn except seeking Thy acceptance of my excuse and admitting me into the realm of Thy capacious mercy.

O Allah! Accept my apology and have pity on my intense sufferings and set me free from my heavy fetters (of evil deeds) My Nourisher! Have mercy on the infirmity of my body, the delicacy of my skin and the brittleness of my bones O' Thou! Who originated my creation and (accorded me) my individuality, and (ensured) my upbringing and welfare (and provided) my sustenance (I beg Thee) to restore Thy favours and blessings upon me as Thou didst in the beginning of my life.

O' my God! My master! My Lord! And my Nourisher! What! Wilt Thou see me punished with the fire kindled by Thee despite my belief in Thy unity? And despite the fact that my heart has been filled with (pure) knowledge of Thee and when my tongue has repeatedly praised Thee and my conscience has acknowledged Thy love and despite my sincere confessions (of my sins) and my humble entreaties submissively made to Thy divinity?

Nay, Thou art far too kind and generous to destroy one whom thyself nourished and supported, or to drive away from Thyself one whom Thou has kept under Thy protection, or to scare away one whom Thy self hast given shelter, or to abandon in affliction one Thou hast maintained and to whom Thou hast been merciful.

I wish I had known o' my Master, my God and my Lord! Wilt Thou inflict fire upon faces which have submissively bowed in prostration to Thy greatness, or upon the tongues which have sincerely confirmed Thy unity and have always expressed gratitude to Thee, or upon hearts which have acknowledged Thy divinity with conviction, or upon the minds which accumulated so much knowledge of Thee until they became submissive to Thee, or upon the limbs which strove, at the places appointed for Thy worship, to adore Thee willingly and seek Thy forgiveness submissively? Such sort (of harshness) is not expected from Thee as it is remote from Thy grace, o' generous one!

O' Lord! Thou art aware of my weakness to bear even a minor affliction of this world and its consequence and adversity affecting the denizen of this earth, although such afflictions are momentary, short-lived and transient How then can I bear the retributions and the punishments of the hereafter which are enormous and of intensive sufferings, of prolonged period and perpetual duration, and which shall never be alleviated for those who deserve the same as those retributions will be the result of Thy wrath; and Thy punishment which neither the heavens nor the earth can withstand and bear! O' my God! My Lord! My King! And Master! Which of the matters shall I complain to Thee and for which of them shall I bewail and weep? shall I bewail for the pains and pangs of the punishment and their intensity or for the length of sufferings and their duration? Therefore (my Lord!) If Thou wilt subject me to the penalties (of hell) in company of Thy enemies and cast me with those who merited Thy punishments and tear me apart from Thy friends and those who will be near to Thee, then my God, my Lord and my Master, though I may patiently bear Thy punishments, how can I calmly accept being kept away from Thee? I reckon that though I may patiently endure the scorching fire of Thy hell, yet how can I resign myself to the denial of Thy pity and clemency? How can I remain in the fire while I have hopes of Thy forgiveness?

O' my Lord! By Thy honour truly do I swear that, if Thou wilt allow my power of speech to be retained by me in the hell, I shall amongst its inmates cry out bewailingly unto Thee like the cry of those who have faith in Thy kindness and compassion And I shall bemoan for Thee (for being deprived of nearness to Thee) the lamentation of those who are bereaved, and I shall keep on calling unto Thee: "Where art Thou o' Friend of the believers!

O' (Thou who art) the last hope and resort of those who acknowledge Thee and have faith in Thy clemency and kindness; o' Thou who art the helper of those seeking help!

O' Thou who art dear to the hearts of those who truly believe in Thee! And o' Thou who art the Lord of the universe." My Lord! Glory and praise be to Thee, wouldst Thou (wish) to be seen (disregarding) the voice of a muslim bondman, incarcerated therein (the hell) for his disobedience and imprisoned within its pits for his evildoings and misdeeds, crying out to Thee the utterance of one who has faith in Thy mercy and calling out to Thee in the language of those who believe in Thy unity and seeking to approach Thee by means of Thy epithet "the Creator, the Nourisher, the Accomplisher and the Protector of the entire existence"?

My Lord! Then how could he remain in torments when he hopefully relies upon Thy past forbearance, compassion and mercy? And how can the fire cause him suffering when he hopes for Thy grace and mercy and how can its roaring flames char him when Thou hearest his voice and sees his plight? And how can he withstand its roaring flames when Thou knowest his fraility?

And how can he be tossed about between its layers when Thou knowest his sincerity? And how can the guards of hell threaten him when he calls out to Thee? "My Lord", and how would Thou abandon him therein (the hell) when he has faith in Thy grace to set him free?

Alas! And Thou, exalted be Thy praises, hadst made manifest, out of Thy generosity and kindness, that a believer is not like unto him who is an evil-liver.

My Lord! My Master! I, therefore implore Thee by that power which Thou determineth and by the decree which Thou hast finalised and ordained whereby Thou hath prevailed upon whom.
