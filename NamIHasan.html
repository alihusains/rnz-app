Offer 4 rakat namaz with the unit of 2, in each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1), recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) 25 times. After namaz recite this dua…
4 Rakat namaz padhe, 2x2 rakat kar ke har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 25 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) padhe. Namaz ko tamaam kare aur yeh dua padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰهُمَّ اِنِّي اَتَقَرَّبُ اِلَيْكَ بِجُوْدِكَ وَكَرَمِكَ وَاَتَقَرَّبُ اِلَيْكَ بِمُحَمَّدٍ عَبْدِكَ وَرَسُوْلِكَ وَاَتَقَرَّبُ اِلَيْكَ بِمَلَآئِكَتِكَ الْمُقَرَّبِيْنَ وَاَنْبِيَآئِكَ وَرُسُلِكَ اَنْ تُصَلِّيَ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ عَبْدِكَ وَرَسُوْلِكَ وَعَلٰى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاَنْ تُقِيْلَنِيْ عَثْرَتِيْ وَتَسْتُرَ عَلَيَّ ذُنُوْبِيْ وَتَغْفِرَهَا لِيْ وَتَقْضِيَ لِيْ حَوَآئِجِيْ وَلَا تُعَذِّبَنِيْ بِقَبِيْحٍ كَانَ مِنِّيْ فَاِنَّ عَفْوَكَ وَجُوْدَكَ يَسَعُنِيْ اِنَّكَ عَلٰى كُلِّ شَيْٓءٍ قَدِيْرٌ۔

اَللّٰهُمَّ اَنْ تُصَلِّيَ عَلٰى مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O you Allah! I come near to Thee, towards Thy liberal beneficence and generous kindness. I come near to Thee, in the name of Muhammad, Thy servant, Thy Messenger. I come near to Thee, in the name of Thy close Angels, And Prophets, And Messengers, That blessings are sent on Muhammad, Thy servant and Thy Messenger, and on the children of Muhammad, and that comfort and happiness are made available for me, My sins are kept hidden, And forgiven; My wants and needs favourably taken care of, And I am not punished for shortcomings. Do what is good for me for surely Thou very kindly and lovingly takes care of me. Certainly Thou art able to do all things.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
