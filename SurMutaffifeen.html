Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Veil (jahannam) hai unke liye jo naap-toal mein kami karne waale hai.

2. Yeh jab logo se naap kar lete hai to pura maal le lete hai.

3. Aur jab unke liye naapte ya tolte hai to kam kar dete hai.

4. Kya inhe yeh khayaal nahi hai keh yeh ek roz dobaara uthaaye jaane waale hai.

5. Bade sakht din mein.

6. Jis din sab Rabbul aalameen ki baargah mein haazir hoge.

7. Yaad rakkho keh badkaaro ka naama-e-aamaal sijjeen mein hoga.

8. Aur tum kya jo ke sijjeen kya hai.

9. Ek likha huwa daftar hai.

10. Aaj ke din un joothlaane waalo ke liye barbaadi hai.

11. Jo log roze jaza ka inkaar karte hai.

12. Aur uska inkaar sirf wohi karte hai jo hadd se guzar jaane waale gunah-gaar hai.

13. Jab unke saamne aayaat ki tilaawat ki jaati hai to kehte hai keh yeh to puraane afsaane hai.

14. Nahi-nahi bal-keh unke dilo par unke aamaal ka zang (rust) lag gaya hai.

15. Yaad rakkho inhe roz-e-qayamat Parwardigaar ki rehmat se mehjoob (mehroom) kar diya jaayega.

16. Phir iske baad yeh Jahannam mein jhonke jaane waale hai.

17. Phir un se kaha jaayega keh yahi woh hai jiska tum inkaar kar rahe thay.

18. Yaad rakkho keh nek kirdaar afraad ka naama-e-aamaal illiyeen mein hoga.

19. Aur tum kya jaano ke illiyeen kya hai.

20. Ek likha huwa daftar hai.

21. Jiske gawah malaayeka-e-muqarrabeen hai.

22. Be-shak nek log nemato mein hoge.

23. Takhto par baithay huwe nazaare kar rahe hoge.

24. Tum unke chehro par nemat ki shaadaabi ka mushaheda karoge.

25. Unhe sar-be-mohr (seal) khaalis sharaab se seyraab kiya jaayega.

26. Jiski mohar mushk ki hogi aur aisi cheezo mein shauq karne waalo ko aapas mein sabqat aur raghbat karni chaahiye.

27. Is sharaab mein Tasneem (jannat ki naher) ke paani ki ameezash (taste) hogi.

28. Yeh ek chashma hai jis se muqarrabe baargah, bande paani peete hai.

29. Be-shak yeh mujremeen, sahebaan-e-imaan ka mazaaq udaaya karte thay.

30. Aur jab woh unke paas se guzarte thay to ishaare kinaaye (code word mein baate) karte thay.

31. Aur jab apne ahel ki taraf palat kar aate thay to khush-o-khurram hote thay.

32. Aur jab momenin ko dekhte to kehte thay keh yeh sab asli gumraah hai.

33. Haala keh inhe unka nigra bana kar nahi bheja gaya tha.

34. To aaj imaan laane waale bhi kuffaar ka mazhaka udaayege.

35. Takhto par baithay huwe dekh rahe hoge.

36. Ab to kuffaar ko unke aamaal ka pura-pura badla mil raha hai.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Woe to the defrauders,

2. Who, when they take the measure (of their dues) from men take it fully,

3. But when they measure out to others or weigh out for them, they are deficient.

4. Do not these think that they shall be raised again,

5. For a mighty day,

6. The day on which men shall stand before the Lord of the worlds?

7. Nay! most surely the record of the wicked is in the Sijjin.

8. And what will make you know what the Sijjin is?

9. It is a written book.

10. Woe on that day to the rejecters,

11. Who give the lie to the day of judgment.

12. And none gives the lie to it but every exceeder of limits, sinful one.

13. When Our communications are recited to him, he says: Stories of those of yore.

14. Nay! rather, what they used to do has become like rust upon their hearts.

15. Nay! most surely they shall on that day be debarred from their Lord.

16. Then most surely they shall enter the burning fire.

17. Then shall it be said: This is what you gave the lie to.

18. Nay! Most surely the record of the righteous shall be in the Iliyin.

19. And what will make you know what the highest Iliyin is?

20. It is a written book,

21. Those who are drawn near (to Allah) shall witness it.

22. Most surely the righteous shall be in bliss,

23. On thrones, they shall gaze;

24. You will recognize in their faces the brightness of bliss.

25. They are made to quaff of a pure drink that is sealed (to others).

26. The sealing of it is (with) musk; and for that let the aspirers aspire.

27. And the admixture of it is a water of Tasnim,

28. A fountain from which drink they who are drawn near (to Allah).

29. Surely they who are guilty used to laugh at those who believe.

30. And when they passed by them, they winked at one another.

31. And when they returned to their own followers they returned exulting.

32. And when they saw them, they said: Most surely these are in error;

33. And they were not sent to be keepers over them.

34. So today those who believe shall laugh at the unbelievers;

35. On thrones, they will look.

36. Surely the disbelievers are rewarded as they did.
