بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَااَمِیْنَ اللہِ فِیْ اَرْضِهٖ وَحُجَّتَهٗ عَلٰی عِبَادِهٖ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَااَمِیْرَ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ اَشْهَدُ اَنَّكَ جَاهَدْتَ فِی اللہِ حَقَّ جِهَادِهٖ وَعَمِلْتَ بِكِتَابِهٖ وَاتَّبَعْتَ سُنَنَ نَبِیِّهٖ صَلَّ اللہُ عَلَیْهِ وَاٰلِهٖ حَتّٰی دَعَاكَ اللہُ اِلٰی جِوَارِهٖ فَقَبَضَكَ اِلَیْهِ بِاِخْتِیَارِهٖ وَاَلْزَمَ اَعْدَآئَكَ الْحُجَّۃَ مَعَ مَالَكَ مِنَ الْحُجَجِ الْبَالِغَۃِ عَلٰی جَمِیْعِ خَلْقِهٖ۔ اَللّٰهُمَّ فَاجْعَلْ نَفْسِیْ مُطْمَئِنَّۃً بِقَدَرِكَ رَاضِیَۃً بِقَضَآئِكَ مُوْلَعَۃً بِذِكْرِكَ وَدُعَآئِكَ مُحِبَّۃً لِصَفْوَۃِ اَوْلِیَآئِكَ مَحْبُوْبَۃً فِیْ اَرْضِكَ وَسَمَآئِكَ صَابِرَۃً عِنْدَ نُزُوْلِ بَلَآئِكَ شَاكِرَۃً لِفَوَاضِلِ نَعْمَآئِكَ ذَاكِرَۃً لِسَوَابِغِ اٰلَآئِكَ مُشْتَاقَۃً اِلٰی فَرْحَتِ لِقَآئِكَ مُتَزَوِّدَۃً التَّقْوٰی لِیَوْمِ جَزَآئِكَ مُسْتَنَّۃً بِسُنَنِ اَوْلِیَآئِكَ مُفَارِقَۃً لِاَخْلَاقِ اَعْدَآئِكَ مَشْغُوْلَۃً عَنِ الدُّنْیَا بِحَمْدِكَ وَثَنَآئِكَ۔

اَللّٰهُمَّ اِنَّ قُلُوْبَ الْمُخْبِتِیْنَ اِلَیْكَ وَالِهَۃٌ وَسُبُلَ الرَّاغِبِیْنَ اِلَیْكَ شَارِعَۃٌ وَّاَعْلَامَ الْقَاصِدِیْنَ اِلَیْكَ وَاضِحَۃٌ وَّاَفْئِدَۃَ الْعَارِفِیْنَ مِنْكَ فَازِعَۃٌ وَّاَصْوَاتَ الدَّاعِیْنَ اِلَیْكَ صَاعِدَۃٌ وَّاَبْوَابَ الْاِجَابَۃِ لَهُمْ مُفَتَّحَۃٌ وَّدَعْوَۃَ مَنْ نَاجَاكَ مُسْتَجَابَۃٌ وَّتَوْبَۃَ مَنْ اَنَابَ اِلَیْكَ مَقْبُوْلَۃٌ وَّعَبْرَۃَ مَنْ بَكٰی مِنْ خَوْفِكَ مَرْحُوْمَۃٌ وَّالْاِغَاثَۃَ لِمَنِ اسْتَغَاثَ بِكَ مَوْجُوْدَۃٌ وَّالْاِعَانَۃَ لِمَنِ اسْتَعَانَ بِكَ مَبْذُوْلَۃٌ وَّعِدَاتِكَ لِعِبَادِكَ مُنْجَزَۃٌ وَّزَلَلَ مَنِ اسْتَقَالَكَ مُقَالَۃٌ وَّ اَعْمَالَ الْعَامِلِیْنَ لَدَیْكَ مَحْفُوْظَۃٌ وَّاَرْزَاقَكَ اِلَی الْخَلَآئِقِ مِنْ لَّدُنْكَ نَازِلَۃٌ وَّعَوَآئِدَ الْمَزِیْدِ اِلَیْهِمْ وَاصِلَۃٌ وَّذُنُوْبَ الْمُسْتَغْفِرِیْنَ مَغْفُوْرَۃٌ وَّحَوَآئِجَ خَلْقِكَ عِنْدَكَ مَقْضِیَّۃٌ وَّجَوَآئِزَ السَّآئِلِیْنَ عِنْدَكَ مُوَفَّرَۃٌ وَّعَوَآئِدَ الْمَزِیْدِ اِلَیْہِمْ وَاصِلَۃٌ وَّمَوَآئِدَ الْمُسْتَطْعِمِیْنَ مُعَدَّۃٌ وَّمَنَاهِلَ الظِّمَآءِ مُتْرَعَۃٌ۔ اَللّٰهُمَّ فَاسْتَجِبْ دُعَآئِیْ وَاقْبَلْ ثَنَآئِیْ وَ اجْمَعْ بَیْنِیْ وَبَیْنَ اَوْلِیَآئِیْ بِحَقِّ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّعَلِیٍّ وَّفَاطِمَۃَ وَالْحَسَنِ وَالْحُسَیْنِ اِنَّكَ وَلِیُّ نَعْمَآئِیْ وَمُنْتَهٰی مُنَایَ وَغَایَۃُ رَجَآئِیْ فِیْ مُنْقَلَبِیْ وَمَثْوَایَ۔

AMINULLAH

Imam Ali (a.s)

Yeh Ziyarat-e-Aminullah ke naam se mashoor hai jiske raawi Jabir (a.r) jo Imam Baqir (a.s) aur Imam Zainul Aabedeen (a.s) se nakal karte huwe kaha keh dono Imam (a.s) qabr ke paas khade ho kar rote huwe yeh ziyarat ko padha…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Salaam ho aap par ay Allah ke zameen par uske ameen aur uske bando par uski hujjat. Salaam ho aap par ay Ameerul momineen (a.s). Mai gawaahi deta hu keh aap ne raahe Khuda mein jehaad kiya jo jehaad ka haq tha. Aur uski kitaab par amal kiya aur Allah ke Nabi (s.a.w.a) ki sunnato ka itteba kiya (Allah ka durood-o-salaam ho unpar aur unke Aal par). Yahan tak keh Allah ne aap ko apne jawaar mein bula liya aur apne iraade se aap ki ruh ko qabz kar liya. Aur aap ke dushmano par hujjat ko tamaam kar diya. Alawa un hujjato keh jo aap ke liye tamaam makhluqaat par hai. Lehaaza Khudaaya mere nafs ko apne qaza-o-qadr par mutmaeen-o-raazi kar de. Apne zikr-o-dua ka shahdaayi (chahne waala) bana de. Aur apne khaalis-o-barguzidah auliya ka mohib bana de. Aur apne aasmaan-o-zameen mein mehboob bana de. Apne bala ke nuzool par saabir aur apne behtareen nemato par shaakir bana de. Apni tamaam nemato ka yaad karne waala aur apni mulaqaat ki khushi ka mushtaaq bana de. Aur roze jaza ke liye namaaz ko zaad-e-raah banaane waala aur apne auliya ki sunnat par amal karne waala aur apne dushmano ke akhlaaq-o-amal se dur rehne waala, aur apni hamd-o-sana ke sahaare duniya se be-zaar rehne waala bana de.

Phir Imam (a.s) ne apne rukhsar ko qabr par rakh kar yeh kaha…

Ay Ma’bood! Be-shak muttaqi-o-parhez-gaar logo ka dil Teri taraf be-inteha taur par motawajjeh hai. Aur mushtaqeen ki raah Teri taraf khuli huwi hai. Aur Teri taraf iraada karne waalo ke liye nishaaniya waazeh hai. Aur Teri maarefat rakhne waalo ke dil darey huwe hai. Aur Teri taraf dua karne waalo ki aawaaze buland hai. Aur unke liye qabooliyat ke darwaaze khule huwe hai aur jo Tujh se munajaat karte hai unki dua qabool hai. Aur jo Tujh se tauba karte hai unki dua maqbool hai. Aur jo Tere khauf se roney waale hai unke aansoo (tears) maqaam-e-rahem mein hai. Aur jo Tujh se faryaad karne waale hai, unke liye faryaad-rasi maujood hai. Aur jo Tujh se madad chahe uske liye madad haazir hai. Aur Tere waade Tere bando ke liye mukammal hai. Aur jo Tujh se uzr (excuse) talab karte hai unki lagzisho (khataawo) ko bakhsha gaya hai. Aur amal karne waalo ke aamaal Teri baargah mein mehfuz hai. Aur Teri rozi Teri jaanib se makhluq par naazil hoti rahi hai. Aur mazeed (more) nemate unko milti rehti hai. Aur isteghfaar karne waalo ke gunah bakhshe huwe hai. Aur Teri makhluq ki haajate Teri baargah mein puri ho chuki hai. Aur sawaal karne waalo ke in’aam Tere paas waafir (farawaan) hai. Aur mazeed ataaya bhi motawaati (lagataar) hai. Aur rozi talab karne waalo ke liye (khwaan-e-ahsaan) behtareen dastarkhwaan mohiya (available) hai. Aur pyaaso ke liye sar-chashma-e-aab (paani ka jarna) bhara huwa hai. Lehaaza ay Ma’bood! Meri dua ko qabool kar le, aur meri hamd-o-sana ko bhi qabool farma. Aur mujh ko mere mehboob afraad ke saath jama kar de. Mohammad (s.a.w.a), Ali (a.s), Fatema (s.a), Hasan (a.s) aur Husain (a.s) ke waaste se, be-shak Tu mera wali-e-nemat hai. Aur meri aarzoo ki inteha aur ummeed ki manzil hai. Is duniya mein aur aakherat mein bhi.

Kameluz Ziyaraat mein yeh fikhra bhi bayaan kiya gaya hai…

Tu mera ma’bood aur mera sardaar-o-maula hai. Mere dosto ko bakhsh de aur ham se hamaare dushmano ko rok de. Aur unko hamein takleef dene se dur rakh, aur kalema-e-haq ko zaahir kar de aur usko buland qaraar de-de. Aur kalema-e-baatil ko palta de aur usko past kar de, be-shak Tu har cheez par qaadir hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

AMINULLAH

Imam Ali (a.s)

It is famous as Ziyarat-e-Ameenullah and it is related by Jabir (a.r.) from Imam Baqir (a.s.) and Imam Zainul Aabedeen (a.s.) that both the Imams (a.s.) stood near the grave, cried and then recited the ziyarat as follows…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Peace be upon you, O trustee of Allah on His lands and argument of Allah against His servants. Peace be upon you, O Commander of the Faithful. I bear witness that you strove for the sake of Allah as it ought to be striven, acted upon His Book, and followed the instructions of His Prophet, peace of Allah be upon him and his Household, until Allah called you to be in His vicinity. So, He grasped you to Him by His will and put your enemies under the claim although you have inclusive claims against all of His creatures. O Allah, (please do) cause my soul to be fully tranquil with Your decrees, satisfied with Your acts, fond of mentioning and praying to You, bearing love for the choicest of Your intimate servants, beloved in Your lands and heavens, steadfast against the affliction of Your tribulations, thankful for Your graceful bounties, always bearing in mind Your incessant gifts, longing for the gladness of meeting You, supplied with piety for the day of Your rewarding, pursuing the morals of Your intimate servants, quitting the conducts of Your enemies, and distracted from this world by praising and thanking You.

Then Imam (a.s) put his cheek on the tomb and said…

O Allah, the hearts of those humbling themselves to You are fascinated, the paths of those desiring for You are open, the sings of those directing to You are evident, the hearts of those having recognition of You are resorting to You, the voices of those beseeching You are mounting up to You, the doors of responding to them are wide open, the prayer of him who speaks to You confidentially is responded, the repentance of him who turns to You modestly is admitted, the tear of him who weeps on account of fear from You is compassionated, the aid of him who seeks Your aid is available, the help of him who seeks Your help is obtainable, Your promises to Your servants are fulfilled, the slips of him who implore You to excuse him are forgivable, the deeds of those who act for You are preserved, Your sustenance to the creatures are descending from You, Your gifts for further conferrals are reaching them, the sins of those imploring Your forgiveness are forgiven, the requests of Your creatures are granted by You, the prizes of those begging You are offered, Your gifts for further conferrals are uninterrupted, the dining tables for those seeking Your feeding are prepared, and the springs of quenching their thirst are brimful. O Allah, (so) respond to my prayer, accept my thanksgiving for You, and join me to my masters, [I beseech You] in the name of Muhammad, Fatimah, al-Hasan, and al-Husayn. You are verily the only source of my boons, the ultimate goal of my wishes, and the target of my hope in my recourses and settlement.

In the book of Kamil al-Ziyarat, the following statements are added…

You are verily my God, Master, and Lord. (Please) forgive our friend, prevent our enemies against us, distract them from harming us, give prevalence to the Word of Truth and make it the supreme, and frustrate the word of falsehood and make it the lowliest. Verily, You have power over all things.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
