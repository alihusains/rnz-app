DUA-E-SAFAR

Safar ke waqt yeh dua padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Paak o be-neyaaz hai woh Khuda jisne is sawaari ko hamare liye musakhkhar (stable) kar diya hai warna ham is ko qaabu mein la sakne waale nahi thay. Aur baher-haal ham apne Parwardigaar hi ki baargah mein palat kar jaane waale hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DUA-E-SAFAR

While travelling recite this dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Glorified be He who has subdued these unto us, and we were not capable (of subduing them), and verily, unto our Lord we return.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
