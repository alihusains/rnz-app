SUBH-E-ASHUR
Morning of Ashur

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

سُبْحَانَ اللہِ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلہِ وَلَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اللہُ وَ اللہُ اَکْبَرُ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّۃَ اِلَّا بِاللہِ الْعَلِّیِ الْعَظِیْمِ۔ سُبْحَانَ اللہِ اٰنَآئَ الَّیْلِ وَاَطْرَافَ النَّہَارِ سُبْحَانَ اللہِ بِالْغُدُوِّ وَالْاٰصَالِ سُبْحَانَ اللہِ حِیْنَ تُمْسُوْنَ وَ حِیْنَ تُصْبِحُوْنَ وَلَہُ الْحَمْدُ فِیْ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضِ وَعَشِیًّا وَّحِیْنَ تُظْہِرُوْنَ یُخْرِجُ الْحَیَّ مِنَ الْمَیِّتِ وَیُخْرِجُ الْمَیِّتَ مِنَ الْحَیِّ وَیُحْیِ الْاَرْضَ بَعْدَ مَوْتِہَا وَکَذٰلِکَ تُخْرَجُوْنَ۔ سُبْحَانَ رَبِّکَ رَبِّ الْعِزَّۃِ عَمَّا یَصِفُوْنَ وَسَلَامٌ عَلَی الْمُرْسَلِیْنَ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلہِ رَبِّ الْعٰلَمِیْنَ۔ اَلْحَمْدُ لِلہِ الَّذِیْ لَمْ یَتَّخِذْ وَلَدًا وَّلَمْ یَکُنْ لَّہٗ شَرِیْکٌ فِی الْمُلْکِ وَلَمْ یَکُنْ لَّہٗ وَلِیٌّ مِّنَ الذُّلِّ وَکَبِّرْہُ تَکْبِیْرًا۔ عَدَدَ کُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ وَّمِلْاَ کُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ وَاَضْعَافَ ذٰلِکَ اَضْعَافًا مُّضَاعَفَۃً اَبَدًا سَرْمَدًا کَمَا یَنْبَغِیْ لِعَظَمَتِہٖ۔ سُبْحَانَ ذِی الْمُلْکِ وَالْمَلَکُوْتِ سُبْحَانَ ذِی الْعِزَّۃِ وَالْجَبَرُوْتِ سُبْحَانَ الْحَیِّ الَّذِیْ لَا یَمُوْتُ سُبْحَانَ الْقُدُّوْسِ سُبْحَانَ الْقَآئِمِ الدَّآئِمِ سُبْحَنَا الْحَیِّ الْقَیُّوْمِ سُبْحَانَ الْعَلِیِّ الْاَعْلٰی سُبْحَانَہٗ وَتَعٰلٰی سُبْحَانَ اللہِ سُبُّوْحٌ قُدُّوْسٌ رَبُّ الْمَلٰئِکَۃِ وَالرُّوْحِ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ اِنِّیْ اَصْبَحْتُ فِیْ مِنَّۃٍ وَّمِعْمَۃٍ وَّعَافِیَۃٍ فَاَتْمِمْ عَلَیَّ نِعْمَتَکَ یَا اَللہُ وَمَنَّکَ وَعَافِیَتَکَ وَارْزُقْنِیْ شُکْرَکَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ بِنُوْرِ وَجْہِکَ اَھْتَدَیْتُ وَبِفَضْلِکَ اَسْتَغْنَیْتُ وَبِنِعْمَتِکَ اَصْبَحْتُ وَاَمْسَیْتُ اَصْبَحْتُ اُشْھِدُکَ وَکَفٰی بِکَ شَھِیْدًا وَّاُشْھِدُ مَلٰٓئِکَتَکَ وَحَمَلَۃَ عَرْشِکَ وَجَمِیْعَ خَلْقِکَ وَسَمَآئَکَ وَاَرْضَکَ وَجَنَّتَکَ وَنَارَکَ بِاَنَّکَ اَنْتَ اللہُ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّآ اَنْتَ وَحْدَکَ لَاشَرِیْکَ لَکَ وَاَنَّ مَا دُوْنَ عَرْشِکَ اِلٰی قَرَارِ اَرْضِکَ مِنْ مَعْبُوْدِ دُوْنَکَ بَاطِلٌ مُّضْمَحِلٌّ۔ وَاَشْھَدُ اَنَّ مُحَمَّدًا عَبْدُکَ وَرَسُوْلُکَ وَاَنَّ السَّاعَۃَ اٰتِیَۃٌ لَّارَیْبَ فِیْہَا وَاَنَّکَ بَاعِثُ مَنْ فِی الْقُبُوْرِ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ فَاکْتُبْ شَہَادَتِیْ ھٰذِہٖ عِنْدَکَ حَتّٰی اَلْقَاکَ بِہَا وَقَدْ رَضِیْتَ عَنِّیْ یَااَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِیْنَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَ فَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ حَمْدًا تَضَعُ لَکَ السَّمٰوٰتُ کَنَفَیْہَا وَتُسَبِّحُ لَکَ الْاَرْضُ وَمَنْ عَلَیْھَا حَمْدًا یَّصْعَدُ وَلَا یَنْفَدُ حَمْدًا یَّزِیْدٌ وَّلَا یَبِیْدُ حَمْدًا سَرْمَدًا لَا اِنْقِطَاعَ لَہٗ وَلَا نَفَادَ حَمْدًا یَّصْعَدُ اَوَّلُہٗ وَلَا یَفْنٰی اٰخِرُہٗ وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ عَلَیَّ وَفَوْقِیْ وَمَعِیْ وَاَمَامِیْ وَقَبْلِیْ وَلَدَیَّ وَاِذَا مِتُّ وَفَنَیْتُ وَبَقَیْتُ یَامَوْلَایَ وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ بِجَمِیْعِ مَحَا مِدِکَ کُلِّہَا عَلٰی جَمِیْعِ نَعْمَآئِکَ کُلِّہَا وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ فِیْ کُلِّ عِرْقٍ سَاکِنٍ وَّفِیْ کُلِّ اَکْلَۃٍ وَّشَرْبَۃٍ وَّلِبَاسٍ وَّقُوَّۃٍ وَّبَطْشٍ وَّعَلٰی مَوْضِعِ کُلِّ شَعْرَۃٍ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ لَکَ الْحَمْدُ کُلُّہٗ وَلَکَ الْمُلْکُ کُلُّہٗ وَبِیَدِکَ الْخَیْرُ کُلُّ اِلَیْکَ یَرْجِعُ الْاَمْرُ کُلُّہٗ عَلَا نَیَتُہٗ وَسِرُّہٗ وَاَنْتَ مُنْتَہَی الشَّئْانِ کُلُّہٗ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ لَکَ الْحَمْدُ عَلٰی حِلْمِکَ بَعْدَ عِلْمِکَ وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ عَلٰی عَفْوِکَ بَعْدَ قُدْرَتِکَ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ لَکَ الْحَمْدُ یَابَاعِثَ الْحَمْدِ وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ یَاوَارِثَ الْحَمْدِ وَبَدِیْعَ الْحَمْدِ وَمُنْتَہَی الْحَمْدِ وَمَبْدَاَ الْحَمْدِ وَفِیَّ الْعَھْدِ صَادِقَ الْوَعْدِ عَزِیْزَ الْجَدِّ وَقَدِیْمَ الْمَجْدِ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ لَکَ الْحَمْدُ رَفِیْعَ الدَّرَاجَاتِ مُجِیْبَ الدَّعْوَاتِ مُنْزِلَ الْاٰیَاتِ مِنْ فَوْقِ سَبْعِ سَمٰوٰتٍ مُّخْرِجَ مَنْ فِی الظُّلُمٰتِ اِلَی النُّوْرِ مُبَدِّلَ السَّیِّئَاتِ حَسَنَاتٍ وَّجَاعِلَ الْحَسَنَاتِ دَرَجَاتٍ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ لَکَ الْحَمْدُ غَافِرَ الذَّنْبِ وَقَابِلَ التَّوْبِ شَدِیْدَ الْعِقَابِ ذَا الطَّوْلِ لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ اِلَیْکَ الْمَصِیْرُ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ لَکَ الْحَمْدُ فِی اللَّیْلِ اِذَا یَغْشٰی وَفِی النَّہَارِ اِذَا تَجَلّٰی وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ فِی الْاٰخِرَۃِ وَالْاُوْلٰی۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ لَکَ الْحَمْدُ عَدَدَ کُلِّ نَجْمٍ فِی السَّمَآئِ وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ بِعَدَدِ کُلِّ مَلَکٍ فِی السَّمَآئِ وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ عَدَدَ کُلِّ قَطْرَۃٍ فِی الْبَحْرِ وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ عَدَدَ اَوْرَاقِ الْاَشْجَارِ وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ عَدَدَ الْجِنِّ وَالْاِنْسِ وَعَدَدَ الْبَہَآئِمِ وَالسِّبَاعِ وَالطَّیْرِ وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ عَدَدَ مَا فِیْ جَوْفِ الْاَرْضِ وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ عَدَدَ مَا عَلٰی وَجَہِ الْاَرْضِ وَلَکَ الْحَمْدُ عَدَدَ مَا اَحْصٰی کِتَابُکَ وَاَحَاطَ بِہٖ عِلْمُکَ وَزِنَۃَ عَرْشِکَ حَمْدًا کَثِیْرًا مُّبَارَکًا فِیْہِ۔ اَللّٰہُمَّ لَکَ الْحَمْدُ عَدَدَ مَا تَقُوْلُ وَعَدَدَ مَالَمْ تَعْلَمُ وَعَدَدَ مَا یَعْمَلُ خَلْقَکَ کُلُّہُمُ الْاَوَّلُوْنَ وَالْاٰخِرُوْنَ وَبَزَنَۃِ ذٰلِکَ کُلِّہٖ وَعَدَدَ مَا سَمَّیْنَا کُلُّہٗ وَاِذَا مِتْنَا وَفُنَیْنَا لَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اللہُ وَحْدَہٗ لَا شَرِیْکَ لَہٗ وَلَہُ الْمُلْکُ وَلَہُ الْحَمْدُ یُحْیِیْ وَیُمِیْتُ وَھُوَ عَلٰی کُلِّ شَیْٓءٍ قَدِیْرٌ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

SUBH-E-ASHUR

Morning of Ashur

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Glory be to Allah. Praise be to Allah. There is no God Save Allah. Allah is great. There is no might and no power, except (with) Allah, the Highest Supreme. Glory be to Allah, hour by hour in the night, and hour after hour in the day. Glory be to Allah, when day breaks, and at the time of sunset. Glory be to Allah, in the shadows of early evening, in the twilight of morning.

Glory be to Allah, when the evening passes away, and the morning departs (All) praise is unto Him, in the heavens and the earth, whether it is dark night, or broad daylight, He makes life come out from death, and lets death destroy life, He makes the soil grow fruitful again, after it becomes barren, like that shall you come out to face the Trial.

Glory be to your lord, the lord of Glory, who is above that which they attribute to Him, and peace be on (His) Messengers. All Praise be to Allah, the lord of the worlds. All praise be to Allah Who has not be taken son, who has no partner in the sovereignty nor does He have any protecting friend through dependence. And Magnify Him with all magnificence. His greatness is more worthy to the number of each thing and place and double, thrice to it, forever praise be to him.

Glory be to the lord of Kingdom and Realm. Glory be to the lord of Majesty and Magnificence. Glory be to Allah, the Sovereign, the Ever living who never dies. Glory be to Allah, the ever pure Lord. Glory be to the Everlasting Eternal. Glory be to Ever living, the Self-Existent. Glory be to the Exalted, the Most High. Glory be to Him, and Exalted be Him. Glory be to Him, Glorified (is He), Holy (is He). The Lord of the angels and the Spirit. O Allah, I begin my day with kindness, grace and good health from You. Make perfect Your kindness, grace and good health for me. O Allah cover me with well being and grant me thanksgiving to You.

O Allah, only by Your Light have I been guided. Only by Your grace have I been opulent, and only by Your grace have I lived in eves and morns. O Allah, I ask You to witness for me, and You are surely sufficient as a witness, and I also choose as witnesses Your angels, the bearers of Your Throne and all Your creatures, your skies and your earth, your heaven and your hell, that You are Allah, there is no God save You, One and Only Lord, having no partner, You are one and only one and have no partner and from earth to sky whoever is worshiped in invalid and week. And I witness that Muhammad, peace be upon him and his Household, is Your servant and Messenger, and definitely the Hour is coming, there is no doubt about it, and Allah shall raise up those who are in graves.

O Allah, (please do) certify this witness for me with You. So that You shall meet you with this witness and see that you are pleased and happy with me and You are the most merciful.

O Allah, to You be praise due to which the heavens humbles itself to You and the earth as well as all those on it glorify You. O Allah, to You be praise which starts but does not end. O Allah, to You be praise-eternal, endless. Glory which shall remain forever, having neither cessation nor exhaustion. Glory be to you whose starting is developing and whose end is far from annihilation. And your glory is deep and above me, with me and in front of me, in my heart and near me, always glorifying You. And when I die, perish, and shall be left. O my Allah, to You be all praise in all forms of praising You, for all your favours. To you be all praise for each breath and every food, drink, clothes and strength, extension, and for each place of a single hair.

O Allah! For You is all praise and in your hands is all goodness and all the people have to return back to you whether openly or secretly and You are the extreme height for all excellence. To You be all praise for You act forbearingly though You know. To You be all praise for You pardon though You have power (not to do). O Allah! To You be all praise and You are the source of praise. To You be all praise and You are the inheritor of praise. You are the fashioner of praise. You are the utmost of praise. You are the author of praise. You are truthful in promise, fulfiller of pledge, mighty in power, and subsistingly glorious. O Allah! To You be all praise and You are the highest in ranks, the responsive to prayers, the descender of sings (or Verses) from above seven skies, the bringer forth of those who are in darkness to light, the changer of evil deeds into good deeds, and the maker of good deeds raise to high ranks.

O Allah, to You be all praise and You are the Forgiver of the faults, the Acceptor of repentance, severe to punish, and Lord of bounty. There is no god save You; to You shall be the return. O Allah, to You be all praise in the night when it draws a veil and in the day when it shines in brightness. To You be all praise in the Hereafter and the former life. To You be all praise as many as the numbers of all stars and angels in the heavens. To You be all praise as many as the numbers of angels in the heavens. To You be all praise as much as the drops in the sea. To You be all praise as many as leaves of trees. To You be all praise as many as the numbers of jinn, men, the number of pests, birds, animals, and beasts. To You be all praise as many as all that which exists inside the earth surface. To You be all praise as many as all that which exists on the earth surface. To You be all praise as much as that which has been counted by Your Book and as much as all that which is comprehended by Your Knowledge and equal to the weight of Arsh, abundant praise, and as blessed as You.

O Allah! For you is all praise equal to the number of your saying and the your knowledge and praise be to You as per the number of deeds done by your complete creation, the before ones and the later ones and as per their weight and all those which we have named and equal to the numbers when we die and perish.

There is no god save Allah; One and Only and having no partner. To Him is the Kingdom and to Him is (all) praise. He gives life and causes to die and He has power over all things.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

Then recite 10 times each of the following…

I ask forgiveness of Allah

O Allah! O Allah!

O Beneficent! O Beneficent!

O Merciful! O Merciful!

O Giver! O Bestower!

O There is no god save Thou.

And there is neither power nor might save with Allah, the Most High, the All-magnificent.

So be it, So be it.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

And blessings of Allah and peace be on Muhammad, His Prophet, and on his children.
