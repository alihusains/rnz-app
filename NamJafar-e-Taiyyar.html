JAFAR-E-TAYYAR

Most preferable time for this namaz is Friday morning. It consists of 4 rakat and has to be recited in the form of 2x2. In 1st rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite Surah Zilzaal (Chp.99), in 2nd rak'at after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite Surah A'adiyaat (Chp.100), in 3rd rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite Surah Nasr (Chp.110) and in 4th rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) once. In each rakat after the 2nd surah, recite Tasbeehaat-e-Arba 15 times.

Is namaz ka behtareen waqt Juma ki subh. Yeh 4 rakat namaz, 2-2 kar ke padhi jaaye. 1st rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad Surah Zilzaal (Chp.99), 2nd rak'at mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad Surah A'adiyaat (Chp.100), 3rd rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad Surah Nasr (Chp.110) aur 4th rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) padhe. Har rakat mein Surah ke baad 15 martaba yeh Tasbeehaat-e-Arba padhe.

سُبْحَانَ اللہِ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلہِ وَلَا اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اللہُ وَاللہُ اَكْبَرُ۔

Then in each rakat recite 10 times Tasbeehaat-e-Arba in Ruku, while standing from Ruku, in 1st sajdah, while getting up from 1st sajdah, in 2nd sajdah, while getting up from 2nd Sajdah. In this way the total number of Tasbeehaat-e-Arba will be 300.

Iske baad 10 martaba yeh Tasbeehaat-e-Arba har rakat ke Ruku mein, Ruku se khade ho kar, 1st Sajdeh mein, 1st Sajdeh is uth kar, 2nd Sajdeh mein, 2nd Sajdeh se uth kar padhe. Is tarah 300 martaba Tasbeehaat-e-Arba hogi.

When your finish reciting Tasbeehat-e-Arba in the last sajdah of 4th rakat then continue to recite this dua…

Jab tum 4th rakat ke aakhri sajdeh mein Tasbeehat-e-Arba padh le to yeh dua bhi padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

سُبْحَانَ مَنْ لَبِسَ الْعِزَّ وَالْوَقَارَ سُبْحَانَ مَنْ تَعَطَّفَ بِالْمَجْدِ وَتَكَرَّمَ بِہٖ سُبْحَانَ مَنْ لَّا یَنْبَغِیْ التَّسْبِیْحُ اِلَّا لَہٗ سُبْحَانَ مَنْ اَحْصٰی كُلَّ شَیْ ئٍ عِلْمُہٗ سُبْحَانَ ذِی الْقُدْرَۃِ وَالْكَرَمِ۔ اَللّٰھُمَّ اِنِّیْ اَسْئَلُكَ بِمَعَاقِدِ مِنْ عَرْشِكَ وَمُنْتَھَی الرَّحْمَۃِ مِنْ كِتَابِكَ وَاسْمِكَ الْاَعْظَمِ وَكَلِمَاتِكَ التَّآمَّۃِ الَّتِیْ تَمَّتْ صِدْقًا وَعَدْلًا صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاَھْلِ بَیْتِہٖ وَافْعَلْ بِیْ كَذَا وَكَذَا۔

Then complete your namaz and ask for your needs to your Lord.
Iske baad namaz tamam kare aur apni haajaat talab kare.

Then raise your hands and repeat each zikr till your breath breaks…
Phir apne haatho ko utha kar yeh har ek zikr saans tutne tak kahe…

يَارَبِّ يَارَبِّ
يَارَبَّاهٗ يَارَبَّاهٗ
رَبِّ رَبِّ
يَااَللهُ يَااَللهُ
يَاحَىُّ يَاحَىُّ
يَارَحِيْمُ يَارَحِيْمُ
يَارَحِمَانُ يَارَحِمَانُ
يَااَرْحَمَ الرّاحِمِيْنْ
The recite this dua…
Phir yeh dua padhe…
اَللّٰھُمَّ اِنِّیْ اَفْتَتِحُ الْقَوْلَ بِحَمْدِكَ وَاَنْطِقُ بِالثَّنَآءِ عَلَیْكَ وَاُمَجِّدُكَ وَلَا غَایَۃَ لِمَدْحِكَ وَاُثْنِیْ عَلَیْكَ وَمَنْ یَبْلُغُ غَایَۃَ ثَنَآئِكَ وَاَمَدَ مَجْدِكَ وَاَنّٰی لِخَلِیْقَتِكَ كُنْہُ مَعْرِفَۃِ مَجْدِكَ وَاَیَّ زَمَنٍ لَمْ تَكُنْ مَمْدُوْحًا بِفَضْلِكَ مَوْصُوْفًا بِمَجْدِكَ عَوَّادًا عَلَی الْمُذْنِبِیْنَ بِحِلْمِكَ تَخَلَّفَ سُكَّانُ اَرْضِكَ عَنْ طَاعَتِكَ فَكُنْتَ عَلَیْھِمْ عَطُوْفًا بِجُوْدِكَ جَوَادًا بِفَضْلِكَ عَوَّادًا بِكَرَمِكَ یَالَآ اِلٰہَ اِلَّا اَنْتَ الْمَنَّانُ ذُوْ الْجَلَالِ وَالْاِكْرَامِ۔
Then prayer for your needs Insha-Allah will be fulfill.
Phir apni haajaat talab kare Insha-Allah qabool hogi.
