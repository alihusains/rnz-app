اَللّٰھُمَّ اِنَّہٗ لَیْسَ لِیْ عِلْمٌۢ بِمَوْضِعِ رِزْقِیْ وَاِنَّمَا اَطْلُبُہٗ بِخَطَرَاتٍ تَخْطُرُ عَلٰی قَلْبِیْ فَاَجُوْلُ فِیْ طَلَبِہِ الْبُلْدَانَ فَاَنَا فِیْمَآ اَنَا طَالِبٌ كَالْحَیْرَانِ لَا اَدْرِیْ اَفِیْ سَھْلٍ ھُوَ اَمْ فِیْ جَبَلٍ اَمْ فِیْ اَرْضٍ اَمْ فِیْ سَمَآءٍ اَمْ فِیْ بَرٍّ اَمْ فِیْ بَحْرٍ وَّعَلٰی یَدَیْ مَنْ وَّمِنْ قِبَلِ مَنْ وَّ قَدْ عَلِمْتُ اَنَّ عِلْمَہٗ عِنْدَكَ وَاَسْبَابَہٗ بِیَدِكَ وَاَنْتَ الَّذِیْ تَقْسِمُہٗ بِلُطْفِكَ وَتُسَبِّبُہٗ بِرَحْمَتِكَ۔ اَللّٰھُمَّ فَصَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّآلِہٖ وَاجْعَلْ یَارَبِّ رِزْقَكَ لِیْ وَاسِعًا وَمَطْلَبَہٗ سَھْلًا وَمَاخَذَہٗ قَرِیْبًا وَّلَا تُعَنِّنِیْ بِطَلَبِ مَا لَمْ تُقَدِّرْ لِیْ فِیْہِ رِزْقًا فَاِنَّكَ غَنِیٌّ عَنْ عَذَابِیْ وَاَنَا فَقِیْرٌ اِلٰی رَحْمَتِكَ فَصَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِہٖ وَجُدْ عَلٰی عَبْدِكَ بِفَضْلِكَ اِنَّكَ ذُوْ فَضْلٍ عَظِیْمٍ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلیٰ مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

TAQIBAAT-E-ISHA

Namaz-e-Isha ke baad yeh dua padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Khuda! Mai apne rizq ke maqam ko nahi jaanta aur mere dil mein na jaane kaise-kaise ajab khayalaat aate rehte hai (meri is haalat ke saath) mai apne rizq ko talaash karta rehta hu, aur rozi ki talaash mein shahar ba shahar ghumta hu. Pas jis ka mai talab-gaar hu, is ke baare mein hairaan wa pareshaan hu (is liye keh) mai nahi jaanta keh meri rozi sehra mein hai ya pahaado par, zameen ki taho mein hai ya aasmaano par, khushki mein hai ya dariyaao ki ghahraaiyo mein, aur kis ke paas hai (mujhe kuch bhi ilm nahi hai) lekin haa’n mai yeh jaanta hu (aur mera yeh aqeeda hai keh) meri is rozi ka ilm Tujhe hai aur is ke asbaab Tere qabze aur ikhteyaar mein hai aur Tu hi isey Apne lutf-o-karam se taqseem karta hai aur Apni rehmat se uske asbaab faraaham karta hai. Khudaaya! Pas Tu Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur (unke sadqe mein) mere Allah! Apne rizq ko mere liye wasee farma de aur us ki talab ko mere liye aasaan kar de aur us ke haasil kar ne ki jagah ko mujh se qareeb farma de. Aur jis cheez mein (mera) rizq nahi hai, us ke talab karne mein mujhe takleef mein na daal. Is liye keh Tu mere uppar azaab karne aur mere ranjh-o-gham se be-neyaaz hai, haala’n keh mai Teri rehmato ka mohtaaj hu. Pas Tu Muhammad (s.a.w.s) aur unki Aal (a.s) par durood naazil farma (aur unke tufaiyl) Tere bandhe ko Tera fazl-o-karam ataa farma. Be-shak Tu bade fazl-o-karam waala hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

TAQIBAAT-E-ISHA

1) Recite the following dua after Namaz-e-Isha…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah! surely, I have no knowledge of the main source of my subsistence, and I seek it only through the passing thoughts that come to my heart, and I wander about in cities searching for it, and I amperplexed in regard to what I search for.

I do not know whether it is in a valley or in the mountains, in the earth or in the heavens, in the continent or in the sea, and in whose hands it is, and from whom it is. And surely I know that Thou hast its Knowledge, and its sources are in Thy hand, and Thou alonedistributes it by Thy kindness, and creates opportunities through Thy mercy.

O Allah therefore bless Muhammad and his progeny; and O Lord! increase my sustenance, apportioned by Thee, and make easy to search and the source near; and do not make me strive in the search of what Thou hast not decreed from my sustenance. For, surely: Thou art indifferent to chastise me and I need Thy mercy. Bless Muhammad and his progeny and by Thy grace show generosity to Thy slave, surely Thou art the most generous.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
