WEDNESDAY

Wednesday has been assigned to Imam Moosa Kazim (a.s), Imam Ali Reza (a.s), Imam Muhammad Taqi (a.s) & Imam Ali Naqi (a.s).
Wednesday Imam Moosa Kazim (a.s), Imam Ali Reza (a.s), Imam Muhammad Taqi (a.s) aur Imam Ali Naqi (a.s) se mansub hai.

NAQSH…
One who looks at this Naqsh on Wednesday will be safe from misfortune, to enjoy goodwill and to receive satisfaction of all legitimate desire.

Jo shakhs is Naqsh ko baroz Wednesday dekhe to woh balaawo’n aur aafato’n se mehfooz rahega aur makhluq ki nazar mein mohtaram rahega aur Khuda uski haajat ko qabool karega.

ZIKR…
Recite 1000 times…
1000 Martaba padhe…

یَاحَیُّ یَاقَیُّوْمُ

YA HAYYO YA QAYYUM
O! The Ever-Living, O! The Ever-Lasting.
Ay hamesha zinda rehne waale, Ay hamesha Baaqi rehne waale.

NAMAZ…
Recite 4-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp.1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) and Surah Qadr (Chp.97) 1 time each.
4 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 1 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp.112) aur Surah Qadr (Chp.97) padhe.

DUA…
Recite Wednesday Dua.
Wednesday ki Dua padhe.

ZIYARAT…
Recite Wednesday Ziyarat.
Wednesday ki Ziyarat padhe.
