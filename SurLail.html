92 | LAIL | 21

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Raat ki qasam jab woh din ko dhaanp le.

2. Aur din ki qasam jab woh chamak jaaye.

3. Aur uski qasam jisne mard aur aurat ko paiyda kiya hai.

4. Be-shak tumhaari koshishe mukhtalif qisam ki hai.

5. Phir jisne maal ataa kiya aur taqwa ikhtiyaar kiya.

6. Aur neki ki tasdeeq (confirmation) ki.

7. To uske liye Ham aasaani ka intezaam kar dege.

8. Aur jisne bukhl (kanjoosi) kiya aur la-parwaahi barti.

9. Aur neki ko joothlaaya hai.

10. Is ke liye sakhti ki raah hamwaar kar dege.

11. Aur uska maal kuch kaam na aayega jab woh halaak ho jaayega.

12. Be-shak hidaayat ki zimme-daari hamare upar hai.

13. Aur duniya-o-aakherat ka ikhtiyaar hamare haatho mein hai.

14. To ham ne tumhe bhadak-ti huwi aag se daraaya.

15. Jis mein koi na jaayega siwaaye badbakht shakhs ke.

16. Jisne joothlaaya aur mooh pher liya.

17. Aur us se an-qareeb saheb-e-taqwa ko mehfooz rakkha jaayega.

18. Jo apne maal ko de kar pakeezgi ka ehtemaam karta hai.

19. Jab keh uske paas kisi ka koi ehsaan nahi hai jiski jaza di jaaye.

20. Siwaaye yeh keh woh Khudaaye buzurg ki marzi ka talab-gaar hai.

21. Aur an-qareeb woh raazi ho jaayega.

92 | LAIL | 21

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. I swear by the night when it draws a veil,

2. And the day when it shines in brightness,

3. And the creating of the male and the female,

4. Your striving is most surely (directed to) various (ends).

5. Then as for him who gives away and guards (against evil),

6. And accepts the best,

7. We will facilitate for him the easy end.

8. And as for him who is Parsimonius and considers himself free from need (of Allah),

9. And rejects the best,

10. We will facilitate for him the difficult end.

11. And his wealth will not avail him when he perishes.

12. Surely Ours is it to show the way,

13. And most surely Ours is the hereafter and the former.

14. Therefore I warn you of the fire that flames:

15. None shall enter it but the most unhappy,

16. Who gives the lie (to the truth) and turns (his) back.

17. And away from it shall be kept the one who guards most (against evil),

18. Who gives away his wealth, purifying himself.

19. And no one has with him any boon for which he should be rewarded,

20. Except the seeking of the pleasure of his Lord, the Most High.

21. And he shall soon be well-pleased.
