TAWASSUL
Allama Majlisi has mentioned this dua who confidently says that, the merciful Allah (s.w.t) is beseeched in the name of and for the sake of the Holy prophet (s.a.w.a) and his Ahlul Bayt (a.s), who are amongst the best human beings in status with Him. It is also known as "quick-fulfillment-of-all-legitimate-desires" dua...
Yeh sareeul-asar (jaldi asar karne waali) dua, Aimma-e-Masumeen (a.s) se waarid huwi hai. Aur is dua ko qabooliyate dua-o-takmeele haajaat aur pareshani-o-mushkilaat se najaat paane ke liye behtareen zariya qaraar diya gaya hai.

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a)
اَللّٰہُمَّ اِنِّیْ اَسْئَلُکَ وَاَتَوَجَّہٗ اِلَیْکَ بِنَبِیِّکَ نَبِیِّ الرَّحْمَۃِ۔ مُحَمَّدٍ صَلَّی اللہُ عَلَیْہِ وَاٰلِہٖ۔ یَااَبَا الْقَاسِمِ یَارَسُوْلَ اللہِ یَااِمَامَ الرَّحْمَۃِ یَا سَیِّدَنَا وَمَوْلَانَا اِنَّا تَوَجَّہْنَا وَاسْتَشْفَعْنَا وَتَوَسَّلْنَا بِکَ اِلَی اللہِ وَقَدَّمْنَاکَ بَیْنَ یَدَیْ حَاجَاتِنَا۔ یَاوَجِیْھًا عِندَ اللہِ اشْفَعْ لَنَا عِنْدَاللہِ۔

TAWASSUL

Yeh sareeul-asar (jaldi asar karne waali) dua, Aimma-e-Masumeen (a.s) se waarid huwi hai. Aur is dua ko qabooliyate dua-o-takmeele haajaat aur pareshani-o-mushkilaat se najaat paane ke liye behtareen zariya qaraar diya gaya hai.

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a)
Ay Ma’bood! Mai Tujh se sawaal karta hu, aur Teri taraf motawajjeh hota hu, Tere Paighambar, Nabi-e-rehmat Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) ke wasile se. Ay Abul Qasim, ay Allah ke Rasool (s.a.w.a), ay rehmato ke Imam. Ay hamaare sayyed-o-sardaar (ay hamaare aaqa-o-maula). Ham aap ki taraf motawajjeh hai aur aap ko baargah-e-ilaahi mein apna sifaarishi aur wasila qaraar dete hai aur ham ne aap ko apni haajato ke pesh-pesh (aaghe) rakkha hai. Ay Allah ke nazdeek barguzidah (izzat waale) aap Allah se hamaare liye sifaarish kijiye.

Ay mere sardaar-o-aaqa! Mai aap ke zariye (wasile se) tawajjoh karta hu (Khuda ki taraf). Ay mere Aimma (a.s) jo mera zakhira-e-aakherat (sarmaya) hai, us din jab mere paas kuchh na hoga. Allah ki baargah mein aap ke wasile ka mutamanni (khwahish-mand) hu, aur Allah ki baargah mein shifa’at chahta hu.

Pas aap sab Hazraat meri shifa’at Allah se farma dijiye aur mujhe mere gunaho se bacha lijiye Allah ki baargah mein. Is liye keh aap hi log to mere liye wasila-e-najaat hai Allah ki baargah mein, aur aap logo ki mohabbat-o-taqarrub se hi mujhe najaat ki ummeed hai Allah ki baargah mein.

Pas aap hi log Allah ke nazdeek meri ummeed ban jaaye, ay mere sardaaro! ay Allah ke dosto! Allah ki rehmat naazil ho un sab par. Khuda ke dushmano aur aap (Hazraat) par zulm karne waalo par Allah ki laanat ho. Chahe woh awwaleen mein se ho, Ya aakhereen mein se. Ay tamaam aalameen ke Parwardigaar aisa hi kar unke saath. Aameen!
