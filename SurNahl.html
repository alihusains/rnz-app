بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَتٰٓى اَمْرُ اللهِ فَلَا تَسْتَعْجِلُوْهُط سُبْحٰنَهٗ وَتَعٰلٰى عَمَّا يُشْرِكُوْنَﯜ1ﯛ يُنَزِّلُ الْمَلٰۗئِكَةَ بِالرُّوْحِ مِنْ اَمْرِهٖ عَلٰى مَنْ يَّشَاۗءُ مِنْ عِبَادِهٖٓ اَنْ اَنْذِرُوْٓا اَنَّهٗ لَٓا اِلٰهَ اِلَّآ اَنَا فَاتَّقُوْنِﯜ2ﯛ خَلَقَ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضَ بِالْحَقِّط تَعٰلٰى عَمَّا يُشْرِكُوْنَﯜ3ﯛ خَلَقَ الْاِنْسَانَ مِنْ نُّطْفَةٍ فَاِذَا هُوَ خَصِيْمٌ مُّبِيْنٌﯜ4ﯛ وَالْاَنْعَامَ خَلَقَهَاج لَكُمْ فِيْهَا دِفْءٌ وَّمَنَافِعُ وَمِنْهَا تَاْكُلُوْنَﯜ5ﯛ وَلَكُمْ فِيْهَا جَمَالٌ حِيْنَ تُرِيْحُوْنَ وَحِيْنَ تَسْرَحُوْنَﯜ6ﯛ وَتَحْمِلُ اَثْقَالَكُمْ اِلٰى بَلَدٍ لَّمْ تَكُوْنُوْا بٰلِغِيْهِ اِلَّا بِشِقِّ الْاَنْفُسِط اِنَّ رَبَّكُمْ لَرَءُوْفٌ رَّحِيْمٌﯜ7ﯛ وَالْخَيْلَ وَالْبِغَالَ وَالْحَمِيْرَ لِتَرْكَبُوْهَا وَزِيْنَةًط وَيَخْلُقُ مَا لَا تَعْلَمُوْنَﯜ8ﯛ وَعَلَى اللهِ قَصْدُ السَّبِيْلِ وَمِنْهَا جَاۗئِرٌط وَلَوْ شَاۗءَ لَهَدٰكُمْ اَجْمَعِيْنَﯜ9ﯛ هُوَ الَّذِيْٓ اَنْزَلَ مِنَ السَّمَاۗءِ مَاۗءً لَّكُمْ مِّنْهُ شَرَابٌ وَّمِنْهُ شَجَرٌ فِيْهِ تُسِيْمُوْنَﯜ10ﯛ يُنْۢبِتُ لَكُمْ بِهِ الزَّرْعَ وَالزَّيْتُوْنَ وَالنَّخِيْلَ وَالْاَعْنَابَ وَمِنْ كُلِّ الثَّمَرٰتِط اِنَّ فِيْ ذٰلِكَ لَاٰيَةً لِّقَوْمٍ يَّتَفَكَّرُوْنَﯜ11ﯛ وَسَخَّرَ لَكُمُ اللَّيْلَ وَالنَّهَارَلا وَالشَّمْسَ وَالْقَمَرَط وَالنُّجُوْمُ مُسَخَّرٰتٌۢ بِاَمْرِهٖط اِنَّ فِيْ ذٰلِكَ لَاٰيٰتٍ لِّقَوْمٍ يَّعْقِلُوْنَﯜ12ﯛ وَمَا ذَرَاَ لَكُمْ فِي الْاَرْضِ مُخْتَلِفًا اَلْوَانُهٗط اِنَّ فِيْ ذٰلِكَ لَاٰيَةً لِّقَوْمٍ يَّذَّكَّرُوْنَﯜ13ﯛ وَهُوَ الَّذِيْ سَخَّرَ الْبَحْرَ لِتَاْكُلُوْا مِنْهُ لَحْمًا طَرِيًّا وَّتَسْتَخْرِجُوْا مِنْهُ حِلْيَةً تَلْبَسُوْنَهَاج وَتَرَى الْفُلْكَ مَوَاخِرَ فِيْهِ وَلِتَبْتَغُوْا مِنْ فَضْلِهٖ وَلَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُوْنَﯜ14ﯛ وَاَلْقٰى فِي الْاَرْضِ رَوَاسِيَ اَنْ تَمِيْدَ بِكُمْ وَاَنْهٰرًا وَّسُبُلًا لَّعَلَّكُمْ تَهْتَدُوْنَﯜ15ﯛ وَعَلٰمٰتٍط وَبِالنَّجْمِ هُمْ يَهْتَدُوْنَﯜ16ﯛ اَفَمَنْ يَّخْلُقُ كَمَنْ لَّا يَخْلُقُط اَفَلَا تَذَكَّرُوْنَﯜ17ﯛ وَاِنْ تَعُدُّوْا نِعْمَةَ اللهِ لَا تُحْصُوْهَاط اِنَّ اللهَ لَغَفُوْرٌ رَّحِيْمٌﯜ18ﯛ وَاللهُ يَعْلَمُ مَا تُسِرُّوْنَ وَمَا تُعْلِنُوْنَﯜ19ﯛ وَالَّذِيْنَ يَدْعُوْنَ مِنْ دُوْنِ اللهِ لَا يَخْلُقُوْنَ شَيْئًا وَّهُمْ يُخْلَقُوْنَﯜ20ﯛ اَمْوَاتٌ غَيْرُ اَحْيَاۗءٍج وَمَا يَشْعُرُوْنَلا اَيَّانَ يُبْعَثُوْنَﯜ21ﯛ اِلٰهُكُمْ اِلٰهٌ وَّاحِدٌج فَالَّذِيْنَ لَا يُؤْمِنُوْنَ بِالْاٰخِرَةِ قُلُوْبُهُمْ مُّنْكِرَةٌ وَّهُمْ مُّسْتَكْبِرُوْنَﯜ22ﯛ لَا جَرَمَ اَنَّ اللهَ يَعْلَمُ مَا يُسِرُّوْنَ وَمَا يُعْلِنُوْنَط اِنَّهٗ لَا يُحِبُّ الْمُسْتَكْبِرِيْنَﯜ23ﯛ وَاِذَا قِيْلَ لَهُمْ مَّاذَآ اَنْزَلَ رَبُّكُمْلا قَالُوْٓا اَسَاطِيْرُ الْاَوَّلِيْنَﯜ24ﯛ لِيَحْمِلُوْٓا اَوْزَارَهُمْ كَامِلَةً يَّوْمَ الْقِيٰمَةِلا وَمِنْ اَوْزَارِ الَّذِيْنَ يُضِلُّوْنَهُمْ بِغَيْرِ عِلْمٍط اَلَا سَاۗءَ مَا يَزِرُوْنَﯜ25ﯛ قَدْ مَكَرَ الَّذِيْنَ مِنْ قَبْلِهِمْ فَاَتَى اللهُ بُنْيَانَهُمْ مِّنَ الْقَوَاعِدِ فَخَرَّ عَلَيْهِمُ السَّقْفُ مِنْ فَوْقِهِمْ وَاَتٰهُمُ الْعَذَابُ مِنْ حَيْثُ لَا يَشْعُرُوْنَﯜ26ﯛ ثُمَّ يَوْمَ الْقِيٰمَةِ يُخْزِيْهِمْ وَيَقُوْلُ اَيْنَ شُرَكَاۗئِيَ الَّذِيْنَ كُنْتُمْ تُشَاۗقُّوْنَ فِيْهِمْط قَالَ الَّذِيْنَ اُوْتُوا الْعِلْمَ اِنَّ الْخِزْيَ الْيَوْمَ وَالسُّوْۗءَ عَلَى الْكٰفِرِيْنَﯜ27ﯛلا الَّذِيْنَ تَتَوَفّٰهُمُ الْمَلٰۗئِكَةُ ظَالِمِيْٓ اَنْفُسِهِمْص فَاَلْقَوُا السَّلَمَ مَا كُنَّا نَعْمَلُ مِنْ سُوْۗءٍ بَلٰٓى اِنَّ اللهَ عَلِيْمٌۢ بِمَا كُنْتُمْ تَعْمَلُوْنَﯜ28ﯛ فَادْخُلُوْٓا اَبْوَابَ جَهَنَّمَ خٰلِدِيْنَ فِيْهَاط فَلَبِئْسَ مَثْوَى الْمُتَكَبِّرِيْنَﯜ29ﯛ وَقِيْلَ لِلَّذِيْنَ اتَّقَوْا مَاذَا اَنْزَلَ رَبُّكُمْط قَالُوْا خَيْرًاط لِلَّذِيْنَ اَحْسَنُوْا فِيْ هٰذِهِ الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةٌط وَلَدَارُ الْاٰخِرَةِ خَيْرٌط وَلَنِعْمَ دَارُ الْمُتَّقِيْنَﯜ30ﯛ جَنّٰتُ عَدْنٍ يَّدْخُلُوْنَهَا تَجْرِيْ مِنْ تَحْتِهَا الْاَنْهَارُ لَهُمْ فِيْهَا مَا يَشَاۗءُوْنَط كَذٰلِكَ يَجْزِي اللهُ الْمُتَّقِيْنَﯜ31ﯛلا الَّذِيْنَ تَتَوَفّٰهُمُ الْمَلٰۗئِكَةُ طَيِّبِيْنَلا يَقُوْلُوْنَ سَلٰمٌ عَلَيْكُمُلا ادْخُلُوا الْجَنَّةَ بِمَا كُنْتُمْ تَعْمَلُوْنَﯜ32ﯛ هَلْ يَنْظُرُوْنَ اِلَّآ اَنْ تَاْتِيَهُمُ الْمَلٰۗئِكَةُ اَوْ يَاْتِيَ اَمْرُ رَبِّكَط كَذٰلِكَ فَعَلَ الَّذِيْنَ مِنْ قَبْلِهِمْط وَمَا ظَلَمَهُمُ اللهُ وَلٰكِنْ كَانُوْٓا اَنْفُسَهُمْ يَظْلِمُوْنَﯜ33ﯛ فَاَصَابَهُمْ سَيِّاٰتُ مَا عَمِلُوْا وَحَاقَ بِهِمْ مَّا كَانُوْا بِهٖ يَسْتَهْزِئُوْنَﯜ34ﯛ وَقَالَ الَّذِيْنَ اَشْرَكُوْا لَوْ شَاۗءَ اللهُ مَا عَبَدْنَا مِنْ دُوْنِهٖ مِنْ شَيْءٍ نَّحْنُ وَلَٓا اٰبَاۗؤُنَا وَلَا حَرَّمْنَا مِنْ دُوْنِهٖ مِنْ شَيْءٍط كَذٰلِكَ فَعَلَ الَّذِيْنَ مِنْ قَبْلِهِمْج فَهَلْ عَلَى الرُّسُلِ اِلَّا الْبَلٰغُ الْمُبِيْنُﯜ35ﯛ وَلَقَدْ بَعَثْنَا فِيْ كُلِّ اُمَّةٍ رَّسُوْلًا اَنِ اعْبُدُوا اللهَ وَاجْتَنِبُوا الطَّاغُوْتَج فَمِنْهُمْ مَّنْ هَدَى اللهُ وَمِنْهُمْ مَّنْ حَقَّتْ عَلَيْهِ الضَّلٰلَةُط فَسِيْرُوا فِي الْاَرْضِ فَانْظُرُوْا كَيْفَ كَانَ عَاقِبَةُ الْمُكَذِّبِيْنَﯜ36ﯛ اِنْ تَحْرِصْ عَلٰى هُدٰهُمْ فَاِنَّ اللهَ لَا يَهْدِيْ مَنْ يُّضِلُّ وَمَا لَهُمْ مِّنْ نّٰصِرِيْنَﯜ37ﯛ وَاَقْسَمُوْا بِاللهِ جَهْدَ اَيْمَانِهِمْ ۙ لَا يَبْعَثُ اللهُ مَنْ يَّمُوْتُ ط بَلٰى وَعْدًا عَلَيْهِ حَقًّا وَّلٰكِنَّ اَكْثَرَ النَّاسِ لَا يَعْلَمُوْنَﯜ38ﯛ لِيُبَيِّنَ لَهُمُ الَّذِيْ يَخْتَلِفُوْنَ فِيْهِ وَلِيَعْلَمَ الَّذِيْنَ كَفَرُوْٓا اَنَّهُمْ كَانُوْا كٰذِبِيْنَﯜ39ﯛ اِنَّمَا قَوْلُنَا لِشَيْءٍ اِذَآ اَرَدْنٰهُ اَنْ نَّقُوْلَ لَهٗ كُنْ فَيَكُوْنُﯜ40ﯛ وَالَّذِيْنَ هَاجَرُوْا فِي اللهِ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ مَا ظُلِمُوْا لَنُبَوِّئَنَّهُمْ فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةًط وَلَاَجْرُ الْاٰخِرَةِ اَكْبَرُم لَوْ كَانُوْا يَعْلَمُوْنَﯜ41ﯛ الَّذِيْنَ صَبَرُوْا وَعَلٰى رَبِّهِمْ يَتَوَكَّلُوْنَﯜ42ﯛ وَمَآ اَرْسَلْنَا مِنْ قَبْلِكَ اِلَّا رِجَالًا نُّوْحِيْٓ اِلَيْهِمْ فَاسْئَلُوْٓا اَهْلَ الذِّكْرِ اِنْ كُنْتُمْ لَا تَعْلَمُوْنَﯜ43ﯛ بِالْبَيِّنَاتِ وَالزُّبُرِط وَاَنْزَلْنَآ اِلَيْكَ الذِّكْرَ لِتُبَيِّنَ لِلنَّاسِ مَا نُزِّلَ اِلَيْهِمْ وَلَعَلَّهُمْ يَتَفَكَّرُوْنَﯜ44ﯛ اَفَاَمِنَ الَّذِيْنَ مَكَرُوا السَّيِّاٰتِ اَنْ يَّخْسِفَ اللهُ بِهِمُ الْاَرْضَ اَوْ يَاْتِيَهُمُ الْعَذَابُ مِنْ حَيْثُ لَا يَشْعُرُوْنَﯜ45ﯛ اَوْ يَاْخُذَهُمْ فِيْ تَقَلُّبِهِمْ فَمَا هُمْ بِمُعْجِزِيْنَﯜ46ﯛ اَوْ يَاْخُذَهُمْ عَلٰى تَخَوُّفٍط فَاِنَّ رَبَّكُمْ لَرَءُوْفٌ رَّحِيْمٌﯜ47ﯛ اَوَلَمْ يَرَوْا اِلٰى مَا خَلَقَ اللهُ مِنْ شَيْءٍ يَّتَفَيَّؤُا ظِلٰلُهٗ عَنِ الْيَمِيْنِ وَالشَّمَاۗئِلِ سُجَّدًا لِّلهِ وَهُمْ دٰخِرُوْنَﯜ48ﯛ وَلِلهِ يَسْجُدُ مَا فِي السَّمٰوٰتِ وَمَا فِي الْاَرْضِ مِنْ دَاۗبَّةٍ وَّالْمَلٰۗئِكَةُ وَهُمْ لَا يَسْتَكْبِرُوْنَﯜ49ﯛ يَخَافُوْنَ رَبَّهُمْ مِّنْ فَوْقِهِمْ وَيَفْعَلُوْنَ مَا يُؤْمَرُوْنَ۩ﯜ50ﯛالسجدۃ وَقَالَ اللهُ لَا تَتَّخِذُوْٓا اِلٰهَيْنِ اثْنَيْنِج صَدَقَ اللهُ الْعَلِیُّ الْعَظِیْمُ

16 | NAHL | 128

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Amr-e-ilaahi aa gaya hai lehaaza ab bila-wajah jaldi na machaawo keh Khuda unke shirk se paak-o-pakeeza aur buland-o-baala hai.

2. Woh jis bande par chahta hai apne hukm se malaayeka ko rooh ke saath naazil kar deta hai keh un bando ko daraawo aur samjha’o keh mere alaawa koi Khuda nahi hai lehaaza mujhi se darey.

3. Usi Khuda ne zameen-o-aasmaan ko haq ke saath paiyda kiya hai aur woh unke shariko se bohot buland-o-baalaatar hai.

4. Us ne insaan ko ek qatr-e-najas se paiyda kiya hai magar phir bhi woh khullam-khulla jaghda karne waala ho gaya hai.

5. Aur usi ne chopaayo (animals) ko bhi paiyda kiya hai jin mein tumhaare liye garam libaas aur digar munaafe ka saamaan hai aur baaz ko to tum khaate bhi ho.

6. Aur tumhaare liye un hi jaanwaro mein se zeenat ka saamaan hai jab tum shaam ko unhe waapas laate ho aur subah ko charaa-gah ki taraf le jaate ho.

7. Aur yeh haiwaanaat tumhaare bojh ko utha kar un shahero tak le jaate hai jahan tak tum jaan jokham mein daale bagair nahi pohonch sakte thay be-shak tumhaara Parwardigaar bada shafeeq aur maher-baan hai.

8. Aur us ne ghode, khachar aur gadhe ko paiyda kiya ta-keh us par sawaari karo aur usey zeenat bhi qaraar do aur woh aisi cheezo ko bhi paiyda karta hai jin ka tumhe ilm bhi nahi hai.

9. Aur darmiyaani raasteki hidaayat Khuda ki apni zimme-daari hai aur baaz raaste kaj bhi hote hai aur woh chahta to tum sab ko zabardasti raahe raast par le aata.

10. Woh wohi Khuda hai jisne aasmaan se paani naazil kiya hai jiska ek hissa peene waala hai aur ek hisse se darakht paiyda hote hai jin se tum jaanwaro ko charaate ho.

11. Woh tumhaare liye zara’at, zaitoon, khurme, angoor aur tamaam phal usi paani se paiyda karta haiis amr mein bhi sahebaan-e-fikr ke liye uski qudrat ki nishaaniya paayi jaati hai.

12. Aur usi ne tumhaare raat-din aur aaftaab-o-mehtaab sab ko musakhkhar (stable) kar diya hai aur sitaare bhi usi ke hukm ke taabe haibe-shak is mein bhi sahebaan-e-aql ke liye qudrat ki bohot si nishaaniya paayi jaati hai.

13. Aur jo kuch tumhaare liye is zameen ke andar mukhtalif rango (colours) mein paiyda kiya hai us mein bhi ibrat (sabak/lesson) haasil karne waali qaum ke liye uski nishaaniya paayi jaati hai.

14. Aur wohi woh hai jisne samandro ko musakhkhar (stable) kar diya hai ta-keh tum us mein se taaza gosht kha sako aur pahenne ke liye zeenat ka saamaan nikaal sako aur tum to dekh rahe ho keh kashtiya kis tarah uske seeneh ko chirti huwi chali ja rahi hai aur yeh sab is liye bhi hai keh tum uske fazl-o-karam ko talaash kar sako aur shaayad isi tarah uske shukr-guzaar bande bhi ban jaawo.

15. Aur us ne zameen mein pahaado ke langar daal diye ta-keh tumhe le kar apni jagah se hatt na jaaye aur nehre aur raaste bana diye ta-keh manzil-e-safar mein hidaayat paa sako.

16. Aur alamaat mo’ayyan kardi aur log sitaro se bhi raaste daryaaft kar lete hai.

17. Kya aisa paiyda karne waala unke jaisa ho sakta hai jo kuch nahi paiyda kar sakte aakhir tumhe hosh kyu’n nahi aa raha hai.

18. Aur tum Allah ki nemato ko shumaar bhi karna chaaho to nahi kar sakte ho be-shak Allah bada maher-baan aur bakhshne waala hai.

19. Aur Allah hi tumhaare baatin-o-zaahir dono se ba-khabar hai.

20. Aur uske alaawa jinhe yeh mushreqeen pukaarte hai woh khud hi makhlooq hai aur woh kisi cheez ko khalq nahi kar sakte hai.

21. Woh to murda hai un mein zindagi bhi nahi hai aur na unhe yeh khabar hai keh murde kab uthaaye jaayege.

22. Tumhara Khuda sirf ek hai aur jo log aakherat par imaan nahi rakhte hai unke dil munkir qism ke hai aur woh khud magroor-o-mutaqabbir hai.

23. Yaqeenan Allah un tamaam baato ko jaanta hai jinhe yeh chhipaate hai ya jin ka izhaar karte hai woh mutakabberin ko harghiz pasand nahi karta hai.

24. Aur jab un se poocha jaata hai keh tumhaare Parwardigaar ne kya kya naazil kiya hai to kehte hai keh yeh sab pichle logo ke afsaane hai.

25. Ta-keh yeh mukammal taur par apna bhi bojh uthaaye aur apne un mureedo ka bhi bojh uthaaye jinhe bila-ilm-o-feham ke gumraah karte rahe hai. Be-shak yeh bada bad-tareen bojh uthaane waale hai.

26. Yaqeenan un se pehle waalo ne bhi makkaariya ki thi to azaab-e-ilaahi unki taamiraat tak aaya aur usey jadd se ukhaad phaink diya aur unke saro par chhat gir padi aur azaab aise andaaz se aaya keh unhe shaoor bhi na paiyda ho saka.

27. Is ke baad woh roz-e-qayamat unhe ruswa karega aur poochhega kaha’n hai woh mere shareek jin ke baare mein tum jaghda kiya karte thay us waqt sahebaan-e-ilm Kahenge keh aaj ruswaai aur buraayi un kaafiro ke liye saabit ho gayi hai.

28. Jinhe malaayeka is aalam mein uthaate hai keh woh apne nafs ke zaalim hote hai to us waqt ita’at ki pesh-kash karte hai keh ham to koi buraayi nahi karte thay. Be-shak Khuda khoob jaanta hai keh tum kya Kya karte thay.

29. Jaawo ab Jahannam ke darwaazo se daakhil ho jaawo aur hamesha wahi raho ke mutakabberin ka thikaana bohot bura hota hai.

30. Aur jab sahebaan-e-taqwa se kaha gaya ke tumhaare Parwardigaar ne kya naazil kiya hai to unho ne kaha keh sab khair haibe-shak jin logo ne is duniya meinnek aamaal kiye hai unke liye neki hai aur aakherat ka ghar to baher-haal behtar hai aur woh muttaqeen ka behtareen makaan hai.

31. Wahan hamesha rehne waale baaghaat hai jin mein yeh log daakhil hoge aur unke neeche nehre jaari hogi woh jo kuch chahenge sab unke liye haazir hoga ke Allah isi tarah un sahebaan-e-taqwa ko jaza deta hai.

32. Jinhe malaayeka is aalam mein uthate hai keh woh paak-o-pakeeza hote hai aur un se malaayeka kehte hai keh tum par salaam ho ab tum apne nek aamaal ki bina par jannat mein daakhil ho jaawo.

33. Kya yeh log sirf is baat ka intezaar kar rahe hai keh unke paas malaayeka aa jaaye ya hukm-e-Parwardigaar aa jaaye to yahi unke pehle waalo ne bhi kiya tha aur Allah ne un par koi zulm nahi kiya hai bal-keh yeh khud hi apne nafs par zulm karte rahe hai.

34. Nateeja yeh huwa keh unke aamaal ke bure asraat un tak pohonch gaye aur jin baato ka yeh mazaaq udaaya karte thay un hi baato ne unhe apne ghere mein le liya aur phir tabaah-o-barbaad kar diya.

35. Aur mushreqeen kehte hai keh agar Khuda chahta to ham ya hamare buzurg uske alaawa kisi ki ibaadat na karte aur na uske hukm ke bagair kisi shaiy ko haraam qaraar deteisi tarah unke pehle waalo ne bhi kiya tha to kya Rasoolo ki zimme-daari waazeh elaan ke alaawa kuch aur bhi hai.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Allah’s commandment has come, therefore do not desire to hasten it; glory be to Him, and highly exalted be He above what they associate (with Him).

2. He sends down the angels with the inspiration by His commandment on whom He pleases of His servants, saying: Give the warning that there is no god but Me, therefore be careful (of your duty) to Me.

3. He created the heavens and the earth with the truth, highly exalted be He above what they associate (with Him).

4. He created man from a small seed and lo! he is an open contender.

5. And He created the cattle for you; you have in them warm clothing and (many) advantages, and of them do you eat.

6. And there is beauty in them for you when you drive them back (to home), and when you send them forth (to pasture).

7. And they carry your heavy loads to regions which you could not reach but with distress of the souls; most surely your Lord is Compassionate, Merciful.

8. And (He made) horses and mules and asses that you might ride upon them and as an ornament; and He creates what you do not know.

9. And upon Allah it rests to show the right way, and there are some deviating (ways); and if He please He would certainly guide you all aright.

10. He it is Who sends down water from the cloud for you; it gives drink, and by it (grow) the trees upon which you pasture.

11. He causes to grow for you thereby herbage, and the olives, and the palm trees, and the grapes, and of all the fruits; most surely there is a sign in this for a people who reflect.

12. And He has made subservient for you the night and the day and the sun and the moon, and the stars are made subservient by His commandment; most surely there are signs in this for a people who ponder;

13. And what He has created in the earth of varied hues most surely there is a sign in this for a people who are mindful.

14. And He it is Who has made the sea subservient that you may eat fresh flesh from it and bring forth from it ornaments which you wear, and you see the ships cleaving through it, and that you might seek of His bounty and that you may give thanks.

47. Or that He may not seize them by causing them to suffer gradual loss, for your Lord is most surely Compassionate, Merciful.

50. They fear their Lord above them and do what they are commanded.

51. And Allah has said: Take not two gods, He is only one God; so of Me alone should you be afraid.

53. And whatever favor is (bestowed) on you it is from Allah; then when evil afflicts you, to Him do you cry for aid.

60. For those who do not believe in the hereafter is an evil attribute, and Allah’s is the loftiest attribute; and He is the Mighty, the Wise.

61. And if Allah had destroyed men for their iniquity, He would not leave on the earth a single creature, but He respites them till an appointed time; so when their doom will come they shall not be able to delay (it) an hour nor can they bring (it) on (before its time).

64. And We have not revealed to you the Book except that you may make clear to them that about which they differ, and (as) a guidance and a mercy for a people who believe.

65. And Allah has sent down water from the cloud and therewith given life to the earth after its death; most surely there is a sign in this for a people who would listen.

66. And most surely there is a lesson for you in the cattle; We give you to drink of what is in their bellies— from betwixt the feces and the blood— pure milk, easy and agreeable to swallow for those who drink.

67. And of the fruits of the palms and the grapes— you obtain from them intoxication and goodly provision; most surely there is a sign in this for a people who ponder.

68. And your Lord revealed to the bee saying: Make hives in the mountains and in the trees and in what they build:

69. Then eat of all the fruits and walk in the ways of your Lord submissively. There comes forth from within it a beverage of many colours, in which there is healing for men; most surely there is a sign in this for a people who reflect.

70. And Allah has created you, then He causes you to die, and of you is he who is brought back to the worst part of life, so that after having knowledge he does not know anything; surely Allah is Knowing, Powerful.

71. And Allah has made some of you excel others in the means of subsistence, so those who are made to excel do not give away their sustenance to those whom their right hands possess so that they should be equal therein; is it then the favor of Allah which they deny?

75. Allah sets forth a parable: (consider) a slave, the property of another, (who) has no power over anything, and one whom We have granted from Ourselves a goodly sustenance so he spends from it secretly and openly; are the two alike? (All) praise is due to Allah! Nay, most of them do not know.

And Allah has made for you of what He has created shelters, and He has given you in the mountains places of retreat, and He has given you garments to preserve you from the heat and coats of mail to preserve you in your fighting; even thus does He complete His favor upon you, that haply you may submit.

128. Surely Allah is with those who guard (against evil) and those who do good (to others).
