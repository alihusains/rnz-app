DAHWUL ARZ

25th Zilqadah

This night is called Laylatul Dahwul-Arz. Imam Reza (a.s) said, “At the 25th night of Zilqadah, Prophet Abraham was born, Prophet Jesus the son of Mary was born, and the earth was spread on the water where the Holy Kaba is now situated. Hence, if one observes fasting on this day, he will be given the reward of observing fasting in 60 months”. According to another narration, Imam Reza (a.s) added, “Verily, on this day, Al-Qaem (Imam Mahdi (a.t.f.s)) will appear.”
The 25th Zilqadah is one of the four days on which fasting is highly recommended. According to a narration, to observe fasting on this day is equal to 70 years of fasting. It also erases the sins of 70 years. To practice acts of worship at its night is equal (in reward) to 100 years of worship. Everything that is between the sky and the earth will ask God’s forgiveness for one who does these rites. On this day, the mercy of Almighty Allah has been spread.

Yeh shab Laylatul Dahwul Arz khelaati hai. Imam Reza (a.s) farmate hai keh 25th Zilqadah ki shab mein Paighamber Ibrahim (a.s), Paighamber Isa ibne Maryum ki wiladat huwi thi, aur zameen ko Khana-e-Ka’ba ki taraf se paani par bichaya gaya tha. Niz agar koi is din roza rakkhe to usko 60 mahino ke rozo’n ka sawaab milega. Dusri riwayat se hai keh Imam-e-Reza (a.s) ne yeh bhi farmaya keh Al-Qaem (Imam Mahdi (a.t.f.s)) is din zuhoor farmayenge.>

Niz 70 saal ke gunah bakhsh diye jaayenge. Is shab mein ibaadat karna 100 saal ki ibaadat ke bara-bar hai.
