111 | LAHAB | 5

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Abu-lahab ke haath toot jaaye aur woh halaak ho jaaye.

2. Na uska maal hi uske kaam aaya aur na uska kamaaya huwa saamaan hi.

3. Woh an-qareeb bhadakti huwi aag mein daakhil hoga.

4. Aur uski biwi jo lakdi dhoond-ne waali hai.

5. Uski gardan mein batty huwi rassi bandhi huwi hai.

111 | LAHAB | 5

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Perdition overtake both hands of Abu Lahab, and he will perish.

2. His wealth and what he earns will not avail him.

3. He shall soon burn in fire that flames,

4. And his wife, the bearer of fuel,

5. Upon her neck a halter of strongly twisted rope.
