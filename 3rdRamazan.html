NAMAAZ...

1) 10 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 50 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) padhe.

SHAB KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Ibrahim (a.s) ke Khuda, ay Ishaaq (a.s) ke Khuda, ay Yaqoob (a.s) ke Khuda aur unke asbaat (nawaaso) ke Khuda, ay Malaayeka aur Roohul Quds ke Parwardigaar.

Ay sunne waale, ay jaanne waale, ay hikmat waale, ay karam karne waale, ay buzurg o bartar, mai ne Tere hi liye roza rakkha aur Teri hi di huwi rozi se iftaar kiya aur Tere hifz o amaan mein panah-guzi ho gaya hu aur Teri bargah mein mai ne rujoo kiya aur Teri hi taraf baaz-gasht (return) hai.

Tu hi maher-baan aur rahim hai mujh ko namaaz aur roze ki taaqat de aur baroz-e-qayaamat mujhe ruswa na karna be-shak Tu waada khilaafi nahi karta.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Aaj ke din mujhe hosh aur aagaahi ataa farma, mujhe har tarah ki na-samjhi aur be-raah-ravi (misbehave) se bacha ke rakh, aur mujh ko har us bhalaayi mein se hissa de jo aaj Teri ataawo se naazil ho, ay sab se zeyaada ataa karne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

NAMAZ…

1) Recite 10-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) 50 times.

NIGHT DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O God of Ibrahim and O God of Ishaq. And O God of Yaqub and the tribes. Lord of the angels and the Holy spirit. The Hearer, the All-knowing, the Wise, the Noble, the High and the Great. I kept fast for You and with Your sustenance I end my fast and I seek refuge with You and I entered Your court and You are the Kind and Merciful. Make me strong enough to fast and pray and do not expose me on Judgment Day. Indeed you do not break Your promise.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah keep me alive in this month, in a state of alertness and watchfulness; and keep me away from impudence and cynicism; let me share everything good that is there in this month, which is in plenty, O the Liberal and Most Beneficent.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
