AYAAT

Namaz-e-Ayaat consists of two rakats, and there are five Ruku in each rakat. In 1st rakat after Surah Fateha and the other Surah, and then perform the Ruku. Thereafter, he should stand and recite Surah Fateha and any Surah and then perform another Ruku. He should repeat this action five times, and when he stands after the fifth Ruku, he should perform two Sajdah, and then stand up to perform the 2nd rakat and repeat the same as he did in the 1st rakat. Then he should recite tashahhud and Salam..

Yeh Namaz-e-Ayaat 2 rakat hai, jiski har rakat mein 5 Ruku hai. Har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad koi bhi Surah padhe aur Ruku mein jaaye, iske baad phir se sidha khade ho aur Surah Fateha aur koi bhi Surah padhe aur phir se Ruku mein jaaye. Is tarah 5 martaba is ko dauraaye aur baad mein khade ho kar Sujood mein jaaye aur 2 sajdeh kare, iske baad 2nd rakat ke liye khade ho aur 1st rakat ki tarah 2nd rakat ko baja laaye, phir Tashahud aur Salaam padh kar namaz ko tamaam kare.
