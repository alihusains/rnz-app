SHAB-E-ARAFAH

1) Yeh dua padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.
Paak hai woh zaat jiska arsh aasmaan mein hai. Paak hai woh zaat jiska hukm zameen mein naafiz hai. Paak hai woh zaat jiska faisla qabro’n mein naafiz hai. Paak hai woh zaat jiska dariya (river) mein raasta hai. Paak hai woh zaat jo Jahannam par ikhtiyaar rakhta hai. Paak hai woh zaat Jannat mein jiski rehmat hai. Paak hai woh zaat qayamat mein jiska adl hai. Paak hai woh zaat jisne aasmaan ko buland kiya. Paak hai woh zaat jisne zameen bichhaayi. Paak hai woh zaat jisse panah-o-najaat nahi magar usi ke yahan se.
Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

2) Phir yeh dua padhe. Allahumma Man Ta’abba…

3) Phir yeh dua padhe. Allahumma Ya Shahida…

4) Ziyarat-e-Arafah: Imam Hussain (a.s) padhe.

SHAB-E-ARAFAH

1) Recite the following dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.
Glory be to He whose Arsh is in the heavens! Glory be to He whose Will operates in the earth! Glory be to He who examines (the dead) in the graves! Glory be to He who surfaced a path over the ocean! Glory be to He who has the total control over the Fire! Glory be to He who surrounds the Paradise with (His) mercy! Glory be to He who will do justice on the Day of Judgment! Glory be to He who raised the sky! Glory be to He who spread the earth! Glory be to He from whom there is no running away unless (one takes refuge) with Him!
Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

2) Then recite this Dua. Allahumma Man Ta’abba…

3) Then recite this Dua. Allahumma Ya Shahida…

4) Recite Ziyarat-e-Arafah: Imam Hussain (a.s).
