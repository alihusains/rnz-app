Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Mai roz-e-qayamat ki qasam khaata hu.

2. Aur buraaiyo par malaamat karne waale nafs ki qasam khaata hu.

3. Kya yeh insaan yeh khayaal karta hai keh Ham uski haddiyo ko jama na kar sakayge.

4. Yaqeenan Ham is baat par qaadir hai keh uski unglio ke pur (end of the fingers) tak durust kar sakay.

5. Bal-keh insaan yeh chahta hai keh apne saamne buraayi karta chala jaaye.

6. Woh yeh puchhta hai keh yeh qayamat kab aane waali hai.

7. To jab aankhe chaka-chond ho (phati rahe) jaayegi.

8. Aur chaand ko gehan (eclipse) lag jaayega.

9. Aur yeh chaand, suraj ekhatta kar diye jaayege.

10. Is din insaan kahega keh ab bhaag-ne ka raasta kidhar hai.

11. Hargiz nahi ab koi thikaana nahi hai.

12. Ab sab ka markaz tumhaare Parwardigaar ki taraf hai.

13. Is din insaan ko bataaya jaayega keh us ne pehle aur baad kya-kya aamaal kiye hai.

14. Bal-keh insaan khud bhi apne nafs ke haalaat se khoob ba-khabar hai.

15. Chahe woh kitne hi uzr (excuse) kyu’n na pesh kare.

16. Dekhe aap Qur’an ki tilaawat mein ujlat (hurry) ke saath zabaan ko harkat na de.

17. Yeh hamari zimme-daari hai keh Ham usey jama kare aur padhwaaye.

18. Phir jab ham padhwa de to aap uski tilaawat ko dohraaye.

19. Phir iske baad uski wazaahat karna bhi hamari hi zimme-daari hai.

20. Nahi bal-keh tum log duniya ko dost rakhte ho.

21. Aur aakherat ko nazar-andaaz kiye huwe ho.

22. Is din baaz chehre shadaab hoge.

23. Apne Parwardigaar ki nemato par nazar rakkhe huwe hoge.

24. Aur baaz chehre afsur-daah (maayus) hoge.

25. Jinhe yeh khayaal hoga keh kab kamar-tod museebat waarid ho jaaye.

26. Hoshiyaar! Jab jaan gardan tak pohonch jaayegi.

27. Aur kaha jaayega keh ab kaun jhaad-phoonk (magic) karne waala hai.

28. Aur marne waale ko khayaal hoga keh ab sab se judaayi hai.

29. Aur pindli-pindli se lipat jaayegi.

30. Aaj sab ko Parwardigaar ki taraf le jaaya jaayega.

31. Us ne na kalaam-e-Khuda ki tasdeeq (confirmation) ki aur na namaaz padhi.

32. Bal-keh takzeeb ki aur mooh pher liya.

33. Phir apne ahel ki taraf akhad-ta huwa gaya.

34. Afsos hai tere haal par bohot afsos hai.

35. Haif hai aur sad-haif (afsos) hai.

36. Kya insaan ka khayaal yeh hai keh usey isi tarah azaad chhod diya jaayega.

37. Kya woh us manee ka qatraa nahi tha jise rahem mein daala jaata hai.

38. Phir alqaah (leech) bana phir usey khalq kar ke baraabar kiya.

39. Phir us se aurat aur mard ka joda tayyar kiya.

40. Kya woh Khuda is baat par qaadir nahi hai keh murdo ko dobaara zinda kar sakey.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Nay! I swear by the day of resurrection.

2. Nay! I swear by the self-accusing soul.

3. Does man think that We shall not gather his bones?

4. Yea! We are able to make complete his very fingertips,

5. Nay! man desires to give the lie to what is before him.

6. He asks: When is the day of resurrection?

7. So when the sight becomes dazed,

8. And the moon becomes dark,

9. And the sun and the moon are brought together,

10. Man shall say on that day: Whither to fly to?

11. By no means! there shall be no place of refuge!

12. With your Lord alone shall on that day be the place of rest.

13. Man shall on that day be informed of what he sent before and (what he) put off.

14. Nay! man is evidence against himself,

15. Though he puts forth his excuses.

16. Do not move your tongue with it to make haste with it,

17. Surely on Us (devolves) the collecting of it and the reciting of it.

18. Therefore when We have recited it, follow its recitation.

19. Again on Us (devolves) the explaining of it.

20. Nay! But you love the present life,

21. And neglect the hereafter.

22. (Some) faces on that day shall be bright,

23. Looking to their Lord.

24. And (other) faces on that day shall be gloomy,

25. Knowing that there will be made to befall them some great calamity.

26. Nay! When it comes up to the throat,

27. And it is said: Who will be a magician?

28. And he is sure that it is the (hour of) parting

29. And affliction is combined with affliction;

30. To your Lord on that day shall be the driving.

31. So he did not accept the truth, nor did he pray,

32. But called the truth a lie and turned back,

33. Then he went to his followers, walking away in haughtiness.

34. Nearer to you (is destruction) and nearer,

35. Again (consider how) nearer to you and nearer.

36. Does man think that he is to be left to wander without an aim?

37. Was he not a small seed in the seminal elements,

38. Then he was a clot of blood, so He created (him) then made (him) perfect.

39. Then He made of him two kinds, the male and the female.

40. Is not He able to give life to the dead?
