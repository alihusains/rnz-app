Chp. 3:Ver. 9
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَآ اِنَّكَ جَامِعُ النَّاسِ لِيَوْمٍ لَّا رَيْبَ فِيْهِط اِنَّ اللهَ لَا يُخْلِفُ الْمِيْعَادَ۔
Our Lord! Surely Thou art the Gatherer of men on a day about which there is no doubt; surely Allah will not fail (His) promise.

Khudaaya! To tamaam insaano ko us din jama karne waala hai jis mein koi shak nahi hai. Aur Allah ka waada galat nahi hota.
