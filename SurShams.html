91 | SHAMS | 15

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Aftaab aur uski roshni ki qasam.

2. Aur chaand ki qisam jab woh uske peechhe chale.

3. Aur din ki qasam jab woh roshni bakhshe.

4. Aur raat ki qasam jab woh usey dhaank le.

5. Aur aasmaan ki qasam aur jisne usey banaya hai.

6. Aur zameen ki qasam aur jisne usey bichhaaya hai.

7. Aur nafs ki qisam aur jisne usey durust kiya hai.

8. Phir badi (buraayi) aur taqwa (achchaayi) ki hidaayat di hai.

9. Be-shak woh kaam-yaab ho gaya jisne nafs ko pakeeza bana liya.

10. Aur woh na-muraad ho gaya jisne usey aaluda (ganda/dirty) kar diya hai.

11. Samood ne apni sar-kashi ki bina par Rasool ki takzeeb ki.

12. Jab unka bad-bakht uth-khada huwa.

13. To Khuda ke Rasool ne kaha keh Khuda ki oontni aur uski seyraabi ka khayaal rakhna.

14. To un logo ne uski takzeeb ki aur uski konche (rage) kaat daali to Khuda ne unke gunah ke sabab un par azaab naazil kar diya aur unhe bilkul baraabar kar diya.

15. Aur usey iske anjaam ka koi khauf nahi hai.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. I swear by the sun and its brilliance,

2. And the moon when it follows the sun,

3. And the day when it shows it,

4. And the night when it draws a veil over it,

5. And the heaven and Him Who made it,

6. And the earth and Him Who extended it,

7. And the soul and Him Who made it perfect,

8. Then He inspired it to understand what is right and wrong for it;

9. He will indeed be successful who purifies it,

10. And he will indeed fail who corrupts it.

11. Samood gave the lie (to the truth) in their inordinacy,

12. When the most unfortunate of them broke forth with mischief.

13 So Allah’s apostle said to them (Leave alone) Allah’s she-camel, and (give) her (to) drink.

14. But they called him a liar and slaughtered her, therefore their Lord crushed them for their sin and levelled them (with the ground).

15. And He fears not its consequence.
