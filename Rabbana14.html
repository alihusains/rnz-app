Chp. 3:Ver. 192
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَآ اِنَّكَ مَنْ تُدْخِلِ النَّارَ فَقَدْ اَخْزَيْتَهٗط وَمَا لِلظّٰلِمِيْنَ مِنْ اَنْصَارٍ۔
Our Lord! surely whomsoever Thou makest enter the fire, him Thou hast indeed brought to disgrace, and there shall be no helpers for the unjust.

Parwardigaar! Tu jise Jahannam mein daal dega goya usey zaleel-o-ruswa kar diya aur zaalemeen ka koi madad-gaar nahi hai.
