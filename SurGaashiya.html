Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Kya tumhe dhaanp lene waali qayamat ki baat maaloom hai?

2. Us din bohot se chehre zaleel aur ruswa hoge.

3. Mehnat karne waale thake huwe.

4. Dahekti huwi aag mein daakhil hoge.

5. Unhe kholte huwe paani ke chashme se seyraab kiya jaayega.

6. Unke liye koi khaana siwaaye khaar-daar jaadhi ke na hoga.

7. Jo na motaayi paiyda kar sakey aur na bhook ke kaam aa sakey.

8. Aur kuch chehre tar-o-taaza hoge.

9. Apni mehnat-o-riyaazat se khush.

10. Buland-tareen jannat mein.

11. Jahan koi lagw aawaaz na soonaayi de.

12. Wahan chashme jaari hoge.

13. Wahan unche-unche takht hoge.

14. Aur atraaf mein rakkhe huwe pyaale hoge.

15. Aur qataar se lagey huwe gaao-takiye hoge.

16. Aur bichhi huwi behtareen masnade (gaddiya/mattresses) hogi.

17. Kya yeh log oont ki taraf nahi dekhte hai keh usey kis tarah paiyda kiya gaya hai.

18. Aur aasmaan ko kis tarah buland kiya gaya hai.

19. Aur pahaad ko kis tarah nasb kiya gaya hai.

20. Aur zameen ko kis tarah bichhaaya gaya hai.

21. Lehaaza tum nasihat karte raho keh tum sirf nasihat karne waale ho.

22. Tum un par musallat aur unke zimme-daar nahi ho.

23. Magar jo mooh pher le aur kaafir ho jaaye.

24. To Khuda usey bohot bade azaab mein mubtala karega.

25. Phir hamari hi taraf un sab ki baaz-gasht hai.

26. Aur hamare hi zimme un sab ka hisaab hai.

88 | GAASHIYA | 26

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Has not there come to you the news of the overwhelming calamity?

2. (Some) faces on that day shall be downcast,

3. Laboring, toiling,

4. Entering into burning fire,

5. Made to drink from a boiling spring.

6. They shall have no food but of thorns,

7. Which will neither fatten nor avail against hunger.

8. (Other) faces on that day shall be happy,

9. Well-pleased because of their striving,

10. In a lofty garden,

11. Wherein you shall not hear vain talk.

12. Therein is a fountain flowing,

13. Therein are thrones raised high,

14. And drinking-cups ready placed,

15. And cushions set in a row,

16. And carpets spread out.

17. Will they not then consider the camels, how they are created?

18. And the heaven, how it is reared aloft,

19. And the mountains, how they are firmly fixed,

20. And the earth, how it is made a vast expanse?

21. Therefore do remind, for you are only a reminder.

22. You are not a watcher over them;

23. But whoever turns back and disbelieves,

24. Allah will chastise him with the greatest chastisement.

25. Surely to Us is their turning back,

26. Then surely upon Us is the taking of their account.
