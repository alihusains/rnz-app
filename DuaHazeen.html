Is dua ko Namaz-e-Watr ke baad padhe…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اُنَاجِیْكَ یَامَوْجُوْدُ فِیْ كُلِّ مَكَانٍ لَّعَلَّكَ تَسْمَعُ نِدَآئِیْ فَقَدْ عَظُمَ جُرْمِیْ وَقَلَّ حَیَآئِیْ مَوْلٰایَ یَامَوْلٰایَ اَیَّ الْاَھْوَالِ اَتَذَكَّرُ وَاَیَّھَا اَنْسٰی وَلَوْ لَمْ یَكُنْ اِلَّا الْمَوْتَ لَكَفٰی كَیْفَ وَمَا بَعْدَ الْمَوْتِ اَعْظَمُ وَاَدْھٰی مَوْلٰایَ یَامَوْلٰایَ حَتّٰی مَتٰی اَقُوْلُ لَكَ الْعُتْبٰی مَرَّۃًۢ بَعْدَ اُخْرٰی ثُمَّ لَا تَجِدُ عِنْدِیْ صِدْقًا وَّلَا وَفَآءً فَیَاغَوْثَاہُ ثُمَّ وَاغَوْثَاہُ بِكَ یَااَللہُ مِنْ ھَوَیٍ قَدْ غَلَبَنِیْ وَمِنْ عَدُوٍّ قَدِ اسْتَكْلَبَ عَلَیَّ وَمِنْ دُنْیَا قَدْ تَزَیَّنَتْ لِیْ وَمِنْ نَفْسِ اَمَّارَۃٍۢ بِالسُّوٓءِ اِلَّا مَا رَحِمَ رَبِّیْ۔ اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

مَوْلٰایَ یَامَوْلٰایَ اِنْ كُنْتَ رَحِمْتَ مِثْلِیْ فَارْحَمْنِیْ وَاِنْ كُنْتَ قَبِلْتَ مِثْلِیْ فَاقْبَلْنِیْ یَاقَبِلَ السَّحَرَۃِ اِقْبَلْنِیْ یَامَنْ لَّمْ اَزَلْ اَتَعَرَّفُ مِنْہُ الْحُسْنٰی یَامَنْ یُغَذِّیْنِیْ بِالنِّعَمِ صَبَاحًا وَّمَسَآءً اِرْحَمْنِیْ یَوْمَ اٰتِیْكَ فَرْدًا شَاخِصًا اِلَیْكَ بَصَرِیْ مُقَلَّدًا عَمَلِیْ قَدْ تَبَرَّءَ جَمِیْعُ الْخَلْقِ مِنِّیْ نَعَمْ وَاَبِیْ وَاُمِّیْ وَمَنْ كَانَ لَہٗ كَدِّیْ وَسَعْیِیْ فَاِنْ لَمْ تَرْحَمْنِیْ فَمَنْ یَرْحَمُنِیْ وَمَنْ یُّوْنِسُ فِیْ الْقَبْرِ وَحْشَتِیْ وَمَنْ یُنْطِقُ لِسَانِیْ اِذَا خَلَوْتُ بِعَمَلِیْ وَسَآئَلْتَنِیْ عَمَّا اَنْتَ اَعْلَمُ بِہٖ مِنِّیْ فَاِنْ قُلْتُ نَعَمْ فَاَیْنَ الْمَھْرَبُ مِنْ عَدْلِكَ وَاِنْ قُلْتُ لَمْ اَفْعَلْ قُلْتَ اَلَمْ اَكُنِ الشَّاھِدَ عَلَیْكَ فَعَفْوُكَ عَفْوُكَ یَامَوْلٰایَ قَبْلَ سَرَابِیْلِ الْقَطِرَانِ عَفْوُكَ عَفْوُكَ یَامَوْلٰایَ قَبْلَ جَھَنَّمَ وَالنِّیْرَانِ عَفْوُكَ عَفْوُكَ یَامَوْلٰایَ قَبْلَ اَنْ تُغَلَّ الْاَیْدِیْ اِلَی الْاَعْنَاقِ یَااَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِیْنَ وَخَیْرَ الْغَافِرِیْنَ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

HAZEEN

Is dua ko Namaz-e-Watr ke baad padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay har makaan ke maujood! Tujse munaajaat karta hu, (aur) ummeed-waar hu keh Tu meri aawaaz soon lega, kyu keh yakeenan mera gunah bahot badha hai aur meri (haya) sharm kam ho gayi hai.

Mere Maula, Ay mere Maula! Kin-kin daro ko yaad rakkhu, aur kin ko bhul jaau, haala keh agar maut ke siwa koi dar na hota to yehi ek khauf kaafi tha, aur maut ke baad ka kauf to bahot badha aur bahot hi shadid hai.

Mere Maalik, Ay mere Maalik! Kab se kab tak kahe jaau keh Teri reza chahata hu, aur baar-baar yahi kahu, phir uske baad bhi Tu mere paas sachchaayi aur iqraare wafa na paaye! Pas panah hai aur panah hai Tujh se ay Ma’bood us khwahishe nafs se jo mujh par hamla-aawar huwa, aur duniya se jo mere liye aarasta huwi, aur nafs se jo sarkashi karne waala hai badi (buraayi) ke saath, Lekin yeh keh mere Rab ne mujh par rahem kiya.

Mere Maula, Ay mere Maula! Agar Tu ne mujh jaise kisi par rahem kiya, to mujh par bhi rahem kar, agar mujh jaise ki tauba qubool kar li hai, to mujh se bhi qubool kar le, Ay sabki dua qubool karne waale meri bhi dua qubool kar le.

Ay woh zaat! Jis se mai hamesha neki hi ko jaanta hu, Ay woh zaat! Jo subh o shaam mujhe gizaam-e-ne’mate deta hai, mujh par us din rahem karna jab mai Teri taraf nazr jamaaye Tere paas tanha aaunga galey mein apne aamaal daale huwe (aaunga) aur us waqt keh jab tamaam makhlooq mujh se bezaar hogi, ha yahan tak keh mera baap, aur meri maa aur (woh log) keh jinke liye duniya mein meri koshish aur mahenat thi (woh bhi mujh se bezaar hoge).

Pas (us din) agar Tu mujh par rahem na karega to kaun mujh par rahem karega? Aur qabr mein kaun mera munees-e-tanhaayi (hamdard) hoga? Aur kaun meri zabaan ko goya karega (bolne ki quwwat ataa karega) jab keh mai apne amal ke saath hunga aur Tu mujh se us umoor ki baaz-purs karega keh jis ko Tu mujh se ziyaada jaanta hai.

Pas agar mai (apne amal ka) ikraar karunga to adl se mafar kaha hai? Aur agar mai inkaar karunga, to Tu kahega keh kya mai tere amal dekhta na tha?

Pas bakhsh de, Ay mere Parwardigaar, Ay mere Maalik! Libaas-e-Jahannum pehnaane se pehle, bakhsh de. Ay mere maula qabl iske keh mere haath gardano ki taraf baande jaaye, Ay bade rahem karne waale, aur behtareen bakhshne waale.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

HAZEEN

One should recite this dua after Namaz-e-Watr…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah bless Muhammad and his family I whisper unto You O One Who is present in every place so that You may hear my call for surely my sin is excessive and my shame is less. My Master, O my Master which of the terrifying states shall I remember and which of them shall I forget for if there was nothing except death it would be enough then what about after death greater and much worse?

My master O my Master up to when and till when will I say, I am to blame, again and again, but then You do not find any truth or loyalty in me? I call for help and I call for help, O Allah from desires which have overpowered me and from the enemy which has pounced on me and from the world which attracts me and from the soul that leads towards evil except that on which my Lord has mercy (12:53).

My master O my master if You have had mercy on the likes of me then have mercy on me and if You have accepted from the likes of me then accept from me O One Who accepts the early morning prayer accept me, O One who, I still know only good from Him.

O One who nourishes me with blessings morning and evening have mercy on me when I come to You alone, my glance fixed on You my actions carried on my neck When all of creation will withdraw away from me yes, even my father and mother and those for whom I worked and struggled then if You will not have mercy on me who will have mercy on me who will give me solace from the loneliness of the grave and who will make me speak when I am alone with my deeds and when You will ask me about what You know better than me?

Then if I say yes (to my sins) where will be the escape from Your Justice? And if I say I did not commit it You will say was I not a witness over you? So (I beseech) Your Forgiveness Your Pardon O my master before the wearing of the clothes of Hell Your Forgiveness Your Pardon O my Master before the Hell and the Fire Your Forgiveness Your Forgiveness O my Master before the hands are tied to the necks O the most Merciful and the best of Forgivers.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
