Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Jab zameen zoro ke saath zal-zale mein aa jaayegi.

2. Aur woh saare khazaane nikaal daalegi.

3. Aur insaan kahega keh usey kya ho gaya hai.

4. Us din woh apni khabre bayaan karegi.

5. Keh tumhaare Parwardigaar ne usey ishaara kiya hai.

6. Us roz saare insaan giroh-dar-giroh qabro se niklege ta-keh apne aamaal ko dekh sakay.

7. Phir jis shakhs ne zarra baraabar neki ki hai woh usey dekhega.

8. Aur jisne zarra baraabar buraayi ki hai woh usey dekhega.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. When the earth is shaken with her (violent) shaking,

2. And the earth brings forth her burdens,

3. And man says: What has befallen her?

4. On that day she shall tell her news,

5. Because your Lord had inspired her.

6. On that day men shall come forth in sundry bodies that they may be shown their works.

7. So he who has done an atom’s weight of good shall see it.

8. And he who has done an atom’s weight of evil shall see it.
