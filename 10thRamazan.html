10 | MAHE RAMAZAN

1) 20 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat karke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp-1) ke baad 30 martaba Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) padhe.

SHAB KI DUA...

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Khuda-wanda! Ay salaamati dene waale aur amn dene waale, ay hifaazat karne waale, ay zabardast, ay badaayi waale, ay yagaana, ay be-neyaaz, ay ek (yekta), ay tanha.

Ay bakhsh ne waale, ay rahem karne waale, ay mohabbat karne waale aur burd-baar karne waale, is mahe mubarak mein se ek-tihaayi (1/3) hissa rukhsat ho (chala) gaya magar mai yeh nahi jaanta keh ay mere Aaqa aakhir meri talab-e-haajat ke baare mein Tu ne kya faisala kiya hai, aaya mujhe bakhsh diya?

To agar mere gunah bakhsh diye, to mai khush-nasib hu, lekin agar Tu ne unko nahi bakhsha to haai meri bad-bakhti. Khair is waqt ay mere Aaqa bakhsh de mere gunah ko aur rahem kar mujh par aur qubool kar meri tauba ko aur meri madad tark (reject) na kar.

Ay mujhe meri lagzisho se bacha le aur apni parda-poshi se mere aiybo (faults) ko chupa le aur apni sifat-e-afvo taqseer se mere gunah ko maaf kar aur apni qudrat se meri khataawo ko dar-guzar kar aur hukm deta hai (yaqeenan Tu hi faisala karta hai) aur koi Tujh par kisi qism ka koi hukm nahi laga sakta aur Tu har cheez par qudrat rakhta hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

DIN KI DUA…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Aaj ke din mujhe un logo mein rakh jo Tujh par bharosa karte hai, us mein mujhe un mein qaraar de jo Tere huzoor kaamiyaab hai aur us mein Apne ehsaan wo karam se mujhe Apne nazdeeki logo mein qaraar de. Ay talab-gaaro ke manzil-e-maqsood.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

10 | MAHE RAMAZAN

NAMAZ…

1) Recite 20-rakat namaz (in form of 2-2-rakats). In each rakat after Surah Fateha (Chp-1) recite Surah Ikhlaas (Chp-112) 30 times.

NIGHT DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O the one who gives security; O the one who gives peace. O the one who protects, O the one who is powerful, O the one who is proud. O the One, O the needless, O single, O Unique, O forgiving, O merciful, O the one who loves, O the forbearing, one third part of this blessed month has passed away but I don’t know what decision You have taken about the fulfillment of my needs. Have You forgiven me? If You have forgiven my sins, I am blessed and if You have not forgiven my sins, O woe be to my wretchedness. So at this time, O my Chief, forgive my sins and have mercy on me and accept my taubah and do not refrain from helping me and save me from my mistakes and hide my defects by your covering and through Your quality of forgiving the mistakes remit my sins and overlook my faults through Your power. Indeed You the one who issues the judgment and You are not bound by the judgment of anyone else and You are having power over everything.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

DAY DUA...

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, in this month, let me be among those who rely on Thee, let me be among those who succeed in Thy way, let me be among those who are allowed nearness to Thee, as a favor, O the Last Resource of those who are needy.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
