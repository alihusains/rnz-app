بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

طٰسۗمّۗﯜ1ﯛ تِلْكَ اٰيٰتُ الْكِتٰبِ الْمُبِيْنِﯜ2ﯛ نَتْلُوْ عَلَيْكَ مِنْ نَّبَاِ مُوْسٰى وَفِرْعَوْنَ بِالْحَقِّ لِقَوْمٍ يُّؤْمِنُوْنَﯜ3ﯛ اِنَّ فِرْعَوْنَ عَلَا فِي الْاَرْضِ وَجَعَلَ اَهْلَهَا شِيَعًا يَّسْتَضْعِفُ طَاۗئِفَةً مِّنْهُمْ يُذَبِّحُ اَبْنَاۗءَهُمْ وَيَسْتَحْيٖ نِسَاۗءَهُمْط اِنَّهٗ كَانَ مِنَ الْمُفْسِدِيْنَﯜ4ﯛ وَنُرِيْدُ اَنْ نَّمُنَّ عَلَى الَّذِيْنَ اسْتُضْعِفُوْا فِي الْاَرْضِ وَنَجْعَلَهُمْ اَئِمَّةً وَّنَجْعَلَهُمُ الْوٰرِثِيْنَﯜ5ﯛلا وَنُمَكِّنَ لَهُمْ فِي الْاَرْضِ وَنُرِيَ فِرْعَوْنَ وَهَامٰنَ وَجُنُوْدَهُمَا مِنْهُمْ مَّا كَانُوْا يَحْذَرُوْنَﯜ6ﯛ وَاَوْحَيْنَآ اِلٰٓى اُمِّ مُوْسٰٓى اَنْ اَرْضِعِيْهِج فَاِذَا خِفْتِ عَلَيْهِ فَاَلْقِيْهِ فِي الْيَمِّ وَلَا تَخَافِيْ وَلَا تَحْزَنِيْج اِنَّا رَاۗدُّوْهُ اِلَيْكِ وَجَاعِلُوْهُ مِنَ الْمُرْسَلِيْنَﯜ7ﯛ فَالْتَقَطَهٗٓ اٰلُ فِرْعَوْنَ لِيَكُوْنَ لَهُمْ عَدُوًّا وَّحَزَنًاط اِنَّ فِرْعَوْنَ وَهَامٰنَ وَجُنُوْدَهُمَا كَانُوْا خٰطِئِيْنَﯜ8ﯛ وَقَالَتِ امْرَاَتُ فِرْعَوْنَ قُرَّتُ عَيْنٍ لِّيْ وَلَكَط لَا تَقْتُلُوْهُ عَسٰٓى اَنْ يَّنْفَعَنَآ اَوْ نَتَّخِذَهٗ وَلَدًا وَّهُمْ لَا يَشْعُرُوْنَﯜ9ﯛ وَاَصْبَحَ فُؤَادُ اُمِّ مُوْسٰى فٰرِغًاط اِنْ كَادَتْ لَتُبْدِيْ بِهٖ لَوْلَٓا اَنْ رَّبَطْنَا عَلٰى قَلْبِهَا لِتَكُوْنَ مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنِيْنَﯜ10ﯛ وَقَالَتْ لِاُخْتِهٖ قُصِّيْهِز فَبَصُرَتْ بِهٖ عَنْ جُنُبٍ وَّهُمْ لَا يَشْعُرُوْنَﯜ11ﯛ وَحَرَّمْنَا عَلَيْهِ الْمَرَاضِعَ مِنْ قَبْلُ فَقَالَتْ هَلْ اَدُلُّكُمْ عَلٰٓى اَهْلِ بَيْتٍ يَّكْفُلُوْنَهٗ لَكُمْ وَهُمْ لَهٗ نٰصِحُوْنَﯜ12ﯛ فَرَدَدْنٰهُ اِلٰٓى اُمِّهٖ كَيْ تَقَرَّ عَيْنُهَا وَلَا تَحْزَنَ وَلِتَعْلَمَ اَنَّ وَعْدَ اللهِ حَقٌّ وَّلٰكِنَّ اَكْثَرَهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُوْنَﯜ13ﯛع وَلَمَّا بَلَغَ اَشُدَّهٗ وَاسْتَوٰٓى اٰتَيْنٰهُ حُكْمًا وَّعِلْمًاط وَكَذٰلِكَ نَجْزِي الْمُحْسِنِيْنَﯜ14ﯛ وَدَخَلَ الْمَدِيْنَةَ عَلٰى حِيْنِ غَفْلَةٍ مِّنْ اَهْلِهَا فَوَجَدَ فِيْهَا رَجُلَيْنِ يَقْتَتِلٰنِ ڭ هٰذَا مِنْ شِيْعَتِهٖ وَهٰذَا مِنْ عَدُوِّهٖج فَاسْتَغَاثَهُ الَّذِيْ مِنْ شِيْعَتِهٖ عَلَى الَّذِيْ مِنْ عَدُوِّهٖلا فَوَكَزَهٗ مُوْسٰى فَقَضٰى عَلَيْهِ ڭ قَالَ هٰذَا مِنْ عَمَلِ الشَّيْطٰنِط اِنَّهٗ عَدُوٌّ مُّضِلٌّ مُّبِيْنٌﯜ15ﯛ قَالَ رَبِّ اِنِّيْ ظَلَمْتُ نَفْسِيْ فَاغْفِرْ لِيْ فَغَفَرَ لَهٗ ط اِنَّهٗ هُوَ الْغَفُوْرُ الرَّحِيْمُﯜ16ﯛ قَالَ رَبِّ بِمَآ اَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيَّ فَلَنْ اَكُوْنَ ظَهِيْرًا لِّلْمُجْرِمِيْنَﯜ17ﯛ فَاَصْبَحَ فِي الْمَدِيْنَةِ خَاۗئِفًا يَّتَرَقَّبُ فَاِذَا الَّذِي اسْتَنْصَرَهٗ بِالْاَمْسِ يَسْتَصْرِخُهٗط قَالَ لَهٗ مُوْسٰٓى اِنَّكَ لَغَوِيٌّ مُّبِيْنٌﯜ18ﯛ فَلَمَّآ اَنْ اَرَادَ اَنْ يَّبْطِشَ بِالَّذِيْ هُوَ عَدُوٌّ لَّهُمَالا قَالَ يٰمُوْسٰٓى اَتُرِيْدُ اَنْ تَقْتُلَنِيْ كَمَا قَتَلْتَ نَفْسًۢا بِالْاَمْسِ ڰ اِنْ تُرِيْدُ اِلَّآ اَنْ تَكُوْنَ جَبَّارًا فِي الْاَرْضِ وَمَا تُرِيْدُ اَنْ تَكُوْنَ مِنَ الْمُصْلِحِيْنَﯜ19ﯛ وَجَاۗءَ رَجُلٌ مِّنْ اَقْصَا الْمَدِيْنَةِ يَسْعٰىز قَالَ يٰمُوْسٰٓى اِنَّ الْمَلَاَ يَاْتَمِرُوْنَ بِكَ لِيَقْتُلُوْكَ فَاخْرُجْ اِنِّيْ لَكَ مِنَ النّٰصِحِيْنَﯜ20ﯛ فَخَرَجَ مِنْهَا خَاۗئِفًا يَّتَرَقَّبُز قَالَ رَبِّ نَجِّنِيْ مِنَ الْقَوْمِ الظّٰلِمِيْنَﯜ21ﯛ وَلَمَّا تَوَجَّهَ تِلْقَاۗءَ مَدْيَنَ قَالَ عَسٰى رَبِّيْٓ اَنْ يَّهْدِيَنِيْ سَوَاۗءَ السَّبِيْلِﯜ22ﯛ وَلَمَّا وَرَدَ مَاۗءَ مَدْيَنَ وَجَدَ عَلَيْهِ اُمَّةً مِّنَ النَّاسِ يَسْقُوْنَ ۋ وَوَجَدَ مِنْ دُوْنِهِمُ امْرَاَتَيْنِ تَذُوْدٰنِج قَالَ مَا خَطْبُكُمَاط قَالَتَا لَا نَسْقِيْ حَتّٰى يُصْدِرَ الرِّعَاۗءُسكتۃ وَاَبُوْنَا شَيْخٌ كَبِيْرٌﯜ23ﯛ فَسَقٰى لَهُمَا ثُمَّ تَوَلّٰىٓ اِلَى الظِّلِّ فَقَالَ رَبِّ اِنِّيْ لِمَآ اَنْزَلْتَ اِلَيَّ مِنْ خَيْرٍ فَقِيْرٌﯜ24ﯛ فَجَاۗءَتْهُ اِحْدٰهُمَا تَمْشِيْ عَلَى اسْتِحْيَاءٍز قَالَتْ اِنَّ اَبِيْ يَدْعُوْكَ لِيَجْزِيَكَ اَجْرَ مَا سَقَيْتَ لَنَاط فَلَمَّا جَاۗءَهٗ وَقَصَّ عَلَيْهِ الْقَصَصَلا قَالَ لَا تَخَفْوقفۃ نَجَوْتَ مِنَ الْقَوْمِ الظّٰلِمِيْنَﯜ25ﯛ قَالَتْ اِحْدَاهُمَا يٰٓاَبَتِ اسْتَاْجِرْهُز اِنَّ خَيْرَ مَنِ اسْتَاْجَرْتَ الْقَوِيُّ الْاَمِيْنُﯜ26ﯛ قَالَ اِنِّيْٓ اُرِيْدُ اَنْ اُنْكِحَكَ اِحْدَى ابْنَتَيَّ هٰتَيْنِ عَلٰٓى اَنْ تَاْجُرَنِيْ ثَمٰنِيَ حِجَجٍج فَاِنْ اَتْمَمْتَ عَشْرًا فَمِنْ عِنْدِكَج وَمَآ اُرِيْدُ اَنْ اَشُقَّ عَلَيْكَط سَتَجِدُنِيْٓ اِنْ شَاۗءَ اللهُ مِنَ الصّٰلِحِيْنَﯜ27ﯛ قَالَ ذٰلِكَ بَيْنِيْ وَبَيْنَكَط اَيَّمَا الْاَجَلَيْنِ قَضَيْتُ فَلَا عُدْوَانَ عَلَيَّط وَاللهُ عَلٰى مَا نَقُوْلُ وَكِيْلٌﯜ28ﯛع فَلَمَّا قَضٰى مُوْسَى الْاَجَلَ وَسَارَ بِاَهْلِهٖٓ اٰنَسَ مِنْ جَانِبِ الطُّوْرِ نَارًاج قَالَ لِاَهْلِهِ امْكُثُوْٓا اِنِّيْٓ اٰنَسْتُ نَارًا لَّعَلِّيْٓ اٰتِيْكُمْ مِّنْهَا بِخَبَرٍ اَوْ جَذْوَةٍ مِّنَ النَّارِ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَصْطَلُوْنَﯜ29ﯛ فَلَمَّآ اَتٰهَا نُوْدِيَ مِنْ شَاطِئِ الْوَادِ الْاَيْمَنِ فِي الْبُقْعَةِ الْمُبٰرَكَةِ مِنَ الشَّجَرَةِ اَنْ يّٰمُوْسٰٓى اِنِّيْٓ اَنَا اللهُ رَبُّ الْعٰلَمِيْنَﯜ30ﯛلا وَاَنْ اَلْقِ عَصَاكَط فَلَمَّا رَاٰهَا تَهْتَزُّ كَاَنَّهَا جَاۗنٌّ وَّلّٰی مُدْبِرًا وَّلَمْ يُعَقِّبْ ط يٰمُوْسٰٓى اَقْبِلْ وَلَا تَخَفْقف اِنَّكَ مِنَ الْاٰمِنِيْنَﯜ31ﯛ اُسْلُكْ يَدَكَ فِيْ جَيْبِكَ تَخْرُجْ بَيْضَاۗءَ مِنْ غَيْرِ سُوْۗءٍز وَّاضْمُمْ اِلَيْكَ جَنَاحَكَ مِنَ الرَّهْبِ فَذٰنِكَ بُرْهَانٰنِ مِنْ رَّبِّكَ اِلٰى فِرْعَوْنَ وَمَلَائِهٖط اِنَّهُمْ كَانُوْا قَوْمًا فٰسِقِيْنَﯜ32ﯛ قَالَ رَبِّ اِنِّيْ قَتَلْتُ مِنْهُمْ نَفْسًا فَاَخَافُ اَنْ يَّقْتُلُوْنِﯜ33ﯛ وَاَخِيْ هٰرُوْنُ هُوَ اَفْصَحُ مِنِّيْ لِسَانًا فَاَرْسِلْهُ مَعِيَ رِدْاً يُّصَدِّقُنِيْٓز اِنِّيْٓ اَخَافُ اَنْ يُّكَذِّبُوْنِﯜ34ﯛ قَالَ سَنَشُدُّ عَضُدَكَ بِاَخِيْكَ وَنَجْعَلُ لَكُمَا سُلْطٰنًا فَلَا يَصِلُوْنَ اِلَيْكُمَا ڔبِاٰيٰتِنَا ڔاَنْتُمَا وَمَنِ اتَّبَعَكُمَا الْغٰلِبُوْنَﯜ35ﯛ فَلَمَّا جَاۗءَهُمْ مُّوْسٰى بِاٰيٰتِنَا بَيِّنٰتٍ قَالُوْا مَا هٰذَٓا اِلَّا سِحْرٌ مُّفْتَرًى وَّمَا سَمِعْنَا بِهٰذَا فِيْٓ اٰبَاۗئِنَا الْاَوَّلِيْنَﯜ36ﯛ وَقَالَ مُوْسٰى رَبِّيْٓ اَعْلَمُ بِمَنْ جَاۗءَ بِالْهُدٰى مِنْ عِنْدِهٖ وَمَنْ تَكُوْنُ لَهٗ عَاقِبَةُ الدَّارِط اِنَّهٗ لَا يُفْلِحُ الظّٰلِمُوْنَﯜ37ﯛ وَقَالَ فِرْعَوْنُ يٰٓاَيُّهَا الْمَلَاُمَا عَلِمْتُ لَكُمْ مِّنْ اِلٰهٍ غَيْرِيْج فَاَوْقِدْ لِيْ يٰهَامٰنُ عَلَى الطِّيْنِ فَاجْعَلْ لِيْ صَرْحًا لَّعَلِّيْٓ اَطَّلِعُ اِلٰٓى اِلٰهِ مُوْسٰىلا وَاِنِّيْ لَاَظُنُّهٗ مِنَ الْكٰذِبِيْنَﯜ38ﯛ وَاسْتَكْبَرَ هُوَ وَجُنُوْدُهٗ فِي الْاَرْضِ بِغَيْرِ الْحَقِّ وَظَنُّوْٓا اَنَّهُمْ اِلَيْنَا لَا يُرْجَعُوْنَﯜ39ﯛ فَاَخَذْنٰهُ وَجُنُوْدَهٗ فَنَبَذْنٰهُمْ فِي الْيَمِّج فَانْظُرْ كَيْفَ كَانَ عَاقِبَةُ الظّٰلِمِيْنَﯜ40ﯛ وَجَعَلْنٰهُمْ اَئِمَّةً يَّدْعُوْنَ اِلَى النَّارِج وَيَوْمَ الْقِيٰمَةِ لَا يُنْصَرُوْنَﯜ41ﯛ وَاَتْبَعْنٰهُمْ فِيْ هٰذِهِ الدُّنْيَا لَعْنَةًج وَيَوْمَ الْقِيٰمَةِ هُمْ مِّنَ الْمَقْبُوْحِيْنَﯜ42ﯛع وَلَقَدْ اٰتَيْنَا مُوْسَى الْكِتٰبَ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ مَآ اَهْلَكْنَا الْقُرُوْنَ الْاُوْلٰى بَصَاۗئِرَ لِلنَّاسِ وَهُدًى وَّرَحْمَةً لَّعَلَّهُمْ يَتَذَكَّرُوْنَﯜ43ﯛ وَمَا كُنْتَ بِجَانِبِ الْغَرْبِيِّ اِذْ قَضَيْنَآ اِلٰى مُوْسَى الْاَمْرَ وَمَا كُنْتَ مِنَ الشّٰهِدِيْنَﯜ44ﯛلا وَلٰكِنَّآ اَنْشَاْنَا قُرُوْنًا فَتَطَاوَلَ عَلَيْهِمُ الْعُمُرُج وَمَا كُنْتَ ثَاوِيًا فِيْٓ اَهْلِ مَدْيَنَ تَتْلُوْا عَلَيْهِمْ اٰيٰتِنَالا وَلٰكِنَّا كُنَّا مُرْسِلِيْنَﯜ45ﯛ وَمَا كُنْتَ بِجَانِبِ الطُّوْرِ اِذْ نَادَيْنَا وَلٰكِنْ رَّحْمَةً مِّنْ رَّبِّكَ لِتُنْذِرَ قَوْمًا مَّآ اَتٰهُمْ مِّنْ نَذِيْرٍ مِّنْ قَبْلِكَ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَتَذَكَّرُوْنَﯜ46ﯛ وَلَوْلَٓا اَنْ تُصِيْبَهُمْ مُّصِيْبَةٌۢ بِمَا قَدَّمَتْ اَيْدِيْهِمْ فَيَقُوْلُوْا رَبَّنَا لَوْلَٓا اَرْسَلْتَ اِلَيْنَا رَسُوْلًا فَنَتَّبِعَ اٰيٰتِكَ وَنَكُوْنَ مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنِيْنَﯜ47ﯛ فَلَمَّا جَاۗءَهُمُ الْحَقُّ مِنْ عِنْدِنَا قَالُوْا لَوْلَٓا اُوْتِيَ مِثْلَ مَآ اُوْتِيَ مُوْسٰىط اَوَلَمْ يَكْفُرُوْا بِمَآ اُوْتِيَ مُوْسٰى مِنْ قَبْلُج قَالُوْا سِحْرٰنِ تَظَاهَرَاوقفۃ وَقَالُوْٓا اِنَّا بِكُلٍّ كٰفِرُوْنَﯜ48ﯛ قُلْ فَاْتُوْا بِكِتٰبٍ مِّنْ عِنْدِ اللهِ هُوَ اَهْدٰى مِنْهُمَآ اَتَّبِعْهُ اِنْ كُنْتُمْ صٰدِقِيْنَﯜ49ﯛ فَاِنْ لَّمْ يَسْتَجِيْبُوْا لَكَ فَاعْلَمْ اَنَّمَا يَتَّبِعُوْنَ اَهْوَاۗءَهُمْط وَمَنْ اَضَلُّ مِمَّنِ اتَّبَعَ هَوٰهُ بِغَيْرِ هُدًى مِّنَ اللهِط اِنَّ اللهَ لَا يَهْدِي الْقَوْمَ الظّٰلِمِيْنَﯜ50ﯛع وَلَقَدْ وَصَّلْنَا لَهُمُ الْقَوْلَ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَتَذَكَّرُوْنَﯜ51ﯛط اَلَّذِيْنَ اٰتَيْنٰهُمُ الْكِتٰبَ مِنْ قَبْلِهٖ هُمْ بِهٖ يُؤْمِنُوْنَﯜ52ﯛ وَاِذَا يُتْلٰى عَلَيْهِمْ قَالُوْٓا اٰمَنَّا بِهٖٓ اِنَّهُ الْحَقُّ مِنْ رَّبِّنَآ اِنَّا كُنَّا مِنْ قَبْلِهٖ مُسْلِمِيْنَﯜ53ﯛ اُوْلٰۗئِكَ يُؤْتَوْنَ اَجْرَهُمْ مَّرَّتَيْنِ بِمَا صَبَرُوْا وَيَدْرَءُوْنَ بِالْحَسَنَةِ السَّيِّئَةَ وَمِمَّا رَزَقْنٰهُمْ يُنْفِقُوْنَﯜ54ﯛ وَاِذَا سَمِعُوا اللَّغْوَ اَعْرَضُوْا عَنْهُ وَقَالُوْا لَنَآ اَعْمَالُنَا وَلَكُمْ اَعْمَالُكُمْز سَلٰمٌ عَلَيْكُمْز لَا نَبْتَغِي الْجٰهِلِيْنَﯜ55ﯛ اِنَّكَ لَا تَهْدِيْ مَنْ اَحْبَبْتَ وَلٰكِنَّ اللهَ يَهْدِيْ مَنْ يَّشَاۗءُج وَهُوَ اَعْلَمُ بِالْمُهْتَدِيْنَﯜ56ﯛ وَقَالُوْٓا اِنْ نَتَّبِعِ الْهُدٰى مَعَكَ نُتَخَطَّفْ مِنْ اَرْضِنَاط اَوَلَمْ نُمَكِّنْ لَّهُمْ حَرَمًا اٰمِنًا يُّجْبٰٓى اِلَيْهِ ثَمَرٰتُ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ رِّزْقًا مِّنْ لَّدُنَّا وَلٰكِنَّ اَكْثَرَهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُوْنَﯜ57ﯛ وَكَمْ اَهْلَكْنَا مِنْ قَرْيَةٍۢ بَطِرَتْ مَعِيشَتَهَاج فَتِلْكَ مَسٰكِنُهُمْ لَمْ تُسْكَنْ مِّنْۢ بَعْدِهِمْ اِلَّا قَلِيْلًاط وَكُنَّا نَحْنُ الْوٰرِثِيْنَﯜ58ﯛ وَمَا كَانَ رَبُّكَ مُهْلِكَ الْقُرٰى حَتّٰى يَبْعَثَ فِيْٓ اُمِّهَا رَسُوْلًا يَّتْلُوْ عَلَيْهِمْ اٰيٰتِنَاج وَمَا كُنَّا مُهْلِكِي الْقُرٰٓى اِلَّا وَاَهْلُهَا ظٰلِمُوْنَﯜ59ﯛ وَمَآ اُوْتِيْتُمْ مِّنْ شَيْءٍ فَمَتَاعُ الْحَيٰوةِ الدُّنْيَا وَزِيْنَتُهَاج وَمَا عِنْدَ اللهِ خَيْرٌ وَّاَبْقٰىط اَفَلَا تَعْقِلُوْنَﯜ60ﯛع اَفَمَنْ وَّعَدْنٰهُ وَعْدًا حَسَنًا فَهُوَ لَاقِيْهِ كَمَنْ مَّتَّعْنٰهُ مَتَاعَ الْحَيٰوةِ الدُّنْيَا ثُمَّ هُوَ يَوْمَ الْقِيٰمَةِ مِنَ الْمُحْضَرِيْنَﯜ61ﯛ وَيَوْمَ يُنَادِيْهِمْ فَيَقُوْلُ اَيْنَ شُرَكَاۗئِيَ الَّذِيْنَ كُنْتُمْ تَزْعُمُوْنَﯜ62ﯛ قَالَ الَّذِيْنَ حَقَّ عَلَيْهِمُ الْقَوْلُ رَبَّنَا هٰٓؤُلَۗاءِ الَّذِيْنَ اَغْوَيْنَاج اَغْوَيْنٰهُمْ كَمَا غَوَيْنَاج تَبَرَّأْنَآ اِلَيْكَز مَا كَانُوْٓا اِيَّانَا يَعْبُدُوْنَﯜ63ﯛ وَقِيْلَ ادْعُوْا شُرَكَاۗءَكُمْ فَدَعَوْهُمْ فَلَمْ يَسْتَجِيْبُوْا لَهُمْ وَرَاَوُا الْعَذَابَج لَوْ اَنَّهُمْ كَانُوْا يَهْتَدُوْنَﯜ64ﯛ وَيَوْمَ يُنَادِيْهِمْ فَيَقُوْلُ مَاذَآ اَجَبْتُمُ الْمُرْسَلِيْنَﯜ65ﯛ فَعَمِيَتْ عَلَيْهِمُ الْاَنْۢبَاۗءُ يَوْمَئِذٍ فَهُمْ لَا يَتَسَاۗءَلُوْنَﯜ66ﯛ فَاَمَّا مَنْ تَابَ وَاٰمَنَ وَعَمِلَ صَالِحًا فَعَسٰٓى اَنْ يَّكُوْنَ مِنَ الْمُفْلِحِيْنَﯜ67ﯛ وَرَبُّكَ يَخْلُقُ مَا يَشَاۗءُ وَيَخْتَارُط مَا كَانَ لَهُمُ الْخِيَرَةُط سُبْحٰنَ اللهِ وَتَعٰلٰى عَمَّا يُشْرِكُوْنَﯜ68ﯛ وَرَبُّكَ يَعْلَمُ مَا تُكِنُّ صُدُوْرُهُمْ وَمَا يُعْلِنُوْنَﯜ69ﯛ وَهُوَ اللهُ لَٓا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا هُوَط لَهُ الْحَمْدُ فِي الْاُوْلٰى وَالْاٰخِرَةِز وَلَهُ الْحُكْمُ وَاِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُوْنَﯜ70ﯛ قُلْ اَرَاَيْتُمْ اِنْ جَعَلَ اللهُ عَلَيْكُمُ الَّيْلَ سَرْمَدًا اِلٰى يَوْمِ الْقِيٰمَةِ مَنْ اِلٰهٌ غَيْرُ اللهِ يَاْتِيْكُمْ بِضِيَاۗءٍط اَفَلَا تَسْمَعُوْنَﯜ71ﯛ قُلْ اَرَئَيْتُمْ اِنْ جَعَلَ اللهُ عَلَيْكُمُ النَّهَارَ سَرْمَدًا اِلٰى يَوْمِ الْقِيٰمَةِ مَنْ اِلٰهٌ غَيْرُ اللهِ يَاْتِيْكُمْ بِلَيْلٍ تَسْكُنُوْنَ فِيْهِط اَفَلَا تُبْصِرُوْنَﯜ72ﯛ وَمِنْ رَّحْمَتِهٖ جَعَلَ لَكُمُ الَّيْلَ وَالنَّهَارَ لِتَسْكُنُوْا فِيْهِ وَلِتَبْتَغُوْا مِنْ فَضْلِهٖ وَلَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُوْنَﯜ73ﯛ وَيَوْمَ يُنَادِيْهِمْ فَيَقُوْلُ اَيْنَ شُرَكَاۗئِيَ الَّذِيْنَ كُنْتُمْ تَزْعُمُوْنَﯜ74ﯛ وَنَزَعْنَا مِنْ كُلِّ اُمَّةٍ شَهِيْدًا فَقُلْنَا هَاتُوْا بُرْهَانَكُمْ فَعَلِمُوْٓا اَنَّ الْحَقَّ لِلهِ وَضَلَّ عَنْهُمْ مَّا كَانُوْا يَفْتَرُوْنَﯜ75ﯛع اِنَّ قَارُوْنَ كَانَ مِنْ قَوْمِ مُوْسٰى فَبَغٰى عَلَيْهِمْص وَاٰتَيْنٰهُ مِنَ الْكُنُوْزِ مَآ اِنَّ مَفَاتِحَهٗ لَتَنُوْۗءُ بِالْعُصْبَةِ اُوْلِي الْقُوَّةِق اِذْ قَالَ لَهٗ قَوْمُهٗ لَا تَفْرَحْ اِنَّ اللهَ لَا يُحِبُّ الْفَرِحِيْنَﯜ76ﯛ وَابْتَغِ فِيْمَآ اٰتٰكَ اللهُ الدَّارَ الْاٰخِرَةَ وَلَا تَنْسَ نَصِيْبَكَ مِنَ الدُّنْيَا وَاَحْسِنْ كَمَآ اَحْسَنَ اللهُ اِلَيْكَ وَلَا تَبْغِ الْفَسَادَ فِي الْاَرْضِط اِنَّ اللهَ لَا يُحِبُّ الْمُفْسِدِيْنَﯜ77ﯛ قَالَ اِنَّمَآ اُوْتِيْتُهٗ عَلٰى عِلْمٍ عِنْدِيْط اَوَلَمْ يَعْلَمْ اَنَّ اللهَ قَدْ اَهْلَكَ مِنْ قَبْلِهٖ مِنَ الْقُرُوْنِ مَنْ هُوَ اَشَدُّ مِنْهُ قُوَّةً وَّاَكْثَرُ جَمْعًاط وَلَا يُسْئَلُ عَنْ ذُنُوْبِهِمُ الْمُجْرِمُوْنَﯜ78ﯛ فَخَرَجَ عَلٰى قَوْمِهٖ فِيْ زِيْنَتِهٖط قَالَ الَّذِيْنَ يُرِيْدُوْنَ الْحَيٰوةَ الدُّنْيَا يٰلَيْتَ لَنَا مِثْلَ مَآ اُوْتِيَ قَارُوْنُلا اِنَّهٗ لَذُوْ حَظٍّ عَظِيْمٍﯜ79ﯛ وَقَالَ الَّذِيْنَ اُوْتُوا الْعِلْمَ وَيْلَكُمْ ثَوَابُ اللهِ خَيْرٌ لِّمَنْ اٰمَنَ وَعَمِلَ صَالِحًاج وَلَا يُلَقّٰهَآ اِلَّا الصّٰبِرُوْنَﯜ80ﯛ فَخَسَفْنَا بِهٖ وَبِدَارِهِ الْاَرْضَقف فَمَا كَانَ لَهٗ مِنْ فِئَةٍ يَّنْصُرُوْنَهٗ مِنْ دُوْنِ اللهِق وَمَا كَانَ مِنَ الْمُنْتَصِرِيْنَﯜ81ﯛ وَاَصْبَحَ الَّذِيْنَ تَمَنَّوْا مَكَانَهٗ بِالْاَمْسِ يَقُوْلُوْنَ وَيْكَاَنَّ اللهَ يَبْسُطُ الرِّزْقَ لِمَنْ يَّشَاۗءُ مِنْ عِبَادِهٖ وَيَقْدِرُج لَوْلَٓا اَنْ مَّنَّ اللهُ عَلَيْنَا لَخَسَفَ بِنَاط وَيْكَاَنَّهٗ لَا يُفْلِحُ الْكٰفِرُوْنَﯜ82ﯛع تِلْكَ الدَّارُ الْاٰخِرَةُ نَجْعَلُهَا لِلَّذِيْنَ لَا يُرِيْدُوْنَ عُلُوًّا فِي الْاَرْضِ وَلَا فَسَادًاط وَالْعَاقِبَةُ لِلْمُتَّقِيْنَﯜ83ﯛ مَنْ جَاۗءَ بِالْحَسَنَةِ فَلَهٗ خَيْرٌ مِّنْهَاج وَمَنْ جَاۗءَ بِالسَّيِّئَةِ فَلَا يُجْزَى الَّذِيْنَ عَمِلُوا السَّيِّاٰتِ اِلَّا مَا كَانُوْا يَعْمَلُوْنَﯜ84ﯛ اِنَّ الَّذِيْ فَرَضَ عَلَيْكَ الْقُرْاٰنَ لَرَاۗدُّكَ اِلٰى مَعَادٍ قُلْ رَبِّيْٓ اَعْلَمُ مَنْ جَاۗءَ بِالْهُدٰى وَمَنْ هُوَ فِيْ ضَلٰلٍ مُّبِيْنٍﯜ85ﯛ وَمَا كُنْتَ تَرْجُوْٓ اَنْ يُّلْقٰٓى اِلَيْكَ الْكِتٰبُ اِلَّا رَحْمَةً مِّنْ رَّبِّكَ فَلَا تَكُوْنَنَّ ظَهِيْرًا لِّلْكٰفِرِيْنَﯜ86ﯛ وَلَا يَصُدُّنَّكَ عَنْ اٰيٰتِ اللهِ بَعْدَ اِذْ اُنْزِلَتْ اِلَيْكَ وَادْعُ اِلٰى رَبِّكَ وَلَا تَكُوْنَنَّ مِنَ الْمُشْرِكِيْنَﯜ87ﯛ وَلَا تَدْعُ مَعَ اللهِ اِلٰهًا اٰخَرَم لَٓا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا هُوَقف كُلُّ شَيْءٍ هَالِكٌ اِلَّا وَجْهَهٗط لَهُ الْحُكْمُ وَاِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُوْنَﯜ88ﯛع

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Ta seem meem.

2. Yeh kitaabe mobin ki aayate hai.

3. Ham aap ko Moosa aur Firon ki sachi khabar soona rahe hai un logo ke liye jo imaan laane waale hai.

4. Firon ne ruye-zameen par bulandi ikhtiyaar ki aur us ne Ahle zameen ko mukhtalif hisso mein taqseem kar diya keh ek giroh ne dusre ko bilkul kamzor bana diya woh ladko ko zibah kar diya karta tha aur aurto’n ko zinda rakkha karta tha. Woh yaqeenan mufsedeen (fasad karne waalo) mein se tha.

5. Aur Ham yeh chahte hai keh jin logo ko zameen mein kamzor bana diya gaya hai un par ehsaan kare aur unhe logo ka peshwa banaaye aur zameen ka waaris qaraar de de.

6. Aur unhi ko ruye-zameen ka iqtedaar de aur Firon wa Haamaan aur unke lashkaro ko un hi kamzoro ke haatho se woh manzar dikhlaaye jis se yeh darr rahe hai.

7. Aur Ham ne maadar-e-Moosa ki taraf wahi (revelation) ki keh apne bachche ko doodh pilaawo aur iske baad jab uski zindagi ka khauf paiyda ho to usey dariya mein daal do aur bilkul daro nahi aur pareshaan na ho keh Ham usey tumhaari taraf palta dene waale aur usey mursaleen mein se qaraar dene waale hai.

8. Phir Firon waalo ne usey utha liya ke anjaame-kaar unka dushman aur unke liye baais-e-ranj-o-alam bane. Be-shak Firon aur Haamaan aur unke lashkar waale sab galti par thay.

9. Aur Firon ki zauja ne kaha keh yeh to hamari aur tumhaari aankho ki thandak hai lehaaza usey qatl na karo keh shaayad yeh hamein faaida pohonchaaye aur Ham usey apna farzand bana le aur woh log kuch nahi samajh rahe thay.

10. Aur Moosa ki maa ka dil bilkul khaali ho gaya keh qareeb tha keh woh is raaz ko faash kar deti agar Ham unke dil ko mutmaeen na bana dete ta-keh woh imaan laane waalo mein shaamil ho jaaye.

11. Aur unho ne apni bahen (sister) se kaha keh tum bhi unka peecha karo to unho ne dur se Moosa ko dekha jab-keh un logo ko is ka ehsaas bhi nahi tha.

12. Aur ham ne Moosa par doodh pilaney waaliyo ka doodh pehle hi se haraam kar diya to Moosa ki bahen (sister) ne kaha keh kya mai tumhe aise ghar waalo ka pata bataawu jo iski kifaalat kar sakay aur woh iske khair khwaah bhi ho.

13. Phir Ham ne Moosa ko unki maa ki taraf palta diya ta-keh unki aankh thandhi ho jaaye aur woh pareshan na rahe aur unhe maaloom ho jaaye keh Allah ka waada baher-haal sachcha hai agar-che logo ki aksariyat usey nahi jaanti hai.

14. Aur jab Moosa jawaani ki tawaanaaiyo ko pohonche aur tandurast ho gaye to Ham ne unhe ilm aur hikmat ataa kar di aur Ham isi tarah nek amal waalo ko jaza diya karte hai.

15. Aur Moosa shaher mein us waqt daakhil huwe jab log ghaflat ki neend mein thay to unho ne do admiyo ko ladte huwe dekha ek unke shiyo mein se tha aur ek dushmano mein se to jo unke shiyo mein se tha us ne dushman ke zulm ki faryaad ki to Moosa ne usey ek ghoonsa (fight) maar kar uski zindagi ka faisla kar diya aur kaha keh yeh yaqeenan shaitaan ke amal se tha aur yaqeenan shaitaan dushman aur khula huwa gumraah karne waala hai.

16. Moosa ne kaha keh Parwardigaar mai ne apne nafs ke liye museebat mol le-li lehaaza mujhe maaf kar de to Parwardigaar ne maaf kar diya keh woh bohot bakhshne waala aur maher-baan hai.

17. Moosa ne kaha keh Parwardigaar Tu ne meri madad ki hai lehaaza mai kabhi mujreemo ka saathi nahi banuga.

18. Phir subah ke waqt Moosa shaher mein daakhil huwe to khauf-zadah aur haalaat ki nigraani karte huwe keh achaanak dekha ke jisne kal madad ke liye pukaara tha woh phir faryaad kar raha hai Moosa ne kaha keh yaqeenan tu khula huwa gumraah hai.

19. Phir jab Moosa ne chaaha ke us par hamla-aawar ho jo dono ka dushman hai to us ne kaha keh Moosa tum isi tarah mujhe qatl karna chahte ho jis tarah tum ne kal ek be-gunah ko qatl kiya hai tum sirf ruye-zameen mein sarkash (rebellious) haakim ban kar rehna chahte ho aur yeh nahi chahte ho keh tumhaara shumaar islaah karne waalo mein ho.

20. Aur idhar aakhire shaher se ek (1) shakhs daudta huwa aaya aur us ne kaha keh Moosa shaher ke bade log bahami mashwara kar rahe hai keh tumhe qatl kar de lehaaza tum shaher se baahar nikal jaawo mai tumhaare liye nasihat karne waalo mein se hu.

21. To Moosa shaher se baahar nikle khauf-zadah aur daaye-baaye dekhte huwe aur kaha keh Parwardigaar mujhe zaalim qaum se mehfooz rakhna.

22. Aur jab Moosa ne Madyan ka rukh kiya to kaha keh an-qareeb Parwardigaar mujhe seedhe raasteki hidaayat kar dega.

23. Aur jab Madyan ke chashme par waarid huwe to logo ki ek jamaat ko dekha jo jaanwaro ko paani pila rahi thi aur un se alag do aurte’n thi jo jaanwaro ko roke khadi thi. Moosa ne poochha ke tum logo ka kya masla hai un dono ne kaha keh ham us waqt tak paani nahi pilaate hai jab tak saari qaum hatt na jaaye aur hamare baba ek zaeef-ul-umr aadmi hai.

24. Moosa ne dono ke jaanwaro ko paani pila diya aur phir ek saay mein aakar panah le li arz ki Parwardigaar yaqeenan mai us khair ka mohtaaj hu jo Tu meri taraf bhej de.

25. Itne mein dono mein se ek ladki kamaale sharam-o-haya ke saath chalti huwi aayi aur us ne kaha keh mere baba aap ko bula rahe hai keh aap ke paani pilaney ki ujrat de-de phir jo Moosa unke paas aaye aur apna qissa bayaan kiya to unho ne kaha keh daro nahi ab tum zaalim qaum se najaat paa gaye.

26. Un dono mein se ek ladki ne kaha ke baba aap inhe nokar rakh lijiye keh aap jise bhi nokar rakhna chahe un mein sab se behtar woh hoga jo saheb-e-quwwat bhi ho aur amaanat-daar bhi ho.

27. Unho ne kaha keh mai in dono mein se ek beti ka aqd aap se karna chahta hu ba-sharte keh aap aath saal (8 years) tak meri khidmat kare phir agar das saal poore kar de to yeh aap ki taraf se hoga aur mai aap ko koi zehmat nahi dena chahta hu insha-Allah aap mujhe nek bando mein se paayege.

28. Moosa ne kaha keh yeh mere aur aap ke darmiyaan ka mohaaida (agreement) hai mai jo muddat bhi poori kar du mere upar koi zimme-daari nahi hogi aur mai jo kuch bhi kahe raha hu Allah is ka gawah hai.

29. Phir jab Moosa muddat ko pura kar chuke aur apne ahel ko le kar chale to Toor ki taraf se ek aag nazar aayi unho ne apne ahel se kaha keh tum log thairo mai ne ek aag dekhi hai shaayad us mein se koi khabar le aawu ya koi chingaari hi le aawu ke tum log us se taap (warmth) ne ka kaam le sako.

30. Phir jo us aag ke qareeb aaye to waadi ke daahine rukh se ek mubaarak maqaam par ek darakht se aawaaz aayi Moosa mai Aalameen ka paalne waala Khuda hu.

31. Aur tum apne asaa ko zameen par daal do aur jo Moosa ne dekha to woh saanp (snake) ki tarah lehra raha tha. Yeh dekh kar Moosa peechhe hatt gaye aur phir mood kar bhi na dekha to phir aawaaz aayi keh Moosa aaghe badho aur daro nahi keh tum bilkul mamoon-o-mehfooz ho.

32. Zara apne haath ko gharibaan mein daakhil karo woh bagair kisi bimaari ke safaid aur chamak-daar ban kar baraamad hoga aur khauf se apne baazuwo ko apni taraf samaitt lo tumhaare liye Parwardigaar ki taraf se Firon aur uski qaum ke sardaaro ki taraf yeh do daleele hai keh yeh log sab faasiq aur bad-kaar hai.

33. Moosa ne kaha keh Parwardigaar mai ne unke ek aadmi ko maar daala hai to mujhe khauf hai keh yeh mujhe qatl kar dege aur kaar-e-tableegh ruk jaayega.

34. Aur mere bhai Haaroon mujh se zeyada fasih zabaan ke maalik hai lehaaza unhe mere saath madad-gaar bana de jo meri tasdeeq (confirmation) kar sakay keh mai darta hu keh kahi yeh log meri takzeeb na kar de.

35. Irshaad huwa keh Ham tumhaare baazuwo ko tumhaare bhai se mazboot kar dege aur tumhaare liye aisa ghalba qaraar dege keh yeh log tum tak pohonch hina sakay aur hamaari nishaaniyo ke sahaare tum aur tumhaare pairo-kaar hi ghaalib rahege.

36. Phir jab Moosa unke paas hamari khuli huwi nishaaniya le kar aaye to un logo ne kahe diya keh yeh to sirf ek ghada huwa jadoo (magic) hai aur ham ne apne guzishta buzurgo se is tarah ki koi baat nahi sooni hai.

37. Aur Moosa ne kaha keh mera Parwardigaar behtar jaanta hai keh kaun uski taraf se hidaayat le kar aaya hai aur kis ke liye aakherat ka ghar hai yaqeenan zaalemeen ke liye falah (khair) nahi hai.

38. Aur Firon ne kaha keh mere zoama-e-mamlekat! Mere ilm mein tumhaare liye mere alaawa koi Khuda nahi hai lehaaza Haamaan! Tum mere liye mitti ka pajaawa (dher) lagaawo aur phir ek qilah tayyar karo keh mai us par chadh kar Moosa ke Khuda ki khabar le aawu aur mera khayaal to yahi hai ke Moosa joothay hai.

39. Aur Firon aur uske lashkar ne na-haq guroor se kaam liya aur yeh khayaal kar liya keh woh palta kar hamari baargah mein nahi laaye jaayege.

40. To Ham ne Firon aur uske lashkaro ko apni giraft mein le liya aur sab ko dariya mein daal diya to dekho ke zaalemeen ka anjaam kaisa hota hai.

41. Aur Ham ne un logo ko jahannam ki taraf daawat dene waale peshwa qaraar de diya hai aur qayamat ke din unki koi madad nahi ki jaayegi.

42. Aur duniya mein bhi Ham ne unke peechhe laanat ko laga diya hai aur qayamat ke din bhi unka shumaar un logo mein hoga jin ke chehre bigaad diye jaayege.

43. Aur Ham ne pehli naslo ko halaak kar dene ke baad Moosa ko kitaab ataa ki jo logo ke liye baseerat ka saamaan aur hidaayat-o-rehmat hai aur shaayad isi tarah yeh log nasihat haasil kar le.

44. Aur aap us waqt Toor ke maghribi rukh par na thay jab Ham ne Moosa ki taraf apna hukm bheja aur aap us waaqiye ke haazreen mein se bhi nahi thay.

45. Lekin Ham ne bohot si qaumo ko paiyda kiya phir un par ek taweel zamaana guzar gaya aur aap to ahel Madyan mein bhi muqeem nahi thay ke unhe hamaari aayate padh kar soonaate lekin ham Rasool banaane waale to thay.

46. Aur aap Toor ke kisi jaanib us waqt nahi thay jab Ham ne Moosa ko aawaaz di lekin aap ke Parwardigaar ki rehmat hai keh aap is qaum ko daraaiye jiski taraf aap se pehle koi Paighambar nahiaaya hai keh shaayad woh is tarah ibrat (sabak/lesson) aur nasihat haasil kar le.

47. Aur agar aisa na hota keh jab un par guzishta aamaal ki bina par koi museebat naazil hoti to yahi kehte ke Parwardigaar Tu ne hamari taraf koi Rasool kyu’n nahi bheja hai keh ham Teri nishaaniya ki pairwi karte aur sahebaan-e-imaan mein shaamil ho jaate.

48. Phir iske baad jab hamari taraf se haq aa gaya to kehne lagey keh inhe woh sab kyu’n nahi diya gaya hai jo Moosa ko diya gaya tha to kya un logo ne is se pehle Moosa ka inkaar nahi kiya aur ab to kehte hai keh Taurait aur Qur’an jadoo (magic) hai jo ek dusre ki taaid karte hai aur ham dono hi ka inkaar karne waale hai.

49. To aap kahe dijiye keh achcha tum Parwardigaar ki taraf se koi kitaab le aawo jo dono se zeyada sahih ho aur mai uska itteba kar lu agar tum apni baat mein sachche ho.

50. Phir agar qabool na kare to samajh lijiye keh yeh sirf apni khwaahishaat ka itteba karne waale hai aur us se zeyada gumraah kaun hai? Jo Khudai hidaayat ke bagair apni khwahishaat ka itteba kar le jab-keh Allah zaalim qaum ki hidaayat karne waala nahi hai.

51. Aur ham ne musalsal un logo tak apni baate pohonchaayi ke shaayad isi tarah nasihat haasil kar le.

52. Jin logo ko Ham ne is se pehle kitaab di hai woh is Qur’an par imaan rakhte hai.

53. Aur jab unke saamne uski tilaawat ki jaati hai to kehte hai keh ham imaan le aaye yeh hamare rab ki taraf se bar-haq hai aur ham to pehle hi se tasleem (submission) kiye huwe thay.

54. Yahi woh log hai jinko dohri jaza (double reward) di jaayega keh unho ne sabr kiya hai aur yeh nekiyo ke zariya buraaiyo ko dafa karte hai aur Ham ne jo rizq diya hai us mein se kharch karte hai.

55. Aur jab lagw (be-huda) baat soonte hai to kinaara-kashi ikhtiyaar karte hai aur kehte hai keh hamare liye hamare aamaal hai aur tumhaare liye tumhaare aamaal hai tum par hamaara salaam ke Ham jaahilo ki sohbat pasand nahi karte hai.

56. Paighambar be-shak aap jise chahe usey hidaayat nahi de sakte hai bal-keh Allah jise chahta hai hidaayat de deta hai aur woh un logo se khoob ba-khabar hai jo hidaayat paane waale hai.

57. Aur yeh kuffaar kehte hai keh ham aap ke saath haq ki pairwi karege to apni zameen se uchak liye jaayege to kya ham ne unhe ek mehfooz haram par qabza nahi diya hai jiski taraf har shaiy ke phal hamari di huwi rozi ki bina par chale aa rahe hai lekin unki aksariyat samajh-ti hi nahi hai.

58. Aur Ham ne kitni hi bastiyo ko unki maeeshat (zindagi) ke guroor ki bina par halaak kar diya ab yeh unke makanaat hai jo unke baad phir abaad na ho sakey magar bohot kam aur dar-haqeeqat ham hi har cheez ke waaris aur maalik hai.

59. Aur aap ka Parwardigaar kisi basti ko halaak karne waala nahi hai jab tak keh uske markaz mein koi Rasool na bhej de jo unke saamne hamari aayaat ki tilaawat kare aur Ham kisi basti ke tabaah karne waale nahi hai magar yeh keh uske rehne waale zaalim ho.

60. Aur jo kuch bhi tumhe ataa kiya gaya hai yeh chand roza lazzat-e-duniya aur zeenat-e-duniya hai aur jo kuch bhi Khuda ki baargah mein hai woh khair aur baaqi rehne waala hai kya tum itna bhi nahi samajh-te ho.

61. Kya woh banda jis se Ham ne behtareen waada kiya hai aur woh usey pa bhi lega uske maanind hai jise ham ne duniya mein thodi si lazzat de di hai aur phir woh roz-e-qayamat Khuda ki baargah mein kheech kar haazir kiya jaayega.

62. Jis din Khuda un logo ko aawaaz dega keh mere woh shorka (partners) kaha’n hai jin ki shirkat ka tumhe khayaal tha.

63. To jin shorka par azaab saabit ho chuka hoga woh Kahenge keh Parwardigaar yeh hai woh log jinko ham ne gumraah kiya hai aur isi tarah gumraah kiya hai jis tarah ham khud gumraah huwe thay lekin ab un se baraa’at chahte hai yeh hamaari ibaadat to nahi kar rahe thay.

64. To kaha jaayega ke achcha apne shorka ko pukaaro to woh pukaarenge lekin koi jawaab na paayege bal-keh azaab hi ko dekhenge to kaash yeh duniya hi mein hidaayat yaafta ho jaate.

65. Aur jis din Khuda un se pukaar kar kahega keh tum ne hamare Rasoolo ko kya jawaab diya tha.

66. To un par saari khabre taareek ho jaayega aur woh aapas mein ek dusre se sawaal bhi na kar sakayge.

67. Lekin jisne tauba kar-li aur imaan le aaya aur nek amal kiya woh yaqeenan falah paane waalo mein shumaar ho jaayega.

68. Aur aap ka Parwardigaar jise chahta hai paiyda karta hai aur pasand karta haiun logo ko kisi ka intekhaab karne ka koi haq nahi hai Khuda unke shirk se paak aur buland-o-bartar hai.

69. Aur aap ka Parwardigaar un cheezo ko bhi jaanta hai jo yeh apne seeno mein chhupaaye huwe hai aur unhe bhi jaanta hai jin ka yeh elaan-o-izhaar karte hai.

70. Woh Allah hai jiske alaawa koi dusra Khuda nahi hai aur awwal-o-aakhir aur duniya-o-aakherat mein saari hamd usi ke liye hai usi ke liye hukm hai aur usi ki taraf tum sab ko waapas jaana padega.

71. Aap kahe keh tumhaara kya khayaal hai agar Khuda tumhaare liye raat ko qayamat tak keh liye abdi banade to kya uske alaawa aur koi maabood hai jo tumhaare liye roshni ko le aa sakey to kya tum baat soonte nahi ho.

72. Aur kahe keh agar woh din ko qayamat tak keh liye daayemi bana de to uske alaawa kaun maabood hai jo tumhaare liye woh raat le aayega jis mein sukoon haasil kar sako kya tum dekhte nahi ho.

73. Yeh uski rehmat ka ek hissa hai keh us ne tumhaare liye raat aur din dono banaaye hai ta-keh aaraam bhi kar sako aur rizq bhi talaash kar sako aur shaayad uska shukriya bhi adaa kar sako.

74. Aur qayamat ke din Khuda unhe aawaaz dega keh mere woh shareek kaha’n hai jin ki shirkat ka tumhe khayaal tha.

75. Aur Ham har qaum mein se ek gawah nikaal kar laayege aur munkereen se Kahenge keh tum bhi apni daleel le aawo tab unhe maaloom hoga keh haq Allah hi ke liye hai aur phir woh jo iftara (jooth) pardaaziya (bayaan) kiya karte thay woh sab goom ho jaayegi.

76. Be-shak Qaaroon Moosa ki qaum mein se tha magar us ne qaum par zulm kiya aur Ham ne bhi usey itney khazaane de diye thay keh ek taaqat war jamaat se bhi uski kunjiya nahi uth sakti thi phir jab us se qaum ne kaha keh is qadr na itraao keh Khuda itraane waalo ko dost nahi rakhta hai.

77. Aur jo kuch Khuda ne diya hai us se aakherat ke ghar ka intezaam karo aur duniya mein apna hissa bhool na jaawo aur neki karo jis tarah keh Khuda ne tumhaare saath nek bartaaw kiya hai aur zameen mein fasaad ki koshish na karo keh Allah fasaad karne waalo ko dost nahi rakhta hai.

78. Qaaroon ne kaha keh mujhe yeh sab-kuch mere ilm ki bina par diya gaya hai to kya usey yeh maaloom nahi hai keh Allah ne us se pehle bohot si naslo ko halaak kar diya hai jo us se zeyada taaqatwar aur maal ke etebaar se daulat-mand thi aur aise mujreemo se to unke gunaho ke baare mein sawaal bhi nahi kiya jaata hai.

79. Phir qaroon apni qaum ke saamne zaib-o-zeenat ke saath baraamad huwa to jin logo ke dil mein zindagaani-e-duniya ki khwaahish thi unho ne kehna shuru kar diya ke kaash hamare paas bhi yeh saaz-o-saamaan hota jo Qaaroon ko diya gaya hai yeh to bade azeem hisse ka maalik hai.

80. Aur jinhe ilm diya gaya tha unho ne kaha keh afsos tumhaare haal par Allah ka sawaab sahebaan-e-imaan-o-amal-e-saaleh ke liye is se kahi zeyada behtar hai aur woh sawaab, sabr karne waalo ke alaawa kisi ko nahi diya jaata hai.

81. Phir Ham ne usey aur uske ghar-baar ko zameen mein dhansa diya aur na koi giroh Khuda ke alaawa bachaane waala paiyda huwa aur na woh khud apna bachao karne waala tha.

82. Aur jin logo ne kal uski jagah ki tamanna ki thi woh kehne lagey keh maaz-Allah yeh to Khuda jis bande ke liye chahta hai uske rizq mein wus’at paiyda kar deta hai aur jiske yahan chahta hai tanghi paiyda kar deta hai aur agar us ne ham par ehsaan na kar diya hota to hamein bhi dhansa diya hota. Maaz-Allah kaafiro ke liye waaqeyi falah nahi hai.

83. Yeh dar-e-aakherat woh hai jise Ham un logo ke liye qaraar dete hai jo zameen mein bulandi aur fasaad ke talab-gaar nahi hote hai aur aaqebat to sirf sahebaan-e-taqwa ke liye hai.

84. Jo koi neki karega usey us se behtar ajr milega aur jo koi buraayi karega to buraayi karne waalo ko utni hi sazadi jaayega jaise aamaal woh karte rahe hai.

85. Be-shak jisne aap par Qur’an ka fareeza aaed (waajib) kiya hai woh aap ko aap ki manzil tak zaroor waapas pohonchaayega. Aap kahe dijiye keh mera Parwardigaar behtar jaanta hai keh kaun hidaayat le kar aaya hai aur kaun khuli huwi gumraahi mein hai.

86. Aur aap to is baat ke umeed-waar nahi thay keh aap ki taraf kitaab naazil ki jaaye yeh to rehmate Parwardigaar hai lehaaza khabar-daar! Aap kaafiro ka saath na dijiyega.

87. Aur harghiz yeh log aayaat ke naazil hone ke baad unki tableegh se aap ko rokne na paaye aur aap apne rab ki taraf daawat dijiye aur khabar-daar! Mushreqeen mein se na ho jaaiye.

88. Aur Allah ke saath kisi dusre Khuda ko mat pukaaro ke uske alaawa koi maabood nahi hai. Uski zaat ke maasiwa (dusri) har shaiy halaak hone waali hai aur tum sab usi ki baargah ki taraf paltaaye jaawoge.

28 | QASAS | 88

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. Ta sin Mim.

2. These are the verses of the Book that makes (things) clear.

3. We recite to you from the account of Musa and Firon with truth for people who believe.

4. Surely Firon exalted himself in the land and made its people into parties, weakening one party from among them; he slaughtered their sons and let their women live; surely he was one of the mischiefmakers.

5. And We desired to bestow a favor upon those who were deemed weak in the land, and to make them the Imams, and to make them the heirs,

6. And to grant them power in the land, and to make Firon and Haman and their hosts see from them what they feared.

7. And We revealed to Musa’s mothers, saying: Give him suck, then when you fear for him, cast him into the river and do not fear nor grieve; surely We will bring him back to you and make him one of the apostles.

8. And Firon’s family took him up that he might be an enemy and a grief for them; surely Firon and Haman and their hosts were wrongdoers.

9. And Firon’s wife said: A refreshment of the eye to me and to you; do not slay him; maybe he will be useful to us, or we may take him for a son; and they did not perceive.

10. And the heart of Musa’s mother was free (from anxiety) she would have almost disclosed it had We not strengthened her heart so that she might be of the believers.

11. And she said to his sister: Follow him up. So she watched him from a distance while they did not perceive,

12. And We ordained that he refused to suck any foster mother before, so she said: Shall I point out to you the people of a house who will take care of him for you, and they will be benevolent to him?

13. So We gave him back to his mother that her eye might be refreshed, and that she might not grieve, and that she might know that the promise of Allah is true, but most of them do not know.

14. And when he attained his maturity and became full grown, We granted him wisdom and knowledge; and thus do We reward those who do good (to others).

15. And he went into the city at a time of unvigilance on the part of its people, so he found therein two men fighting, one being of his party and the other of his foes, and he who was of his party cried out to him for help against him who was of his enemies, so Musa struck him with his fist and killed him. He said: This is on account of the Shaitan’s doing; surely he is an enemy, openly leading astray.

16. He said: My Lord! surely I have done harm to myself, so do Thou protect me. So He protected him; surely He is the Forgiving, the Merciful.

17. He said: My Lord! because Thou hast bestowed a favor on me, I shall never be a backer of the guilty.

18. And he was in the city, fearing, awaiting, when lo! he who had asked his assistance the day before was crying out to him for aid. Musa said to him: You are most surely one erring manifestly.

19. So when he desired to seize him who was an enemy to them both, he said: O Musa! do you intend to kill me as you killed a person yesterday? You desire nothing but that you should be a tyrant in the land, and you do not desire to be of those who act aright.

20. And a man came running from the remotest part of the city. He said: O Musa! surely the chiefs are consulting together to slay you, therefore depart (at once); surely I am of those who wish well to you.

21. So he went forth therefrom, fearing, awaiting, (and) he said: My Lord! deliver me from the unjust people.

22. And when he turned his face towards Madyan, he said: Maybe my Lord will guide me in the right path.

23. And when he came to the water of Madyan, he found on it a group of men watering, and he found besides them two women keeping back (their flocks). He said: What is the matter with you? They said: We cannot water until the shepherds take away (their sheep) from the water, and our father is a very old man.

24. So he watered (their sheep) for them, then went back to the shade and said: My Lord! surely I stand in need of whatever good Thou mayest send down to me.

25. Then one of the two women came to him walking bashfully. She said: My father invites you that he may give you the reward of your having watered for us. So when he came to him and gave to him the account, he said: Fear not, you are secure from the unjust people.

26. Said one of them: O my father! employ him, surely the best of those that you can employ is the strong man, the faithful one.

27. He said: I desire to marry one of these two daughters of mine to you on condition that you should serve me for eight years; but if you complete ten, it will be of your own free will, and I do not wish to be hard to you; if Allah please, you will find me one of the good.

28. He said: This shall be (an agreement) between me and you; whichever of the two terms I fulfill, there shall be no wrongdoing to me; and Allah is a witness of what we say.

29. So when Musa had fulfilled the term, and he journeyed with his family, he perceived on this side of the mountain a fire. He said to his family: Wait, I have seen a fire, maybe I will bring to you from it some news or a brand of fire, so that you may warm yourselves.

30. And when he came to it, a voice was uttered from the right side of the valley in the blessed spot of the bush, saying: O Musa! surely I am Allah, the Lord of the worlds.

31. And saying: Cast down you staff. So when he saw it in motion as if it were a serpent, he turned back retreating, and did not return. O Musa! come forward and fear not; surely you are of those who are secure;

32. Enter your hand into the opening of your bosom, it will come forth white without evil, and draw your hand to yourself to ward off fear: so these two shall be two arguments from your Lord to Firon and his chiefs, surely they are a transgressing people.

33. He said: My Lord! surely I killed one of them, so I fear lest they should slay me;

34. And my brother, Haroun, he is more eloquent of tongue than I, therefore send him with me as an aider, verifying me: surely I fear that they would reject me.

35. He said: We will strengthen your arm with your brother, and We will give you both an authority, so that they shall not reach you; (go) with Our signs; you two and those who follow you shall be uppermost.

36. So when Musa came to them with Our clear signs, they said: This is nothing but forged enchantment, and we never heard of it amongst our fathers of old.

37. And Musa said: My Lord knows best who comes with guidance from Him, and whose shall be the good end of the abode; surely the unjust shall not be successful.

38. And Firon (Pharaoh) said: O Chiefs! I do not know of any god for you except myself: Therefore, O Haman! Fire up a (large oven to make bricks) from the clay for me, and build me a high palace, so that I may climb up to the Allah of Musa: but as far as I know, I think (Musa) is a liar.

39. And he was unjustly proud in the land, he and his hosts, and they deemed that they would not be brought back to Us.

40. So We caught hold of him and his hosts, then We cast them into the sea, and see how was the end of the unjust.

41. And We made them Imams who call to the fire, and on the day of resurrection they shall not be assisted.

42. And We caused a curse to follow them in this world, and on the day of resurrection they shall be of those made to appear hideous.

43. And certainly We gave Musa the Book after We had destroyed the former generations, clear arguments for men and a guidance and a mercy, that they may be mindful.

44. And you were not on the western side when We revealed to Musa the commandment, and you were not among the witnesses;

45. But We raised up generations, then life became prolonged to them; and you were not dwelling among the people of Madyan, reciting to them Our communications, but We were the senders.

46. And you were not on this side of the mountain when We called, but a mercy from your Lord that you may warn a people to whom no warner came before you, that they may be mindful.

47. And were it not that there should befall them a disaster for what their hands have sent before, then they should say: Our Lord! why didst Thou not send to us an apostle so that we should have followed Thy communications and been of the believers!

48. But (now) when the truth has come to them from Us, they say: Why is he not given the like of what was given to Musa? What! did they not disbelieve in what Musa was given before? They say: Two magicians backing up each other; and they say: Surely we are unbelievers in all.

49. Say: Then bring some (other) book from Allah which is a better guide than both of them, (that) I may follow it, if you are truthful.

50. But if they do not answer you, then know that they only follow their low desires; and who is more erring than he who follows his low desires without any guidance from Allah? Surely Allah does not guide the unjust people.

51. And certainly We have made the word to reach them so that they may be mindful.

52. (As to) those whom We gave the Book before it, they are believers in it.

53. And when it is recited to them they say: We believe in it; surely it is the truth from our Lord; surely we were submitters before this.

54. These shall be granted their reward twice, because they are steadfast and they repel evil with good and spend out of what We have given them.

55. And when they hear idle talk they turn aside from it and say: We shall have our deeds and you shall have your deeds; peace be on you, we do not desire the ignorant.

56. Surely you cannot guide whom you love, but Allah guides whom He pleases, and He knows best the followers of the right way.

57. And they say: If we follow the guidance with you, we shall be carried off from our country. What! have We not settled them in a safe, sacred territory to which fruits of every kind shall be drawn?— a sustenance from Us; but most of them do not know.

58. And how many a town have We destroyed which exulted in its means of subsistence, so these are their abodes, they have not been dwelt in after them except a little, and We are the inheritors,

59. And your Lord never destroyed the towns until He raised in their metropolis an apostle, reciting to them Our communications, and We never destroyed the towns except when their people were unjust.

60. And whatever things you have been given are only a provision of this world’s life and its adornment, and whatever is with Allah is better and more lasting; do you not then understand?

61. Is he to whom We have promised a goodly promise which he shall meet with like him whom We have provided with the provisions of this world’s life, then on the day of resurrection he shall be of those who are brought up?

62. And on the day when He will call them and say: Where are those whom you deemed to be My associates?

63. Those against whom the sentence has become confirmed will say: Our Lord! these are they whom we caused to err; we caused them to err as we ourselves did err; to Thee we declare ourselves to be clear (of them); they never served Us.

64. And it will be said: Call your associate-gods. So they will call upon them, but they will not answer them, and they shall see the punishment; would that they had followed the right way!

65. And on the day when He shall call them and say: What was the answer you gave to the apostles?

66. Then the pleas shall become obscure to them on that day, so they shall not ask each other.

67. But as to him who repents and believes and does good, maybe he will be among the successful:

68. And your Lord creates and chooses whom He pleases; to choose is not theirs; glory be to Allah, and exalted be He above what they associate (with Him).

69. And your Lord knows what their breasts conceal and what they manifest.

70. And He is Allah, there is no god but He! All praise is due to Him in this (life) and the hereafter, and His is the judgment, and to Him you shall be brought back.

71. Say: Tell me, if Allah were to make the night to continue incessantly on you till the day of resurrection, who is the god besides Allah that could bring you light? Do you not then hear?

72. Say: Tell me, if Allah were to make the day to continue incessantly on you till the day of resurrection, who is the god besides Allah that could bring you the night in which you take rest? Do you not then see?

73. And out of His mercy He has made for you the night and the day, that you may rest therein, and that you may seek of His grace, and that you may give thanks.

74. And on the day when He shall call them and say: Where are those whom you deemed to be My associates?

75. And We will draw forth from among every nation a witness and say: Bring your proof; then shall they know that the truth is Allah’s, and that which they forged shall depart from them.

76. Surely Qaroun was of the people of Musa, but he rebelled against them, and We had given him of the treasures, so much so that his hoards of wealth would certainly weigh down a company of men possessed of great strength. When his people said to him: Do not exult, surely Allah does not love the exultant;

77. And seek by means of what Allah has given you the future abode, and do not neglect your portion of this world, and do good (to others) as Allah has done good to you, and do not seek to make mischief in the land, surely Allah does not love the mischief-makers.

78. He said: I have been given this only on account of the knowledge I have. Did he not know that Allah had destroyed before him of the generations those who were mightier in strength than he and greater in assemblage? And the guilty shall not be asked about their faults.

79. So he went forth to his people in his finery. Those who desire this world’s life said: O would that we had the like of what Qaroun is given; most surely he is possessed of mighty good fortune.

80. And those who were given the knowledge said: Woe to you! Allah’s reward is better for him who believes and does good, and none is made to receive this except the patient.

81. Thus We made the earth to swallow up him and his abode; so he had no body of helpers to assist him against Allah nor was he of those who can defend themselves.

82. And those who yearned for his place only the day before began to say: Ah! (know) that Allah amplifies and straitens the means of subsistence for whom He pleases of His servants; had not Allah been gracious to us, He would most surely have abased us; ah! (know) that the ungrateful are never successful.

83. (As for) that future abode, We assign it to those who have no desire to exalt themselves in the earth nor to make mischief and the good end is for those who guard (against evil)

84. Whoever brings good, he shall have better than it, and whoever brings evil, those who do evil shall not be rewarded (for) aught except what they did.

85. Most surely He Who has made the Quran binding on you will bring you back to the destination. Say: My Lord knows best him who has brought the guidance and him who is in manifest error.

86. And you did not expect that the Book would be inspired to you, but it is a mercy from your Lord, therefore be not a backer-up of the unbelievers.

87. And let them not turn you aside from the communications of Allah after they have been revealed to you, and call (men) to your Lord and be not of the polytheists.

88. And call not with Allah any other god; there is no god but He, everything is perishable but He; His is the judgment, and to Him you shall be brought back.
