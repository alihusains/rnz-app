Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Qasam hai anjeer aur zaitoon ki.

2. Aur toor-e-sineen ki.

3. Aur is amn waale shaher ki.

4. Ham ne insaan ko behtareen saakht (best form) mein paiyda kiya hai.

5. Phir Ham ne is ko past tareen haalat ki taraf palta diya hai.

6. Alaawa un logo ke jo imaan laaye aur unho ne nek aamaal anjaam diye to unke liye na-khatm hone waala ajr hai.

7. Phir tum ko roz-e-jaza ke baare mein kaun joothla sakta hai.

8. Kya Khuda sab se bada haakim aur faisla karne waala nahi hai.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.

1. I swear by the fig and the olive,

2. And mount Sinai,

3. And this city made secure,

4. Certainly We created man in the best make.

5. Then We render him the lowest of the low.

6. Except those who believe and do good, so they shall have a reward never to be cut off.

7. Then who can give you the lie after (this) about the judgment?

8. Is not Allah the best of the Judges?
