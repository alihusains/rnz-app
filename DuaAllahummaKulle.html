بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَللّٰھُمَّ كُنْ لِّوَلِیِّكَ الْحُجَّۃِ بْنِ الْحَسَنِ صَلَوَاتُكَ عَلَیْہِ وَعَلٰی آبَآئِہٖ فِیْ ھٰذِہِ السَّاعَۃِ وَفِیْ كُلِّ سَاعَۃٍ وَّلِیًّا وَّحَافِظًا وَّقَآئِدًا وَّنَاصِرًا وَّدَلِیْلًا وَّعَیْنًا حَتّٰی تُسْكِنَہٗ اَرْضَكَ طَوْعًا وَّتُمَتِّعَہٗ فِیْھَا طَوِیْلًا۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

ALLAHUMMA KULLE

Salaamati Imam-e-Zamana a.t.f.s)

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Ma’bood! Tu mohaafiz (protector) ban ja, apne Wali Hazrat Hujjat ibnil Hasan Al-Askari (a.t.f.s) ka. Teri rehmat naazil ho unpar aur unke Aaba-o-Ajdaad par, is waqt aur har aane waale waqt mein unka sarprast (guardian), nigheh-baan (care-taker), peshwa (leader), madad-gaar (helper), rehnuma (guide) aur nigheh-daar (dekh-bhaal karne waala) ban ja. Yahan tak keh Tu logo ki chaahat se unhe zameen ki hukumat de aur muddato uspar bar-qaraar rakkhe.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Mohammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aale paak (a.s) par.

FARAJ
ALLAHUMMA KULLE

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah! Be for Your servant, Hujjat bin al-Hasan, Your blessings be upon him and upon his forefathers, in this time and all times, a Master, a Protector, a Leader, a Helper, a Guide and a Guardian, till You make him dwell in Your earth and provide him with a long life in it.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
