1) 100 Rak’at Namaz, 2x2 rakat kar ke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 3 martaba Surah Ikhalaas (Chp.112) padhe. Namaz ke baad har ek zikr 70 martaba padhe…

سَبْحَانَ اللهِ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلهِ وَلَا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا اللهُ وَاللهُ اَكْبَرُ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ اِلَّا بِاللهِ الْعَلِيِّ الْعَظِيْمِ۔
اَسْتَغْفِرُ اللهَ رَبِّيْ وَاَتُوْبُ اِلَيْهِ۔

2) Pray 4 Rakat Namaz, in the form of 2 rakat, in every rakat, after reciting Surah Fateha (Chp.1), recite Aayaatul Kursi, Surah Ikhalaas (Chp.112), Surah Falaq (Chp.113), Surah Naas (Chp.114), 10 times each. After the salaam again recite Surah Ikhalaas (Chp.112) 100 times.

2) 4 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat kar ke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad Aayatul Kursi, Surah Ikhalaas (Chp.112), Surah Falaq (Chp.113) aur Surah Naas (Chp.114) har ek 10 martaba padhe. Namaz ke baad 100 martaba Surah Ikhalaas (Chp.112) padhe.

3) Pray 4 rak’at Namaz, in the form of 2 rakat, in every rakat, after the recitation of Surah Fateha (Chp.1), recite Surah Ikhalaas (Chp.112) 50 times. (This Namaz is known as Namaz of Ameerul Mo’amineen a.s). After namaz recite Salwaat as many times as possible...

3) 4 Rakat namaz, 2x2 rakat kar ke, har rakat mein Surah Fateha (Chp.1) ke baad 50 martaba Surah Ikhalaas (Chp.112) padhe. Is namaz ko namaz-e-Ameerul Mo’amineen (a.s) kehte hai. Namaz ke baad jitni martaba ho sake’n salwaat padhe…

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

4) Pronounce “La’nat” to condemn and curse the killers of Imam Hussain (a.s) as under…

4) Imam Husain (a.s) ke qaatilo’n par laanat kare…

اَللّٰهُمَّ الْعَنْ قَتَلَةَ الْحُسَیْنِ عَلَیْهِ السَّلَامُ۔

5) Recite Ziyarat Ashura.

5) Ziyarat Ashura padhe.

6) One of the most rewarding aamaal tonight, is Namaz-e-Shab.

SHAB-E-ASHURA

Eve of 10th Muharram

1) Pray 100 Rak’at Namaz, in the form of 2 rakat, in each rak’at, after reciting of Surah Fateha (Chp.1), recite Surah Ikhalaas (Chp.112) 3 times. After namaz, recite the following 70 times…

All glory be to Allah; all praise be to Allah; there is no god save Allah; Allah is the Greatest and there is neither might nor power save with Allah, the All-high the All-great.

I seek the forgiveness of Allah, my Lord, and I repent before Him.

2) Pray 4 Rakat Namaz, in the form of 2 rakat, in every rakat, after reciting Surah Fateha (Chp.1), recite Aayaatul Kursi, Surah Ikhalaas (Chp.112), Surah Falaq (Chp.113), Surah Naas (Chp.114), 10 times each. After the salaam again recite Surah Ikhalaas (Chp.112) 100 times.

3) Pray 4 rak’at Namaz, in the form of 2 rakat, in every rakat, after the recitation of Surah Fateha (Chp.1), recite Surah Ikhalaas (Chp.112) 50 times. (This Namaz is known as Namaz of Ameerul Mo’amineen a.s). After namaz recite Salwaat as many times as possible...

Allah's blessings be upon Mohammed (s.a.w.a) and his holy progeny (a.s).

4) Pronounce “La’nat” to condemn and curse the killers of Imam Hussain (a.s) as under…

O Allah condemn and lay a curse upon the killers of Husain (a.s).

5) Recite Ziyarat Ashura.

6) One of the most rewarding Aamaal tonight, is Namaz-e-Shab.
