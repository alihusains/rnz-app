Imam Husain (a.s)

Shaykh Al-Tusi, in Tahdhib Al-Ahkam and Misbah Al-Mutahajjid, from Safwan Al-Jammal who said: My master Imam al-Sadiq (pbuh), instructed me to visit Imam Husayn (a.s) on the Arbaeen Day, advisably in the early forenoon, and to recite this ziyarat…

Shaykh Al-Tusi apni kitab Tahzib Al-Ahkam aur Misbah Al-Mutahajjid mein, Safwan Al-Jammal se riwayat karte hai keh Imam Sadiq (a.s) ne unhe Arbaeen ko Imam Husain (a.s) ke qabr Mubarak par ja kar yeh ziyarat padhe. Is ko qabl-e-Zohar padhne ki ziyada taaqid hai…

بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰی وَلِیِّ اللہِ وَحَبِیْبِهٖ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰی خَلِیْلِ اللہِ وَنَجِیْبِهٖ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰی صَفِیِّ اللہِ وَ ابْنَ صَفِیِّهٖ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَی الْحُسَیْنِ الْمَظْلُوْمِ الشَّھِیْدِ۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلٰی اَسِیْرِ الْكُرُبَاتِ وَقَتِیْلِ الْعَبَرَاتِ۔ اَللّٰھُمَّ اِنِّیْٓ اَشْھَدُ اَنَّهٗ وَلِیُّكَ وَابْنُ وَلِیِّكَ وَصَفِیُّكَ وَابْنُ صَفِیِّكَ الْفَآئِزُ بِكَرَامَتِكَ اَكْرَمْتَهٗ بِالشَّھَادَۃِ وَحَبَوْتَهٗ بِالسَّعَادَۃِ وَاجْتَبَیْتَهٗ بِطِیْبِ الْوِلَادَۃِ وَجَعَلْتَهٗ سَیِّدًا مِّنَ السَّادَۃِ وَقَآئِدًا مِّنَ الْقَادَۃِ وَذَآئِدًا مِّنَ الذَّادَۃِ وَاَعْطَیْتَهٗ مَوَارِیْثَ الْاَنْبِیَّآءِ وَجَعَلْتَهٗ حُجَّۃً عَلٰی خَلْقِكَ مِنَ الْاَوْصِیَآءِ فَاعْذَرَ فِیْ الدُّعَآءِ وَمَنَحَ النُّصْحَ وَبَذَلَ مُھْجَتَهٗ فِیْكَ لِیَسْتَنْقِذَ عِبَادَكَ مِنَ الْجَھَالَۃِ وَحَیْرَۃِ الضَّلَا لَۃِ وَقَدْ تَوَازَرَ عَلَیْهِ مَنْ غَرَّتْهُ الدُّنْیَا وَبَاعَ حَظَّهٗ بِالْاَرْذَلِ الْاَدْنٰی وَشَرٰی اٰخِرَتَهٗ بِالثَّمَنِ الْاَوْكَسِ وَتَغْطَرَسَ وَتَرَدّٰی فِیْ ھَوَاهُ وَاَسْخَطَكَ وَاَسْخَطَ نَبِیَّكَ وَاَطَاعَ مِنْ عِبَادِكَ اَھْلَ الشِّقَاقِ وَالنِّفَاقِ وَحَمَلَۃَ الْاَوْزَارِ الْمُسْتَوْجِبِیْنَ النَّارَ فَجَاھَدَھُمْ فِیْكَ صَابِرًا مُّحْتَسِبًا حَتّٰی سُفِكَ فِیْ طَاعَتِكَ دَمُهٗ وَاسْتُبِیْحَ حَرِیْمُهٗ۔ اَللّٰهُمَّ فَالْعَنْهُمْ لَعْنًا وَّبِیْلًا وَّعَذِّبْهُمْ عَذَابًا اَلِیْمًا۔ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ رَسُوْلِ اللہِ اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَیْكَ یَابْنَ سَیِّدِ الْاَوْصِیَآءِ اَشْهَدُ اَنَّكَ اَمِیْنُ اللہِ وَابْنُ اَمِیْنِهٖ عِشْتَ سَعِیْدًا وَّمَضَیْتَ حَمِیْدًا وَّمُتَّ فَقِیْدًا مَّظْلُوْمًا شَهِیْدًا وَّاَشْهَدُ اَنَّ اللہَ مُنْجِزٌ مَّا وَعَدَكَ وَمُهْلِكٌ مَّنْ خَذَلَكَ وَمُعَذِّبٌ مَّنْ قَتَلَكَ وَاَشْهَدُ اَنَّكَ وَفَیْتَ بِعَهْدِ اللہِ وَجَاهَدْتَ فِیْ سَبِیْلِهٖ حَتّٰی اَتٰاكَ الْیَقِیْنُ فَلَعَنَ اللہُ مَنْ قَتَلَكَ وَلَعَنَ اللہُ مَنْ ظَلَمَكَ وَلَعَنَ اللہُ اُمَّۃً سَمِعَتْ بِذٰلِكَ فَرَضِیَتْ بِهٖ۔ اَللّٰهُمَّ اِنِّیْ اُشْهِدُكَ اَنِّیْ وَلِیٌّ لِّمَنْ وَالَاهُ وَعَدُوٌّ لِّمَنْ عَادَاهُ بِاَبِیْ اَنْتَ وَاُمِّیْ یَابْنَ رَسُوْلِ اللہِ۔ اَشْهَدُ اَنَّكَ كُنْتَ نُوْرًا فِی الْاَصْلَابِ الشَّامِخَۃِ وَالْاَرْحَامِ الْمُطَهَّرَۃِ لَمْ تُنَجِّسْكَ الْجَاهِلِیَّۃُ بِاَنْجَاسِهَا وَلَمْ تُلْبِسْكَ الْمُدْلَهِمَّاتُ مِنْ ثِیَابِهَا وَاَشْهَدُ اَنَّكَ مِنْ دَعَآئِمِ الدِّیْنِ وَاَرْكَانِ الْمُسْلِمِیْنَ وَمَعْقِلِ الْمُؤْمِنِیْنَ وَاَشْهَدُ اَنَّكَ الْاِمَامُ الْبَرُّ التَّقِیُّ الرَّضِیُّ الزَّكِیُّ الْهَادِی الْمَهْدِیُّ وَاَشْهَدُ اَنَّ الْاَئِمَّۃَ مِنْ وُلْدِكَ كَلِمَۃُ التَّقْوٰی وَاَعْلَامُ الْهُدٰی وَالْعُرْوَۃُ الْوُثْقٰی وَالْحُجَّۃُ عَلٰی اَهْلِ الدُّنْیَا وَاَشْهَدُ اَنِّیْ بِكُمْ مُؤْمِنٌ وَّبِاِیَابِكُمْ مُّوْقِنٌۢ بِشَرَایِعِ دِیْنِیْ وَخَوَاتِیْمِ عَمَلِیْ وَقَلْبِیْ لِقَلْبِكُمْ سِلْمٌ وَّاَمْرِیْ لِاَمْرِكُمْ مُّتَّبِعٌ وَّنُصْرَتِیْ لَكُمْ مُّعَدَّۃٌ حَتّٰی یَاْذَنَ اللہُ لَكُمْ فَمَعَكُمْ لَا مَعَ عَدُوِّكُمْ صَلَوَاتُ اللہِ عَلَیْكُمْ وَعَلٰی اَرْوَاحِكُمْ وَاَجْسَادِكُمْ وَشَاهِدِكُمْ وَغَآئِبِكُمْ وَظَاهِرِكُمْ وَبَاطِنِكُمْ اٰمِیْنَ رَبَّ الْعَالَمِیْنَ۔

اَللّٰھُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّاٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ۔

You may then offer 2 rakat namaz and then pray for your needs.

ARBAEEN

Imam Hussain (a.s)

Shaykh Al-Tusi apni kitab Tahzib Al-Ahkam aur Misbah Al-Mutahajjid mein, Safwan Al-Jammal se riwayat karte hai keh Imam Sadiq (a.s) ne unhe Arbaeen ko Imam Husain (a.s) ke qabr mubarak par ja kar yeh ziyarat padhe. Is ko qabl-e-Zohar padhne ki ziyada taaqid hai…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Salaam ho Allah ke wali o habib par. Salaam ho Allah ke Khalil aur bar-guzida par. Salaam ho Allah ke mukhlis aur mukhlis ke farzand par. Salaam ho Husain-e-mazloom shahid par. Salaam ho aseer, ranj o gham aur kushta-e-girya pat.

Khudaya mai gawahi deta hu keh yeh Tera wali aur Tere wali ka laal (beta) tha. Tera mukhlis aur Tere mukhlis ka laal tha aur Teri karamat par faayez tha aur Tu ne usey shahadat ki karamat ataa ki hai aur sa’adat se nawaza hai uski wiladat ko Tayyab o taahir banaya aur usey sardaro’n mein sardar aur qaaedo (rehbaro’n) qaaid (rehbar) banaya aur difa karne waalo’n mein difa karne waala banaya, usey ambiya ki miraaz di hai aur tamaam makhulqat par apni hujjat bana diya hai, us ne daawat dene ka haq adaa kiya, logo’n ko nasihat ki aur Teri raah mein apna khoon-e-dil de diya ta keh Tere bando’n ko jehaalat aur zalaalat ki hairaani se nikaal le. Magar logo’n ne uske khilaaf ijtema kar liye aur un ko duniya ne behka diya aur apne hissa-e-aakherat ko nehaayat hi adna moaweza par bechh diya aur aakherat ko maamuli qimat par farokht kar diya aur khwaaishat mein halaak ho gaye.

Tujhe aur Tere nabi ko naaraaz kiya aur Tere bando’n mein ahle sheqaaq o nefaaq ki ita’at kar li jo apna bojh uthaane waale aur Jahannam ke haq-daar thay. Tere Husain ne Teri raah mein sabr ke saath jihad kiya yahan tak-keh Teri ita’at mein unka khoon baha diya gaya aur un ki hurmat ko halaal samajh liya gaya. Khudaaya unke qaatilo’n par sakht laanat aur dard-naak azaab naazil farma.

Salaam ho aap par ay farzand-e-Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a). Salaam ho aap par ay farzand-e-Sayyadul Ausiya. Mai gawahi deta hu keh aap Allah ke ameen hai aur amin-e-Khuda ke farzand hai. Aap ki zindagi saeed aur aap ki maut qaabil-e-taarif thi aur aap duniya se shahid o mazloom gaye. Mai gawahi deta hu keh Allah aap ke wade ko pura karne waala aur aap ko chhod ne waalo’n ko halaak karne waala aur aap ke qaatilo’n par azaab naazil karne waala hai aur mai gawahi deta hu keh aap ne ahed-e-Khuda ko pura kiya, raahe Khuda mein jihad kiya yahan tak-keh maut aa gayi.

Allah aap ke qaatilo’n par, aap ke zaalimo par aur is haadse ko soon kar raazi ho jaane waalo’n par laanat kare. Khudaaya mai Tujhe gawah qaraar deta hu keh mai in ke dost ka dost aur inke dushman ka dushman hu. Mere maa-baap aap par qurbaan ay farzand-e-Rasool-e-Khuda (s.a.w.a). Mai gawahi deta hu kea ap buland tareen aslaab (sulbo’n) aur paakeeza tareen arhaam (rahemo) mein noor-e-Khuda thay. Jaahiliyat ki najaasat aap ko chu nahi saki aur uski taarikiyo’n ke libaas aap ko dhaanp nahi sake’n.

Mai gawahi deta hu keh aap deen ke sutoono aur musalmaano ke arkaan aur monenin ki panah-gaaho’n mein se hai. Mai gawahi deta hu keh aap Imam nek kirdaar, parhez-gaar, pasandida sifaat, paakeeza aur haadi o Mahdi hai. Mai gawahi deta hu keh aap ki aulaad ke aimma kalma-e-taqwa, parcham-e-hidaayat, resmaan-e-mohkam-e-imaan aur ahle duniya par Allah ki hujjat hai aur mai gawahi deta hu keh mein aap par imaan rakhta hu aur aap ki raj’at (palatne) ka yaqeen rakhta hu.

Apne deen ke aayin aur apne anjaam-e-kaar ke saath mera dil aap ke dil ke liye taslim hai aur mera amr aap ke awaamir ka taabe hai aur meri nusrat aap ke liye haazir hai yahan tak-keh Khuda sab ka faisla kar de. Mai aap ke saath hu, aap ke saath hu na keh aap ke dushman ke saath. Allah ki salwaat aap par, aap ke arwaah o ajsaam par, aap ke shahid o ghaayab par aur aap ke zaahir o baatin par ho, aameen ya rabbal aalameen.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par.

Phir 2 rakat namaz padhe aur Parwardigaar se apni haajaat talab kare.

ARBAEEN

Imam Hussain (a.s)

Shaykh Al-Tusi, in Tahdhib Al-Ahkam and Misbah Al-Mutahajjid, from Safwan Al-Jammal who said: My master Imam al-Sadiq (pbuh), instructed me to visit Imam Husayn (a.s) on the Arbaeen Day, advisably in the early forenoon, and to recite this ziyarat…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Peace be on the favourite of Allah, His dearest darling! Peace be on the beloved friend of Allah, His distinguished hero! Peace be on the choicest confidant of Allah, sincerely attached precisely like his father! Peace be on Husayn, who established the true faith by giving his life in the way of Allah, a martyr, underwent untold hardships at the hand of rouges and lawbreakers! Peace be on the hostage surrounded by the tightening circle of sorrow and grief, killed by a horde of savages. O my Allah I give witness that beyond a shadow of doubt he is Thy favourite and choicest confidant, who enjoys Thy confidence and favour, precisely like his father! Thou looked upto him and elected him in Thy cause, picked and chose him for the good fortune, selected for him the best purified parents, appointed him guardian, leader, and defender of rights, a true representative (inheritor and progenitor) of guardians, leaders and defenders of rights, gave him much and more from the inheritance of the Prophets, put him forward as a decisive argument, along with the other successors (of the Holy Prophet-the twelve Imaams) to the mankind. He met with deadly dangers, acted justly and fairly, made use of everything belonging to him to pay full attention to give sincere advice; took pains, made every effort, and put his heart, mind, soul and life at the disposal of Thy mission to liberate the people from the yoke of ignorance and evil of bewilderment, but an evildoer, deceived with empty hopes of mean and worthless worldly gains, had pressed heavily on him, and sold out his share (eternal bliss) for the meanest and lowest bargain, betrayed his "day of judgement" for a vulgar return, took pride in insolence, fell into the fathom well of silly stupid follies, provoked Thee and Thy Prophet to anger, did as the harsh discordant, the hypocrite, the heavily burdened bearers of sin, condemned to Hellfire, advised to him, however, he (the Holy Imam), steadily, rightly and justly coped with them, till, in Thy obedience, gave his life after which his family was set adrift. O my Allah, therefore, condemn them to hell as a denunciation and conviction; and crack down on them with a painful punishment. Peace be on you O the son of the Messenger of Allah! Peace be on you O the son of the first of the successors (of the Holy Prophet)! I bear witness that Allah put faith in you like He had full confidence in your father, and that you always looked for and collected good and virtue, lived a highly praiseworthy life, and departed from this world a martyr, forsaken and abused; I bear witness that Allah will promptly fulfil the promise He made to you, and destroy those who left you helpless and punish those who killed you; I bear witness that you kept your promise made with Allah, and strived in His way till what was certain came upon you, so curse of Allah be on those who killed you, curse of Allah be on those who oppressed you, curse of Allah be on the people who came to know and approved (of it all). O my Allah be my witness that I make friends with those who love him and oppose those who deny him. I, my father and mother, are at your disposal O the son of the Messenger of Allah. I know and bear witness that you were "light" in the sublime loins and in the pure wombs, never touched you the dirt of ignorance, nor ever obscurity concealed you in its folds; I bear witness that you are the pillar of "Deen", support of the Muslims, refuge of the faithful’s; I bear witness that you are a truthful, well-aware, content, intelligent, rightly guided guide (Imam); I bear witness that the Imams among your descendants are the symbols of "conscious piety" and signs of "true guidance", the "safe handle"-Islam, and the decisive arguments over mankind; I declare positively that I have full faith in you, and I know for certain that you shall return. I am, fully committed to the laws of my religion, certain of my deeds, my mind and heart ready for your return, and my affairs carried out in the light of your instructions, till Allah gives you permission, together with you, along with you, not at the same time with your enemies. Blessings of Allah be on you, on your souls, on your bodies, when you are visible, when you are invisible, on your perceivable aspects, on your innermost genius, be it so, O Lord of the worlds!

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

You may then offer 2 rakat namaz and then pray for your needs.
