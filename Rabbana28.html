Chp. 18:Ver. 10
بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ
رَبَّنَآ اٰتِنَا مِنْ لَّدُنْكَ رَحْمَةً وَّهَیِّءْ لَنَا مِنْ اَمْرِنَا رَشَدًا۔
Our Lord! Grant us mercy from Thee, and provide for us a right course in our affair.

Parwardigaar! Ham ko apni rehmat ataa farma aur hamare liye hamare kaam mein kaam-yaabi ka saamaan faraaham kar de.
