Eve of Friday

Holy Prophet (s.a.w.a) said, “Verily, Friday is the master of days. On Fridays, Almighty Allah doubles up the rewards (for good deeds), erases, forgivingly, the punishments (for evildoings), raises the ranks (of faith), responds to prayers, relieves from misfortunes, and fulfills the grand requests.

Hazrat Rasool Khuda (s.a.w.a) ne farmaya, Yaqeenan Juma dino ka sardar hai. Juma ke din Allah (s.w.t) (amaale khair ke) sawaab ko dugna, gunaho ki maghferat, imaan ki bulandi, Dua’o ki qubooliyat, pareshani se najaat aur nek khwahishaat ko pura karta hai.

SHAB-E-JUMA

Hazrat Rasool Khuda (s.a.w.a) ne farmaya, Yaqeenan Juma dino ka sardar hai. Juma ke din Allah (s.w.t) (amaale khair ke) sawaab ko dugna, gunaho ki maghferat, imaan ki bulandi, Dua’o ki qubooliyat, pareshani se najaat aur nek khwahishaat ko pura karta hai.

Shab ke Amaal…

1) Shaikh Saduq riwaayat karte hai keh, Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) ne farmaya keh apni shab-e-Juma ki waajib namazo’n mein Surah Juma (Chp.62) aur Surah Aala (Chp.87) padha karo.

2) Musalsal yeh Zikr dauraaya kare…

Paak hai Khuda. Allah buzurg-tar hai. Nahi koi Mabood siwaye Allah ke.

3) Yeh Zikr bhi jitni martaba ho sake’n dauraaye…

Ay Mabood! Rehmat farma Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par aur unke Zahoor mein taajil (jaldi) farma aur halaak kar Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Muhammad (a.s) ke dushmano ko, jinnato’n aur insaano se awwalin aur aakherin se.

4) Is maghferat ki dua ko padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Us Khuda se taalib-e-maghferat hu jiske siwa koi Mabood nahi jo zinda o paayenda hai mai uske huzoor aise bande ki tarah tauba karta hu jo zaleel, mohtaj aur be-chaara hai. Jo apne kaar o baar aur nafa o nuqsaan ka maalik nahi. Apni maut, zindagi aur Qayamat ke din apne hashr o nashr par kuch ikhteyaar nahi rakhta aur Khuda ki rehmat ho Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki paak o paakeeza aur nek o paak Aal (a.s) par aur salaam ho jo salaam ka haq hai.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par.

6) Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) se taaqid hai keh is zikr ko Shab-e-Juma ki Namaz-e-Isha ke aakhri sajdeh mein 7 martaba padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Khuda-wanda! Mai Teri zaat-e-karim aur Tere bar-tar naam ke waaste se sawaal karta hu keh Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Muhammad (a.s) par rehmat naazil farma aur mere bade-bade gunaho’n ko bakhsh de.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par.

7) Hazrat Rasool Khuda (s.a.w.a) se riwaayat hai keh, jo shakhs Shab-e-Juma mein 7 martaba is dua ko padhe to usey Jannat mein jaane ko milega agar woh us shab mein intekaal kar gaya. Aur jo Juma ke deen 7 martaba padhe to usey bhi Jannat mein jaane ko milega agar woh us deen intekaal kar gaya…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Tu hi mere Rab hai, Tere siwa koi Mabood nahi, Tu ne hi mujhe paida kiya aur mai Tera banda aur Teri kaneez ka beta hu aur Tere qaabu mein hu. Meri mehaar Tere dast-e-qudrat mein hai. Mai apni taaqat ke mutabiq Tere ahed o paimaan par qaayem raha hu jo buraayi karta hu us mein Teri raza ki panah chahta hu. Teri neymat ka baar (wazn) aur mere gunah ka bojh mere kandeh (shoulder) par hai. Pas mere gunah maaf kar de keh Tere siwa koi gunaho’n ko maaf nahi kar sakta.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par.

9) Dua-e-Kumail padhe.

10) Dua Allahumma Ya Shahida padhe.

11) Is dua ko 10 martaba padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay woh Zaat jiska ehsaan hamesha makhluq par hai. Ay woh jiske dono haath ataa ke liye khule hai. Ay badi-badi neymate’n dene waale Khuda-wand! Muhammad (s.a.w.a) wa Aale Muhammad (a.s) par rehmat farma jo makhluqaat mein sabse behtar hai aur ay bulandiyo’n ke Maalik! Is raat mere gunah bakhsh de.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par.

Mai apne Rab se taalib-e-maghferat hu aur uske huzoor tauba karta hu.

14) Is dua ko 1 martaba padhe…

(Shuru karta hu) Allah ke naam se Jo bada maher-baan aur rahem karne waala hai.

Ay Mabood! Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Muhammad (a.s) par rehmat farma. Aaj ki subah (morning) mujhe apni reza ataa farma. Mere dil ko apne khauf se bhar de aur usey apne gair se hata de, hatta keh woh Tere siwa kisi se ummeed aur khauf na rakkhe.

Ay Khudaaya! Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Muhammad (a.s) par rehmat farma aur mujhe yaqeen mein pukhtagi (mazbooti) ataa farma. Mujhe khuloos o akhlaas de, yekta parasti ka sharaf bakhsh de, hamesha ki saabit-qadami se nawaz aur sabr o reza ka khazaana ataa kar, keh jis se qaza o qadr par raazi rahu.

Ay saayelo’n (sawaal karne waalo’n) ki haajaat bar-laane (puri karne) waale! Ay woh jo khaamosh rehne waalo’n ke mudda’a (maqsad) ko jaanta hai. Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Muhammad (a.s) par rehmat farma aur meri dua qabool kar, mere gunah bakhsh de, mere rizq mein wus’at paida kar aur meri, mere deeni bhaiyo’n aur mere ahle khaandaan ki haajaat puri farma. Khudaaya! Teri madad ke bagair badi-badi ummeede’n na-tamaam aur buland himmate’n be-faaida o be-kaar ho jaati hai. Teri taraf tawajjoh ke bagair aqlo’n ki julaaniya (taaqat) na-rasa rahe jaati hai.

Pas Tu hi ummeed aur Tu hi panah-gah hai. Ay buzurg-tar Mabood aur sakhi-tar daata, ay panah lene waalo’n ki panah-gah mai apne gunaho’n ke bojh ke saath Teri taraf bhaaga huwa aaya hu. Tere huzoor mera koi sifaarishi nahi siwaye us maarefat ke, keh Tu ahle haajat ki ummeedo’n ke bohot qareeb hai aur raghbat karne waalo’n ko dhaaras deta hai. Ay woh jisne aqlo’n ko apni maarefat ke liye khola, zabaano ko apni hamd par rawa kiya aur bando’n ko apne haq ki adaayi ki himmat de kar un par ehsaan-e-azeem farmaya hai. Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur Aale Muhammad (a.s) par rehmat farma aur shaitaan ko meri aql mein daraane ki raah na de aur baatil ko mere amal o kirdaar mein daakhil na hone de.

Parwardigaar Tu rehmat naazil farma Hazrat Muhammad (s.a.w.a) aur unki Aal (a.s) par.

SHAB-E-JUMA

Eve of Friday

Holy Prophet (s.a.w.a) said, “Verily, Friday is the master of days. On Fridays, Almighty Allah doubles up the rewards (for good deeds), erases, forgivingly, the punishments (for evildoings), raises the ranks (of faith), responds to prayers, relieves from misfortunes, and fulfills the grand requests.

1) Shaikh Saduq has narrated that Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) said that one should recite Surah (Chp.62) and Surah Aala (Chp.87) in the obligatory prayers at Friday nights.

2) Repeat very often the following Zikr…

All Glory be to Allah. Allah is the Most Great. There is no god save Allah.

3) Also repeat the following Zikr as many times you can…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, send blessings to Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad, hasten their Relief, and destroy their enemies from both jinn and human beings, both the foremost and the later ones.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

4) One must recite the below forgiveness dua…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

I pray the forgiveness of Allah; there is no god but He, the Ever-living, the Self-Subsisting, and I repent to Him as a slave who is submissive, acquiescent, and poor, not controlling for himself any harm or profit, or benefit or damage and not controlling death nor life, nor raising (the dead) to life. May Allah send peace upon Muhammad and his Household; the pure, the immaculate, the choicest, and the pious; and may He spread thorough blessings over them.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

5) It is recommended to recite the following chapters (Surah) of the Holy Qur’an:

a) Surah Bani Israeel (Chp.17)

b) Surah Kahf (Chp.18)

c) Surah Sho’ara (Chp.26)

d) Surah Naml (Chp.27)

e) Surah Qasas (Chp.28)

f) Surah Sajdah (Chp.32)

g) Surah Yaasin (Chp.36)

h) Surah Saad (Chp.38)

k) Surah Fusselat (Chp.41)

l) Surah Dukhaan (Chp.44)

i) Surah Ahqaaf (Chp.46)

m) Surah Toor (Chp.52)

n) Surah Qamar (No.54)

j) Surah Waqea (Chp.56)

o) Surah Jum’a (No.62)

6) It is recommended by Imam Jafar Sadiq (a.s) to recite below zikr in the last sajdah of Namaz-e-Isha on Shab-e-Juma and repeats it 7 times…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, I beseech You in the name of Your Noble Face and in the name of Your Sublime Name to send blessings to Muhammad and his Household and to forgive me my grave sin.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

7) The Holy Prophet (s.a.w.s) is reported to have said that one who repeats the following dua 7 times at Friday night will be allowed Paradise if he departs life at that night; and one who repeats it 7 times on Friday will be also allowed Paradise if he departs life on that day…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, You are my Lord; there is no god save You; You have created me, and I am Your slave and the son of Your bondmaid; and I am in Your grip; and my forehead is in Your Hand; I come to observe my covenant and promise to You as much as I can. I take refuge in Your pleasure against the evil of my deeds. I acknowledge Your favors and confess of my sins. So, (please) forgive my sins, for none can forgive sins save You.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

8) It is recommended to recite Allahumma Man Ta’abaa.

9) Recite Dua-e-Kumail.

10) Recite dua Allahumma Ya Shahida.

11) Recite this dua 10 times…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O He Who is ever-Favorer on the beings! O He Who is openhandedly Bestower of gifts! O He Who is the Giver of sublime donations! Bless Muhammad and his Household, the best of all creatures in traits, and forgive us, O Lord of Highness, at this evening.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

12) Following Imam Ja’far Sadiq (a.s) it is recommended to have pomegranate at Friday nights.

13) Recite the following zikr 100 times each…

All glory be to Allah, the All-great and with the Praising of Him.

I pray the forgiveness of Allah, my Lord, and I turn repentant to Him.

14) Recite this dua once…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, send blessings to Muhammad and his Household, grant me Your satisfaction this very early morning, place fear of You in my heart, and separate it from (leaning to) anyone other than You, so that I will hope for and fear none save You.

O Allah, send blessings to Muhammad and his Household and grant me resolute faith, pure sincerity (to You), honorable belief in (Your) oneness, persistent straightness, essential tolerance, and satisfaction with (Your) predetermination and decrees.

O He Who settles the needs of His beseechers; O He Who knows what is in the innermost of the silent; send blessings to Muhammad and his Household, respond to my prayers, forgive my sins, expand my means of livelihood, and settle the needs of myself and my brethren-in-faith and my family members.

O Allah, all ambitions of expectations are disappointed except those addressed to You, all resolute determinations are paralyzed except those relied upon You, and all reason-based tendencies are wasted except those directed to You. So, You are the ultimate hope and to You is the resort.

O He Who is the noblest objective and the most magnanimous desired, I am fleeing to You from myself. O shelter of those who flee overburdened with the heavy loads of sins, carrying my sins on my back, I cannot find any interceder for me except that I know for sure that You are the nearest of all those who are hoped by beseechers and of all those whose donations are expected by the desirous.

O He Who has split the intellects on the recognition of Him, made tongues articulate with the praising of Him and made that which He has conferred upon His servants enough means for the fulfillment of their duties towards Him; (please) send blessings to Muhammad and his Household, do not give Satan any way (of success) over my reason, and do not give wrongness a way to reach my deeds.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.

15) Recite below zikr before the Dawn (Fajr) Prayer on Friday…

I pray the forgiveness of Allah save Whom there is no god; the Ever-living and Ever-subsisting, and I turn in repentance to Him.

16) During Friday dawns, it is recommended to say the following…

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

O Allah, whatever saying I have uttered this week, whatever oath I have taken during it, and whatever vow I have made during it, verily, it is Your will that is before all that. Hence, only that which You will to have effect will come to pass and whatever You do not will to have effect will never come to pass.

O Allah, (please) forgive me and overlook my offenses. O Allah, whosoever You bless, then I invoke Your blessings upon him and whosoever You curse, then I invoke Your curse upon him.

Allah's (s.w.t) choicest blessings be upon Mohammed and his holy progeny.
