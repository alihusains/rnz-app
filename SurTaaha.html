بِسْمِ اللہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ

طٰهٰﯜ1ﯛج مَآ اَنْزَلْنَا عَلَيْكَ الْقُرْاٰنَ لِتَشْقٰىﯜ2ﯛلا اِلَّا تَذْكِرَةً لِّمَنْ يَّخْشٰىﯜ3ﯛلا تَنْزِيْلًا مِّمَّنْ خَلَقَ الْاَرْضَ وَالسَّمٰوٰتِ الْعُلٰىﯜ4ﯛط اَلرَّحْمٰنُ عَلَى الْعَرْشِ اسْتَوٰىﯜ5ﯛ لَهٗ مَا فِي السَّمٰوٰتِ وَمَا فِي الْاَرْضِ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا وَمَا تَحْتَ الثَّرٰىﯜ6ﯛ وَاِنْ تَجْهَرْ بِالْقَوْلِ فَاِنَّهٗ يَعْلَمُ السِّرَّ وَاَخْفٰىﯜ7ﯛ اَللهُ لَٓا اِلٰهَ اِلَّا هُوَط لَهُ الْاَسْمَاۗءُ الْحُسْنٰىﯜ8ﯛ وَهَلْ اَتٰكَ حَدِيْثُ مُوْسٰىﯜ9ﯛ اِذْ رَاٰى نَارًا فَقَالَ لِاَهْلِهِ امْكُثُوْٓا اِنِّيْٓ اٰنَسْتُ نَارًا لَّعَلِّيْٓ اٰتِيْكُمْ مِّنْهَا بِقَبَسٍ اَوْ اَجِدُ عَلَى النَّارِ هُدًىﯜ10ﯛ فَلَمَّآ اَتٰهَا نُوْدِيَ يٰمُوْسٰىﯜ11ﯛ اِنِّيْٓ اَنَا رَبُّكَ فَاخْلَعْ نَعْلَيْكَج اِنَّكَ بِالْوَادِ الْمُقَدَّسِ طُوًىﯜ12ﯛ وَاَنَا اخْتَرْتُكَ فَاسْتَمِعْ لِمَا يُوْحٰىﯜ13ﯛ اِنَّنِيْٓ اَنَا اللهُ لَٓا اِلٰهَ اِلَّآ اَنَا فَاعْبُدْنِيْلا وَاَقِمِ الصَّلٰوةَ لِذِكْرِيْﯜ14ﯛ اِنَّ السَّاعَةَ اٰتِيَةٌ اَكَادُ اُخْفِيْهَا لِتُجْزٰى كُلُّ نَفْسٍۢ بِمَا تَسْعٰىﯜ15ﯛ فَلَا يَصُدَّنَّكَ عَنْهَا مَنْ لَّا يُؤْمِنُ بِهَا وَاتَّبَعَ هَوٰهُ فَتَرْدٰىﯜ16ﯛ وَمَا تِلْكَ بِيَمِيْنِكَ يٰمُوْسٰىﯜ17ﯛ قَالَ هِيَ عَصَايَج اَتَوَكَّؤُاعَلَيْهَا وَاَهُشُّ بِهَا عَلٰى غَنَمِيْ وَلِيَ فِيْهَا مَاٰرِبُ اُخْرٰىﯜ18ﯛ قَالَ اَلْقِهَا يٰمُوْسٰىﯜ19ﯛ فَاَلْقٰهَا فَاِذَا هِيَ حَيَّةٌ تَسْعٰىﯜ20ﯛ قَالَ خُذْهَا وَلَا تَخَفْوقفۃ سَنُعِيْدُهَا سِيْرَتَهَا الْاُوْلٰىﯜ21ﯛ وَاضْمُمْ يَدَكَ اِلٰى جَنَاحِكَ تَخْرُجْ بَيْضَاۗءَ مِنْ غَيْرِ سُوْۗءٍ اٰيَةً اُخْرٰىﯜ22ﯛلا لِنُرِيَكَ مِنْ اٰيٰتِنَا الْكُبْرٰىﯜ23ﯛج اِذْهَبْ اِلٰى فِرْعَوْنَ اِنَّهٗ طَغٰىﯜ24ﯛع قَالَ رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِيْ صَدْرِيْﯜ25ﯛلا وَيَسِّرْ لِيْٓ اَمْرِيْﯜ26ﯛلا وَاحْلُلْ عُقْدَةً مِّنْ لِّسَانِيْﯜ27ﯛلا يَفْقَهُوْا قَوْلِيْﯜ28ﯛ وَاجْعَلْ لِّيْ وَزِيْرًا مِّنْ اَهْلِيْﯜ29ﯛلا هٰرُوْنَ اَخِيﯜ30ﯛلا اشْدُدْ بِهٖٓ اَزْرِيْﯜ31ﯛ وَاَشْرِكْهُ فِيْٓ اَمْرِيْﯜ32ﯛلا كَيْ نُسَبِّحَكَ كَثِيْرًاﯜ33ﯛلا وَّنَذْكُرَكَ كَثِيْرًاﯜ34ﯛ اِنَّكَ كُنْتَ بِنَا بَصِيْرًاﯜ35ﯛ قَالَ قَدْ اُوْتِيْتَ سُؤْلَكَ يٰمُوْسٰىﯜ36ﯛ وَلَقَدْ مَنَنَّا عَلَيْكَ مَرَّةً اُخْرٰٓىﯜ37ﯛلا اِذْ اَوْحَيْنَآ اِلٰٓى اُمِّكَ مَا يُوْحٰٓىﯜ38ﯛ اَنِ اقْذِفِيْهِ فِي التَّابُوْتِ فَاقْذِفِيْهِ فِي الْيَمِّ فَلْيُلْقِهِ الْيَمُّ بِالسَّاحِلِ يَاْخُذْهُ عَدُوٌّ لِّيْ وَعَدُوٌّ لَهٗط وَاَلْقَيْتُ عَلَيْكَ مَحَبَّةً مِّنِّيْ ڬ وَلِتُصْنَعَ عَلٰى عَيْنِيْﯜ39ﯛ اِذْ تَمْشِيْٓ اُخْتُكَ فَتَقُوْلُ هَلْ اَدُلُّكُمْ عَلٰى مَنْ يَّكْفُلُهٗط فَرَجَعْنٰكَ اِلٰٓى اُمِّكَ كَيْ تَقَرَّ عَيْنُهَا وَلَا تَحْزَنَ ڬ وَقَتَلْتَ نَفْسًا فَنَجَّيْنٰكَ مِنَ الْغَمِّ وَفَتَنّٰكَ فُتُوْنًاقف فَلَبِثْتَ سِنِيْنَ فِيْٓ اَهْلِ مَدْيَنَ ڏ ثُمَّ جِئْتَ عَلٰى قَدَرٍ يّٰمُوْسٰىﯜ40ﯛ وَاصْطَنَعْتُكَ لِنَفْسِيْﯜ41ﯛج اِذْهَبْ اَنْتَ وَاَخُوْكَ بِاٰيٰتِيْ وَلَا تَنِيَا فِيْ ذِكْرِيْﯜ42ﯛ اِذْهَبَآ اِلٰى فِرْعَوْنَ اِنَّهٗ طَغٰىﯜ43ﯛ فَقُوْلَا لَهٗ قَوْلًا لَّيِّنًا لَّعَلَّهٗ يَتَذَكَّرُ اَوْ يَخْشٰىﯜ44ﯛ قَالَا رَبَّنَآ اِنَّنَا نَخَافُ اَنْ يَّفْرُطَ عَلَيْنَآ اَوْ اَنْ يَّطْغٰىﯜ45ﯛ قَالَ لَا تَخَافَآ اِنَّنِيْ مَعَكُمَآ اَسْمَعُ وَاَرٰىﯜ46ﯛ فَاْتِيٰهُ فَقُوْلَٓا اِنَّا رَسُوْلَا رَبِّكَ فَاَرْسِلْ مَعَنَا بَنِيْٓ اِسْرَاۗئِيْلَ ڏ وَلَا تُعَذِّبْهُمْط قَدْ جِئْنٰكَ بِاٰيَةٍ مِّنْ رَّبِّكَط وَالسَّلٰمُ عَلٰى مَنِ اتَّبَعَ الْهُدٰىﯜ47ﯛ اِنَّا قَدْ اُوْحِيَ اِلَيْنَآ اَنَّ الْعَذَابَ عَلٰى مَنْ كَذَّبَ وَتَوَلّٰىﯜ48ﯛ قَالَ فَمَنْ رَّبُّكُمَا يٰمُوْسٰىﯜ49ﯛ قَالَ رَبُّنَا الَّذِيْٓ اَعْطٰى كُلَّ شَيْءٍ خَلْقَهٗ ثُمَّ هَدٰىﯜ50ﯛ قَالَ فَمَا بَالُ الْقُرُوْنِ الْاُوْلٰىﯜ51ﯛ قَالَ عِلْمُهَا عِنْدَ رَبِّيْ فِيْ كِتٰبٍج لَا يَضِلُّ رَبِّيْ وَلَا يَنْسَىﯜ52ﯛز الَّذِيْ جَعَلَ لَكُمُ الْاَرْضَ مَهْدًا وَّسَلَكَ لَكُمْ فِيْهَا سُبُلًا وَّاَنْزَلَ مِنَ السَّمَاۗءِ مَاۗءًط فَاَخْرَجْنَا بِهٖٓ اَزْوَاجًا مِّنْ نَّبَاتٍ شَتّٰىﯜ53ﯛ كُلُوْا وَارْعَوْا اَنْعَامَكُمْط اِنَّ فِيْ ذٰلِكَ لَاٰيٰتٍ لِّاُوْلِي النُّهٰىﯜ54ﯛع مِنْهَا خَلَقْنٰكُمْ وَفِيْهَا نُعِيْدُكُمْ وَمِنْهَا نُخْرِجُكُمْ تَارَةً اُخْرٰىﯜ55ﯛ وَلَقَدْ اَرَيْنٰهُ اٰيٰتِنَا كُلَّهَا فَكَذَّبَ وَاَبٰىﯜ56ﯛ قَالَ اَجِئْتَنَا لِتُخْرِجَنَا مِنْ اَرْضِنَا بِسِحْرِكَ يٰمُوْسٰىﯜ57ﯛ فَلَنَاْتِيَنَّكَ بِسِحْرٍ مِّثْلِهٖ فَاجْعَلْ بَيْنَنَا وَبَيْنَكَ مَوْعِدًا لَّا نُخْلِفُهٗ نَحْنُ وَلَٓا اَنْتَ مَكَانًا سُوًىﯜ58ﯛ قَالَ مَوْعِدُكُمْ يَوْمُ الزِّينَةِ وَاَنْ يُّحْشَرَ النَّاسُ ضُحًىﯜ59ﯛ فَتَوَلّٰی فِرْعَوْنُ فَجَمَعَ كَيْدَهٗ ثُمَّ اَتٰیﯜ60ﯛ قَالَ لَهُمْ مُّوْسٰى وَيْلَكُمْ لَا تَفْتَرُوْا عَلَى اللهِ كَذِبًا فَيُسْحِتَكُمْ بِعَذَابٍج وَقَدْ خَابَ مَنِ افْتَرٰىﯜ61ﯛ فَتَنَازَعُوْٓا اَمْرَهُمْ بَيْنَهُمْ وَاَسَرُّوا النَّجْوٰىﯜ62ﯛ قَالُوْٓا اِنْ هٰذٰنِ لَسٰحِرٰنِ يُرِيْدٰنِ اَنْ يُّخْرِجٰكُمْ مِّنْ اَرْضِكُمْ بِسِحْرِهِمَا وَيَذْهَبَا بِطَرِيْقَتِكُمُ الْمُثْلٰىﯜ63ﯛ فَاَجْمِعُوْا كَيْدَكُمْ ثُمَّ ائْتُوْا صَفًّاج وَقَدْ اَفْلَحَ الْيَوْمَ مَنِ اسْتَعْلٰىﯜ64ﯛ قَالُوْا يٰمُوْسٰٓى اِمَّآ اَنْ تُلْقِيَ وَاِمَّآ اَنْ نَّكُوْنَ اَوَّلَ مَنْ اَلْقٰىﯜ65ﯛ قَالَ بَلْ اَلْقُوْاج فَاِذَا حِبَالُهُمْ وَعِصِيُّهُمْ يُخَيَّلُ اِلَيْهِ مِنْ سِحْرِهِمْ اَنَّهَا تَسْعٰىﯜ66ﯛ فَاَوْجَسَ فِيْ نَفْسِهٖ خِيْفَةً مُّوْسٰىﯜ67ﯛ قُلْنَا لَا تَخَفْ اِنَّكَ اَنْتَ الْاَعْلٰىﯜ68ﯛ وَاَلْقِ مَا فِيْ يَمِيْنِكَ تَلْقَفْ مَا صَنَعُوْاط اِنَّمَا صَنَعُوْا كَيْدُ سٰحِرٍط وَلَا يُفْلِحُ السَّاحِرُ حَيْثُ اَتٰیﯜ69ﯛ فَاُلْقِيَ السَّحَرَةُ سُجَّدًا قَالُوْٓا اٰمَنَّا بِرَبِّ هٰرُوْنَ وَمُوْسٰىﯜ70ﯛ قَالَ اٰمَنْتُمْ لَهٗ قَبْلَ اَنْ اٰذَنَ لَكُمْط اِنَّهٗ لَكَبِيْرُكُمُ الَّذِيْ عَلَّمَكُمُ السِّحْرَج فَلَاُقَطِّعَنَّ اَيْدِيَكُمْ وَاَرْجُلَكُمْ مِّنْ خِلَافٍ وَّلَاُصَلِّبَنَّكُمْ فِيْ جُذُوْعِ النَّخْلِز وَلَتَعْلَمُنَّ اَيُّنَآ اَشَدُّ عَذَابًا وَّاَبْقٰىﯜ71ﯛ قَالُوْا لَنْ نُّؤْثِرَكَ عَلٰى مَا جَاۗءَنَا مِنَ الْبَيِّنٰتِ وَالَّذِيْ فَطَرَنَا فَاقْضِ مَآ اَنْتَ قَاضٍط اِنَّمَا تَقْضِيْ هٰذِهِ الْحَيٰوةَ الدُّنْيَاﯜ72ﯛط اِنَّآ اٰمَنَّا بِرَبِّنَا لِيَغْفِرَ لَنَا خَطٰيٰنَا وَمَآ اَكْرَهْتَنَا عَلَيْهِ مِنَ السِّحْرِط وَاللهُ خَيْرٌ وَّاَبْقٰىﯜ73ﯛ اِنَّهٗ مَنْ يَّاْتِ رَبَّهٗ مُجْرِمًا فَاِنَّ لَهٗ جَهَنَّمَط لَا يَمُوْتُ فِيْهَا وَلَا يَحْيٰىﯜ74ﯛ وَمَنْ يَّاْتِهٖ مُؤْمِنًا قَدْ عَمِلَ الصّٰلِحٰتِ فَاُوْلٰۗئِكَ لَهُمُ الدَّرَجٰتُ الْعُلٰىﯜ75ﯛ جَنّٰتُ عَدْنٍ تَجْرِيْ مِنْ تَحْتِهَا الْاَنْهَارُ خٰلِدِيْنَ فِيْهَاط وَذٰلِكَ جَزٰۗؤُ امَنْ تَزَكّٰىﯜ76ﯛع وَلَقَدْ اَوْحَيْنَآ اِلٰى مُوْسٰىلا اَنْ اَسْرِ بِعِبَادِيْ فَاضْرِبْ لَهُمْ طَرِيْقًا فِي الْبَحْرِ يَبَسًالا لَّا تَخٰفُ دَرَكًا وَّلَا تَخْشٰىﯜ77ﯛ فَاَتْبَعَهُمْ فِرْعَوْنُ بِجُنُوْدِهٖ فَغَشِيَهُمْ مِّنَ الْيَمِّ مَا غَشِيَهُمْﯜ78ﯛ وَاَضَلَّ فِرْعَوْنُ قَوْمَهٗ وَمَا هَدٰىﯜ79ﯛ يٰبَنِيْٓ اِسْرَاۗئِيْلَ قَدْ اَنْجَيْنٰكُمْ مِّنْ عَدُوِّكُمْ وَوٰعَدْنٰكُمْ جَانِبَ الطُّوْرِ الْاَيْمَنَ وَنَزَّلْنَا عَلَيْكُمُ الْمَنَّ وَالسَّلْوٰىﯜ80ﯛ كُلُوْا مِنْ طَيِّبٰتِ مَا رَزَقْنٰكُمْ وَلَا تَطْغَوْا فِيْهِ فَيَحِلَّ عَلَيْكُمْ غَضَبِيْج وَمَنْ يَّحْلِلْ عَلَيْهِ غَضَبِيْ فَقَدْ هَوٰىﯜ81ﯛ وَاِنِّيْ لَغَفَّارٌ لِّمَنْ تَابَ وَاٰمَنَ وَعَمِلَ صَالِحًا ثُمَّ اهْتَدٰىﯜ82ﯛ وَمَآ اَعْجَلَكَ عَنْ قَوْمِكَ يٰمُوْسٰىﯜ83ﯛ قَالَ هُمْ اُوْلَۗاءِ عَلٰٓى اَثَرِيْ وَعَجِلْتُ اِلَيْكَ رَبِّ لِتَرْضٰىﯜ84ﯛ قَالَ فَاِنَّا قَدْ فَتَنَّا قَوْمَكَ مِنْۢ بَعْدِكَ وَاَضَلَّهُمُ 20 | TAAHA | 135

Azeem aur daayemi rehmato waale Khuda ke naam se.

1. Taaha

2. Ham ne aap par Qur’an is liye nahi naazil kiya hai ke aap apne ko zehmat mein daal de.

3. Yeh to un logo ki yaad-dahaani ke liye hai jin ke dilo mein khauf-e-Khuda hai.

4. Yeh us Khuda ki taraf se naazil huwa hai jisne zameen aur buland-tareen aasmaano ko paiyda kiya hai.

5. Woh Rehmaan arsh par ikhtiyaar-o-iqtedaar rakhne waala hai.

6. Uske liye woh sab-kuch hai jo aasmaano mein hai ya zameen mein hai ya dono ke darmiyaan hai aur zameeno ki teh mein (niche) hai.

7. Agar tum buland aawaaz se bhi baat karo to woh raaz se bhi makhfi-tar baato ko jaanne waala hai.

8. Woh Allah hai jiske alaawa koi Khuda nahi hai uske liye behtareen naam hai.

9. Kya tumhaare paas Moosa ki daastaan aayi hai.

10. Jab unho ne aag ko dekha aur apne ahel se kaha keh tum isi maqaam par thairo mai ne aag ko dekha hai shaayad mai us mein se koi angaara le aawu ya us manzil par koi rehnumai haasil kar lu.

11. Phir jab Moosa us aag ke qareeb aaye to aawaaz di gayi ke ay Moosa!

12. Mai tumhaara Parwardigaar hu lehaaza apni jootiyo (shoes) ko utaar do keh tum tuwa naam ki ek muqaddas aur pakeeza waadi mein ho.

13. Aur Ham ne tum ko muntakhab kar liya hai lehaaza jo wahi (revelation) ki ja rahi hai usey ghaur se soono.

14. Mai Allah hu mere alaawa koi Khuda nahi hai meri ibaadat karo aur meri yaad ke liye namaaz qaayem karo.

15. Yaqeenan woh qayamat aane waali hai aur mai usey chupaaye rahunga ta-keh har nafs ko uski koshish ka badla diya ja sakey.

16. Aur khabar-daar! tumhe qayamat ke khayaal se woh shakhs rok na de jiska imaan qayamat par nahi hai aur jisne apne khwahishaat ki pairwi ki hai keh is tarah tum halaak ho jaawoge.

17. Aur ay Moosa yeh tumhaare daahine haath mein kya hai.

18. Unho ne kaha keh yeh mera asaa hai jis par mai takya (pellow) karta hu aur is se apni bakriyo ke liye darakhto ki pattiya jhaadta hu aur is mein mere aur bohot se maqaasid hai.

19. Irshaad huwa to Moosa usey zameen par daal do.

20. Ab jo Moosa ne daal diya to kya dekha keh woh saanp (snake) ban kar daud raha hai.

21. Hukm huwa ke usey le lo aur daro nahi ke ham an-qareeb usey uski purani asl (asliyat) ki taraf palta dege.

22. Aur apne haath ko samait kar bagal mein kar lo yeh bagair bimaari ke safaid ho kar niklega aur yeh hamari dusri nishaani hogi.

23. Ta-keh ham tumhe apni badi nishaaniya dikha sakey.

24. Jaawo Firon ki taraf jaawo keh woh sarkash (rebellious) ho gaya.

25. Moosa ne arz ki Parwardigaar mere seeneh ko kushadah kar de.

26. Mere kaam ko aasaan kar de.

27. Aur meri zabaan ki giraah ko khol de.

28. Keh yeh log meri baat samajh sakay.

29. Aur mere ahel mein se mera wazeer qaraar de-de.

30 Haaroon ko jo mera bhai bhi hai.

31. Us se meri pusht ko mazboot kar de.

32. Usey mere kaam mein shareek bana de.

33. Ta-keh ham Teri bohot zeyada tasbeeh kar sakay.

34. Aur Tera bohot zeyada zikr kar sakay.

35. Yaqeenan Tu hamare haalaat se behtar ba-khabar hai.

36. Irshaad huwa Moosa Ham ne tumhaari muraad tumhe de di hai.

37. Aur Ham ne tum par ek aur ehsaan kiya hai.

1. Ta Ha.

2. We have not revealed the Quran to you that you may be unsuccessful.

3. Nay, it is a reminder to him who fears:

4. A revelation from Him Who created the earth and the high heavens.

5. The Beneficent God is firm in power.

6. His is what is in the heavens and what is in the earth and what is between them two and what is beneath the ground.

7. And if you utter the saying aloud, then surely He knows the secret, and what is yet more hidden.

8. Allah— there is no god but He; His are the very best names.

9. And has the story of Musa come to you?

10. When he saw fire, he said to his family: Stop, for surely I see a fire, haply I may bring to you therefrom a live coal or find a guidance at the fire.

11. So when he came to it, a voice was uttered: O Musa:

12. Surely I am your Lord, therefore put off your shoes; surely you are in the sacred valley, Tuwa,

13. And I have chosen you, so listen to what is revealed:

14. Surely I am Allah, there is no god but I, therefore serve Me and keep up prayer for My remembrance:

15. Surely the hour is coming— I am about to make it manifest— so that every soul may be rewarded as it strives:

16. Therefore let not him who believes not in it and follows his low desires turn you away from it so that you should perish;

17. And what is this in your right hand, O Musa!

18. He said: This is my staff: I recline on it and I beat the leaves with it to make them fall upon my sheep, and I have other uses for it.

19. He said: Cast it down, O Musa!

20. So he cast it down; and lo! it was a serpent running.

21. He said: Take hold of it and fear not; We will restore it to its former state:

22. And press your hand to your side, it shall come out white without evil: another sign:

23. That We may show you of Our greater signs:

24. Go to Firon, surely he has exceeded all limits.

25. He said: O my Lord! Expand my breast for me,

26. And make my affair easy to me,

27. And loose the knot from my tongue,

28. (That) they may understand my word;

29. And give to me an aider from my family:

30. Haroun, my brother,

31. Strengthen my back by him,

32. And associate him (with me) in my affair,

33. So that we should glorify Thee much,

34. And remember Thee oft.

35. Surely, Thou art seeing us.

36. He said: You are indeed granted your petition, O Musa;

37. And certainly We bestowed on you a favor at another time;

38. When We revealed to your mother what was revealed;

39. Saying: Put him into a chest, then cast it down into the river, then the river shall throw him on the shore; there shall take him up one who is an enemy to Me and enemy to him, and I cast down upon you love from Me, and that you might be brought up before My eyes;

40. When your sister went and said: Shall I direct you to one who will take charge of him? So We brought you back to your mother, that her eye might be cooled and she should not grieve and you killed a man, then We delivered you from the grief, and We tried you with (a severe) trying. Then you stayed for years among the people of Madyan; then you came hither as ordained, O Musa.

41. And I have chosen you for Myself:

42. Go you and your brother with My communications and be not remiss in remembering Me;

43. Go both to Firon, surely he has become inordinate;

44. Then speak to him a gentle word haply he may mind or fear.

45. Both said: O our Lord! Surely we fear that he may hasten to do evil to us or that he may become inordinate.

46. He said: Fear not, surely I am with you both: I do hear and see.

47. So go you both to him and say: Surely we are two apostles of your Lord; therefore send the children of Israel with us and do not torment them! Indeed we have brought to you a communication from your Lord, and peace is on him who follows the guidance;

48. Surely it has been revealed to us that the chastisement will surely come upon him who rejects and turns back.

49. (Firon) said: And who is your Lord, O Musa?

50. He said: Our Lord is He Who gave to everything its creation, then guided it (to its goal).

51. He said: Then what is the state of the former generations?

52. He said: The knowledge thereof is with my Lord in a book, my Lord errs not, nor does He forget;

53. Who made the earth for you an expanse and made for you therein paths and sent down water from the cloud; then thereby We have brought forth many species of various herbs.

54. Eat and pasture your cattle; most surely there are signs in this for those endowed with understanding.

55. From it We created you and into it We shall send you back and from it will We raise you a second time.

56. And truly We showed him Our signs, all of them, but he rejected and refused.

57. Said he: Have you come to us that you should turn us out of our land by your magic, O Musa?

58. So we too will produce before you magic like it, therefore make between us and you an appointment, which we should not break, (neither) we nor you, (in) a central place.

59. (Musa) said: Your appointment is the day of the Festival and let the people be gathered together in the early forenoon.

60. So Firon turned his back and settled his plan, then came.

61. Musa said to them: Woe to you! do not forge a lie against Allah, lest He destroy you by a punishment, and he who forges (a lie) indeed fails to attain (his desire).

62. So they disputed with one another about their affair and kept the discourse secret.

63. They said: These are most surely two magicians who wish to turn you out from your land by their magic and to take away your best traditions.

64. Therefore settle your plan, then come standing in ranks, and he will prosper indeed this day who overcomes.

65. They said: O Musa! will you cast, or shall we be the first who cast down?

66. He said: Nay! cast down. then lo! their cords and their rods— it was imaged to him on account of their magic as if they were running.

67. So Musa conceived in his mind a fear.

68. We said: Fear not, surely you shall be the uppermost,

69. And cast down what is in your right hand; it shall devour what they have wrought; they have wrought only the plan of a magician, and the magician shall not be successful wheresoever he may come from.

70. And the magicians were cast down making obeisance; they said: We believe in the Lord of Haroun and Musa.
